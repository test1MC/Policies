Store Computer Overview

The Store Computer Systemwas designed to provide a consistent, user-friendly, and highly supportable POS system within Sherwin-Williams stores. With all locations using the same software, Headquarters is able to:

  • Centrally update store systems
  • Create comparable and accurate measurement and reporting tools
  • And provide a knowledgeable Help Desk staff

Tightly managed controls placed on the system [specific hardware, software, network connectivity, and user access] maintain and protect the integrity of Company data and systems from unwanted programs, data, viruses and other threats. Lookup functionality is available even certain hardware and devices are not attached (such as Tint and Color Eye). This configuration ensures that the POS will function reliably on a daily basis providing the highest level of support for employees and service to customers. Centralized control of the system also makes specialized applications and tools such as the Forms Repository, Store Reports, SWeep, Shelf Labels on Demand, Price Record Cards, and Customer Invoices possible.
