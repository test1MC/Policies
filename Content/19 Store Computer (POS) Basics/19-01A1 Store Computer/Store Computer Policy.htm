Store Computer Policy

  1. Never shut off the system unless instructed by Store Computer Operations.
  2. Always leave air space around all equipment. The equipment needs to breathe.
  3. To clean the screen, spray the cleaner onto a soft cloth. Wipe the screen clean. Don't spray cleaner directly on the screen.
  4. To prolong the life of the keyboard, always use the keyboard skin.
  5. Use a Mouse Pad to keep the mouse operating properly.

  6. Keep the area clean and clear around the hardware. Proper airflow is very important around the Central Processing Unit and Printer.

    G. Don't fasten NOTES or MESSAGES to any of the equipment.
