SherLink Sales Tools Overview

Store employees are automatically logged in to SherLink after selecting Sales Tools Home from the Sales Tools area on the SOURCE Intranet homepage. SherLink can also be accessed on a non-store computer system.

SherLink provides helpful business tools, such as SherCall, to assist in the management of stores. SherCall enables you to document daily sales call activity. Presentation Tools, such as SherSpec, are also available on SherLink. SherSpec is a comprehensive specification-writing tool. There are many other tools available under the Presentation Tools Banner, as well as under the other tabs and banners.

Note: Review Access SherLink from Non-Store Computer System Procedure for more information on logging in to Sales Tools from a non-store computer.
