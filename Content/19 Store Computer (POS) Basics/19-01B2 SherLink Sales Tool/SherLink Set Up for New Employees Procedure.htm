SherLink Set Up for New Employees Procedure

This process does not apply to The Americas Group store employees and sales representatives.

Step

Action

1

Request SherLink access by going to www.sherlink.com.

2

Select on Contact Us Today in the Sign Up Now area.

3

Complete the profile and add a note to include your SherLink requirements.

4

You may receive a phone call or email requesting additional information.

5

Your SherLink login information will be emailed to your company email.
