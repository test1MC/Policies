Access SherLink from Non-Store Computer System Procedure

This section is intended for Columbia Paints, Duron Paint and Coatings, MAB Paints and SW Paint Store Management. This section does not apply to Automotive and Product Finishes employees.

Store management employees are permitted to access SherLink from outside of the store.

Step Action

1

In the browser address area, type www.sherlink.com.

2

When prompted for your User ID, enter the letter [s], your [store number], then the letter [r].

Example: Store Number 1234 = [s1234r].

3

Enter your SherLink password and select Login.

Note: If you do not know your password, follow the instructions in the SherLink Password Update Procedure to retrieve it.

Note: If you have any problems, select the Contact Us Today Button on the SherLink login page.
