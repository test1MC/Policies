Target Fund Replenishment Overview

Target Funds need to be replenished when more cash is disbursed from a point-of-sale terminal than received.

There may be a time when more cash was disbursed from a point-of-sale terminal than received. If this occurs, it may be necessary to replenish the target fund.

If

 Then

Your location has more than one point-of-sale terminal with established target funds and one terminal with sufficient funds (disbursement of funds will not take the terminal below the target fund) but has fallen below its target fund

Money may be moved between point-of-sale terminals using Miscellaneous Disbursement and Miscellaneous Receipt transactions at the respective terminals. See Target Fund Replenishment by Donor Terminal Procedure
You need to request another store provide assistance in replenishing your operating fund, contact your District Manager See Target Fund Replenishment by Donor Store Procedure

You need to obtain money from a Store Draft taken to the bank

See Target Fund Replenishment by Store Draft Procedure
