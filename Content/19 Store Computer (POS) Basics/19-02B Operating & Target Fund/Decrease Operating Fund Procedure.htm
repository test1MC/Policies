Decrease Operating Fund Procedure

Step Action

1

Obtain written approval from the District Manager. A copy of this approval must be retained in a Target Fund folder in Drawer Three for Filing and Retention for up to six years. The District Manager will forward a copy of the approval letter to Accounting.

2

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access the Special Menu -> select Set Target Fund and enter the Manager Password.

3

Enter the amount of the New Target Fund.

4

Press Page Down to access the Special Menu -> select Miscellaneous Cash Receipt.

5

Enter Dist & Whse - Other - 7599-099 and the decrease amount.

6

Enter Decrease Operating Fund in the comments section.

7

Total the transaction, sign the form, and place forms in batching.
