Target Fund Replenishment by Draft Procedure

This section does not apply to Duron or MAB Paints

Step Action
Process a Miscellaneous Disbursement Transaction to issue the Store Draft

1

Verify available funds for the terminal requiring replenishment.

2

From POS desktop, select Store.

3

Select Point of Sale from the Main Menu. Enter your Employee Number and press Enter.

4

Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

5

Select Disbursement from the Special Menu.

6

Select Miscellaneous by Store Draft from the Disbursement Type Menu.

7

Enter the Manager Password when prompted.

8

Input the Pay-To Information and issue the draft to either the Store Manager or the Assistant Manager.

Note: Do not make the check out to Cash as well as issue the check to yourself.

9

Press Enter at the GL Account List Window to view all available General Ledger Entries. Select the line for Dist & Whse - Other - Prime-Sub 7599-099.

10

Go to the bank to acquire cash in exchange for the Store Draft.

For more information, see Obtain Change from Bank Overview.

Process a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt upon returning from the bank

1

Verify available funds for the terminal requiring replenishment.

2

 From POS desktop, select Store.

3

Select Point of Sale from the Main Menu. Enter your Employee Number and press Enter.

4

Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

5

Select Misc Cash Receipt from the Special Menu.

6

Input the Bank Information as the BillTo Adress. Press ACCEPT [F1].

7

Press Enter at the GL Account List Window to view all available General Ledger Entries. Select the line for Dist & Whse - Other - Prime-Sub 7599-099.

8

Press COMMENT [F9] -> Type Replenishing Operating Fund as the reason and include the Draft Number. 

9

Press ACCEPT [F1].

10

Verify that the amount of cash being placed into the drawer and the replenishment amount are the same.

11

Place the money in the Cash Drawer and close it once you are done.

Note: Contact your District Manager to verify replenishment if your store has been contacted by a Duron or MAB Paints store requiring assistance in replenishing their operating fund. Once verified, process a Miscellaneous Disbursement by Store Draft transaction. Contact Stores Group Accounting at 216-515-4660 to have the draft expense transferred from the draft issuing store to the requesting store.
