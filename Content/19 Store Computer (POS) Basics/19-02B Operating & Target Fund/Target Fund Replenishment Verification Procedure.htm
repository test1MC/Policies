Target Fund Replenishment Verification Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store.

2

Select Point of Sale from the Main Menu.  Enter your Employee Number and press Enter.

3

Select No Sale/Interim Deposit from the Sale/Payment Menu.

4

Select Other from the No-Sale Reason Codes Menu.

5

When prompted to enter in an Other Description, input Verify Target Fund Amount.

6

Enter your Employee Password.

7

Count only the available cash when the drawer opens. Do not consider customer checks or any credit or charge sales into the available amount.

8

Return the money to the Cash Drawer and close it once you have counted the money.
