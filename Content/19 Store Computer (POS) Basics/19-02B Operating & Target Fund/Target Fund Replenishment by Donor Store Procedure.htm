Target Fund Replenishment by Donor Store Procedure

Step Action
Requesting Store - Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement by Store Draft

1

Contact your District Manager and ask for written approval to request another local store for assistance in replenishing your operating fund. 

2

Once approved, contact a neighboring store (you may ask any SW Paint Store in your area) to help you replenish your operating fund. If the neighboring store is able to assist you, they will need to process a Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement by Store Draft.

Note: For more information, see Target Fund Replenishment by Store Draft Procedure.
Neigboring Store – Miscellaneous Disbursement by Store Draft

1

From POS desktop, select Store.

2

Select Point of Sale from the Main Menu.  Enter your Employee Number and press Enter.

3

Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

4

Select Disbursement from the Special Menu.

5

Select Miscellaneous by Store Draft from the Disbursement Type Menu.

6

Input the Store Address as the BillTo Information. Press ACCEPT [F1].

7

Press Enter at the GL Account List Window to view all available General Ledger Entries. Select the line for Dist & Whse - Other - Prime-Sub 7599-099.

8

Enter the amount received from the donor terminal.

9

Press COMMENT [F9] and input the Neighboring Store Employee’s Name and Store Number.

10

Press ACCEPT [F1] twice.

11

Complete the transaction by issuing the Store Draft.

12

Contact Stores Group Accounting at 216-515-4660 to have the draft expense transferred from the draft issuing store to the requesting store.
Process a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt at your store once you have cashed the check and received the replenishment funds from the neighboring store

1

From POS desktop, select Store.

2

Select Point of Sale from the Main Menu.  Enter your Employee Number and press Enter.

3

Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

4

Select Misc Cash Receipt from the Special Menu.

5

Input the Store Address as the BillTo Information. Press ACCEPT [F1].

6

Press Enter at the GL Account List Window to view all available General Ledger Entries. Select the line for Dist & Whse - Other - Prime-Sub 7599-099.

7

Enter the amount received from the donor terminal.

8

Press COMMENT [F9] and input the Neighboring Store Employee’s Name and Store Number.

9

Press ACCEPT [F1] twice.

10

Select Cash from the Form of Payment Menu and press TOTAL [F12].

11

Verify that the amount of cash being placed into the drawer and the replenishment amount are the same.

12

Place the money in the Cash Drawer and close it once you are done.
