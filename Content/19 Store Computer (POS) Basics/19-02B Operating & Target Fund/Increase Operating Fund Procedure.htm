Increase Operating Fund Procedure

Step Action

1

File and retain per policy and retention schedule requirements.

2

Follow the correct procedure to obtain the new Target Funds:

Division Procedure
Automotive, Chemical Coatings, and SW Paints

Issue a payment draft payable to either the Store Manager or the Assistant Manager by processing a Disbursement by Store Draft transaction. Do not issue the check to yourself. Use Dist & Whse - Other - 7599-099.
Duron and MAB Paints

Follow the DM Approval Replenishment Procedure.

3

After returning from the bank or once you have received the currency from your District Manager:

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select No Sale from the Sale/Payment Menu to place the cash in the cash drawer.

4

Select Other as the Reason Code, and enter Increase Operating Fund. The Store Manager must approve the transaction.

5

Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

6

Select Set Target Fund from the Special Menu.

7

If

 Then

You are increasing Operating Funds by Store Drafts or DM Replenishments

Enter the amount of the New Target Fund.

You are increasing Operating Funds by using daily sales
  • Enter the amount of the New Target Fund or the Available Increased Amount.
  • Perform a cash disbursement for the dollar amount of the increased amount coded to Dist & Whse - Other - 7599-099.
  • Forward a copy of the transaction and the authorization letter to Marcy Lee marcy.strojin@sherwin.com in Stores Group Accounting.
