Manual Forms Inquiry Procedure

Step

Action

1

Press Inquiry [F4] -> Store Accountability Menu -> Manual Forms Inquiry -> Enter the date range.

2

When you view the report on the screen, notice any blank lines. It is possible the blank lines indicate missing validated Manual POST forms.

3

All Manual POST forms must be validated through the store computer system by selecting Enter and Validate Manual Form from the Sale/Payment Menu.

4

If your validation printer is not working you are required to write the terminal/transaction and date on the Manual POST form.
