View Price Record Cards on Inquiry Menu Procedure

Step

Action

1

Press Inquiry [F4] -> Select Price Record Cards -> Enter the customer’s Sherwin-Williams Account Number or press Lookup [F3].

2

Enter the A/R Job Number and Price Record Card Number. Comments and Authorized Buyer information will return to the screen. Select either A) Display Prices for this PRC or B) View on this screen.

If

Then

A

Enter the sales number or press Lookup [F3]. Product specific information will return to the screen. Enter the purchase quantity. The customer’s price will return to the screen. Press any key to continue. Press Cancel [F5] to return to the menu.

B

View the customer’s Price Record Card on this screen. Press Cancel [F5] to return to the menu.

Note: No changes can be made to the Price Record Card in this area. This feature is for viewing only.
