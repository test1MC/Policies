Access Inquiry During Downtime Procedure

Do not create Quick Orders during downtime because you could lose all of the information when the system is restored. During downtime, complete all manual POST Forms for all Point of Sale transactions.

Step

Action

1

During downtime, from the POS desktop, select Quick Order.

2

Press INQUIRY [F4].

3

This provides access to all inquiry capabilities, even during downtime.

For example: Select LOOKUP [F3] to retrieve customer transaction information. There are also options to price the customer’s manual transaction. Scan the product barcode when prompted to enter the sales number, just like in Point of Sale.
