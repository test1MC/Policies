Book Plan Wallcovering Books Overview

Book Plan Wallcovering books are sent to you on your weekly STAR order from the DSC. When you accept the receipts on the STAR purchase order, the new wallcovering book is automatically added to your Wallcovering Book Register.

The Wallcovering Book Register will automatically update each time you properly receive a new wallcovering book against the purchase order. The book register is created after accepting the receipts on the purchase order. Because updating is an automatic process, there is no need to add wallcovering books to the register if they are received properly on the purchase order. The expiration date will automatically update from the SWAT database. However, if there is no expiration date on the SWAT database, you will be required to manually enter it at the store.

If the store computer system is not able to retrieve the book number from SWAT, a WC BOOKS RECEIVED - NOT REGISTERED report will generate that shows the 7-digit sales number and the 6-digit book number of the book it cannot locate. Retrieve this report from the Reports File to research the number and then contact SWAT to determine the correct number.

Wallcovering books received directly from the distributor can also be added to the register.
