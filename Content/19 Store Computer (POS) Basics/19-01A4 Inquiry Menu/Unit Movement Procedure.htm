Unit Movement Procedure

Unit Movement will help you to identify all activity for a specific sales numbered product.

Step

Action

1

Press Inquiry [F4] -> Select Unit Movement -> Enter a Specific Sales Number to research all activity for the product.

2

Leave the date blank if you want to review all history.

3

Press [Enter] with the highlight bar on a specific line to move to the bottom section of the Unit Movement Inquiry.

4

Once in the bottom section of the Unit Movement Inquiry, press [Enter] again to review specific information related to each type of transaction or function.

5

This process will enable you to review Inventory History by Sales Number and quickly identify discrepancies.

6

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Select Reports -> Select Unit Movement Report to print the report for a specific sales numbered product.
