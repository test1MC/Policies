After Hours Sales Procedure

If

Then

Customer paid by cash or check

Make the proper change or ask the customer for proper identification. Then complete a separate bank deposit form for the "after hours" transaction. Place the separate bank deposit and cash or check in an envelope and include it in the nightly deposit bag.

Customer paid by credit card

Call for voice authorization. Then complete a credit card form and receive a credit card imprint to verify that the credit card was in your store. Place the completed POST and credit card form in the safe or locked filing cabinet.

Customer paid by S-W Charge

Verify that the customer's account is in good standing if the customer pays by S-W Charge. Place the completed POST form in the safe or locked filing cabinet.

Note: Process the sale as soon as possible the following day. After processing the sale, process an interim deposit transaction to reflect the previous days "after hours" bank deposit.
