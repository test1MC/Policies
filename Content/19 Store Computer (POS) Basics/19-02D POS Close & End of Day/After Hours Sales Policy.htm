After Hours Sales Policy

  1. If the store computer system is closed for the day, follow downtime procedures for any transaction processed after closing the system.
  2. Complete a Manual Point of Sale Transaction (POST) form capturing the entire sale. If the customer pays by cash the employee must prepare a separate bank deposit form for the "after hours" transaction and include it in the nightly deposit bag.
  3. After processing the transaction the following day the employee must perform an Interim Deposit transaction for the "after hours" transaction deposit amount.
  4. Any manual form containing the complete customer credit card number cannot be retained in the forms container. Retain these forms in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe until you’re ready to batch the forms. All manual forms must be retained in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe after batching in a folder titled Processed Manual Forms.
  5. If the delivery is paid by cash or check, the payment should be returned to the store and processed the same day.
    1. If the payment cannot be returned the same day, it must be returned and processed within two hours of the driver's start time the following business day.
    2. The start time will be determined by the Attendance and Time start of day for the employee.
  6. Regular use of this procedure requires: 
    1. District Management approval must be on file. 
    2. Regular use should only be taken under consideration when there is considerable personal risk to the employees making the deposit nightly, security of SW funds while in employee possession is assured, and deposit will be made the following morning at bank open.
    3. Money must be handled by store management only.
