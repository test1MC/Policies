Manual Close Policy

  1. The Manual Close procedures MUST be followed when the store computer system is not operating properly. Cash Drawer balancing MUST be completed through the computer system or in the manual format.
  2. Make sure that you have several printed copies of the Manual Administrative Close Worksheet printed from word processing. To view, print, or complete this document on your system, access the Forms Repository on SOURCE. Search the form name or keyword “close”  in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word(s) will appear below in the form description field.
  3. All customer check and credit card information MUST be captured on the Manual POST Form for each transaction.
  4. If the equipment is still down at the close of the business day, follow manual Close procedures. See Processing a Manual Close Procedure.
  5. If downtime consists of one or more days, bank deposits MUST be prepared at the conclusion of each business day and taken to the bank nightly after closing the store.
  6. Interim deposits are required if the cash count is $500 over the operating fund during the business day. For more information, see Make an Interim Deposit Procedure.
