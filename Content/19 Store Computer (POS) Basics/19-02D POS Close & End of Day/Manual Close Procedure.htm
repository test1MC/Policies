Manual Close Procedure

Step Action
Completing a Manual Close

1

Make sure that all customer, check, and credit card information has been captured on the Manual POST Forms for each transaction. Customer information includes: transaction date, name, address, city, state, phone number, and proper identification.

Note: This information will be used when entering the transaction into the computer system.

2

Use the Manual Administrative Close Worksheet to assist in closing the POS. Enter all areas as outlined on the form.

3

Prepare a bank deposit by adding all Cash and subtracting the Cash Total from the Operating Fund (do not include the operating fund in the deposit). Retain a copy of the deposit slip in the safe.

4

Store all SW forms used for transactions and all Credit Card forms in the safe.

5

Put the store’s operating fund in the safe.

6

Take the Bank Deposit (currency and checks) to the bank after closing the store.

Note: For more information see 01-02A Bank Deposits.
After the Computer System is Operable

1

Process each Manual POST form. From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select Enter & Validate Manual Form from the Sale/Payment Menu.

2

Total each Bank Deposit and enter the total amount as one Interim Deposit when closing for the current day.
