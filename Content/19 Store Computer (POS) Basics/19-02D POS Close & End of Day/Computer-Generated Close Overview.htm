Computer-Generated Close Overview

The Computer-Generated Close will print on the continuous forms printer after the Drawer Count and Over/Short Amount have been accepted.

Features Descriptions
Accounts Receivable

Reference daily receivable movements
Control Totals

Net totals by accounting category
Transactions

First and last transaction number and total transactions
Statistics

Number of Voids and No Sales processed between Open and Close. Number and dollar amount for Cash Refunds and Disbursements processed between Open and Close

Note: Each of the transactions in the Statistics area are Non-Sale related. The transactions in the Statistics area should be audited on a regular basis to check the number of Voids, No Sales, etc.

System Totals

Computer-generated total for business between Full Open and Full Close
Drawer Count

Total drawer count entered into the Drawer Count screen

Over/Short

Drawer Count + Accountability = Over/Short
Deposit Totals

Deposit for checks and currency/coin for transactions processed between Open and Close

Drafts

Breakdown of Store Drafts processed between Open and Close
Comments

Over/Short reason, Cash/Check Variance Reason, and Close Comments will display in this area
Interim Deposits

Recap of up to four (4) Interim Deposits processed between Open and Close
