Over/Short at Close Policy

You will be prompted to enter a reason for any over/short amount. To easily identify the status of the drawer closing amount, color has been added to the displayed amount. The key is as follows:

  • Overage = Yellow
  • Shortage = Red
  • Zero Balance = Green
  1. Anytime an over/short amount of a penny or more is accepted the computer system will prompt for the reason. This reason must be filled out before continuing.
  2. Any unresolved discrepancy over/short (+/-) $25.00 MUST be reported to the District Manager via email. Retain a copy of this report for 18 months.
  3. The Loss, Offense & Incident Reporting process must be completed through the Quick Entry System on SOURCE and submitted within 3 days for any unresolved discrepancy over/short (+/-) $75.00. Loss Prevention will monitor unresolved discrepancies. For more information see Loss Prevention Overview.
  4. The Store Manager is responsible for reviewing Over/Short activity on the Store Computer System. For more information see Over/Short at Close Procedure.
