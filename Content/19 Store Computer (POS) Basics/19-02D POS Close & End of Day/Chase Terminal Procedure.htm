Chase Terminal Procedure

This section applies to General Paint Stores only

This procedure will process all sales that have been entered since the last time the terminal was balanced. It will ensure that any charges/credits are applied to customers’ cards and any funds due to you are sent to your account. As part of the balancing process, you should print a Totals or Detail report to confirm your batch totals. If you have processed transactions on the Chase terminal, you must ensure that you balance and close it DAILY in order to receive timely deposit of your funds.

Step Action
Chase Terminal

1

Did you process debit card sales today?

If Then
Yes Continue to Step 2.
No Proceed to Close Transaction from Main POS Procedure.

2

Press the 4th Purple Key from the left to open the Reports Menu.

3

Select the report you wish to run by pressing F2 or F3 or F4, or press the 1st Purple Key (arrow key) to move through additional reports.

4

The selected report will print.

Note: Additional prompts may appear for options, depending on the report that is selected.

5

Press the Red Cancel Key on the bottom left to return to the main menu.

6

Press F4 for SETTLEMENT –> A totals report will print.

7

Enter the Batch Total from your Totals or Details reports and press OK.

8

The batch will settle and the current batch is then deleted from the terminal.
Complete Interim Cash Deposit

1

Print the summary payment by-type report from the POS and compare to Chase's terminal summary report for transactions by-type (VISA, MC, Debit, Others-gift card) during the drawer reconciliation.

2

Do the Chase and POS summaries match?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 3.
No Identify the discrepancy, contact the customer and resolve the issue.

Note: If help is needed, contact Chase at 1-800-265-5158.

3

Process an Interim Cash Deposit using the total of the debit transactions from the debit machine for that day.

Note: This process deducts these debit transactions from the drawer and records them as a bank deposit transfer.

4

The Debit machine report should match to the customer receipts in the drawer. Retain this with your daily batching.
