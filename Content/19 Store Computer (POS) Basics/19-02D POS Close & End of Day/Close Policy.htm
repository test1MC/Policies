Close Policy

Close is a transaction that is run at the end of each business day to balance the stores’ daily totals. To review, see Close Transaction and End of Day Training. Each store computer terminal must be closed each night.

  1. Before closing the Point of Sale, all Credit Card transactions using Manual POST forms and Credit Card Slips must be processed.
  2. After the last terminal is closed, a Bank Deposit Slip must be filled out including store information, date, and amount of deposit for all of the terminals. 
    1. The bank deposit slip must be completed in duplicate. The bank will return one validated bank deposit slip.
    2. The store copy of the Check Deposit Log shows the total cash and check deposit information, therefore it is not necessary to retain an invalidated copy of the deposit slip until you receive the validated bank deposit slip. For more information, see Bank Deposit Procedures. 
  3. Once the close has been completed, the nightly deposit is to be taken to the bank and dropped in the night deposit box.
  4. The Cash Drawer must be left open after closing the point of sale.
