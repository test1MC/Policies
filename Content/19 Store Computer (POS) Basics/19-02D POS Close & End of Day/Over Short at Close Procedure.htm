Over/Short at Close Procedure

Step Action
1 From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select Close from the Sale/Payment Menu -> Enter drawer count.

2

If the amount over/short is unacceptable, enter the exact amount.

3

Recount and enter the Cash, Checks, and Credit Cards. Make sure that all funds in the safe are included in the count. Verify that all Miscellaneous Receipts or Disbursement Transactions were processed.

4

If the amount is acceptable, enter the exact amount.

5

If applicable, enter the reason for the over/short. Also, if applicable, complete the Complete an LOI Report Procedure reporting process.
