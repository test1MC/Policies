Change Store Hours in SOURCE Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the SOURCE Desktop -> Browser Button -> enter Employee Number and Password.

Note: The Store Hours display is in the upper left-hand corner.

2

Click Adjust Store Hours and make the necessary adjustments.

3

Press Submit.

Note: The store hours noted on SOURCE are used by the 1-800-4SHERWIN hotline number. Callers to this number will hear the store hours as they are noted on SOURCE.

Note: It will take 24 hours for the change to reflect online.
