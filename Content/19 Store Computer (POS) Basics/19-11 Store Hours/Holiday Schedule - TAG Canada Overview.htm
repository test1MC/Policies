  • Regular part-time and full-time employees who meet the work requirements will receive holiday pay for the above designated holidays.
  • Branches normally closed on Sunday should schedule employees for another day off during the same bi-weekly period.
  • If a non-exempt employee works on a designated holiday, the employee will be paid for hours worked in addition to the holiday pay.
  • Part-time employees will receive holiday pay based upon provincial regulations.
  • Although not designated as official holidays (no holiday pay), the hours of operation for all stores on the following dates are as follows (hours should be made up in the same week):
