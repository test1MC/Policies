Store Hours Overview

If the normal store hours are changed, they must be changed to reflect the new store hours. For example, adjust the store hours if the store hours change between winter and summer or the District Manager states that will be open on Sunday.

It is critical to maintain accurate store hours in both SOURCE and the POS because:

  • The hours maintained on SOURCE feed the brand website store locator used by customers to locate the store
  • The hours in the POS are used for the reporting and receipt functions in POS - the hours in the Open/Close Schedule will be printed on store receipts.
