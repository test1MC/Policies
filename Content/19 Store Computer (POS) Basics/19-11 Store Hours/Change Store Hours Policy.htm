Change Store Hours Policy

  1. Store Managers are responsible for making any necessary store hours changes.
  2. Whenever store hours are adjusted, store management must:
    1. Adjust the Open/Close Schedule in POS
    2. Adjust Store Hours on the SOURCE Intranet
    3. Change hours posted on Store Front
