Look Up Price Record Card Procedure

Step

Action

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Pricing -> Price Record Card-View.

2

Enter the customer account number or click on the magnifying glass icon to perform a Customer Lookup.

Note: For a Customer Lookup, enter the Home Store and/or the customer's zip and area code and press the SEARCH button when complete.

3

Click on the correct Job Number and/or Price Record Card.

Note: In addition to viewing the customer's Price Record Card, enter a Sales or Product Number, Size, and Quantity, to look up pricing for a product.

Note to Non-Store locations: Price Record Cards can be viewed at the store or by accessing the SOURCE Intranet while using a personal SherLink User ID.
