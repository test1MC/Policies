Print Price Schedules Policy

  1. Price schedules must be kept off the sales counter and out of viewing range of the customer.
  2. The District Manager will give printing instructions for Sales Representative copies of price schedules.
  3. The latest version of the price schedule should always be printed.
    1. The price schedule reprints log can be reviewed by pressing Inquiry[F4] -> Schedule Print Log.
    2. A Table of Contents will always print followed by the Price List.
