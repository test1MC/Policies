Print Current Price Schedules Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Store Button -> Manager Functions -> Print Price Schedules.

2

Select the price schedule to be printed by pressing Enter to change from Print/Do Not Print. Press Accept.

Note: The price schedules will be sent to the Reports File.

3

Press the Reports key to access the price schedules to begin printing.

Note: Print one set of each price schedule IMMEDIATELY. Place the price schedules in the Price Schedule Book.

  • Do not give price schedules to customers.
  • Do not print more price schedules than needed in the store.
