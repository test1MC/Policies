Adjust Store Specials Procedure

This section only applies to Automotive Branches and Product Finishes Facilities

Store Special Price Lists are used to assist in better management of inventory and to help eliminate overstock and reduce the time it takes to closeout merchandise.

Step Action
1 The following options will be available for maintaining Store Special Price Lists (SPL). Select one of these options and the Store Special Maintenance window will be returned to the screen.
2 Follow the system prompts in the lower corner of the screen to modify the Store Specials file. Change or Delete an existing Store Special by first pressing the Lookup [F3] to view all current Store Specials.

Note: Anytime a Store Special is added, the store computer system will generate a new Version Number. To delete a Store Special, press Lookup [F3] when prompted for the Version Number to review all store specials.
3

After making a special display on the sales floor, enter the Managers Special Prices. The computer system will automatically retrieve these prices to all designated customers.

Note: The Expiration Date should not be set for a period greater than 30 days. When stock is depleted, delete the Store Special Pricing.
