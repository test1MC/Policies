Manager Functions Policy

  1. Only store employees with job titles of Manager, Assistant Manager, or Operations Manager will have knowledge of the manager password and have access to Manager Functions.
    1. Any employee whose job title is not of Manager, Assistant Manager, or Operations Manager must have approval on file from District Management permitting them access to Manager Functions.
  2. Store management must enter the system manager's password to gain access to the Manager Function. An audible beep will sound and an error message will appear on the monitor after entering the wrong password.
