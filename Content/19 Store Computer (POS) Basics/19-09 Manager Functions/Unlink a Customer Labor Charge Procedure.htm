Unlink a Customer Labor Charge Procedure

“Unlink" an installer from a point of sale transaction if they should not be paid for that specific entry and has not yet been paid.

Step Action
1

From the POS Dektop -> Store Button -> Manager Functions -> File Maintenance -> Customer Labor.

Note: Once a labor charge is unlinked, it will no longer appear in the installer payment list or on the customer transaction not paid to installer report.
