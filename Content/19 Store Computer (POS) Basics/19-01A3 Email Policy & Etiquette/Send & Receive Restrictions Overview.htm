Send & Receive Restrictions Overview

Because of the number of stores and Email users, there are certain Restrictions for the Storage of Email and the Sending and Receiving of Email. Storage restrictions are broken down into two areas, Retentions and Quotas, applying to messages already sent or received.

Retentions refer to the length of time messages are maintained on the email system.

Quotas refer to the total number of messages stored and the total amount of space used.

  • Total size limit of any one message and attachment sent or received is 50 MB.

    • Note: Use caution when sending large attachments to multiple e-mail addresses as it will significantly affect network performance across all stores.

  • Maximum number of people you can address on an outbound email is currently 41, including To:, CC:, BCC.
  • Only business-related attachment types are allowed to be routed to the store mailbox. Non-business-related attachments will be stripped from the message.
    Note: Examples of attachments that will be automatically stripped: .avi, .mpegs = movie files, .mp3 = audio files.
  • There is a physical limit to space for email. After receiving an email reminder that the mailbox is almost full, delete unnecessary emails. If old emails are not deleted, the Inbox might not be able to receive any new messages.
