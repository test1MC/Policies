Send an Email Procedure

Step

Action
1

From the SOURCE Homescreen, select the Email button -> enter employee number and password.

Note: District Managers and Auditors can access Email by using their District Manager or Auditor password.

2

On the main Email desktop screen, click on the Write icon.

3

To send an email to another store, simply enter the store number in the TO: field. Be patient, the store email address will load to the screen. Alternatively, add recipients to the email from the Address Book (more info on the Address Book below).

4

Type in the subject and compose the message.

5

Do you want to attach a file?

If Then
Yes Continue with Step 5.
No Proceed to Step 8.

In the open message, click the Attach icon. This will open the store folders.

6

Click on the folder with the document.

7

Locate the file to attach to the email, select it, and then click Open. The document name appears in the Attachments box next to the message.

8

Click send.

Utilize these basic practices when Receiving and Replying to Email:

  • Whenever logging in to Email, new messages will automatically be listed in bold.
  • If expecting a message that you know was recently sent, click on Get Mail. This actively checks for any new mail.
  • Any email that received in the Inbox is automatically sorted by date. Change the order by clicking on any column.
  • To open an email, double-click on the entry in the Inbox. The message, along with the sender's name, will appear on the screen. To close the message, click the [X] in the top right of the screen. The screen will return to the Inbox. Now you're ready to process the next email request.
  • If the store receives an email message with a document attached to it, there will be an attachment box next to the message. Click on the document name once, then click on File, and Save As. Save it to a folder, and then open it from there.
  • All email messages, whether read or unread, will be automatically removed from the system after 60 days. To keep an email message, please print a hard copy.
  • The computer has an Address Book to store email addresses for future use. To add an address, click on Address Book and then New Card.
