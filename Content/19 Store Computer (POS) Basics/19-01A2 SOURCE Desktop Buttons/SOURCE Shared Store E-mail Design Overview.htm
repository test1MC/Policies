SOURCE Shared Store E-mail Design Overview

Shared store e-mail accounts provide the most reliable method of e-mail communications for Sherwin-Williams' stores, headquarters, field, and customers. Store e-mail communications, much like the store phone or fax, are an open line of communication designed so that e-mail communication is processed quickly and efficiently. Shared accounts ensure that headquarters and field communications will be received in a timely manner and provide customers with the assurance that there will always be an employee available to respond to their communications. A shared store email account also maintains consistent store operations and communications when employee turn-over occurs.
