Search Capabilities Overview

There are three types of Search available for store use:

Part

Function

 Example

Hot Search

Allows interruption of whatever is being run to find the information requested.

An employee is entering a STAR ORDER and receives a phone call from a customer wanting to know the price of an item currently on sale. The employee can press the INQUIRY key to search for pricing for any sales numbered product. After getting the answer for the customer, the employee can press the CANCEL key and return to entering the STAR Order.

Lookup [F3]

Allows finding information on the Computer System that previously would have been referenced manually.

 If the contractors Account Number is not known, while running a charge sale at the Point of Sale, press the LOOKUP[F3] key and enter all or part of the customer name. A list of contractors will then be returned to the screen based on the information entered. Move the highlight bar using the ARROW keys. Press ENTER to insert the information to the Point Of Sale screen.

Wild Card Search

Is used when having difficulty locating a product when going through the regular look-up procedures.

By entering the * (Asterisk-shifted 8) and then a sequence of numbers or letters while working in the Customer or Product field, you will be able to perform look ups related to the sequence.

Note: The Wild Card Search is a slow process and is not recommended when in a hurry at the Point of Sale.

Full Screen Processing

  • Full screen processing enables viewing the complete transaction on the display screen. Full screen processing will allow you to add/delete line items and change any line item entry before totaling the Sale. By entering the Sales Number, the Computer System will retrieve the correct price from memory, and automatically display the correct product description and size. The customer's receipt will print on the Continuous Forms Printer at the end of the transaction.
