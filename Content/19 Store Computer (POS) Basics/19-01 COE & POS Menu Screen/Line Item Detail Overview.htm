Line Item Detail Overview

Area

Description

1

Indicates keys that can be used for transaction in progress.

Note: Press COMMENTS [F9] to add comments to the line item entry, which, when printed, will appear on the line below the related retail item.

2

Up to a fifteen digit sales number that identifies the type of merchandise purchased. The 12-digit Universal Product Code (UPC/Bar Code) number can also be entered.

Note:: Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for products

3

A two-digit size abbreviation of the item being purchased (ex. 14 - Quarts, 16 - Gallons, 20 - 5's).

Note: Size codes will be used during entry, but the size of item being purchased will print on the customers invoice. Press LOOKUP [F3] to launch the Size List Menu or access size codes via INQUIRY [F4] -> Size Codes.

4

Up to a twenty-character description of the product being purchased

5

Number of items being purchased.

6

Unit selling price of each item being purchased. To review the pricing method for a product, arrow up to the appropriate line item and press Shift + PRC Method [F9]. The system will retrieve the price’s source and display in the following fields:

Codes

Definition

Version

The system assigned number to a schedule that returned the price.

*

Not priced from a schedule. Method will explain where price came from.

Examples

DX060ABC

A Market Segment Residential Repaint

Level A from the RR Market Segment Schedule

Br060MV1

LP-15.0%

ProDiscounts

*

Exception

Price pulled from customer’s PRC

7

Extended price of items being purchased. (Quantity X Net Selling = Extended Price)

8

Individual flag for tax rule on each line item.

Note: For more information on tax—including exceptions and exemptions—see section 13.0 in the Policy and Procedure Manual.

9

Continuous before tax amount of purchase.

10

Standard sales tax applied to the Sale.

11

Amount owed by customer, including any discounts and sales tax, for the transaction being processed.

Note: For information on Form of Payment, see 08-01C Form of Payment.
