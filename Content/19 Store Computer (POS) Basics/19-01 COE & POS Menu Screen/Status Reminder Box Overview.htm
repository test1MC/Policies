Status Reminder Box Overview

The status reminder box details important sales and order info, including:

  • Purchase Orders Sent to Vendors
  • Customer Orders (including E-Commerce)
  • Receiving Information
  • SWAT Orders
  • Reports
  • Wallcovering Book Lending Information

Note: Store managers are responsible for checking the status of and responding to all open orders placed in your store, including E-Commerce orders. For more information on orders, see Customer Order Entry (COE) Overview.
