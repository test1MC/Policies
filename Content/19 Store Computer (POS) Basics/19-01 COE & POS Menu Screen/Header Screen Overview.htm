Header Screen Overview

Area

Description

1

The employee name will return to the screen after entering the employee number. This will verify that the correct employee number was entered. The employee's first name will print on the invoice.

2

Information relating to the customer making the purchase. This information can be manually entered or automatically returned to the screen from the Price Record Card. Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for Account Number or Account Name.

Field Description For more information...
Acct No Number that identifies the customer's personal account.  
Job No Number that identifies which job is being handled by the store.  
PRC The store's record for an account with established special selling prices. They are managed using PRC Maintenance on SOURCE. Price Record Card (PRC) Maintenance Overview.
Customer Identifies the name of the customer, either an individual or business.  
Terr A territory number is a four-digit number that identifies each Sales Representative's territory, and ensures that proper credit for the sale is given to the Sales Representative. The numbers are assigned through the District Credit Office.
CAC Customer Account Classification codes, used to classify customers into groups that have the same focus and interests, and who would most likely buy the same products. Customer Account Classification (CAC) Codes Overview

Note: For more information on customer accounts, see Cash Account Policy and Commercial Credit Account Overview. For more information on PRC, see Price Record Card (PRC) Maintenance Overview.

3

Store number, terminal, and transaction number will be on the screen at all times. No entry will be required in this area.

4

Enter the customer's purchase order number if presented at the time of purchase. You will be reminded to enter the purchase order number if the Price Record Card designates that a purchase order is required. If applicable, enter any Comments that pertain to the purchase, which will print on the customer Invoice.

5

Customer billing address can be manually entered or it will automatically fill in when retrieved from a Price Record Card.

6

Customer shipping address, if different from the billing address. When retrieving information from a Price Record Card this information will automatically fill in or can be manually entered.

Note: Price Record Card Comments and Authorized Buyers will display if originally entered on the customer's Price Record Card. This information will not print on the customer invoice. The Jobs/PRC Menu will return after entering the customer’s account number. For more information on PRCs, see Price Record Card (PRC) Maintenance Overview.
