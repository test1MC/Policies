Suspend or Recall Procedure

If Then
Quick Order
StepAction

1

Press the Suspend/ Recall [F10]to postpone completing the current Quick Order so that you can wait on another customer.

2

After waiting on the other customer resume the previous Quick Order by pressing the Shift + F10 Suspend/Recall key to recall the order.

Note: All Quick Orders that have not been linked to a point-of-sale transactions return to the screen.

3

Select the correct order.
Sales Transaction

Step

Action

1

Press Suspend/ Recall [F10] to postpone a transaction and then confirm your selection.

Note: The transaction in process will be suspended and held in the Suspend/Void file. Suspending transactions allow the system to perform other functions and recall this transaction later in the day to avoid re-entering the information.

2

To re-launch order, press Shift + Suspend/ Recall [F10] on the Sale/Payment Menu. Be aware that the transaction number is different than the suspended transaction number. The transactions will be cross-referenced internally to ensure that you have a good audit trail.

Note: You will be reminded at the end of the day if there are any suspended transactions that have not been recalled. Any transaction that is not recalled by the end of the day will remain a voided transaction.
