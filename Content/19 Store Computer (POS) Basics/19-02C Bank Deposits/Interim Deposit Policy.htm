Interim Deposit Policy

Interim Deposit allows employees to count the drawer and process an Interim Deposit in one step.

  1. An Interim Deposit must be made if $500.00 in currency over the store’s operating fund accumulates prior to 3:00 P.M.
  2. The deposited amount should be a rounded figure (i.e. $500, $550, etc.).
  3. Always leave a small amount in excess of the operating fund.
  4. Immediately take the Interim Deposit to the bank. Do not hold the currency in the store safe.
  5. Employees may perform no more than four Interim Deposits per terminal each day.
  6. Only approved keyholders are to make deposits. 
    1. If a keyholder is not present at the time an interim deposit is needed, the deposit should be prepared and placed in the safe until the keyholder is available to make the deposit.

Note: An explanation is required on the Close Transaction if an Interim Deposit is processed in error. TAG Accounting will contact the store regarding any questions about the variance.
