Bank Questions Help

Any banking questions related to the depository account (i.e. NSF check questions, unexplained variances, bank related charges/credits to your P&L, problems with the deposit verification process) should be emailed to the Bookkeeping Dept., FSS –Cash and Banking at bank.questions@sherwin.com. In the email, include the following information:

  • Store number and contact name
  • Bank name
  • Date of the deposit
  • Amount in question
  • Specific issues or concerns

Note: Do not contact the bank. An FSS Bookkeeper will respond to the email within 1-2 business days.
