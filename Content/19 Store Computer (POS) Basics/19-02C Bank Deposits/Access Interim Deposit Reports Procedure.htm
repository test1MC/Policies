Access Interim Deposit Reports Procedure

Step

Action
1 Select the Store button from the POS desktop.

2

Press INQUIRY [F4] from behind the Main Menu.

3

Select Inquiry Menu and then POS – Point of Sale.

4

Press ENTER through the Generalized Transaction Inquiry fields until the Sale/Payment Menu appears at the Transaction ID field.

5

Select No Sale/Interim Deposit. Press ACCEPT [F1] and sort according to the specifications.

Note: Notice that the processing employee’s name returns to the screen and prints on all reports for Interim Deposits.

6

Choose to view the transaction on the screen, send to the printer, or press CANCEL [F5] to send a journal to the Reports File.
