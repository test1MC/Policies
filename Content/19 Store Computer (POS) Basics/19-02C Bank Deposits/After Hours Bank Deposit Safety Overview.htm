After Hours Bank Deposit Safety Overview

Nightly bank deposits must be safe.  Please exercise discretion when using an after hours deposit facility and follow these best practices:

  • Avoid making after-dark deposits.
  • Minimize the time spent at the facility.
  • At a drive-up facility, keep all the car doors locked and roll windows down only when making the deposit.
  • Be familiar with the facility’s surroundings and aware of any irregularities in the building.
  • Notify the bank or the district manager of any minor discrepancies with the lights or facility (i.e. damaged, burnt out).
  • Immediately alert the local authorities if there is any suspicious damage to the building that might suggest a break-in or suspicious activity by people loitering in the vicinity. Follow up with the District Manager and Loss Prevention.
