SherSpec Overview

Accessible from SherLink, SherSpec is a comprehensive specification-writing tool. A specification outlines the preparation, products, and process needed to fulfill a customer’s request. Specifications are written so the customer may measure the degree of conformance as the job is completed, but it does not serve as a contract. After entering the necessary information into SherSpec, the sales representative has a completed specification that can be given directly to the customer.
