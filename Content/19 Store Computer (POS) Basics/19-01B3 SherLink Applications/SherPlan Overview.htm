SherPlan Overview

Accessible from SherLink, SherPlan is a comprehensive planning-tool that allows sales representatives to determine sales potential and develop a sales plan with specific goals for the current or upcoming year. As the year progresses, SherPlan automatically updates sales information and representatives have the opportunity to edit and revise their plans when necessary. This tool can also be used when representatives meet with their sales managers to discuss sales goals and opportunities.
