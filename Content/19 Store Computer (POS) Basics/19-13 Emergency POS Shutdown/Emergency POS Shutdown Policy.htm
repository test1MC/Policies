Emergency POS Shutdown Policy

  1. All employees must follow the directions of all civil authorities (police, fire, civil defense) before, during, and after a hurricane, severe weather, or other emergency situations that present a physical threat to people’s welfare.
    1. Be sure to communicate any evacuation orders that affect the branch or employees to the Area Sales Manager as soon as possible.
  2. If the store is ordered to close or evacuate by civil authorities, immediately notify District or Area Management.
