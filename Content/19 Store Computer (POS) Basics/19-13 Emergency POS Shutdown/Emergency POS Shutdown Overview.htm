Emergency POS Shutdown Overview

  • During a natural disaster or civil emergency, the primary concern is the welfare of Sherwin-Williams employees and their families, customers, and others the company can reasonably assist. Please review and implement the following action plans in the event you are notified by either your management or civil authorities of a need to evacuate or close your store: Emergency POS Shutdown, Emergency Situation Preparation, & Evacuation POS Shutdown.
  • Maintain constant communication with both managers and employees throughout this emergency. Exchange home & cell phone numbers to keep in contact.
