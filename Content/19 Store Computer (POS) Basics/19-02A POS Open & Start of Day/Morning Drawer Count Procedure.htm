Morning Drawer Count Procedure

After the Start of Day Procedures are completed, the following steps for the morning drawer count must be completed before Point-of-Sale transactions can begin. The morning drawer count procedure is for audit purposes and will not change the count in the drawer.

Step

Action

1

Enter your Employee Number.

2

Enter the cash and coin amount into the appropriate fields. Enter $0.00 for checks. Select Accept [F1].

Note: Selecting Cancel [F5] during the counting portion of the morning drawer count will create a voided transaction, and you will have to re-enter the drawer count.

3

Enter Y/N to accept.

If

Then

Y(es)

Start POS transactions

No

Return to the drawer count screen for editing

4

If

Then

The morning drawer count is entered and accurately reflects the close drawer count of the previous day

No error message will appear.

The morning drawer count does not reflect the close drawer count from the previous day

A warning will appear stating that the operating fund does not match.  If so, try and rectify the overage/shortage.  If it can’t be rectified, the close transaction will be over/short for that drawer at the close of business day.

Note: Once the Morning Drawer Count is complete, employees can generate a GTI Inquiry for open transactions and overages/shortages.
