POS Employee Passwords Policy

The combination of Employee Number and Password identifies which transactions or functions were performed on the Store Computer System.  The password must be at least six characters and it can be any combination of letters and numbers.  

  1. All exempt and non-exempt store employees including the Store Manager, Assistant Manager, and Operations Manager are required to have a password.
  2. Employees are not permitted to save their passwords.
