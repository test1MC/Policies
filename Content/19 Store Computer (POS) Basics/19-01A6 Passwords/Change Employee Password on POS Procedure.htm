Change Employee Password on POS Procedure

Step

Action

1

Enter the Employee Number at any authentication prompt in POS.

2

Press CLEAR [F8] when prompted for the password.

3

Enter the old Employee Password.

4

Enter a new Employee Password twice.

Note: The old and new password will not display on the screen.
