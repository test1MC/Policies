SOURCE Intranet Passwords Overview

Since areas within the computer system may contain confidential information, security restrictions allow only the employee with the password to access the information. Please refer to the chart below to get specific password update information for all required passwords.

GEMS Password

Passwords in GEMS can be changed by the user, but they will not expire.

To have the password reset, call the S-W Help Desk (1-800-566-2997) and choose option #2.  The Operations Group will reset your password.

MySherwin Password

Employees are prompted to change MySherwin password every 90 days.

For forgotten passwords, go to www.mysherwin.com and click on Forgot Password. Follow the steps provided to reset the password.
Sales Representative SOURCE Password

The Sales Representative SOURCE Password is changed once a year. Sales Representatives should contact Store Computer Operations at 216-566-2740 for forgotten passwords.

All Sales Representatives will receive an advanced notice email when the Sales Representative SOURCE Password changes. This password will give Sales Representatives access to the OFFICE and BROWSER buttons at the bottom of the SOURCE desktop. Follow these instructions to use the password to gain access to OFFICE and BROWSER:

  1. Enter your sales territory number when prompted
            to enter your employee / territory number.
  2. Enter the Sales Representative SOURCE password
           when prompted to Enter Employee Password,
            press ENTER.
  3. Enter SherLink User ID and SherLink Password.
