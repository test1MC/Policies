Training Mode Policy

  1. The Store Manager must enter the System Manager Password to activate the Training Mode.
  2. Any transactions run during training must be reviewed by the Store Manager.
  3. Training transactions will not print after completing the transaction. Transactions run during the training mode cannot be used for customer purchases.
