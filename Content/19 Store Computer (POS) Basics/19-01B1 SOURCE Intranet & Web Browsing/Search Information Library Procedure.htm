Search Information Library Procedure

Information Library provides up-to-date reference material needed to operate Sherwin-Williams facilities. The Forms Repository, Information Directory, and Policy and Procedures Manual are valuable databases used for gathering information:

Part

Function

Forms Repository

Forms Repository is a database of current forms. Some can be completed, edited, and saved on screen. Others will need to be printed, completed by hand, and filed manually. Depending on the form, editing and saving files may not be possible.

Follow these steps to Search the Forms Repository:

Step

Action

1

Use the search parameters and tips to narrow the search. Enter the information known.

2

The system will automatically search for keywords from the Form Name or Form Description fields.

Information Directory

The Information Directory provides phone numbers and contact information for Sherwin-Williams employees. Please review the Information Directory features. Providing updated information ensures that others will be able to find correct contact information in the Information Directory.

Follow these steps to Search the Information Directory:

Step

Action

1

Click on Store Search or Territory Search to make a broader query.

2

Enter the criteria known and click Search.

3

To generate a document for printing, click on the PDF or Excel link.

Policy and Procedures Manual

The Policy and Procedures Manual provides instructions for nearly every situation. Use the following methods to narrow your search:

  • Click any of the Index Links to access a chapter.
  • Select from the Master Index or click By Chapter to sequentially browse the manual.
  • Enter a keyword in the Search Field.
  • After opening a document, hold down [Ctrl] and press [F]. Enter a search word to find each time the word is referenced within the document.

To increase or decrease the screen information on the browser screens:

  • Hold down [Ctrl] and press [+] to enlarge the type size.
  • Hold down [Ctrl] and press [-] to decrease the size.

Note: It is extremely important that you regularly review Recent Updates. This is a quick way to check for any updates without having to read the entire manual.
