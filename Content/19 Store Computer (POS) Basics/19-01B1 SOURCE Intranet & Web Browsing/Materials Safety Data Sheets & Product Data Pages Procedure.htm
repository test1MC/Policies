Materials Safety Data Sheets (SDS) & Product Data Pages Procedure

Follow these steps to Look Up, Print, Email, and Fax Material Safety Data Sheets:

Step

Action

1

There are three ways to search for SDS:

  • Enter a Product Name.
  • Enter a Sales, REX, or SMIS Number.
  • Click the magnifying glass next to the search field to access Product Lookup.

Note: If needing more than one SDS or Data Page, enter all of the numbers at once—just be sure to separate them with commas.

2

When the desired product is found, the search will return the MSDS and Data Page. View these documents by clicking the retrieved file. If needed, enter an email address, fax number, and/or notes to be forwarded to a recipient.

Note: If unable to find the needed information, access HEARS on the Web from SOURCE (Homepage -> SDS/Data Page -> HEARS on the Web).

Customers can quickly find product documents at www.paintdocs.com. Click here for more information.
