SOURCE Intranet Banner Overview

The SOURCE Intranet Banner is located across the top of the screen, and it allows for quick viewing of both Company and store specific information:

Part

Function

Employee Identification

Log in information is located in the center of the banner. It shows the employee's position, store number, and store location. Each employee has special tools available on SOURCE Intranet, so it's very important to close the browser when finished. Otherwise, the next employee will have access to your intranet tools and privileges.

Store Hours

Adjusting store hours allows the manager to easily record the store’s operational hours for display on the SOURCE Intranet. These hours will also display in the Store Locator on the Sherwin-Williams website. The Adjust Store Hours Link will display when managers log in using their employee number and password. Managers should adjust these hours to match the posted hours at the front of the store.

Note: For more information, see Store Hours.

Stock Price

Sherwin-Williams’ most recent stock price is located below store hours.

Calendar

Click on a day or Open Cal to add, edit, print, or view events on your calendar.

SOURCE Search

SOURCE Intranet Search enables quick access to information. Enter a keyword and the system will return all published manuals and documents on the SOURCE Intranet.

SOURCE Self-Help

SOURCE Self-Help links to detailed SOURCE Intranet instructions.
