SOURCE Intranet Tabs Overview

Each tab provides access to a range of information specific to it. Depending on an employee's position with the Company, they will have access to various links within each tab. This access is dictated by their login information which is why logging out of SOURCE when finished is so vital. The main tabs are fairly static, but the links under them and the sites they point to are often in flux. Also, because of the depth of information located on the intranet, many tabs have sub tabs that will help to narrow the point of inquiry.

Part

Function

Home

Home Tab is extensive, including everything from the Forms Repository to Price Record Cards. Employees will likely visit this tab frequently, and its primary components are covered in the proceeding section of this chapter. 

Human Resources
  • Employee Programs enables employees to update personal information in the HR System. They can also learn about Sherwin-Williams’ feedback process, employee referral program, sales achievement programs, and how to provide employee verification of employment to lenders, landlords, and potential employers.
  • Employee Recruitment contains information for Store Managers on recruiting materials, new employee orientation, and the new hire process.
  • Human Resource Policies and Procedures provides a list of HR documents.
  • Manage Your Career allows employees to review job descriptions for positions of interest and learn how to use CareerSource to apply for open positions.
  • Sherwin-Williams University has an extensive offering of training courses that assist in mastering product knowledge and gaining the skills necessary to successfully run a Sherwin-Williams business.

Marketing

Marketing Tab allows employees to view marketing databases for Architectural and Industrial & Marine Coatings. It also provides access to Quality, Color, and National Accounts Information:

  • Architectural Tab provides access to Promotions, Marketing Tools, and New Product Information.
  • Industrial & Marine Tab provides Forms, Market Information, and Competitive Cross Reference Tools.
  • Quality Tab provides information on recalls and other quality control instructions.
  • Color Tab provides access to SherColor Resources, Color Marketing Services, and other miscellaneous Color Information.
  • National Accounts Tab provides access to the National & Accounts Specification List and other Accounts News.  

Advertising

Advertising Tab provides Retail Communications, Promotional Information, and Trade Communications. It is an efficient way to research past, present, and future advertising programs.

LIVESAFE

LIVESAFE Tab provides access to a wealth of safety information, including Emergency Response Info, Accident Reporting, and Safety Manuals.
