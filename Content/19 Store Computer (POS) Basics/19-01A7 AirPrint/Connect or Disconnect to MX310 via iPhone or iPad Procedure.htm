Connect or Disconnect to MX310 via iPhone or iPad Procedure

AirPrint allows employees to print from any wireless device to the MX310 in a store when connected wirelessly. Content must be on the device before connecting to the MX310 Wi-Fi network.  The device will lose its internet connection when connected to the MX310.
