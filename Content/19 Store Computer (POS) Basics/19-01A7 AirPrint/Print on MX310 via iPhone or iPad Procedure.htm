Print on MX310 via iPhone or iPad Procedure

 

 

Not all apps support AirPrint. Check the app’s User Guide or Help for details.

Step

Action

1

Open the app to print from.

 

2

To locate print, select the app’s Share Icon.

  or    or 

3

Select Print.

 

4

Tap Select Printer. Select the MX310.

 

5

Select Print Options.

 

6

Select Print in the upper-right corner.
