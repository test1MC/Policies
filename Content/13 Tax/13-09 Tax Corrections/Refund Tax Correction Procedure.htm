Refund Tax Correction Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the SOURCE Desktop -> Store Button -> Main Menu -> Point of Sale.

3

Enter Employee Number when prompted.

4

Press PAGE DOWN at the Sale / Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

5

Select Correction from the Special Menu.

6

Press CANCEL [F5] at the All Order Browse Window to bypass the Customer Order browse.

7

At the Correction Entry Window:

If

Then

The original sales receipt is available

Scan the original sales receipt.

The original sales receipt is not available

Enter the original terminal number, transaction number, date, account number, and job number.

This information is not readily available

Use INQUIRY [F4] to access Inquiry Menu to search for the information.

Once the information has been entered, press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

8

Enter the customer Name, Address, City, State, and Zip Code if the information was not automatically returned to the screen.

9
Process the Negative (Original) Side of the Correction Transaction:
1 Enter 9999-9999-8 when prompted to enter the Sales Number.
2 Always enter 1 for the quantity.
3 Enter the sub-total amount from the original transaction as the price.
4 Press ACCEPT [F1].
5 Select the Tax Type selected on the original sales transaction.

10
Process the Positive Side of the Correction Transaction:
1

Enter 9999-9999-8 when prompted to enter the Sales Number.
2 Always enter 1 for the quantity.
3 Enter the sub-total amount from the original transaction as the price. 
4 Press ACCEPT [F1].
5

Select the Tax Type, which should have been applied to the original sale.  If the customer requires a correction based on their jurisdiction, it may also be required to input the new zip code information.

11

Refund the amount indicated by the computer system.

12

Have the customer sign a copy of the Correction Transaction for retention.
