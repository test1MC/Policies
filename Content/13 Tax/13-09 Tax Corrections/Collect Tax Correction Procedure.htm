Collect Tax Correction Procedure

Step

Action

1

Was the transaction(s) being corrected originally sold at this store?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 2.
No The store cannot process this transaction.

Note: The correction must be processed at the store which processed the original sale.

2

Press PAGE DOWN at the Sale / Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

3

Select Correction.

4

Press CANCEL [F5] at the All Order Browse Window to bypass the Customer Order browse.

5

Is this a mass correction?

If Then  
Yes

Field

Entry
 

Terminal Number

99999
 

Transaction Number

99999
 

Date

Today’s Date
 

Account Number

Customer’s Account Number
 

Job Number

Job Number
No
  • If available, scan the original transaction receipt.
  • Enter the following information from the original transaction: Terminal Number, Transaction Number, Date, Account Number, and Job Number.
  

Note:  Mass corrections may only be processed for ten or more transactions placed on the same Account Number and Job Number.

6

Did the customer’s BillTo Name and Address populate?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 7.
No Enter the customer’s BillTo Name and Address.

7

Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

8

Complete the Negative Side Detail:

Step

Action

1

Enter Class Number 9999-99998 in the Sales Number field.

2

Enter a Quantity of 1.

3

Total the Extended Price(s) of all line items requiring correction on the transaction(s).

4

Enter the amount totaled in step 3 in the Price field.

5

Is this a mass correction?

If Then
Yes Press COMMENT [F9] and enter the transaction number(s) of all transaction(s) being corrected.
No Proceed to step 6.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1].

7

Select the Tax Type applied to the original transaction(s) from the Tax Type Window.

9

Complete the Positive Side Detail:

Step

Action

1

Enter Class Number 9999-99998 in the Sales Number field.

2

Enter a Quantity of 1.

3

Enter the Extended Price Total calculated in step 3 of the Negative Side Detail.

4

Press ACCEPT [F1].

5

Is this a correction to refund tax to the customer?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 6.
No Proceed to Step 7

6

Does the store use the Tax Record Card (TRC) System?

If Then
Yes The applicable Tax Record Card rules will be applied to this transaction in order to refund tax.  Proceed to Step 10.
No Proceed to Step 7.

7

Select the Tax Type which should be applied to the original transaction(s) from the Tax Type Window.

10

Select the Form of Refund or Form of Payment for the transaction and complete the Correction Transaction.

Note:  If the store is assuming the tax for a customer without a Sherwin-Williams Charge Account that was originally exempted from tax, select Cash as the Form of Payment - no cash will be placed in the Cash Drawer.

11

Was this Correction Transaction run to collect tax withheld in the original transaction from the customer?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 12.
No This process is complete. No further action required.

12

Was the tax payment collected in the Correction Transaction charged to the customer’s Sherwin-Williams Charge Account or collected from the customer using another form of payment?

If Then
Yes This process is complete.  No further action required.
No The store must assume the tax for the original transaction.  Proceed to Step 13.

13

Select Disbursement from the Special Menu.

14

Select Miscellaneous by CASH as the Disbursement Type.

15

Enter the store information in the BillTo Address. Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

16

Press ENTER at the Prime Sub Description field to view the available listing. Select SERV & SUPPLIES - OTHER (7402-099) from the GL Account List.

17

Enter the Amount of the tax collected-  this amount should be the same as the cash payment amount required in the Correction Transaction.

18

Press COMMENT [F9] and type the following: CASH ACCOUNT TAX CORRECTION. Press ACCEPT [F1] to save the comment. Press TOTAL [F12] to proceed.

19

Enter Employee Password and press ENTER. Staple a copy of the original cash exempt transaction and a copy of the tax correction together and retain in the Tax Exemption Certificate File Folder for audit purposes.
