Use Tax Exemptions Overview

Refer to the Use Tax charts in Chapter 13-01 Tax Information and Tax Bulletins in Chapter 13-03 State Tax Bulletins & Forms.  In some states, if a customer possesses a current and valid Tax Exemption Form for the state in which the store is located, Sherwin-Williams does not need to pay use tax on the transaction. Be careful when the customer is not located in the same state as the store - any exemption form presented by the customer MUST be for the state in which the store is located. Do not accept an exemption form for another state unless the store delivers and installs in that state.
