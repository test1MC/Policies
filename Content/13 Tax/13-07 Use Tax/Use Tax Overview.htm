Use Tax Overview

  • Use tax is due when items which are purchased ex-tax are put to a taxable use.
  • Use tax is typically calculated at the same rate as the sales tax and applies to the same tax base.
  • Use tax does not apply to services rendered such as labor charges, but be aware that sales tax may still apply to these charges. 
  • Use tax or sales tax may apply to supplies and materials used to complete the job.  Refer to Locate State Tax Bulletin Information Procedure for more information.
  • Use tax must be paid whenever Sherwin-Williams arranges for the installation of any floorcovering or window treatment in a state that requires that use tax be applied to materials sold and installed as a construction contract or improvement to real estate. 
  • When Sherwin-Williams arranges for installation of product, the company is considered to be the end user of the product and therefore must pay use tax on the landed cost of the product being installed. In instances where use tax is applied, the customer is not charged sales tax on the materials.
