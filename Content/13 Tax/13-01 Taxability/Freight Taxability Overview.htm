Freight Taxability Overview

The taxability of a freight charge is determined based on both the state in which the shipment is made and the non-merchandise code used to note the freight charge. 

Non-merchandise codes to be used are as follows:
Common Carriers

3

FREIGHT-FOB DEST - Shipping charges for goods shipped via common carrier - title passes at the customer’s location.

4

FREIGHT-FOB ORIGIN - Shipping charges for goods shipped via common carrier - title passes at the store location.
Sherwin-Williams Truck

9

DELIVERY BY SW TRUCK - Delivery charges for merchandise delivered in SW vehicle.

Please refer to Freight Class Numbers for information on passing along freight charges from the vendor. In most cases, tax would apply for freight charges being passed along from the vendor for shipping to the store location.
