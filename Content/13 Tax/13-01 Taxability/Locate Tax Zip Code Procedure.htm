Locate Tax Zip Code Procedure

For help in determining a location's correct zip code, refer to the United States Postal Service Website Zip Code Locator.

Step

Action

Recommended Address Entry Method

1

When entering the customer shipping address, press the TAB key through the City Field and State Field. Enter only the zip code.

Note: If more than one taxing jurisdiction exists for the zip code, a selection must be made in the Taxing Jurisdiction Selection Window.  It may be necessary to know the customer’s county location and whether the given address is located inside or outside of city limits.

Complete Address Entry Method

2

If manually entering all information (city, state, zip code) for the shipping address or updating the customer’s price record card, be aware that the city name must be spelled out completely.  Taxware does not accept abbreviations and the entry will need to be corrected.
