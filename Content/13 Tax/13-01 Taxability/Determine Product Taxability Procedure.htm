Determine Product Taxability Procedure

Step

Action

1

Find the Tax Group Code of the product needing review by selecting the Products and Prices option available in SOURCE using INQUIRY [F4] and searching for the product. Then, View the Priv Tax Grp Field on the product record.

2

Find the customer’s Exemption Reason.

Note: This should be stated on the tax exemption certificate, or, you may view the certificate online in the Tax Record Card System.

3

From the SOURCE Homepage -> Source Operations -> Processes -> Tax Record Card System.

4

Select Taxability Rules Matrix.

5

From the drop-down listing, select the State for which you wish to establish taxability.  Click Show Tax Rules.

6

The Tax Group Code (Priv Tax Grp) can be found in the leftmost column and the Exemption Reason for the customer along the top row.

7

Find where the product’s Priv Tax Grp Code and the customer’s Exemption Reason meet in the matrix to determine taxability. Click on the Taxable Status in the matrix to review details of the taxability for the Tax Group Code and Exemption Reason.

Monitor invoices to verify that products are properly taxed or not taxed because not every tax exemption situation has been automated.  The Tax Record Card System is designed to assist with taxing decisions.  Store personnel must continue to monitor exemptions and product taxability. 

Note: Questions or concerns about any of the matrices can be addressed to the Corporate Tax Department at 216-566-2577.
