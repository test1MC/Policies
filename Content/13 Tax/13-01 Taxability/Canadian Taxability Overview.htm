Canadian Taxability Overview

  • In Canada, the taxing jurisdiction is calculated provincially and is based on the first letter of the postal code. Make sure this first letter is correct. Do not use the postal code to verify a taxing jurisdiction because Canadian postal codes are not verified by VeraZip.
  • Do not enter any spaces, dashes, or special characters. Only enter the six-character postal code.
  • While setting up a price record card, set the GST flag to YES. Otherwise, the customer will not be charged Goods and Services Tax [GST].
