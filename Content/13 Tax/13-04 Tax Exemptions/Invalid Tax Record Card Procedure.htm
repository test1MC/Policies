Invalid Tax Record Card Procedure

Step

Action

1

Stores may receive invalidated Tax Record Card information in one of two ways:

  • Via e-mail notification when a Tax Record Card has been invalidated.  This e-mail will identify the customer account, reason(s) for invalidation, and may contain notes from the Tax Record Card Administration Group on how to resolve the issue and obtain a valid certificate.
  • From the TRC Main Menu, select TRC VALIDATION -> VALIDATE TRCS to view information on invalid certificates.

Use the TRC Search to enter search criteria and search for the Tax Record Card for which you would like more information.

Note the VALIDATION column in the search results.  The colored icon will tell you the Validation Status of the Tax Record Card.

  • CREATED – NOT YET REVIEWED (YELLOW)
  • REVIEWED – VALID (GREEN)
  • REVIEWED – INVALID (RED)

Click on the Red Button to see the reason(s) for invalidation and any notes from the Tax Record Card Administration Group.  Reason(s) for invalidation will be marked with a check. This is the same information sent in the e-mail notification.

2

Use the provided reason(s) and notes to obtain a valid certificate from the customer.

3

Once the customer has provided correct and complete documentation, submit your Tax Record Card for re-validation. Create a new Fax Coversheet by clicking on the Fax icon.

4

Fax BOTH the new coversheet and customer certificate to 216-830-5239.

Note: If you do NOT fax both the coversheet and certificate, the Tax Record Card cannot be validated. Fax ONLY to the Fax Number on the coversheet.  Faxes made to other numbers cannot be validated.
