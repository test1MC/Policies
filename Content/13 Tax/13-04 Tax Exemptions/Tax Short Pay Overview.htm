Tax Short Pay Overview

Some customers who claim exemptions, but do not provide supporting documentation may “short pay” their invoices by the amount of tax charged.  The Credit Center will notify the store of customers who have “shorted” their invoice by the tax amount. 

Step Action
1

Contact the customer to notify them that they must provide support for the exemption they are claiming and that otherwise, the store has a legal obligation to collect the tax.
2

Refer the customer to a state or local website or office providing tax information.

Note: If the customer knows which document they require, store employees may print the document and provide it to the customer if it is available in Locate State Tax Bulletin Information Procedure.  However, do not provide a customer guidance on selecting a document for their state or assist them in completion of a document. 

3

If a customer does not provide documentation after being contacted, the store must continue to collect tax on their invoices.

Note: The uncollected tax will remain as an open item on the account until it has been paid or adjusted through the Credit Office. Contact the Credit Office with any questions regarding tax short-pays.
