Purchase Order Use Tax Exemption Policy

  1. If a customer will not fill out the tax exemption certificate and they wish to use their purchase order for tax exemption, as may be the case with sales made to government agencies, the following information must be included on the purchase order: customer name and address, tax number, nature of business, reason for exemption, customer signature date, and, a complete listing of the items for which tax will not be applied. A copy of each purchase order must be kept in the Tax Exempt File.
  2. If the customer is unable to provide the required information, process the sale with tax.
  3. Once the required information is provided, give the customer credit for the amount of tax charged for the sale via a correction transaction.
  4. A Tax Record Card must be created for each purchase order the customer submits.
