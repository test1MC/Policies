Tax Record Card System Overview

The Tax Record Card System is an information repository that will work with SOURCE to automate tax exemptions at point-of-sale. Store personnel will create Tax Record Cards by inputting information from the exemption documentation provided by the customer.  When creating a customer order or running a sale for the customer in SOURCE, employees can select a Tax Record Card to apply the allowed exemptions to the sale transaction.

All store employees are able to access the Tax Record Card System.
