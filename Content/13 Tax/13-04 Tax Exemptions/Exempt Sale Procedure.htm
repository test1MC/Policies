Exempt Sale Procedure

Step

Action

Exempt Sale

1

When selecting the Price Record Card to be used for the order, review the TRC column of the Job/Price Record Cards Window to see if a Tax Record Card exists for the job.  If the TRC column displays YES, a Tax Record Card could be applied to the order. 

Note: Don't forget that a Tax Record Card will only be applied if the card is valid for the taxing jurisdiction for the sale transaction.

2

Verify that a Tax Record Card has been applied to the order or transaction through any of the following signs:

  • At the header, the Tax Exception field will display the Tax Record Card # being used.
  • The Exception Flag for Tax will be set for the order.
  • Products eligible for exemption will display and N in the Tax column of the Product Detail Window.

Note: To see all cards for a customer, use Main Menu -> Miscellaneous Menu -> Tax Record Cards or use the Tax Record Card System.

Line Item Overrides

1

If it is necessary to change the tax status of a line item use TAX? [SHIFT+F11] at the line item in Order Detail.

2

Select No-Tax from the Tax Exception Menu.
