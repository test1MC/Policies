Tax Override Policy

  1. All tax overrides will be monitored by the Corporate Tax Department, internally audited by Corporate Audit, and subject to state and/or local tax audit. 
  2. Stores are required to retain any supporting documentation for the override. 
    1. Example: If the customer is a contractor with which Sherwin-Williams has a signed contract stating that he/she is entitled to purchases at a prior tax rate, stores must keep this contract on file in perpetuity.
