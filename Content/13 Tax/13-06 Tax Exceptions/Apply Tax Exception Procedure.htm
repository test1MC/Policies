Apply Tax Exception Procedure

Step Action

1

Press SHIFT + TAX? [F11] at the order/sale header.  Verify that the EXCEPTION Flag is set.

2

Press ENTER to select a Tax Record Card.  Answer NO to the prompt Do you wish to use this Tax Record Card?

3

Answer NO to the prompt Do you wish to create a NEW Tax Record Card?

4

Explain the reason for the tax rate override in Special Instruction on the customer order or Comments on the sale.

5

The Tax Type Window will appear once away from the Product Detail Window. Make the selection that best fits the scenario.

6

Based on the selection in the Tax Type Window, it may be necessary to also complete the Tax Exception Window.

Note: To override the tax status of a line item, press SHIFT + TAX? [F11] at the line item of the Order Detail to make a selection of No Tax from the Tax Exception Box. The No Tax option is ONLY available for a line item override when a Tax Record Card has been applied to the order or sale.
