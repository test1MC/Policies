Non-Standard Tax Overview

Acceptable non-standard tax transactions include:

  • Refunding tax after a tax rate change for the jurisdiction.
  • Contractor entered into a contract before a tax rate change and is entitled to purchase at a prior tax rate.
  • Customer picks up order originally marked for delivery.
  • Customer requests BOLDOC shipping paperwork with an order sold in the store.
  • Customer requests tax to be charged when Tax Record Card for valid exemption exists.

Note: If the tax rate is different, ensure that that the taxing jurisdiction is correct or run the risk of understated or overstated tax on the customer’s invoice.
