Tax Levy Policy

  1. Usually, the first warning of a Tax Levy is a telephone call from Stores Group Bookkeeping asking whether or not an employee owes a particular installer any money currently or will in the near future.  From that time on, the store cannot make any payments to that installer without permission from the Corporate Tax Department or a written release from the taxing authority.  Failure to comply could result in the store being charged for the payment illegally made to the installer plus a fifty percent penalty.
