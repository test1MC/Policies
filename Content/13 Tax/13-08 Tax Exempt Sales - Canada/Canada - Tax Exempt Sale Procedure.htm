Canada - Tax Exempt Sale Procedure

Process a POS Tax Exempt Sale through a Customer Order or Sale:

Step Action

1

What type of exemption is being processed?

If Then
Provincial Sales Tax (PST) or Quebec Sales Tax (QST) Proceed to Step 2.
Good and Services Tax (GST) Proceed to Step 4.
Harmonized Sales Tax (HST) Proceed to Step 4.

2

Validate that the PST registration number is noted in the PRC. 

3

Is this an exemption for Saskatchewan?

If Then
Yes Review and validate the vendor license number and ensure that it has been noted on a PRC Job or Roll Up Job. Be sure to select this job for the order / sale.
No Proceed to step 4.

4

Will the exemption be applied to all line items on the order?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 5.
No Proceed to Step 6.

5

Is there a green box with the word Exception on the Order Header Screen?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 6.
No Press SHIFT + Tax [F11] prompt a tax exception for the entire order.  This exception will be designated as an as an exemption in a later step.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] to move to the Sale Detail Screen.

7

Scan the product barcodes or key in the Sales Number at the Sales Number Field.  Enter the quantity for each product and press Enter.

8

Will the exemption be applied to all line items on the order?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 10.
No Proceed to Step 9.

9

For line items which will be exempt, arrow up to the line item and press SHIFT + Tax [F11] and select No Tax from the Tax Exception Menu.

10

Press TOTAL [F12] to proceed.

11

Will the exemption be applied to all line items on the order?

If Then
Yes Select No Tax from the Tax Exception Menu and complete the order/sale.
No Complete the order/sale.
