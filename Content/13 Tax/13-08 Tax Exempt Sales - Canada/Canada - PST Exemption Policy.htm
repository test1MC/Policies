Canada - PST Exemption Policy

The provinces of British Columbia (BC), Manitoba (MB), and Saskatchewan (SK) allow for exemptions of PST.  Please be aware of the following policies regarding exemptions:

  1. Customer's PST registration number must be on the price record card of the customer in a field that is printed on the invoice.
  2. Exemptions processed for Saskatchewan require:
    1. Customers to provide their vendor license number to Sherwin-Williams.
    2. Sherwin-Williams to verify the vendor license number as active.
    3. Sherwin-Williams to print the vendor license number on the invoice.
  3. In British Columbia, if the customer does not have a PST number, they can complete a Certificate of Exemption – General Form (FIN490).
  4. If the goods are being purchased by a First Nation individual and delivered to First Nation Land for use by First Nation individuals, then PST is also exempt. First Nation customers must provide their Certificate of Indian Status Card and sign the copy kept at the store. The First Nation's 10-digit number must be on the price record card in a field that is printed on the invoice.
