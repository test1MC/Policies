Canada - Validate PST Vendor License Number Procedure

Saskatchewan requires that the vendor license number be validated for each sale and printed on the invoice.

Step

Action

1

Has the customer provided their vendor license number?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 2.
No It is required to collect PST. STOP.

2

Access the Provincial Sales Tax On-Line Registry (http://www.skpstregistry.gov.sk.ca/).

3

Scroll to the bottom of the screen and agree to the registry’s Conditions of Use & Disclaimer.

4

Search for the customer you wish to validate. 

5

Did the customer's file appear in the search?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 6
No It is required to collect PST. STOP.

6

Create / modify a PRC Job as noted:

Cash Account

Charge Account
