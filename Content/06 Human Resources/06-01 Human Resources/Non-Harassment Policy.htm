Non-Harassment Policy

  1. As an equal opportunity employer, The Sherwin-Williams Company recruits, selects and hires applicants for employment based on individual qualifications.
  2. Company policy prohibits unlawful discrimination based on race, color, creed, sex, status as a special disabled veteran or veteran of the Vietnam era, age, national origin, physical handicap, disability, or ancestry or any other consideration made unlawful by federal, state or local laws.
  3. After employment, an employee's progress and compensation will be related to his or her qualifications and job performance.
  4. Company policy further prohibits harassment of any employee. This commitment applies to all persons involved in the operations of the Company and prohibits unlawful discrimination by any employee of the Company, including supervisors and co-workers, as well as vendors and customers. See EEO & Non-Harassment Policies for a copy of these two policies.
  5. An employee who believes he or she has been subjected to unlawful discrimination should use the Problem Resolution Procedure (PRP) to bring the problem to Management's attention.
  6. If you need assistance using the Problem Resolution Procedure, contact your Area Human Resource Manager or the Division VP of Human Resources. See Problem Resolution Procedure (PRP) for a complete copy of the procedure.
  7. If Management determines that unlawful discrimination has occurred, effective remedial action will be taken, commensurate with the severity of the offense. Appropriate action will also be taken to deter any future discrimination or retaliation.
