Crisis Management Overview

The Crisis Management Program allows Sherwin-Williams to respond to unpredictable, unknown situations that can rapidly change. Refer to the PCG Emergency Response Plan to prepare for challenging, yet predictable situations.

During a natural disaster, our primary concern is the welfare of our employees, their families, our customers, and others we can reasonably assist.

In the event of a crisis, take the following steps:

  • Report hurricane, tornado, earthquake, to incidentreport@sherwin.com or call 216-855-4225.
  • PCG Crisis Management Lead will notify PCG & Corporate Crisis team.
  • Account for all employees in the affected area.

Mobilize required resources and constant updates until crisis is over.
