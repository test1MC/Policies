Emergency Cleanup Procedure

Step Action
1 Open the doors in the front and back of the store to increase ventilation and fresh air.
2

Wear the proper PPE:

  • Required: long pants, long sleeves, gloves, safety glasses, close toe shoes
  • Recommended: hard hat, dust mask, chemical resistant gloves, steel toe shoes, rubber over boots, high visibility vest or makings
3

Review EHS Considerations below with employees and contractors performing cleanup.

Note: Be mindful of the highest injury causes: slip/trips/falls and cuts.

4

Move the product and equipment to be disposed to a secure storage area and separate by solid wastes (trash), non-hazardous liquid wastes (latex) and hazardous wastes (solvent waste, caustics and acids, paint strippers, aerosols, fluorescent lamps, batteries, etc.).

Note: Dispose of all materials that have absorbed flood water. For more information, see Re-open Store after a Shutdown Overview.

5 Use a bleach & water solution (1 cup bleach/4 gallons water) to clean hard non-porous surfaces impacted by water (do not mix any other cleaner with the bleach and water)
6 Contact your Area/Regional manager with an update once all cleaning activities are complete.
