Employee Termination Procedure

Step

Action

1

Notify the appropriate Payroll Processor or the Payroll Supervisor in Corporate Payroll, Cleveland, and  the person responsible for processing the Employee Profile. (Example: The District Office must be contacted when terminating a store employee, who in turn will notify Equifax, the consultants who handle the unemployment claims for Sherwin-Williams).

2

Complete the Employee Profile immediately; otherwise, there will be a delay in processing the final paycheck and benefits.

Note: When the Employee Benefits Department receives the properly completed GEMs transaction, a letter explaining the status of the various benefits upon termination will be mailed to the employee's last known home address. The vendor that handles health care conversions for Sherwin-Williams, will mail a health care conversion notice to the employee's last known home address.

Note: Questions regarding benefits prior to the employee receiving the termination packet should be referred to the Employee Benefits Service Center at 1-800-792-1110.
