Corporate Policies Policy

The Sherwin-Williams Company is committed to ensuring that our employees work in a safe, courteous, and respectful environment where all employees are aware of the rules and expectations governing their employment. As a result, Sherwin-Williams has adopted policies to foster this goal on an enterprise-wide basis.

The policies are designed to ensure consistency and fair treatment of employees, to avoid exposure to substantial legal or financial risk, and to prevent doing irreparable damage to Sherwin-Williams reputation or otherwise negatively impacting the well-being of Sherwin-Williams. These policies serve as guideposts for employee and managerial action and are intended to provide a clear sense of appropriate handling of the particular situations covered by the policy.
