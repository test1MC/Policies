Employee Discipline Policy

  1. Any discipline, except informal counseling, should be agreed to by two levels of management (Example: Store Manager and District Manager with input from the Area Human Resource Manager).
  2. The Division VP of Human Resources or the Area Human Resource Manager must be consulted before any employee (both full-time & part-time) is discharged.
  3. The Division Loss Prevention Director and Division VP of Human Resources must be consulted if the unacceptable behavior involves theft or misuse of Company assets.
  4. An employee may be given the opportunity to resign in lieu of discharge if, in the opinion of the Division VP of Human Resource, the best interests of the Company are served by such an action.
  5. The immediate supervisor must keep a written record of the marginal performance or unacceptable behavior, as well as all disciplinary action taken. This information can be captured on the Written Summary of Unsatisfactory Conduct and Required Corrective Action Form.
    1. When an employee is discharged, all written documentation, including the manager's copy of the employee's personnel file, will be sent immediately to the Division VP of Human Resources.

Note: All inquiries including reference checks about former employees will be referred to the Human Resources Shared Services’ office for response.
