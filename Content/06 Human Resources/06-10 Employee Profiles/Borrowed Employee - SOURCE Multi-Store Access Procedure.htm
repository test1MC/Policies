Borrowed Employee – SOURCE Multi-Store Access Procedure

Employees working at multiple stores have the ability to view more than one home store within SOURCE.

Step Action

1

Contact the Sherlink Help Desk:

  • 1-800-566-2997 or
  • sherlink@sherwin.com

2

Provide the additional store numbers to be added to your profile.

3

Login to SOURCE.

4

Select the Store Number from the Drop Down Box.

Note: Once you select a home store, this will be the acting home store for the entire session. If you want to switch stores or you selected the wrong store number, you will need to log out and back in again.
