Claim of Discrimination or Termination Procedure

Claims of Discrimination are used for all cases alleging discrimination (treating someone differently) or retaliation in any aspect of employment on a basis prohibited by law, except for those involving terminations. To prevail in arbitration in a dispute alleging a violation of law, the employee must prove under the same burden of proof that would be applicable if the employee had brought the claim in court that the law was violated under the legal principles applicable to such claim.

Termination claims are filed when it is alleged that the Company did not have cause for termination or there was a violation of law.

Step

Action

Human Resources Level

1
If Then
Filing Claim of Discrimination

Complete an Employee Complaint Form or Harassment Claim Form within thirty (30) calendar days of the incident.

Note: The Area Human Resources Manager or Division VP of HR will immediately acknowledge receipt of complaint and respond with a decision within sixty (60) calendar days.
Filing Termination Claim

Complete an Employee Complaint Form within thirty (30) calendar days of termination.

Note: The Human Resources/Group Director or Division VP will immediately acknowledge receipt of complaint and respond with a decision within sixty (60) calendar days.

If

Proceed to Step 3.

Management Review Panel Level

3
If Then
Regarding Claim of Discrimination

Complete a Decision Appeal Form with thirty (30) days of Human Resources’ response.

Note: The Management Review Panel, which consists of the Group Director, Human Resource Services, a Senior Manager from the employee’s functional area, and a third member selected by the employee from a list of Senior Managers, must confer and review all documents and facts collected to this point, seek any additional facts or witnesses and, if necessary, interview the complainant or any other individuals involved in the matter in order to make a determination and provide a written response within sixty (60) business days.
Regarding Termination Claim

Complete a Decision Appeal Form with thirty (30) days of Human Resources’ response.

Note: The Management Review Panel will make a determination within sixty (60) calendar days.

4

If

Then

The issue is resolved

Procedure is complete.

External proceedings are required

Proceed to Step 5.

Mediation / Arbitration

5
If Then
Regarding Claim of Discrimination Request mediation and/or arbitration within thirty (30) calendar days from the date the Management Review Panel’s decision was received.
Regarding Termination Claim

Request mediation and/or arbitration within thirty (30) calendar days from the date the Management Review Panel’s decision was received.

Note: To prevail in a dispute alleging that the Company did not have cause for termination, the employee must prove by a preponderance of the evidence that the Company did not have cause for termination, taking into account (a) the nature of the employee’s position and responsibilities, and (b) the Company’s stated policies.  In a dispute alleging a violation of law, the employee must prove under the same burden of proof that would be applicable if the employee had brought the claim in court, that the law was violated under the legal principles applicable to such claim.
