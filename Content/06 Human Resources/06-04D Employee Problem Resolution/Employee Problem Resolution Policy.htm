Employee Problem Resolution Policy

  1. The Store Manager is responsible for ensuring that each store employee understands the open communications process.
  2. The Problem Resolution Procedures cover all employees of TAG who are not subject to a grievance procedure under a collective bargaining agreement. 
    1. This policy does not apply to Headquarters employees who are covered under the Headquarters Site Problem Resolution Procedures.
    2. With respect to Sherwin-Williams' temporary employees, this policy only applies to disputes regarding an employee's termination that allege a violation of law such as discrimination or retaliation relating to an employee's termination or any other aspect of employment on a basis prohibited by law.

Note: This policy, nor any other Company policy, procedure, or document, prevents employees from exercising protected rights to file a charge or complaint or to otherwise participate in any manner in investigations, hearings, or proceedings with administrative agencies including, but not limited to, the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission, state or local agencies handling discrimination claims, the National Labor Relations Board, etc.
