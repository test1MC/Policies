Types of Concerns & Complaints Overview

Concerns/complaints are organized into four categories, each with varying levels of appeal:

  • General Complaints/Concerns
  • Performance Evaluation / Promotion Objections
  • Claims of Discrimination
  • Termination

Claims that fall under the following legislation are not handled through Problem Resolution Procedures:

  • Immigration Reform and Control Act
  • Fair Credit Reporting Act
  • Workers' compensation  or unemployment compensation statues
  • Occupational Safety and Health Act or state and local laws covering safety
  • Employment Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA)
  • Unfair competition and/or the use and/or unauthorized disclosure of trade secrets or confidential information

Employee can request mediation or arbitration for discrimination or termination cases in which the employee feels the issue cannot be resolved through the Company’s internal proceedings.

Note: The forms used in the PRP process as well as Headquarter’s Final Position are available in the Human Resources section of the Forms Repository.
