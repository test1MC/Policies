General Complaint or Performance Evaluation Procedure

General Complaints/Concerns are filed to determine the appropriateness of a Manager's actions regarding application of the policy, procedure, practice, or work rule in question.

Performance Evaluation / Promotion Objections are filed to determine if the policies governing the evaluation given were correctly and consistently followed where a claim of discrimination or termination is not involved.
