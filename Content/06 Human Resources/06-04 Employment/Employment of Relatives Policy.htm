Employment of Relatives Policy

Relatives of current employees will be considered to be hired, promoted, or transferred if they will not be working in the same store or department or under the supervision of a relative.

  1. The normal recruitment and placement procedure will not be circumvented in order to hire, promote, or transfer a relative of an employee.
  2. A candidate who is a relative of an employee will be considered along with non-relative candidates, and will be hired or promoted only if he/she is as well qualified as any other candidate.
  3. Persons placed in violation of this policy will be discharged or transferred to another location where placement is not in violation of this policy.
  4. When an employee becomes married to another employee in the same store or department, the Company may transfer one of the employees to another store or department.
  5. Once terminated, current employees working in the same store who have been "grandfathered" under this policy, will not be rehired.
