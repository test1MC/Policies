Residential Repaint Sales Specialist Policy

This position requires the employee to work at the store part of the day and perform the duties of a sales representative the rest of the day.

  1. The Residential Repaint Specialist is permitted to process point-of-sale transactions and must have his or her own employee number and employee password on the store computer system.
  2. The Residential Repaint Specialist is not permitted to:
    1. Have access to the System/Manager Password
    2. Create Cash Accounts that are assigned to his or her own territory
    3. Override customer pricing
  3. Attendance and Time is tracked at the District Office for this position.
  4. The Specialist must bypass the message, "You have not recorded Attendance for Today," while processing transactions and is categorized as Unknown to Corporate Payroll.
  5. Each week, the Specialist must provide a time sheet documenting hours worked to the District Office. Similarly, this employee will have Level 2 MMS Security.
