Types of Employment Overview

Type of Employment Description

Store Management

The Store/Branch manager, whether exempt or nonexempt, is regularly scheduled to work 96 hours biweekly. Assistant Store Managers and Operations Managers, whether exempt or nonexempt, are regularly scheduled to work 88 hours biweekly.

  • The biweekly salary for Store/Branch Managers and Assistant/Operations Managers is inclusive of scheduled overtime pay at one and one-half times the base rate of pay (16 hours for Store/Branch Managers and 8 hours for Assistant/Operations Managers).

Note: Please refer to Canadian supplement for rules on employees within Canadian Provinces.

Regular Full-Time

The employee is hired or reclassified to work an indefinite period of time expected to last more than 120 days, and is scheduled to work no less than 40 hours per week.

Regular Part-Time

The employee is hired or reclassified to work an indefinite period of time expected to last more than 120 days. A regular part-time employee will typically work an average of 28 hours or less per week, but will not exceed more than 30 hours per week during a pre-determined measurement period.

Temporary Employee

The employee, including interns, is hired or reclassified to work a specified period of time, not less than 11 days or more than 120 days.  Interns are not subject to the time period specified.

Casual Employee

The employee is hired to work a specified period of time, not to exceed ten days during any one period of employment.
