ACORN Overview

ACORN, the Association of Community Organizations for Reform Now, occasionally announces its plans to protest at select locations. Even though ACORN announces target cities, we should be ready for the unexpected. In the past, ACORN has shown up unannounced at S-W facilities in cities not on their original target list. For this reason it is advisable for all facilities to review the proper response for dealing with demonstrations and civil disturbances. If ACORN demonstrators show up at your location, please alert Mike Conway, Director Corporate Communications & Investor Relations.
