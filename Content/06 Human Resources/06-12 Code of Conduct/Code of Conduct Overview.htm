Code of Conduct Overview

This Code of Conduct makes clear that adherence to the highest standards of integrity and the law are critical to Sherwin-Williams success.

Compliance does not just happen. It requires a commitment by every Sherwin-Williams employee. That is why we require every employee to certify that they have read and understand the Code of Conduct. It signals an individual commitment to act responsibly on behalf of the company.

  • Collective actions impact Sherwin-Williams' reputation and credibility with customers, suppliers, communities and shareholders. Therefore, work-related activities must reflect standards of honesty, loyalty, concern for others and accountability. Employees must be sensitive to situations that can adversely impact Sherwin-Williams' reputation, and use good judgment and common sense in the way they conduct business. Aligning Sherwin-Williams' guiding values with its business activities provides a foundation for continued growth as the premier global coatings company.
  • The Code of Conduct is designed to help employees understand what is meant by good judgment and ethical behavior and outlines how to align employee actions with the guiding values. It covers many different situations employees may find themselves in during the course of business and outlines principles that help deal with those situations.
  • The Code provides a clear set of expectations and responsibilities. The Code also highlights the need for each employee to act ethically, to build the company on mutual trust and openness, and to be forthright about business behavior and actions.
  • The Code of Conduct also introduces a number of important laws and policies that apply to Sherwin-Williams and its business activities.
  • However, the Code is more than just a “rulebook.”  The Code is, first and foremost, a resource. It applies globally to help every employee. The Code of Conduct applies to all directors, officers, and employees of Sherwin-Williams and all its subsidiaries wherever located.
