Garnishments Overview

A garnishment is a court order attaching an employee's earnings to satisfy a debt incurred. Employers are notified of the garnishment by service of papers. These papers vary from state to state:

  • Garnishment Summons
  • Writ of Garnishment
  • Writ of Attachment

Other types of wage attachments requiring payroll deduction are:

  • Child Support Order
  • Federal Tax Levy
  • State Tax Levy
