Tuition Reimbursement Application Procedure

The Americas Group Tuition Reimbursement Program

Step Action

1

Complete the employee section of the Tuition Reimbursement Application Form.

2

Forward the completed form to your District Office and/or Division Manager for approval.

Note: District and/or Division Management will complete appropriate sections, as applicable and will send the completed form to the Division Human Resources Administrator and/or their designee for approval.

3

The appropriate Human Resources representative will then notify you and your Manager whether or not your request was approved.

Note: Upon manager’s approval, 50% of the tuition total will be reimbursed in the employee’s next paycheck. After completing the course and submitting the grade to Division Human Resources, the remaining unreimbursed portion of tuition costs will be paid to the employee in their next pay.
