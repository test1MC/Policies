Tuition Reimbursement Policy

The Americas Group Tuition Reimbursement Program

  1. Courses taken, whether through a traditional university program or an online university, must be scheduled during non-working hours.
  2. Tuition will be reimbursed for qualified courses taken either at an accredited local state university or college or through a regionally accredited online university or college.  Questions regarding accreditation should be directed to the designated Division Managers or Human Resource Administration. 
  3. Reimbursement must be handled through the tuition reimbursement form.
  4. For each semester or quarter, reimbursement will be limited to: tuition and registration and lab fees for two courses (a maximum of six (6) semester hours or eight (8) quarter hours).
  5. Reimbursements will be limited to $5,250 per calendar year.
  6. Reimbursement for  language learning CD programs will be at a maximum of $500 per program.
  7. Employee must receive a satisfactory grade and complete the course (obtaining a “C” or better letter grade for undergraduate course or “B” or better for graduate courses), and provide the Division Human Resources with the original grade report issued by the school.
  8. If the course is not completed by the employee (i.e. dropped) or the minimum grade is not achieved, any amounts/dollars previously reimbursed by the Company for the course must be returned to the Company. The Company has the right to recover any tuition reimbursements that have been paid out if the employee leaves voluntarily within one-year after completing a course, program or degree.
