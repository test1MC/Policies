Qualified Courses for Tuition Reimbursement Overview

The Americas Group Tuition Reimbursement Program

Tuition reimbursement is paid at the lowest cost of any local school, whether that cost is at a local college, state university or private institution. This program covers the cost of tuition and lab fees, but does not include the cost of books and incidental fees.

Qualified courses are ones that are needed to obtain an undergraduate or graduate degree in the following areas of study:

  • Marketing
  • Finance
  • Business administration
  • Other work-related degrees as approved by Division management 

Employees wishing to learn Spanish or another foreign language will be reimbursed for taking such classes through the approved institutions listed above or through an adult education program. Employees wishing to learn Spanish or another foreign language through a CD immersion program (Berlitz, Rosetta Stone, etc.) will also be eligible for reimbursement. Please visit the following websites from a non-store computer system for information on learning a foreign language.  http://www.berlitz.com/  and http://www.rosettastone.com/

Note: For employees not pursuing a degree, courses related to marketing, finance, general business, or other work-related courses (such as scientific courses) may also be reimbursed as approved by Division Management.
