Tuition Reimbursement Overview

The Americas Group Tuition Reimbursement Program

Sherwin-Williams encourages employees to acquire additional skills and knowledge that will contribute to individual development and the growth of the corporation.  To assist our employees, we offer a tuition reimbursement program whereby our employees can obtain a degree through a traditional university curriculum or an online university program. Reimbursement under this policy is available to employees in The Americas Group field organization who meet the following requirements:

  • Full-time employment status
  • Employed by Sherwin-Williams for at least one year
  • Current overall performance rating of “3” or better
  • “Job certified” in current position (all required click S.T.A.R.T. modules completed)
  • District and/or Division Management approval

The Division Vice President of Human Resources will approve or deny requests for tuition aid based on:

  • Current performance and future potential in the company
  • The recommendation of the District Manager, Area Vice President of Sales or Division President
  • Availability of tuition aid funds, which are part of the Division budget
  • Consideration of any other financial assistance the employee may be receiving for the same courses.
