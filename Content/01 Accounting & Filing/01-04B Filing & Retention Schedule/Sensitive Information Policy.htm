Sensitive Information Policy

Customer and employees may provide you with documents (not identified in our retention schedule) containing sensitive and restricted information which could be used to identify their person or business or compromise their privacy. 

For more information on what types of documents and information may be considered sensitive and restricted and how to handle these documents within the store beyond securing, review the Data Governance policies found in Corporate Policies Policy.

For more information on handling sensitive customer information, please review our Credit Card Transaction Security Policy and Red Flag Rule Overview.

  1. Customer or employee documents containing sensitive or restricted information must always be secured in a locking file cabinet.
