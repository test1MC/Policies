Pre-Numbered Forms Policy

All system-generated forms contain a system-generated number. For audit purposes, Manual Purchase Orders, Store Drafts, and Manual POST Forms are pre-numbered.

  1. The Store Manager is responsible for all pre-numbered forms and that they are used in numerical sequence.
  2. Payment Drafts, unused Purchase Orders, and unused forms must be stored in the safe or a secure area.
  3. Packages of forms should not be opened prior to using.
  4. No merchandise, fixed assets or Sherwin-Williams' property may be removed from the store without the use of a pre-numbered form or system generated transaction.
  5. Contact the District Manager and the Division Loss Prevention Director if forms are lost or stolen.
    1. If the form cannot be found, list the numbers of the lost forms in a letter (approved by the District Manager) and retain in the Audit file.
  6. Store Manager approval and signature is required for all voided forms.
    1. Voided pre-numbered forms are not to be destroyed and must be kept intact.
    2. Unapproved destruction or disposal of voided forms is in violation of company policy.
    3. Voided pre-numbered forms must be cross-referenced to the appropriate transaction. 
  7. Before destroying any pre-numbered form, written authorization must be obtained from the District Manager. 
  8. Forms to be retired or destroyed should be forwarded to the District Office for retention or destruction.
  9. When transferred to another store, form numbers should be recorded on a Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Items, located in theForms Repository.
    1. An employee from both locations must sign and date the Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Asset/Expense Form. The copies must be distributed to the District Office.
  10. Store Drafts cannot be transferred because they are printed specifically for each store.

Note: For more information, see Filing & Retention Overview
