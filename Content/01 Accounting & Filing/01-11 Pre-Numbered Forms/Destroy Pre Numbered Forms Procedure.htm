Destroy Pre-Numbered Forms Procedure

Step Action

1

List the number and name of the forms on an Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Items Report.

2

Include the reason for destroying the form on the report.

3

Attach the letter of authorization from the District Manager.

4

Place forms in a sealed carton and forward to the District Office.

Note: Store Drafts and Purchase Orders are to be destroyed (shredded or torn in pieces) only if instructed by Headquarters and if new drafts or a new format were issued. For more information, see Void Drafts Overview.
