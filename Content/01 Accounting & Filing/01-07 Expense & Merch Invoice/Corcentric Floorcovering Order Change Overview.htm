Corcentric Floorcovering Order Change Overview

Floorcovering vendors are able to convert the unit of measure to agree with the purchase order either on the EDI invoice or for non-EDI vendors, on the Corcentric website.

  • A higher quantity is allowed on the vendors’ invoice to allot for uncut rolls of carpet and other differences in unit of measure between the purchase order and the vendor’s invoice. 
  • It is always important when entering the receipt that the quantity reflects exactly what was received, not ordered.
  • While creating the floorcovering purchase order make sure that you know the difference between the Vendor, Manufacturer and Distributor. In most cases, the invoice is generated by the Vendor/Manufacturer, not the Distributor. Therefore, the purchase order must be made out to the Vendor/Manufacturer, not the Distributor.
