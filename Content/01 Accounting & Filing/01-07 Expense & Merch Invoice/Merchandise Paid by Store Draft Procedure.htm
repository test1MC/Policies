Merchandise Paid by Store Draft Procedure

You are not permitted or able to write a store draft for merchandise purchases. Store Drafts cannot be coded to any merchandise prime-sub, such as 0406-xxx, 0401-xxx, 0400-xxx.

Step

Action

1

After receiving the merchandise, ensure that the correct freight amount on the purchase order is received.

2

Forward the freight invoice to Accounts Payable for payment. Include a note on the freight invoice – Freight Invoice Please Pay. Ensure the Purchase Order Number and Store Number are CLEARLY shown on the original freight invoice.

3

We have already made this agreement with some of the freight companies that required a store draft when merchandise was delivered, including the following freight companies:

  • American Freight Ways
  • Yellow Freight
  • U.S. Freight
  • Central Freight
  • Conway

Note: If you encounter a freight carrier that requires payment at the time of delivery, you are not permitted to write a store draft. Please use one of the approved freight companies. If you are not able to use one of the approved freight companies, contact Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297 and Complete a new vendor request. For more information, see New Vendor Request Procedure.
