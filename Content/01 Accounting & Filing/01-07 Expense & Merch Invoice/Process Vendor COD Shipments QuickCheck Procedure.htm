Process Vendor COD Shipments QuickCheck Procedure

Step Action

1

Create a Purchase Order to the vendor. Enter the following information:

  • Product Sales Number
  • Quantity
  • Dollar amount, including freight

2

Ask the vendor to fax a copy of the prepayment invoice or sales order that supports the amount of the purchase.

3

Release the Purchase Order.

4

Receive against the Purchase Order.

Note: Ensure the amount of the received Purchase Order is the same dollar amount of the requested check and accept the receipts, which will electronically send the received portion of the Purchase Order to Accounts Payable for payment.

5

Make a note on the copy of the printed Purchase Order: PrePaymentâ€”Please Pay Immediately.

6

Call 216-566-2297 and ask Accounts Payable to write the prepayment check. Ensure that you talk with a and give the details of the purchase to the correspondent. Then, fax the invoice/sales order and the Purchase Order to the correspondent 216-566-3939.

Note: The check will be mailed using standard US Mail service unless otherwise specified. Any Federal Express requests will require a contact and address (no PO Box numbers will be accepted). Federal Express charges will be the responsibility of the store.

7

Maintain a copy of the printed Purchase Order, Prepayment Vendor Invoice and customer transaction in a folder (retain for 1 year plus current) titled PrePayment Merchandise for auditor review.

Note: It takes approximately four days for Accounts Payable to receive and prepare the check. They will then mail the check.
