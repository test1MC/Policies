Manual B Prefix Purchase Order Overview

Manual B Prefix Purchase Orders should only be used for the following three scenarios:

If

Then

Loss of electrical power and the store computer system is unavailable.

Once the power is available, the Manual B Prefix Purchase Order must be entered into the system. For more information, see EDI B Prefix Purchase Order Overview. The store must inform the vendor that they should ship product. This is the only instance that a vendor should ship product on a B prefix purchase order.

Product is shipped that was not on the original purchase order

The additional product will go on a Manual B Prefix Purchase Order. In the notes portion, reference the original purchase order number.  The vendor will need to acknowledge the Purchase Order and invoice against this PO. After entering the receipts, if you decide to return the product obtain authorization and a Return Authorization [RA#] from the vendor and then create the Chargeback.

Receive product directly from a Vendor Sales Rep at your store

Create a B-type purchase order for the product delivered and enter the necessary receipts. The vendor will invoice against the manual purchase order. The purchase order is for billing purposes only; no additional product will get shipped.

Note: The manual B prefix purchase order must be entered into the store computer system. For more information, see EDI B Prefix Purchase Order Overview

Order B Prefix Purchase Orders while placing your STAR Order. For sales number information see Automotive – Manual Forms in 19-10 Ordering POS Manual Forms & Supplies.
