Corcentric & Mailing Invoices Overview

Corporate Shared Services has partnered with Corcentric to provide an electronic solution to the manual invoices. Any vendors who enroll in Corcentric will send their invoices directly to Corcentric via EDI, fax/email or through the web. Corcentric will then convert the invoice into an electronic document.

Vendors will be contacted individually and, as vendors enroll, the link below will be updated. Check regularly when creating purchase orders to confirm that the vendor is using Corcentric. In the beginning we will focus on non-EDI vendors.  View the current list of participating Corcentric Vendors.

Note: Window treatment and floor covering vendors will be the last group of vendors to go onto Corcentric due to the complexity of the orders.
