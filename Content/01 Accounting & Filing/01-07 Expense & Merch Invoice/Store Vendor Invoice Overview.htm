Store/Vendor Invoice Overview

Vendors will be able to log into Corcentric to see if an invoice is unmatched or matched to a store generated External Purchase. The vendor is also able to see if the invoice is ready to pay.  In addition, the vendor can view payment remittance information and check numbers. The vendor will also be able to see Purchase Order cancellations, and/or the reason their invoice has not paid. The following chart indicates the follow-up procedure:

If

Then

After 5 business days (every Friday)

They will be documented on a Held Invoice Report and will be e-mailed to the store. The report will include a reason the invoice has not matched and paid. This report will need to be reviewed by the store.  The store is expected to enter receipts if the product has been received.

After 10 business days (every Friday)

They will be documented on a Held Invoice Report and will be e-mailed to the store, which is expected to enter receipts if the product has been received. The vendor is expected to contact the store for cost or QTY issues.

After 15 business days (every Friday)

They will be documented on a Held Invoice Report and will be e-mailed to the store, which is expected to enter receipts if the product has been received.

After 20 business days (every Friday)

They will be will be followed up on by the FSS Retail Disbursements Correspondents to assist in getting the invoice paid.

In addition, stores will receive an email report from Corcentric advising when a purchase order has been sent to the vendor and Corcentric has not received an 855 Acknowledgement back from the vendor within 24 hours. The vendor will also receive an e-mail reminding them to create the 855.

Note: Invoices will not be removed from the Held Invoice Report until receipts are entered in POS, the vendor corrects the invoice or the invoice is deleted, if appropriate. Questions about the Corcentric process can be directed to Debbie Brucato at 216-566-3871.
