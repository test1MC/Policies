Non-EDI Corcentric Purchase Order Overview

All orders will be handled through a third-party company named Corcentric regardless of whether the vendor has the EDI system or not. After creating an External Purchase Order to a non-EDI supplier, it will be sent electronically to Corcentric and emailed to the vendor, who is required to create an account on the Corcentric website. Once complete, the acknowledgement will be sent electronically to the store.

If

Then

Vendor accepts

PO is confirmed via an 855 Acknowledgement. Stores will receive the product as ordered.

Note: Store users will no longer have the ability to update or change line item costs at time of receiving.  This means the invoice and receipt cost will match and the invoice will successfully process through the 3-Way Match into the payables system.

Vendor denies

The reason will be noted on the acknowledgement and the purchase order will be canceled. If the vendor cannot fulfill the order, the manager will need to find another supplier.

Vendor accepts or denies only certain line items A Purchase Order Acknowledgement is sent back to the store. The vendor will arrange shipment and issue an invoice.
Vendor indicates a discrepancy to MFG Part Number A MFG part number change reported by the vendor on an 855 Acknowledgement will now show on the stores acknowledgement report to make stores are aware of what the correct MFG part number is for future orders. The part numbers will be sent to the Inventory Control Department to update in our system if they are not already set up.

Vendor raises the cost on the PO Acknowledgement being returned to the store

The PO will be cancelled automatically.

Note: On cancelled POs, do not enter “0” quantity receipts. Allow the vendor to send back the cancellation acknowledgement, which will then automatically cancel the PO.

Cost returned from the vendor is lower than the original PO cost

PO line item's cost will automatically be changed to reflect the cost returned from the vendor on the 855 Acknowledgment.

Note: This will ensure the invoice and receipt system match at the header level.

Note: The POS will now display if vendor is a Corcentric vendor. Do no release PO’s with a generic SKU number since you will no longer be able to add it at time of receipt.  Do not release PO until you have the correct SKU number.
