EDI Vendor Purchase Order Overview

  • The cost and quantity on the vendor’s invoice must be less than or equal to the store cost and quantity on the Purchase Order Acknowledgement sent to the store. If not, the vendor’s invoice will be put on hold, need to be deleted from Corcentric and the vendor must re-invoice with the corrected cost and quantity. This will force the vendors to bill what they acknowledged on the 855 PO Acknowledgement.
