Merchandise Invoices Policy

  1. Vendors using Corcentric will not accept phone orders from our stores unless the power is out or the store computer system is down. 
    1. This will be reinforced strongly with the vendors using Corcentric in order to reduce the large numbers of cost variances that Accounts
              Payable must resolve with the vendors and stores.
  2. Store Managers should call the vendors for current pricing if they do not have cost information available when the purchase order is created.
