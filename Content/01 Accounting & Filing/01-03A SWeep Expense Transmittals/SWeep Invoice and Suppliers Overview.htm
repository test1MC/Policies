SWeep Invoice and Suppliers Overview

Sherwin-Williams Enterprise Exchange Process [SWeep] provides an automated method to quickly share information over the SOURCE Intranet. Benefits of this system include:

  • Expense Transmittals automatically submitted to Accounts Payable.
  • Expense Transmittals are processed as invoices come in and services are rendered.
  • Each employee in store management is empowered in the payable process.
  • Timely and accurate feedback on the payment status of related invoices.

Current List of EDI Suppliers:

  • Cintas
  • Office Depot

Supplier Invoices:

This information is for Automotive Branches, Product Finishes Facilities and The Americas Group locations.

Certain suppliers submit invoices centrally to Headquarters for processing. The invoice amount is uploaded into accounts payable and registered for payment. The invoice detail is then entered into SWeep for viewing on the Invoice History page.

Stage

Description

New Invoice

Entry of invoice details.

Awaiting Submittal

Invoice details have been entered, but they have not been submitted; either by file upload or fax.

In Process

Invoice has been routed to Accounts Payable for payment.

Invoice In Review

Invoice amounts that are over the requestor’s approval limit and require further review/approval.

Invoice History

Invoice details are available for lookup dating back 2 ½ years.

Note: Cintas Invoice processing is handled separately. For more information, please see Cintas Invoicing.
