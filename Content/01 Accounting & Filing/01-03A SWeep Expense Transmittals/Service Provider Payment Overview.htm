Service Provider Payment Overview

A W-9 form must be secured from all vendors/independent contractors providing services to The Sherwin-Williams Company. Failure on the part of the company to secure a correct Taxpayer Identification Number from a service provider can result in severe penalties to The Sherwin-Williams Company and your store.

For more information, see 12-01 Store Maintenance.

Anyone providing a service to Sherwin-Williams such as: window cleaning, floor cleaning and waxing should be paid by submitting the invoice through SWEEP on the Source Intranet. Capture the Tax ID/Social Security Number while processing on SWeep.

For more information, see SWeep Invoice and Suppliers Overview.
