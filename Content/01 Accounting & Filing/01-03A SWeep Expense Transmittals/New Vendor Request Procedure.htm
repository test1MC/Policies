New Vendor Request Procedure

Step

Action

1

Select New Vendor Request from the SWeep Menu.

2

The following information is required for the Vendor Payee General Information section:

  • Vendor/Payee Name
  • Phone
  • Fax (optional)
  • Vendor Type: Expense or Merchandise

Note: If you select merchandise for vendor type, you are required to choose a product type from the drop down box.

3

The following information is required for the Remit to Vendor Information section:

  • First name
  • E-mail (Required for Merchandise Vendors – effective 1/2/2012)
  • Verify E-mail (Required for Merchandise Vendors)
  • Address
  • City
  • State/Province
  • Zip

Note: Payments are made in U.S. currency only.

4

Enter the Request Reason in the Comments Field.

5

Is this a merchandise vendor?

If

Then

Yes

Enter the Product Numbers and Description

No

Proceed to Step 6

Note: If this product is available from a current Sherwin-Williams vendor, click the check box.

6

Click Submit to send the request.

Note: A W-9 is required for all new vendor requests submitted through SWeep. For more information, please see W-9 Compliance & Tax.
