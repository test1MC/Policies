New Product Request Procedure

Step

Action

1

SOURCE -> SOURCE Operations -> Processes -> SWeep -> New Product Request

2

If

Then

Product with existing manufacturer

Search partial/whole manufacturer name and select the appropriate manufacturer

Product with new manufacturer

Select (A New MFG Request)

3

Enter/confirm Manufacturer Information fields.

Note: Enter Vendor/Distributor name, even if it is the same as the Manufacturer name.

4

Enter Product Information fields. All fields with red arrows are required.

Note: It is the store’s responsibility to ensure information is correct and provided by the manufacturer, not the distributor.

5

Select Add/Save.

Note: You will receive an email confirming that the request is in process.

6

Once the product is approved, you will be notified that the request is complete and provided the new sales number via email.
