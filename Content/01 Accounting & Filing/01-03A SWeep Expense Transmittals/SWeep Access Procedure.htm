SWeep Access Procedure

SWeep provides two access levels per store location: Manager and Associate. Access levels are determined by the employee's position at the store and SWeep will apply the correct access level/permissions when a user logs on with his or her Employee Number and Password.

Step

Action

1

Click on the Browser button from the POS.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

From SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Processes -> SWeep.

4

When you have completed your work in SWeep, log off by clicking the Logout Button located in the upper right hand corner of the screen.

Note: if you have questions, call the Accounts Payable Help Line at 216-566-2297.
