  • Vendor details,

Faxing Invoices
16

When the Click here to Fax Attachments box is selected, a fax coversheet  is created for vendor invoices.  Each fax coversheet has a unique barcode to identify the corresponding SWeep expense transmittal. 
17

Before faxing vendor invoices, check the resolution on the fax machine. Since faxing is processed electronically, if your fax cannot be read by the system, your invoice may not be paid.

Ensure that your faxes are legible.  Verify that your fax machine's resolution is set to "fine" or "300x300." DO NOT use PHOTO quality setting. The documents must be transmitted as standard FAX documents.
18 The fax number for the Financial Shared Services Group prints on the fax coversheet.   This fax number can only be used for submitting expense transmittals.  Do NOT fax SWeep Expense Transmittal coversheets and invoices to any other number.  They will not be processed for validation.
19

Positioning the paper appropriately will be unique to your machine.  The coversheet must enter the machine so the barcode is scanned first, which will create a record for that image, and it must go on top of or be faxed before the invoice.

Note: Multiple invoices may be sent in a single fax transmission; however, each invoice must have its own coversheet.
20

You will receive an e-mail once your fax has been processed and the invoice image is attached to the invoice record.

Note: After the invoice has been submitted, It takes approximately 5 to 10 minutes for it to become available through SWeep.
