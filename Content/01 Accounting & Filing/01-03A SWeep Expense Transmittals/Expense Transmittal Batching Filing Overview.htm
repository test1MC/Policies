Expense Transmittal Batching & Filing Overview

All physical invoices and hard copy documentation must be retained for [5 + current year] at the store for auditor review. The Accounts Payable Department will also conduct reviews and may request that a copy of the original information be sent to the department for further research.

Physical invoices and hard copy documents should be filed in the Store Purchases Report Review folder. Each month, use the invoices in the folder along with the Stores Purchase Report Review Journals available on the INQUIRY Menu [32. SPR Review] to guide your monthly SPR review process. Once cleared, retain the documentation with the monthly SPR information for the month in which they cleared. Refer to Filing & Retention Batch Box Overview for specific retention information.
