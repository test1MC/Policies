Form Retention Policy

You are required to retain a numeric store file for all manual downtime forms both used and unused. Please refer to the following chart. Sort the forms according to the Filing & Retention Batch Box Overview.

  1. Once validated, all Manual POST forms, including; imprinted manual credit card forms, must be retained in numeric sequence in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe to protect customer information. Staple a copy of the computer generated transaction and credit card form to the completed POST form. Retain the processed forms in a file folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS - USED. 
  2. Perform a Manual Forms INQUIRY to verify the validation of all manual forms by selecting Store Accountability Inquiry from the INQUIRY Menu. Press ENTER to access the five selections.
  3. All copies of Voided Manual POST Forms must be kept in numerical order. To void a Manual POST Form, write VOID across the face of the form then have the Store Manager sign the voided form. All voided forms must be cross-referenced to the correct transaction (if applicable).
  4. Blank Manual POST Forms should be retained in a folder entitled MANUAL POST FORMS - UNUSED. Blank Manual B-Prefix Purchase Order Forms should be retained in a folder entitled B-PREFIX PURCHASE ORDER FORMS - UNUSED. However, the manual credit card imprint may be cross-cut shredded after 18 months

    1. If you have more than a one four-drawer filing cabinet, this space may be used to store all "blank" stationery forms.

    2. Forms that cannot be filed (i.e., Computer Forms, Computer Paper, envelopes, etc.) should be stored in a safe area within the store
  5. Original [white] copies of B-Prefix Purchase Order forms should be attached to a special order form, if required. This copy must either be mailed to the vendor, if requested, or destroyed. The Receiving Tally [yellow] copy of the B-Prefix Purchase Order Form should be retained until the merchandise ordered has been received in Merchandise Management and, then, destroyed. All Store [pink] copies of used Manual B-Prefix Purchase Order forms must be retained in numeric sequence in a folder entitled B-PREFIX PURCHASE ORDER- USED.
