Destroy Records Policy

Discarding old documents will ensure that valuable storage space will not be taken up by unneeded documentation.

  1. Records must be disposed of in sealed containers. If possible, documents should also be shredded before disposal.
  2. Confidential documents must be destroyed before disposal.
