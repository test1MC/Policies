Destroy Records Procedure

Step

Action

1

If possible, shred all documents before disposal.

2

Box records in containers and seal the containers tightly before placing out the day of the Store's regular rubbish pickup.

3

Destroy all old confidential documents such as Contracts, Customer Credit Card Information, Price Record Cards, Computer Generated Reports, Personnel Records, and Payroll Records. Items that are destroyed should be placed out on the day of pickup.
