Filing & Retention Overview

Under normal circumstances, all batching and approvals should be completed every day for the previous day. Batching timeliness is important; each employee stands a better chance of remembering and making any necessary corrections when batching and approvals are completed next day. On occasion, approvals might not be able to be completed next day. For example, if the manager is out on sales calls or on vacation, approvals might need to be held until the manager returns to the store. If this is the case, batching should be approved within 24-48 hours after the manager returns to the store.
