Reprint Registers Procedure

Transaction Registers automatically print after closing the point of sale and completing End of Day. Occasionally, you may need to reprint the Registers used for batching purposes.

Step

Action

1

Select Inquiry [F4] from the POS.

2

Select Reprint Registers Menu from Miscellaneous.
