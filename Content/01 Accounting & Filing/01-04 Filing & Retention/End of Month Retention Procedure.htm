End of Month Retention Procedure

Step

Action

1

Place the dated Daily Transaction Envelopes with the monthly batching.

Note: These daily envelopes are to be stored in a Batching Box (Batching Box = Any Box).

2

Remove all Registers and place them on top of the monthly batching excluding the Store Drafts Register.

3

Retain the STORE DRAFTS REGISTER folder for an entire year.

Note: Do Not place the contents of the STORE DRAFTS REGISTER folder with the monthly batching.
