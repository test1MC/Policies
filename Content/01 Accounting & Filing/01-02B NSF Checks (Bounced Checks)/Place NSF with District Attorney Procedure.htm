Place NSF with District Attorney Procedure

This section applies to all Paint Stores except the Eastern Division Paint Stores.

Written District Manager approval is required before implementing this process. Prior to placing an NSF check with the District Attorney or Sheriff’s department , verify that NSF Check Management System has not already placed it with the collection agency.

If Then

The check has been placed with the primary collection agency

Do not place it with any other third party.

If the check has not been placed with the primary collection agency

Place it with the District Attorney and email the details to nsfchecks@sherwin.com.

Note: If the location chooses to always place unpaid NSF checks with the district attorney, email the above address so that they can stop the automated placement of NSF checks with a collection agency.
