Report NSF Designated Fraud Procedure

This Fraud process is only for Paint Stores located in the Midwestern and South Western Divisions.

Step Action

1

Attempt to contact the customer before notifying the authorities.

2

Contact Loss Prevention for guidance regarding any fraud checks over $100.00 or multiple checks by the same customer.

3

Complete an LOI.
