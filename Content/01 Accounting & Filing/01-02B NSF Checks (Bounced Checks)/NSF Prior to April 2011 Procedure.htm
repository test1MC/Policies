NSF Prior to April 2011 Procedure

This section only applies to US Paint Stores located within the fifty states.

Product Finishes facilities have two features available on the NSF Check Management System: locations get a notification of an NSF check and are able to view an image of the check through the NSF Check Management System. Letters will not be sent automatically and NSF will not be placed automatically with collection.

Any NSF check that has not been turned over for collection prior to April 01, 2011 should be mailed to Sarah Bradic at the address below. The location should keep a copy of the check and note it has been forwarded to SW Cleveland:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
