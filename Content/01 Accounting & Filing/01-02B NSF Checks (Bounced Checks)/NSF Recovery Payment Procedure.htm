NSF Recovery Payment Procedure

Locations must complete the procedure to Record Payment in the NSF Check Management System. Canadian Paint Stores must contact the collection agency to notify them of the payment. When a customer makes a partial or full payment on an NSF Check and fee, it is processed as a Miscellaneous Receipt transaction and the collections process can be stopped:

If... Then...

The location uses the NSF Check Management System

Process the Miscellaneous Receipt and record the payment in the NSF Check Management System (procedure below).

Note: Recording the payment stops the automated notification process.
The recovery payment is made at a different store than the location for the original purchase and the collecting store uses the NSF Check Management System

Process the Miscellaneous Receipt and record the payment in the NSF Check Management System (procedure below).

Note: Bookkeeping will handle re-coding recovery NSF payments that were received at a location other than where the original transaction was processed. Recording the payment stops the automated notification process.

The location does not use the NSF Check Management System and has placed a collection with Staber Inc, United Collection Bureau, D&B, or another approved collection agency

Contact the collection agency to inform them of the payment.
Step Action

Process Miscellaneous Receipt

1

Main Menu -> Point of Sale -> Sale/Payment Menu.

2

Press Page Down to access the Special Menu.

3

Select Misc Cash Receipt.

4

Enter the appropriate parameters and press Accept [F1] .

5

Select Other from GL Account List.

6

Select NSF Recoveries 7510-005 for both lines.

7

Enter the NSF check amount and NSF fee on separate lines.

8

Press Comment [F9] to enter the original transaction number and date.

9

Select the correct Form of Payment to complete the transaction and stop the collection process.

Record a Payment in NSF Check Management System:

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Credit/Pricing -> NSF Check Management System.

2

Click Receive a Payment.

3

Locate and select the correct account from the list.

4

Record the payment being applied to the principal owed.

5

Enter the NSF Fee amount.

6

Enter the Transaction Number from the Miscellaneous Receipt.

7

Click Record the Payment.

8

Click NSF Main Menu.

Note: All checks and fees must be paid before any additional checks may be accepted for that customer.
