NSF Payment on Account Procedure

If the NSF check was for a payment on account transaction, there is no need to process a transaction to reverse the payment; however, the NSF check must still be processed.

Step Action

1
If Then

FSS Bookkeeping mailed the NSF check

The National Credit Center in Cleveland has already reversed the payment on the customer’s account and has contacted the DCO.

The local bank returned the NSF check

Contact Cleveland at 1-800-538-8242 x3411. They will reverse the payment on the customer’s account.

Note: Contact your local Financial Services Center/ District Credit Office.

2

Contact the Credit Office to inform them that the payment has been charged back to the customer’s account.

3

Notify the customer that their check has been returned and their Sherwin-Williams account has been charged with the original payment.  Ask them how they would like to handle the repayment on the account and the $10-$50 Returned Check Fee.

4

Process a Miscellaneous Receipt transaction, using the prime-sub 7510-005, for the returned check fee. Do not include the fee as part of the payment on account.

Note: If a customer uses a NSF check for a check drawn on a closed checking account for payment on account, contact the Credit Office, they must approve further credit sales or acceptance of checks.

Note: All checks and fees must be paid before any additional checks may be accepted from that customer.
