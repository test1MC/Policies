Draft Log Inquiry Procedure

Step

Action

1

From any POS screen, press the Inquiry key.

2

Select Draft Log > By Date by Draft Number.

3

Enter the date range and press Enter.

4

Select View on this Screen from the Options Menu.

Note: The complete store draft information is available for each transaction paid by store draft.  Do not include the last digit of the draft number to review in numeric sequence.
