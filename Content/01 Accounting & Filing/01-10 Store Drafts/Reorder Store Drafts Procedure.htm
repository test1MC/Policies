Reorder Store Drafts Procedure

Drafts should be ordered when the store has approximately a two week supply of Store Drafts.  The order will take approximately one week (from the date of the order) to arrive at the store.

Step

Action

1

Complete the Reorder Form that is attached to the draft book by including the following information:

  • Store number
  • Store type
  • Last draft number in the book

2

Email or fax the form to StoreDrafts@sherwin.com (Fax: 216-566-3084).

Note: For more information or questions, call 216-515-4660.

Note: Only the Manager or Assistant Manager may complete the reorder form.
