Draft Downtime Policy

Downtime processing instructions should be followed if the Store Computer System is not working for more than one day.

  1. Manual POST Forms must be completed to capture all transaction information.
  2. Call 216-515-4660 (216-566-2287 stores in Canada ONLY) after completing the Store Draft.
  3. An approval number must be obtained and recorded on the Manual POST form.
  4. When the Store Computer System is operating, all downtime store draft payments must be processed by first selecting [Enter and validate a manual form]. The approval number must be captured in the comments area.
