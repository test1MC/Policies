Check Payment Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Enter customer information and items for purchase -> Select Check from the Form of Payment Menu.

2

When the prompt appears, insert the check into the Validation Printer. Enter the paper vertically and line it up against the right hand plate. Move it forward until the check stops.

3

The check will be imprinted with the “Sherwin-Williams Deposit Only” message. The Validation Printer will print the store information. Reference SOURCE Training for instructions on how to properly feed a check into the Validation Printer.

Note: The imprinted message indicates that the check may only be deposited in a Sherwin-Williams store account.

4

If a check is inadvertently entered as cash, enter the correct check amount while processing the Close Transaction. You will be prompted to enter the reason for the Cash/Check Variance.

5

Verify the “Draw Count Accountability” check amount with the “actual drawer count” check amount to find any variance.

Note: For more information, see Computer-Generated Close Overview and NSF Check Management Overview .

6

If the computer system is not working properly, stamp the customer’s check with the [For Deposit Only] stamp. Capture all customer information on the Manual POST Form, including Transaction Date, Customer Name, Address, City, State, Phone Number, and Proper Identification. This will be used when entering the transaction into the computer system.

7

If you are unable to process a sale for merchandise, remember that you are not permitted to hold customer checks. If necessary, process a deposit transaction the day you receive the customer check. Then, process a correction transaction to use the deposit on future purchases.
