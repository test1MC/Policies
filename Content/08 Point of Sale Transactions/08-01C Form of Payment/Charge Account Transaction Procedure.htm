Charge Account Transaction Procedure

Step

Action

1

If the customer has a commercial account, a purchase order or job number may be provided for the transaction.

2

When processing the charge sale, make sure to select the proper job number.

3

Enter the purchase order number on the invoice.

Note: Method of payment is decided at the end of the transaction. Entering the customer’s Account Number will activate their pricing information and allow tracking of all purchases. The use of a customer’s Charge Account Number will not limit the customer at Form of Payment. Choices include Cash, Check, Credit Card, and S-W Charge.

4

Enter the driver’s license number in the Comments section of the invoice for any authorized buyer that does not look familiar.

5

Enter the name of the person picking up the merchandise before completing a sale. The following option window will be available for non-centrally invoiced customers, and it can be accessed by pressing LOOKUP [F3] or ENTER:

"SIGNATURE LINE CAPTION

Delivered to: (Name of receiver)

Sign and print name here

Customer Signature"

6

The following option window is available for centrally invoiced customers, and it can be accessed by pressing LOOKUP [F3] or ENTER:

"SIGNATURE LINE CAPTION

Delivered to: (Name of receiver)

Ordered by: (Name of purchaser)"
