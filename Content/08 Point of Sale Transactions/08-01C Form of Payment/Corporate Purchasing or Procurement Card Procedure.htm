Corporate Purchasing or Procurement Card Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

A Corporate Purchasing or Procurement Card is a Visa/MasterCard or American Express credit card designed specifically for the purchasing process. Currently, Sherwin-Williams is able to provide Level 2 information. Employees throughout an organization may receive individual card accounts and use them to purchase small-dollar goods and services.

Step Action

1

When an individual uses the assigned card, the card issuer will send a detailed report of each purchase to their employer. The employer receiving the report will enter the details into their accounting system.

2

You will be prompted to enter a Point of Sale Account Code for each transaction paid by a Purchasing or Procurement Card. The POS Accounting Code is assigned by the cardholder’s company to enable them to track credit card purchases.

3

You will see the following message when you are prompted to enter the POS Account Code:

"Ask the customer for their POS Accounting Code provided by their employer. The Accounting Code can be up to 17 alpha/numeric characters. You are required to ask the customer for the Accounting Code each time a purchase is made. If the customer does not have an Accounting Code, press ENTER to continue."
