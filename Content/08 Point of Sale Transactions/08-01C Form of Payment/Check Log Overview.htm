Check Log Overview

When processing check transactions:

  • The computer system will generate a Check Log from the information entered.
  • The customer’s name, phone number, check number, and the amount of the sale will be captured and printed after the Close transaction.
  • Capture all check information on the Manual POST Form if the computer system is not operating. When the computer system resumes functionality, select Enter and Validate Manual Form from the Sales Payment Menu (from POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Enter and Validate Manual Form).
