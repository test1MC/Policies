Credit Card Authorization Numbers Procedure

When customers change their mind about a purchase or decide on a different form of payment after an authorization against their credit card has been received, it is necessary for the store to contact the credit card company to have the charges released. In the past, unless the sale was completed and the receipt printed, the authorization number was unavailable. The authorization number is needed in order to have the charges against the credit card released. To view credit card authorization numbers, use INQUIRY [F4].

Step Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Press INQUIRY [F4] from any POS screen.

2

Select Credit Card Authorizations:

3

A window will display the date, time, terminal number, transaction number, partially masked number, authorization number, and amount. Transactions will be sorted by the most recent authorizations.

Note: You are still required to call the credit card company to have the charges removed from the credit card.
