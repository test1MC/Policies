Partial Credit Approval Procedure

If Then

A credit card is only authorized for a partial payment, and the customer wants to put the remainder of the total on another form of payment

Process another form of payment for the remainder of the total.

A credit card is only authorized for a partial payment, and the customer wants to put the entire amount on another form of payment
  • Press Cancel [F5] to return to the line item screen
  • Acknowledge the Held Funds warning
  • Process another form of payment

Note: The system will now automatically release the held funds back onto the customer’s original card.

A credit card is only authorized for a partial payment, and the customer wants to cancel the transaction
  • Press Cancel [F5] to return to the line item screen
  • Acknowledge the Held Funds warning
  • Cancel the transaction

Note: The system will now automatically release the held funds back onto the customer’s original card.
