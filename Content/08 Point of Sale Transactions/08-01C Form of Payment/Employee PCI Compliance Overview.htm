Employee PCI Compliance Overview

Employee PCI Compliance is part of the annual employee Policy Review. All employees must read and acknowledge the Employee PCI Compliance annually:

"I hereby acknowledge and agree that I have reviewed and understand: (a) the terms and my obligations under the Credit Card Security/Payment Card Industry Policy of Sherwin-Williams (the 'Policy'); and (b) the security standards established by major credit card companies regarding the use and retention of customer credit card information by Sherwin-Williams. I further acknowledge and agree that I will comply with the Policy by adhering to the following practices:

  • The customer’s completed and signed Authorization to Retain and Use Credit Card Information Form, located in the Company’s Forms Repository, is the only permissible method to collect and to retain customer credit card information.
  • Every customer that completes and submits a signed form to Sherwin-Williams will immediately be informed that the Sherwin-Williams Privacy Policy is available online at www.sherwin.com/privacy.jsp.
  • To prevent unauthorized access to information, all forms retained by Sherwin-Williams must be stored in a secured location, such as a locked safe or locked filing cabinet."
