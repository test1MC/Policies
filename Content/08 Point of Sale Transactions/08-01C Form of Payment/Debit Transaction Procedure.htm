Debit Transaction Procedure

When the Debit Terminal is first turned on, whether during the initial installation or when electrical power is restored, the screen will display the Date, Time, Commservice, Softpay=FDCC, and Optional selections. Select Softpay-FDCC by pressing [F3].

Step Action

1

Press [F2] for Purchase.

2

Using the keypad, enter the amount of the sale. Press the green OK key.

3

Enter the Clerk/Server ID number and press OK.

4
If Then

The card is a CHIP card

Pass customers the terminal and have them insert their card, confirm the amount, and enter their PIN.

The card is not a CHIP card

Swipe the magnetic stripe or key enter the card number

5

The cardholder should press YES [F1] to process the transaction or NO [F2] to decline the transaction.

6

The screen will display the transaction status and a merchant receipt will print. Retain this copy in the cash drawer. Collect the terminal from the customer.

Note: If the card is declined, ask the customer for another form of payment. 

7

Tear off the merchant copy of the receipt and press OK to print the customer copy.
