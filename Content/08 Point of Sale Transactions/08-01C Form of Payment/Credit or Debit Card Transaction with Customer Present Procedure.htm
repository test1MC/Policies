Credit or Debit Card Transaction with Customer Present Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Enter customer information and items for purchase -> Select Credit/Debit at the Form of Payment Menu. The customer has the option to place the entire amount on one or more credit cards, or to pay with a combination of cash, check, and credit card.

2

If

Then

Chip-Enabled Card

The customer should insert the chip

Not Chip-Enabled Card

The customer should swipe the card

Note: The terminal will timeout after 30 seconds of inactivity. To cancel payment, have the customer press the X button. If the card is damaged, customers may key in card numbers. They must then enter the card’s expiration date, CVV, and zip code. The zip code must be the same one given to the credit card issuer when the customer originally opened the credit card account. The zip code will not be required for SWAT transactions (when they are processed at the store), E-Commerce transactions, credits to the customer, and Wallcovering Book Checkout. After three failed attempts at entering the chip, request a different form of payment.

3

If

Then

Debit

Customer should enter PIN on PAX

Debit but want to run as Credit

 Customer should press the green button to bypass PIN entry

Credit

 Customer should sign the PAX

4

The PAX Credit Card Machine will then automatically call out for authorization and return it in about thirty (30) seconds.

5
If Then

The credit card transaction is approved

The system will return the following message: Credit card transaction is APPROVED.

The credit card transaction is declined

Follow the Declined Credit Card Transaction Procedure.

The credit card is only approved for partial payment

Follow the Partial Credit Approval Procedure.

6

Give the customer a copy of the transaction and place the remaining copies of the invoice in the forms container.

Note:  All processed manual forms, including imprinted manual credit card forms and processed manual POST forms, must be retained in a folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS – USED and placed in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe to protect customer information. The processed manual POST form must be retained for 5 years plus the current year. However, the manual credit card imprint may be cross-cut shredded after 18 months. For more information see Credit Card Information Retention and Disposal Policy and Destroy Records Policy.
