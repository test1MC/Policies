Comchek Payment Policy

  1. Comcheks should not be accepted if the Authorization Number is filled in when the check is presented at the time of sale. The customer may not complete this part of the check.
  2. Do not cash a Comchek before calling the toll-free number to obtain an authorization number. The Comchek is not valid without obtaining an authorization number before cashing or accepting for payment.
  3. All customer information including:
    • Card Number/Express Code
    • Payee’s Home Phone Number, State
    • Identification Number
    • Dollar amount must be complete and legible
  4. Comcheks can be obtained just about anywhere (i.e. truck stops).
  5. The Comchek will have a Code Number if the check is written from an individual’s account (i.e. individual’s company spending account). The Comchek will have an Express Code if the check is written from a company’s account.
  6. The customer must provide a current in-state driver’s license for identification. The Comchek will not be honored without government issued photo identification being recorded on the check. The store must verify that the information on the check is accurate.
