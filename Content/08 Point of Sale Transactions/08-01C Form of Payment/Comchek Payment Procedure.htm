Comchek Payment Procedure

Step Action

1

When processing a Comchek, ensure that the Authorization Number field is blank on the check and that all other information is complete when the check is presented. Completed customer information must be verified with valid in-state government issued photo identification.

2

Before processing the Comchek, call the toll-free number on the check to obtain a valid authorization number. The customer’s account will be reviewed to verify that funds are available in the amount of the check:

If Then

No authorization number is given

The Comchek may not be processed.

Given the authorization number

Document it on the check.

3

Once the authorization number is documented, the check may be processed like any other customer check.
