Card on File with Customer Not Present Sales Procedure

Step Action

1

When processing sales using payment information collected via phone, third party authorization, and retain and use credit card transactions, you will need to complete the sale on behalf of the customer on the PAX Credit Card Machine.

2

Process sale on POS -> select Card on File as Form of Payment -> Enter Employee Password.

Note: Manual entry on the PAX will be disabled until Card on File is selected. Canadian stores will then need to specify Credit or Debit.

3

On the PAX payment terminal, enter the credit card number (per the customer on the phone, via email, or previously collected on the third party authorization or retain and use forms).

Note: Do not write down card numbers given over the phone. Do not print or retain emailed information.

3
If Then

Phone

Ask for and enter zip and CVV (do not write down or retain either)
  • Retain and use
  • Third party authorization
  • Email

Use the green button to bypass the zip and CVV field.

4

Press Cancel or the X button on the PAX terminal on the Signature Screen.

5

Select the appropriate Override option on the POS.
