Embossed Credit Card Downtime Policy

  1. The Manual POST Form must be used for all embossed credit card transactions when the computer system is not working. If an unembossed credit card magnetic stripe cannot be read by the electronic credit card reader, ask the customer for another form of payment. There are no downtime processing instructions for unembossed credit cards.
  2. Complete a credit card sales form before you receive an authorization number for the credit card purchase.
  3. All processed manual forms, including imprinted manual credit card forms and processed Manual POST Forms, must be retained in a folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS – USED and placed in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe to protect customer information. The processed Manual POST Form must be retained for 5 years plus the current year. However, the manual credit card imprint may be cross-cut shredded after 18 months. For more information, see Credit Card Information Retention and Disposal Policy and Destroy Records Policy.
