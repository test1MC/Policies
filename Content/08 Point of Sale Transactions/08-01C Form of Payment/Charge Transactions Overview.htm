Charge Transactions Overview

In addition to using Cash, Checks, and Credit Cards to make purchases, customers may also establish a Sherwin-Williams Charge Account. Sherwin-Williams does not issue charge cards to its charge customers. Once a Sherwin-Williams account is opened, the customer is issued an account number. The customer will supply the names of their employees authorized to charge to the account.
