Correct a Debit Transaction Procedure

If sales were performed incorrectly, follow the procedures below. Reversals can only be done if the terminal has not been balanced since the incorrect transaction was made. If the terminal was balanced, refer to the . The card used to purchase merchandise must be present.

Step Action

1

Press the 1st purple key from left (arrow key).

2

Press [F1] for correction.

3
If Then

You wish to correct the last transaction

Press YES [F1].

You wish to correct any other transactions in the batch

Press NO [F2].

4

Press [F1] to search for transactions by invoice numbers or [F2] to search by account numbers.

5

Enter the invoice number or the last four digits of the account number. Press OK to continue.

6

If the transaction appears, press YES [F1]. Press NO [F2] to cancel the correction. Press NEXT [F3] to toggle to the next transaction in the batch.

7

Repeat Step 6 until you locate the correct transaction.

8

The correction will reverse the transaction and update the batch. Tear off the merchant copy receipt and press OK to print the customer copy.
