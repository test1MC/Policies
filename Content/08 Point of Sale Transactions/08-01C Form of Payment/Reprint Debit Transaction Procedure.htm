Reprint Debit Transaction Procedure

Note: This procedure can only be done for debit transactions made since the last balance of a terminal.

Step Action

1

Press the 3rd purple key from the left to access Reprint.

2

Press [F2] to print the last receipt or press [F3] to print any other transaction in the open batch.

3

Enter the invoice number for the transaction you would like to reprint and press OK.
