Daily Close Procedure

The procedures below will process all sales that have been entered since the last time the terminal was balanced. It will ensure that any charges/credits are applied to the customer’s card. Any funds due to your store are then sent to your account. As part of the balancing process, you should print a Totals or Detail Report to confirm batch totals. If you process transactions, balance and close daily to receive timely deposits to your funds.

Step Action

1

Press the 4th purple key from the left to open the Report Menu.

2

Select the report you wish to run by pressing [F2], [F3], or [F4] or press the first purple key to move through additional reports.

3

Select a report to print.

Note: Additional prompts may appear, depending on the selected report.

4

Press [F4] for SETTLEMENT. A Totals Report will print.

5

Enter the total of the batch from your Totals or Details Report and press OK.

6

The batch will settle and the current batch is then deleted from the terminal.

7

Process an Interim Cash Deposit for the daily Debit Card Total using the debit transaction total from the debit machine for a given day.

8

The Interim Cash Deposit amount will include the daily deposit, but it will not negatively affect the Close Transaction cash count.

9

The Debit Machine Report should be matched to the customer receipts in the drawer. Retain this with your daily batching.
