Canadian Debit Card Policy

This information is for locations in Canada ONLY.

Regarding debit terminals, note the following:

  • For assistance with any debit terminal issues, contact First Data Customer Service at 1-888-263-1938. This number is available every day of the year, twenty-four hours a day. Provide your 11-digit Debit Merchant ID—it should be affixed to the side of your debit terminal. If you do not have this ID number, please contact Pat Bodle at 216-566-3284.
  • This terminal is for debit transactions only. Use the Mag Stripe Reader connected to SOURCE to process credit transactions.
  • For more information, see the VeriFone Debit Terminal Pamphlet and VeriFone Downtime Processing Instructions.
  • If you experience issues with your debit terminal, please refer to the VeriFone Trouble Shooting Guide.

Policy:

  1. PLEASE NOTE: This terminal is for debit transactions only. Use the Mag Stripe Reader connected to SOURCE to process credit transactions.
  2. All processed manual forms, including imprinted manual credit card forms and processed manual POST forms, must be retained in a folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS – USED and placed in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe to protect customer information. The processed manual POST form must be retained for 5 years plus the current year. However, the manual credit card imprint may be cross-cut shredded after 18 months. For more information see Credit Card Information Retention and Disposal Policy and Destroy Records Policy
  3. You may only process a refund transaction up to the limit of $500.00 per transaction. If a refund exceeds this amount, you must process the refund payment as a Store Draft. There are no exceptions to this policy.
