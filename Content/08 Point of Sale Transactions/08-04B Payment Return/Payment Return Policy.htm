Payment Return Policy

This section applies only to Columbia Paint and Coatings, Duron Paints, and MAB Paints store locations.

  1. A Payment Return can be issued for an overpayment, duplicate payment, or a payment applied to the wrong account. Payment Returns must be referenced to the original payment.
  2. The Payment Return can be paid with cash, if under $100, or by Credit Card. Any cash refund over $100 must be paid using SWEEP.
  3. The customer must sign the store copy of the receipt as proof of payment for the refund. Include the home/business address and phone number.
