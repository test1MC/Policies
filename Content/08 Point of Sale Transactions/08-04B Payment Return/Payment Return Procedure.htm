Payment Return Procedure

This section applies only to Columbia Paint and Coatings, Duron Paints, and MAB Paints store locations.

Step Action
Process Payment Return Under $100
1 The refund should be paid with cash, but, before refunding, make sure the target fund will not be affected.
2 From the POS Desktop -> Sale/Payment Menu -> Payment Return.
3 Follow the prompts at the bottom of the screen.
4 The customer, employee, and store manager must sign the store copy of the receipt.
Process Refund Over $100
1

Select Cash as the Form of Refund, but do not actually give any cash to the customer.

 

Note: Headquarters Checks must be used for any cash refund over $100.

2

Both you and the customer must sign the Payment Return Receipt. Make the customer aware of the note at the bottom of the receipt: “Refund Check mailed from the main office within 15 working days.”

 

Note: Customers do not need to remain in the store for the following steps.

Process a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt
1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Point of Sale -> Enter you Employee Number and press ENTER.

 

Note: The Miscellaneous Cash Receipt will allow your cash drawer to balance at the end of the day.

2 Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.
3 Select Misc Cash Receipt.
4 Enter complete customer information when prompted and press ACCEPT [F1].
5 Press ENTER to view the GL Account List. Select Dist & Whse – Other (Prime-Sub 7599-099/P&L Line #64). All check returns from the main office must be itemized under this entry.
6 Enter the total refund dollar amount that displayed in the Customer Return Transaction.
7 Press COMMENTS [F9] and type, “Customer Refund – Main Office.” Press ACCEPT [F1].
8 Select Cash.
9 Press TOTAL [F12] for the total amount.
Create New Vendor Requests for Customers in SWeep
1 Select New Vendor Request from the SWeep homepage.
2 Enter the customer name, address, city, state, zip code, and phone number.
3

Select Customer Refund as the Vendor Type.

 

Note: This will give the request the highest priority. The Vendor Request Status will display a fire icon next to the customer’s name.

4 Enter your name in the Contact field. If the remit address is the same as the customer address provided, select Remit address same as above. If necessary, enter a new remit-to address.
5

In the Comments field, note the following:

  • Original Sale Terminal Number, Transaction Number, and Date.
  • Return Terminal Number, Transaction Number, and Date.
  • If the customer did not have the original receipt, include the reason for the return.
6 Submit your New Vendor Request.
Submit Invoice Information in SWEEP
1 Only the Store Manager will be able to complete the Invoice Information process.
2

When the customer name appears in the Vendor List, you are ready to enter the check request information.

 

Note: Typically, customer information will appear in the Vendor List within 24 hours after having requested the New Vendor Setup.

3 Submit a new invoice in SWEEP to request the check be cut for the customer.
4 Select the G/L Entry Dist & Whse – Other (Prime-Sub 7599-099).
5 Submit the Invoice Request.
6 Any special handling or mailing instructions must be communicated to Accounts Payable: 216-566-2297.
7 Once all of the above information is entered into the SWEEP system, customers should receive their return check within 3-5 business days. All approved refund requests will have payments generated the next business day. If you need a quicker turn-around, contact Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297 and ask to speak with an Expense Correspondent.
