Refund Installer Retainage Procedure

Step

Action

1

Contact the District Credit Office to determine what amount, if any, should be returned to the installer. Do not refund any money until the District Credit Office has approved the amount.

2

Select Retainage Refund from the Adjustment Reason Menu, which can be accessed from the Sale/Payment Menu.

Note: If you do not know the original terminal/transaction number, enter the terminal/transaction number for the Account Adjustment transaction that you are processing, which can be found in the upper right corner of the screen.

3

Process the transaction for the retainage amount to be refunded to the installer.

Note: This amount is usually the difference between the cost of any repairs and the amount originally retained.

4

Press COMMENT [F9] to enter an explanation for the transaction.

5

The installer and Sherwin-Williams’ employees must sign the form. Staple the story copy of the draft to the Store Draft Register.
