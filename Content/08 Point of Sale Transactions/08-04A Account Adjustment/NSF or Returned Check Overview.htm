NSF or Returned Check Overview

The following message will display on the screen after selecting [3. NSF/Returned Check] from the Account Adjustments Menu:

"There is no need for you to process a transaction to reverse the payment on account. Contact the National Credit Center to have the payment on account charged back to the customer's account and obtain the ARJV number. Contact your District Credit Office to inform them that the payment has been charged back to the customer's account and ask them how to proceed."

Customers must be told that their check was returned by the bank. They must redeem it from your store within 48 hours in cash, certified check, or money order and pay the $10.00 - $40.00 fee when picking up the returned check.

Note: NSF rates are listed by state. Please see NSF Check Policy.
