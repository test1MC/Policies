NSF Check Security Overview

If a customer has given an NSF check or a check on a closed account as a payment on account, then contact your District Credit Office immediately. Further credit sales or acceptance of checks must be approved by the District Credit Office. It is imperative that you validate each check in the store computer system. If your system is down, use the For Deposit Only check stamp to mark the check. Remember to capture customer information on the manual POST form.
