Account Adjustment Policy

  1. Employees are not permitted to process their own point of sale transactions. This includes ALL point of sale transactions. Another employee must process the point of sale transaction.
  2. Account Adjustments can be issued for an overpayment, duplicate payment, refund of joint check amount, and refund of installer retainage.
  3. DO NOT refund any amount to the customer without first checking with the District Credit Office. DO NOT make an adjustment to a customer’s account without first contacting the District Credit Office to receive an approval code. Refer to the following Approval Types:
    1. Payment/Credit Balance Refund - the store employee should know who they talked to at the DCO and capture the information on the transaction.
    2. Joint Check - the store employee should know who they talked to at the DCO and capture the information on the transaction.
    3. NSF/Return Check - the store employee should notify the DCO
    4. Retainage Refund - store employee should verify the balance through a phone call to DCO or by reviewing through A/R Access on the SOURCE Intranet.
    5. Account Adjustments must be referenced to the original transaction.
  4. Account Adjustments must be referenced to the original transaction.
  5. Account Adjustments can be credited by cash, Store Draft or Credit Card. DO NOT refund by cash if the refund amount will take you below your operating fund.
  6. The customer MUST sign the computer-generated form as proof of account adjustment and receipt for any payment refund. Include the home/business address and phone number.
