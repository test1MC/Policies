Account Adjustment Overview

This section does not apply to Columbia Paint & Coatings, Duron Paints, and MAB Paints.

Account Adjustments are modifications made against a customer’s Sherwin-Williams account. Account adjustments can be accessed from the Sale/Payment Menu. Options include:

  • Payment/Credit Balance Refund
  • Joint Check
  • NSF/Returned Check
  • Retainage Refund
