Payment or Credit Balance Refund Procedure

Step

Action

1

Call the District Office to verify the adjustment amount. A message at the bottom of the screen will remind you to call the District Credit Office.

2

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Account Adjustments -> Select Payment/Credit Balance Refund from the Adjustment Reason Menu.

3

Follow the prompts.

4

The employee and the Store Manager must sign the form. The customer should also sign, if available.

5

Press COMMENT [F9] and enter the District Credit Office approval number. Validate the Store Draft.

6

Staple the validated store copy of the draft to the (pink) store copy of the Account Adjustment by Store Draft transaction.

7

Pass the drafts on to the Store Manager for approval and then file according to batching instructions.

8

If

Then

The original payment was with a credit card

Refund using the customer’s credit card. Do not issue a store draft.

The original payment was with cash

Refund in cash. Do not issue a store draft unless the refund amount will take you below your operating fund. The customer must sign for cash refunds.
