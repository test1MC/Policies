Joint Check Refund Procedure

Step

Action

1

After the painter endorses the check, process the entire amount of the check as a Payment on Account.

2

Full payment for the merchandise must be made before refunding any money to the customer on a joint check. Contact the District Credit Office to determine what amount, if any, should be returned to the contractor. The District Credit Office will provide an approval code. Do not refund any money until the District Credit Office has approved the amount.

3

Select Joint Check from the Adjustment Reason Menu, which can be accessed from the Sale/Payment Menu. Process the transaction for the amount to be refunded to the customer. The store draft should be issued to the name of the person sharing the joint check with Sherwin-Williams.

Note: The refund amount is usually the difference between the cost of material used and the amount of the joint check.

4

Press COMMENT [F9] to enter an explanation for the transaction and the District Credit Office approval number.

5

Validate the story copy of the draft.

6

The customer, employee, and store manager must sign the form. If the customer is not present, then contact the District Credit Office for instructions.

7

Staple the validated story copy of the draft to the (pink) story copy of the Account Adjustment (Store Draft) transaction.

8

Have the Store Manager approve the transaction, and then file according to batching instructions. Cross-reference all documents.
