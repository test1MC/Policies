Store Expense Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.

2

Enter your Employee Number.

3

Press the Page Down Key at the Sale/Payment Menu.

4

Select Store Expense from the Special Menu.

5

Select the correct G/L Account from the G/L Account List.

When Then

An error was made during the mistint process.

Mistints

Expensing tint for a manual tinter.

Tint expense

Donations of less than perfect material such as mistints, obsolescence, and clearance merchandise can be made to charitable organizations with the approval of the District Manager.

Merchandise donations

Samples given to a customer.

Customer samples

Paint or other supplies used for redecorating or store maintenance.

Merchandise used for store maintenance

A theft has occurred at the store.

Stolen inventory

Floorcovering Installation complaint.

Merchandise given to a customer to settle a complaint (LABOR)

An item damaged in the store, i.e., broken roller frame, customer damages a display.

Unsalable, defective, or damaged floorcovering, wallcovering, paint and associated products.

Store has used material to demonstrate a product to a customer.

Merchandise used for demonstrations

A product does not live up to the paint can guarantee.

Guarantee policy adjustments

Note: If the G/L account is not on the list, select Other to view all available prime-sub accounts.

6

Enter the Bill to and Ship to information.

7

Enter the Sales Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the sales number.

8

Enter each item being expensed.

9

Enter the cost as $0 for all sales numbered merchandise.

Note: Enter the specific store cost for any class numbered merchandise.

10

Press ACCEPT [F1] to complete the transaction.

11

Re-label the merchandise with the appropriate Sales Number.
For more information see Reclassified Merchandise Procedure.

12

Place the merchandise in the location for Obsolete/Discontinued merchandise.
