Store Expense Policy

  1. Store Expenses should be processed as they occur.
  2. Mistinted product may be sold as long as it is not hazardous to the customer, or a hazardous waste.
  3. Store Expenses MUST be approved by the Store Manager.
  4. Any product expensed that was purchased locally MUST have the correct store cost entered in the price field.
  5. DO NOT process a store expense to Theft unless you are certain the merchandise was stolen. For more information see Inventory Adjustment Overview. If the merchandise is determined to be stolen, you are required to complete your Division LOI process. For more information see nLoss, Offense, & Incident Policy.
  6. During an inventory, mistinted merchandise must be counted and recorded on the appropriate page of the inventory package if on hand. Any other expensed merchandise should have been removed from the store (demo, complaint, donation) or consumed (store use).

Additional Policy - Applies to South Western Division Only

  1. The store must obtain approval from the District Manager or District Financial Manager prior to running a Store Expense Transaction for either 80 gallons or more of a single SKU or when a store needs to Store Expense a spray rig or any other piece of equipment that is tracked by a serial number.
