Automotive Store Expense Policy

Applies to Automotive Branches Only.

  1. All Store Expense Transactions, including Promos, FCAs, and other expenses over $500 require Management Approval using the eAccounting System. This approval must be obtained in advance for all expenses, regardless of the reason. Once approved, the eAccounting system will provide a valid, approved six digit number to process the Store Expense Transaction.
