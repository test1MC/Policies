Expensed Merchandise Sale Policy

  1. Mistinted merchandise may be sold as long as it is not hazardous to the customer, or a hazardous waste.
  2. Use the Obsolete/Discontinued Merchandise Sales Numbers when entering into the computer and should be independent of the store expense process.
  3. The customer may purchase this merchandise with Cash, Check, Credit Card, or Sherwin-Williams Charge.
  4. If you purchase expense items from the DSC that are subsequently sold to a customer, a Sherwin-Williams employee, or IST'd to another store, process the transaction using the assigned sales number. This process will relieve the processing store’s expenses.
