Miscellaneous Cash Receipt Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down on the Sale/Payment Menu.

2

Select Misc Cash Receipt from the Special Menu.

3

When prompted, enter complete customer information.

4

Select the correct GL Account Description from the GL Account Listing.

Note: For more information, see 04-02 General Ledger by Subject.

5

Enter the dollar amount.

6

Press COMMENTS [F9] to capture the receipt reason, the original term/tran number, and the date for NSF Check payments. Press ACCEPT [F1] when complete.

7

Press TOTAL [F12] to complete the transaction.

8

The Store Manager or Assistant Manager must sign the form.

9

Place all forms in the forms container under the store computer system equipment.
