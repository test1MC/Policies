Miscellaneous Cash Receipt Policy

A Miscellaneous Cash Receipt transaction is used to account for the miscellaneous receipt of funds in Sherwin-Williams stores. This includes reimbursement for personal emergency phone calls, charges for returned checks, etc.

  1. BillTo: customer information MUST be completed when processing a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt.
  2. Employees are not permitted to process their own POS transactions. This includes all POS transactions. Another employee must process the POS transaction.
  3. A Store or Assistant Manager’s signature is required for all Miscellaneous Cash Receipts.
  4. Amount received MUST be coded to the proper General Ledger Account Number.
