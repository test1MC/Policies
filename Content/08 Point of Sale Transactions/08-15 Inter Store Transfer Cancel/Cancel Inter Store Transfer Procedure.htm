Cancel Inter Store Transfer Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to open Special Menu -> Select IST Cancel -> The shipping store must enter the original terminal and transaction number and the original date.

2

Complete store information for all Sherwin-Williams stores resides in your store computer system. Enter the store number or press LOOKUP [F3] to look at specific store information.

3

Use the original store number, sales number(s), quantity(s), and cost(s) that were shown on the original IST.

4

Notify the requesting store to cancel their ISR Purchase Order.
