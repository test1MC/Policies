Inter Store Transfer Cancel Policy

  1. Inter Store Transfers cannot be corrected. They must be canceled and then re-run as a new transaction.
  2. The shipping store must cancel the transfer.
  3. This procedure may be used to cancel just one item, or several items in a transaction.
  4. IST Cancels can be used to correct errors that are discovered after the original IST is run.
  5. IST Cancels MUST reference the original transaction.
  6. Corrections to an IST should only be performed by the shipping store.
