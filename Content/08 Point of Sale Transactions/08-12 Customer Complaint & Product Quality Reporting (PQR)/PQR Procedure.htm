PQR Procedure

Process one of the four options on the Product Quality Reporting (PQR) Menu: In Store Resolution – Not a Product Failure, I Need Help – Possible Product Failure, Quality Notification, or POS Settlement of an Open PQR

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select PQR – Product Quality Reporting from the Sale/Payment Menu.

4
If Then
In Store Resolution - Not a Product Failure Select In Store Resolution – Not a Product Failure from the Product Quality Reporting Menu -> Proceed to Step 6.
I Need Help – Possible Product Failure

Select I Need Help – Possible Product Failure from the Product Quality Reporting Menu -> Proceed to Step 5.
Quality Notification

Select Quality Notification from the Product Quality Reporting Menu -> Proceed to Step 6.
POS Settlement of an Open PQR

Select POS Settlement of an Open PQR from the Product Quality Reporting Menu -> Proceed to Step 7.
5

Is this a new or existing PQR?

If Then

New PQR

Select Record a New PQR.

Existing  PQR

Select Update/View an Existing PQR.

Note: Selecting an existing PQR will take you directly to SWEEP.

6

Look up the Customer Transaction:

If Then

You have a receipt

Scan the barcode on the Customer Receipt -> Proceed to Step 8.

The receipt will not scan

Enter the Terminal Id, Transaction Number, and Date -> Proceed to Step 8.

No receipt available

Press Lookup [F3] to look up the Customer Account -> Proceed to Step 8.

Paint has not been sold

Enter 1 in the Account Number field and enter the store information in the address fields -> Proceed to Step 8.
7

Select the Settlement Type to be processed from the Product Quality Reporting Settlement Menu:

If Then

Customer wants replacement material

Store Expense

Refer to Store Expense Procedure

Customer wants a refund

Return

Refer to Return Transaction Procedure

Customer wants labor reimbursement < $100

Miscellaneous Disbursement by Store Draft

Refer to Store Draft Disbursement Procedure

Customer wants labor reimbursement > $100

Request check from Cleveland

Refer to Submit Expense Transmittals Policy

Note: Use the PQR # for the invoice number

Customer wants labor reimbursement (credit to account

Request ARJV

Note: The AR Journal Voucher Display is used to view created ARJVs. The screen displays the details of the ARJV adjustment, a brief explanation as to why it was created and per whom, the dollar amount, and the prime-sub the entry was debited/credited to in the general ledger. To view, type in the account number, job number, and ARJV number.

For authority and charge account information, please see: PQR Settlement Type Overview.
8
If Then
In Store Resolution - Not a Product Failure Enter the Sales Number or press Lookup [F3] -> Press Accept [F1] to continue to the next step.
I Need Help – Possible Product Failure & Quality Notification

Enter the Sales Number or press Lookup [F3] -> Press Accept [F1] to submit the PQR -> Select OK to enter PQR Details on SWeep Procedure -> This procedure is complete.

Note: Do not use Account Number 9 (Employee) for paint not sold. The system will register the transaction as an employee purchase and not a possible product failure.
POS Settlement of an Open PQR

When finished, select  Close PQR – Return to POS -> This procedure is complete.

9

Identify and enter the Batch Number:

If Then

The number cannot be found

Type Unknown

Note: The batch number is located on either the top or side of the can.

10

Enter the problem: Press Lookup [F3] to display the Reason Codes -> Using the arrow keys, select the Reason why the customer is returning the product -> Press Enter to complete the transaction -> Select OK to return to POS.
Additional Steps

1

Did a products release form print?

If Then
Yes Have the customer sign the form
No From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Select Forms Repository from Information Library -> Search for the form by typing “settlement” in the form name field -> Print a copy of the form that applies to your store location.

Note:  To make this form a favorite, select Add to Favorites.

Note:  A signed release form must be obtained for all settlements > $100.00
