Product Quality Reporting (PQR) Overview

This section does not apply to Floorcovering Facilities. For more information, please see Floorcovering Customer Orders Policy.

PQR is used for all customer complaints and quality issues at your store. The problem can be as simple as a customer returning a gallon because he/she did not like the color to as complex as a product failure with a potential liability of $20,000. The PQR should be entered by the store that sold the material directly to the purchasing customer. Whenever you have a customer unhappy with a product requesting merchandise or financial reimbursement, need help figuring out a problem, or just need to make sure someone is aware of a product issue, PQR is the answer. Customer concerns addressed through PQR may include:

  • label guarantee
  • warranty
  • mistints
  • all other product issues

In this situation, there is no concern of a product failure and settlement is immediate for merchandise, but not for labor. If help is needed in determining a potential product failure, the original transaction is entered into the PQR website and specific detail concerning the product is recorded, including the potential liability involved.
