Customer Complaint & PQR Policy

  1. All possible product failures must be reported. Investigation and follow-up will occur for all complaints.
  2. All transactions must be signed by the customer, the employee completing the transaction, and the Store Manager.
  3. Reimbursements of $100 or more require a signed customer release form and must be retained at the store.
  4. Store drafts over $100 and store expenses over $750 require City or District Manager approval. Settlements through SWeep will follow the SWeep approval chain.
  5. Returns must be entered through POS to relieve inventory and process settlements.
  6. Guarantee Policy Adjustment should be used when the product does not hold up to the guarantees of performance we claim such as coverage, hide, and warranty years. Customer complaints should be used to settle other issues where the customer just did not like the product for some reason not covered by the label guarantee policy.
