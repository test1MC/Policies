PQR Type of Help Overview

The following chart explains the Type of Help available. Based on the failure reason selected in SWeep, one or more of these options will be available.

Type of Help Description

Retain Check

Test done on a small sample from every batch. Verifies the product was produced to correct specifications. A written response will be received within 5 days.

Field Sample Check

Select this option when the retain check sample is too small for testing, or the retain sample does not match what the store sees.

Coated/Uncoated Part Analysis (Powder)

Used for potential powder coating failures such as poor adhesion and a retain check cannot be used.

Chip Analysis

Paint chips may be sent for analysis to determine the cause of peeling complaints and to confirm the actual paint used or mix ratios.

Laboratory Service Request Available only to Product Finishes Facilities (Product Finishes – Liquid)

Only used by Product Finishes locations. Evaluation of a competitor product, best method of application, or investigation of a complaint.

Picture Review

A digital picture can be electronically attached to the PQR, sent either by e-mail, or printed and mailed.

Color  Check

To be used when the color is believed not to match the color feature (chip).

DSC Check

Inventory will be checked in the DSC to verify if a problem exists.

Division Review

If a failure reason is not chosen, someone from the Division will receive the PQR, review your comments, and reassign the proper reason. This takes time and will delay your response.
