No Sale Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select No Sale/Interim Deposit from the Sale/Payment Menu.

2

After making a selection from the No-Sale Reason Codes Menu, you will be required to enter your employee password.

3

The selection will return to the screen and automatically print on the invoice.

Note: Any time Other is selected as a reason, it is required that a reason be typed in.
