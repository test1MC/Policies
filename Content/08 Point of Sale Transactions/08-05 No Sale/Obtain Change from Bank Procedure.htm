Obtain Change from Bank Procedure

Determine the type and amount of change needed.

Step Action
1

Determine the type and amount of change needed.

If Then

The money is taken from the cash drawer

Proceed to Step 2.

They money is taken from the cash box in the safe

Record the amount of money being taken to the bank and sign the form. Place that information in the safe.

2

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select No Sale from the Sale/Payment Menu.

3

Select Took Cash to Bank for Change from the No-Sale Reason Codes Menu.

4

Input the amount of money being removed from the cash drawer.

5

Enter your Employee Password.

6

Have the employee taking the money sign the computer-generated form and write down the amount of money being taken.

Note: If the money is taken from the cash box in the safe, record the same information and place it in the safe.

7

When the employee returns with the change, verify that the amount returned is the same as the No Sale Receipt.

8

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select No Sale from the Sale/Payment Menu.

9

Select Return Cash from Bank from the No-Sale Reason Codes Menu.

10

Place the cash in the drawer and close it when done.

11

Sign the computer-generated form and place into batching.
