No Sale Policy

A No Sale is a non-sale related transaction that allows access to the cash drawer.

  1. Perform a No Sale in order to open the cash drawer for a non-sales transaction.
  2. No Sale reason codes must be completed correctly. If OTHER is selected from the menu, an explanation must be typed in.
  3. You are required to enter your employee password when prompted.
  4. All No Sales must be approved and signed by the Store Manager.
  5. Opening the cash drawer while the computer system is operating properly MUST be completed by using the No Sale transaction.
