Resale Policy

Except for the statement regarding ‘selling of mistints,’ this section does not apply to the following locations: Automotive Branches, M.A.B. Paints selling to M.A.B. Dealers, Mautz Stores selling to Mautz Dealers, or Flexbon Stores selling to Flexbon Dealers.

  1. No Sherwin-Williams employee can sell any paint or non-paint products to a customer knowing that they intend to resell those products out of the store, over the Internet, through a telemarketing call center, or any other place of business.
  2. Merchandise cannot be sold to a customer with the purpose of the customer using the products for resale. This includes selling mistints to a discount re-seller that sell at flea markets. For more information, refer to Materials Acceptance Agreement Purchase.
  3. The following chart shows examples of acceptable and non-acceptable selling arrangements. Please ask your District Manager if you have questions about acceptable resale arrangements. Your District Manager will send an email to methods@sherwin.com if clarification is needed.
