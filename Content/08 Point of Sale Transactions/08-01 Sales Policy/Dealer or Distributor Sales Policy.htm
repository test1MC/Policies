Dealer or Distributor Sales Policy

This policy is for Columbia, Duron, and MAB Paint locations only.

Exclusive Sherwin-Williams products are only available through Company owned stores.

  1. It is against Company policy to sell Sherwin-Williams exclusive products to any dealer or distributor account.
  2. Any exclusive S-W brush or roller item [Contractor Series, Proval, Premium], Purdy hand tool, S-W branded caulk or tape, or any other private label item cannot be extended to dealers or distributors for resale.
