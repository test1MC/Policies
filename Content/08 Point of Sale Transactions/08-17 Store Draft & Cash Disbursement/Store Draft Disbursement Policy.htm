Store Draft Disbursement Policy

  1. Bill To: (where purchase was made) area must be filled when processing a Store Draft Disbursement.
  2. Employees are not permitted to process their own point of sale transactions. This includes ALL point of sale transactions. Another employee must process the point of sale transaction.
  3. The Store Manager signature is required on every Store Draft Register.
  4. The disbursement amount must be coded to the proper GL Account.
  5. A receipt is required for any item purchased. The person who purchases anything for the store must sign the back of the receipt and staple the receipt to the register.
  6. The store copy of the draft must be stapled to the Store Draft Register (OLD VERSION of draft) or retained in the store draft book (NEW VERSION of draft) in either the store safe or locked filing cabinet.
  7. Store Drafts may NOT be used by the Store Manager to reimburse employees for travel expenses using TRAVEL-INSIDE PERSONNEL - 7140-002. DO NOT reimburse an employee by processing a Cash Disbursement. For more information, see 06-06 Global Corporate Travel.
  8. Store Drafts are not to be used to pay Workers Compensation invoices. If you have questions related to Workers Compensation, please call 1-800-543-1463.
  9. Anyone providing a service to Sherwin-Williams, such as window cleaning, floor cleaning and waxing should be paid by submitting the invoice on an Expense Transmittal to Stores Group Account Payable. Please include the taxpayer identification information.
  10. The store draft book must remain in the store at all times. Do not take the entire store draft book out of the store when making a purchase.
  11. An individual draft may be removed from the store draft book when making a purchase prior to processing the transaction.
  12. A note must be placed in the store draft book after removing a draft.
  13. The note must contain the draft number, date, reason for removing the draft, and your signature.
  14. The Disbursement by Store Draft transaction must be processed immediately when you return to the store. The store copy of the draft must contain the term/tran number and date.
