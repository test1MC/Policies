Cash Disbursement Policy

  1. Bill To: (where purchase was made) area must be completed.
  2. Employees are not permitted to process their own point of sale transactions. This includes ALL point of sale transactions. Another employee must process the point of sale transaction.
  3. The Store Manager signature is required for all Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement Registers. Receiving employee signature is required on the computer-generated form acknowledging receipt of the cash.
  4. The cash disbursement amount must be coded to the proper General Ledger [GL] number.
  5. The reason for each disbursement must be captured in the [comments section] after entering the disbursement amount.
  6. All disbursements made for gasoline for the store truck must include the license number and mileage of the vehicle being filled with the receipt stapled to the pink copy of the transaction.
    1. The receipt must contain the number of gallons, price per gallon and the total purchase price.
    2. For more information on personal travel and personal vehicle usage, see 06-06 Global Corporate Travel.
  7. A receipt is required for any item purchased. The person purchasing anything for the store must sign the back of the receipt before processing the disbursement.
  8. Miscellaneous Cash Disbursements may not exceed $50.00 for any one purchase without written District Manager approval on file.
  9. Anyone providing a service to Sherwin-Williams, such as window cleaning, floor cleaning and waxing, should be paid by submitting the invoice on SWeep.
  10. Meals of any type cannot be paid by processing a Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement Transaction. The manager must submit for meals on a Travel Expense Report for approval by the District Manager.
