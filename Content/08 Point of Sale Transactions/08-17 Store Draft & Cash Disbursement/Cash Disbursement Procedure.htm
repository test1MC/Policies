Cash Disbursement Procedure

Step Action

Known Amount

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to open Special Menu-> Disbursement -> Miscellaneous by Cash

2

Enter the customer or business name in the Bill To section of the transaction -> Accept [F1].

3

Select the correct GL account.

Note: For more information, see 04-02 GL Alpha Codes Alpha.

4

Enter Amount -> Press Enter.

5

Press Comment [F9] to provide a brief explanation -> Accept [F1].

6

When you have entered all lines, press Accept [F1] -> Enter manager password.

7

Staple the receipt to the Cash Disbursement Register.

Note: The Store Manager must approve the Disbursement Register. Cross-reference all documents.

Unknown Amount

1

Before leaving the store to make the purchase, process a No Sale transaction for the approximate amount.

Note: For more information, see No Sale Procedure.

2

Select Other and enter the reason for taking the money from the drawer. Also include the dollar amount at the end of the reason.

Example: Bathroom supplies from Target $20.00

3

Select Y when asked if you want to print a receipt.

4

Place the receipt in the cash drawer and remove the dollar amount noted on the No Sale receipt.

5

When you return to the store after making the purchase, process a Miscellaneous Disbursement transaction (procedure above) for the purchase amount.

Note: The Miscellaneous Disbursement transaction amount and the change must equal the original amount taken from the drawer.

6

Place the change in the cash drawer and remove the No Sale transaction receipt.

7

Staple the No Sale transaction, Miscellaneous Disbursement transaction, and purchase receipt together. Place the stapled copies in the batching bin.
