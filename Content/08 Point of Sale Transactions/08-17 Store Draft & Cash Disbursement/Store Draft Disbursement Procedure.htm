Store Draft Disbursement Procedure

Step Action

Known Amount

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to open Special Menu-> Disbursement -> Miscellaneous by Store Draft -> Enter Manager Password.

2

Enter Pay-To Info.

3

Enter the customer or business name in the Bill To section of the transaction -> Accept [F1].

4

Select the correct GL account.

Note: For more information, see 04-02 GL Alpha Codes Alpha. If the draft is for a service like window cleaning or floor cleaning and waxing, then process the payment through SWeep. For more information, see 12-01 Store Maintenance.

5

Enter Amount -> Press Enter.

6

Press Comment [F9] to provide a brief explanation -> Accept [F1].

7

Attach the store copy of the store draft and receipt to the Store Draft Register. Store Manager initials are required on the Register.

8

Retain all store copies of store drafts in the store draft book. Return the store draft book to the store safe or locked filing cabinet (the store draft will eventually only contain the store [yellow] copies). Retain the partially used store draft book in the store safe or locked filing cabinet until all drafts have been used. After using all store drafts in the store draft book, retain the draft book in the Validate Store Draft folder with the Store Draft Registers for the entire year. Store Manager initials are required on the Register.

Note: Cross-reference all documents

Unknown Amount

1

Before leaving the store to make the purchase, remove a single draft and store copy from the store draft book.

2

Insert a note in the store draft book. Provide the draft number, date, reason for removal and signature.

3

When you return to the store after making the purchase, process a Disbursement by Store Draft transaction (see procedure above).

Note: The employee who made the purchase should sign the back of the receipt.
