Validate Manual POST Form Policy

  1. Any point of sale transaction using a manual POST form must be validated through the computer system as soon as it is operating.
  2. If a manual transaction is run without selecting Enter & Validate Manual Form from Sale/Payment menu, a correction must be processed.
  3. Enter all manually-written transactions as soon as possible in the following order of priority:
    • Cash Sales
    • Cash Refunds
    • Payment on Account and Payment Refunds
    • Miscellaneous Receipts and Disbursements
    • Charge Sales - Charge Returns  
    • Inter-Store Transfers and Cancellations
    • Store Expenses Merchandise and Cancellations
    • IBARS
  4. Store employees must call for voice authorization anytime a manual POST form is used for a credit card sale.
    1. A manual credit card form must be used each time you use a manual POST form for a credit card sale. The cash drawer will not open when validating a manual POST form because it assumes that you have already received authorization for the credit card purchase.
  5. Any manual form containing the complete customer credit card number cannot be retained in the forms container. Retain these forms in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe until you re ready to batch the forms. All manual forms must be retained in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe after batching in a folder titled PROCESSED MANUAL FORMS.
