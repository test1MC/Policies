Validate Manual POST Form Procedure

When the store computer system is not operating properly or during heavy retail hours, process transactions on the manual POST form.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select Enter & Validate Manual Form from the Sale/Payment menu.

2

Enter the manual POST form number and select the type of transaction.

3

Follow the prompts to insert the form into the validation printer.

Note: Feed the paper vertically and line it up against the right hand plate. Move it forward until the form stops.

4

Validate the credit card or check.

Note: If the credit card or check is not available, leave the manual POST form in the Validation Printer for endorsements.

Note: All manual POST forms MUST be validated through the store computer system.

Note: Any manual form containing the complete customer credit card number cannot be retained in the forms container. Retain these forms in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe until you are ready to batch the forms. All manual forms must be retained in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe after batching in a folder titled PROCESSED MANUAL FORMS
