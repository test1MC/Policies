Gift Card Help

First Data Corporation is Sherwin-Williams’ vendor for Gift Card support. If the POS is unavailable or not functioning properly, contact the First Data Corporation Help Desk at 1-800-544-7456. You will be required to enter the S-W Merchant ID (97134700004) and your store number. Three options will return:

  • Gift Card Authorization (only use during downtime)
  • Balance Inquiry (use INQUIRY [F4] and select Giftcard Balance Inquiry to avoid fees associated with this option)
  • Talk to a Service Representative

The First Data Corporation Help Desk associate can assist stores with the following:

  • Freeze Gift Card Account Activity
  • Gift Card Account History
  • Gift Card Account Detail
  • Report Lost or Stolen Gift Card
  • Void Gift Card Activation
