General Paints Gift Card Sale Procedure

These instructions are for General Paint Stores only.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store ‐> Point of Sale ‐> Special Menu ‐> Misc Cash Receipt.

2

Enter the Store Number in the BillTo Name Field. Press ACCEPT [F1].

3

Select Other from the G/L Account List.

4

Select Misc Income‐Other 1639‐107 from the G/L Account List.

5

Enter the amount the customer wishes to apply to the Gift Card. Press ENTER.

6

Press COMMENT [F9] and enter General Paints Gift Card Sale as the reason for the receipt transaction and enter the Gift Card Number. Press ACCEPT [F1] twice.

7

Select Cash or Credit Card as the Form of Payment from the Form of Payment List and complete the transaction.

Note:  Checks are not permitted as a form of payment for Gift Card purchases.

8

Print a copy of the receipt for the customer.

9

Apply the funds to the Gift Card using the Chase Payment Terminal.
