General Paints Gift Card Redemption Procedure

These instructions are for General Paint Stores only.

Step Action

1

Process a sales transaction.

2

Before selecting the form of payment, ask customers how they wish to use the Gift Card:

If Then

The customer chooses to apply only a portion of the Gift Card to the purchase

Process the cash, credit card, or check payment first. The amount due can then be paid on the Gift Card.

The customer chooses to apply the balance of the Gift Card

Process the Gift Card as the first form of payment. Then, if necessary, process the cash, credit card, or check payment.

Note: The card balance will always print on the customer’s receipt.
