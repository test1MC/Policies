Inactive Gift Card Policy

In select states, customers are permitted to receive cash for the remaining amount on the Gift Card. If your state requires refunds of Gift Card amounts, you are permitted to process a Return Transaction to refund the Gift Card amount.

A. Only refund Gift Card balances if your store is located in a state requiring cash redemption, and according to the following specifications:

State Specification
California

Requires cash redemption of the full value of the Gift Card at any time upon demand.
Maine

Requires cash redemption if requested in person and the original value of the Gift Card was more than $5 and the remaining value is less than $5.
Massachusetts

Requires the option of cash redemption only when 90% of the balance has been used.
Montana

Requires the option of cash redemption if the original value of the Gift Card was more than $5 and the remaining value is less than $5.
Rhode Island

Requires the option of cash redemption when less than a $1 balance remains on the Gift Card.
Vermont

Requires the option of cash redemption  when less than a $1 balance remains on the Gift Card.
Washington

Requires the option of cash redemption in two circumstances:

If Then

The Gift Card holder makes a purchase which leaves a value on the card

The purchaser may elect to receive the balance in cash.

A balance of less than $5 is on the Gift Card

The Gift Card holder may elect to receive the balance in cash at any time (i.e. without a purchase).
