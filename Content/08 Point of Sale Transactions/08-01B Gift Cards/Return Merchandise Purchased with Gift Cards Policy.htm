Return Merchandise Purchased with Gift Cards Policy

  1. Returns involving Gift Cards as the form of payment are subject to all return policies documented in Return Transaction Policy.
  2. If the customer chooses to return merchandise that was originally purchased on a Gift Card and has their receipt, you are required to refund the purchase by activating a replacement card for the original Gift Card purchase amount. You are not permitted to choose any other type of customer refund.
  3. If multiple forms of payment were used on the original sales transaction, you are required to refund the purchase price to the original forms of payment. For example, credit card purchases must be returned to the same credit card and redeemed Gift Card purchases must be returned by activating a new Gift Card for the Gift Card purchase price.
  4. If a customer returns a portion of the merchandise purchased on one sales transaction where the original form of payment included both a Gift Card and cash or credit card:
    1. If the return is for the entirety of the original purchase, the refund must be provided in the same forms of payment as the purchase. For example, if the original purchase involved $50 Gift Card and $50 credit card, the refund must be $50 Gift Card and $50 credit card.
    2. If the return is for a partial amount of the original purchase, the refund may be granted following the instructions below:
      1. Ask the customer if they would like the refund amount placed on a Gift Card.
      2. If the customer does not want the refund amount placed on a Gift Card, the refund amount must be provided in the original form of payment (cash or credit card.) 
      3. The amount refunded to the credit card may not exceed the amount placed on the credit card in the original purchase.
