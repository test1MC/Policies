Gift Card Overview

These instructions are for Columbia Paints, General Paints, Duron Paints, MAB Paints, and SW Paint Stores

Easy to give and easy to redeem, Sherwin-Williams Gift Cards are a useful gift for new homeowners, holiday gift giving, or any occasion. Please take every opportunity to tell your customers about the availability of Gift Cards.

Gift Cards are sold and activated by processing a sales transaction. After selling the Gift Card and entering the dollar amount, the card will automatically be activated after receiving payment from the customer. The amount the Gift Card holder spends is automatically deducted from the card when the Gift Card is redeemed. Customers may check their Gift Card balance by presenting the card at any The Americas Group location in the U.S. and Puerto Rico, or by calling 1-800-544-7476.

Review all Gift Card activity at your location using INQUIRY [F4]:

  • From Store Accountability Menu (16), access the Gift Card Report.
  • To check a Gift Card status or balance for a customer, select Gift Card Balance Inquiry (38) and swipe the Gift Card or enter its number. The balance will return to the screen (customers are also able to check their balance by calling 1-800-544-7476. They will need their Gift Card number. Be aware: Sherwin-Williams is charged a fee for each inquiry and authorization).
