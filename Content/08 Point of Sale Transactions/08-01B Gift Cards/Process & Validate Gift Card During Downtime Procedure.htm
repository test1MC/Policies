Process & Validate Gift Card During Downtime Procedure

Step Action
Processing Gift Cards During Downtime

1

Complete a Manual POST Form to document the purchased merchandise.

2

Call 1-800-544-7456 to verify a Gift Card balance.

3

You will be required to enter the S-W Merchant ID [97134700004#], your store number, and the Gift Card Number.

4

You will receive a 6-digit manual authorization code.

5

Write the authorization code, Gift Card Number, and Gift Card redemption amount on the Manual POST Form.

6

If necessary, collect a balance payment from the customer.

7

Ask the customer to sign the Manual POST Form and give the customer a copy.

8

If there is no balance on the Gift Card, retain it with the Manual POST Form.
Validating Downtime Sale

9

When the system is working properly, enter the sales transaction.

10

Select Enter & Validate Manual Form from the Sale/Payment Menu.

11

Enter the Manual POST Form Number, the customer’s information, and each line item documented on the Manual POST Form.

12

Select Gift Card at the Form of Payment Menu and manually enter the authorization number.

13

Staple the customer copy of the system generated receipt to the store copy of the Manual POST Form. Retain all used store copies of the Manual POST Forms in numeric sequence. Do not place the store copy of the Manual POST Form in the monthly batching box.

Note: Gift Cards may also be sold and activated during system downtime by contacting Bank of America at 1-800-544-7456. Select the option to speak to an operator and ask for a Gift Card to be activated. Be sure to receive an authorization number from the Bank of America representative.
