Gift Card Policy

  1. The Gift Card Counter Display must be positioned on the wrap counter for easy customer access.
  2. Gift Cards are valid at any Sherwin-Williams owned paint store in the United States and Puerto Rico. Gift Cards are not available for purchase or redemption in:
    1. Automotive Branches
    2. Product Finishes Facilities
    3. Paint Stores in Canada, Virgin Islands and Mexico
  3. A sales transaction must be processed to purchase and activate Gift Cards.
    1. Gift Cards may be purchased for any amount from $5.00 to $500.00.
    2. There is no limit to the number of Gift Cards that can be sold on a sales transaction.
    3. Customers may purchase Gift Cards on the same transaction with other merchandise.
    4. Employee discounts do not apply to the purchase of Gift Cards, but does apply on product purchased with a Gift Card used as the form of payment.
    5. The forms of payment allowable when purchasing a gift card are limited.
      1. Gift Cards can be purchased by cash or credit card. Credit card purchases of Gift Cards must have the purchaser and the credit card present along with signature of purchaser.
      2. Customer check is not permitted as a form of payment for the purchase of a Gift Card.
      3. SW Charge is not permitted as a form of payment for the purchase of a Gift Card.
      4. Gift Cards may not be sold over the phone or delivered to customers.
  4. The Gift Card has no value until activated by the associate.
  5. Gift Cards are redeemable for merchandise and may not be redeemed for cash, except where required by law. 
  6. After making a purchase, the customer receipt will display the remaining balance. Purchases will be deducted from Gift Cards until the card balance reaches zero.
  7. Gift Cards can be used to make a payment on account or to pay for previously purchased merchandise.
  8. There are no service fees or expiration dates connected with the use of the Gift Card.
  9. Gift Cards with no activity for a prolonged period of time will be released as unclaimed funds in accordance with state law.
    1. Customers are entitled to use the Gift Card balance even if there has been no activity on a card for a prolonged period of time and the unclaimed funds have been turned over to the state.
  10. Lost, stolen, or damaged Gift Cards may be canceled and replaced with a new Gift Card in the amount of the then remaining balance upon presentation of a receipt as required by Sherwin-Williams policy.
  11. Gift cards cannot be reissued or reused after being activated. Returned Gift Cards should be discarded.
