Gift Card Sale Procedure

This section does not apply to General Paint Stores

Gift Cards may be sold on the same transaction as other products or multiple Gift Cards. Selling multiple Gift Cards will require multiple line items (one per Gift Card).

Step Action

1

Scan the barcode on the back of the Gift Card Presentation Holder.

Note: Be aware that Gift Cards will not be activated until the transaction is complete.

2

Press ENTER in the Sales/Product Number Field. Select Gift Card from the Non-Merchandise Menu.

3

You will be prompted to swipe the Gift Card to record the Gift Card number.

If Then

Half-moon presentation carrier

Remove the Gift Card from the presentation holder and swipe the card.

Folding presentation carrier

Fold the top of the carrier to expose the barcode and swipe the card.

Note: Do not manually enter the Gift Card number unless the MagScan Reader is broken or you are entering the sale as a downtime sale.

4

Enter the amount of the Gift Card.

5

After scanning the Gift Card, present to the customer.

Which type of Gift Card Holder do you have?

If Then

Half-moon presentation carrier

Remove the glue dots from the presentation holder and place the gift card in the diecut gift card holders.

Folding presentation carrier

Hand to the customer.

6

If multiple cards are being purchased for multiple amounts, ask the purchaser if they would like for you to record the Gift Card amount on the Gift Card holder.

7

After entering the form of payment, the system will return confirmation for the Gift Card:

8

The printed receipt will display the Gift Card balance and the Gift Card number, excluding the last four digits.
