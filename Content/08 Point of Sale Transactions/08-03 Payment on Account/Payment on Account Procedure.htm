Payment on Account Procedure

Process a Standard Payment on Account or a Discount Payment on Account.

Step Action
Standard

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select Payment on Account from the Sale/Payment Menu.

2

Follow the system prompts in the lower left corner of the screen.

3

Select or enter the correct job and enter the specific POS transaction numbers on which the customer is paying -> Enter the payment amount.

Note: If you are unable to enter specific transaction information, press CANCEL [F5] to bypass the transaction number entry. Press COMMENTS [F9] to enter a reason for not including specific information. For example, “No remittance information provided” or “Remittance information too big to enter and is attached.” 

4

Enter any notes by pressing COMMENTS [F9].

5

Give the customer a copy of the payment and place the store copy of the computer generated form in the forms container.

6

You will be prompted to enter the customer’s transaction number(s) while processing a payment on account transaction.

7

If a customer wants to give a post-dated check for a payment on account, then contact the District Credit Office for approval.

Discount

1

Press DISCOUNT [F11] after entering both the total amount of the payment and the transaction numbers referenced on the customer’s remit.

2

In the Discount Window, enter the appropriate Percent Discount for the transaction:

Code Terms

121

1% discount if payment is received within 10 days of the invoice date.

221

1% discount if payment is received within 10 days of the invoice date.

122

2% discount if payment is received within 10 days of the invoice date.

222

2% discount if payment is received within 10 days of the invoice date.

522

2%  discount if payment is received by the 15th of the month following the invoice date.

Note: These terms are not offered on any new accounts as of 2012.
