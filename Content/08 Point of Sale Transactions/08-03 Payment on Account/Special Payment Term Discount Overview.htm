Special Payment Term Discount Overview

Special payment terms are determined when an account is opened. Special payment term discounts must always be entered as a dollar discount. This discount may not exceed 2 percent for payments on accounts or payment refunds. Many customers round to the nearest dollar when calculating discounts.

Note: If a customer takes an excessive or unearned discount, contact the National Credit Center or your District Credit Office.
