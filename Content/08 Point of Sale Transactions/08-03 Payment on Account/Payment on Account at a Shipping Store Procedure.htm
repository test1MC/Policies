Payment on Account at a Shipping Store Procedure

Step Action

1

If an account payment is made at the shipping store, notify the home store and send a copy of the payment receipt.

Note: The parent (home) store is where a customer’s Account Receivable balance is kept. The shipping (visiting) store does not maintain a customer’s S-W charge, but it can process a customer’s account payment.

2

When the home store receives a copy of the payment receipt, it should update the trial balance.
