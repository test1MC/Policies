Customer Payment Information Overview

Follow these guidelines when handling customer information:

  • Do not mail in payment receipt disbursements (including close transactions) and/or customer remittance to the Cleveland Credit Center.
  • Retain all customer payment information at your store (file by transaction number).
  • When processing a payment, type invoice numbers in the [Remit] area and any other relevant information in the [Comments] section.
  • Fax customer payment information as soon as possible when it is requested by the Credit Center.

Note: Customer Payment Information is anything customers have given you to designate invoices they are paying. It could be a check stub, a statement stub, a list they compiled on a separate sheet of paper, etc. If you have any questions, call the National Credit Center.
