Customer Payment Information Retention Procedure

Step Action

1

Staple a copy of the payment on account transaction to the customer payment information and retain in a monthly folder titled Customer Payment Information.

Note: The customer payment information could include the check stub or any other information provided by the customer.

2

At the end of the month, retain the payment information with your monthly batching.

Note: You must save customer payment information at the store and it can be used to properly key payments. Properly keyed payments will result in improved collectability of customer accounts as well as reduce past due issues.

3

Do not throw away check stubs or any other information provided by the customer to support their payment on account.

4

The National Credit Center will communicate all requests for needed customer remittance information to the stores via email.

Note: Please respond promptly to email requests.
