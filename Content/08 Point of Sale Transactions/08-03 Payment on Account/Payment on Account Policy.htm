Payment on Account Policy

  1. Payments on account are made against a balance due on an established Sherwin-Williams account. Payments may be made with Cash, Check, Credit Card, Gift Card, or a combination of these.
  2. Employees are not permitted to process their own point of sale transactions. This includes ALL point of sale transactions. Another employee must process the point of sale transaction.
  3. If a payment is made in person, it MUST be entered through the store computer system before the customer leaves the store.
  4. Payments received in the mail MUST be processed on the day received.
  5. Notify the Home store if one of their customers makes a payment at your store.
  6. Record the transaction numbers being paid. If unavailable press CANCEL [F5], press the COMMENTS [F9] key to enter the reason for not including specific transaction information.
  7. If the payment is made by check, record the check number in the Check Log window during Form of Payment.
  8. Payments by check cannot be post-dated unless District Credit gives approval. Once approval is received from District Credit a Post-Dated Check form may need to be completed and signed by the customer and processing employee (check with the District Credit Office to see if the form is required). If required, the original copy of the Post-Dated Check form must be retained at the store. A copy must be forwarded to the District Credit Office.
    1. Post-Dated Check Form is located in the Forms Repository: from POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Information Library
      -> Forms Repository.
  9. You are required to retain the customer's remittance in a folder at the store. Note the customer account number, payment transaction number, and payment date on the remittance or staple a copy of the payment on account transaction to the remittance and file by date. For more information, see Customer Payment Information Overview or Customer Payment Information Policy.

Note: Customers can send payments through the mail or make payments on accounts at Sherwin-Williams stores.
