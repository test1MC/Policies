Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) Procedure

Step Action

1

Complete the Request for Electronic Funds Transfer Form found in the Forms Repository. Search using keyword "EFT" in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

Any letters or forms sent by customers regarding their request should also be faxed (fax information located on the form).

2

Store Group Credit Operations will make the banking arrangements when a customer requests to pay with an Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT).

3

Each month, the Stores Group Credit Center will process an ARJV to post electronic payments to customer accounts.

4

Call (216) 566-3409 if you have any questions about EFT payments or access the appropriate EFT Information Guide:

  • US Stores
  • Canada Stores
  • Canada Automotive & Product Finishes
