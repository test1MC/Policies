Payment on Account Discount Policy

  1. A customer must pay within the discount terms timeframe in order to take a discount.
  2. Only your Division Credit Office has the authority to approve an unearned discount. If a customer takes an excessive or unearned discount, contact the National Credit Center and/or your Division Credit Office.

Note: Customers paying invoices within a specific time period of the invoice date may be eligible for discounts. POS is able to accept up to a 2% early payment  discount. Unearned discounts will be charged back to the customer’s account.
