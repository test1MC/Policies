Miscellaneous Return Procedure

Process a Return and Miscellaneous Cash Receipt, Create a New Vendor Request, and Submit Invoice Information.

Step Action

Processing Returns

1

Follow the Return Transaction Process. From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select Return from the Sale/Payment Menu -> Enter the customer name, address, and telephone number -> Follow the system prompts to complete the return.

2

Select Cash as the Form of Refund, but do not actually give any cash to the customer.

3

Both you and the customer must sign the Return Receipt. Point out the note at the bottom of the receipt that reads, “Refund Check mailed from main office within 15 working days.”

Note: The customer does not need to remain in the store for the following steps.

Processing Miscellaneous Cash Receipts

4

The Miscellaneous Cash Receipt will allow your cash drawer to balance at the end of the day without an overage.

5

Select Point of Sale from the Main Menu. Enter your Employee Number and press ENTER.

6

Press PAGE DOWN at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu.

7

Select Misc Cash Receipt from the Special Menu.

8

Enter complete customer information when prompted, including name and address. Press ACCEPT [F1].

9

Press ENTER to view the GL Account List. Select Dist & Whse – Other, Prime Sub 7599-099. All check returns from the main office must be itemized under this entry.

10

Enter the total refund dollar amount that displayed in the Customer Return Transaction.

11

Press COMMENTS [F9] and type “Customer Refund – Main Office.” Press ACCEPT [F1].

12

Select Cash from the Form of Payment Menu.

13

Press TOTAL [F12] for the total amount.

Creating New Vendor Requests for Customers (SWeep)

14

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Source Operations -> Processes -> SWeep -> Select New Vendor Request from the SWeep homepage.

15

Enter the customer’s contact information.

16

Select Customer Refund as the Vendor Type. This will give the request the highest priority. The Vendor Request Status will display as a fire icon next to the customer’s name.

17

Enter your name in the Contact Field.Note the original sale terminal number, transaction number, date, return terminal number, transaction number, date, and return amount. If the customer does not have the original receipt, include the reason for the return in the Comments Field.

18

Submit your New Vendor Request.

Submitting Invoice Information in SWeep

19

When the customer name appears in the Vendor List, you can enter the check request information. The customer information should appear in the Vendor List within 24 hours after you have requested the New Vendor Setup.

Note: Only the Store Manager will be able to complete the Invoice Information process.

20

Submit a new invoice in SWeep to request the check to be cut for the customer.

21

Enter the Invoice Number (your Store Number) and the date of your request.

22

Select the G/L Entry Miscellaneous Sundry Exp (Prime-Sub 7599-099).

23

Submit the Invoice Request.

24

Any special handling or mailing instructions must be communicated to Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297.

25

Once all of the above information is entered into the SWeep system, the customer should receive their return check within 3-5 business days. All approved refund requests will have payments generated the next business day. If you require a quicker turn-around, contact Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297 and ask to speak with an expense correspondent.
