Create eExpense Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Log on to eAccounting.

2

Select on the eExpense tab.

3

Select the New button located on the sub-tab menu.

4

The application will confirm that you want to create a new eExpense. Select Yes.

Note: An eExpense Approval number will be automatically assigned to the document at this time.

5

Complete all applicable fields. The following information should be used when completing an eExpense for rebate approval:

  • Originating Location: Branch Number
  • Division Name: Automotive Rebate Approvals
  • Site Name/Number: Servicing Branch
  • Division: C101
  • Account: 0597313
  • Cost Center: 799900 (not your Branch Number)
  • Future, Project, and InterCompany: 0000
  • Account Amount: Projected Rebate (Annualized Amount)
  • Brief Description: Enter the Customer’s POS Account Number, Name, and Type of Discount (i.e. 6112-1111-1, ABC Body Shop, 5% Paint Discount)

6

Attach the completed Rebate Request Form to the eExpense.

7

Please add the Area Sales Manager to the Approval list for all rebate requests. The Region Director of Sales should be added for all rebates with an estimated annual rebate amount greater than $1,000.

8

After completion, you have the option of saving or submitting the eExpense. If you save the document, you can access and edit before submitting for approval.

Note: The eAccounting application times out after 15 minutes, so you must save your document if working in the application longer than 15 minutes. Otherwise your work will not be saved.

9

Once the eExpense has been submitted, a member of the Automotive Financial team will reach out to the originator of the eExpense with a completed Rebate Agreement Form.
Rebate Agreement Form

1

This document will be completed by a member of the Automotive Financial team and will be provided to the originator of the eExpense.

2

The originator can then proceed in retrieving the customer’s signature. Once the document has been signed by the customer it can be returned to the Automotive Financial team via email (automotiverebates@sherwin.com) or by fax (216-586-1917).

3

The Rebate Agreement Form must be signed by the customer and by the President or Vice President of the Automotive Division of Sherwin-Williams before a rebate can be issued to the customer.
Customer Expenses

1

Remember that all expense reimbursements, such as internet reimbursement, should not be processed as a rebate. These need to hit the back side of the P&L (expense) and should be processed in the following manner:

Complete a manual ARJV request monthly

  • Use CODE 17 (0300-003) Other – Must Specify Prime – Sub: C101-7304099-79XXXX (XXXX = Your branch number).
  • Be sure to note the month that is being reimbursed.
  • Example Explanation for Adjustment: MAR2014 DSL Reimbursement John Doe Auto Body  eEXP#XXXXXXX

Fax the completed signed form and the support (copy of the customer’s invoice) to one of the following. Fax Numbers: (216) 586-8652 Vicki Shumpert or (216) 586-8632 Janis Roberts.
