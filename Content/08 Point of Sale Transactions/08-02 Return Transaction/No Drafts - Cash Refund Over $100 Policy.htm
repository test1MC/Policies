No Drafts – Cash Refund Over $100 Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive, Chemical Coatings, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores.

  1. The No Drafts – Cash Refund Over $100 Procedure is to be used by Duron, MAB Paints, Columbia Paint and General Paint.
  2. Cash refunds are available for cash and check purchases under $100.
  3. Headquarters Checks must be used for any cash refund over $100. 
  4. Headquarters Checks may also be used if the cash refund would deplete the operating fund. 
  5. Checks will be mailed from Headquarters within fifteen business days. 

If the customer also wishes to purchase product at the time of the return, it is permissible to complete a Correction Transaction and apply the refund towards the new purchase. This may eliminate the need for a check to be cut from HQ.
