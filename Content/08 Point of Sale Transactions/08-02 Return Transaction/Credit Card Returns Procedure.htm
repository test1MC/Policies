Credit Card Returns Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Select Return from the Sale/Payment Menu -> Enter the customer name, address, and telephone number. 

2

Follow the system prompts to complete the return. Select Credit Card at the Form of Payment window. Either swipe the card or enter its number and follow the prompts.

3

Sign the invoice and provide a customer copy. Put the store copy in the Store Computer System forms container for the Store Manager’s approval. All processed manual forms, including both imprinted manual credit card forms and processed manual POST forms containing the complete customer credit card number cannot be retained in the forms container. Retain these forms in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe until you are ready to batch forms. All completed manual forms must be retained in a folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS – USED and locked in a filing cabinet or locked store safe.
