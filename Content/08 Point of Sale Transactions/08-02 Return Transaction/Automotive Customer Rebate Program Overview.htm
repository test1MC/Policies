Automotive Customer Rebate Program Overview

This section does not apply to Chemical Coatings Facilities, Duron Stores, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores.

The Automotive Customer Rebate program is designed as an incentive to secure business and to generate a return for the division. Customers currently under a Supply Agreement are not eligible for additional discounts outside of the agreement. If the rebate has already been approved via a Supply Agreement, a separate eExpense will not be required.
