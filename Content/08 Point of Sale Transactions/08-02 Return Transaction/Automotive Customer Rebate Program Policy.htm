Automotive Customer Rebate Program Policy

This section does not apply to Chemical Coatings Facilities, Duron Stores, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores.

  1. Rebates can be offered on a quarterly, semiannual, or annual basis.
  2. Rebates may only be offered based on a percentage to sales of automotive paints and coatings manufactured and sold by Sherwin-Williams under the “Sherwin-Williams” label.
  3. Jobbers may qualify for a fleet C, G, or M rebate. These rebates cannot be calculated at headquarters. Once the customer has an approved eExpense on file, the branch will be required to supply the following information by sending an email to automotiverebates@sherwin.com or fax to 216-586-1917.
    1. Account Name/Number
    2. Amount of eligible sales used to calculate the rebate
    3. Percentage used to calculate the rebate
    4. Note: all rebate information is due by the 10th business day following the close of the rebate period
  4. Only one eExpense will need to be processed for each rebate agreement. However, if the original terms of the agreement have been altered or the actual annual rebate significantly exceeds the estimate, the Automotive Financial team may request that a new eExpense be completed.
