Cash Refund Verification Procedure

Step

Action

1

Ensure that the customer name, address, city, state, zip code, and phone number are complete and accurate (no abbreviations) anytime the customer receives (cash or credit card) card credit or a store draft on a Cash Refund or Cash Correction Transaction.

2

Have the customer sign the Cash Refund or Cash Correction Transaction prior to refunding the money.

3

Report any customer response to the Cash Refund Verification Letter to the District Manager. Forward any Cash Refund Verification letters to the Division office.
