Charge Returns Policy

  1. The customer should have proof of purchase. If the customer DOES NOT have a copy of the invoice, recall the invoice and review it on the screen. This procedure can be performed for any charge purchase from your store.
  2. If no receipt is available, Store Manager approval is required before processing the return.
