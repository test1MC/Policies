Lump Sum Sales Correction Policy

  1. Customer product (gallon) history is distorted when Class Numbers are used to make corrections. Therefore, do not use Class Numbers to make Lump Sum Corrections.
  2. A Correction transaction must be processed to correct each incorrect transaction.
  3. Do not "lump" products together to make one correction.
  4. Do not use sales number 9999-9999-8.
