Correction Transaction Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.

2

Enter to the Sale/Payment Menu.

3

Press Page Down to access the Special Menu.

4

Select Correction.

5

Press Cancel [F5] when prompted to select a Customer Order if no order is available.

6

Scan the barcode at the bottom of the receipt when prompted to enter the Original Terminal Number.

7

Enter the customer information, if needed.

Note: Scanning the barcode on the receipt will only bring back the original terminal, transaction number, and date if the original purchase was not made at that store.

8

Process the correction:

If Then

Business-to-Business

Change the Bill-To information to reflect the customer who is picking up the merchandise to ensure the Cash Refund Verification Letters are sent to the purchaser rather than the main office of the account.

Apply a Customer Deposit

Process the Correction transaction using the original Terminal/Transaction number for the Deposit. After entering the Customer Deposit on the Negative side of the transaction, enter the customer's purchase on the Positive side of the transaction.

Note: See 07-03A Customer Order Entry for more information.

Changing Sales Credit

Contact your District ManCorrection Transaction Procedure to discuss any territory sales corrections.
