Inter Store Transfer (IST) Overview

An ISR is used when a store has an immediate need for goods and cannot wait for fulfillment from the DSC.  This process allows you to acquire merchandise from other stores.  The requesting store will generate a Stock Transfer Order and the sending store processes an Inter Store Transfer (IST) using the receiving store’s Order number. You should contact the receiving store to confirm that stock is available.
