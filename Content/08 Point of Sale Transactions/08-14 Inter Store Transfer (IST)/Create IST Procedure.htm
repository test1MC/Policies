Create IST Procedure

Step Action

1

Carefully review the purchase order. Look for any Special Instructions noted by the requesting store. Complete the Ship To section if the merchandise is to be picked up by or shipped directly to the customer.

Note:  If the IST is to be delivered to a customer site rather than the requesting store, the address will appear in Special Instructions.

2

Using the purchase order as a guide, enter the product sales number and quantities in the detail section:

  • GOM merchandise and DSC-stocked vendor merchandise will be transferred to the receiving store at current weighted average cost. Enter the cost only if it is different from the current store cost.
  • If the merchandise being transferred was originally purchased direct from the vendor, enter the agreed upon transfer cost.
  • For any hard equipment that is transferred the serial and model numbers must be entered when prompted or by pressing COMMENT [F9].

3

Select IST from the Special Menu.

4

Select Yes and press Enter to the prompt “Select a Transfer Order?”

5

Select the order and products to transfer from the All Active Transfer Order Menu.

6

The Receiving Store and ISR Number will automatically populate. Press ACCEPT [F1].

7

The products to be transferred will appear in the window. Press ACCEPT [F1].

8

Select Y to begin Bill of Lading paperwork. Follow the prompts to create a BOL document using BOLDOC.

9

The customer or employee collecting the merchandise must sign the IST slip.

10

Make shipping arrangements as follows:

If Then

Stores that do not use the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service

There are three options:

  • Send the shipment collect (if the transfer was requested by the store receiving the merchandise).
  • Send the shipment prepaid (if the transfer was due to an overstock). Issue a payment draft (3861-001) for the freight charge. Automotive Branches must contact Accounting for an approval number before issuing the draft for the freight charge.
  • Ship according to whatever shipping arrangements (including charges) were agreed upon.

Stores that use the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service

The store creating the IST should submit the order for delivery using Customer Order Entry. In COE, select the option to submit an order for delivery (IST’s will appear as orders). Select Submit Delivery Order Request. Follow the standard procedure for submitting an order for delivery.

Note: If the requesting store has noticed that the order should be shipped direct to the customer, then override the delivery address when submitting the order. Ensure that the address is formatted correctly.
