Inter Store Transfer Policy

  1. Merchandise may NOT be transferred between stores without the agreement of both participating Store Managers or specific instructions from the District Manager.
  2. When transferring sales numbered GOM merchandise or DSC-stocked vendor merchandise, enter the cost as “0.00.”
  3. While transferring merchandise purchased direct from the vendor, you are required to enter the agreed upon transfer cost. Do NOT enter “0.00” as the transfer cost.
  4. The computer-generated form MUST be signed by the customer or employee.
  5. All Inter Store Transfers must be processed through the computer system on the day the merchandise is shipped.
  6. The Bill-of-Lading for an IST must be created by the store transferring the merchandise to the store creating the IST.
  7. For stores participating in the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service:
    1. Requesting stores are NOT to submit ISTs for delivery. The store creating the IST MUST submit the order for delivery.
    2. Requesting stores will assume the delivery costs for ISTs.
