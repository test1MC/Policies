Transfers to Non-Store Locations Overview

An Inventory Billing Adjustment Report (IBAR) is used to process a transfer to a non-store location. Non-store locations include Districts, Divisions, Corporate, Stores Group Headquarters, etc. For more information, see Transfer to Non-Store in Store-Generated IBAR Overview ,

Note: Process an IST when transferring merchandise between all store locations, including Automotive Branches, Chemical Coatings Facilities, and The Americas Group Stores.
