Invoice Correction Overview

These instructions are for Columbia Paints, Duron Paints, MAB Paints, and S-W Paint Stores

If an invoice has been rung in err on an incorrect job number, the invoice must be moved to the correct job in POS. By following these steps, the invoices will not re-age, the customer will not be re-billed, the rep will receive proper credit and budgeting for next year’s sales, and the company’s lien rights will be protected.
