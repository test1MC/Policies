Materials Acceptance Agreement – Purchase Procedure

Step

Action

1

Send your District Manager an email asking for approval to sell the less-than-perfect merchandise at a reduced cost.

2

Complete the Materials Acceptance Agreement - Purchase (available in the Forms Repository) for any less-than-perfect merchandise that will be sold at a reduced selling price.

3

Have the Acceptor [customer] sign the agreement.

4

Retain the signed agreement and District Manager email response approval for 10 years in a folder titled [Materials Acceptance Agreement].

5

Mail a signed copy of the agreement to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
ATTN: Sam Skubak
Corporate Legal
101 Prospect Avenue
Cleveland, Ohio 44115
6

Process the COE in POS

Note: For mistinted merchandise, see Mistints Policy.
