Email Receipts Overview

This section does not apply to the Caribbean and Quebec.

To better serve our customers and to reduce wasted paper, email receipts can now be sent to Preferred Customers and Employees with an Email Address on File when processing sales transactions. In addition, electronic receipts can be re-issued via email using Inquiry Menu Procedure.

Depending on the customer’s status and email info on file, email receipt options vary:

If Then

The customer has already set up a PC account with an email address

The system will retrieve the customer’s email address when the Customer Information header is validated.

The customer has newly added a PC or Employee account or email address on file

  • Process the transaction.

  • After processing the transaction, use Email Receipts Procedure.
