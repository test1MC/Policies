Invoice Receipt & Printing Procedure

Step

Action

1

Select [Prefers Laser] on the customer's Price Record Card.

Note: Laser printed receipts should be the exception. Please DO NOT change to a laser printed invoice unless asked by the customer.

2

Designate if the customer requires a Packing Slip and Additional Copy on the Price Record Card.
3

Retain the laser printed customer invoice copies in a folder titled MAIL TO CUSTOMER until you are able to mail.

Contact the Home Store to make changes to the customer's Price Record Card, if required.
