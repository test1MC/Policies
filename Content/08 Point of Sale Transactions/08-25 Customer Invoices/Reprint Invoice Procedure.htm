Reprint Invoice Procedure

Step Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Press INQUIRY [F4].

2

Select Generalized Inquiry.

3

Enter Inquiry information.

4

Press Accept [F1].

5

Select one of the following:

  • View on the Screen
  • Send to Laster Printer Immediately
  • Send to Receipt Printer

Note: If customer requires mailed invoice, print and retain in folder titled Mail To Customer until you are able to mail.
