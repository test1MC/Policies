Color Eye Repair Procedure

This applies to Automotive Branches Only.

Step Action
1 From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Add New Purchase Order.

2

Create the purchase order for X-Rite Vendor, vendor #0027208436. Below is the list of products and SMIS numbers to be used:

Note: Charger does not come with cord, but if they have the cord from the old charger, it will work. If not, a cord must be ordered separately. Ceramic calibration tile replacement requires the instrument be returned for service. Because each tile is calibrated with a particular instrument, the instrument must be sent to service center (X-Rite) to replace new calibration tile with that instrument.

3

Call 1-800-SW-ULTRA/Customer Service with the purchase order number and the serial number from the Color Eye.  Customer Service will start the paperwork with X-Rite and obtain an RMA number for the Branch. Please follow directions given to you by Customer Service on how to return the product for repair (the Color Eye must be returned with all accessories in the Color Eye case). Due to the fact that repair prices vary, Customer Service will contact the Branch to advise the repair cost/estimate once determined by X-Rite. At this point, the Branch should update the purchase order with the quoted amount.

4

Receive and close the purchase order/repair order  after the equipment is received.

Note: Repaired units are under a limited warranty. The Branch should verify that the unit is fully operational. Contact Customer Service with any warranty repair. The invoice will be sent to Customer Service from X-Rite. In the event the Branch is sent an invoice DO NOT pay the invoice locally. Customer Service will work with Payables to process the invoice.
5 From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down key to access Special Menu -> Store Expense.

6

Process the Store Expense coding the expense to 7400-894, to move the dollars out of inventory. The Branch will need to use the sales numbers, quantity, and costs contained on the purchase order to process the Store Expense.  If this repair is for a customer’s unit and they are being billed, please process through POS.

Note: If ordering a new Color Eye, please contact Customer Service at 1(800) SW-ULTRA (select option #2).
