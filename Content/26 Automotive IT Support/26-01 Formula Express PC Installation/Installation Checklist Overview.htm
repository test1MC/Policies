Installation Checklist Overview

After attaching all the cables and logging on to the computer, you should be able to do the following:

  • Open Sherwin-Automotive.com and log in to Formula Express using your existing ID and password.
  • Print a label on the Zebra Printer (verify orientation and size are correct).
  • Print to wireless Lexmark MX310 Printer.
  • Fax from wireless Lexmark MX310 Printer.
  • Scan to email from wireless Lexmark MX310 Printer.
  • Test internet Scale functionality (if connected).
  • Create and open Microsoft Office files (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint).

Note: The Cisco Wireless Access point makes it possible for anybody with a Sherwin-Williams' issued laptop to print to the Lexmark MX310 wireless printer. In order to do so, SW personnel will need to install the Lexmark MX310 on their laptop. Separate instructions will be provided to assist SW personnel with the installation of the printer.

RECYCLE: Once you are comfortable with your new PC setup, you can use the two boxes that the equipment was sent in to return your old Formula Express computer, fax printer, monitor, mouse, and keyboard equipment for recycling. A return label should have been included. If the box is not big enough for the equipment that must be recycled, please contact the POS Help Desk at 1-216-566-2740 to arrange additional boxes
