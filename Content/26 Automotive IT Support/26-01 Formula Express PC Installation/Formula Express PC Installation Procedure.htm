Formula Express PC Installation Procedure

Note: A Phillips screwdriver will be required for installation

Step

Action

1

Insert the monitor stand into the base and rotate the stand to secure the (3) screws in the keyhole slots. Tighten the thumb screw and (3) Phillips head screws to secure the base to the stand. Attach the stand to the 22" monitor as illustrated in the manufacturer's instructions provided.

2

Assemble the Lenovo M93 mounting bracket on to the monitor stand. You will need to loosen the Phillips head screw holding the strap in place, remove the strap and reinsert in order to attach the bracket to the monitor stand. Insert the strap and tighten the Phillips head screw. The installed bracket should appear as pictured.

3

Slide the mounting bracket on to the Tiny M93 computer, install and secure the screws provided, insert the small rubber grommets and attach the power adapter holder to the mounting bracket as illustrated in the manufacturer's instructions provided. Hang the bracket and M93 Tiny computer on the monitor stand.

4

Insert the power adapter into the power adapter bracket on the M93.
