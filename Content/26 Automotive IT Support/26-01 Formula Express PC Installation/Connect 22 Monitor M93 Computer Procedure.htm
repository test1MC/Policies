Connect 22” Monitor & M93 Computer Procedure

Step

Action

1

Attach the mouse to the yellow USB input on the front of the M93.

2

Attach the USB to monitor cable to the blue USB port on the front  of the M93 and plug the cable into the corresponding input on the bottom of the 22" monitor.

3

Insert the power cord in the bottom of the 22" monitor.

4

Insert the Display Port Monitor Cable into the bottom of the 22" monitor.

5

Attach the Display Port Monitor Cable to the back of the M93 computer.

6

Attach the power adapter to the M93.

7

Attach the Zebra printer to the USB input on the back of the M93 using the Tripp-Lite USB cable provided 

Note: This replaces the cable currently connecting the Zebra printer.

8

Attach the keyboard to USB input on the back of the M93.

9

Attach the network cable to the back of the M93.

10

Attach the Digitus USB to serial Adapter provided with the Metler Toledo scale and barrier box cable and connect the short extension cable provided with the Digitus USB adapter to the USB port on the back of the M93.

11

Attach the wireless antenna provided with the M93.

12

Plug the power cord from monitor and M93 computer into an electrical outlet.

13

If you run into a problem with any of these steps, please call the Global IT Service Desk at 1-800-566-2997.

14

The wireless Lexmark MX310 Printer provided replaces the existing Branch fax/copier/printer. Unbox the printer, connect the power cord, connect the fax line, power up the printer and it should be good to go. In addition to being a fax machine and copier, this printer is now connected directly to the new M93 computer for printing (select the printer named Lexmark).  You also now have the ability to "scan to email", sending the file directly to the SW9xxx@sherwin.com email account. If you run into a problem  with this step please call Global IT Service Desk at 1-800-566-2997.

15

A Cisco Wireless Access Point should already have been sent to the Branch and installed at this time. The wireless access point is required for printing and scan to email on the Lexmark MX310. It should be installed close to the Cisco 300 switch and it should be plugged into Port#13.
