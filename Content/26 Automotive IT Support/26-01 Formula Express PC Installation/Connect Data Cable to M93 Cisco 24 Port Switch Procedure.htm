Connect Data Cable to M93 & Cisco 24 Port Switch Procedure

Step

Action

1

Before unplugging or removing the old Formula Express PC, you will need to identify the port on the Cisco 300 switch to which the old Formula Express PC is connected.

Note: The Cisco 300 switch is located in the wall mounted POS rack or cabinet. Refer to the CISCO 300 24-Port Managed Switch diagram provided for additional detail on this step.

Note: This step requires two people.

2

One person will have to disconnect the network cable from the back of the old Formula Express PC while the other person stands by the Cisco 300 switch. The person at the Cisco 300 switch will need to watch the LED lights on ports 3 through 12 to determine which data cable is connected to the Formula Express PC. When the Formula Express PC data cord is unplugged the light on the port to which the Formula Express PC is connected will go out.

3

Once you determine what port the old Formula Express PC is connected to, unplug the data cable from the Cisco 300 switch and reconnect it to Port #14, which is located on the bottom row of the Cisco 300 switch.
