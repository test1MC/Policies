Store Reports Help

FAQ Answer

Can everyone in the store access the reports?

You will have access to the Store Reports if you are currently the Store Manager, Assistant Manager or Operations Manager at your location. Access to Store Reports is based on the employee number and employee password entered after selecting the BROWSER button at the bottom of the POS desktop.

Can the Auditor, District Manager and Credit Manager access Store Reports when they are in the store?

Yes, they will use their special passwords to gain access to Store Reports on the SOURCE Intranet.

Can I save the report to my system?

We do not recommend saving reports to your system. These Store Reports will be available for at least two years if you need to access the report again (the two-year retention is the same retention period for your current printed reports).

Do I have to retain a printed copy of these reports in the filing cabinet?
  • There is no need to save a printed copy of the reports unless instructed by your District Manager or outlined in the Store Management Policies and Procedures manual.
  • If you are responsible for making collection calls please remember to print the Trial Balance and Open Item Listing.

Note: You are required to follow current policies and procedures related to approving and verifying store reports. For example, if you previously were required to retain an approved and verified copy of a report you will continue this process by printing the report from the Store Reports area of the SOURCE Intranet. Approve, verify, and retain the printed report as outlined in the Store Management Policies and Procedures manual.

How current are these reports going to be?

New reports will be available in Store Reports throughout the month. These reports will be available once processed. You no longer need to wait on the weekly mailing from Headquarters.

Will I be notified that new reports are available on the SOURCE Intranet?

No, once or twice a week check the Store Reports area on the SOURCE Intranet for any new reports.

Can I copy the information from one of these reports into a document?

No, you will be able to view and print any of the Store Reports for up to 2 years.

How can I print a report?

Print the Store Report by selecting the small Printer Icon at the top left of the Adobe Acrobat window.

Who should I contact if I have questions about the reports?
  • Contact your District Manager if you have questions about using or reading one of the Store Reports.
  • Contact Store Computer Operations at 216-566-2740 if you have a problem displaying or retrieving one of your Store Reports.
  • For more information on Discrepancy Reports, refer to Discrepancy Report Overview.

Do I have to keep the new and old reports in the filing cabinet now that the reports are available on SOURCE?

Refer to Filing & Retention Batch Box Overview for more information.
