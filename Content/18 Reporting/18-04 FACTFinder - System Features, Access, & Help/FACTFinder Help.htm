FACTFinder Help

Report issues or make suggestion for FACTFinder using the Problems/Feedback Button on the FACTFinder home. All feedback is handled directly by the FACTFinder Team.

Step Action

1

Select the Problems/Feedback Button.

2

Select your Feedback Type.

3

Compose your question or feedback and send.

4

Write down the Feedback ID for your question. Use this when contacting the FACTFinder Team with further questions.
