FACTFinder Policy

Stores are required to use FACTFinder according to the policy below:

  1. The following FACTFinder reports require review on the schedule noted below:
    1. P&L Report: Monthly
    2. Fixed Assets Report: Review twice a year. 
      1. Fixed Assets should be reviewed in the summer when contacted by Accounting to review and retire assets. 
      2. Fixed Assets also require review in November so that corrections and retirements may be processed before year-end.

Review of FACTFinder reports is to be documented using the FACTFinder Report Level Note Function for the report; do not document on an individual report line. The date reviewed and name of the employee reviewing will automatically be stored with the note.
