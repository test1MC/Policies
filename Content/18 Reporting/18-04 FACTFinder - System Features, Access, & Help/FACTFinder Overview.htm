FACTFinder Overview

FACTFinder allows Store Managers, City Managers, and District Managers to access financial reports and manage expenses. FACTFinder is used to view, annotate, and communicate with others regarding P&L (including P&L Analysis) and Store Billing information. Drill down into the account analysis from any line in the report or get journal entry data for any account. District Managers and City Managers have the ability to run a rollup P&L for all of their stores and will see a single line summarizing activity for the entire district. Drill down from that line and see that single charge broken down by each store.
