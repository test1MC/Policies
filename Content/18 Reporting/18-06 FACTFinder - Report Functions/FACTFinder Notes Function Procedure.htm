FACTFinder Notes Function Procedure

The Notes function allows users to make notes and communicate to other FACTFinder users on any line in any report.  Notes offer the most clear communication method regarding these reports as the recipient is able to directly link to the report line which is in question and both questions and answers are stored together within the report and may be viewed by anyone with access to the report. Questions may easily be managed from FACTFinder Home where current status is easily reviewed. Users may:

  • Add Notes - Notes cannot be edited once saved but new notes can always be added.
  • Send Questions - Email message will be sent to selected FACTFinder users along with a link back to the report line in question.

You will see three note icons in FACTFinder: 

Notes have been added to the report line for the currently selected accounting period. 

Notes have been added to the report line for a different accounting period. 

No notes exist for this repot line. Select icon to add notes.

Send Question

1

Select Send Question in the bottom toolbar.

2

Search for the person to whom you wish to send the question. Check the default store management listing for your contact and select (you should see entries for the Assistant Store Manager, Store Manager, and District Manager).

Note: Depending on the report you’ve selected and the line which you are viewing, you may have additional users to whom you may address your question. The following groups are currently accepting messages from FACTFinder: TAG Accounting, TAG Bookkeeping, Fixed Assets Accounting, Fleet Management, Real Estate, and Audit.

3

Compose your question.

4

Do you want to include an attachment?

If Then
Yes Browse for and select your attachment.
No Proceed to Step 5.

5

Your question will display in the notes window and an email notification will be generated to the recipient. The recipient of your question will be able to: Reply (to you), Forward (to another user). Prior to the recipient replying to your question, you will be able to close your note if you find the answer, or you may send a reminder to the recipient to respond to your note.

Manage Notes from Home Tab

1

Access the Home Tab on FACTFinder.

2

Select which type of note(s) to view:

  • To Me
  • From Me

3

Select the status of the note(s) to view:

  • Open
  • Closed

4

Note chains matching the selected criteria will display. Select to view the notes or the report line which they reference.

Note:  The following functions may be used while on the Notes screen: Search, Print Report, Print Notes, Export (pdf or xls or csv are recommended), and Refresh.
