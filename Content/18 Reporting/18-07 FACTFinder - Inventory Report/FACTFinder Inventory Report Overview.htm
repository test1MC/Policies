FACTFinder Inventory Report Overview

The Inventory Report allows stores to review inventory levels, sales information, performance, and products that were part of the 4-Month write-off process. In addition to reviewing this information, stores can use the Inventory Report to view the Plan-O-Gram items in order to identify slow moving, excessive, negative inventory issues, or any products with an on-hand quantity of zero. A store can only view another store’s inventory on one sales product at a time when analyzing their product needs.

Note: The report automatically updates (the store perpetual is updated as transactions occur). When making a perpetual adjustment, the only time the DM adjustment flag should be marked Y is when the adjustment related to a Pass 1 or Pass 2 adjustment is in SWeep. Otherwise, this will cause Cleveland’s perpetual to be incorrect.
