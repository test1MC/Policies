FACTFinder Inventory Report Summary Overview

The Inventory Report defaults to a summary view, which provides a general overview of a store’s inventory levels and allows users to quickly review the information by Product Department. Select on the Department to view more specific, product details.

Column Headings

Description

Qty

This column displays total perpetual quantities as of the end of the previous business day.

Ext Cost

This column displays total cost/value of perpetual inventory on hand.

Units Sold

This column displays total units sold over the last 12 months.

Sales

This column displays total sales over the last 12 months.

POS Mgn

This column displays total margin dollars over the last 12 months.

Avg Units in Inv

This column displays the average perpetual inventory units on hand (as of the end of the month) over the last 12 months.

Avg Inv Turns

This column displays the average amount that inventory is completely sold during a 12 month period.

Avg Days in Inv

This column displays the average amount of days it would take to completely sell through inventory just one time.

Note: Every blue, underlined number or word can be selected on for more specific information and every product has a Notes icon to ask a question or to make a comment. Export the report to a PDF or Excel to print it.
