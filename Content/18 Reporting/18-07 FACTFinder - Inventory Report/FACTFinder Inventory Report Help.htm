FACTFinder Inventory Report Help

When reviewing the Inventory Report on FACTFinder, store managers should:

  • Review Plan-O-Gram products with zero quantity Monthly
  • Review Negative Quantity weekly during Cycle Counts and reconcile through a correcting POS transaction or inventory adjustment.
  • Identify Excess Inventory (use Month Supply > 10)
  • Identify Slow Moving Products (use No Sales – 12 Months)
  • Identify Written Off Products
  • Identify ways to reduce Discontinued products
  • Identify ways to reduce Slow Moving/ Excess Inventory
  • Tie the Write Off dollar amount to the 4-Month Writeoff Report (IMS-OB STR)
