Discrepancy Report Overview

The Discrepancy Report is being replaced by the Notes/Questions feature in FACTFinder where stores can send a question or comment to the appropriate group in order to correct expense errors on the Stores Purchases Report. The Notes/Questions feature permits questions for both External and Internal Purchases. The expense errors could be:

  • The store was charged for an expense invoice in error.
  • An expense invoice is charged to the wrong Prime-Sub Account.
  • The wrong amount was charged.
  • The store was charged for the same expense merchandise twice.

Note: IST transactions billed for an incorrect quantity, amount, etc. should be corrected with an IST cancel transaction, followed by a subsequent IST transaction to bill the items correctly. Expense transactions that were processed for an incorrect quantity or to an incorrect prime sub should be corrected with an Expense Cancel transaction, followed by a subsequent Expense transaction to expense the items correctly.
