Stores Purchases Report (SPR) Policy

All internal and external purchases are recapped on the Stores Purchases Report. The Stores Purchases Report lists all purchases charged to your location and enables you to quickly and accurately identify all purchases.

  1. A member of store management is required to review all items on the Stores Purchases Report monthly and sign off via FACTFinder.
    1. Store managers responsible for Terminal Cost Centers must review and sign off on the Stores Purchases Report for the center as well as the store.
    2. Any line, either internal or external, above $10,000 must be individually checked off as reviewed, or have a comment that provides a reason why an item is not marked for review.
  2. Each accounting period must be reviewed by the following month.
    1. For example: Accounting period 201512 (Dec. 2015) must be reviewed by Jan. 31st 2016.
  3. Stores no longer need to print and retain a physical copy of the report.
