Store Lighting Policy

  1. Store lighting must be maintained in good working order, while burned-out lamps should be replaced immediately (use same lamp watt, temperature and color rendering as found in other fixtures in the same space). 
  2. Following federal and local regulations, possible and/or required lamps and ballasts are to be disposed and recycled accordingly. Please review Disposal of Spent Fluorescent Lamp & Ballasts for more information on disposal.

Note: Do not be fooled by questionable lamp-selling organizations preying on store personnel. They present themselves as existing vendors and will attempt to sell lamps to the local management at many times the fair market value of their products. It is best to purchase from the organizations found in this document or from a local trusted source.
