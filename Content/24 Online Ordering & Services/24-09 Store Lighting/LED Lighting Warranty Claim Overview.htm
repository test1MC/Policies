LED Lighting Warranty Claim Overview

This information applies to TAG U.S. Stores Only

All U.S. stores are going through a LED overhead lighting retrofit due to a contract with Illumetek. Lamps in your light fixtures are covered by a Manufacturer’s limited material warranty from USHIO for a period of (7) years from the date of installation. A warranty card will be provided upon completion. If you experience any lighting outages with the new LED lights, please call Illumetek at 1-800-644-2566 or send a request for lighting repairs to sherwinwilliams@illumetek.com.  You may also call SW Stores Services at 877-470-4615 or email stores.services@sherwin.com for assistance with your facility lighting.

Note: Illumetek is responsible for replacing the defective tube within the first (12) months. Beyond that, the Sherwin-Williams store is responsible for installation. 

Important Safety Information: The linear light fixtures at your store have been modified. These fixtures no longer operate fluorescent lamps. Use only ballast-bypass LED lamps for replacement.
