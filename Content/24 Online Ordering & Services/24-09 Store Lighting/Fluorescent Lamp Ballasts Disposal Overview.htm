Fluorescent Lamp & Ballasts Disposal Overview

Many states and/or municipalities consider spent lamps and ballasts hazardous waste. Since laws vary based on the make-up of the lamps and ballasts, though it may be difficult to discern whether a given lamp or ballasts is hazardous, it is best to recycle all lamps and ballasts in most cases. Sherwin’s partnership with Republic Services serves to aid you in this process.

Republic Services offer lined boxes for recycling lamps and plastic buckets for ballasts. There is no additional cost associated with the process once ordered and invoiced. Mail Back Kits include: Labeling, packaging, instructions, to/from shipping, recycling fees, and certificate of recycling.

Please print the Republic Services information. A recycling kit can be ordered via phone: 1-800-840-5409.
