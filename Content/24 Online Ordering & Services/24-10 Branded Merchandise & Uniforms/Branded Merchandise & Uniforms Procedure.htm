Branded Merchandise & Uniforms Procedure

Step Action
Placing

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access SOURCE Homepage -> SOURCE Operations -> Order -> Branded Merchandise & Uniform Program.

2

Under Employee Uniforms, select Order UNIFORMS.

3

Logon using your mySherwin credentials.

4

Use the site to browse for and select your shirts. 

5

Once selected, you’ll need to customize your product options:

  • Logo
  • Color
  • Size
  • Spanish Language Ability
  • Employee Name

6

Update your Quantity.  Select Add to Cart.

Note:  You need to confirm your product options before you can add to cart.

7

Once you have made all your selections, select Checkout.

8

Enter your Store Number.  Select Continue.

9

Enter your store address as the Shipping Address.  Select Next Step.

Note: Select the box to save your address information.

10

Under Payment, select the same as shipping address box for the Billing Address.

Note:  Select the box to save your address information.

11

Select Shipping Method and select Continue.

12

Under Billing Method, enter your store cost center.  Select Continue.

Note: The format is A10070####.

13

Review your order to make sure that all your information is correct. Select Place Order Now.

Receiving

14

Once order is received, compare original emailed Order Confirmation with Packing Slip/items received.

If Then

There is a discrepancy

Call ProForma at 1-888-521-0199 to discuss the issue and, if applicable, ProForma should issue a credit to Accounts Payable.

There is no discrepancy

Proceed to Step 15.

Note: Save Order Confirmation/Packing Slip to reconcile P&L.

Note: ProForma will mail the invoice directly to Accounts Payable:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
101 Prospect Ave. NW
15 Guildhall attn: Mike Henik
Cleveland, OH 44101

Then, Accounts Payable will pay the invoice and charge the respective cost center. 

15

Compare order confirmation amount to P&L entry.

If Then

There is a discrepancy or charges need to be charged to a different account

Complete a Discrepancy Report and send to Accounts Payable. Then, contact A/P after charge appears on P&L and a JV transfer entry can be implemented. 

Note: Admins should send changes to the Division Analyst for a JV entry.

There is no discrepancy

Procedure is complete.
