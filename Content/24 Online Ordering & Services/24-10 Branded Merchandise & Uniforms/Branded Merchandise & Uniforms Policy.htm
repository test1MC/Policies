Branded Merchandise & Uniforms Policy

  1. All orders for branded merchandise and uniforms should be placed with ProForma, our national selected supplier and approved licensee of Sherwin-Williams logos, via the branded merchandise and uniform websites accessible on SOURCE or SherLink.
  2. All employees must adhere to the Human Resource Uniform Policy when purchasing uniforms. For more information see Human Resource Uniform Policy.
  3. Store Managers will be responsible for ordering uniform shirts for the employees in their store.
  4. A yearly branded merchandise budget is assigned to each store and each sales representative for business use purchases only. The budget is monitored and controlled by each Division Controller.
  5. All shirts must be embroidered with the employee’s first name. If the employee speaks Spanish, the shirts can be embroidered with “Yo hablo español” underneath the employee’s name.  
  6. Any purchase request exceeding the pre-established budget limit will be automatically forwarded to the District Office for approval or denial.  The purchaser will be notified via email when District approves/denies the purchase request. 
    1. Business Use purchases are required to be charged to the purchaser's cost center.
    2. Personal purchases (for non-business use) require a personal credit card as the only form of payment (Cost Centers cannot be charged for personal purchases).
  7. Employees may purchase appropriate pants at their own expense from a vendor of their choice, or they may purchase khaki pants at their own expense by using the “Branded Merchandise – Personal Use” option.
