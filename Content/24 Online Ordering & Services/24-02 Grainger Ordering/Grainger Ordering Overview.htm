Grainger Ordering Overview

This office supply ordering process is for all U.S. Stores and Product Finishes locations.

Grainger is a preferred vendor for most of you store’s day-to-day business needs. Items identified with a red SWP are contracted items. These products have negotiated pricing specifically for Sherwin-Williams and represent a best value for your store. Please order these items whenever possible. Examples of products include:

  • First aid and safety supplies
  • Spill kits
  • Signs
  • Store van supplies

Online ordering is the only method of placing orders. Phone, fax, and in-store external orders through POS are no longer accepted. In the event of an emergency, you can place a will call order online that can be picked up at your local Grainger location.

Note: External orders placed through POS will be automatically canceled. Please contact your District Coordinator for assistance.
