Grainger Store Invoicing Procedure

Step

Action

1

When the merchandise arrives at your location please take time to verify the order against the confirmation email of your order.

2

Is there a problem with the order?

If Then
Yes Call Grainger Customer Service at 1-844-820-9244 to discuss the issue and, if applicable, Grainger should issue a credit to Financial Shared Services.
No Proceed to Step 3.

3

Is there a discrepancy or do charges need to be moved to a different account?

If

Then

Store employee

Complete a discrepancy report and send to the Financial Shared Services department.

Administrative employee

Send changes to the Division Analyst for a journal voucher [JV] entry.

All other employees

Contact A/P after charge appears on the SPR and a JV transfer entry can be implemented.

4

Are there any issues regarding product pricing or quantity variance?

If Then
Yes

Call Grainger Customer Service at 1-844-820-9244 to obtain credit.
No Proceed to Step 5.

5

Do you need to return an item back to Grainger?

If Then
Yes Call Grainger Customer Service at 1-844-820-9244 for a Return Authorization Number. They will send you a return shipping label to send the merchandise back.
No The procedure is complete.
