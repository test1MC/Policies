Grainger Resale Merchandise Policy

  1. All Resale merchandise must be on a Valid PO generated out of MMS on the POS. The Grainger employee taking the order will not be allowed to process your order without a valid PO number.

    2. When

    Then

    Valid PO

    • It will be generated from MMS.
    • Contain 13 characters beginning with the prefix A0.
    • Invoice will be processed through Cleveland as before.

    MMS Orders

    • Placed by:
      • Faxing the order to 1-608-757-8410.
      • Calling or bringing your valid MMS PO number into your local Grainger branch.

    Valid SMIS number is not available and the product is needed the same day.
    • Manual B Purchase Order may be used to purchase the item.
    • Manual B PO Orders can be placed by:
      • Faxing to 1-608-757-8410.
      • Calling or bringing your valid Manual B PO into your local Grainger branch.
