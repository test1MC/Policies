Grand & Toy Office Supplies Policy

Applies only to Canadian Paint Stores, Product Finishes Facilities, and Automotive Branches.

  1. Only the Store/Branch Manager, Assistant Manager, and Operations Manager will be able to view and place orders on the Grand and Toy [Canada] ordering websites.
  2. Xerox FAX machine toner cartridges must be ordered directly from Xerox. Xerox FAX machine toner cartridges cannot be ordered from Office Depot.
