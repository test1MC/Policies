Office Depot Non-Store Invoicing Procedure

Applies to all divisions except Retail Stores, Automotive Branches, and Store Administration.

These procedures do not apply to Office Depot furniture purchases. When furniture is purchased, the invoice will go directly to the person ordering the furniture for approval and processing.

Step

Action

1

When the merchandise arrives at your location, verify the order against the confirmation email of your order.

2

Is there a discrepancy?

If Then
Yes Call Office Depot Customer Service at 1-800-817-8136 to discuss the issue and, if applicable, Office Depot will issue a credit to Financial Shared Services.
No Proceed to Step 3.

Note: Office Depot will forward your Office Depot invoice to Financial Shared Services.

3

Use the Office Depot order confirmation and packing slip to verify and review your monthly charges for office supplies (acct. x404001). 

4

Is there is a discrepancy or charges need to be moved to a different account?

If Then
Yes Contact your SW Regional Financial Analyst.
No Proceed to Step 5.

5

Is there is a discrepancy or charges need to be moved to a different account?

If Then
Yes Contact your SW Regional Financial Analyst to obtain credit.
No Proceed to Step 6.

Note: Office Depot will issue a credit memo for these errors within 30 days. If you do not see a credit within 30 days, contact Fran Zarzeczny at 216-566-2301.

6

Do you need to return an item back to Office Depot?

If Then
Yes Please call Office Depot Customer Service at 1-800-817-8136 for a Return Authorization Number. Office Depot will send someone to pick up the goods based on the request for a Return Authorization Number.
No The procedure is complete.

Please use the following return address on the package(s):
