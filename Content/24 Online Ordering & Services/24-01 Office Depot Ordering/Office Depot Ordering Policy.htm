Office Depot Ordering Policy

  1. Only the Store/Branch Manager, Assistant Manager, Operations Manager and District Coordinators will be able to view and place orders on the Office Depot ordering websites.
  2. There is a $400 per order limit with Office Depot.
  3. Certain items do not always ship the next day. Depending on your location, it may take three days for you to receive an item. Therefore, do NOT place a second order for an item that may be coming from a different warehouse. Stores located in Alaska will have a 2-3 week lead time to receive their order.
  4. All Lexmark printer toner cartridges must be ordered from the Office Depot website. See 19-01A1 Store Computer System for current printer models and part numbers.
  5. All coffee, office, and restroom supplies must be ordered from Office Depot as they are the preferred vendor.
  6. Do not order ink cartridge refills for Xerox or Panasonic multi-function printers (stores only). Contact the SCO Help Desk for a new Lexmark MX3P multi-function printer.
