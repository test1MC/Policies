Office Depot Store Invoicing Procedure

DISTRICT OFFICE NOTE: These procedures do not apply to Office Depot furniture purchases through the District Office. When furniture is purchased at the District Office, the invoice will go directly to the person ordering the furniture for approval and processing.

Step

Action

1

When the merchandise arrives at your location, verify the order against the confirmation email of your order.

2

Is there a problem with the order?

If Then
Yes Call Office Depot Customer Service at 1-800-817-8136 to discuss the issue and, if applicable, Office Depot should issue a credit to Financial Shared Services.
No Proceed to Step 3.

3

Is there a discrepancy or do charges need to be moved to a different account?

If

Then

Store employee

Complete a discrepancy report and send via email – discrepancy.reports@sherwin.com to the Financial Shared Services department.

Administrative employee

Send changes to the Division Analyst for a journal voucher [JV] entry.

All other employees

Contact A/P after charge appears on the SPR and a JV transfer entry can be implemented.

4

Are there any issues regarding product pricing or quantity variance?

If Then
Yes Call Office Depot Customer Service at 1-800-817-8136 to obtain credit.
No Proceed to Step 5.

5

Do you need to return an item back to Office Depot?

If Then
Yes Please call Office Depot Customer Service at 1-800-817-8136 for a Return Authorization Number. They will send you a return shipping label to send the merchandise back.
No The procedure is complete.

Please use the following return address on the package(s):

The Sherwin-Williams Company
Financial Shared Services
101 W. Prospect Avenue
1530 Guildhall
Cleveland, Ohio, 44115

Note: Office Depot will issue a credit memo for these errors within in the next 30 days. If you do not see a credit on the following month's SPR, contact Fran Zarzeczny at 216-566-2301.
