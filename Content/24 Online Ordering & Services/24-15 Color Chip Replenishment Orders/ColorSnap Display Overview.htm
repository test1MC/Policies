ColorSnap Display Overview

A well-stocked ColorSnap Display is a powerful selling tool. The display should be reviewed for stocking needs at least once a week and, if required, a replenishment order for color chips, cards, and books placed with ChromaSource.

To ensure a full-stocked display, it is recommended that you stock from the bottom up;  utilize the storage in the bottom of the ColorSnap Display to stock additional packets of color chips.  As color chips require replenishment, restock from the bottom and be sure to re-order to stock both your top and bottom. You may want to consider stocking several packets of the most popular colors.

You should be aware of the following order restrictions when placing orders via ChromaSource:

  • You may order only 5 packets of each color per order.
  • You must order at least 10 items per order.
  • You may order only 200 lines (colors) per order.
  • You are permitted to place three orders per calendar week.
