Office Depot Coffee Program Policy

Office Depot has a large selection of coffee with competitive prices. As a result, Stores Operations has partnered with Office Depot to launch a national coffee program. The program is not mandatory. Stores that wish to participate in this program will receive a free coffee brewer from Office Depot.

  1. Stores that receive a free brewer from Office Depot are required to order all store coffee through Office Depot.
  2. If Office Depot is not the sole provider of a store’s coffee and supplies, the brewer must be returned. If it is not, the store will be charged.
