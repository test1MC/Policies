Create New mySherwin Account Procedure

New hires should use this procedure to create a mySherwin WebSSO account. Do not call the Global Service Desk for a password reset or to aid in creating your account as this process is not supported by IT.

Step

Action

1

Go to http://my.sherwin.com.

2

Select Create New Account.

3

Enter your first name, last name, date of birth, and last 4 digits of your SSN/CIN/NID.

4

The system will look for the matching record.

Note: Should mySherwin not find a match, your manager should verify your identity information in GEMS MDA, contact your District Coordinator, or contact HRSS to correct it.

5

You will be required to choose security questions and answers.

Note: It is extremely important to carefully choose security questions and answers that only you can answer in the event you ever forget your password. This will enable you to reset you own password in the future. These will also be utilized by the Global Service Desk to validate the identity of the caller to ensure they are who they state they are.

5

Create your password.
