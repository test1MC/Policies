mySherwin Logon Authentication Procedure

You are required to confirm your log-on with a separate verification code sent to you via text or email when accessing mySherwin from an internet connection outside of the Sherwin network (such as a personal or public network or Wi-Fi connection). To receive a verification code:

Step Action

1

Logon with user ID and password.

2

Register your contact information.

3

Choose method for receiving your verification code.

4

Enter your verification code within 30 minutes of the request.
