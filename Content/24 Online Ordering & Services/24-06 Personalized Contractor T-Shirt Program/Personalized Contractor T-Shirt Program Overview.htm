Personalized Contractor T-Shirt Program Overview

This section applies to Canadian Paint Stores only.

The Sherwin-Williams Personalized T-Shirt Program offers customers the opportunity to purchase Sherwin-Williams (or GP branded) t-shirts at a discounted price, as well as, personalize the back of these shirts with their company information.

T-shirts are plain white, short-sleeved shirts which will display the Sherwin-Williams logo (or General Paint Logo) on the front left chest of the shirt and the customer’s personalized information on the center back. This personalized information may include an image as well. T-shirts cost five dollars per shirt with a minimum order of twenty-five t-shirts for one color artwork on the back and fifty shirts for multi-colour artwork. Additional charges apply for multi-colour requests on artwork and on sizes XXL and up. These additional charges are available on the ordering website. The t-shirts will be delivered two week after payment is received.
