Order Personalized Contractor T-Shirts Procedure

This section applies to Canadian Paint Stores only.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Order -> Personalized Contractor T-Shirts - Canada

2

Enter the store email address and the password: Welcome123.

Note: The password can be changed in the Profile section of the account.

3

Select Create Order

4

Enter the appropriate parameters.

5

Select the branding to appear on the front left chest of the shirt.

Note: The Sherwin-Williams logo is pre-selected.

6

Select Next to enter the order details.

7

Enter the appropriate parameters.

Note: There is a minimum order of twenty-five t-shirts for one color artwork on the back and fifty shirts for multi-colour artwork.

8

Enter the number of colors needed for the personalized information.

Note: By default, it is set to 1 color (PMS 293C), which is the standard blue. Choose Other to select and specify another.

9

Select Next to enter the message on the back of the t-shirt.

10

Enter the appropriate message.

Note: The message is allowed to be three lines long and can be in either UPPER or lower case letters.

11

Select whether or not an image should be added.

Note: The store can attach an image or have an automated message sent to customers to have them upload their own image.

12

Select Submit.

Note: Once both the order and artwork are approved, an automated email request from the vendor will be sent to the customer requesting payment. Customers are required to submit payment online and cannot use PPC points towards the payment.
