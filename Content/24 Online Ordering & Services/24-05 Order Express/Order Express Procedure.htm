Order Express Procedure

Step

Action

Searching and Adding to Cart

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Source Operations -> Order -> OrderExpress.

When you log in for the first time, go to My Profile, fill out your information, and setup any addresses to be used in the ordering process. This will expedite the checkout process.

Note: Order Express can also be accessed via mySherwin.

2

If

Then

You know the product number or product name

Use the search function at the top of the main page.

You know key words associated with the product

Use the search function at the top of the main page -> Adjust advanced search settings to locate the item

You do not know the product name/number or any key words associated with it

Browse using the Products or Category features.

3

Once you find the product you are looking for, you can open the product listing for more details or add the item to your cart.

Checkout

4

Select the shopping cart icon on the top bar.

5

The system will return a quick glance of the products you already have in your cart, as well as the sub-total.

6

If

Then

Every product you need is present

Select the Go To Cart button.

You need additional products

Continue browsing.

7

After you select Go To Cart, you will be brought to the Shopping Cart page, where you can update/edit your cart/quantity, preview items, add any special comments, or choose to continue shopping.

8

Select Checkout.

Note: Items may ship from Cleveland, OH, South Bend, IN, or both depending on where that item is warehoused.

9

Complete the billing address and shipping address section.

Note: You can select from the addresses you have setup in your profile or enter a new address.

10

Select a Delivery Date.

11

On the Payment Information page, edit or confirm the cost center to be billed for the order.

12

Confirm and complete the order.
