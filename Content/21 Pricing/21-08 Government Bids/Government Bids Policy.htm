Government Bids Policy

  1. Read the bid to see if an officer of the company should sign. No store employee has the authority to sign a Federal Government Bid.
  2. If more than one store wishes to bid on the same Federal Government project, the Wholesale Marketing Department will decide which store will service the bid.
  3. Store Managers and Sales Representatives must review all City, State, and County Bids with the District Manager / District Sales Manager prior to signing.
