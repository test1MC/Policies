National Account Discount Policy

Some National Account customers may receive an extended discount. These customers are set up by a National Account Executive under CAC 1141 (10% discount) and 1142 (15% discount).

  1. In order to receive the National Account Extended Discount, the account must be assigned to either CAC 1141 or 1142. This assignment is completed by HQ Financial Services.
  2. In order to receive the National Account Extended Discount, the sale must be run against the Account Number.
  3. The customer claiming the discount must produce either a barcoded coupon or barcoded discount card. The barcode on these coupons and cards represent not a line item or total discount but the actual account number and may be scanned at the Customer Account Number Field.
  4. Discounts will apply per the Price Record Card for the account. Exclusions may apply. For additional information, please refer to the documentation provided by the customer.

Note: To apply a discount, scan either the barcoded coupon or barcoded discount card at the Account Number Field in the Order Header. This will ensure the correct account is used and discount pricing is applied to the sale. The barcode on these coupons and cards represent the Account Number, not a coupon for a line item or total sale.
