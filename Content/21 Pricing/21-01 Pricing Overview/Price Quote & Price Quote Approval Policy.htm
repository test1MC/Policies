Price Quote & Price Quote Approval Policy

  1. The wording for the "In Lieu Of" document cannot be changed for any reason. This document has been carefully drafted and is intended to protect the interests of the Company.
  2. Credit approval is required for all Quotes and Contracts prior to releasing any material.
  3. Sales Representatives and store employees are not authorized to sign any non-Sherwin-Williams documentson behalf of Sherwin-Williams. Any "external" contract must be approved and signed by the proper level of management. For example: Division Vice President of Sales and Division President. Credit must be approved prior to submitting the "external" contract for approval.
  4. Division guidelines for acceptable gross profit percentages must be followed when writing quotes and completing Sherwin-Williams contracts.
  5. The District Manager must review written quotes and Sherwin-Williams contracts when a price is given for an extended period and the price will be held to cover the time period of the quote or contract.
