Pricing Logic Overview

Customers purchasing product without an account will be priced according to promotions and list price. Customers with accounts will receive pricing automatically retrieved from promotions, Price Schedules, or Price Record Cards (PRCs). The CAC Maintenance Procedure is used in conjunction with Volume Tags to provide specific Market Segment or National Schedule pricing when a Dollar Value Exception (DVE) is not necessary. The customer’s Price Record Card is the store's record for all accounts with negotiated selling prices. However, the customer’s schedule price should be applied whenever possible.

Note: For more information on Price Record Cards, see Price Record Card (PRC) Maintenance Overview.
