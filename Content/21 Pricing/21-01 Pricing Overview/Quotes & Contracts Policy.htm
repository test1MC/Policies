Quotes & Contracts Policy

Sales Representatives and store employees write quotes to communicate the merchandise and services offered to the customer. Contracts are a legal agreement between the Company and the customer outlining products, services, and payment terms.

  1. The Sherwin-Williams Price Quotation form shall be used anytime a quote is given to a customer.
  2. A Sherwin-Williams Proposal and Contract is the only approved contract to be used for a customer purchase which store employees are authorized to sign.
  3. Store Managers and Sales Representatives are authorized to submit Price Quotes (without prior approval) only if pricing is taken from a current approved Price Schedule. District Manager/Sales Manager approval is required if quoted prices are different than published on a current Price Schedule.  Locations utilizing the Pricing Approval System are to submit all customer pricing quotes via the Pricing Approval System if quoted prices are different from the customers current Price Schedule.
