Price Quotes Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Locations.

  1. For Regular Customer Quotes, the home store or Sales Representative determines customer pricing regardless of which store will be servicing a particular job or order. This policy applies to all routine customer pricing, which has been approved at the appropriate level per current Division gross margin guidelines.
  2. All Large Job Quotes (1,000 gallons or more) outside of the district should be communicated through the Division Marketing Department. The Division Marketing Department will track all large jobs to ensure that prices are determined by the appropriate person and that all customers are quoted consistently. Division Marketing will also approve any special large job credit.
  3. The Manager is responsible for selling merchandise profitably. The Manager must ensure that discounts are given to customers within established guidelines.
  4. In order to give a customer a price other than those on the established schedules, the Manager must receive approval from the District Manager via the Pricing Approval System.
  5. A Customer Price Record Card must be on file for any account that receives special selling prices.
  6. It is against policy to set up generic cash/charge accounts or PRCs.
  7. Sherwin-Williams' Price Record Cards and Price Lists are confidential and must not be viewed by customers.
