Price Quote Procedure

Step

Action

1

Obtain written product guarantees and/or warranties from suppliers before submitting a quote or contract.

Note: Review all local fire regulations before recommending products.

2

Review the store prices for the requested items. Obtain written guarantees on prices from the suppliers before entering the quote or writing a contract. If the delivery date is far in the future, you may need to get an escalation clause from the supplier

3

Contact the District Manager if a competitive price is below store authority.

Note: Find the Price Quote or Price Quote Approval form in the Forms Repository.

4

When quoting on an installation job, get a firm price from the installer for all installation costs, so that extra charges do not reduce the store's profit. Ensure the store has a sub-contractor's agreement on file for all installers used on a government project.

Note: For more information on sub-contractors, see 01-08 Installer Payments and Retainage.

5

All contracts that are supplied by the customer (external contracts) must be forwarded to Division Management for approval and signature, after first obtaining approval from District Credit.
