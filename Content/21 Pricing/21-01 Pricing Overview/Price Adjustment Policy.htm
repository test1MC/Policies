Price Adjustment Policy

  1. Under normal circumstances, pricing should not be adjusted while processing a point of sale transaction.
  2. The Pricing Approval System process should be used to request special customer pricing below the floor.
  3. All pricing adjustments should be made on the customer's Price Record Card.
  4. Any employee can adjust the selling price as long as the adjusted price is above the National Schedule price for the product.
  5. Store management (including assistant and operations managers) can adjust the selling price as long as the adjusted price is between the National Schedule and the Override Floor.
  6. Use a Pricing Remedies Procedure for cases where the price must be adjusted at the POS.
