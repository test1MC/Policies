Gross Profit Policy

  1. The Store Manager is responsible for ensuring that the gross profit is properly calculated and processed through the Point of Sale.
  2. Do not sell below the approved gross profit guidelines, established by the District Manager, without written approval from the District Office. The [Low Price Guarantee] on wallcovering will not be included in the low gross profit guidelines as long as proper documentation is on file.
  3. The Low Price Guarantee states that Sherwin-Williams will match any competitor's price on Wallcovering or Borders at the time of purchase, or for up to 30 days after your purchase.
