Volume Tags Overview

This sections does not apply to Automotive.

Volume Tags represent the sales potential of an account and provide a pricing schedule based on that potential:

Correctly identifying customers’ sales potential will provide accurate pricing for the customer and lead to consistent Market Pricing. It also determines schedule pricing that is automatically returned in COE and POS. Use the Qualifying Customers to Determine Total Sales Potential handout as a guide to appropriately identify customer potential.
