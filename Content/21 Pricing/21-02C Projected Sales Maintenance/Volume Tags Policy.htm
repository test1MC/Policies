Volume Tags Policy

  1. The annual total potential sales volume includes all product types (paint, sundries, equipment, wallcovering, and floorcovering) and must be determined when creating a new account.
  2. The total potential sales volume should include both the amount the customer is likely to spend at Sherwin-Williams and the amount the customer may spend with competitors.
  3. District Management must review changes and approve adjustments.
  4. Stores and Reps can assign Volume classifications at the A and B level.
  5. DM approval is required for volume classes C, D, and E.
