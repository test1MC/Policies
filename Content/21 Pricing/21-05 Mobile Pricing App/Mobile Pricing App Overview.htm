Mobile Pricing App Overview

All TAG Divisions in the US and Canada can use Mobile Pricing. Does not apply to Automotive, Product Finishes, and P&M.

The Mobile Pricing App's purpose is to do the following:

  • Improve the sales force’s ability to access pricing using mobile devices and PCs
  • View and obtain pricing for existing and new customers
  • Determine the source of the price (i.e. – Schedule, Exception)
  • Compare current prices to segment schedule pricing
