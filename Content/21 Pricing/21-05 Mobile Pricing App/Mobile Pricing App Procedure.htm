Mobile Pricing App Procedure

All TAG Divisions in the US and Canada can use Mobile Pricing. Does not apply to Automotive, Product Finishes, and P&M.

Step Action Screenshots

1

Go to the SherLink Mobile App on your mobile device -> Log in with MySherwin credentials -> Select Mobile Pricing

2

Select the appropriate Product Pricing Type (Price With An Account or Price Without An Account)

Price With An Account (existing customer) – Preloaded Accounts

1

Select Price With An Account -> Your accounts are preloaded on the screen -> Select the correct Job # & PRC #. The system default is automatically Job/PRC #1.

2

Search by Sales # or by Product/Rex # -> Select the appropriate Size Code

3

When all fields have been completed, press Submit. -> The sample results will display Account Number, Name, Job/PRC, and Market Segment.  In addition, it will display the Current Customer Price and Price Source, Current Schedule Price, Current List Price, and Price at each Volume Tag Level.

Price With An Account (existing customer) – Manual Search

1

Select Price With An Account -> Manually search accounts by entering information in the fields -> Corresponding accounts will appear, sorted A-Z

2

Select the appropriate account -> Select the appropriate Job # and PRC #

3

Search Product/Sales # by Lookup or by Name, Rex #, Size, etc. -> Select the appropriate Product, sorted A-Z

4

When all fields have been completed, press Submit.

5

The sample results will display differently for Non-Paint.  The results include: Account Number, Name, Job/PRC, and Market Segment.  In addition, it will display the Current Customer Price and Price Source, and ProDiscount Price.

Price Without An Account (potential customer) – Home Store, Segment, CAC, and VT Selection

1

Select Price Without An Account -> Enter Home Store -> Select appropriate Segment and CAC -> Select appropriate Customer Volume Size. -> For paint, Select Home Store, Segment, CAC, VT and Product.

2

When all fields have been completed, press Submit -> The sample results will display Customer Price, Price Source, Big Box Price (if it exists), Current Schedule Price, Schedule Name, Current List Price, and Price at each Volume Tag Level.
