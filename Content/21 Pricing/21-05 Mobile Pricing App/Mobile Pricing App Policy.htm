Mobile Pricing App Policy

All TAG Divisions in the US and Canada can use Mobile Pricing. Does not apply to Automotive, Product Finishes, and P&M.

Since the content of the app is highly confidential, please comply with the following guidelines:

  1. Employees must not share search screen results with customers, i.e. exchanging screen shots.
  2. The results display schedule levels, big box pricing, and pricing content that may not directly pertain to a customer and should not be shared.
  3. Pricing schedules and lists are company confidential and must be protected. Unauthorized or inappropriate sharing of pricing information could result in disciplinary action.
