CAC Maintenance Procedure

Step Action

1

Access PRC Maintenance -> Lookup the customer -> Select the appropriate account and PRC.

Note: For more information on PRC Access and Maintenance, see Price Record Card (PRC) Maintenance Overview.

2

Use the corresponding dropdowns to specify the new CAC Group and the new CAC Code.

3

Use the corresponding dropdown to specify the Reason for Change.

Note: If Reason for Change is not listed, select Other and enter your reason as a comment.

4

Select Apply CAC/Sales Change.

Note: If the request does not process, review error results on the top of the screen.
