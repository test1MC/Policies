Job & PRC Naming Guidelines Overview

Adhere to the following guidelines when selecting Job and PRC names:

  • Use business appropriate names
  • Each Job and PRC name should have meaning to the customer so that they can remotely identify the appropriate one
  • PRC names with just numbers should not be used unless they have specific meaning to the customer
  • Naming a Job or PRC with “Split XXXX” should not be used because it is not relevant to the customer
  • Job and PRC names for an account need to be unique—you cannot have two or more PRCs or Jobs on the same account with the same name
  • If there is only one PRC tied to a job, the PRC will have the same name as the job
  • PRC names should have no reference to PRC in the name
  • Do not leave PRC name blank
  • Delete PRCs once they are no longer necessary
  • Contact your District Credit Office to delete Jobs if they are no longer needed
