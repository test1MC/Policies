CPR & PRC Cleanup Disabled Time Frames Overview

To accommodate account processing, CPR and PRC Cleanup are disabled during the following times:

  • Nightly, 8 PM – 6 AM EST
  • Once a month for month end processing
  • On the 21PstP of each month for retail statement processing (this only applies to retail charge accounts, and does not affect commercial charge accounts or cash accounts).

Note: You will be notified if monthly downtime dates fall during your cleanup.
