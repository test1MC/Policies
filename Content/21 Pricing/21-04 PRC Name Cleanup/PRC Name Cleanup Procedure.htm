PRC Name Cleanup Procedure

Familiarize yourself with the application’s navigation before completing the review:

  • Double click to select an editable field. Fields highlighted in yellow (with bolded text) need to be reviewed.
  • Once an editable field has been entered, arrow keys are the easiest way to navigate the application.
  • Select on a column heading to sort in ascending/descending order.
  • Select on the arrow in the upper right corner of a column heading to simultaneously prioritize and sort several columns.
  • Enter an attribute or search term in the box immediately below the column heading to apply a filter.
  • Hover the cursor over the territory number to show the reps name.  
  • Toggle the Show All/Show Remaining button for the corresponding view.

5

If no changes are necessary, select the check box () in the far left column and the line will be marked complete.

6

Wherever appropriate, flag cards and jobs for deletion.

Note: Deletion is not automatic, and may take up to 20 days to process.

7

The cleanup is complete when the counter at the top of the screen (Items for Review) shows 0.

Note: Review does not need to be completed in one session, and lines marked completed will be logged at the bottom of the list. Sort the status column to bring all outstanding review lines to the top of the review screen.
