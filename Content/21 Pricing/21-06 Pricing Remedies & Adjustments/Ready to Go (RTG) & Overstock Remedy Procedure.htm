Ready to Go (RTG) & Overstock Remedy Procedure

Step Action
Requesting Additions to RTG

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Pricing Approval System.

2

Select Pricing Approval and select Submit.

3

Select Create a Ready to Go/Overstock Request.

4

Enter the required information in the Product Requests - Ready To Go field and select Add Product.

Note: If the product has a WAC cost, enter it instead of the product file cost.

5

A confirmation message will appear when you have successfully added the product.

Note: Once the District Manager approves the product request, you will be able to use the Price Remedy- Ready to Go barcode.
Processing RTF/Overstock Remedy

6

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Enter customer information -> On the Line Item Screen, highlight the line that needs to be adjusted.

7

Press Disc [F11] -> Price Remedy.

8

Scan or enter the RTG/Overstock remedy barcode.

9

Select and enter the remedy price as either a percentage discount, dollar discount, or new price.
