Review Pricing Accommodation Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Manager Functions -> Remedy Reports -> Review Accommodation Remedy.

2

Highlight the appropriate remedy and press View [F7] to access the Remedy Accommodation Detail screen.

3

Press Enter.

4

Press Spacebar -> Accept [F1] to note that the remedy has been reviewed.

5

Enter your Employee Number to verify your status.
