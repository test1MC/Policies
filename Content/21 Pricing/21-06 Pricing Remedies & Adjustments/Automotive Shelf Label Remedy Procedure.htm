Automotive Shelf Label Remedy Procedure

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Enter information and adjust the price of the product to reflect the lower shelf label price.

2

Press Comment [F9] on the line item and enter Shelf Label Adjustment.

3

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down key to access Special Menu -> Correction.

4

Enter the original transaction information on the negative side of the correction.

5

On the positive side of the correction, adjust the price of the product to reflect the lower shelf label price.

6

Press Comment [F9] on the line item and enter Shelf Label Adjustment.
