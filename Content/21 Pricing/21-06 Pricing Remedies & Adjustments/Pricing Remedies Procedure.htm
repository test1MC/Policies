Pricing Remedies Procedure

Process a Correction Remedy, Post Sale Remedy, Pricing Accommodation, Substitution Remedy, Unregistered Coupon Remedy, or a Visiting Customer Remedy.

Step

Action
1
If Then
Correction Remedy

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down key to access Special Menu -> Correction.

For additional information, review Correction Transaction Overview.
Post Sale Remedy

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Scan the Post Sale remedy at any point in the transaction -> Applicable discounts will automatically apply -> Procedure is complete.

Pricing Accommodation, Substitution Remedy, Unregistered Coupon Remedy, or Visiting Customer Remedy From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Enter customer information.

2

On the Line Item Screen, highlight the line that needs to be adjusted.

3

Press Disc [F11] -> Price Remedy.

4
If Then
Correction Remedy

Scan or enter the Correction remedy barcode.
Pricing Accommodation Scan or enter the Pricing Accommodation remedy barcode.
Substitution Remedy

Scan or enter the Substitution remedy barcode -> Follow the prompt to enter the substituted product, either by typing the sales number or scanning the product’s UPC barcode -> Enter the quantity of the substituted product.

Note: If the two products are incompatible, then the remedy will be rejected. The original product quantity and substituted product quantity must match.
Unregistered Coupon Remedy

Scan or enter the Unregistered Coupon remedy barcode.
Visiting Customer Remedy

Scan or enter the Visiting Customer remedy barcode.

5

Select and enter the remedy price as either a percentage discount, dollar discount, or new price.
