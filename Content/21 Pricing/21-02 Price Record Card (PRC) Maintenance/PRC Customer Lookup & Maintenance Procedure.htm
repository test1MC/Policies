PRC Customer Lookup & Maintenance Procedure

Step

Action

1

Access PRC Maintenance.

2

Enter the customer’s 9 digit account number (with or without dashes) or their name and location.

3

Select Search.

4

Select the appropriate account number.

5

Once you select an account, all PRCs assigned to the customer will appear. From here, you can:

  • Add PRC
  • Delete PRC
  • Edit PRC Headers
  • Maintain PRC Details
  • Maintain Dollar Value Exception (DVE) Overview, Take Aim Overview, Volume Tags Overview, and Customer Account Classification (CAC) Codes Overview.

Note: If you want a new individual PRC to replace an existing PRC, you must first delete the old PRC. To submit changes and approval requests during a transaction, suspend the transaction, complete the maintenance request, and then recall and complete the sale.
