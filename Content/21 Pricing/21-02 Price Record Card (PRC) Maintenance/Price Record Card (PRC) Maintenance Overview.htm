Price Record Card (PRC) Maintenance Overview

Price Record Cards (PRCs) are the store's record for all accounts with established special selling prices, and they are managed using PRC Maintenance on SOURCE. PRC Maintenance provides:

  • Consistent pricing between home stores and visiting stores
  • A single source for information needed to make pricing decisions
  • Tools for Stores/Reps to update PRCs
  • Detailed product sales information by customer
  • Estimated financial impact of changes
  • Control over effective dates of changes

For users new to PRC maintenance, please review PRC Maintenance and Brand Pricing.
