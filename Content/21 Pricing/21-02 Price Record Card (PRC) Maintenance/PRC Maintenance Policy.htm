PRC Maintenance Policy

  1. It is Store Management’s and Store Reps’ responsibility to create and maintain PRCs.
  2. Store Management and Reps must name PRCs so that they are original to the customer’s account and meaningful to the customer.
  3. Reps can only maintain PRCs for accounts they are tied to.
  4. Stores can only maintain PRCs for accounts for which they are the home store.
  5. Management is only able to create and maintain PRCs for those accounts for which they are the home store location.
  6. Only certain portions of the PRC may be maintained at the store, including PRC Header Information, Pricing Exceptions, Comments, and Authorized Buyers.
  7. Modifications to Customer Contact and billing information and PID must be requested through the Division Pricing Analyst. Applicable changes will be made in the A/R System.
  8. You must contact your District Credit Office to add a job.
