Copy PRC Policy

  1. Requests to copy a PRC must be sent to your Division Pricing Analyst.
  2. Do NOT copy PRCs between accounts. Copying between accounts circumvents the approval process. DVEs are approved for a specific customer. One customer’s price is not necessarily another customer’s price, due to size, purchasing volume, or market segment filtering.
  3. It is acceptable to copy cards when:
    1. Accounts change from cash to charge
    2. The same customer has multiple accounts
    3. Multiple accounts paint for the same builder
