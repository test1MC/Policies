Add PRC Procedure

Step

Action

1

Access PRC Maintenance.

2

Lookup the customer and select the appropriate account.

3

Select the appropriate Job Number from the dropdown menu.

4

Select Add PRC for Job.

5

Complete the PRC header information:

Field

Description

Wholesale Promotion

Customer wants to receive wholesale promotional pricing.

D.I.Y. Promo

Customer wants to receive retail promotions. 

Packing Slip

Customer wants to receive a packing slip with orders.

Additional Copy

Customer wants to receive a second invoice copy.

Laser

Customer wants invoice printed from the Laser Printer rather than the Epson.

PO Required

Customer requires a supplied purchase order number on all customer orders and invoices.

Review / Expiration Date

Date for PRC review/expiration, which defaults to one year from the day of creation (but can be adjusted).

Contact

Enter the person opening the account as the main contact.

Comments

Will appear whenever the customer’s PRC is pulled into COE or POS.

Authorized Buyers

Specify all approved and/or restricted purchasers for the account.

6

Select Apply Changes.

Note: This section pertains to adding a PRC to an existing job. You must contact your District Credit Office (DCO) to add a new job.
