Brand Pricing Overview

The purpose of Brand Pricing is to provide:

  • Consistent customer pricing on negotiated products between sheens and sizes within a product line
  • Standardized dollar amounts among sheen levels
  • Standardized dollar amounts between singles and fives
  • Less time spent updating PRCs
  • Streamlined DM Approval Process
  • Increased efficiency in Price Quote Process
  • Decreased Correction Transactions

Brand pricing also allows users to create multiple category/size exceptions on the Main PRC (Shop Job) during one request. Based on user input, the PRC system will calculate suggested prices for an entire product line. For example , an exception for Pro Mar 200 Zero Flat will calculate exceptions extending to Pro Mar 200 Low- Sheen, Pro Mar 200Zero Eggshell, and Pro Mar 200 Zero Semi-Gloss in both singles and fives.

Note: For more information on exceptions, see Dollar Value Exception (DVE) Overview.
