Big Box Policy

  1. Accounts can be added to Big Box pricing groups if they are:
    1. In a residential Repaint or Property Maintenance CAC.
    2. Producing rolling year sales less than or equal to $5,000.
  2. If an account is outside this criteria and the store feels they could grow the customer’s business by having them on Big Box pricing, then they can get an exception approved through their District Manager. If approved, the DM will have the Division Pricing analyst add the customer to Big Box Pricing.
