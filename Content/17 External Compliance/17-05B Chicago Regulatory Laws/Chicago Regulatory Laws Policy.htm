Chicago Regulatory Laws Policy

  1. Chicago has placed restrictions on the purchase, receipt, or acquisition of any corrosive or caustic acid:
    1. Stores are not to knowingly sell more than one package in a 24-hour period to a customer with the exception of manufacturing and wholesale druggists, retail druggists, physicians, chemists, regularly established laboratories, or to persons who have procured any of said substances for medicinal purposes upon a prescription of a physician regularly licensed under the laws of the state.
    2. Customers wishing to purchase corrosive / caustic acid must provide a driver's license or other government-issued identification showing the person's name, date of birth, and photograph.
    3. Customers wishing to purchase corrosive / caustic acid must sign a log documenting the name and address of the person, date and time of the transaction, and brand and product name and total quantity distributed.
  2. Aerosols can not be on the sales floor. All aerosols are to be stored in the back room / warehouse.
