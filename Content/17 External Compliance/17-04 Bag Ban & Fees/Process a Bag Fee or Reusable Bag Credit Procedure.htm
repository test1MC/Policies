Process a Bag Fee or Reusable Bag Credit Procedure

Step Action
Process Bag Fee

1

While on a blank line item, press ENTER to access the Non-Merchandise Code Menu.

2

Select  the appropriate Non-Merchandise Code:

  • 20 for Carryout Plastic Bag Fee.
  • 21 for Carryout Paper Bag Fee.

3

Input the quantity of bags used to package the customer’s goods.

4

Charge the amount required by law per bag (see chart above).
Process Reusable Bag Credit

1

The sale must be processed as a Correction Transaction.

2

Enter 99999 for the Terminal Number and the Transaction Number.

3

Enter the Date as today’s date.

4

Select the customer’s account number or select DIY.

5

NEGATIVE SIDE:  Enter the Reusable Bag Credit.

6

While on a blank line item, press ENTER to access the Non-Merchandise Code Menu.

7

Select  the appropriate Non-Merchandise Code:

22 for Rebate – Recycle Bag.

8

Input the quantity of bags used to package the customer’s goods.

9

Charge the amount required by law per bag (see chart above).

10

POSITIVE SIDE:  Scan the items to be purchased.
