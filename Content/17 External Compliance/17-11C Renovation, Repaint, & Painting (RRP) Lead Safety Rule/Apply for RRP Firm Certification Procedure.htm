Apply for RRP Firm Certification Procedure

Certified firms will be able to advertise that they are certified by EPA under the RRP program and will also be given rights to use EPA’s new Lead-Safe Certified Firm logo.

Step

Action

1

Access the certification link:  http://www.epa.gov/lead/getcertified

2

Complete the online application.

Note: Each firm must have at least one certified renovator.
