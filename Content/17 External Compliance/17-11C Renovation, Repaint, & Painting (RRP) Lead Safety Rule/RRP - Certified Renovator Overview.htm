RRP - Certified Renovator Overview

The certified renovator must:

  • Make sure the job is conducted using lead-safe work practices
  • Do the work personally or direct workers to do the job
  • Perform the post-renovation cleaning verification
  • Provide training to un-certified workers on lead-safe work practices
  • Make himself or herself available on-site or by telephone
  • Use an acceptable test kit to determine whether lead-based paint is present in affected areas

Note: Renovators who complete the online training will be certified for three years; renovators who complete the hands-on training will be certified for five years. For more information, please visit http://www.epa.gov/lead/getcertified .
