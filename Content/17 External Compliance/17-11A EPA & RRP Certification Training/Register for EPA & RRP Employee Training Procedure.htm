Register for EPA & RRP Employee Training Procedure

Step

Action

1

Contact the District Coordinator to obtain printed registration forms.

Note: Once the date, time, and location have been identified, the District Coordinator must include the information on the log and registration form. Print the registration form for distribution.

2

Collect a completed and signed EPA, RRP Certification Training Registration form from the customer, including the following information:

  • Name of the account
  • Account number and job
  • Number of attendees
  • Form of payment

3

Check class and room availability before processing the transaction for attending the session.

4

Step

Action

1

Use the customer S-W Account number to process a Sale Transaction.

2

When prompted to ENTER SALES/CLASS/PRODUCT NUMBER, press ENTER.

3

From the Non-Merchandise Codes menu, select Certification Training. [This non-merchandise code and all expenses clear under general ledger code 1699-113. The revenues and costs associated with an attendance will not be credited or charged to the home store. These will be pooled at the district, and Group levels. Our expectation is that the net cost will be close to break-even. Any costs beyond break-even will be shared by Stores Group and the District.]

Note: Cash, Check, Credit Card, or S-W Charge payment is acceptable.

4

Staple a copy of the transaction to the back of the registration form.

5

Submit the completed registration form and a copy of the sales transaction to the District Coordinator for processing.
