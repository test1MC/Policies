Conduct SW Sponsored Certified Renovator Training Procedure

Step

Action

1

A Sherwin-Williams representative should open the session by welcoming customer and passing out the RRP Painting Supplies promotion brochure and advising attendees that all RRP supplies can be purchased at their local Sherwin-Williams store.

2

A Sherwin-Williams representative should also close the session at the end of the day.

3

The EPA must receive notification of classes at least 3 weeks prior to the start of the classes. The notification must include the name, address and telephone number of the venue and the hours of class. Send to Al Blanchard at least 3 weeks prior to a scheduled class start date.

4

Room set-up includes:

  • Tables and chairs for the students
  • Four tables for the instructor
  • Whiteboards or flip charts
  • Sufficient space for the hands-on training and assessment activities for the number of students in the class
  • Adequate lighting
  • A screen or light colored surface large enough to project slides on
  • An electrical outlet for the computer and LCD projector

5

There is additional equipment needed for the hands-on portion of training listed here. It will take the instructor approximately 2-3 hours to set up the site for training and hands-on activities. In order to get training started promptly, the instructor should have access to the classroom the evening before or in the early morning on the day of the class to get everything in place and set up. It is requested that the S-W personnel assisting with the hands-on meet with the Principle Instructor to review the hands-on activities during this time.

6

The class size is limited to a maximum of 25 with the exception of those listed in Register for EPA Customer Training Procedure. The approved student-teacher ratio for the hands-on portion of training for Renovator Initial is 10:1. This is why it will be important to know well in advance the projected number of students for the class. For a class of 25, 2 representatives must assist with the training. These instructors may be SW representatives with the exception of those listed above. They need to be present the entire day and assist with the hands-on portion of training.

7

The EPA requires color photos to be included on the certificate (as well as the Post Training Report sent to them), so the certificates will have to be prepared after the class with the student information and pictures from the instructor. The Principle Instructor or others will take pictures of the students prior to class commencing. Pictures must be taken against a white or light colored background.

8

For the refresher class, attendees must bring their current certification to the session.  They will not be allowed to take the refresher class without producing their current certificate.  If they have lost their certificate, the can contact:
