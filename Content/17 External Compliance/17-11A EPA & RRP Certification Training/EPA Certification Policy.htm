EPA Certification Policy

  1. Under EPA's [Environmental Protection Agency] RRP [Renovation, Repair, and Painting] Program, both construction firms and individual workers who perform (or direct other craft workers to perform) renovations must be certified by EPA.
  2. All craft workers must be trained on the lead-safe work practices that they must use when performing their assigned tasks.
