Iowa - Household Hazardous Materials Policy

  1. Stores located in the state of Iowa are required to post the Educational HHM Poster and have available to customers the Consumer Information Brochure:  Household Hazardous Materials.
    1. Post the Educational HHM Poster in a prominent place where customers are able to see the information- near your wrap counter or coffee station.
    2. Have the Consumer Information Brochure:  Household Hazardous Materials available for customers at the wrap counter.
    3. Replacement posters and additional brochures may be obtained from your District Office.
  2. Corporate Tax coordinates application for and holding of the permits required by Iowa for all Paint Stores and Automotive locations within Iowa.  Please contact Corporate Tax for additional information.
