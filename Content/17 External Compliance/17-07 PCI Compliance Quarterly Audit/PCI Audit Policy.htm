PCI Audit Policy

  1. All store locations utilizing POS software and hardware are required to complete a quarterly equipment review to ensure no unauthorized (rogue) or malicious equipment is attached to their Local Area Networks and that no tampering of Pax Payment Terminals has occurred from the time of the last review. 
  2. To complete the audit, the store must conduct a visual inspection and verify this inspection by completing the audit survey every quarter.
    1. Every quarter, stores will receive the audit survey via email and will be reminded to comply on a weekly basis until complete.
  3. No individuals should be permitted to physically maintain or modify PAX Payment Terminals.  All maintenance will be handled remotely through software updates or will be directed through the SCO Help Desk. 
  4. Stores without PAX Payment Terminals should comply with the following policies regarding the visual inspection:
    1. Review of equipment connections on the store network must cover all devices within the location, including: all wrap-counter POS terminal stations, the manager POS station, the tinter POS station, the floorcovering (backroom/cut machine) station, and Phoenix stations.
    2. Pole displays do not need to be counted.
  5. Stores with PAX Payment Terminals should comply with the following policies regarding the visual inspection:
    1. Review must include all PAX Payment Terminals and cabling.
