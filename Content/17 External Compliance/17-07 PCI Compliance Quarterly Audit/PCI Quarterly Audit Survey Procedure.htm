PCI Quarterly Audit Survey Procedure

Step Action

1

Every quarter, the store will be sent an email including a link to the PCI Compliance Audit Survey.

Note: If the link does not open, try copy and pasting the link location into the browser URL.

2

Provide store information, including the store number only. Do not include alpha characters (ex. 1234).

3

Answer each question and submit.

4

If unapproved (rogue) devices are found or an employee has found evidence of tampering, complete either a Follow-up Survey or be surveyed by the SCO HelpDesk by telephone.
