POS Hardware Visuals for PCI Audit Overview

This section applies only to stores without PAX Payment Terminals.

Diagrams A and B (below) show both new and dated POS equipment. Each store's equipment may differ slightly from the examples. Please use these as a guide to help to identify the store's equipment and connections.

Older Main POS Systems and Clients (IBM 6790):

Figure A.

Most of the peripherals for older stations are plugged into a device called a Hub (above).  A Hub connects to a station’s USB port, linking it to additional USB ports. Anything plugged into the Hub is an extension of the PC. 

Note: This will result in an additional cable on your total count.
