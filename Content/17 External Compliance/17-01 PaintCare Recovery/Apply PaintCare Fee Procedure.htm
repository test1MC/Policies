Apply PaintCare Fee Procedure

For stores in participating states, a PaintCare Recovery Fee will be added to the cost of paint. This Recovery Fee will fund the collection, transportation, recycling, and proper disposal of architectural paint in the state. For more information on included products, see What Product Types Are Covered.

Note the following:

  • Fees and program products will be specified on invoices for distributors and retailers.
  • PaintCare Fees are the same for each state and are assigned by sales number.
  • Although half pint and smaller containers carry no fees, they must still be reported.
