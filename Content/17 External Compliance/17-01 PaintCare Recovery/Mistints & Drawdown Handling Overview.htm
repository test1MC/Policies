Mistints & Drawdown Handling Overview

If a mistint or unused drawdown product is in program, it can go into the bin for recycling. 

Mistints and drawdowns are considered inventory and must be properly accounted for upon disposal. All mistints or drawdowns recycled through PaintCare must be recorded on the PaintCare Mistint and Drawdowns Recycling Log found in the Forms Repository.

Note: If the product is not in program, determine appropriate waste disposal and dispose of through an approved vendor.
