PaintCare Recovery Program Overview

The following information applies to all TAG locations in participating states: California, Colorado, Connecticut, Maine, Minnesota, Oregon, Rhode Island, Vermont, Washington DC. This information also applies to any Sherwin-Williams location shipping products to participating states.

The American Coatings Association (ACA) has worked with various stakeholders interested in the management of post-consumer paint to develop and implement an industry led Paint Stewardship Pilot Program in the US. ACA and its new non-profit stewardship organization–PaintCare–are in the process of implementing the program nationwide.

The Program is entirely funded by the PaintCare Recovery Fee which is paid to PaintCare by the manufacturers based on their architectural paint sales in participating states. PaintCare engages and manages all contracts for collection, transportation, recycling, and proper disposal of Program Products. PaintCare submitted a Program Plan for approval by the Department of Environmental Quality (DEQ) and will submit reports on the Program on an annual basis.

Under the PaintCare Program, paint is defined as interior and exterior coatings sold in containers of five gallons or less. It does not include industrial coatings, specialty coatings, or original equipment. Below are examples of Program Products and Non-Program Products.

Program Products (maximum container size of 5 gallons):

  • Interior and exterior paints: latex, acrylic, water-based, alkyd, oil-based, and enamel (all types of finishes and sheens, including textured coatings)
  • Deck coatings and floor paints (including elastomeric)
  • Primers, undercoaters, and sealers
  • Stains
  • Shellacs, lacquers, varnishes, and urethane (single component)
  • Waterproofing concrete/masonry/wood sealers and repellents (not tar-based or bitumen-based)
  • Swimming pool paints (single component)
  • Varnishes and urethanes (single component)
  • Metal coatings and rust preventatives

Non-Program Products (regardless of container size):

  • Industrial Maintenance (IM) coatings labeled with one of the following:
    1. For industrial use only
    2. For professional use only
    3. Not for residential use
    4. Not intended for residential use
  • OEM and industrial surface coating (shop application) paints and finishes
  • Aerosol paints (spray cans)
  • Automotive & marine paints
  • Arts & crafts paints
  • Caulking compounds, epoxies, glues, or adhesives
  • Colorants and tints
  • Resins
  • Paint thinners, mineral spirits, or solvents
  • Paint additives
  • Other chemical products (motor oil, pesticides, cleaning chemicals, etc.)
  • Roof patch or repair
  • Tar-based or bitumen-based products
  • Two-component coatings
  • Deck cleaners
  • Traffic paints
  • Wood preservatives

For more information, please contact a PaintCare representative.
