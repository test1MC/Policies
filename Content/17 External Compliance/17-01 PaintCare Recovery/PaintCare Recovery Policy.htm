PaintCare Recovery Policy

  1. All store employees in designated PaintCare facilities must be trained about PaintCare and training must be documented on the PaintCare Program Training Record for Collection Facility Staff form in the PaintCare binder. The PaintCare binder has all required training materials enclosed.
  2. The PaintCare tri-fold brochure must be displayed on the front sales counter (or another area where it is visible to customers).
  3. The collection area should not contain any non-program products. All collection and storage requirements for the state must be met.
  4. All Bills-of-Lading for PaintCare pickups must be retained.
  5. Use the CESQG log (included in the PaintCare binder) to document businesses that drop off solvent/oil based paint.
  6. All mistints and unused drawdowns recycled must be recorded on the PaintCare Mistint and Drawdowns Recycling Log.
  7. In addition to adding the PaintCare Recovery Fee to Program Products that sell in participating states, it is required to ensure that the manufacturers of any Program Products the store is selling in the state have or are participating in the Program–otherwise the products can not be sold.
  8. It is required to provide information about the Program to consumers, which is available through PaintCare.
  9. All customers, including contractors, must bring their Program Products into the collection site store to be recycled.  Picking up customers’ paint and transporting it to a collection site/store for PaintCare recycling is prohibited.
