Apply PaintCare Fee Tax Exemptions Procedure

It is possible to exempt certain customers (such as those using products in a manufacturing process) from PaintCare fees. However, exemption requires approval from the PaintCare Regulatory Team.

Step

Action

Request a PaintCare Recovery Fee Exemption:

1

Access the Forms Repository

2

Search the Form Name field for “Paintcare Recovery Fee Exemption”

3

Complete the form and fax it to 216-566-2730

Note: Retain this form indefinitely

4

The exemption will be applied to the customer’s account number by Stores IT. This usually takes a day or two, and its completion will be confirmed via email.

Note: Access the PaintCare website for state specific information on PaintCare Recovery.

Then

2
