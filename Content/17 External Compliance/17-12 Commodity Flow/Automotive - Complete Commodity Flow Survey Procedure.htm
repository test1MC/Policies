Automotive – Complete Commodity Flow Survey Procedure

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Step Action

1

Email automotive.operations@sherwin.com when the store receives a letter instructing a store employee to complete the Commodity Flow Survey, including the applicable time period of the Survey (Item C).

Note: The Automotive team will provide the Commodity Flow Survey Report for the applicable time period which the employee will use to complete the survey. Notify the Automotive team immediately upon receipt of the letter to allow time for the automotive team to process and return the report and for an employee to complete the survey by the due date noted on the letter.

2

Launch the Census website according to the instructions in the letter, using the User ID and Password provided.

3

Select the first survey that appears for the store that shows a status of Not Attempted -> Click Start.

4
Survey Item Description

Item A – Verification of Shipping Address

Verify or update address accordingly

Item B – Verification of Mailing Address

Select the option to have the remaining quarterly surveys sent to the shipping address.

Item C – Operating Status
  • Fill in survey reporting dates (week of).
  • Check the In Operation box.

Item D – Total Number of Outbound Shipments
  • Fill in survey reporting dates (week of).
  • Enter the number of outbound shipments.
  • Enter the value of outbound shipment invoices.
  • Check the applicable box to indicate if total outbound shipments were 40 or less or 41 or more.

Item E – Sampling Instructions

Check the applicable box for the total number of outbound shipments.

Note: The table indicates the number of outbound shipments to report in Item F – Shipment Characteristics.

Item F – Shipment Characteristics

Enter the total outbound information on line 1 (as noted in Item E – Sampling Instructions). This will include all shipments of 40 or less, or the highlighted shipments on the Commodity Flow Survey Report provided.

Characteristic Description / Corresponding HEARS Report Field

Shipment ID

Column B

Shipment Date

Shipment Value

Note: This information is not included on the Commodity Flow Survey Report and must be obtained from the Branch POS system or files.

Net Shipment Weight

SCTG Commodity Codes

Column F

Complete one summary line for each shipment and only list one of the two SCTG codes as noted below:

  • 23110 – Paint and Varnish (if paint, related material, consumer commodity ORM-D, Limited Quantity, UN/NA Number
  • 43999 – Mixed Freight Miscellaneous (if none of the above descriptions appear in the Shipping Description column)

Commodity Description

Column G

Complete one summary line for each shipment and list only one of the three description types as noted below (in the order as ranked):

  • Shipments with UN/NA number -> Enter only the general description noted in the Shipping Description column of the Commodity Flow Survey Report (i.e. Paint, Paint Related Material, Corrosive, Xylenes, Acetone, and Solids)
  • Shipments labeled Consumer Commodity ORM-D or Limited Quantity (no UN/NA number noted) -> Enter Paint as the description.
  • Any shipment that does not include a UN/NA Number or Consumer Commodity ORM-D or Limited Quantity -> Enter Mixed Freight Miscellaneous as the description.

Item G – Verification of Primary Industry Activity

Enter Yes when prompted for the primary activity.

Item H – Time to Complete Survey

Enter the amount of time required to complete the survey.

Item I – Contact Information

Enter the information for the Branch Manager.

Item J -- Remarks

Leave this section blank.

5

Print a copy of the survey for the store's records.

6

Notify automotive.operations@sherwin.com that the survey is complete.
