Complete Commodity Flow Survey Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

Step

Action

1

Notify your DM when you receive a letter instructing you to complete the Commodity Flow Survey.

2

To complete the survey, you will need to generate a HEARS Report. Launch the Source homepage -> HEARS on the Web.

3

Select The Department of Commerce (under the Reporting Header).

4

Enter your store/facility number and the dates provided in the commodity flow survey request letter.

Note: The report will take a couple of minutes to process. Once complete, it will appear in your HEARS inbox.

5

Locate the generated report in the HEARS inbox (the folder icon in the top right hand corner).

6

Select the Export in Excel button.

7

The exported excel document will have everything required to complete the survey except for shipment value.  Use the control number provided in Column E to locate the shipment value via Inquiry [F4] -> Inquiry Menu.

Note: The control number will be 1-6 digits depending on how many Customer Orders your store has processed. It does not require leading zero’s and is preceded by the letters OE, unless the shipment was an IST.

8

Write down the shipment values as they correspond to each shipment.

9

Launch the Census website according to the instructions in the letter, using the User ID and Password provided.

10

Select the first survey that appears for your store that shows a status of Not Attempted -> Click Start.

11

Confirm or edit the Store Address and Attention Name fields.

Note: Attention Name should be the current store manager.

12

Answer the questions about the total number of outbound shipments and the total value of the shipments.  This can be done by counting the number of individual BOL codes in Column D and summing up the value of the shipments you recorded via Inquiry [F4].

13

Continue to the next section -> Select Option 1 (Report using an Excel Spreadsheet)  -> Select Download Spreadsheet

14

Complete the Commodity Flow Survey spreadsheet, using the corresponding information on the report you generated from HEARS, where applicable:

15

Save the Commodity Flow Survey spreadsheet -> return to the browser -> Select Upload Spreadsheet -> Browse for and select the saved Commodity Flow Survey file -> Click Next.

16

Select Finalize Shipments.

17

Follow the instructions on the website, answering survey questions to the best of your knowledge.

18

On the final page, verify that there are no issues with your responses and then Submit.

19

Print a copy of the survey for your records.

20

Notify your DM that you have completed the survey for the quarter.
