Commodity Flow Survey Overview

The Commodity Flow Survey (CFS) provides important statistics about the movement of goods in the United States and how businesses use transportation to ship these goods. This data is used by policy makers and transportation planners to make informed decisions for improving transportation infrastructure.

If selected for the survey, participation is required quarterly for the span of one year. Once notified that the store has been selected to participate in the survey, follow the Complete Commodity Flow Survey Procedure.

Contact stores.operations@sherwin.com for any questions regarding the Commodity Flow Survey.
