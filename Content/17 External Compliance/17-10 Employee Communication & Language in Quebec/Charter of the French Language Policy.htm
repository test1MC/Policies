Charter of the French Language Policy

  1. All communications (to either customers or employees) created at the store level must be in French. This includes, without limitation, all bulletin board and back office postings, signs, and messages.
  2. All written communications between employees at the store level must be in French. This includes handwritten notes.
  3. All communications received from Corporate should be distributed or published to employees in French.   If received in English, do not distribute or post.  Contact the sender to request a French language translation for distribution.
