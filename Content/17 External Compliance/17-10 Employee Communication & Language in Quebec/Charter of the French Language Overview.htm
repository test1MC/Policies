Charter of the French Language Overview

In compliance with French language laws, Sherwin-Williams is committed to providing customers and employees in Québec with exceptional service and work environment  through support of the French language as the first language of Québec.  To demonstrate Sherwin-Williams' commitment to preserving the vitality of French as the first language of Québec, SW strives to comply with the Charter of the French Language in the context of our operations in Québec. 

Employees working within and supporting operations in Québec should be aware that compliance is required for Sherwin-Williams to operate in this province.  Failure to comply could affect Sherwin- Williams' ability to bid on provincial/municipal projects and result in the incurrence of fines.
