Shelf Label Stock Policy

  1. The Store Manager must verify that all merchandise is labeled with the correct sales number and selling price (unless a shelf label is in use and the product is bar coded).
  2. When a price increase occurs on merchandise, the Store Manager must make sure that all existing stock is updated with the most recent Shelf Label or Individual Price Sticker. Inaccurate Shelf Labels or Individually Priced Items could result in fines levied against the store.
