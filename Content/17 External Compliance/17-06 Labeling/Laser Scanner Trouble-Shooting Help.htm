Laser Scanner Trouble-Shooting Help

When using the Laser Scanner, store employees may occasionally encounter a number that is not in the system. Products that do not scan and have a SW sales number and a UPC code on the package should be documented on the Universal Product Code – Add Form. Immediate attention will ensure that bar codes remain accurate in the Store Computer System. Mail or fax the completed form to the address displayed on the bottom of the form. Please use a photocopy if additional copies are needed.

Scenario

Response

Sometimes it’s easier to enter the Sales Number

The Laser Scanner is to be used as a tool to assist in processing customer transactions. Laser Scanners guarantee accuracy, so use them whenever possible.

The cord is not long enough to reach 5 gallon containers

When necessary, manually enter Sales Numbers.

It always takes several attempts for the Laser Scanner to read the Bar Code

This can occur when the Laser Scanner is not being held properly. Hold the Laser Scanner at a slight angle to the Bar Code. Pull the trigger, move the Laser Scanner close, then pull back until the Sales Number registers.

The paint is usually in the shaker when an employee is ringing up sales

The Laser Scanner is most beneficial when a customer is purchasing associated products, brushes, and rollers. Choose to scan the paint after it comes out of the shaker or enter the sales number manually while the paint is shaking.
