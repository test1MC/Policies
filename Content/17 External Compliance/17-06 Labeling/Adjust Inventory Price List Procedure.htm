Adjust Inventory Price List Procedure

The selling price for locally purchased stock merchandise is placed in a special Inventory Price List. The selling price entered on the Purchase Order is used to price all customers. It is required to correct the selling price on the Inventory Price List before printing Shelf Labels. This will ensure that the Shelf Labels and the Inventory Price List match.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management.

2

Select Maintenance.

3

Select Inventory Price List.

4

To modify the manufacturer’s suggested selling (list) price originally entered on the Purchase Order:

Press [End] to move to a blank line.

  • Input the sales number for the product that needs adjusting and press [Enter]. The system will queue the appropriate line to begin the adjustment process.
  • Change the selling price and press [Enter]. Press F1 [ACCEPT] when the selling price has been adjusted.
  • The new sell price will be used to price all sales transactions. After correcting the selling price, create an accurate Shelf Label for the product.
