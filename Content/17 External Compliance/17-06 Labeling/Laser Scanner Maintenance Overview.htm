Laser Scanner Maintenance Overview

A dirty window may affect scanning accuracy. Cleaning the exit window on the Laser Scanner is the only maintenance required. To keep clean:

  • Do not allow any abrasive material to touch the window.
  • Remove any dirt particles with a damp cloth.
  • Wipe the window using a tissue moistened with ammonia/water.
  • Do not spray water or other cleaning liquid directly onto the window.
