Order Monthly Maintenance Labels Procedure

Maintenance labels are run on the 15th of each month. They are printed and mailed to arrive before the first of the preceding month. This means new products sold/received in the store after the 15th of the month will not be included. To prevent confusion when updating labels, dispose of all old, unused labels once you receive your monthly package.

Appoint someone in the store to routinely maintain Shelf Labels. It is critical that Maintenance Labels be put in place when they are received, but not before the Shelf Label Effective Date.

  • Check the Effective Date on Shelf Labels. If the Effective Date has passed, immediately remove and replace any updated Labels.
  • If the Effective Date is in the future, set aside the maintenance Shelf Labels until the Effective Date. Remove and replace the updated Shelf Labels on the Effective Date.
  • Verify that all locations have updated Shelf Labels.

Step

Action

1

A monthly maintenance package of labels will be sent to the store for all products in which:

  • A product is added to the perpetual inventory, received from a DSC or IST, or sold in the prior month.
  • The product description, size code description, UPC Code, or List Price has changed since the previous month.

2

If a Shelf Label is unavailable in the monthly maintenance label package, there are 3 options:

  • Make a photocopy of an existing label and place the photocopied Shelf Label in front of the product until the store receives new labels.
  • Create a new shelf label from Shelf Labels on Demand.
  • Individually price each item with a Price Sticker.

3

Occasionally, the store may need replacement labels from Headquarters due to damage or additional stocking locations. Please complete a Shelf Label Order Form and fax it to the number on the bottom of the form. Find the Shelf Label form in the Forms Repository.

Search form name or keyword “shelf” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing the search word(s) will appear below in the form description field.

The store can also Order Blank Label Stock. Either request a full set of shelf pricing labels for the store, or request a set of shelf labels for a specific product department.

For example: With Department  11 - Architectural Products, the store can request labels for several product departments on the Shelf Label Order form.
