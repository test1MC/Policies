Plan-O-Gram Labels Overview

Labels will be printed in Plan-O-Gram sequence and identified by Plan-O-Gram page numbers. The store will not receive a label if the product is not in the perpetual inventory OR if the store has not received or sold the product three months prior to printing. If a product is in multiple locations in the Plan-O-Gram, labels are printed for each location.

For Non Plan-O-Gram Labels:

Additional labels are printed in size code/sales number sequence for all Architectural Equipment, Brushes, Rollers, Associated Products, and Spray Equipment. The store will not receive a label if the product is not in the perpetual inventory OR if the store has not received or sold the product three months prior to printing. Labels for a select list of Industrial Maintenance and Chemical Coatings products that could end up on the sales floor will be printed in size code/sales number sequence.

For products in the Plan-O-Gram, the store will receive a quantity of labels corresponding to the number of product facings in the Plan-O-Gram. Non Plan-O-Gram quantities are based on the chart below:
