LabelMaster PQR Shipping Procedure

Step Action

1

Order one PQR kit for Non-Regulated paint from Labelmaster (form found in the Forms Repository).

2

Record the PQR number on the can.

3

Once you receive the kit, place the can clips/lid ring on the can and put the cans in the bag using the twist tie to close it.

4

Erect the box from the kit using heavy-duty tape.

5

Place the bagged can inside the  box.

Note: Use cardboard or foam to fill empty space to secure the can from moving during transport.

6

Close and seal the box using the heavy-duty tape.

7

Using a black marker, write the Contact's Name and address on top of the box and record the PQR# on the side of the box in the space provided.

8

Weigh  the box and record the weight on the BOL or look up the weight on HEARS.

9

On a Manual BOL (found in the Forms Repository), write the description, "PAINT AND RELATED MATERIAL,"  in a line on the lower right-hand section and record the appropriate information on the corresponding lines (carton, number of cartons,  weight) .

Note: Do not mark this as hazardous.

10

Be sure that all the required information has been completed on the BOL, sign, and make 3 copies.

11

Arrange for a Ground Service pickup through your FedEx Online Account at www.fedex.com:

  • User ID: SW9708fdx(substitute your 4 digit store #)
  • Password: Sherw1234

Note: If this UN/Password combination does not work, please email methods@sherwin.com.

12

Print the FedEx label/barcode and attach it to the box.

13

Complete the PQR Shipping Log located under the Technical Review Tab.

Note:  For all other Solvent/ALKYD/OIL based paints, please email Shay.roseman@sherwin.com the container size (quarts, single, and five gallon pail) and the product/REX number to obtain shipping instructions. These products may require special shipping instructions, please do not skip this step.
