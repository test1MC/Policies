Caribbean Export Overview

Shipments originating from U.S. warehouses can now be shipped directly to customers’ U.S. freight forwarders. The store’s customer will then coordinate shipments to be imported into their country and delivered to their location. This process ensures:

  • U.S. Export Declaration (EEI) is filed using the correct information
  • U.S. Export Declaration ITN Number is retained for proper record keeping
  • Customer does not have access to the product’s Store Costs
  • Correct documentation is used at the time for importation
