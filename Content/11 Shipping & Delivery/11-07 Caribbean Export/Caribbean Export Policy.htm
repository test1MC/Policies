Caribbean Export Policy

  1. This procedure applies to Caribbean stores when the final destination is not located on the same country or island as the servicing Sherwin-Williams store.
  2. If the customer is located in the same island or country as the servicing store, the normal SW approved forwarders and brokers should be used to import the shipment while additional communication from the servicing store directs them to reconsign the shipment to the final destination.
  3. This procedure does not apply to Puerto Rico, where our normal less-than-truckload carriers provide door to door service.
