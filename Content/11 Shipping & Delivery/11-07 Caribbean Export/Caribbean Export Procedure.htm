Caribbean Export Procedure

Step Action

1

Identify an applicable drop shipment from a US warehouse.

2

Create a customer direct purchase order with the appropriate U.S. freight forwarder.

3

Release the customer direct purchase order via EDI.

4

Create an international drop ship notification email noting the following:

  • Customer direct purchase order number
  • U.S. freight forwarder  name and address
  • U.S. freight forwarder contact name, phone number, and email information
  • Final destination contact name, address, phone number, and email

Note: Please confirm who will be the Importer of record: SW Store or Customer.

5

Send the international drop ship notification email to: stores.caribbean.cs@sherwin.com

Note: Once the Stores Caribbean Customer Service team reviews the shipment and the order, they will advise regarding availability issues. Once the order leaves the warehouse, Customer Service will inform the store about which products were included in the shipment.

6

Use the product list to create a commercial invoice.

Note: The customer will use this invoice for shipment importation.
