Driver Titles Overview

In order to enable upgraded technology, new driver titles were developed based on Driver’s Qualifications. These Driver Qualifications are based on the type of vehicle being driven and type of load being transported. There are two sets of titles, one for retail stores with drivers and one for Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Drivers.
