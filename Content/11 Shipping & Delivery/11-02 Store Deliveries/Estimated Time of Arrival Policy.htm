Estimated Time of Arrival Policy

You are able to check the status of a delivery directly from the system. Note the following policy on estimated time of arrival:

  1. The Delivery Status feature provides an estimated time only. Delivery is only guaranteed to arrive within the time dictated by the order cut-off selected unless you have been notified otherwise by Central Dispatch.

To check the status of a delivery:

  • From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Submit Order for Delivery -> Check Status of Delivery Orders
  • If you currently have orders which have been submitted for delivery, their current delivery status will display along with an estimated time of arrival.
