Mileage Entry Procedure

The Delivery and Pickup Schedule Mileage Entry must be completed once the route has been completed. The driver, Store Manager, or Assistant Manager may complete mileage entry. The driver will need to produce all COD funds and receipts for any fuel or miscellaneous expenses incurred.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Delivery & Pickup Schedule -> Delivery & Pickup Schedule Mileage Entry.

2

Enter control number of the route or press LOOKUP [F3]. Press Enter.

3

Information for each stop on the Schedule must be recorded: actual piece count delivered, COD amount collected, and the odometer reading for each stop. Press ACCEPT [F1] once information has been recorded for each stop.

4

In the Mileage Entry Screen, complete the following required information:

  • Vehicle Time In
  • Vehicle Time Out
  • Beginning Mileage
  • Ending Mileage
  • Gas Expenses [Y/N]
  • Miscellaneous Travel Expenses [Y/N]

Press ACCEPT [F1] to complete the Mileage Entry. If you are a Manager [MMS Security Level 4], you will also be prompted at this time to approve the Mileage entries.
