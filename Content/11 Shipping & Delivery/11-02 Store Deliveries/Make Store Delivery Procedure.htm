Make Store Delivery Procedure

Step

Action

Log On (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

1

Select MobileCast from the main screen of the mobile device.

2

Enter your MySherwin ID  in the ID Field. Leave the Password Field blank. Select Log On. The route created for you by dispatch should display.

Preview Route (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

3

Drivers may preview their daily route before beginning their day. They should always be aware that this is just a preview; new stops will be added throughout the day, which may affect the original order.

4

Select Menu -> Preview Route or Stops.

6

Highlight a stop and select Details to preview detailed information for the stop. Once completed, select Return to go back to all stops on the route.

7

When exiting the preview, select Reset Route.

Load Route (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

8

Select Load Route to display the Ready to Start Route screen.

9

Select Start to show the Ready to Depart screen.

10

Select Depart to leave the SW Delivery Terminal for the first stop.

Use Route GPS (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

12

GPS should be used to navigate to all stops since it is configured to select the most time and fuel efficient route. 

Note: Remember that you must be outside in order for the GPS to function.

13

While proceeding to a stop, select Menu -> Navigate. The GPS will provide voice and image directions to the stop.

15

To stop using GPS, select Menu -> Hide Copilot.  Do not select Quiet Copilot.

Geocode Location (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

16

The Geocode function identifies a location with a bad or non-exact address so that your GPS will take you to the coordinates you identify for the location rather than the provided address for the next delivery to the customer.

17

After arriving at the location, select Arrive -> Menu -> Geocode -> Yes.

Dispatch Messages (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

20

To view messages received from Dispatch on the mobile device:

  • Select Menu -> Inbox, then Acknowledge to verify receipt of it.

21

To send messages to Dispatch on the mobile device:

  • Select Menu -> Send Text Message, then Send once completed.

Order Pick-Up

23

Verify that the store has provided the correct paperwork for each order:

  • Customer Orders: Two copies of the Confirmation of Delivery Ticket (non Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service orders only) and two copies of the Bill-of-Lading.
  • IST: Copy of the IST Transaction and two copies of the Bill-of-Lading.

Sign the third copy of the Bill-of-Lading for each order/IST and return this to the employee assisting you at the store.

24

Crosscheck the paperwork and the products to be delivered for verification.

25

Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Drivers Only:

  • Select arrive immediately upon reaching destination.
  • Take the mobile device into the delivery with you.
  • Verify that the order number on the delivery paperwork matches that noted in the mobile device for the stop.
  • Capture the store staff signature. Select Menu -> Signature.  Enter the employee’s name and have them sign. Select Save.
  • Once the pick-up is complete, select Depart to proceed.

Delivery

26

Obtain the customer or store employee signature:

  • Non-Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service:  Gather signatures on the store copy of the Confirmation of Delivery ticket or IST/ISR.
  • Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Drivers Only: The MC75 should be used to gather signatures. Follow the procedure in Step 25.

27

Give the customer the unsigned copy of the Confirmation of Delivery Ticket (non Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service orders) and their copy of the BOL.  If delivery is for an IST, provide the store with a copy.

Undeliverable Order (Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service only)

28

Contact Dispatch immediately for instructions on how to proceed.

Note: Remember that you are never permitted to leave orders unsigned for unless an Authorization & Release Form is on file at the originating store.

29

On the mobile device, select Arrive -> Menu -> Undeliverable Stop.

30
If Then
You still plan to make the stop at the end of your route Select YES
You will not be delivering the order Select NO

Note: You MUST have approval from Dispatch before selecting this option.

Post Delivery

33

TAG Stores NOT participating in the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service ONLY:

Return signed Confirmation of Delivery Tickets, ISTs, COD funds collected, and receipts for all expenses (fuel purchases and miscellaneous expenses) with the Daily Delivery and Pickup Schedule form to the Store Manager.

35

TAG Stores participating in the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service ONLY:

  • The electronic signature will be forwarded by the MC75 to the POS of the originating store. All other documentation, such as ISTs and receipts for all expenses should be returned to the SW Delivery Terminal Manager or Lead Driver. Signed documents will be organized and returned to the originating store via IST, postal service, or via regular business efforts. Signed documents will be filed and returned back to the originating store.
  • After the last stop, return to the Delivery Terminal. Select Proceed to Depot and then Arrive.
  • Select Complete at the Ready to Complete Route screen.
