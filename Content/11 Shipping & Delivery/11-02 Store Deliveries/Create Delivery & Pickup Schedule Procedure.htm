Create Delivery & Pickup Schedule Procedure

This section does NOT apply to TAG Stores participating in the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service.

The daily Delivery and Pickup Schedule allows users to schedule customer orders for delivery and ISTs for a delivery route. Users are also able to schedule miscellaneous stops to accommodate situations that may arise, such as a customer return from a job site. The Delivery & Pickup Schedule can be created and managed on POS. If the system is unavailable, the manual Delivery & Pickup Schedule (located in the Forms Repository) may be used. Open Delivery Schedules created in the system display in the Status Reminder Box.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Delivery Menu -> Internal Vehicle Delivery & Pickup Schedule -> Create Delivery and Pickup Schedule.

Note: The Delivery and Pickup Schedule header pager will include default information, which may be overridden, if needed.

2

The Driver's Name Field must be completed. Use LOOKUP [F3] to select an employee. Press Enter to continue.

3

View ISTs and ISRs:

If

Then

You wish to include ISTs and ISRs

Choose YES and select a Date Range. You will view ISTs, ISRs, and Customer Orders (only Customer Orders that have been marked for Delivery and have a Ready for Delivery Status) within the Date Range at this time. You will be able to save the Schedule without releasing it. Additional items may be included using the Delivery & Pickup Schedule Release/Print option.

You only wish to view customer orders marked for delivery

Choose NO. You will only be able to select orders which have been marked for delivery and are in a Ready for Delivery Status.

Note: You can save the Schedule without releasing it. Additional items may be included using the Delivery & Pickup Schedule Release/Print option.

4

Scroll through the listing of available ISTs/ISRs and/or customer orders displayed in the uppermost window. Products included on the order will display in the middle window.

5

To select an order to include, press Enter. Orders selected for the schedule will display in the lowermost window with the stop number noted.

6

Edit the schedule by moving to the lower window, and then use the ARROW keys to navigate to specific fields:

  • Delete an unwanted entry using the DELETE key.
  • To change schedule stop order, press Enter and type in the new stop number. All other stops will adjust accordingly. Schedule stops logically by customer requested delivery time and location.
  • Add a note or comment to a stop by pressing Comment [F9].  Comments will appear on the printed schedule for the driver.
  • Miscellaneous stops may be added to the schedule. This function may be used when stopping at a location not related to a Customer Order or an IST/ISR.

7

Press VIEW [F7]. Indicate if the stop is a Delivery or Pickup.

8

Additional required information: Store/Customer Name, City, and Comments. Press ACCEPT [F1] to complete.

9

Enter the Piece Count, Bill-of-Lading Number, and Estimated Cash on Delivery [COD] Amount for each stop. Piece Count is required for all Customer Order stops, but not for ISTs/ISRs. Bill-of-Lading Number and Cash on Delivery amount should be completed as appropriate for each stop.

Note: If a Cash Account, you will be prompted to indicate if a Cash on Delivery amount should be collected.

10

If the Schedule is complete, choose YES to release the Schedule and generate the printed Delivery and Pickup Schedule for the driver; no further changes will be allowed once the Schedule has been released.

Note: Additions and changes may be made to the Schedule before releasing it. Use the Delivery & Pickup Schedule Release/Print screen to query for and adjust/release a schedule.
