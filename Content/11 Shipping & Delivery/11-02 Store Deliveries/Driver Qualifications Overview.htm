Driver Qualifications Overview

Title

Qualifications

.Driver..I.
  • A legible photocopy of the Driver’s License
  • 21 years old

..II DOT Med
  • A legible photocopy of their Driver’s License (Front and Back)
  • A legible copy of their DOT Medical Card
  • 21 years old

..III.CDL/Hazmat
  • A legible photocopy of their Driver’s License (Front and Back), clearly denoting the H endorsement
  • A legible copy of the DOT Med card
  • Supplemental CDL Application (SWTS-1)
  • Certification of Violations (SWTS-2)
  • DOT Drug and Safety Release (SWTS-3)
  • 21 years old

Note: Driver Qualifications are the same for both SWDS and non SWDS drivers. For questions, contact TAG Transportation and Safety Department at 216-566-2319 or shay.roseman@sherwin.com
