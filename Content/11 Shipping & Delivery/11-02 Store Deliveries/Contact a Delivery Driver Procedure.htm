Contact a Delivery Driver Procedure

Step

Action

1

To contact the driver, call Dispatch at 877-707-8116 and follow the prompts.

2

Instruct Dispatch to have the driver call your store whenever safely possible.

3

Dispatch will send a text message to the driver’s phone, which can only be received and read when the vehicle is stopped.

Note: For safety reasons, stores/reps/customers are not allowed to call or text drivers at any time.
