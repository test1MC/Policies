Approve or Cancel Delivery & Pickup Schedule Procedure

This section does NOT apply to TAG Stores participating in the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service.

The Delivery and Pickup Schedule must be approved by the Store Manager or another manager with MMS Security Level 4 once the Delivery and Pickup Schedule Mileage Entry has been completed.

  • If the Store Manager is completing the Mileage Entry, a prompt to approve the Delivery and Pickup Schedule will appear as part of the Mileage Entry. The Delivery and Pickup Schedule should not be approved until the manager has received the completed Delivery and Pickup Schedule, all signed Confirmation of Delivery Tickets, signed ISTs, COD funds, and receipts for any fuel or miscellaneous expenses incurred.
  • If there are released schedules in the system, which are older than twenty-four hours, a count of those schedules will display in the Status Reminder Box.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Delivery Menu -> Internal Vehicle Delivery & Pickup Schedule.

2

Enter the control number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search. Press ENTER.

If Then
Approving

Select Approve Delivery and Pickup Schedules -> Enter the control number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search. Press ENTER
->Proceed to Step 3.
Canceling

Select Cancel Unnecessary Delivery & Pickup Schedules -> Query for the schedule to be adjusted or released. Press ACCEPT [F1]
-> Choose the schedule to be canceled. Select YES to confirm the deletion -> Enter a reason for the cancellation.  Press ACCEPT [F1]
-> Procedure is complete.

3

Review the Schedule and Mileage Entry information:

If

Then

Edits are required

These cannot be made through the Approval function and must be made by using the Delivery and Pickup Schedule Mileage Entry function. If this process is used, the approval may also be completed through the Delivery and Pickup Schedule Mileage Entry function.

No edits are required

Approve the Schedule. Once the Schedule has been approved, no further changes may be made.

4

Print a final Daily Delivery & Pickup Schedule from the Release/Print option.

Note: This document will display all stop information, including: Customer Order or IST/ISR Number, Piece Count per stop, COD Amount collected per stop, Odometer reading at each stop, Vehicle Time In/Out, Beginning/Ending Mileage, and any Fuel/Miscellaneous Expenses. It should be signed by the Driver and Store Manager and filed in Daily Delivery and Pickup Schedule.
