Air/Cargo Shipments Policy

  1. Federal law and Sherwin-Williams' policy prohibit the shipment of any paint, coatings, aerosol, or paint-related material (including powder), regardless of quantity or size, by air or sea unless you have been properly trained and certified. This prohibition includes the use of any air delivery or express service offered by such companies as the U.S. Postal Service, Federal Express, or UPS.  
  2. Allowed training and certifications for the shipment of product by air or sea include the SW IATA Program or the IMDG Cargo Shipping Program. Other courses may be approved by the Corporate Director of Transportation.
  3. Copies of certifications received through the aforementioned approved courses must be on file with both Stores Safety & Environmental and Corporate Transportation before any air or cargo shipment may be approved.
  4. Stores wishing to ship product by air or sea are required to:
    1. Have District Manager approval for each shipment.
    2. Have prior approval from Stores Safety & Environmental at 216-566-2319.
    3. Annually refresh the aforementioned training.
    4. No shipment of a hazardous material by air may be made by any store without prior approval from stores environmental.
  5. Failure to comply with Sherwin-Williams’ policy on air shipments and/or cargo shipments of product can subject you to disciplinary action, including dismissal from the company. You and the company may also be subject to criminal and civil penalties.
