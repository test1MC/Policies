Material Safety Data Sheets (SDS) Policy

  1. Upon request from a commercial customer, an employee, or a government representative, a copy of the product's current SDS shall be provided.
  2. An "SDSs Are Available" sign shall be posted in ALL stores in the sales floor area.
  3. SDSs shall be readily accessible to ALL store employees during their work shifts. All  employees should be trained on how to access and print SDS on demand via the SDS/Data Page Quick Link on the SOURCE Intranet.
  4. ALL store employees shall be trained in the use and understanding of the information presented on the SDS upon initial employment and when a new chemical is introduced into the workplace.
