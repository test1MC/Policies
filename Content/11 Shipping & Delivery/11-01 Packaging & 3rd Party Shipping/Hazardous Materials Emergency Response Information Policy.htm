Hazardous Materials Emergency Response Information Policy

  1. All stores that transport or offer for transport, hazardous materials, regardless of the quantity, must have available for immediate use emergency response information.
    1. Material Safety Data Sheets (SDS) must be readily available for emergency responses on the store's premises or for customer use.  Note that SDS sheets may be automatically printed for all products in a shipment when using BOLDOC to create a bill-of-lading.
    2. The North American Emergency Response Guidebook, version 2016 (NAERG) must be maintained in the store transport vehicle while transporting the hazardous materials.
      1. Contact Fleet Operations at 855-373-1763 for a new ERG. The ERG is published every 4 years.
    3. The guidebook must be maintained in the same manner as the hazardous materials shipping papers.
