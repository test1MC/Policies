Export Orders Procedure

To mark an order as export in POS:

Step

 

 Action

1

 

From POS desktop, select Store ->  Customer Order Entry -> Add Order.

2

Select Delivery for Export from Customer Service Options.

3

When prompted, type Y to confirm that the order is for export.

4

When prompted, enter Special Instructions for the order to note the final destination.  Use format:  Final Destination: XXX.  Press ACCEPT [F1].

5

 

Submit the export order for review to GS Customer Service.

Recognize ITAR Products in POS:

COE

Pop-up indicator for Defense Article Products

POS

Pop-up indicator for Defense Article Products

Receipts

POS sale invoice printed message when Defense Article is sold

Intermixes, Store Expenses, & IBARs

No pop-up indicator at this time- manual review of controlled list needed.

Process an export order with Customer Service:

Step Who Action

1

Store

Identify if store will be the Shipper of Record or USPPI for the shipment.

  • Is the store billing a US company?
  • Is there a US address as billing party on invoices?
  • Are funds for the transaction being received from a US company?
If Then
Yes The transaction is a domestic sale and the US customer will need to be the Export of Record. Proceed with the shipment.
No Proceed to Step 2.

2

Store

Submit CS002 Form to GSC Export Customer Service at stores.export@sherwin.com.  Identify any licensable items on the form.

Note: For new opportunities, District Manager approval is required.

3

Customer Service

GSC Customer Service will review the order for Denied Parties and Chemical Inventories. If any licensable products are on the order, GSC will guide store though process.

Note: This process can take 4+ weeks to complete depending on situation.

4

Customer Service

GSC will send communicate to the store via email indicating:

  • All checks made
  • Shipment (or certain products) can continue
  • Shipment (or certain products) cannot continue
  • Requests for additional information

Note: Initial response back within twenty-four hours, final approval up to two weeks +

5

Store

Store will create needed Export Documents such as Commercial Invoice and packing list.

6

Store

Determine who has the responsibility of filing the shipment with the US Census (Foreign Customer or Sherwin-Williams) and mark appropriately on Commercial Invoice.

7

Store

Is the final destination of the shipment Canada?

If Then
Yes

Provide Export Documents to the freight forwarder with instructions to:

  • File AES with Census if applicable.
  • Provide AES Report and ITN number to store for proper record keeping and audit.
No Ensure inclusion of the ITN number and Anti-Diversionary statement on the roadway BOL as stated on the Commercial Invoice. Commercial Invoice must travel with the shipment.
