Freight Forwarders Overview

By using only approved freight forwarders and repackers we gain:

  • Established and documented processes – ensure compliance with export regulations.
  • Double and triple checks at their sites in order to ensure packaging, labeling and pallets types are all correct.
  • Gain expertise on how to properly block and brace our shipments within containers.
  • Gain leverage when negotiating contracts and gain preferred lanes.

Broker/Freight Forwarders are approved by:

  • Director of Trade Compliance, Regulatory
  • Director of Transportation, GCS

To request a Freight Forwarder be added to the approved list, send request with a detailed business reason to stores.export@sherwin.com.
