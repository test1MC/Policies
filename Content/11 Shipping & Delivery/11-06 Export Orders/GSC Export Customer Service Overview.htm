GSC Export Customer Service Overview

GSC Export Customer Service helps facilitate the required checks for the export shipment based on a store’s completion of the CS002 Form. They will provide the following support:

  • Chemical Inventory Review
  • Denied Party Screening (DPL)
  • Embargoed country list
  • Review for red flag indicators
  • Business Decision Checks
  • Export licensing requirements (GSC will guide through entire process)
  • Support Concerning Export Process Matters

Contact GSC Customer Service at stores.export@sherwin.com.
