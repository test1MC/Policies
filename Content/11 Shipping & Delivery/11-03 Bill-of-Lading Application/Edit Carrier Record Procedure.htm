Edit Carrier Record Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Manager Functions -> File Maintenance -> Carriers -> Change.

2

Enter the 4 position alphanumeric SCAC or press Lookup [F3].

3

Edit the record as required:

If Then

CTS (SCAC Code: YFSY)

Enter a Preferred Status of YES and Shipment Terms of Prepaid.

Yellow Freight (SCAC Code:  YFSY)

Enter a Preferred Status of YES and Shipment Terms of Prepaid.

A Local Common Carrier

Enter a Preferred Status of YES and default their Shipping Terms to the appropriate value.

Press ACCEPT [F1] to save the changes.
