BOLDOC Overview

BOLDOC is an intranet application accessible only from within Customer Order Entry or where the system prompts exist in certain transactions.  There are no intranet links to this application. Access to BOLDOC is allowed even when another user is browsing via SOURCE.  Also, multiple sessions of BOLDOC may be open at the same time under different user logons.  Here are the best ways to access BOLDOC depending on the nature of the Bill-of-Lading request:

If Then

Submitting items from a Customer Delivery Order (Transfer and Expense, included), Customer Request for an in-store Pickup, or Returns

Use the Create Bill of Lading Request option from the Bill of Lading Menu. Whenever a BOL is submitted to BOLDOC through the Create Bill of Lading Request option, you may either open BOLDOC at that point or choose to continue submitting requests and to open it at a later point.

Processing either an ISR or an IBAR

Open BOLDOC once the IST or IBAR is finalized.

Note: IBARs are not submitted to BOLDOC through the Create Bill of Lading Request method.  The only method of submission is through the prompt.

ISTs may be submitted to BOLDOC through the Create Bill of Lading Request method; this method should only be used if the aforementioned prompt is missed in the IST Transaction.

Note: The Open BOLDOC option on the Bill-of-Lading Menu takes the user directly to the BOLDOC application without having to submit a request. All previously submitted BOL requests may be reviewed/edited using Open BOLDOC.
