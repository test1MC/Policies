Limited Quantity Procedure 

This applies to Canadian facilities only.

At the request of Corporate Regulatory Affairs Transportation and the Canadian Business Unit, HEARS IT has implemented a Limited Quantity functionality in the BOLDOC program for Canadian Facilities. Canadian regulations have recognized the limited quantity shipping exception for some time but the Canadian stores have not had access to the Limited Quantity functionality in BOLDOC.  Use of the Limited Quantity exception provides both financial and regulatory advantages. With this improvement, we are able to apply the Limited Quantity exception to qualified goods regardless of whether they are of US or Canadian manufacture, thereby streamlining shipments of goods between the United States and Canada.  Use of the Limited Quantity exception on qualified goods in Canada will also provide financial benefits as the goods will not incur a hazardous materials shipping surcharge.

Step Action

1

With a shipment in a status of Confirmed first select Canadian Land from the regulatory rule drop down box.

2

Check Enable limited quantity to enable Limited Quantity.

3

Select OK.
