HEARS Bill-of-Lading Application Overview

Bills of Lading may be automatically created from SOURCE. Through SOURCE, a user may submit order information to BOLDOC. This information is used by BOLDOC to generate a preliminary Bill-of-Lading document which must be reviewed and edited in BOLDOC before a final Bill-of-Lading may be generated and printed.  BOLDOC provides increased Bill-of-Lading accuracy and an automated and streamlined method to creating BOL documentation for shipments.  Among the benefits of BOLDOC are:

  • Automatic updates of current BOL Codes
  • Single source document for both Bills-of-Lading and Hazardous Materials Shipping Papers
  • Capture of all required information leads to increased accuracy and efficiency in BOL generation
  • Automated Preliminary BOL Generation
  • Options to generate Carton Markings and SDS papers for shipments
  • Protects Sherwin-Williams and its stores by ensuring that all shipments under store transport are correctly documented

Review the on-line BOLDOC Training for a quick review of basic Bill-of-Lading functionality.
