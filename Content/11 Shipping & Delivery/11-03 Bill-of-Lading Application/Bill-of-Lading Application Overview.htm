Bill-of-Lading Application Overview

To ensure that products under our transport are properly packaged, documented, and compliant with federal and state regulations, HEARS Group developed the Automated Bill-of-Lading Document Application (BOLDOC) as Sherwin-Williams' transport documentation solution. This single source document captures all required information for both Bills-of-Ladings and Hazardous Materials Shipping Papers in order to increase accuracy and efficiency. When the store gets busy, time to create good paperwork may seem to be a loss of time and productivity. But, when needed, a complete and correct Bill-of-Lading will:

  • Save the Sherwin-Williams' company from receiving hefty fines.
  • Protect employee drivers from fines and legal action, including possible loss of commercial license.
  • Aid health-care and emergency workers responding to the scene of an accident.
  • Help to prevent negative press that may accompany accidents involving potentially harmful product.

A good Bill-of-Lading protects company assets and reputation, employee health and livelihood, and shows the Sherwin-Williams Company espousing high standards of civic responsibility.
