Vehicle Accident Reporting Procedure

Step

Action

1

Attend to first aid of others and call local police.

2

Do not admit fault nor discuss the accident with anyone except the police.

3

Obtain complete identity, vehicle identification, and insurance information of drivers and passengers of other vehicles and your store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicle.

Note: Refer to the chart below if a party involved in an accident asks for Sherwin-Williams’ contact information.

If

Then

You are at the scene of the accident

Share the information on the insurance card located in your glove box referencing the Hylant Group which manages our insurance claims.

Note:  If you cannot locate the insurance card, share Hylant Group’s phone number: 216-447-1050.

You are receiving a call about a past accident
  1. Verify that the person has the Accident Claim Number; they will need to reference this when they call Fleet Response. 
  2. Direct them to call Fleet Response at 1-855-373-1763.

4

Obtain complete identification of witnesses.

5

Lock the store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicle if it must be left, or coordinate towing if required.

6

Report accident to the Store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal Manager or Lead Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Driver and District Manager.

7

Complete Sherwin-Williams Accident Report Form and forward a copy to Fleet Response, the District Manager, and Loss Prevention (keep a copy for your records).

8

Report accident as soon as possible to Fleet Response and give the following information:

  • Identify yourself as a Sherwin-Williams driver
  • Division name and, if the vehicle is for a store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service, provide the store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal number.
  • Vehicle Number
  • Details of the accident
  • Accurate location of your vehicle for further towing as required

Note: Fleet Response will report the accident to the appropriate Sherwin-Williams’ claims handler.

9

Follow Fleet Response’s instructions to obtain estimate or appraisal from a Fleet Response designated body shop.

10

Schedule repair. Fleet Response will assist with scheduling by confirming availability of parts.

11

Inspect repairs before accepting vehicle. The repair shop will bill Fleet Response, not the driver. Call Fleet Response immediately if there are any problems with the repair of your vehicle.
