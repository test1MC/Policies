Canada Imports Overview

This section applies to all Canadian sites purchasing items from non-Canadian, Unrelated Suppliers.

  • Employee Rewards Group
  • Personnel at the Automotive, Product Finishes, TAG stores in Canada ordering from unrelated suppliers
  • Personnel at the Automotive, Product Finishes, and TAG stores who are initiating shipments to Canada of IT equipment, marketing materials, and other not-for-sale items

All products that are imported into the commerce of Canada must be in compliance with the Canada Border Service Agency (CBSA) and the Canada Environmental Protection Agency (CEPA) regulations and laws. Following a standard process ensures that all SW sites are in compliance with the regulatory requirements of the CBSA and the CEPA and that The Sherwin-Williams Company minimizes its duty expenses as well as exposure to fines and penalties.
