Canada Imports Help

SW Internal Questions:

  • All questions from anyone within The Sherwin-Williams Company can be addressed to the Trade Compliance Group via tradecompliance@sherwin.com or to a member of the Trade Compliance Team within SW’s Corporate Regulatory.

Supplier Questions:

  • Any supplier questions must first be addressed to the SW individual initiating the order for shipment into Canada. If further clarification is required, supplier can contact Trade Compliance Team within Corporate Regulatory Team by emailing to tradecompliance@sherwin.com.
