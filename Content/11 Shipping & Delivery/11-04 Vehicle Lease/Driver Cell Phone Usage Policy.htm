Driver Cell Phone Usage Policy

  1. The use of a cell phone by the driver of a Sherwin-Williams vehicle is not allowed in areas where prohibited by law. This includes phones that are a component of another device such as a PDA. 
  2. In prohibited areas, the driver must pull off the road and park in a safe and legal manner before using the cell phone. 
  3. In areas where the use of a cell phone is not prohibited while driving, the driver should use a hands-free device or conduct calls from a safe, parked space. 
  4. This policy also applies to the employee’s spouse if he/she has been authorized to drive the company vehicle.
