New Vehicle Request Overview

Option Description
LeasePlan Portal Access LeasePlan portal access is granted if you are a current driver. If you are given access to LeasePlan, you will receive an email with LeasePlan login information and general ordering instructions. With LeasePlan access, you will be able to: 
  • Change your password.
  • Select your vehicle and choices for interior and exterior color.
  • View Standard and Optional Equipment.
  • Verify your address, telephone numbers, and e-mail address and make changes if necessary.
  • Check the status of your order (activated once the order has been approved and sent to the manufacturer).
  • Exit the vehicle ordering system by Logging off, which is located at the top right hand corner of page.
New Vehicle Request

All new requests must be initiated through LeasePlan.  SWEEP Fleet Approval is used for the procurement of company sponsored vehicles.  LeasePlan is the vendor S-W uses to process orders and requests, which is done on LeasePlan’s ePlan Website.

Refer to the LeasePlan Driver Order Instructions for 2013 for detailed information on placing your request.

After submitting your order, you will receive a confirmation that your request has been sent to the appropriate parties for review. Once a decision has been made, you will receive an email accepting or denying your request.
Check Vehicle Order Status

Access Sweep Fleet Approval to check on the status of an order.

Note: Fleet Operations can only access orders already approved by district or division management. Contact your district management to verify that the request has been approved. If it has, contact your division coordinator to confirm that the division has received the request and has approved the request.  You can find your division coordinator’s contact information by searching by your division in the Information Directory.
