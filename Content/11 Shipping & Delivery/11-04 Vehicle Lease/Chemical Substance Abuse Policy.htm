Chemical Substance Abuse Policy

  1. An employee cannot drive a Sherwin-Williams leased vehicle while impaired or under the influence of chemical substances.
  2. An employee cannot be working, driving, possessing, under the influence of, or using any chemical substances, controlled or illicit, including but not limited to: marijuana, cocaine, opiates, phencyclidine (PCP), amphetamines, depressants, stimulants, narcotics, any potential mind or performance altering chemicals, hallucinogens, or any other chemical substance, which to any degree, renders you incapable of safely operating a vehicle.
  3. These requirements also apply to over-the-counter drugs being abused or drugs administered to an employee by or under the instructions of a physician who has advised that the substance will affect your ability to safely operate a vehicle.
  4. Company Management may initiate corrective measures based on driver performance evaluations respective to chemical substance abuse.
