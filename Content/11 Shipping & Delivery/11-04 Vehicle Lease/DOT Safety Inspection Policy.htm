DOT Safety Inspection Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  1. All vehicles must undergo annual safety inspections through programs that are approved by the United States Department of Transportation (DOT). New vehicles that are received during the year should have a DOT inspection performed upon delivery. In January of each year, Fleet Operations will mail a DOT/Safety Inspection Form to each store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal that has a truck. Additional inspection forms may be obtained from Fleet Operations upon request.
  2. The Store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal Manager or Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Lead Driver must ensure that the DOT/Safety inspection is performed and all related repairs made by April 15. The inspection must be done on all trucks regardless of the size or age.
  3. Proof of inspection must be kept with each vehicle. This proof may be an approved decal or a copy of the inspection record.
  4. Failure to produce proof of inspection may subject the vehicle to a $500 a day fine.
  5. If a truck resides in a state where the state inspection does not suffice, then contact an approved inspection facility and have the safety inspection performed.
  6. Once the inspection has been completed, the distribution of the form is as follows:
    1. Original - Inspecting Vendor/Garage
    2. Duplicate - Store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal File
    3. Vehicle Copy - Kept in Truck
