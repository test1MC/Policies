Refuel Safety Policy

  1. All Sherwin-Williams Company Vehicles should use the fuel grade recommended in the owner’s manual supplied by the manufacturer. Use of unleaded plus or premium grades should not be used unless specified in the owner’s manual. 
  2. Whenever available, self-service fuel pumps should be used.  The use of full service fuel pumps is prohibited unless the driver is physically unable to use self-service pumps.
  3. The following procedures shall be followed at all times when fueling vehicles:
    1. Comply with all posted warning on or near fueling pumps.
    2. Refrain from using cell phones or smoking during fueling operations.
    3. Touch a grounded object before starting the fuel pump to discharge any accumulated static electricity.
    4. Remove portable fuel containers from vehicle before filling.
