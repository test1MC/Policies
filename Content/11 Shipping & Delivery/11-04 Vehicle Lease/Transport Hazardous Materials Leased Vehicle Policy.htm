Transport Hazardous Materials Leased Vehicle Policy

  1. The transportation of hazardous materials must be in conformance with the standards of the LiveSafe Administrator's Guide (LiveSafe).
  2. Employees packing, marking, labeling, loading, or completing shipping documents shall have satisfactorily completed the training requirements of LiveSafe prior to transporting the load.
  3. All drivers must be trained in the procedures set forth in the LiveSafe Administrator's Guide section on Emergency Response & Contingency Plan.
  4. All drivers must have completed the Stores Learning System -- Safety and Loss Prevention prior to operating a vehicle.
  5. Drivers transporting placarded loads (in excess of 1000# of hazardous materials) must hold a valid commercial drivers license with a current hazardous materials endorsement.
