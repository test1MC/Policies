Replacement, Termination, or Transfer of Leased Vehicles Policy

  1. Prior to the transfer of any vehicle from one store to another, Fleet Operations must be made aware of the transfer.
  2. Repairs and service required to bring transferred vehicles to an acceptable level will be charged to the transferring store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal cost center.
  3. Terminations of leased vehicles must be immediately communicated to Fleet Operations. Vehicles are to be secured and the ending mileage communicated to Fleet Operations.
  4. The responsible District Manager must advise Fleet Operations of any unassigned, open vehicles.
  5. The requirements for ordering a new vehicle vary with each location. The District Manager will determine when to purchase a new vehicle.
    1. Contact Fleet Operations at 855-373-1763 for an application. 
  6. Minimum guidelines to assist the District Manager replacement of pickups, vans, and larger delivery vehicle are 130,000 miles or 60 months in service.
    1. The criteria for the replacement of current fleet vehicles are at least 80,000 miles driven, at least 48 months in service with no mileage limitation, and at least 95,000 miles with no months in service limitation.
    2. Our current replacement schedule is optimized to prevent company exposure to larger than necessary repair bills, additional tire replacements, and a loss in resale value for the vehicles.
