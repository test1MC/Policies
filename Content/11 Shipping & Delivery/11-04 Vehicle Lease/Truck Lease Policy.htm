Truck Lease Policy

  1. All store vehicles are to be leased through the Fleet Operations Department of Sherwin-Williams. Leasing or purchasing store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicles through sources other than Fleet Operations is prohibited.
    1. If you or a family member is interested in purchasing a Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicle, contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 for available vehicles and pricing. 

    2. Vehicles are sold “as is.” Quoted prices are based on auction pricing and are non-negotiable. 

  2. It is at the discretion of each division to set policy as it relates to the criteria for a store to receive a truck.
  3. A Leased Truck Application Packet must be completed and approved for each truck ordered and consists of two forms:
    1. Leased Truck Application
    2. Vehicle Condition Report
  4. The approval of both the Vice-President of Sales and the Division Controller is necessary on all truck/cargo van applications. 
  5. Lead time for vehicle orders will be at least 12 – 14 weeks.
  6. The additional notes apply to leases of vehicles in the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service:
    1. Fleet Operations will provide each Division Controller with a quarterly vehicle report which will include a suggestion of which Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicles are ready to be replaced.
    2. If the District Manager, Vice-President of Sales, and Division Controller all approve, the process of ordering a replacement will begin. The District Manager may also initiate a request for replacement of any vehicle at any time.
