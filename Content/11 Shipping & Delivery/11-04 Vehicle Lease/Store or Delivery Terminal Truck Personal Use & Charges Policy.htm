Store or Delivery Terminal Truck Personal Use & Charges Policy

  1. Leased vehicles are authorized for store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service use only. Personal use of a store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicle is prohibited in all cases. The store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service are to use the vehicle assigned, only for lawful purposes, and only within the continental limits of the United States and Canada. The vehicle is not to be used for the transportation for hire of either goods or passengers.
  2. The vehicle is owned or leased by the Sherwin-Williams Company and is used in connection with the company's trade or business.
  3. Fleet Operations must be made aware of any vehicle that is removed from the store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal location at night for security reasons.
