Daily Truck Inspection Policy

  1. Vehicle inspections are to be conducted daily prior to commencing a delivery or delivery route.  Drivers should utilize the Daily Vehicle Inspection Form found in the Forms Repository. 
  2. A truck should not be driven unless the driver is satisfied that the vehicle is safe, roadworthy, and that the condition of equipment such as lights, signals, and horns conforms to all local laws regarding the working operation of such equipment.
  3. To ensure the safety of the driver in both vehicle and on the job site, all vehicles are to be outfitted with gear including, but not limited to, a spill kit, cargo straps, hand truck, and snow brushes/ice scrapers. 
  4. Whenever there are two or more store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicles routinely used to transport placarded loads of hazardous materials, and two or more DOT qualified drivers, a written daily truck inspection report must be completed for each truck.  If applicable, contact the Safety/Environmental Compliance Department (216-566-1710) for further assistance.
