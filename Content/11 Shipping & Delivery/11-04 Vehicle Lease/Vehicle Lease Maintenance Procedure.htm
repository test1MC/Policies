Vehicle Lease Maintenance Procedure

The LeasePlan Maintenance Guide shows maintenance items and the recommended mileage as to when the maintenance should be done. The coupons are good at any of the national account facilities that are part of the LeasePlan Program. Please review these recommended services and coupons, which are provided as a convenient reminder and means of payment for maintenance that should be done. At the bare minimum, ensure the following maintenance is done:

  • Oil changes at a minimum of 5,000 mile intervals (this is based on estimated hours of operation in “normal” conditions)
    •  Several factors determine if more frequent oil changes are required:
      • Extreme weather fluctuations
      • Driving in heavy traffic with the significant time spent idling
      • Vehicle Owner’s Manual maintenance schedule
  • Tire replacement when tread depth is recorded at or below 1/16 of an inch
  • Have brakes checked at least every 15,000 miles or when the brakes are making noise

Step

Action

1

Take your truck to one of the approved repair facilities.

Note: Our approved repair facilities, Sears, Firestone, and Goodyear, offer expedited routine services for Fleet. If unsure of the nearest approved shop, please call LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 and ask for the mechanical department. 

2

Once at the servicing facility, call LeasePlan at the toll free number (1-855-373-1763) and identify yourself as a Sherwin-Williams' driver.

3

Give the LeasePlan advisor the LeasePlan identification number for your truck.

Note: If you do not know the identification number, contact Sherwin-Williams Fleet Operations.

4

Tell the advisor what repairs or maintenance you feel need to be done.

Note:  In order for the LeasePlan Program to work properly, it is extremely important that the Store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal Manager or Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Lead Driver (not the servicing facility) contacts LeasePlan initially. This ensures needless repairs are not done to your truck. LeasePlan will then issue a purchase order to the servicing facility to cover the cost of the repairs.

5

Pick up the truck after the work is completed. Do not pay the servicing facility at that time.

Note: The servicing facility will bill LeasePlan who will bill your store or Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service cost center through Accounts Payable.
