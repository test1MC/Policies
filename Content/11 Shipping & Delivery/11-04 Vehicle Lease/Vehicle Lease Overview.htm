Vehicle Lease Overview

This section applies to Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Vehicles only.

Vehicle Type

Description

Administrative

A car, truck, or cargo van assigned to an individual Sherwin-Williams’ employee to be used for business and allowed personal use.

Store

A truck or cargo van assigned to a store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service for the sole purpose of making deliveries from the store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service to the customer. The store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicle will remain at the store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Terminal location each night and cannot be driven for any personal use unless other arrangements have been made through Fleet Operations.
