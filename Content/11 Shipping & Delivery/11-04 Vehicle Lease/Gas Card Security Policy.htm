Gas Card Security Policy

  1. Do not write the 6-digit PIN on the Gas Card. The PIN should be written down and retained in a secure area, such as a locked filing cabinet or safe.
  2. Do not store the PIN and the Gas Card in the same secure area.
  3. The Gas Card must be retained in the glove box of the delivery vehicle at all times.
  4. The Gas Card - Log Out/In Sheet, located in the Forms Repository, must be completed each time the Gas Card is used to fuel the vehicle. Include the vehicle/unit number, driver's signature, manager's signature, date, time and amount of fuel up.
  5. Supporting receipts are required for all gas purchases and must be attached to the appropriate Gas Card - Log Out/In Sheet. You are permitted to place the receipts in an envelope and attach the sealed envelope to the Gas Card Security Log.
  6. Retain current and completed Gas Card - Log Out/In Sheets along with supporting receipts in a folder titled Gas Card - Log Out/In Sheet for audit review.
