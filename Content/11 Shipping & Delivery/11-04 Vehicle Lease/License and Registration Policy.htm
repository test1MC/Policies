License and Registration Policy

  1. All leased vehicles are enrolled in an automatic licensing program with the leasing company. The leasing company will handle the licensing and registration requirements with each state and mail the new plates and/or new registration to the driver’s home address prior to the expiration of the old plates and/or registration.
  2. If there are additional requirements that are necessary for registration, such as a state inspection, the leasing company will notify the driver at his/her home address of the requirements. The driver will be instructed to supply the leasing company with any additional information that is needed to register the vehicle.
  3. If there is a need for the leasing company to send the driver the title for the vehicle in order to meet a requirement for registration, the title must be sent back to the leasing company when the registration process is completed.
  4. Vehicle registration and insurance packets should be kept in the glove compartment of the vehicle at all times.
