Driver Abuse or Neglect Policy

  1. If details are needed on a vehicle charge that appears on a driver's P&L account, contact Fleet Operations at 855-373-1763. They can review details with you and provide further documentation if needed.
  2. Damage caused by driver abuse and/or failure to properly maintain a leased vehicle, may be charged directly to the store and may be reported to Loss Prevention. Examples of driver abuse or neglect are as follows:
    1. Failure to follow manufacturer’s recommended maintenance schedules especially oil changes.
    2. Holes drilled in the dash or floor on a vehicle.
    3. Rips, tears, and holes in seats or interior of a vehicle, or any damage caused to the interior other than through normal wear.
    4. Damage (dents, scratches) to the exterior of a vehicle other than normal wear.
