Store or Delivery Terminal Truck Business Use Policy

  1. Store vehicles may only be used to transport employees as passengers, to transport company products, and to further any other enterprises in the enhancement of company business.
  2. The store truck is not to be used for any other purpose outside of company business. The store truck is not to be used for personal use.
  3. Store delivery vehicle keys must be kept in the store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Terminal safe or locked filing cabinet when not in use.
