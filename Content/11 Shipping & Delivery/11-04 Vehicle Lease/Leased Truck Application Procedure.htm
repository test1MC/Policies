Leased Truck Application Procedure

Step

Action

1

Obtain copies of the Leased Truck Application Packet from any District Office, Division Office, or Fleet Operations.

Note: The District Manager must complete the sections as they pertain to shipment information, type of truck requested, and if the old truck will be retired when the new truck is delivered.

2

Complete this section in its entirety with the following information:

  • Store Number or Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service Cost Center and location (state and city)
  • Date the application was prepared
  • Store Street Address (City, State, and Zip Code)
  • Store Phone Number
  • Type of truck requested

3

Mail the completed application to the District Manager for approval. Retain a copy of the application in the store files for future reference.
