Refuel with Gas Card Procedure

Step

Action

1

Provide the six digit Personal Identification Number (PIN) and the current mileage in order to make a gas purchase using the Lease Plan/Wright Express Gas Card. 

Note: Your PIN is a six-digit number that should have been previously communicated to you. If you do not know your PIN, contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 for assistance. For security reasons, if this is a store vehicle, the administrator will not provide the PIN to a caller. They will call your store directly on your request and provide them with the PIN number. Then, you can call your store for the number.
2
If Then
Fuel card was declined at the pump

First, verify that the station accepts our WEX card.

Contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 for assistance. Your card may have been compromised or damaged. Fleet Operations may be able to resolve the issue immediately.  If not, you will need to use an alternate form of payment and submit the expense through Concur.

NOTE:  For your own safety and compliance with local ordinances, please observe all postings asking customers to refrain from cell phone usage while pumping gas or in the area of the gas pumps.
Fuel card was accepted at the pump Proceed to Step 3.

3

If

Then

Gas station has an electronic card reader on the gas pump

Insert and remove the Lease Plan/Wright Express Gas Card into the card reader. When prompted, enter your PIN and the current odometer mileage using the keypad on the gas pump.

Note: If you are prompted for a vehicle number, enter the Vehicle Card Number located in the middle of your fuel card.

You have any problems using the “pay at the pump” option

Pump the gas and see the station attendant to use the fuel card for payment.

You have any difficulties with card acceptance

Have the attendant call the 1-800 number on the back of your fuel card. A customer service representative will help the attendant complete the transaction.

Note: If you happen to lose your fuel card, contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 immediately so that they can cancel the card and order you a new one. If lost on the weekend, call LeasePlan so that they can cancel the card and then contact Fleet Operations on the next business day to order a new card.
