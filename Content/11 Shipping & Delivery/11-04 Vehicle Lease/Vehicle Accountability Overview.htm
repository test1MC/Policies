Vehicle Accountability Overview

Responsible Party

Description of Responsibilities

Employees
  • Adhering to the policies and procedures if the employee routinely drives store/SW Delivery Service vehicles.
  • Reducing the risk of a critical accident involving passengers, pedestrians, other vehicles, or material being transported.
  • Taking immediate action to assist injured persons or clean up spills of transported material.

SW Delivery Terminal Manager
  • Assuring that all policies and procedures are met and taking appropriate action when employees are observed violating the safety standards or operational procedures stipulated in training by notifying the appropriate Company.
  • Managing repeat or critical violations.
  • Providing the drivers the educational and instructional information necessary.
  • Notifying the proper Company Management of critical incidences and accidents involving persons or transported material that threatens the environment, natural resources, or public/human health and welfare.

Assistant Store/ SW Delivery Terminal Manager
  • Assisting the Store/SW Delivery Service Manager in taking appropriate action when employees are observed violating the policies and procedures or safety standards by notifying the appropriate Company Management of repeat or critical violations.
  • Assuming the responsibilities of the Store/SW Delivery Service Manager in that person’s absence.
