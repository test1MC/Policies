Leased Truck Condition Report Policy

  1. A Leased Truck Condition Report must be completed and signed by the Store Manager if a current truck will be turned back in to the leasing company for disposal once the new truck arrives at the store.
    1. The current odometer reading on the current truck must be disclosed and verified to ensure that the minimum mileage for turn-in has been obtained. The application for a new truck will be returned if the Leased Truck Condition Report does not contain an accurate odometer reading for the truck being returned.
  2. The Vehicle Condition Report is contained in the Leased Truck Application Packet, which can be obtained from any District Office, Division Office, or Fleet Operations.
    1. This report should be included in the packet when forwarded to the District Manager for approval.
  3. Store delivery trucks that are in the process of being replaced are not available to be sold to anyone, including store employees or customers. The old vehicle must be returned to the delivering dealer when the new truck is picked up.
