Gas & Diesel Fuel Policy

  1. All Sherwin-Williams' leased vehicles must use the type of fuel specified in the owner’s manual supplied by the manufacturer.
  2. If there is any question as to the type of fuel to be used, contact Fleet Operations.
  3. Self-service fuel pumps are to be used at all times. The use of full service fuel pumps is prohibited unless the driver is physically unable to use self-service pumps.
  4. Each store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service that has a store/Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service vehicle will receive a Lease Plan/Wright Express gas card as part of the Lease Plan Driver’s Packet. The Lease Plan/Wright Express Gas Card is the only approved method to pay for gas purchases for the company vehicle. Unless the Wright Express card is lost, stolen, or isn't working, gas purchases for the vehicle are not to be reimbursed by a Cash Disbursement transaction or processed on a Travel and Expense Report.
  5. A Lease Plan/Wright Express Gas Card should only be used to purchase fuel for the vehicle number that is shown on the last line of the gas card.  Failure to restrict the gas card usage to only the vehicle number shown on the card will result in discrepancies on the gas card tracking system.
  6. Do not transfer the Lease Plan/Wright Express Gas Card to another vehicle or to another driver.  If the vehicle shown on the bottom line of the gas card is turned in or transferred, destroy the gas card.
  7. Only gas should be purchased using the Lease Plan/Wright Express gas card. No other merchandise or materials such as food, sodas, repairs, oil etc., should be purchased with the Lease Plan/Wright Express gas card. A car wash purchased at the pump is allowed.
