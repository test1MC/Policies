Canadian Exports Policy

This section applies to Sherwin-Williams sites that export goods from Canada

  1. All products exported from Canada must be in compliance with all applicable Canadian Export Laws and Regulations.
  2. All shipments intended for export must be in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations of the final destination countries.
  3. Toxic Substance Control Act (TSCA) Certification is required for all materials that are chemical substances, chemical mixtures, or chemical articles.
