Canadian Exports Overview

This section applies to Sherwin-Williams sites that export goods from Canada, and, specifically, the following scenarios:

  • The shipment of finished goods and/or purchased items by Canadian personnel from Automotive, Product Finishes Division, Global Supply Chain, or The Americas Group to any Sherwin-Williams site or external customers outside Canada.
  • The shipment of miscellaneous items, such as IT equipment, other equipment, spare parts, marketing materials, and any not-for-sale items from any Canadian Sherwin-Williams location to any Sherwin-Williams sites or external customer outside Canada. Computer equipment must not be shipped without direction from Depot.
