Fleet Insurance Cards Overview

All Sherwin-Williams drivers of lease vehicles, including TAG Stores and Automotive Branches, are required to carry insurance cards for their vehicles. Drivers of vehicles in states where insurance cards do NOT require VIN specific certification may print their insurance card from the Fleet Insurance Card File. Please note:

  • Insurance Cards are specific to State.
  • Insurance Cards are specific to group (TAG, Automotive, Columbia, Duron, Lease Plan).  Make sure you print the correct insurance card.
  • PA and TX Only:  Your insurance card has a front and a back.  Be sure to print both pages!
  • Insurance cards may be printed only. You may not save your insurance card to file.

As a reminder, these insurance cards are only for Sherwin-Williams lease and owned vehicles and cannot be used for personal use.

Note:  VIN specific states include CO, DC, MS, NV, NY, ND, TN.
