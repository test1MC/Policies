Fleet Insurance Cards Help

 

Q I need an insurance card for my vehicle.  How can I get one?

A

Contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 for assistance. 

Q The sticker on my vehicle’s license plate is about to expire. What should I do?

A

Contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 for assistance. 

Fleet Operations will confirm if a state inspection is required and provide information on having this inspection completed, and, if necessary, provide you with a Power of Attorney document so that you may register the vehicle yourself at the state agency (e.g.  Department of Motor Vehicles).
Q

LeasePlan has sent me a request regarding the registration of my car.  What do I need to do?

A

LeasePlan requires information about your vehicle in order to assist with your annual vehicle registration process. Complete any forms you have been mailed or call LeasePlan to provide the information they need in order to process your registration renewals. Contact LeasePlan at 855-373-1763 with any questions you may have.

Q

When are insurance cards renewed or updated?

A

Insurance cards are updated annually each April by Hylant.  All eligible drivers/vehicles will receive a mailing with the updated insurance cards.
