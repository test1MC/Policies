Audit Closing Conference Overview

The Manager's signature on the closing conference memo indicates his/her acknowledgment of the Auditor's statements and all Audit Exceptions. Therefore, the Store Manager should be instructed to:

  • Arrange for the store to be adequately staffed so that the conference can proceed without interruptions.
  • Discuss with the Auditor all items on the Closing Conference. The Closing Conference will include Manager Responses to all of the items addressed.
  • Request clarification for items he/she does not agree with or understand. If the Store Manager does not totally agree with the closing conference he/she should be instructed to call the District Manager.
  • District Managers are encouraged to attend the Closing Conference.
