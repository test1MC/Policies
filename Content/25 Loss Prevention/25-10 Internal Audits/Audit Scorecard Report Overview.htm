Audit Scorecard Report Overview

For more information on audit scoring, see 2018 Audit Scorecard

Audits will be scored based on points assigned for each infraction. Total points will be entered on the cover page of each Audit.

Points will not be assessed for newly implemented programs (such as Store Delivery Service, Floorcovering System, total POS implementation, Tax Record Card System) within the first year of implementation of the program within the store.  These items will be reviewed and commented on as necessary by the auditor as a learning experience for the store to assist in improving operations.
