Access Audit Reports on Source Procedure

You are able to review all final audit reports on SOURCE. Each store will be notified by e-mail when the final audit report has been issued and is ready for review.

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Browser -> SOURCE Operations -> Lookup -> Store Audit Reports.

Note: You will only be able to review audit reports for your store.

2

Select the YEAR by using the drop-down arrow or submit to review audit history for your location.

3

Sort the audit list by selecting any of the column headings.

4

Click on the report name in the Reports column to review.

5

The left side of the screen shows the various groups with audit infractions. Click on the links to quickly review the section of the audit report. Or left mouse click on the right side of the screen and use the UP and DOWN Arrow keys to review.

6

From the Audit Report menu, select SUGGESTION to send a suggestion to the Audit Manager. Select Help to review section 1.1 in the Store Management Policies and Procedures manual.

7

Any concerns regarding audit grading should be directed to your District Manager.
