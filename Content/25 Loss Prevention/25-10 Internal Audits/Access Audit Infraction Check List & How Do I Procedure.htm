Access Audit Infraction Check List & How Do I Procedure

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Use the Audit Infraction Check List and How Do I Procedure to ensure operational proficiency and help to avoid the out-of-sight, out-of-mind infractions. They improve the quality of our branch and store employees by promoting discussion and delegation of various operational tasks.
