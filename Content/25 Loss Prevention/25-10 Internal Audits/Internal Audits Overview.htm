Internal Audits Overview

The Sherwin-Williams Corporate Internal Audit Department audits stores. Audits are not announced and may be conducted at any time.

The purpose of an audit is to ensure that company assets are protected. Records are examined to ensure that policies and procedures are followed, and property is protected.

Everything that goes into an audit can be linked back to the policy & procedure manual. In addition, you will notice a Recent Updates link at the top that should be viewed periodically for changes. Utilize the contacts to assist in answering operational questions, in addition to your local auditor.
