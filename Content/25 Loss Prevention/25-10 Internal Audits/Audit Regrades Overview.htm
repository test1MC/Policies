Audit Regrades Overview

  • All Audit regrade requests must be approved by the Division Controller and submitted by Division Loss Prevention.
  • Regrade requests must be submitted within 45 days of the Audit Batch date.
  • Division Loss Prevention requests must be accompanied by a copy of the Store Manager's response *, District Manager approval and supporting documentation. Division will send all requests to:

  • Information requested during the Audit must be provided during the Audit.
  • Information found after the fact will not warrant a re-grade.
