Request Additional Keyholder Procedure

The manager should make all requests for additional keyholders following the process below:

Step Action

1

Complete the Additional Key Holder Request Form fully and submit to the District/Area Manager.

2

If approved by the District/Area Manager, the District Coordinator will ensure the following documents are completed and forwarded to Loss Prevention for review:

  • Auto Insurance DeclarationBackground Investigation

3

If approved by the District Coordinator, the request will be routed to Loss Prevention. 

If Then
Approved
  • Employee will be identified within Gems as an approved keyholder.
  • Manager will be notified of approval.
  • Key and keyholder responsibilities may be given to employee.
  • If your location uses Kronos for Attendance & Time, the manager must change the availability for the new keyholder to include opening and closing availability.
Not Approved
  • The manager will be notified of the declination with a supporting explanation for the inability to approve.
