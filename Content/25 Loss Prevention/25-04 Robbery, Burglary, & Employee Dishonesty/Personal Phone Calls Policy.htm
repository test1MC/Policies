Personal Phone Calls Policy

  1. Store telephones are to be used for business purposes. However, they may be used for personal reasons in emergencies. If personal calls result in additional charges to the store, the responsible employee will pay for the call upon receipt of the phone bill.
  2. All exceptions to this policy must be decided on and approved in writing by the District Manager.
  3. When the telephone bill is received and there are personal, long distance calls on the bill, the calls must be paid for immediately.
  4. Personal Calls should be reimbursed to the Company by performing a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt coded to 7599-099. In the COMMENTS area, enter the following request: "Please recode this receipt to 7154-001." Send the transaction copy to your Division Controller.
