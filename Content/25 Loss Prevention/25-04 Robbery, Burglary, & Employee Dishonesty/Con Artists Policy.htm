Con Artists Policy

Con Artists are individuals who come into the store and attempt to confuse an employee while making change or returning money back to a customer. When a customer attempts to confuse an employee while making change, the drawer should be closed.

  1. Advise the Store Manager of the situation and they will determine how much money is in the register in order to make the appropriate change.
  2. An LOI Report should be completed on any actual or attempted quick change scams.
  3. Complete an LOI Report and attach a copy of the police report. For more information, see 10-01 Loss, Offense & Incident.
