Shoplifting Overview

  • The most effective means of preventing shoplifting is customer greeting and employee awareness. This atmosphere is created by offering customer service when the customer enters the store. It is important to remember that prevention is the desired approach to shoplifting rather than apprehension.
  • If a person is concealing an item, do not accuse that person of theft, but rather greet them and offer your assistance.

  To see more strategies about avoiding shrink or shoplifting, see Shrink Action Plan Overview.
