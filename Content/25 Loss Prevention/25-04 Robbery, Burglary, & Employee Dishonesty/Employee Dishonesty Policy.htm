Employee Dishonesty Policy

  1. If an act of employee dishonesty and / or irregularity is known or suspected, it must be reported to Division Loss Prevention.
  2. The most effective measures in reducing and / or preventing employee theft are:
    1. A thorough intensive screening of all employees per Division / District Policy.
    2. Alertness to possible dishonesty and a consistent and impartial implementation of the Loss Prevention Programs and Sherwin-Williams policy and procedures.
    3. Consistent evaluation of programs designed to provide the needed accountability. Adherence to company policy. Use of the Internal Audit Program.
    4. Accurate accountability of inventory, documents, cash assets, and equipment to ensure those irregularities will be discovered within a reasonable time frame.
  3. Action After Detection: When an employee is suspected or known to be directly or indirectly involved in a dishonest act, contact Division Loss Prevention immediately. The investigation will be conducted and / or supervised by Division Loss Prevention.
