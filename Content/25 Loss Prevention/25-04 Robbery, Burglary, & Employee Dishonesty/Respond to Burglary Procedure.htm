Respond to Burglary Procedure

Step

Action

1

When notified that a facility has been burglarized, request the name and the telephone number of the party calling and immediately call them back to confirm that the notification is valid.

Note: If the notification was received from someone other than the Police Department, notify the Police Department.

2

Report to the facility as soon as possible.

Note: Do not enter the premises until the authorities have arrived and be prepared to identify yourself as a company employee.

3

Assist the police as requested or required but do not jeopardize your safety or the safety of others.

4

After the police have determined it is safe to enter the premises, determine if any money or merchandise is missing.

5

Notify the District Manager and Division Loss Prevention immediately upon the findings.

6

Complete an LOI and attach a copy of the police report. For more information, see 10-01 Loss, Offense & Incident.
