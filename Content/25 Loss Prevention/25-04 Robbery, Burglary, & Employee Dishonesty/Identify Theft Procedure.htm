Identify Theft Procedure

If you witness merchandise being taken and are certain it was concealed and removed from the store, follow these steps:

Step

Action

1

Follow the person out of the store and ask for proof of purchase. If there is no receipt, ask the person to return to the store with you:

If

Then

The person refuses

Attempt to record a description of the vehicle and person and a license plate number. Contact the Police Department and report the incident. Proceed to Step 2.

If the person returns to the store

Ask the person to pay for or return the merchandise.

If

Then

The person pays for the material

Process a sale transaction then complete a LOI Report.

Note: There is no need to call the police in this instance.

The person refuses to pay for or return the merchandise

Contact the police department and request an officer come to the store.

Note: Never leave the customer alone in the office, store, etc.

Note: Never attempt to physically restrain a person.

2

Upon the arrival of the Police Department, advise the officer of the incident and cooperate with their investigation.

3

Notify Division Loss Prevention and the District Manager of the incident as soon as possible.

4

Complete an LOI Report and attach a copy of the police report. For more information, see Loss, Offense, & Incident Policy
