Robbery Overview

In order to discourage theft or robbery, store employees should follow the Open & Close Security Overview guidelines.

In the event of a robbery, however, employee and customer safety is the number one priority and employees should follow these guidelines:

  • Cooperate with the robber. Give the robber all the cash or merchandise s/he demands.
  • Remain calm and do not make any quick moves or gestures.
  • Observe the robber’s physical appearance, noting any distinctive physical characteristics, dress, or accents.
  • As soon as the robber has left the store, check on all other employees or customers. If anyone is injured, get immediate medical help.
  • Notify the police and ask everyone to remain in the store until the authorities arrive.
  • Cooperate fully with the investigating police officers.
  • Close the store until the police have conducted their investigation.
  • Do not discuss the incident with the news media. Refer them directly to the District Manager or Division Loss Prevention.
  • Complete an LOI Report and attach a copy of the police report. For more information, see Loss Prevention Overview.
