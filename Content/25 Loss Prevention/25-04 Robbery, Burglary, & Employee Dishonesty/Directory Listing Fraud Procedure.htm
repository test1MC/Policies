Directory Listing Fraud Procedure

Any directory listing orders, forms, contracts, invoices, confirmations, or delinquency notices received by fax or any other medium from a directory listing service should be reported immediately:

Step Action

1

Report suspected scam to your Loss Prevention representative.

Note:  This will give LP the opportunity to alert other targeted stores and mitigate or prevent loss. 

2

Contact Jill Host in Advertising. 

If contact is made via mail, email, or fax, forward copies of the correspondence to jrhost@sherwin.com.
