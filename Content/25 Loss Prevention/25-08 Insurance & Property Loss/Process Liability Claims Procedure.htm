Process Liability Claims Procedure

When dealing with liability claims, the information should be gathered in a courteous manner but without admitting any liability or making any commitment as to what the future disposition of the matter might be.

Step

Action

1

After notifying the District Manager, report full details of the event by telephone to the Legal Department and follow their instructions. Reports should give all the facts known about the matter, including:

  • Persons involved
  • Date
  • Time
  • Place
  • Witnesses
  • What happened
  • What was said

Note:  Accidents of a public liability nature should be reported immediately [by telephone if the matter is a serious one] to the Legal Department in Cleveland with copies to the Risk Management Department and your Loss Prevention Director or, if Automotive, your Director of Facilities and Safety.

2

Prepare a Loss Offense and Incident Report (LOI) in writing).

For more information see LOSS, OFFENSE & INCIDENT.
