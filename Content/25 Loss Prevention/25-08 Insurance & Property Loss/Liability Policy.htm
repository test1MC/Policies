Liability Policy

This policy provides guidance in the event that someone is injured in the store, someone claims that his/her property was damaged by our products or employees, or someone claims that they were otherwise harmed by Sherwin-Williams.

  1. The Store Manager must immediately report any accident involving customers or the public to the Loss Prevention Director and the Legal Department at 216-566-2477 or 3802. Automotive Branch managers should report to the Director of Facilities and Safety at 216-332-8901 and the Legal Department.
  2. The Store Manager should never try to determine legal liability or act without first discussing the situation with the Legal Department - Cleveland.
