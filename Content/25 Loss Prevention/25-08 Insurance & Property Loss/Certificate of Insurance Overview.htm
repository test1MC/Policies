Certificate of Insurance Overview

If a good and valid reason exists to not use the Evidence of Insurance, a Certificate of Insurance may be requested using the following form. Due to processing time, allow five business days from the date the request is received by Risk Management, until the requesting party is in receipt of the certificate. This can be done by completing an  “Certificate of Insurance Form for North America”, which can be found in the Forms Repository (keyword: insurance). 

You may be asked to provide proof of the insurance coverage that Sherwin-Williams has for public liability, automobile liability, and worker's compensation.

Customer Requests to be Named as an Additional Insured

In this instance, we could be giving a customer the right to make a claim directly against Sherwin-Williams' insurance which could result in significant added costs and lower profit margin. Question 4 of the Request Form requiring an explanation as to the necessity of granting additional insured stats must be completed.

This type of certificate is usually issued only in conjunction with a written contract.

Step Action

1

Complete the Certificate of Insurance Request Form.

Note: Do not make direct requests to Corporate Risk Management for this type of certificate.

2

Send it to the Division Controller.

Note:  If you have questions, contact the Division Controller.
