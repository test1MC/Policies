Report Loss of Store Funds Procedure

Step Action

1

Contact the District Manager or Division Director of Loss Prevention immediately.

2

Complete an LOI.

Note: For more information, see Loss, Offense, & Incident Policy

3

Write a store payment draft for the amount of cash that is being replace by processing a Disbursement by Store Draft transaction.

Note: See Target Fund Replenishment Overview.

4

The District Manager will send a letter to Accounting (Headquarters) stating the payment draft number and date, as well as the amount issued to replace the fund.
