Request Evidence of Insurance Overview

In the normal course of business, a customer may request that you provide information about the type of insurance that Sherwin-Williams carries. If a customer requests evidence of insurance, direct them to the Sherwin-Williams Evidence of Insurance web site. This site is hosted by Hylant Group, insurance broker for Sherwin-Williams. Give them the following information:

  • Web Address: https://eoi.hylant.com/eoi.aspx
  • Access Number: sw

The Hylant Group web site includes a Frequently Ask Questions {FAQ] page and is the easiest and best way to satisfy a request for insurance. The requestor can be referred to the web site by providing the above information and should be encouraged to use this document to satisfy their needs.
