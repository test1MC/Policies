Loss Prevention Policy

  1. Any act of dishonesty (intentional or negligent), destruction of assets, flagrant violation or disregard of company policies and procedures established to protect assets is cause for immediate dismissal. The company will take the necessary action to recover all losses. If required or if evidence warrants, the company will prosecute.
  2. All employees must report dishonest acts, destruction of assets, and flagrant violation or disregard of company policies and procedures to the Loss Prevention division and supervisor.
  3. All employees must read and complete the “Safety and Loss Prevention” unit of the Store Training and Reference Tool (S.T.A.R.T) within ten days of hire.
  4. The manager must ensure all employees understand their responsibility regarding Loss Prevention.
  5. The Store Manager must notify the District Manager and Division Loss Prevention representative if a conflict of interest exists.
