Loss, Offense, & Incident Policy

  1. All known or suspected losses, disappearances, thefts, criminal offenses, violations of Sherwin-Williams' Loss Prevention policies, customer injury, threats of litigation and other incidents which result or could result in a loss, damage, or compromise of a company asset, must be reported within 48 hours of the incident or discovery of the incident, and where appropriate, investigated.
  2. Any loss must be reported to the Division Loss Prevention Director by completing a Loss, Offense, and Incident Report per the instructions below for your division. LOI Reports may be found in the Forms Repository.
    1. If approved by the Division Loss Prevention Director, simply call in the information to Division Loss Prevention.
  3. Division Loss Prevention will communicate the loss to Risk Management through the Loss Prevention Database. For more information, see Insurance & Property Loss Policy.
  4. For instances involving auto accidents or break-ins/thefts, a police report is also required.
  5. Cash Variance LOI Reports are only to be filed if they are $75 or greater.  Lesser amounts should only be communicated if there is a pattern of activity.
