Complete an LOI Report Procedure

The Store Manager should Complete a Loss, Offense and Incident (LOI) Report:

Step

Action

1

Obtain a police report outlining any accident, theft, or vandalism before completing the LOI Report.

2

Including the following information:

  • facility name
  • division
  • store number
  • Address, city, state, zip code
  • phone number

3

Enter the time/estimated time and date of the occurrence, including the estimated loss.

4

Describe the event: identifying everyone involved in the incident, the property that was lost or damaged, and the protection that was in place, if any.

5

Sign the report.

6

Submit all copies of the Loss, Offense, and Incident Report to Division Loss Prevention per the instructions for the division.

7

Retain the file copy at the store.
