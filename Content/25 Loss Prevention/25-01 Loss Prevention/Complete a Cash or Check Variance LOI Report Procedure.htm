Complete a Cash or Check Variance LOI Report Procedure

Step

Action

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Loss Prevention -> Loss, Offense, Incidents Reporting System.

Note: Currently, this system is only used for Cash Overage / Cash Shortage / Check Discrepancies. If there is another reason to create an LOI, please fill out the LOI form found on the Forms Repository.

2

Log in using the computer credentials. Click Next.

3

Confirm employee name and Store #.


Note: Once completed, the other information will automatically populate.

4

Select the incident type.

5

Enter all the required information.

6

Add all employees who rang on the register by entering their last name, pressing Lookup, then selecting the appropriate employee. Click Add Cashier.


7

Add any additional documentation such as photos, statements, videos, police reports by clicking Add Files.

8

Click Finish and Upload Attachments.

9

Review information on the Incident Preview Screen.

10

Click Submit when finished.

11

Print a copy with the incident number and retain for audit purposes.
