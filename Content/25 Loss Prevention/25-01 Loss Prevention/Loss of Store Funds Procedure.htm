Loss of Store Funds Procedure

Step Action

1

Contact the District Manager or Division Director of Loss Prevention immediately.

2

Complete a Loss, Offense and Incident Report.

Note: For more information, see Complete an LOI Report Procedure.

3

Write a store payment draft for the amount of cash that is being replaced by processing a Disbursement by Store Draft Transaction.

Note: For more information, see Target Fund Replenishment by Store Draft Procedure.

4

The District Manager will send a letter to Accounting (Headquarters) stating the payment draft number and date, as well as the amount issued to replace the fund.  

Note: For more information, see Complete an LOI Report Procedure
