Loss Prevention Overview

Loss Prevention is the coordinated effort of management and employees to create and enforce the policies and procedures required to reduce or prevent costly errors and to protect the company's physical assets, resources, and personnel.

Loss Prevention strives to:

  • Demonstrate competence, integrity and professionalism in all actions
  • Identify and reduce potential risk exposure
  • Form key relationships with both internal and external customers
  • Complete responsibilities in a manner that affirms their role as an integral part of Sherwin-Williams leadership

Sherwin-Williams employees have an obligation to protect the company's assets and to reduce and prevent errors which erode profits. By following Loss Prevention’s policies and procedures, store employees should be able to:

  • Gain awareness about potential issues and risks
  • Identify, prevent, and handle theft situations
  • Identify the necessary measures to enhance profits and minimize shrink

Any questions regarding Loss Prevention should be directed to the store's respective Director of Loss Prevention.
