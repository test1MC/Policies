Shrink Action Plan Overview

This program is designed to help stores reduce overall shrink, improve profit, and store performance. This will be a collective effort to drive down shrink and grow profit; however, it begins with management and store staff.

After priority stores are selected based upon their cumulative inventory results, a plan will be established to help ensure that appropriate inventory controls are in place to assist in reducing inventory variances. A store meeting with the entire staff will need to be held to discuss this program. When possible, Loss Prevention or the District Manager will attend the meeting to provide guidance and/or answer questions, as well as perdiocally check on the program’s progress. The shrink summary will be completed by Loss Prevention during the implementation of this plan.

For any questions regarding this program or inventory, please do not hesitate to call the divisional leadership team, including the Director or Manager of Loss Prevention, the District or City Manager, or the local Auditor.
