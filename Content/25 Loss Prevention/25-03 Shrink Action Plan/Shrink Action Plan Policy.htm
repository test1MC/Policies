Shrink Action Plan Policy

  1. The proposed Shrink Action Plan must be enacted within 2 weeks after the final inventory results have been posted.
  2. The Shrink Action Plan Binder, which will be provided, is to be maintained in the store. Place all completed forms in the binder.
  3. Stores are expected to complete all operations related monthly self audit checklists and review them to identify possible shrink contributors.
  4. The Store Action Plan checklist must be completed as specified on the checklist (i.e. daily, weekly, and monthly). The store action plan checklist includes key operational inventory controls and must be completed throughout the duration of the program.
