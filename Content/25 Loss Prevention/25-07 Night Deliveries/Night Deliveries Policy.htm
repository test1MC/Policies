Night Deliveries Policy

If the store has been notified that it is a participant in the night delivery program, the store must adhere to the following policies:

  1. Prior to being placed in the night delivery program, stores must obtain approval from the DSC and Loss Prevention.
  2. All night delivery locations must be equipped with a burglar alarm and a shunt access key which will allow for drivers to enter through the loading dock or rear entrance. Shunt keys will simultaneously unlock and disarm all or a portion of the alarm for the duration of the delivery.
    a. Store Managers should contact their divisional Loss Prevention staff for assistance with all alarm service and/or shunt install requirements.
  3. Loading dock and parking lot lighting must be in good condition and provide adequate lighting (between 20-50Lux, or as bright as most street lights) within 75 feet of the dock door.
    a. All loading dock lighting must be operated via a “dusk to dawn” switch in order to alleviate any potential outages during delivery. 
  4. Any interior dock areas adjacent to the receiving door(s) must be maintained and free from merchandise that would hinder easy access for unloading the truck.
  5. Any exterior dock areas adjacent to the receiving door(s) must be maintained so as to not impede the delivery driver.
    a. Driveways should be clear of any obstruction that would prevent the driver from easily entering/exiting the dock area.
