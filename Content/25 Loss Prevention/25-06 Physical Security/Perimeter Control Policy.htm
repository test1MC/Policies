Perimeter Control Policy

Sherwin-William’s door control policies were established to ensure that each location is secured properly to reduce potential loss from internal and external theft.

  1. Members of management and approved 3rd Keys are the only people authorized to have keys to the perimeter doors.
  2. All perimeter doors must be kept locked and with the alarms set, if applicable, at your store at all times prior to store opening and after store closing.
  3. Do not open perimeter doors prior to opening or after close unless you can identify who the individual is requesting entrance.
  4. Keep all warehouse windows locked at all times.
  5. Ensure that the back door of the warehouse cannot be opened from the outside.
  6. During hot weather, use a gate or secure screen in place of the rear to permit air circulation.
  7. Install a buzzer at the rear entrance for deliveries. Have chimes at the front door signal when a customer enters the store.
  8. Ensure all skylights and air conditioning vents are secure.
