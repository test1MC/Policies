Product Service Room Policy

Product Service Rooms (PSRs) are typically found in stores that handle large volume mixing, custom blending, or solvent dispensing. The PSRs are designed specifically to meet the strict fire and safety regulations.

  1. PSRs must meet the requirements set forth in the LiveSafe Administrator’s Manual Chapters 3 and 6.
  2. Equipment used in the PSR must be approved for use in flammable and combustible conditions. A list of the equipment may be obtained from Division Installation Managers.
  3. PSR operating procedures are to be strictly followed. The procedures are presented in LiveSafe units 3,6 and 7.
  4. All employees working in the PSR must satisfactorily complete the related Store Training and Reference Tool (S.T.A.R.T) course, Hazardous Materials Management.
  5. Access routes shall be maintained free of all obstructions.
  6. PSR doors shall be self-closing and self-locking. The doors shall remain closed at all times, unless designed by Division Engineering to be opened per applicable fire codes.
  7. All PSR locations must register their facility with the Safety Environmental Compliance department.
