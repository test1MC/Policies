Internal Warehousing Policy

  1. Warehousing of customer property is prohibited on store premises unless the merchandise is being repaired or ready for delivery. Proper repair and delivery documents must be on file if customer merchandise is being retained at the store.
  2. Proper Safety and Security procedures should be followed in order to protect the assets in the warehouse. All merchandise received should be verified with the freight bill or packing slip as store employees unload it. All merchandise shipped should be verified and initialed by two employees.
  3. Only authorized persons should be allowed in the warehouse area.
  4. Signs should be posted indicating Authorized Persons Only. Signs should be posted indicating No Smoking. Spray equipment should be secured by chain.
  5. Flammable Classes 1A, 1B & 1C merchandise is any products with a flash point less than 100 degrees F. Flammable merchandise should not be stored in a warehouse that does not meet local safety and fire codes. Fire extinguishers must be placed in accessible areas. Store personnel should know the location of each extinguisher.
  6. All rubbish should be removed from the store daily to prevent a potential fire hazard.
  7. Mixing areas must be kept clear of oily rags, open containers of solvent, and/or combustible materials.
  8. All flammable materials must be kept in proper storage containers and away from open flames.
  9. For all additional information on safety related issues, reference the Work Safe Administrators Manual.
