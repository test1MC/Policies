Overhead & Loading Dock & Trash Disposal Policy

  1. Overhead/Loading dock doors should be closed and locked at all times when not in use.
  2. Docks with gates present can have the overhead door up as long as the gates are engaged and locked.
  3. A member of management should execute or at minimum be present during the disposal of all trash. No merchandise should be disposed of without the approval of management and proper accounting for the expense.
