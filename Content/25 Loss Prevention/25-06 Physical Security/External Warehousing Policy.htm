External Warehousing Policy

  1. Before securing any external warehouse space, written approval must be on file at your location from the District Manager and Division Loss Prevention. Approval must be received before storing any materials in offsite warehouse space. Risk Management (risk.management@sherwin.com) must then be informed for insurance purposes.
  2. The District Manager and Division Loss Prevention must approve external warehousing.
  3. Inventory records should be maintained in the store for any external warehouse space.
  4. Proper Safety and Security procedures should be followed in order to protect the assets in the warehouse.
  5. All merchandise received should be verified with the freight bill or packing slip as store employees unload it. All merchandise shipped should be verified and initialed by two employees. Only authorized persons are allowed in the warehouse area.
