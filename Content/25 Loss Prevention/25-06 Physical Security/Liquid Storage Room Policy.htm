Liquid Storage Room Policy

Liquid Storage Rooms (LSR) are required due to specific fire codes in certain municipalities. LSRs are isolated rooms, which store large quantities of flammable liquids.

  1. When required by specific fire codes, LSRs shall be operated in the same manner as the product service rooms. Maintenance and operating procedures within the LSR shall minimize leakage and prevent the accidental escape of flammable liquids.
  2. Combustible materials (i.e.; paint saturated trash) are not permitted to accumulate in the LSR.
  3. Access routes shall be maintained free of all obstructions.
  4. LSR doors shall be self-closing and self-locking. The doors shall remain closed at all times.
  5. All employees working in the LSR must satisfactorily complete the training unit, Hazardous Materials Management in WorkSafe.
