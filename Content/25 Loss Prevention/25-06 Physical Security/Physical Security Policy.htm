Physical Security Policy

  1. It is the responsibility of the Store Manager to ensure that the store premises and its contents are secure at all times.
  2. Only the Store Manager, Assistant Manager, or an authorized third key-holder are permitted to have the safe combination or keys to the store. The Store Manager must request written approval from the District Manager for the third key-holder to be given the safe combination or to be assigned a key. The District Manager will inform the Division Office of the store's request.
