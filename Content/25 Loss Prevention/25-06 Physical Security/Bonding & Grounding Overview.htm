Bonding & Grounding Overview

At Sherwin-Williams' facilities that have PSR operations, flammable or combustible liquids may be transferred from one container to another. If this is part of your job, you must carry out the transfer only in approved, properly ventilated areas, away from flammable or combustible storage and any potential ignition sources. You also must use proper bonding and grounding procedures. Follow procedures in WorkSafe chapter 6.
