Mixing Room - Personal Protection Equipment (PPE) Policy

This section applies to Automotive Branches only

  1. It is mandatory for all employees to wear safety glasses and nitrile gloves when working in the mixing room or mixing paint in general.
  2. Safety glasses part numbers are listed below and are stocked at the Richmond DSC. If an employee wears regular prescription glasses s/he must wear safety glasses over their prescription glasses (SW part number SAS5120 below as an example). Glasses must meet ANSI Z87.1 Standard.
  3. Nitrile gloves are required for any operation involving mixing or pouring paint. Glove part numbers and sizes from SAS that are stocked in the DSC are listed below. If electing to use a nitrile glove from other than SAS they must be a minimum of 5 mil thick.
