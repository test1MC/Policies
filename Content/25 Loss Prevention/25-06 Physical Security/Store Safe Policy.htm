Store Safe Policy

  1. The Store Manager and Assistant Manager are the only employees with authorized access to the safe and combination unless proper approval is on file from the Division.
  2. There are few authorized places for cash in the store: store safe, Point of Sale cash drawer, or locking file cabinet.
  3. If a store does not have a safe or locking file cabinet, cash must be stored in another secure area within the store during non-business hours.
  4. The safe must be closed and locking at all times when not in use. Day lock is not permitted.
  5. The safe combination should not be kept in writing.
  6. The safe combination should not be changed when an employee leaves if the Medeco Key Mark Interchangeable Locking System is implemented. 
  7. Remove currency from the cash drawer periodically during the day and place it in the safe.
  8. If the cash accumulates to more than $500.00 in excess of the operating fund during the day, make an interim deposit. For more information, see Interim Deposits Overview.
