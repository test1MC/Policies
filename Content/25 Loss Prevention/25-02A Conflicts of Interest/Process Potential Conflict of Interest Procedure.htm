Process Potential Conflict of Interest Procedure

All employees are provided the opportunity to complete a Potential Conflict of Interest Disclosure Form upon hiring, and are reminded to complete a new form if they encounter any potential conflicts during their career with Sherwin-Williams. The employee should communicate any questions or potential conflicts to their immediate supervisor, Internal Audit, or Loss Prevention Department.

Step Action

1

Disclose the potential conflict of interest to your manager and appropriate Human Resource representative.

2

From SOURCE hompage -> Information Library -> Forms Repository.

3

Enter Potential Conflict of Interest Disclosure Form in Form Name.

4

Select the form and review the first page of the document.

5

Enter a brief description of what the potential conflict may be, then answer the following questions:

  • Have you previously disclosed a Conflict of Interest?
  • Has your supervisor been notified?

6

Provide the following information:

  • Your name in both the signature and name boxes
  • GEMS number
  • Position with the company
  • Department or store location and division 

7

Select Review at the bottom of the page.

8

Select Email after reviewing the document.

Note: Once selecting Email a new box will appear which will allow the employee to add additional emails and comments, or simply select Send Email. After the email is sent, a new box will appear stating that the form was successfully emailed to Potential_COI@sherwin.com.

9

Click the button Close Window exit the remaining open windows.

Note: Once the Potential Conflict of Interest Disclosure Form is received by the Internal Audit / Loss Prevention Representative who monitors the Potenial_COI@sherwin.com email box, the form will be forwarded to the appropriate division for additional review and response. The respective division should evaluate the form to determine if additional information is required.
