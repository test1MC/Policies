Test the Alarm System Procedure

Step

Action

1

Close all doors and ensure the key pad does not show any alerts, bypasses, or zone troubles.

2

Set the alarm.

3

After one or two minutes, activate the motion detectors and all door contacts.

4

Allow the system to sound for two full minutes.

Note: In many stores, the activated points can be verified by scrolling through the alarm system touch pad.

5

Reset all points and de-activate the system.

6

Contact the alarm vendor and confirm all points were received.

Note: Any faulty zones should be serviced immediately.

7

Contact Loss Prevention for assistance.
