Alarm System Overview

An alarm system is installed based on the following factors:

  • Potential risk of crime incidents or burglaries in the area of the store
  • The risk of loss to company assets and products.
  • The potential amount of monetary loss if the store is burglarized.
  • The expense of subsequent repair and restock of stolen items.

Once the decision has been made to invest in an alarm system, the management team will need to ensure that the system functions properly in the event of an emergency. Therefore, timely updates and regular reviews of the alarm system are required.
