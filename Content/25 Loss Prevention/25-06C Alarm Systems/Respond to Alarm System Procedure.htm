Respond to Alarm System Procedure

Step

Action

1

Call alarm vendor to verify the legitmacy of alarm and which areas/zones were activated.

2

Advise alarm vendor that you will respond to the call and provide an estimated time of arrival.

3

Meet the police officer in front of the store.

Note: Never enter the store alone. Wait for the police to arrive before exiting your vehicle or inspecting the store.

4

Once inside, inspect the store and ensure everything is in order.

5

Reset the points, set the alarm system, and lock the store.

6

In the event of an burglary, call Loss Prevention immediately.
