Replace Emergency Lock Procedure

Follow these steps to manage an Emergency Lock Replacement situation based on the service provider for the store's locking system.

Step

Action

Bass Security

1

Contact the District Manager, City Manager, or District Coordinator and request that a spare core be issued immediately for an emergency core swap.

Note:  If the distance between the store and the District Office prevents a quick spare core swap, the store may, with District Manager approval, contact Bass Security (1-866-956-1815, Option #3) to have the lock rekeyed immediately. Bass Security will arrange for a locksmith and the Lost & Unreturned Keys Procedure should be followed.

Midwest Security Inc

1

Contact Loss Prevention to report the occurrence and to obtain replacement lock and key hardware.
