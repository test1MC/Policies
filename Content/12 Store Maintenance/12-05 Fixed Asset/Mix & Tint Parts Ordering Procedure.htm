Mix & Tint Parts Ordering Procedure

Step

Action

1

Complete a Purchase Order and mail the white copy to:

Fluid Management
1020 Noel Avenue
Wheeling, IL, 60090

2

Indicate on the Parts orders: 8% Discount Off List Price.

3

If the order is phoned in, write confirming across the vendor copy of the purchase order prior to mailing.

4

Fluid Management will send the original invoices directly to Stores Group Accounts Payable at Headquarters.

5

The Vendor will send a clearly marked duplicate invoice to the store.

6

Stores Group Accounts Payable will process the original invoice according to established payment terms based on the received quantity and price on the purchase order.

7

If necessary issue a chargeback to the vendor. For more information see VENDOR MERCHANDISE RETURNS. Remember that the invoice is already being processed for payment by the time you receive the merchandise. Therefore, it is important that you issue chargebacks immediately for any related billing or shipment problems.
