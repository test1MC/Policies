Electronic Equipment Disposal (US) Policy

US Automotive Division Only

  1. During the course of normal business Branches may replace Branch equipment or accumulate returned equipment from Customers such as old computers/monitors, fax machines, copiers, printers, and electronic mixing scales.
  2. Old equipment MUST NOT be thrown in the trash as disposing of e-waste unlawfully could result in excessive penalties and fines for the company.
  3. Electronic waste should not be stored in the Branch indefinitely. 
  4. A cleanup of this waste should occur at least once per year, preferably before Physical Inventory.  
  5. If the equipment is on a Fixed Asset or Promo Equipment listing, the asset must be retired per Policy & Procedure Chapter 03-01.
  6. Double check to make sure that printers and copiers are SW equipment and do not belong to a third party vendor. 
  7. There is no charge for disposal.  Please use your approved carrier and ship prepaid to minimize your freight cost (this does not apply to Canadian locations—Canadian locations will be charged for this service).
