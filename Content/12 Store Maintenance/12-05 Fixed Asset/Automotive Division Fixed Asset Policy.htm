Automotive Division Fixed Asset Policy

  1. Branch Managers may initiate requests for the purchase of a Fixed Asset (required if over $5,000).
    1. If additional assistance is required for equipment requiring installation or outside buy of equipment contact the Division Senior Project Engineer at 216-332-8894.
  2. All eFCAs initiated by an Automotive Branch Manager must be routed to the Area Sales Manager and Regional Sales Manager for approval.

Then

Equipment is ordered from the DSC

Receive into Inventory (via MMS) and store expense it to the CIP-CLEARING ACCOUNT (0815-001). Then, fax a copy of the Store Expense Transaction along with the eFCA#, Branch #, and Date Placed in Service to 216-515-5771.

Equipment is ordered by issuing a PO to an outside vendor for a part number that IS already in the system

Receive into Inventory (via MMS) and store expense it to the CIP-CLEARING ACCOUNT (0815-001). Then, fax a copy of the Store Expense Transaction along with the eFCA#, Branch #, and Date Placed in Service to 216-515-5771.

Note: To View, Print or Complete this form on your system, go to eAccounting

Equipment is ordered by issuing a PO to an outside vendor for a part number that IS NOT already in the system

Do not create a part number nor receive into inventory. Instead, cancel the PO. Then write the eFCA#, Branch #, Date Placed in Service, and note the account to charge "C101-0815001-79xxxx" (BR#) for US branches or "C220-0815001-77xxxx" (BR#) for Canadian branches on the invoice and sign.

Fax the unpaid invoice to 216-515-5771. The Fixed Asset department will enter the invoice for payment.

Note: To View, Print or Complete this document on your system. Click eAccounting Link to eFCA.
