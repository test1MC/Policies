Electronic Equipment Disposal (Canada) Overview

Canada Automotive Branches Only

eCycle is the Canadian automotive vendor for  end of life electronic equipment disposal, including:

  • Display devices – monitors and televisions
  • Peripherals – printers, scanners, keyboards, mice, etc.
  • Computers – desktops and laptops
  • Other – paint scales and other equipment as approved by eCycle solutions

Note: All equipment will be responsibly recycled in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations.
