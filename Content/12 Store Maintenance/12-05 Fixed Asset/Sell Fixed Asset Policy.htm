Sell Fixed Asset Policy

  1. Obtain written District Manager approval prior to the sale of a fixed asset.
  2. The Division President or Stores Group Controller must approve, in writing, the sale of a fixed asset if the selling price is less than 85% of current book value listed in the Fixed Asset Register.
  3. Notify Fixed Asset Accounting of the sale of a fixed asset immediately for proper accounting.
