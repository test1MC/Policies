The repair is made by a District or Store Employee that has attended factory training.
  • The Store Manager can order repair parts from the manufacturer up to $500.00.
  • Fluid Management invoices are centrally billed and paid using Prime-Sub number 7400-110. All other invoices should be directed to the person initiating the Purchase Order. For more information, see MIX/TINT PARTS ORDERING POLICY.
  • Use Prime-Sub 7400-110 if using a Store Draft.

There is no factory trained District or Store Employee.
  • Contact the Division Installation Manager to request mixing/tinting equipment repair.
  • When calling, have the equipment Serial Number available.
  • Be able to discuss the nature of the problems you are having regarding the equipment.
  • Leave a message on voice mail if no one is available. Someone will return your call as soon as possible.
  • If necessary outside repair service agencies will be utilized.
  • Needed parts may be obtained from Fluid Management or the repair vendor.
  • All mix and tint costs will be coded to 7400-110.
