Product Finishes Division Fixed Asset Policy

  1. Facility Managers may initiate requests for the purchase of a Fixed Asset (required if over $5,000).
    1. Contact the Division Engineer if additional assistance is required for equipment requiring installation or outside buy of equipment.
  2. All eFCA requests initiated by a Facility Manager must be routed to the Regional Facilities Manager and Regional Sales Director for approval.

Then

Equipment is ordered from the DSC

Receive into Inventory (via MMS) and store expense it to the “CIP-CLEARING ACCOUNT” (0815-001). Then, email a copy of the Store Expense Transaction along with the eFCA#, Facility # and Date Placed in Service to:  stores.faquestions@sherwin.com

Equipment is ordered by issuing a PO to an outside vendor for a part number that IS already in the system

Receive into Inventory (via MMS) and store expense it to the “CIP-CLEARING ACCOUNT” (0815-001). Then, fax a copy of the Store Expense Transaction along with the eFCA#, Branch # and Date Placed in Service to :  stores.faquestions@sherwin.com

Note: To View, Print, or Complete this form on your system go to eAccounting.

Equipment is ordered by issuing a PO to an outside vendor for a part number that IS NOT already in the system

Do not create a part number nor receive into inventory. Instead, cancel the PO. Then write the eFCA#, Branch #, Date Placed in Service, and note the account to charge "C400-0815001-79xxxx" (Facility#) for US branches or "C522-0815001-77xxxx" (Facility#) for Canadian facilities on the invoice and sign.

Email the unpaid invoice to: stores.faquestions@sherwin.com

Note: The Fixed Asset department will enter the invoice for payment. To View, Print, or Complete this document on your system, select eAccounting Link to eFCA.
