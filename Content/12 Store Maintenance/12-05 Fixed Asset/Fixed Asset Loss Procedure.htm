Fixed Asset Loss Procedure

Step

Action

1

Notify Risk Management (risk.management@sherwin.com) and Loss Prevention of the loss. Prepare a Loss, Offense & Incident Report to be filed immediately with both Loss Prevention and Risk Management.
For more information, see Complete an LOI Report Procedure. Send detailed information regarding the insurance claim to both Risk Management and Loss Prevention.

2

Take a physical inventory and make a detailed list of items lost and costs incurred due to the loss. Inventory values should be itemized at store cost. Prepare Time Sheets for employees used in the clean-up effort. Take pictures and/or document the loss as appropriate.

3

Copies of receipts or other proof of expenses incurred should be submitted with the report. Inaccurate reporting or missing support data will only serve to delay credit processing.

4

Notify Fixed Asset Accounting of all Fixed Assets damaged or destroyed. This can be done by completing a Transfer or Retirement of Fixed Asset or Expense Form or by marking destroyed assets on a copy of the store's property listing. Send to:

Shared Services Fixed Assets Department
Sherwin-Williams Company
Fixed Asset Accounting
101 W. Prospect Avenue
732 Midland
Cleveland, Ohio 44115

5

Fixed Asset Accounting will retire these items and will charge the actual cash value to the store's insurance clearing account (0515-006).

6

Upon receipt of properly approved FCA's and related invoices, Fixed Asset Accounting will capitalize replacement Fixed Assets according to normal procedure.

7

Fixed Asset Accounting will forward copies of these invoices to Risk Management.
