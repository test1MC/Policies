Colorant Disposal Overview

Tint machines contain latex or other regulated liquids NOT ALLOWED IN DUMPSTER.

Contact manufacturer and/or solid waste vendor for disposal. Remove as much liquid as possible from machine, fill with concrete, dirt or sand, and dispose as advised. Dispose waste latex or purged colorant as “Non-Hazardous Waste” using an approved vendor.

Contact: Stores Safety & Environmental at (216) 515-7850 or (216) 566-1710 for additional information.
