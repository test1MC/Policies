Accutinter or Sher-Tint Disposal Procedure

Step

Action

1

Complete an Asset Retirement Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Items Form and forward to the Fixed Assets Department. This will remove the tint machine from your Property Record.

2

If

Then

The machine is functional.
  • Dispense all colorant out of the machine.
  • Utilize any usable colorant in other operating tint equipment.

The machine is inoperable and colorant cannot be removed.
  • You must remove as much colorant from the canisters as possible and set aside for proper disposal.

3

Unplug machine.

4

You can add a mixture of kitty litter, clumping version, and Portland cement in equal parts to the canister to solidify any remaining colorant. It may take weeks to months for the colorant to fully solidify (refer to the Corporate Waste handling procedures). Verify it is fully solidified before unit disposal.
