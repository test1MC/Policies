Electronic Equipment Disposal (Canada) Procedure

Canada Automotive Branches Only

Step

Action

1

When pickup is required, contact eCycle Solutions at recycle@eecyclesolutions.com

2

The subject should state: Store Number, Sherwin Williams, Pickup Request (ex.  1234, Sherwin Williams, Pickup Request)

3

The body of the email should state:

  • Pallets available for pickup
  • Listing of assets ready for pickup
  • All necessary contact information

4

eCycle will reach out to discuss any questions and to arrange the details of the pickup.

Note: Stores should expect pickup service within 3 to 10 business days, depending on the location. Store must ensure materials are ready for transport on the date the truck arrives.

5

Transportation and processing costs will be billed to the store directly, and the store will receive the following for each load:

  • Certificate of destruction
  • Scale ticket
  • BOL
  • Invoice (if applicable)
