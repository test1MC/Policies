Sell Fixed Asset Procedure

Step

Action

1

Obtain written approval from the District Manager.

2

Identify the Type of Sale:

If Then
Cash Sale Proceed to Step 3
Charge Sale Proceed to Step 6

3

Process the sale on a Manual POST Form. 

  • Write the word "MEMO" on all copies except the customers copy.  

4

Process in POS as a miscellaneous receipt using account code 0869-003.  

  • Record the Asset Number from the Fixed Asset Register in the Comment Section of the Miscellaneous Receipt Transaction.

5

Give the customer the customers copy and file the utility copy with the Fixed Asset Register. The store copy should be batched according to batching instructions.

6

Prepare a Manual POST form.

7

Write the word "MEMO" on all copies except the customers copy.

8

Call the District Credit Office and ask them for approval to have the amount placed on the customer's account. Once approved, ask them to call the National Credit Center to request an ARJV number to charge the customer’s account.

9

Have the ARJV coded to account code 0300-003 with a notation to re-code the sale to account code 0869-003. Record the ARJV number on the Manual POST Form.

10

Record the description and asset number from the Fixed Asset Register on the Manual POST Form.

11

Give the customer the customer's copy. File the store copy of the Manual POST Form in numeric sequence. Mail the utility copy to Fixed Asset Accounting.

12

The profit or loss on the sale will appear on the store Profit and Loss Analysis under account code 9510-001.

13

Send the utility copy of the transaction to:

Shared Services Fixed Assets Department

Fixed Asset Accounting

732 Midland
Cleveland, Ohio 44115
