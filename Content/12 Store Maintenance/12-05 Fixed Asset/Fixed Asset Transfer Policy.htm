Fixed Asset Transfer Policy

  1. Written approval must be obtained from the District Manager prior to the transfer of a fixed asset.
  2. Notify Fixed Asset Accounting of the transfer of a fixed asset immediately for proper accounting.
  3. Point of Sale, Color Matching and AccuTinters require Headquarters approval prior to any transfers. Strict location control is maintained for all hardware. Call the Store Computer Help Desk (216-566-2740) to request a move or transfer of computer equipment.
