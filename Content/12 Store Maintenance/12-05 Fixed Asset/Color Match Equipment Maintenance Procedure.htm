Color Match Equipment Maintenance Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Step

Action

1

After all electrical work and all boxes are received contact the Application Support Group (1-800-752-6351) for installation. Do not open any boxes before calling.

2

Call the Store Computer Help Desk (216-566-2740) to arrange an installation date. All computers are preloaded with ColorAnswers (Blend-A-Color) program.

3

Save all boxes.

4

Contact the Application Support Group (1-800-752-6351) upon completion of installation.

5

Report equipment failures to the Applications and Support Group (1-800-752-6351). Do not contact the hardware manufacturer.
