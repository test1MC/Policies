Mix & Tint Parts Ordering Policy

  1. Do not process an order to Fluid Management in excess of $500.00.
  2. The Store Manager must have the Division Store Installation Manager place orders in excess of $500.00.
  3. Do not send the duplicate invoice to headquarters for payment.
  4. All centrally billed parts invoices from Fluid Management will be coded to the Mix/Tint Repair Account 7400-110. This will be a direct expense to the store.
