Electronic Equipment Disposal (US) Procedure

US Automotive Division Only

Step

Action

1

Complete the Branch Recycle Form (Electronic Equipment) found in the Forms Repository.

2

Search form name or keyword “recycle” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

3

Contact the people listed on the form for authorization.

4

You must have accurate counts of each type of equipment and the corresponding serial number.

5

The material must be shipped prepaid - Branch Expense, using your approved carrier (the Approved Branch Carrier Shipping Program can be found: POS desktop -> Browser -> SOURCE homepage -> Automotive -> Operations).
