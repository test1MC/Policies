Fixed Asset Disposal Procedure

Step

Action

1

Obtain approval from the District Manager prior to disposing of the fixed asset.

2

Complete an Asset Retirement/Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Item Form. Find the Asset Retirement/Transfer of Fixed Assets in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “fixed” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

3

Provide all information required by the form. Document what was done with the asset next to the description of the item. Obtain the details needed to complete the form from the most current Fixed Asset Register.

4

Distribute the form as indicated at the bottom of the form.

5

Dispose of the obsolete, unusable fixed asset in a proper manner.

6

Verify the fixed asset has been removed from the next Fixed Asset Register.
