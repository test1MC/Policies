Color Match Equipment Maintenance Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

The Store Manager must ensure that:

  1. The equipment is connected to a continuous ground 20 amp isolated circuit and maintained as outlined in the User’s Manual.
  2. Any additional software program must have written approval prior to installation by the Applications and Support Group at Headquarters (1-800-752-6351).
  3. Program disks are stored in a locked filing cabinet or a store safe and that obsolete programs are returned to the Support Group when requested.
  4. The Calibration Tiles are in a safe, clean location.
  5. All three programs are calibrated weekly (these programs are available upon request depending on the store product mix).
  6. Weekly and monthly maintenance is performed on the equipment as stated in the User’s Manual.
  7. Store personnel are responsible for loading calibration disks, with the assistance of the Support Group.
  8. Store personnel are responsible for following correct operating procedures as outlined in the Color Matching Manual and contacting the Support Group for assistance if necessary.
  9. The District Manager is responsible for requesting Product Finishes programs.
  10. Training sessions may be arranged by the District Manager in conjunction with other stores in a geographic area.
