Fixed Asset Loss Policy

The loss amount is determined as follows:

  1. Replacement cost of Fixed Assets replaced.
  2. Net Book Value of Fixed Assets not replaced.
  3. Average selling price of inventory lost or destroyed less salvage value.
  4. Employee wages related to clean-up work.
  5. Cost of outside temporary help related to clean-up work.
  6. Actual disposal costs.
