Fixed Asset Transfer Procedure

Step

Action

1

Obtain written approval from the District Manager for the transfer of the Fixed Assets.

2

Complete an Asset Retirement/Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Item Form. Find the Asset Retirement/Transfer of Fixed Assets in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “fixed” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

3

Provide all information required by the form. Obtain the details needed to complete the form from the most current Fixed Asset Register.

4

If not shipped by store truck, make all necessary shipping arrangements. Complete a Bill of Lading for the item(s) transferred. Write the Bill of Lading number on the Asset Retirement/Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Item Form.

5

All transfers should be shipped prepaid (prime-sub 3861-001). The shipping cost should be added to the Asset Retirement/Inter-Company Transfer of Fixed Assets or Expense Item Form as a separate line item. Fixed Asset Accounting will transfer the expense to the receiving store.

6

When submitting the form to transfer or retire a fixed asset, the form should be accompanied by a copy of the property listing with the requested items to be transferred or retired to be marked with an asterisk.

7

Send the completed form to:

Shared Services Fixed Assets Department
Sherwin-Williams Company
Fixed Asset Accounting
101 W. Prospect Avenue
732 Midland
Cleveland, Ohio 44115

8

Verify on the next Fixed Asset Register that the item transferred has been added or deleted as indicated.

9

Contact the Store Computer HELP Desk 1-216-566-2745 and Applications and Support Group at Headquarters 1-800-752-6351 for transfer approval for Color Matching Equipment.

10

Contact the Store Computer HELP Desk 1-216-566-2745 for transfer approval for Automatic Tint Machines.
