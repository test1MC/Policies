Cell Phone Services Help

The following information is for all The Americas Group locations.

Question

Answer

Who is my long distance provider?
  • In most cases it is Verizon (MCI code 0222). 
  • You can confirm this by checking your billing report analysis or monthly statement. 
  • If unable to isolate the answer on these reports, please call Finance and Procurement at 216-566-1837 for assistance.

Who is my local service provider?
  • Call Telecom to obtain this information. 216-566-1837.

How many phone lines do I have?
  • Call Finance and Procurement (216-566-1837). 
  • They will confirm the number of lines with Telewares.

What should I do if my phone bill seems higher than usual?
  • Call Finance and Procurement (216-566-1837) to confirm monthly charge.

What should I do if I need a copy of my last bill?
  • Call Finance and Procurement (216-566-1837). Obtained from Telewares.

My store is moving, what do I do?
  • Call Finance and Procurement to advise them of the move.
  • Finance and Procurement will look up the cost center code and give the location ALL of their phone numbers. 
  • Advise the store to get (1) the person's name from the Telco provider, (2) order/ticket number, and (3) the due date of the move, when they call the Telco. Once all this information is gathered email it to laura.l.pecnik@sherwin.com. Finance and Procurement will email Telwares to advise them of move and make sure the store does not fall off the MCI inventory for long distance.

How do I add/disconnect some lines?
  • Call Finance and Procurement to advise them of the requested change.
  • Finance and Procurement will look up the cost center code and give the location ALL of their phone numbers. 
  • Advise the store to get (1) the person's name from the Telco provider, (2) order/ticket number, and (3) the due date of the change, when they call the Telco. Once all this information is gathered email it to laura.l.pecnik@sherwin.com. Finance and Procurement will email Telwares to advise them of move and make sure the store does not fall off the MCI inventory for long distance.

How can I change my phone book listing?
  • Call Telecom to obtain this information. 216-566-1837.

What should I do if I want to change my local provider?
  • Contact Finance and Procurement (216-566-1837).

How can I change my long distance provider?
  • Contact Finance and Procurement (216-566-1837).

How do I get new phone equipment?
  • For land lines contact Finance and Procurement (216-566-1837).
  • For Stores cell phones, Stores Services (216-566-2311).
