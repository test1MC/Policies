Cell Phone Services Policy

  1. Unless otherwise directed and approved by the Vice President of Sales (VPS) and acquired through End User Computing [EUC] and/or Stores Services, no Sherwin-Williams Stores Group employee is authorized to purchase and/or enter into any term agreement with a cell phone provider.
  2. If a role within Stores Group requires the use of a cell phone and this has been approved by the VPS, PDA assignments will be made by End User Computing (EUC-800-566-2997 Option #1) while standard cell phones can be acquired by calling Stores Services (216-566-2311). 
  3. Any cell account established outside of these parameters will be considered a fraudulent line and will be terminated immediately.
