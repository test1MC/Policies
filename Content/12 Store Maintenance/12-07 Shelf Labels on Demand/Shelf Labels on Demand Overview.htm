Shelf Labels on Demand Overview

Shelf Labels on Demand allows stores to generate replacement shelf labels when needed. They print with a barcode that can be scanned with the Universal Product Code Bar Code, if available. If not, the barcode will be based on the product sales number.

For more information see Local Pricing Audit Overview.
