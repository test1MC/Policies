Store Opening Inventory Overview

These initial orders for new stores will be handled by the Inventory System Analyst and Customer Service in coordination with the schedule set by the Store Installations Manager at the Division Office. The Inventory System Analyst will attempt to rebalance overstock inventory from nearby district stores to the new store, and the remaining inventory will be shipped directly from the servicing DSC. Rebalanced inventory will have electronic IST's downloaded to the stores and the DSC direct orders will be downloaded into the new store POS with the prefix "F."
