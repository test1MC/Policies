Maintain Wallcovering Area Procedure

Step Action
1 Inspect all in-stock and book areas. Clean any areas with a standard spray cleaner. Throw away any loose plastic roll wrappers, stickers or other debris.
2 Put away all books and in-stock rolls at the end of every day before closing. Also, put away books in the Wallcovering Department when customers have finished looking at them.
3 Check-in all in-stock and new book deliveries against the packing slip and put them in their proper bins within 48 hours.
4 Order missing bin board and bin holders from Order Express.
