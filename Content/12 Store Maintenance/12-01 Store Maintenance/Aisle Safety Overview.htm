Aisle Safety Overview

To prevent tripping and falling accidents:

  • Aisles must be kept clear at all times.
  • Aisles should be at least 36 inches wide to provide a safe walkway.
  • Stock should never be left lying in an aisle unattended.
  • Bulk items such as carpeting should never be stored, even temporarily, in the aisles.
  • Floors must be cleaned regularly and waxed with a nonskid and UL Listed product.
    • Any wet spots or spills on the floors must be dried immediately.
    • Every employee is expected to take responsibility for a clean and safe store.
