Leaks & Spills & Hazardous Materials Policy

  1. All stores must have a spill kit readily accessible to provide a prompt and safe means to clean and dispose of hazardous liquids.
  2. All store employees must be informed of the location of the spill kit.
  3. All store employees must be trained in the safe manner of responding to a spill of hazardous liquids.
  4. All employees shall become familiar with the spill response procedures presented in LiveSafe chapter 2.
