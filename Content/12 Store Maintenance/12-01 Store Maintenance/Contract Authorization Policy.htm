Contract Authorization Policy

  1. The Manager must not sign any legally binding, long-term agreement or service contracts without proper authorization.
  2. District Managers must be informed of any considerations to enter into long-term agreements or contracts with vendors.
  3. Stores Services must be contacted at 1-877-470-4615 for assistance prior to entering into any contractual agreement for services with a local vendor (ex. waste hauler or cell phone provider, coffee, fire extinguishers, floor care, floor mats, HVAC, landscaping, pest control, security alarm, paper shredding, snow removal, sprinkler inspections).
