Confirmation of Responsible Party Policy

TAG locations only. This section does not apply to Automotive and CC locations.

  1. The Manager must confirm the responsible party (Landlord or Sherwin-Williams) for all building repairs.
    1. If necessary, contact Division Real Estate to confirm who should pay for the repair.
    2. Division Real Estate may be a liaison between the store and the landlord.
    3. If the store has never contacted the landlord, call the Division Real Estate office for assistance in locating the landlord's contact information and to confirm that it is okay for to reach out to them directly.
  2. Written [email] District Manager approval is required if, at the time of the needed repair, the broken or damaged asset constituted a threat or risk and the Manager could not take the time to confirm the responsible party.
  3. For more information, see Maintenance & Repair of Fixed Asset Policy.
