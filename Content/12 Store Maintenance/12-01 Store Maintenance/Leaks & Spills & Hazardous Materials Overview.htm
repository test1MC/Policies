Leaks & Spills & Hazardous Materials Overview

Some products in a paint store are considered hazardous because they may be:

  • Flammable or combustible.
  • Contain a chemical that poses a health risk through ingestion, inhalation, or absorption.
  • Contain a chemical that would pose a health or environmental risk if released into the environment.
  • Reactive.

When these products are properly stored in closed containers, they pose no hazard. There is a hazard only when these products must be opened and mixed or transferred, when there is a spill, or when they are stored improperly.

Maintenance and operating procedures within the stores, both the sales area and the warehouse area, shall minimize leakage and prevent the accidental escape of flammable, combustible and hazardous liquids. In the case of leakage, spillage or release of any liquids on the store's premises, the liquids shall be cleaned up safely and promptly.
