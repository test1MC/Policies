Temperature Control Policy

  1. Store temperatures will be controlled by the Bayweb Energy Management System (EMS).
  2. The system will be pre-programmed for settings of 68 degrees for heating and 74 degrees for cooling on the sales floor during normal business hours.
  3. Stores will be able to adjust the temperature by 2 degrees. (i.e they can be turned down to 72 for cooling, or up to 70 for heat).
  4. Sales floor temperatures for hours when the store is closed will be pre-programmed to 78 degrees for cooling and 60 degrees for heat.
  5. Warehouse areas will be set at 57 degrees with the ability to raise it to 60.
    1. Most warehouses do not have AC; however, if they do, they must set it to 74 degrees for cooling.
  6. In order to maintain consistent warehouse and sales floor temperatures, all doors between the sales floor and the warehouse should remain closed at all times.
  7. In the event of an issue, or questions concerning your thermostat or the temperature in your store, please contact Stores Services at 877-470-4615.
