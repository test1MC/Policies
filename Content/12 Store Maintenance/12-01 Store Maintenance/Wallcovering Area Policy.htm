Wallcovering Area Policy

  1. All in-stock bins, closeout racks and wallcovering book areas are to be kept clean and free of debris at all times.
  2. All books and in-stock rolls are to be kept in their proper bins at all times.
  3. STAR deliveries of books and in-stock rolls are to be checked in and put away within 48 hours.
  4. Bin Boards are to be in place on every in-stock bin.
