Dispose of Inventory (Store Closing) Overview

Store employees will be very involved in the disposition of store inventory, including the following tasks:

  • Conduct Store Inventory (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Fixed Asset & Expense Item (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Dispose of Rental Unit (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Dispose of Special Order (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Dispose of Vendor Product (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Dispose of Wallcovering Book (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Dispose of GOM (Store Closing) Procedure

Note: Store employees may also be asked to assist in the disposition of certain inventory after the final day of business.
