Store Closing Return Policy

Follow guidelines regarding the disposal of inventory for Dispose of Inventory (Store Closing) Overview or for Store Blending Overview.  The Store Closing Return, unlike Approved Returns, will accept Associated Products, providing they meet the requirements listed below. 

  1. Store Closing Returns require approval of Store Operations.  Approval must be requested via e-mail to stores.operations@sherwin.com.
  2. The Store Closing Return procedure should be used when the closing Store, with assistance from Store Operations, cannot find a local store to IST remaining merchandise.
  3. The following requirements must be met to be approved for return to the DSC:
    1. No discontinued products
    2. No custom tinted products
    3. All products returned must have at least four months of shelf life remaining at the time the products are received at the DSC. Credit will not be given for returned product with less than four months of shelf life remaining.
    4. Returned product must be in perfect package condition.  Credit will not be given for returned product not in perfect package condition
    5. Products must be returned in original packaging
    6. Cases must be unopened
    7. All packaging must be free of tears, dents, leaks, tape, markings, overspray, rust or any other imperfections
    8. Must be forecasted or stocked at the servicing DSC
  4. Credit will not be given for unauthorized / unsaleable product and will be subject to the following fees and actions:
    1. $3 per gallon handling charge/disposal fee
    2. Restocking Fee of 2x the standard pick rate
    3. Freight Fee
    4. The product will not be returned to the store
    5. The IBAR will be voided and an entry booked to the store's shrink account
  5. When a request is approved for return to the DSC, an IBAR will be downloaded on the following Friday and the store will have two weeks to complete and release the IBAR. If the IBAR is not completed within these two weeks, the IBAR will be closed and the store will have to make another request to return the product.
    1. Standard return charges will apply for any material approved for a return to the DSC.  These charges will include but may not be limited to:
    2. Freight Fee of 30 Cents per gallon of fuel
    3. Restocking Fee of 2x the standard pick rate
    4. A copy of the completed IBAR must accompany the shipment.
    5. If no product is returned, no credit will be given.
