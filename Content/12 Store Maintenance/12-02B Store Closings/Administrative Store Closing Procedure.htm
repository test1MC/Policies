Administrative Store Closing Procedure

On the final day of business, the District Manager may ask store employees to assist with some administrative system functions to close the store.

Step Action

Reset Target Fund

1

On the final day of business, set the Target Fund for each terminal with a cash drawer to $0.01.  This will allow for the deposit of the Target Fund.  Complete this step directly before preparing the final deposit and running the final close transaction.

Final Deposit

1

Process a Close Transaction on the POS. Mark the Close Transaction "Store Closed - Final Deposit".  Make final deposit of all funds, including the Operating Fund for the location.

2

Contact the SCO Helpdesk before beginning polling.  Be sure to notify them if you will be using the system in the next few days to run IST transactions, chargebacks, and / or a Store Closing Return. They will provide instructions on how to open and close the system during this time. 

Note: Do NOT destroy or dispose of any remaining deposit bags.  These can be transferred to a neighboring location.

Final POS Close

1

Once the store is completely finished processing transactions on the POS, process a Close Transaction on the POS. Mark the Close Transaction "Store Closed - Final Report".

2

Contact the SCO Helpdesk before beginning the final polling.  Note that the Final Close may not occur on the last day of business.  The Final Close may occur several days after to account for ISTs, chargebacks, and the Store Closing Return which require completion.
