Dispose of Special Order (Store Closing) Procedure

Step Action
1

One month before closing, alert customers that special order merchandise ordered from this point forward cannot ship direct to customer.  Additionally, the customer may need to pick up their merchandise at another Sherwin-Williams location due to the closing. 

Let the customer know which nearby Sherwin-Williams store will have their order if it arrives after the closing date. 

Note: At this time, store employees should begin logging all special orders on the Closing Store Worksheet for tracking purposes.
2

On the final day of business, make a final attempt to contact all customers with outstanding special order merchandise.  Refer to the Closing Store Worksheet. 

Note: If product is in the store and ready to be picked up, let the customer know that the product will be available in your store for the remainder of the business day, after which time, they will need to go to the specified Sherwin-Williams location to pick up their order. 
3 At the end of the final day of business, print a copy of the Special Order Merchandise Tab of the Closing Store Worksheet.  Enter an IST for all merchandise based on the total landed cost.
4 Send all packages and a copy of both the Special Order Merchandise Tab of the Closing Store Worksheet and copies of all deposit transactions to the receiving store.
5 Provide a copy of the same information to the District Manager.
