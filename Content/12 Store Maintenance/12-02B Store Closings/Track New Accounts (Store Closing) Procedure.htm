Track New Accounts (Store Closing) Procedure

Step Action
1

Begin tracking all accounts opened 8 weeks before the store closing. 

Note: Tracking should coincide with the run date on the Customer Master File Report so that no accounts are missed in the transition.
2

Notify new customers that their location will be closing and be prepared to help the customer decide what other location may best serve their future needs as a home store. 

Note: Suggest locations based on proximity to where the new customer does most of their work or based on specific brands the customer plans to routinely purchase.  Store employees should note what store would best serve the new customer as a new store when tracking new accounts.

3

Be sure to ask the District Manager to what location unassigned accounts will be transferred. If the customer does not want to make a decision or is unable to decide, let them know what store location they will default to as their home store if they do not make a selection. 

Note: All accounts must be assigned to a new location at least one month before the final day of business as Accounts Receivables requires one month of processing time to make the home store location transfer.
