Customer Mail Campaign (Store Closing) Overview

The District Manager will ask either the District Coordinator or the Store Manager of the closing location to undertake a mailing campaign to contact all customers one final time regarding the store closing. If a store employee is asked to handle the mailing campaign, the District Manager will draft a letter for their use. If this is handled at the District Office, be sure to ask for a copy of the letter to answer any questions customers may ask pertaining to the letter. The letter customers receive will:

  • State the store closing date and include other pertinent information such as a date for discontinuation of the rental program or the location at which special order merchandise may be picked up after the closing date.
  • List nearby locations that can provide the same products and services.  Note if any employees from the closing store will be staffed at one of these locations.
  • Ensure each customer that this change is to improve the high levels of service, tinting, and products to which they are accustomed.
  • Thank the customer for their patronage.
