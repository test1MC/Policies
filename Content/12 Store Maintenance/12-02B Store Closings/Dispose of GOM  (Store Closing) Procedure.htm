Dispose of GOM (Store Closing) Procedure

Step Action
1
  • Transfer all current salable merchandise to other stores by processing an Inter Store Transfer (IST).
  • Note: If the merchandise has a sales number it is not necessary to enter store cost unless the item is being transferred at below normal store cost. If the merchandise has a class number, the store cost must be entered.
