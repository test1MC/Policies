Customer Phone Campaign (Store Closing) Overview

Upon receiving the Customer Master File, the District Manager will ask the closing store to communicate the store closing to your customers. All accounts should receive a phone call from the store to notify them of the closing. The District Manager will provide details on the information that should be provided in the conversation with the customer:

  • Advise customer of neighboring locations. Customers often build relationships with S-W employees and come to trust their local manager or long-time employee and rely on the service they receive. If any employees are transferring to a new location from the closing one, let the customer know where they can find these employees in the future.
  • Ask customer for preferred new home store location. Track this on the Closing Store Worksheet.  This task MUST be completed within the first four weeks of the closing process. 
  • If stores are blending, ask customers what products they expect to see in the new blended location to ensure that they will be able to service their customers.  Advise customer of any new product lines or offerings they may see in the blended location.
  • Ensure each customer that this change is to improve the high levels of service, tinting, and products to which they are accustomed.
