Closing Store Worksheet Overview

The District Manager will request a Customer Master File as soon as the decision to close a store has been made. This report will be key in performing several customer relations tasks:  identifying customers for sales rep visit, phone campaign, mail campaign, follow-up communication. The Customer Master File should also be a working document in the closing process. The closing Store Manager and employees should use the Customer Master File as the basis of information for the Closing Store Worksheet which will be provided by the District Manager. There are three tabs on this worksheet and each will serve a different use:

  • Track new customers added to the system after the report date so that they are not missed in the store conversion. Complete all fields of information.
  • When calling customers to inform them of the closing, the District Manager may make the decision to ask the stores where they would like to continue doing business. By providing customers a choice in their new home store, they will be more likely to select a convenient location for their particular needs and will be more likely to continue doing business with Sherwin-Williams. Use the second tab of the workbook to record Customer Accounts and their preferred new home store location.
  • Because special orders may sometimes take longer to ship than expected, begin tracking all special orders and deposit amounts four weeks before closing. This will allow the store to easily transfer information to the location identified to handle all open special orders after closing.
