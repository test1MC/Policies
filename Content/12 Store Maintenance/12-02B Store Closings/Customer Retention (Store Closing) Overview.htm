Customer Retention (Store Closing) Overview

One of the major concerns with any store closing or blending is customer retention.  The closing location will play a major role in reaching out to customers and assuring them that, though their home location is closing, they can and will receive the same products and service they have always enjoyed at their new home store location.  Your District Manager will take several steps to maintain customer relations, including:

  • Requesting a Centralized Invoice Message to print on customer’s monthly statements.  This message will inform customers of the closing and nearby locations, which can continue to serve their needs.
  • A message may be requested to print on any POS receipt.  This message, like the Centralized Invoice Message, will notify customers of the closing and nearby locations which can continue to serve their needs.  This message is particularly important in reaching out to DIY customers who may not shop as often and are harder to communicate with directly.
  • Sales Rep(s) will be asked to personally visit key accounts to discuss the coming changes.

Along with the tasks mentioned above, the Store Manager and employees of the closing location will play a key role in the following tasks:

  • Closing Store Worksheet Overview
  • Track New Accounts (Store Closing) Procedure
  • Customer Phone Campaign (Store Closing) Overview
  • Customer Mail Campaign (Store Closing) Overview
