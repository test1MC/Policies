Store Blending - Customer Relations Overview

Customer Relations

Description

Welcome Campaign

The Welcome campaign is an opportunity for the blending store to introduce themselves to their new customers.  The District Manager will ask either the District Coordinator or the Store Manager of the blending location to undertake a mailing campaign to contact all customers.  If the store is asked to handle the mailing campaign, the District Manager will draft a letter for store use.  If this is handled at the District Office, be sure to ask for a copy of the letter in order to answer any questions customers may ask pertaining to the letter.  The letter customers receive will:

  • Introduce the Store and Store Manager. 
  • Invite the customers to stop by and get to know the staff.
  • Assure the customers that the blending store is doing everything they can to make the transition transparent for the customer.  Note if the phone number of the closing store will remain available.  Mention the training efforts of the blending store staff.
  • Mention if a member of the staff of the closing store will be moving to the blending store.
  • Express excitement at doing business with the new brand and new customers.

Multi-Mission Letter Campaign

The multi-mission letter campaign is critical in retaining current customers of the blending location.  Existing customers of the blending store may be confused by changes.  This letter will address the changes with existing customers and get them excited about the new purchasing opportunities they will be seeing.  The District Manager will ask either the District Coordinator or the Store Manager of the blending location to undertake a mailing campaign to contact all customers.  If the store is asked to handle the mailing campaign, the District Manager will draft a letter for your use.  If this is handled at the District Office, be sure to ask for a copy of the letter in order to answer any questions customers may ask pertaining to the letter.  The letter customers receive will:

  • Inform the customer that they will soon see a new brand in their store.  Let them know what the core product offerings are and state the store's excitement at being able to offer a new product with a great reputation in the painting business.
  • State the date on which the new product line(s) will be available.
  • Inform the customer of any changes they may see in signage to dispel any confusion.
  • Assure the customers that the store will continue to carry all the existing products and that they will continue to receive the same great from the same staff.

Store Signage

You will receive new signs to advertise the blending and the products which will be offered.  For example, if an MAB store is closing and blending with a Duron store, the Duron location may receive a sign stating something to the effect of:  MAB Products Proudly Offered.  Be sure that these signs are prominently displayed in the store.
