Store Blending - Inventory Overview

  • The closing store is not likely to run a closing promotion on core product remaining in their location.  The District Manager will plan on using an IST to move core product from the closing location to the blending location to help stock the shelves for the new brand. The District Manager will provide guidance on placing the store's first STAR Order for the new brand of products. 
  • Within a week or so after blending, the blending store will want to begin review their Min/Max levels on all products.  New Min/Max values will need to be established for all new products being carried.  If most customers from the closing store have moved to the blending store as their home store location, you may adopt the Min/Max values of the closing store for the new product offerings. 
  • Store employees should also review Min/Max values of existing products.  With additional products and customers in the store, buying trends may change.  It may take several ordering cycles for the blending store to establish good Min/Max levels.  During this time, it is strongly recommended that all suggested STAR Orders are very carefully reviewed before release.
