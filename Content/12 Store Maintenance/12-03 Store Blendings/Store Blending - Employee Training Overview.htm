Store Blending - Employee Training Overview

When blending stores, it is imperative that the employees working in the blended store are familiar with the new product line they will be offering to their customers.  The District Manager will set up some opportunities for blending store employees to familiarize themselves with their new brand and learn about:

  • Brand History
  • Selling the new brand and making positive comparisons between all brands sold in the blending location. This will prepare employees to offer helpful advice when customers ask how similar products compare between the different brands offered
  • Core product lines and the important characteristics of the products offered
  • Unique product information
  • The new brand’s color palette and multi-mission tinting software
  • The blending store’s new co-branded mission

Depending on the needs of the district and the details of the blending, other training opportunities may exist.  The Store Manager or Assistant Manager at the closing location may be retained and brought to the blending location as an employee.  This employee brings the following skills to the blended store: 

  • Knowledge and experience with the new product line
  • Familiarity with the new customers and their needs 

Another option the District Manager may choose is to staff the closing store with employees from the blending store during the closing period.  This hands-on training will provide these employees a crash course in products, color, and customers at the closing store.  Additionally, the customers will become acquainted with some of the employees they will be doing business with in the future.
