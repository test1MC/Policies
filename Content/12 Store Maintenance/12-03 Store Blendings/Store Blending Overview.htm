Store Blending Overview

District Managers are responsible for all tasks and duties associated with the closing of a location or the blending of two locations.  There are several tasks, detailed in this chapter, which the District Manager may delegate to the Store Manager.  When the decision is made to blend stores, the employees of the blending location will be informed before an official announcement is made to the Sherwin-Williams community at large.   

For more information on the store role in closing a location, see Store Closing Overview.
