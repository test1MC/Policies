Store Blending - Fixed Assets Overview

  • The District Manager will spend much time in the blended location to determine its future needs.  The blending will bring new business to the location and the store should be as prepared as possible for increased traffic and new stocking needs.  The District Manager will assess the fixed assets of the store to try and anticipate the future needs in terms of warehouse racking, gondolas, displays, shakers, tinters that may be required in order to service the increased customer traffic in the store.  The District Manager will also evaluate how to plan-o-gram the front room and storage areas to display and store the new product lines. 
  • The blending store will also receive multi-mission software for tinting. This software will allow this blending store to tint for multiple brands. For questions on the use of multi-mission software, contact the Color Resources Group at 1-800-232-5192.
