Automotive Break-in or Vandalism Policy

This section is only for Automotive Branches.

  1. In the case of a break-in/vandalism, the Landlord should be notified immediately. A Loss Prevention Loss, Occurrence, & Incident (LOI) Report must also be completed.
    1. If a police report is issued, please include the police report number and a copy of the report.
    2. Include pictures of all the damage caused by the casualty.
  2. Forward LOI Report and all supporting documentation to the Loss Prevention Director and Automotive Real Estate.
