Automotive Branch Repairs Overview

This section is only for Automotive Branches.

The process for addressing repairs at the Branch is very important. Once a repair issue arises at the Branch, review Branch Facility / Building Repairs Memo from Automotive Finishes.

Each lease is different. However, in most of the Sherwin-Williams Leases the Landlord is responsible for the roof, the exterior/structural, unexposed plumbing and unexposed electrical. Generally, S-W is responsible for the repair/replacement of the HVAC, exposed plumbing (faucets, sink, hot water tank) and replacement of the plate glass and doors. S-W is also responsible for any damage that may be caused to the building or premises by S-W employees, customers, etc. If you are unsure if the Landlord is responsible for the repair, please contact Automotive Real Estate directly.
