Gross Profit Worksheet for Rentals Overview

You have the ability for the store computer to help calculate your gross profit by providing you the estimated cost in your Book inventory from a rented piece of equipment when you choose to sell the rental equipment. 

Note: If a Gross Profit Worksheet has not been created, the following message will appear, “There is no gross profit worksheet available for this rental.”  This validation prevents the store from selling a rental equipment product without first creating a Gross Profit Worksheet.
