Rental Equipment Expense Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.

2

Press the Page Down key to access the Special Menu.

3

Select Store Expense from the Special Menu.

4

Select Destroyed Merchandise (G/L account 7599-104) to process the expense.

5

Enter the store information in the Bill To section.

6

Enter the Class Number 770-800 (Rental Equip-Spray).

7

Enter the Quantity and Cost of the unit.

Note: The cost of the unit can be determined by using the Gross Profit Worksheet in the Merchandise Management Menu.

8

Press F9 [COMMENT] and enter the unit description and serial number.

9

Press F1 [ACCEPT] to complete the transaction.

10

Update the term/trans numbers of the expensed rental unit in the Serialized Equipment Rentals/Sale Menu located in the Inventory Adjustment Menu in MMS.

Note: For questions regarding Warranty Claims, see Repair Customer Order in Spray Source Repair Order Procedure.
