Rental Equipment Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Many Sherwin-Williams stores offer a rental equipment program to assist with customers’ spray painting, surface preparation, and cleaning needs. Store rental equipment may include:

  • Paint sprayers
  • Pressure washers (Select TAG locations only)
  • Carpet cleaners
  • Wallpaper steamers
  • Plural pumps (U.S. locations only)

A gross profit worksheet is maintained for rental equipment to help the store with calculating gross profit and maintaining the estimated Book cost. Rental equipment must be sold with a gross profit worksheet. For more information, please see Gross Profit Worksheet for Rentals Overview.

For additional help, please see: Rental Equipment Overview.
