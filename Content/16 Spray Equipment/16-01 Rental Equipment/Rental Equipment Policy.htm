Rental Equipment Policy

  1. District Manager Approval is required to operate serialized and non-serialized rental equipment programs.
  2. A Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement must be completed for each rental transaction.
  3. Rentals are permitted to customers 21 years of age or older.
  4. All required fields in the Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement must be accurately filled out for all cash and charge customers.
  5. Driver license information is required for all rental agreements, regardless of the customer’s charge status.
  6. Billing for a rental must be completed at least monthly if the rental period is longer than one month.
  7. These deposits are required for all customers without a Sherwin-Williams Charge Account in good standing prior to rental:
    1. A $500 deposit for larger equipment (Paint Sprayers or Pressure Washers).
    2. A $100 deposit for all other rental equipment and secure a minimum two day rental fee.
    3. For In-Store Pressure Washer rental programs, refer to Pressure Washer Equipment Overview.
    4. No-charge rentals require a deposit and will be refunded upon return of the equipment.
  8. If a customer rents two spray units, you are required to secure a deposit for each spray unit.
  9. Cash, credit card or certified checks are the only acceptable form of payment.
  10. The deposit amount must be processed on a point of sale transaction prior to the customer leaving the store with the rental equipment. For more information on deposits, refer to Deposit Processing Overview.
  11. The customer must be shown how to operate the equipment and acknowledge understanding prior to the equipment leaving the store.
  12. The customer must sign the Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement agreeing to the following:
    1. "I/WE, the undersigned, do hereby rent and accept the above listed equipment which is in good working order and agree to return said equipment in good working order or pay the reasonable cost of placing said equipment in that condition. If said equipment is lost or destroyed while in my/our possession, payment will be made in cash of its full value at once."
    2. "I/WE do hereby release, indemnify and save harmless The Sherwin-Williams Company, ("owner"), from any and all claims, expenses, damages and liabilities of any nature whatsoever arising out of the rental and use of the equipment and consumable parts."
    3. "Equipment is the property of Owner and shall be returned on or before the Due Back Date, to the Owner's address below. If Customer has given Owner false information, or fails or refuses to return equipment on the Due Back Date, or to pay full Rental Amount, then Customer shall be deemed to have converted the equipment unlawfully, in which case Owner or his agent may peaceably repossess equipment at Customer's sole cost and risk, including attorney's fees. No terms hereof may be waived unless in writing and signed by Owner's Store Manager."
  13. A signed copy of the Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement and a point-of-sale transaction must be retained in the COE RENTAL AGREEMENT folder.
  14. No-charge rentals are not encouraged, but if a no-charge rental is made it should be for only large contractors or preferred customers with accounts in good standing.
  15. You must have District Manager approval to move rental equipment back to the product sales number. The unit cannot have been used.
  16. Items for sale cannot be on the same customer order entry transaction. They must be processed separately.
