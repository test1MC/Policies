Rental Equipment History Overview

Rental equipment activity can be searched by:

  • Serial number
  • Sales number
  • Received date

From the returned query results, you can view or print a detailed rental history report. The Detailed Rental History Report contains a line for each scheduled rental for the piece of equipment and shows information including:

  • Scheduled rental dates
  • Scheduled fees
  • Actual rental fee collected
  • Difference between the scheduled and actual rental fee

There is the option of viewing, printing, or saving the results to a file. The Spray Equipment Identification Log contains information on all of the pieces of equipment that were queried for in the search.
