Rental Equipment Repair Overview

Sherwin-Williams offers repair services on a variety of spray equipment, spray guns, and pressure washers. Spray Source Repair Centers are dedicated locations equipped to perform repair work. The repair of spray equipment is highly specialized and our Spray Source Technicians are trained to handle the multitude of issues that may arise with a unit and the questions that customers may ask. Customers may bring equipment into any Sherwin-Williams store location for to request repair, however, spray equipment repairs should be handled by and transacted at the closest Spray Source Repair Center. It may be necessary due to geographical location for some stores to send repairs to external vendors.

For more information on equipment repairs and Spray Source services, please see Spray Equipment Repair Overview.
