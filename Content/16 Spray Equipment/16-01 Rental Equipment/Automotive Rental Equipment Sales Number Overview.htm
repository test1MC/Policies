Automotive Rental Equipment Sales Number Overview

This only applies to Automotive Branches.

After entering the sales number for the equipment, a window will return to the screen with a list of serial numbers for the specific equipment sales number. After making the proper selection, the serial number will display in line item comments.

Note: Certain Automotive part numbers require a serial number to be recorded in POS in order to sell them. Find the Product Requiring a Serial Number Form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “serial” in the Form Name Field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.
