Update Terminal or Transaction Number for Rentals Procedure

Once the rental unit is sold, you must enter the term/trans number and date for the point-of-sale transaction and CLOSE the transaction to properly relieve your rental inventory.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Adjustment -> Select Update Term/Trans Numbers from the Serialized Equipment Rentals/Sales Menu.

2

Enter the Serial Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the serial number.

3

Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue to the next step.

4

Select Enter POS Term Tran & Date from the Options Menu.

5

Scan the Customer Receipt or enter the Terminal No, Transaction No, and Date.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] OR CANCEL [F5] to cancel the changes.

7

You will receive two warning messages asking if you are sure you want to proceed. 

Note: If you answer Y to both questions, your actions are final and CANNOT be changed.

8

Enter Y to accept the changes, enter N to cancel the changes.
