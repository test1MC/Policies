Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement Procedure

Step

Action

Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select Customer Order Entry from the Main Menu.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select Add Order from the Order Entry Menu.

5

Does the order have an assigned PO Number or Job Number?

If Then
Yes

Enter the PO and/or Job Number.
No Press ENTER to continue.

6

Select Rental Equipment from the Customer Service Options screen.

7

Does the customer have an account?

If Then
Yes

Enter the Customer Number, or press LOOKUP [F3] to look up the customer -> Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue.
No Select the Customer Type and enter the customer information -> Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue.

8

Is there a site associated with the order?

If Then
Yes Enter the Site Details or select a site.
No Continue to Additional Information for Rental Agreement.

Note: Site locations are only available if the Floorcovering Software is installed. See 14-02 Site Repository for more information.

9

Enter the Use Location, or select Yes if the Bill-To and Ship-To addresses are the same. Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue to additional information.

Additional Information for Rental Agreement

1

Enter the Serial Number or Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the equipment.

2

Does this rental include component equipment?

If Then
Yes

Select Yes -> Enter the Serial Number or Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the add-on equipment.

Note: Selecting a rental equipment type (such as spray equipment or pressure washer) will assist with locating the equipment.
No Proceed to Step 3.

Note: Component equipment is used for plural pump components or add-on accessories for standards rentals.

3

Select the Length of the Rental: Day or Week.

4

Enter  the Start Date/Time and Return Due Date/Time.

Note: A return date must be entered, otherwise there is no contract between S-W and the customer. The customer has the option to contact the SW store to request an extension of the rental equipment. In this case, the return date must be updated so the customer can be accurately billed for the rental period and the depreciation is captured.

5

Enter the customer information. Capture complete contact information for the customer.

Note: If the order is released, it cannot be changed. The order would have to be canceled and re-entered as a new order.

6

Identify the Class Number for the type of rental:

If

Then

Pressure Washer

Use Class Number 779925

Spray Equipment

Use Class Number 770825

7

Enter the Class Number in the Sales Field.

8

Enter the quantity and the deposit amount in the price field.

Note: Quantity should be the number of rental days and the price should be the daily rental rate.

9

Process a deposit, if required.  For more information, refer to Deposit Processing Overview.

Note: Do not forget that no-charge rentals require a deposit if the customer does not have a SW Charge Account in good standing.

10

Are you charging the customer for the rental?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 11.
No

Press the DISC [F11] key to dollar discount the rental. Press the COMMENTS key and enter NO-CHARGE.

Note: No-charge Customer Order Entry Rental Agreements must be accommodated in Order Disposition.

11

Press ACCEPT [F1] to accept the rental transaction.

Note: A standard Customer Rental Agreement will print out for the customer to sign. For Pressure Washer equipment, two copies of an additional warning page will print out. Your customer must read this document and initial the warnings, cautions, and instructions acknowledging that they have been informed and understand the information. Retain the initialed Pressure Washer Warning along with the signed Customer Rental Agreement.

12

If required, process the Customer Rental Deposit on a Point-of-Sale Transaction.

For information on recording rental inventory, see Record Rental Equipment Procedure.
