Spray Tip and Accessory Security Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

The Spray Accessories merchandising cabinet is to be stocked and merchandised as per the Spray Accessories Planogram in your Planogram Book. The Titan Spray Tip Display is also an approved form of merchandising these items. 

  1. Spray cabinets may remain unlocked and the glass removed with approval from the District Manager. Decisions regarding the security of these items will be made based on the local market in which a store is located.  Stores are not required to retain any written approval from the DM regarding the decision to unlock the cabinets and remove the glass. 

There are two exceptions to this policy:

  1. All ProShot Tips and Accessories on the free-standing ProShot Display may remain unlocked.
  2. All TrueCoat Plus Tips and Accessories on the Tradeworks Display may remain unlocked

Note: All spray equipment must be secured at all times in all districts. The ProShot and TrueCoat Plus units should be locked to their respective displays. 
The keys to all locked spray units and merchandising cabinets are to be kept in the store safe or locked filing cabinet when not in use.
