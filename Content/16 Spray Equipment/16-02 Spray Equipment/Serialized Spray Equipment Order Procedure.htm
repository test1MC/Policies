Serialized Spray Equipment Order Procedure

Step

Action

Order Serialized Equipment

1

Place an External Purchase Order with the Vendor.

Note: Product serial numbers are not required to place a purchase order to the vendor in Merchandise Management.

For additional help on placing an order, please see: External Purchase Order Procedure.

Receive Serialized Equipment

1

Upon physical receipt of the equipment, receive the Purchase Order.

Note: Serial numbers for equipment must be captured during the receiving process. Enter the Serial Number in the SE Serial Number Field.

For more information, please see:  07-02 A Receive/Verify PO

2

Was the Serial Number entered correctly?

If Then
Yes The procedure is complete.
No From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management.

3

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

4

Select Inventory Adjustment from the Merchandise Management Menu.

5

Select Perpetual Adjustment from the Inventory Adjustment Menu.

6

Enter the Product Sales Number.

7

Enter the updated number (perpetual count minus 1) in the Actual Count Field.

8

Delete the incorrect Serial Number and press ACCEPT [F1].

9

Enter the Unit Cost, PO Number, and an explanation for the adjustment in the Memo Field. Press Accept [F1] to complete the transaction.

Note: You will need to do another perpetual adjustment for Sales Number and add 1 to the Actual Count Field. Enter the correct Serial Number and complete the rest of the fields (Steps 6-9).

Note: If required to complete the Uniform Commercial Code Financing Statement, use prime-sub 7599-099 to process the Miscellaneous Disbursement transaction to pay for the UCC filing.
