Serialized Spray Equipment Log Procedure

When equipment is received on a purchase order, entered in as a customer order, or sold on a point-of-sale transaction, the information is automatically recorded on a system-generated spray equipment log.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select Customer Order Entry from the Main Menu.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select Reports from the Order Entry Menu.

4

Select Serialized Equipment Log from the Order Entry Reports.
