Rental Spray Equipment Sale Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

When you sell or transfer (IST or ISR) rental spray equipment to another store use class number 7708GP. Include the serial number when transferring to ensure that you relieve your inventory properly.

Note: You will not be able to sell rental equipment without a gross profit worksheet. For more information, please see Gross Profit Worksheet for Rentals Overview.
