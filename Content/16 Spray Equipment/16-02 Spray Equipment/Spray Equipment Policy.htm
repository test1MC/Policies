Spray Equipment Policy

  1. Approval to stock, rent, or repair spray equipment must be obtained from the District Manager.
  2. All spray equipment units must be ordered, received, and sold using the correct sales number and serial number. Properly recorded spray equipment will be automatically recorded on the system generated Spray Equipment Log.
  3. Spray equipment must be secured by chain to a fixed object in the store. Equipment stored in boxes must be kept in a safe area away from customers. For example, boxed units should be stored on top shelves in the back room.
    1. The Titan Flex Spray Handheld and Graco Handheld units do not require securement. However, in high theft areas, they can be locked in the Spray Cabinet if needed.
  4. Sale of Spray Equipment must be registered via Uniform Equipment Code Form in your state.
    1. Contact your District Credit Office for filing instructions. This is not required when the equipment is paid for in full at the time of delivery (Cash, Check, or Credit Card). Contact the District Credit Office for your requirements.
    2. Spray equipment fuel tanks must not contain fuel while displayed or warehoused at the store.
