Spray Equipment Rental Conversion Procedure

Spray and Pressure Washer Equipment can be converted to rental status in one function. This feature eliminates the guess work of figuring out the cost of a rental unit.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Adjustment -> Serialized Equipment Rentals/Sales Menu -> Select Move Serialized Equipment to Rental.

2

Does the piece of equipment that you are moving into rental have a serial number?

If Then
Yes Enter the Sales Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to look up the sales number.
No

Enter the Sales Number for the non-serialized piece of equipment or press LOOKUP [F3] to look up the sales number.

Note: The system will only generate a serial number if the product is from department 50 or 53. The following message will appear: “Sales# being moved into rental is not serialized! A serial# will be automatically generated for this unit? Select ‘Y’ to generate a serial number and move the sales number into rental."

3

Select the Equipment Type from Equipment Type List.

4

Select the Serial Number from the list.

5

Enter the make and model for the piece of equipment.

6

Select Yes to move the Spray Equipment into Rental.

Did you select the correct piece of equipment and serial number?

If Then
Yes The conversion process is complete.
No Select Move Spray Equipment to Rental from the Spray Equipment Rentals/Sales Menu.

Note: After successfully moving the equipment into rental, a barcode label will print. If this is a system generated serial number, apply the label to the equipment.

7

Enter Sales Number 400-1624 and select Yes.

8

Select the Serial Number from the list to be reversed.

Note: You can only reverse a piece of equipment if it has not been rented.
