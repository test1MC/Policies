Spray Equipment Demonstration Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  1. Spray Equipment cannot be demonstrated unless the Sales Representative or Store Manager is present.
  2. Demonstrations should only be made to potential customers that have made a reasonable commitment or customers that have Sherwin-Williams accounts in good standing.
  3. The Customer Order Entry Rental Agreement must be completed for a Spray Equipment demonstration. Settle the order line [cancel order line] in Order Disposition if the unit is not transferred or sold.
  4. A cash deposit must be secured or a deposit must be charged to the customer’s account.
  5. The Sherwin-Williams' Sales Representative or Store Manager is responsible for the Spray Equipment and the demonstration.
