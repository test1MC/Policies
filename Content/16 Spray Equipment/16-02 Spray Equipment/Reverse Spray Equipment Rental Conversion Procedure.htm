Reverse Spray Equipment Rental Conversion Procedure

If you made the mistake of moving the incorrect piece of spray equipment or have a piece of rental equipment that was NEVER rented, you can reverse the rental conversion process.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Adjustment -> Serialized Equipment Rentals/Sales Menu -> Select Move Serialized Equipment to Rental.

2

Enter 400-1624 in the Sales Number Field.

3

Do you want to reverse the serial number from rental?

If Then
Yes Select Y to continue to the next step.
No Select N to exit the screen.

Note: Reversals from rental to the original sales number can only be done if there has been no rental activity.

4

Select Perpetual Adjustment from the Inventory Adjustment Menu.

5

Enter sales number 400-1624 in the Sales Number Field.

6

Reduce the sales number count by 1 in the Actual Count Field.

Note: Upon selecting Yes, a list of available serial numbers that can be reversed will be displayed.

7

Select the Serial Number you wish to reverse and press ENTER.

Note: A screen will appear notifying you that the reversal was successful.
