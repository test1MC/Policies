Spray Equipment Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Sherwin-Williams sells spray equipment supplied by manufacturers such as Graco, Titan, Speedflo, and SprayTECH. The wide selection of products offered by these suppliers allows Sherwin-Williams to provide customers with spray equipment that can meet their individual needs. Most equipment is identified by a manufacturer’s serial number. In this situation, the equipment must be received, rented, and sold using this serial number to properly record equipment movement. Refer to Serialized Equipment to set up the equipment. In certain situations, equipment does not come with a manufacturer provided serial number. For example, plural pumps come with additional components that must be tracked in inventory. Non-serialized equipment will be assigned a serial number by the system during the rental conversion process and a barcode label will be printed. Refer to Non-Serialized Equipment.

For additional help, please see: Rental Equipment Help.
