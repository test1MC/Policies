Door Decal (7” x 3 ¾ “)

Clean inside of door. Remove backing and attach to inside of front door.

Back Lit Sign (22”x13”)

Hang in front window using chain shipped with sign.

Signs
  1. Determine the best locations in your store based on its configuration.
  2. Carefully measure the hole locations on the signage and make corresponding marks on the wall where signs are to be positioned.
  3. Push an awl into the marked spots on the wall to create guide holes.
  4. Using a screwdriver, screw the plastic wall anchors provided with the sign all the way into the wall.
  5. Attach signs to the wall anchors using metal screws provided with the sign.
