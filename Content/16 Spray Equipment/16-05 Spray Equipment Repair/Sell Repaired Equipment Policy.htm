Sell Repaired Equipment Policy

  1. The Store Manager is responsible for notifying the District Manager when equipment is not picked within 30 days of customer notification of repair.
  2. Customers should be notified that Sherwin-Williams is not responsible for repaired units left after 90 (ninety) days.
