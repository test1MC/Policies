Spray Source Repair Transfer Order Policy

The process for sending a repair customer order from one store to a repair location is the Transfer Order Process, which allows a single customer order to be viewed by both the originating and repair location.

  1. The transfer process is ONLY available for customer repairs being sent to a repair location. Store equipment requiring repair cannot be sent through the transfer process and must be handled manually.
  2. The transfer process for repairs is not available for all store mission combinations. Reference the following chart to determine to which locations you may transfer a repair order.
  3. If using the manual process, originating stores must still create a customer repair order to generate a repair ticket for the repair location and to obtain the customer signature. The customer order may later be canceled if the billing occurs at the repair location.
  4. When selecting a repair location, consider transportation costs and select the closest location to your store that meets the needs of the repair
