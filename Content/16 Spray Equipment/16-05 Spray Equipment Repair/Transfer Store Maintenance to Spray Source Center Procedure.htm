Transfer Store Maintenance to Spray Source Center Procedure

Stores wishing to transfer repairs to Spray Source locations must first complete a one-time setup in Transfer Store Maintenance. It will define the stores to which you may transfer orders. Include ONLY those stores, which will fulfill your transferred orders.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Select Maintenance from the Customer Order Entry Menu.

2

Select Transfer Store Maintenance from the Order Entry Maintenance Menu.

3

Use the Store Lookup browse to search for and add the stores to which you will transfer orders for repair.

4

Press ACCEPT [F1] to save your entries.
