Deposit on Spray Source Repair at Originating Store Overview

DIY Customer/Cash Customer/SW Charge Customer (Account on Credit Hold)

If

Then

Repair
  • $100 Customer Deposit processed when unit brought in for repair
  • Customer should be charged for labor and parts (notwithstanding warranty claims)
  • Call originating store location for the amount of the original deposit and to ensure that the deposit amount is still available for use. Ask for the terminal and transaction number
  • Email Michael Fuchs at michael.j.fuchs@sherwin.com to request that deposit funds be moved from the originating store to SpraySource.
  • PerformSpray Source Correction Transaction Procedure.

No Repair
  • $100 Customer Deposit processed when unit brought in for repair
  • Customer should be charged Diagnostic Fee only
  • Call originating store location for the amount of the original deposit and to ensure that the deposit amount is still available for use. Confirm the amount of the deposit
  • Request the store refund the customer the remaining amount of the deposit minus the Diagnostic Fee. For example, if a $100 deposit was taken and the Diagnostic Fee is $70, the store would refund the customer $30. 
  • Keep in mind that tax may apply in your jurisdiction
  • Ask for the terminal and transaction number 
  • Email Michael Fuchs at michael.j.fuchs@sherwin.com to request that the remaining deposit funds be moved from the originating store to SpraySource
  • Perform Spray Source Correction Transaction Procedure.

Note: For more information on how to process a deposit, refer to Deposit Processing Overview.
