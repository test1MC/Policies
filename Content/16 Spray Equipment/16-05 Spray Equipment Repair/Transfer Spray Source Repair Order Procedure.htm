Transfer Spray Source Repair Order Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive or Product Finishes.

Complete this step ONLY after you have created the customer order for repair. Be aware that once the order has been transferred, the originating store may no longer make changes to the order. The repair location will complete the repair, adding information for labor charges, warranty information, and parts to the customer order. An ISR is automatically created on the originating store’s system when a transfer is performed.  The repair location will handle the sale of the repair order.  When the order is sold, the originating store's customer order will be automatically closed and the ISR will be automatically canceled. The repair location will ship the repaired equipment back to either the customer or the originating store if it is not being picked up by the customer at the repair location.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select Customer Order Entry from the Main Menu.

2

Select Transfer Order from the Customer Order Menu.

3

Narrow your search in the Order Lookup Browse or press ACCEPT [F1] to view all orders. Navigate to and select your order.

4

Enter the store number for repair location, which will repair the equipment. Press ACCEPT [F1].

5

Select YES at the prompt, TRANSFER THIS ORDER TO?

6

Press ENTER at the confirmation that the order has been transferred.

7

The transferred order will create a corresponding ISR Purchase Order. This purchase order should be canceled as billing will occur at the Spray Source Repair Center.
