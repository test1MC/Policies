Spray Source Safety Policy

  1. All Spray Source facilities must use only non-solvent cleaners in their parts machines. Flammable solvents should never be used in these machines.
  2. Any solvent used in a Spray Source facility must be kept in a METAL container with the lid securely fastened when not in use.
  3. All used solvents must be kept in a steel drum and handled in accordance with our Hazardous Waste Policies.
  4. Stores performing repairs at their location should be aware of the following policies regarding tools:
    1. No personal tools or equipment are permitted at the store.
    2. No Sherwin-Williams' tools will be borrowed by or loaned to anyone.
    3. Sherwin-Williams' tools must be marked with the store number.
  5. Stores performing repairs at their location should be aware of the following safety policies:
    1. The use of flammable and combustible solvents for parts cleaning is strictly prohibited.
    2. It is recommended that stores use water-soluble gun and part cleaners.
    3. Any solvent used in a Spray Source Repair Center must be kept in a metal container with the lid securely fastened when not in use.
    4. All used solvents must be kept in a steel drum and handled in accordance with our hazardous waste policies.
    5. All personnel involved in the repair, testing or demonstration of any equipment will be provided with the proper personal protective equipment.
    6. All Sherwin-Williams' personnel are required to wear such equipment as provided.
    7. Stores should be aware of all policies and procedures in the LiveSafe Administrator’s Guide regarding the usage and handling of hazardous materials.
