Spray Source Signage Policy

  1. All Spray Source Repair Centers should have and display the following signage:
    1. Door Decal (7”x3 ¾”) - Display on main entrance doorway
    2. Back Lit Sign (22”x13”) - Place in front window of store location
    3. Repair Rate Sign (24”x36”) - Display prominently at wrap counter
    4. Rental Equipment Sign (24”x36”) - Place by sales counter or rental unit area
    5. Poly Banner (72”x36”) - For general use in repair area or at Pro Shows
  2. All non-Spray Source stores should display the Spray Source counter display regarding Sherwin-Williams’ Spray Source Repair Center program.  The counter display consists of one acrylic display with graphic insert, address cards with the location information of the nearest Spray Source location, spray equipment decals with the location information of the nearest Spray Source location, and brochures.
  3. Stores with an hourly repair rate greater than $70.00 should call 216-566-2338 or 216-566-2423 to receive numbers to adjust their signage.
