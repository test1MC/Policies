Spray Equipment Incentive Policy

  1. An approved and signed supply agreement is required:
    1. Agreement must clearly state incentives provided by SW.
    2. Agreement must clearly state purchase requirements of customer.
  2. Customers must have an account and that account must be used for the gratis customer order.
  3. Gratis spray equipment product on a customer order must remain on a separate order than products that are not gratis; this means that two separate orders may be needed.
