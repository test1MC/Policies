Large Spray Equipment Program (LSEP) Policy

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

  1. Any LSEP sale greater than $100,000 should be processed through the designated LSEP POS terminal. Any sales less than $100,000 should be processed through the normal facility POS.
  2. All customer set-up(s) relating to the LSEP must be handled by the Product Finishes Finance team.
  3. For sales greater than $200,000, customers will be given a new account number specific to the LSEP and assigned to Job 50, regardless of whether or not they already have an account.
  4. For sales greater than $200,000, the dedicated POS cost center will be the “home store” and the territory will be the house account (0100) for newly created LSEP customer account numbers.
  5. Prior to initiating an LSEP sale, obtain the following documents and save them to the PFFacilties shared drive(\\cgconhq.sw.sherwin.com\PPFacilities\LargeEquipment\Installers):
    1. Installer’s W-9
    2. Current certificate of insurance
    3. Signed, Job-specific subcontractor agreement form
