Invoice the Customer per the Equipment Sales Summary Procedure

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

Step

Action

1

Verify the Weighted Average Cost.

2

Process a Charge Return for each deposit transaction.

 

Invoice all merchandise using a Charge Sale transaction.

Note: If the customer needed freight billed as a separate line of the invoice, use Non-Merchandise Code “4”: Freight—FOB  Origin.

3

Include the rep name and territory in the PO # or Comment section, depending on whether or not the customer requires a PO number on the invoice.

4

Track each Charge Return using a Customer Deposit Log located in the Forms Repository.
