Large Spray Equipment Purchase Order Procedure

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Add New Purchase Order.

2

Select External Order.

3

Enter the appropriate sales numbers, quantities, and costs.

4

Verify the accuracy of the PO.

5

Determine how the customer will pay for freight:

If

Then

The customer needs freight billed as a separate line of the invoice

Do not include the freight on the PO.

Note: The freight will be charged to the customer separately using Non-Merchandise Code “4”: Freight—FOB  Origin.

The customer does not need freight to be billed separately

Include it in the total PO so that it is part of the merchandise Weighted Average Cost.

Note: Ensure that the cost of items plus margin % does not exceed the maximum invoice amount (49,999.99).
