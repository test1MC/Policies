Installer Payment Procedure

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

In order to process installer payments, it must be approved by the following Management members:

Management Position

Approval Amount

Regional Facility Member

Total Amount > $2,500

Regional Sales Director

Total Amount > $35,000

Senior VP of Sales

Total Amount > $40,000

PF Controller

Total Amount > $50,000

PF President

Total Amount > $50,000

GFG Controller

Total Amount > $65,000

GFG President

Total Amount > $65,000

Note: GFG General Accounting is always included in the approval process and has to be the final approver.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Source Operations -> Processes -> SWeep.

2

Select Enter Expense Invoice.

3

Select the correct Installer from the list of vendors/payees.

4

Fill in the required information.

5

Attach the required documents and select Next.

6

Review the prime-sub and amount details. Select Next.

Note: The correct prime-sub to use for installer payments automatically will be generated in the Prime-Sub and Prime-Sub Description boxes. The prime-sub will be 1693-120 (Payment to Installers).

7

Fill in the following information in the Comments box:

  • Customer Account Number and Name
  • Job Number (Job 50)
  • Description of the job/work performed (include percentages)

8

 

Upload the Installer invoice into SWeep before submitting the invoice.

Note: To be approved, each installer payment invoice in SWeep will require an attachment of the Installer invoice.
