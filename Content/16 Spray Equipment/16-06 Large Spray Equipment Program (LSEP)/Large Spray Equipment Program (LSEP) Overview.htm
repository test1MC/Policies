Large Spray Equipment Program (LSEP) Overview

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

Each region will have one dedicated POS in which all LSEP transactions must be processed. These transactions include, but are not limited to:

  • Customer deposits
  • Merchandise orders through MMS
  • Sales/correction transactions
  • Istaller payments through SWeep
