Finishing Systems Equipment Quote Procedure

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

Step

Action

1

Determine the customer’s equipment systems needs.

2

Obtain price quotes from the vendors.

3

Complete the Price Quote Form, including the installation terms, etc.

Note: If anything changes (i.e. equipment, price, installation terms), a new price quote should be issued.

4

Acquire approval from the Product Finishes Divison Finance Department by providing Ben Johannsen or Sam Schirripa with all of the following information via email:

  • Customer’s name, address, and SW account number
  • Equipment sales summary pro forma
  • Copy of equipment quote from the vendors
  • Copy of equipment quote for the customer

Note: Once all the necessary information is received, the approval process begins in EFCA and goes through several contacts. As such, this process can take anywhere between a couple days to a couple weeks. Contact Ben Johannsen intermittently to check on the approval status. 

5

Obtain the installer quote.

6

Confirm customer approval with Ben Johannsen.

7

Have the customer and a member of Sherwin-Williams Management sign the price quote:

If

Then

The equipment sale sub-totals less than 100,000

The Equipment Systems Sales Executive is authorized to sign the quote on behalf of Sherwin-Williams. Forward the fully executed agreement to Ben Johannsen.

The equipment sale sub-totals more than 100,000

Forward the customer signed price quote to Ben Johannsen for review. He will submit it to the E-Expense process.

Note: Any changes to the terms and conditions of the price quote will need to be negotiated between Sherwin-Williams and the customer in an Equipment Purchase Agreement.

8

Forward a copy of the customer’s purchase order and Sherwin-Wiliams’ acknowledgement of purchase order to Ben Johannsen.

9

Prepare the installation agreement.

10

Contact the Credit Office to have a customer job account set up for all systems.

Note: All new accounts will be set up as house accounts (0100).
