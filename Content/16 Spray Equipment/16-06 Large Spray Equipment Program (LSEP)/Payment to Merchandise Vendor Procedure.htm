Payment to Merchandise Vendor Procedure

This section only applies to the LSEP dedicated POS location cost centers, which process equipment sales totals of $100,000 or more.

Step

Action

1

Request that the vendor prepare invoices to match the payment terms (30/30/30/10%).

2

Receive PO line items in accordance with this payment agreement.

Note: The PO line quantities might be received partially  (using decimals, if necessary) in order to match the payment agreement.

3

After receiving all items from the final invoice, close the PO.

Note: For more information on receiving PO, see 07-02A Receive/Verify PO.

4

Ensure Accounts Payable issues a check for payment accordingly.

Note: The total of vendor payments should never be greater than the amount of the customer deposits. For more information about prepayment of merchandise, see 01-07 Vendor COD Shipments Quickcheck Process.
