Pressure Washer Maintenance Procedure

Select TAG locations only.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Adjustment -> Select Serialized Equipment Rental/Sales Menu from the Inventory Adjustment Menu.

2

Select Update Rental Equipment Type from the Serialized Equipment Rental/Sales Menu.

3

Enter the Serial Number that you created for this piece of equipment.

4

Press the Enter Key and select either: Spray Equipment or Pressure Washer.

Note: Select Pressure Washer as the rental equipment type when completing your inventory write-in pages. For more information on creating Gross Profit Worksheets, see 16-04 Gross Profit Worksheet.
