Pressure Washer Equipment Overview

Select TAG locations only

Sherwin-Williams sells pressure washer equipment supplied by M-I-TM. Only select locations participate in the Pressure Washer Rental Program. To become a participating store, contact the District Manager to request approval. Receipt of pressure washers is a two-step process involving both the receipt of the unit into inventory and conversion into a rental unit. Once the pressure washers are received, participating stores will have a display and a 2700PSI rental unit for their sales floor.

Note: For additional help, please see Rental Equipment Help.
