Wallcovering Special Order Policy

  1. Wallcovering special orders may be processed by either an SW store or SWAT.
  2. Special order wallcovering requires a deposit when the customer does not hold an SW Charge Account in good standing.
    1. Cash deposits can only be processed by the store. In the event of a cash deposit, the order cannot be placed with SWAT.
    2. Deposit amounts should be determined based on where the special order merchandise will be delivered by the vendor:                                                           
    If Then

    Delivered to Store

    25% Deposit Required

    Shipped Direct to Customer

    100% Deposit Required
