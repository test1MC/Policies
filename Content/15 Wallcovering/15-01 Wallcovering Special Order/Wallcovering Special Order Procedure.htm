Wallcovering Special Order Procedure

Step Action

Complete Order Header

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry ->Add Order.

2

Select Wallcovering from the Customer Service Options Menu.

3

Select where the merchandise should be delivered:

If Then

To Store

Select None

Ship Direct to Customer

Select All

4

Identify the customer:

  • Enter the Customer Account Number.
  • Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the Customer Account Number.
  • Enter 1 for a DIY Customer with no account.

5

Are you shipping direct to the customer or processing this order for a customer with no account?

If Then
Yes Enter ShipTo Address information- name and full postal address are required.
No Proceed to Step 6.

Note:  A telephone number or e-mail address for quick contact with the customer is recommended for all orders.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed to the Order Detail.

Complete Order Detail

1

Enter merchandise:

  • Enter the Wallcovering Book and Pattern Number.
  • Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the Wallcovering Book and Pattern Number.

2

Does the customer have an SW Charge Account in good standing?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 6.
No Proceed to Step 3.

3

Enter Deposit Line:

  • Press ENTER on a blank line and select the Non-Merchandise Code for Deposit.
  • Enter 5 on a blank line.

4

Enter a Quantity of 1.

5

Enter the amount of the customer deposit as the Price.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

7

Print two copies of the Wallcovering Order Memo.

  • Provide one copy to the customer.
  • Retain one copy for the store in the Open Orders File.

Additional Steps

1

Did your customer provide a deposit?

If Then
Yes

Process a Sales Transaction for the Deposit.

For additional information, please see:

  • Sale Payment Menu Policy
No

Process an External Purchase Order to order the material required for the order from the Vendor.

For additional information, please see:

  • 07-01C Decorative Products
  • 07-01B External PO - Vendor
  • 07-01D2 EDI PO – Vendor
