Sherwin-Williams Answer Team (SWAT) Overview

The Sherwin-Williams Answer Team (SWAT) is a customer service and support group helping store employees and customers find the perfect wallcovering pattern and gathering all the information necessary to place a wallcovering order. SWAT provides the following types of support:

  • Wallcovering Orders & Answers Line

    • This line assists with specific questions about an order’s details, such as merchandise ordered, cost of merchandise, selling price of merchandise, customer information, and shipping information.

Note: Contact the Store Computer Help Desk if you have questions related to processing transactions or accessing transaction information at 216-566-2740 or scohelpdesk@sherwin.com.

Sherwin-Williams store employees and customers can call SWAT to talk with trained wallcovering experts and to gain access to a computerized product database that provides complete detailed wallcovering pattern information. Stores and customers can browse hundreds of collections online.
