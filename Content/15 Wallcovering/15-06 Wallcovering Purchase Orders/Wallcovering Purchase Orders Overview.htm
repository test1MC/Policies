Wallcovering Purchase Orders Overview

There are several ways to purchase wallcovering for stock and special orders:

  • Wallcovering Purchase Order direct to vendor
    • Wallcovering ordered from a vendor should be placed on an external purchase order. For additional information on placing external purchase orders, please see:
      • 07-01B External PO – Vendor
      • 07-01D2 EDI PO – Vendor
    • When estimating freight on an external purchase order for wallcovering, please refer to the 2018 Wallcovering Division Freight Chart to determine the appropriate estimate. 
      • Note: When receiving an external purchase order for wallcovering, wallcovering freight is not calculated in the WAC and goes to the freight variance account. When freight is sold on wallcovering, it offsets the same account.
  • In-Stock Wallcovering Program

    • In-stock wallcovering order should be placed on a STAR Order. For additional information on placing in-stock wallcovering orders, please see: Special Instructions for Purchase Order Overview.
