Wallcovering Book Check-In Procedure

Step

Action

Check-In Transaction

1

From POS desktop, select Store ->   Point of Sale -> Wallcovering Book Check-In.

2

When prompted to scan the wallcovering book barcode:

If Then
Return by identifying book(s)

Scan the wallcovering book barcode or enter the wallcovering book number. 

Note:  If more than one copy of the wallcovering book is out you will be required to select the correct customer. If only one copy is out, the customer is automatically selected.
Return by identifying customer

Press ENTER to continue with the transaction. Identify the customer in one of two ways:

  • Enter the Customer Account Number.
  • Press LOOKUP [F3]. Press ENTER to view a list of all customers that currently have wallcovering books on loan. Select the correct customer and press ENTER.

3

Scan additional barcodes or select other returned wallcovering books by pressing VIEW [F7] or LOOKUP [F3] to scroll through the books checked out by the customer.

4

Press ENTER to select the book to be returned.

5

Press CANCEL [F5] after all books have been selected.

6

Press TOTAL [F12] when complete.

Book Not Checked Out in System

1

If the wallcovering book has not been checked out by the customer or has been checked out at a different store location, you will see the following message: "Book has not been checked out, continue check in?"

  • Select Yes to check in the wallcovering book.
  • Select No to return to the book number entry screen.

2

Did you select to check in the wallcovering book?

If Then
Yes
  • Identify the reason that the wallcovering book did not show in the Books Checked Out Listing once the customer leaves the store.
    		•
    No The transaction is complete.

    Note: The system views Wallcovering Book Check In Transactions as Miscellaneous Cash Disbursements. If the customer does not receive any money when you perform a Wallcovering Book Check-In Transaction you are not required to batch the Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement as no money changed hands. If you do return the deposit, you are required to keep the signed store copy for the transactions and batch according to normal batching instructions for Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement Transactions.
