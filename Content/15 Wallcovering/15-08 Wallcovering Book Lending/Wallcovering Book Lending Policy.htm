Wallcovering Book Lending Policy

The following policies have been set forth to allow for proper maintenance of your wallcovering lending library:

  1. Each Wallcovering Book must be identified with a unique barcode label located on the outside back cover.
  2. Barcodes must be scanned when Wallcovering Books are checked in or out of your store.
  3. A maximum of three wallcovering books may be checked out at a time.
  4. Process all transactions (Sales, Returns, Corrections, Wallcovering Book Lending System, etc.) using the customer’s Sherwin-Williams charge or cash account number.
    1. Customers must sign for wallcovering book check-out.
  5. Customer identification (Driver’s License) is required for SW customers without an SW Charge Account in good standing if you are not able to swipe the customer’s credit card through the store computer system.
  6. Deposits are required for wallcovering book check-out for all customers who do not have an SW Charge Account in good standing.
    1. Your District Manager has established a per book deposit amount your customers should pay when they borrow wallcovering books at your store. You are required to follow your District guidelines for loaning wallcovering books. Check with your District Manager for more details. 
    2. After receiving District Manager approval you are permitted to change the default Wallcovering Book Lending amount. The default amount for the store computer system is $0.00.
    3. You are required to charge all customers not having an SW Charge Account in good standing the same deposit amount for wallcovering book lending. Each Sherwin-Williams store in your area should charge the same dollar amount for wallcovering book lending.
    4. If your customer does not have an SW Charge Account in good standing, the customer’s credit card should be swiped through the credit card reader if you require a deposit for borrowing the wallcovering books. The card swipe will be used only if the customer does not return the wallcovering books.
  7. Books must be returned to the store by the close of business two business days after check-out unless written District Manager approval is on file at your store.
    1. Customers must be contacted by telephone if wallcovering books are not returned to the store within three business days. Customer contact must be documented in the Wallcovering Lending System.
  8. Customers who do not return their wallcovering books within the allowed check-out period and who have not responded to attempts to contact for the return of the book should be charged $50 per book within ninety (90) days of the original transaction date.
    1. Customers should be charged within ten (10) days of contact regarding unreturned books.
    2. If the customer does not return the books and it is past the ninety (90) day window you must process a store expense for the unreturned books as retrieving credit card information past the ninety (90) day window is not possible.
