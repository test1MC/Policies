Monitor Checked-Out Wallcovering Books Procedure

The yellow and black Status Reminder Box will post the number of customers that have overdue wallcovering books. To review these transactions and document your contact to reminder your customers to return lent materials, follow these procedures:

Step

Action

Generate Report of Overdue Books

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Press INQUIRY [F4] -> Wallcovering Book Library -> Overdue Books.

2

Select to print or view the report.

Document Customer Contact

1

Contact the customer. Obtain an agreeable date and time for the customer to return the books. Remind the customer that they will be charged for the books if they are not returned.

2

Document your conversation by selecting Store from the POS desktop -> Miscellaneous -> Notes for WC Book Lending.

3

Enter your Employee Number.

4

Enter the Customer Account Number or use LOOKUP [F3] to search for the customer.

5

Enter notes from the conversation in the Comments Field. 

Note: Notes from previous contact will display.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] to save notes.
