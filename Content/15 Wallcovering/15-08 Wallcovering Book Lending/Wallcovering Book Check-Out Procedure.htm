Wallcovering Book Check-Out Procedure

Step

Action

Standard Operating Procedure

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Wallcovering Book Check-Out.

2

Enter the Customer Account Number or use LOOKUP [F3] to search for your customer.

3

Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

4

Is there a barcode on the inside of the wallcovering book to be lent?

If Then
Yes Scan the barcode.
No Enter the generic book number (989996) and enter the name of the book in the description field. For steps on how to assign a barcode label to a wallcovering book, please see: Label Wallcovering Books Procedure.

Note: The Deposit Amount and Due Date should populate based on your WC Lending System Controls. The generic book number should only be used for wallcovering books without barcodes.

5

Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed once all books being lent have been entered into the system.

6

Are you collecting a deposit for the wallcovering book lending transaction?

If Then
Yes

Select the correct Form of Payment for the deposit.

Note:  It is to your advantage to get a credit card instead of having the customer pay by cash or check. If the books are not returned, you will not be able to charge any additional amount if the customer paid by cash or check.
No

Swipe the customer’s credit card to register the number and expiration date in the system.  Proceed to Step 8.

Note: You have the option to press TOTAL [F12] when prompted to swipe the customer’s credit card if you choose to not register the customer’s credit card in the system. If this is your choice, realize that you have no recourse if the books are not returned.

7

Did your customer select Credit Card for their Form of Payment?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 8.
No The transaction is complete.

8

If the customer pays by credit card, let them know that their credit card will not be charged unless the wallcovering books are not returned. 

  • Credit card information will only be available for ninety (90) days after the wallcovering book check-out transaction.

Note: In order to maintain Sherwin-Williams’ compliance with PCI Standards, credit card information may only be obtained and retained in accordance with certain restrictions and guidelines. Please review the Payment Card Industry Compliance Standards for more information.

Downtime Operating Procedure

1

Complete a Manual Point of Sale (POST) Form if your computer system is not working properly.

  • Remember to capture the wallcovering book information.
  • Enter Wallcovering Lending as the type of transaction.
  • Fill out complete customer information on the Manual POST Form.

2

Make an imprint of the customer’s credit card. 

Note:  Let the customer know that their credit card will not be charged unless the wallcovering books are not returned. 

3

Have the customer sign the Manual POST Form.

4

Enter and validate the Manual POST Form on the system when it is working properly.

Note:  The system views Wallcovering Book Check-Out Transactions as Miscellaneous Cash Receipts. If you do not charge your customers a deposit while processing a Wallcovering Book Check-Out Transaction, you are not required to batch the Miscellaneous Cash Receipt Transaction as no money has changed hands.
