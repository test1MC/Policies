Refund Charge for Wallcovering Books Procedure

If the customer returns the book after you have charged their credit card, please follow this procedure:

Step Action

1

Process a Return Transaction:

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Return -> Press ENTER through the Term/Tran Field and Date Field -> Press ENTER and select Non-Merchandise 7 to process the return -> Press COMMENT [F9] to capture the original terminal, transaction and date.

2

Send a copy of the transaction to Stores Group Accounting at the following address. They will make any necessary entries to correct your accounting information.

The Sherwin-Williams Company
Stores Group Accounting
P.O. Box 5819
Cleveland, Ohio 44101
