Charge for Not Returned Wallcovering Books Procedure

Please follow this procedure to charge a customer for a wallcovering book which has not been returned. Before you begin:

  • If there was no book deposit and no swipe of the customer’s credit card, no provisions are available to recover any additional charges. 
  • If the customer paid the book deposit by cash or check, no provisions are available to recover any additional charges.
  • Have the terminal number, transaction number and transaction date for the original wallcovering lending transaction handy.
  • If you have originally charged the customer for borrowing the wallcovering books, charge only the difference between the original book deposit and the $50.00 per book.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Special Menu [Page Down] -> Miscellaneous Cash Receipt.

2

Enter the customer’s contact information in the BillTo Field of the Transaction Header.

3

Press ACCEPT [F1] at the Transaction Header.

4

Select G/L Account 7303-199 (NC OTHER MDSNG AIDS).

5

Press ENTER while positioned in the Amount Field.

6

Enter Yes when prompted to respond if the transaction is for a non-returned wallcovering book. Press ENTER.

7

Enter the Terminal Number, Transaction Number, and Date for the original wallcovering lending transaction. Once identified, the screen will display the following information:

  • The right side of the screen will show the number of books on the transaction, the number not overdue, the number already charged, and the number still missing.
  • The lower part of the screen lists the books on the transaction one at a time along with the book status. 

8

Is the Status of the book Missing?

If Then
Yes Enter Yes to charge for the book -> Proceed to the next book by pressing any key.
No Proceed to the next book by pressing any key.

9

Once all of the books on the transaction have been displayed, the customer name, partial credit card number, and expiration date of the credit card will be displayed.  Select Yes to the prompt to confirm that the information is correct.

10

The screen will now display a summary of the books that were selected and the total charges. 

Note:  At this point, it is not possible to add additional expenses. The only options are to CANCEL [F5]  or ACCEPT [F1].

11

Press ACCEPT [F1].  Answer Yes to the prompt to confirm that you want to charge the customer's credit card. 

Note: Non-returned wallcovering book deposits will clear on line 53 of your P&L several times a year.
