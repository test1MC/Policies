Wallcovering Book Lending Overview

Wallcovering Book costs are expensed to each store location, therefore, it is critical to properly maintain the Wallcovering Lending System to account for all wallcovering books. Maintaining an adequate book library ensures you will have the required books needed to retain sales levels and to comply with future promotions.
