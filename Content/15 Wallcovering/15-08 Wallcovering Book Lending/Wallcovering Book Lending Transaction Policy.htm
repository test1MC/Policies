Wallcovering Book Lending Transaction Policy

The computer system looks at "Wallcovering Book Check Out" transactions as Miscellaneous Cash Receipts and "Wallcovering Book Check In" transactions as Miscellaneous Cash Disbursements.

  1. Wallcovering Book Lending Transactions need to be retained according to the procedures for all Miscellaneous Cash Receipts and Disbursements.
  2. Wallcovering Book Check In transactions will not display on the Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement Register.
