Damaged SWAT Merchandise Procedure

If SWAT merchandise shipped to your store arrives damaged or is found damaged upon customer inspection, you will need to create a claim with the vendor and re-order the goods for the customer:

Step Action
Process Vendor Claim

1

Follow the Damage Claims Procedure in 07-09 Vendor Ship / Receive.
Process Re-Order

1

Generate a replacement order directly with the vendor.

Note: You will need to use a store purchase order to place the replacement order.
