SWAT Order Overview

Residential and commercial wallcovering orders processed by SWAT are downloaded to stores for processing (order will populate as E-Commerce orders). Before processing the sale for the SWAT Order, the downloaded SWAT Purchase Order must be received. SWAT Purchase Orders can be identified by their format:

For more information on the order, launch COE, pull up the order, and review the comments. Transactions will be held on the store computer system until a store employee finalizes the transaction.
