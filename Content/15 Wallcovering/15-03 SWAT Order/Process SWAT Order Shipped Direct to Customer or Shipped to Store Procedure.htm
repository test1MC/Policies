Process SWAT Order Shipped Direct to Customer or Shipped to Store Procedure

Step Action
 
If Then
Order Shipped Direct to Customer When a direct ship order is downloaded to the POS as an E-Commerce/Direct Shipped SWAT Order, receive merchandise against the AT Purchase Order and then process the order.
Order Shipped to Store Receive the merchandise against the AT Purchase Order when it arrives at the store -> Notify the customer that their merchandise has arrived and is ready for pick-up -> Process the order when the customer arrives to pick-up their merchandise.

Note: The order will download to the POS as a pending E-Commerce Order. The customer does not need to present their credit card when picking up the merchandise because the transaction has already been approved. Make sure the customer signs the continuous feed invoice.

2

Order Shipped Direct to Customer ONLY: Mail the customer their copy of the invoice. Include information about additional services provided at your store and directions to your location.

Note: Changes cannot be made to any SWAT Transaction. Process a Correction Transaction to correct a SWAT Transaction. For example, if a customer were to decide that they only want a portion of the merchandise, process the sale for the SWAT Order. Then, process a Correction Transaction to credit the customer and account for any restocking or return charges, if applicable.
