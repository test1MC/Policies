Expired Credit Card SWAT Transaction Procedure

Transactions are valid up to 30 days after the order was placed with the vendor. After 30 days, the credit card authorization is no longer valid and settlement can no longer occur against the original authorization. If a customer picks up their merchandise after the 30-day period has lapsed, the order must be voided and re-entered as a new sale. The original credit card will have to be re-authorized or another form of payment utilized. 

Step Action
Void Downloaded Transaction

1

Print a copy of the SWAT Wallcovering Order you are about to void. You will need this later to re-enter the order information.

2

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Cancel Order -> Press Enter to cancel the appropriate Order Number

Note: SWAT order numbers begin with the prefix AT.
Process Sale

1

Re-enter the customer order using the information from the original SWAT Customer Order.

2

Complete a Sales Transaction.

Note: You will need to re-authorize the original credit card or select a new form of payment.
