SWAT Order Shipped to Incorrect Store Procedure

Transfer the merchandise to the correct store if the customer is unwilling to pick up the merchandise at the store that received the merchandise.

Step Action

1

Receive the SWAT Purchase Order.  

2

Follow the Void Downloaded SWAT Transaction Procedure noted in Expired Credit Card SWAT Transaction Procedure.

Note: You will not need to process a sale.

3

Process an IST to the store where the customer will pick-up the merchandise:

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Add Stock Transfer Order [ISR/IST] -> Enter appropriate information -> Use the appropriate Class Number and your Cost.  

2

Enter 00 for the Gross Profit.

3

Include any Shipping Charges on the IST.

Note:  The store receiving sales credit should pay for the shipping charges.

4

Send a copy of the IST, SWAT Purchase Order, and Customer Order with the merchandise to the correct store.

5

Retain a copy of the SWAT Purchase Order and Customer Order in the awaiting Store Purchases Report File.

Note:  The Store Transfer Process cannot be used to move the suspended POS Transaction from one store to another.
