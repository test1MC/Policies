SWAT Order Inquiry Procedure

SWAT Purchase Orders and Customer Orders can be reprinted or viewed on the screen at any time prior to completing the transaction.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Press the INQUIRY [F4].

2

Select SWAT Reprints (31).

3

A combined list of all Shipped Direct and Ship to Store transaction information is available.

Note: Adjustment transactions do not have the same support information, therefore, adjustment information is not available to view or print.
