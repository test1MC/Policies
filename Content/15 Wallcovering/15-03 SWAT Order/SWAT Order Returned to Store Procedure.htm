SWAT Order Returned to Store Procedure

SWAT encourages all customer returns to be made at the processing store regardless of whether the merchandise was shipped to the store or direct to the customer. If this is not possible, customers may return it to another store.

When the return is made at the processing store:

  • Follow normal policy and procedures for wallcovering returns.
  • Remember to charge a restocking fee, if appropriate.

When the return is made at a store other than the processing store:

Step Action

1

Call SWAT for information on the merchandise being returned.

2

Call the original processing store to verify the return and original transaction information.

3

Process a Return Transaction to satisfy the customer.

Note:  Remember to charge a restocking fee, if applicable.

4

Contact your District Manager and/or original processing store after the customer leaves to work out final dispensation of the return.
