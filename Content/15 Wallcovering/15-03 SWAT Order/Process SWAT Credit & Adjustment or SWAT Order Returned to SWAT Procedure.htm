Process SWAT Credit & Adjustment or SWAT Order Returned to SWAT Procedure

SWAT Credit & Adjustment: SWAT will initiate a credit or adjustment to the customer if the original order was placed with Wallcovering Orders & Answers and shipped directly to the customer. You will still need to process the credit or adjustment once it is downloaded to your store.

SWAT Order Returned to SWAT: Direct shipped merchandise can be returned directly to SWAT. SWAT will issue a call tag for all merchandise being returned and create the customer credit. The return transaction will download to the store for processing.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.
2
If Then
Credit/Adjustment Select Account Adjustments from Sale/Payment Menu -> Select Adjustment Reason.
Return Select Return from Sale/Payment Menu.

3

Press RECALL[Shift +F10] or enter original terminal number, transaction number, and date and the account number.

4

Select SWAT Transactions Shipped to Store.

5

Press ENTER on the SWAT Transaction to adjust.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] to finalize the transaction.

7
If Then
Credit/Adjustment Staple the appropriate SWAT Credit/Adjustment Customer to the utility copy of the computer-generated invoice. Place the copies in the file awaiting verification of the Stores Purchases Report.
Return

Staple the appropriate SWAT Return Customer Order to the utility copy of the computer-generated invoice. Place the copies in the file awaiting verification of the Stores Purchases Report.

8
If Then
Credit/Adjustment Mail the invoice to the customer.
Return

Mail the customer their copy of the return transaction.

Note: Include information about additional services provided at your store and directions to your location.

9
If Then
Credit/Adjustment If necessary, process a chargeback to the vendor.
Return

Process the chargeback to the vendor immediately.

Note: SWAT encourages all customer returns to be made at the store for faster credit. The customer will receive credit for the returned merchandise after 30 days to allow the vendor to pick up and verify the quantity and condition of the returned merchandise.
