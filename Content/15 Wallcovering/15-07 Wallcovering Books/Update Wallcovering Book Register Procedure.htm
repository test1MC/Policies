Update Wallcovering Book Register Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Miscellaneous -> WC Book Register -> Add/Update Book.

2

Scan the barcode positioned on the back cover of each wallcovering book.

Note: If you are not able to scan the barcode, manually enter the book number when prompted Add Book Number.

3

Enter the quantity and expiration date.

Note: Enter the Total Quantity for all books with the same book number. The expiration date field is optional, but it is highly recommended .

4

Press Cancel [F5] when complete to return to Miscellaneous Menu.
