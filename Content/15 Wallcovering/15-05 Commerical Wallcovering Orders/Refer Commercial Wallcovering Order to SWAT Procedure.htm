Refer Commercial Wallcovering Order to SWAT Procedure

Commercial Wallcovering orders may be placed by referring the order to SWAT. Your store will receive 100% credit for the sale when referring an order to SWAT. Below is a matrix of the referral process between the store and SWAT.

Stage

Who

Description

1

Store
  • Receives commercial wallcovering order request from customer.
  • Completes Commercial SWAT FC Form located in the Forms Repository.
  • Emails Commercial SWAT FC to cwcorder@sherwin.com.

2

SWAT
  • Processes Commercial SWAT FC Form.
  • If customer paying by credit card, obtains credit card approval.
  • Orders merchandise from vendor.
  • Creates customer order and AT Prefix Purchase Order to be downloaded to POS.

3

Store
  • Receives against AT Prefix Purchase Order.
  • Contacts customer to either notify them that merchandise may be picked up or to confirm that a direct shipment has arrived.
  • Processes downloaded customer order.

For more information, please see SWAT Order Overview.
