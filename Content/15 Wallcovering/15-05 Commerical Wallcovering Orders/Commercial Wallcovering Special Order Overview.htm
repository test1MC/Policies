Commercial Wallcovering Special Order Overview

Commercial Wallcovering orders may be placed at the store by placing a Wallcovering Special Order in POS for the customer and placing an order with the

vendor using the External Purchase Order process. For more information, please refer to:

  • 15-01 Wallcovering Special Order
  • 07-01C Decorative Products
  • 07-01B External PO - Vendor
  • 07-01D2 EDI PO – Vendor

Note: SWAT no longer accepts Commercial Wallcovering Transfer Orders.
