Residential Wallcovering Order Low Price Guarantee Procedure

Step Action
Process at Time of Original Sale

1

At the line item in either COE or the Sales Transaction, press DISC [F11] on the line item.

2

Select Other. Enter WC Guarantee as the reason.

3

Attach a copy of the verified competitor’s lower price information to the store copy of the purchase order.
Process within 30 Days of Original Sale

1

Process a Correction Transaction.

2

On the negative side of the correction transaction, enter the original purchase information exactly as shown on the customer’s original invoice.

3

On the positive side of the correction transaction, enter the wallcovering and the corrected purchase price.

4

The customer must sign the completed transaction before receiving their refund.

5

Attach a copy of the verified competitor’s lower price information to the store copy of the purchase order.

Note:  If selling below cost, enter the manufacturer’s list price with the cost G.P. %, listed next to the Wallcovering Book Class Number on the back inside liner page of the wallcovering book. Dollar discount to the net selling price to meet the competitor’s selling price.
