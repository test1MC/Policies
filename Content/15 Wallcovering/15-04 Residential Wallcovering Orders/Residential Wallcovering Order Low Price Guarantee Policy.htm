Residential Wallcovering Order Low Price Guarantee Policy

Sherwin-Williams  advertises the following Low Price Guarantee in reference to its retail wallcovering sales:

"Sherwin-Williams will match any competitor’s price on Wallcovering or Borders at the time of purchase, or for up to 30 days after your purchase! We will not be undersold!

*Products must be of same quality, manufacturer, pattern number and quantity. Clearance ads or closeout sales do not apply. Consumer must present Sherwin-Williams receipt from original purchase for 30 day price guarantee. Sherwin-Williams reserves the right to verify all competitors’ prices. This policy applies to retail sales only."

Sherwin-Williams will meet any competitor’s price on wallcovering under the following conditions:

  1. The Low Price Guarantee applies to retail sales only.
  2. The Wallcovering purchase must be the same quality, manufacturer, pattern number, and quantity as quoted by the competitor.
    1. The Low Price Guarantee does not include 54 vinyl wallcovering.
  3. Prices matched must be for products sold under normal selling conditions. Clearance ads or closeouts sold by a competitor do not apply.
  4. The Low Price Guarantee applies to product only and does not apply to shipping costs. Sales tax must be charged on all purchases, if applicable.
  5. Sherwin-Williams must be able to verify the price quote. This can be accomplished by:
    1. Comparing prices in a printed ad.
    2. Comparing prices on a written price quote provided by the customer (quote must be on letterhead of competitor).
    3. Calling the actual competitor by phone and requesting their prices on the same product with the same quantity.
  6. If you are unable to verify competitor pricing at the time of sale, you must sell the product at our current price. Verify competitor pricing within thirty (30) days of the customer’s final sale invoice date and  follow the procedure to refund the difference to the customer.
  7. If a competitor lowers their price on a wallcovering product(s) purchased at a Sherwin-Williams store for a higher price, you may refund or credit the customer the price difference.
  8. There is a chance that on occasion you may be forced to sell below your store cost to match a competitor. If the customer is a retail customer, and the product is an exact match with the exact same quantity you must match the price in spite of the margin. In this situation:
    1. Before ordering product at room lot costs, verify with SWAT that the patterns are not part of our stocking assortments available at better EDI costs.
    2. When ordering the product, you might also want to contact your local vendor rep to see if they can cut your cost. However, there is no guarantee that this will happen.
