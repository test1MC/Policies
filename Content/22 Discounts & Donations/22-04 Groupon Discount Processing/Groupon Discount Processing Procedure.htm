Groupon Discount Processing Procedure

This section applies only to Frazee and Kwal locations.

Expired Groupons may be processed using the Unregistered Coupon Price Remedy. Each remedy has a limited amount of uses per month for each store. Once the limit has been reached, the barcode will be disabled and will not be available until the first day of the following month. The Groupon must be validated before processing the transaction. The remedy must be applied while processing the Sale Transaction. Once the sale is complete, the Groupon must be processed as redeemed on the Groupon Website.

Is your groupon valid?

If Then
Yes Tell the customer the available balance on their groupon and proceed to Process Sale.
No Let the customer know that the groupon has already been redeemed and cannot be used. 

Note:  We cannot assist with any customer questions about the value, balance, or redemption status of their groupon. Customers must contact Groupon directly.
Process Sale

1

Begin by processing a Sale Transaction through either entry or bringing an existing Customer Order into the Sale Transaction.

2

Navigate to the Transaction Detail.

3

Is the Extended Price of any line item on the order equal to or greater than the purchase amount of the expired Groupon?

If Then
Yes Navigate to a line item where the Extended Price is equal to or greater than the purchase amount of the expired Groupon.
No You will need to apply the Groupon across several line items in order to total the purchase amount. 

4

Press DISCOUNT [F11].

5

Select Price Remedy from the Discount Type Menu.

6

Scan or manually enter the Unregistered Coupon Price Remedy Barcode.

Note:  Print a scan sheet of Price Remedy Barcodes from  07-12 Pricing.

7

Is the Extended Price of the line item equal to or greater than the purchase amount of the expired Groupon?

If Then
Yes Enter the purchase amount of the expired Groupon in the Dollar Field.  Press ACCEPT [F1].
No Enter the lesser amount of the item’s Extended Price or the remaining purchase amount of the Groupon.

8

Press COMMENT [F9].

9

Navigate to the second line of comments and enter the Groupon Redemption Code.  Press ACCEPT [F1].

 

Has the purchase amount of the Groupon been relieved?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 10.
No Repeat Step 4 through Step 9 for additional line items until the Groupon amount has been relieved. 

10

Complete processing of the Sale Transaction.

11

Attach the customer’s Groupon to the store copy of the sale receipt and retain in batching.
