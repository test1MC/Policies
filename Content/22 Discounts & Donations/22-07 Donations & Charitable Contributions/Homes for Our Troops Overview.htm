Homes for Our Troops Overview

Homes for Our Troops is a nonprofit organization that builds mortgage-free, specially designed homes for severely injured veterans who served in Iraq and Afghanistan. Sherwin-Williams has partnered with this great cause to support our veterans and give back to the community. Our involvement in this organization allows us to position premium products such as Emerald and Pro-Classic, as well as take an active role in community events surrounding these homes.
