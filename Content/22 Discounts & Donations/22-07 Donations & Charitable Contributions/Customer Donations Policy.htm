Customer Donations Policy

  1. Customer donation programs require District approval.
  2. Employees may not request and process donations through POS from customers in support of the employees’ personal charitable activities.
  3. Multiple donations may be processed on the same transaction, but require separate line items.
  4. Access to the customer donation procedure is temporary based on the timing of the charitable event.
  5. A Materials Acceptance Agreement - Donate must be completed prior to donating and releasing 25 gallons or more of less-than-perfect product to the Acceptor (customer), for example: clearance or mistinted merchandise.
  6. A completed and signed copy of the Materials Acceptance Agreement - Donate must be retained at the store in a file titled [Materials Acceptance Agreement] for any less-than-perfect donated merchandise.
  7. An email response from your District Manager must be on file approving the donation.
  8. The signed Materials Acceptance Agreement - Donate and District Manager email response must be retained at the store for 10 years.
  9. You are required to forward a copy of the signed agreement to Division Loss Prevention and another copy to Corporate Legal.
  10. A representative of the Company must be present at the time of removal.
