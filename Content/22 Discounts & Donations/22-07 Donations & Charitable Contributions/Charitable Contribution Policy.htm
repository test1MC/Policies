Charitable Contribution Policy

  1. The Charitable Contribution Acknowledgement form must be completed for any cash and/or property contributions made by Sherwin-Williams in order to comply with the requirements of the Internal Revenue Service.
  2. Contributions are extended only to causes that fulfill a recognized need in communities where the Company operates and benefits both the community and the Company.
  3. Care must be taken to see that no organization believes the Company has assumed an obligation for continued support. The receipt of an initial gift does not mean continued support.
  4. Donations of cash or merchandise must be included in the store's budget for the year.
  5. Cash or merchandise donations must not exceed $25.00 (retail value), at a given time, or $100.00 (retail value) total for the year. For a request that is more than the $25.00 (retail value), it must be submitted in writing to the District Manager, who will review it and, if necessary, forward it to the Division for approval.
  6. No consideration will be given to any verbal solicitation, unless confirmed by a written request that identifies the organization, its purpose, and other pertinent information so a fair evaluation can be made.
  7. Telephone calls from professional solicitors selling blocks of advance sale tickets and program ads for circuses and variety shows are not approved.
  8. Corporate Headquarters makes the United Way contribution if there are facilities such as a region office or a DSC in the area. If there are no such facilities in the area, the store may donate $10.00 per full-time employee to the United Way campaign.
  9. Contributions to non-profit organizations that receive United Way funding are not approved, unless the organization has permission from United Way to conduct a fund-raising drive.
  10. Dues to Chambers of Commerce and service or professional organizations must be classified as a business expense.
  11. Retain the signed copy of the Charitable Contributions Agreement in a folder titled Charitable Contributions.
  12. If the store cost is $250 and over, please fax the original copy of the Charitable Contributions Agreement to 216-566-2392, or email to tara.a.laflamme@sherwin.com or cadedo@sherwin.com.  Forms can also be forwarded to: The Sherwin-Williams Company  ATTN: Tax Department: Tara LaFlamme or Craig Dedo PO Box 6027 Cleveland, Ohio 44101-9944
  13. Your store location is not permitted to be a collection site for material donations to any charitable organization.

Note: Find the Charitable Contribution Acknowledgement form in the Forms Repository.
