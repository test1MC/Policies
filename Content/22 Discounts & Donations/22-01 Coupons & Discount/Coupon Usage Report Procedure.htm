Coupon Usage Report Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the Point of Sale desktop, select Store -> Press INQUIRY [F4].

2

Select the Coupon Usage Report under the Store Accountability Menu.

3

Reports return according to one or more of the following parameters, depending on which you query: Transaction Date, Coupon Type, Coupon UPC, Reason for Override, [when applicable] Employee Number, Sales Number, Customer Account Number, Total Discount, and/or Total Sale.

4

Reports may be sorted by Transaction Date or Coupon Type, and they will display by Coupon Category.
