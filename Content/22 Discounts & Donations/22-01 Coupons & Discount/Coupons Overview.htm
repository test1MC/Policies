Coupons Overview

  • A coupon may be scanned at any time while processing a Sales Transaction or Customer Order.
  • Once a coupon is scanned, it will be applied to all products appropriately (as long as the product qualifies for the discount) and stays with the order as long as no changes are made.
  • For backordered items, there is no need to rescan the coupon because the discount will automatically populate when the item is pulled into the POS.
  • If the store creates a delivery order and needs to change the ship quanytity when pulling it into the POS, select Shipped Lines Not Billed to ensure the coupon discount applies to the order.
  • The coupon system does not limit coupons by location. For example, a coupon issued in the New York market may be used in a store in the Texas market.
  • After completing the transaction, staple the redeemed coupon to the store copy and file according to batching instructions.
  • If the customer requests you to scan a coupon on their mobile device, the barcode number must be typed into the POS rather than scanned.
