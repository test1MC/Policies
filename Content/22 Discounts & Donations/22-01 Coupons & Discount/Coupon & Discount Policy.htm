Coupon & Discount Policy

  1. Division Controller approval is required for all Division, District or store printed coupon-related programs prior to implementation. Coupon-related offers include:
    1. Any printed offer to discount from regular (DIY) or sale price.
    2. Any advertised coupon promotion implemented by a Store, District or Division employee.
  2. All coupons are to be processed as a sale and then dollar or percent discount the amount of the "free" merchandise. This process will cause the merchandise to be charged against the store as a cost of doing business. No additional transaction is required.
  3. Coupons have no cash redemption value and are not valid on previous purchases.
  4. New Movers coupons can be redeemed at any Sherwin-Williams' store in the United States.
  5. All new store promotions should be coordinated and approved with help from your division marketing team (Grand Opening Events, First Year Selling Events, New Store Promotional Offers, etc…..)
