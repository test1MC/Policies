Discount Key & Overrides Overview

The Discount Key returns the following choices:

Part

Function

10% Discount

No longer available.

Price Remedy

Price Remedies and Coupons will return a Discount Menu. Input the percent or dollar amount you want to discount, or simply input the price you want to sell the product at.

Coupon

Scan or manually enter the coupon barcode number.

Other

This option is only available for wallcovering products or items that are not on a price schedule. You are required to enter a reason.

Note: Use of this discount is monitored by Division Management and Internal Audit. The reason will not display on the customer receipt.

Normally, the computer system will automatically retrieve sales prices. Non-price listed items and items without a recognizable sales number are the exception. An item is classified as Discounted Merchandise if it is a stock closeout item. The use of the Discount Key is considered an override in the pricing system. Store Managers must monitor Overrides and Discounts, as well as train employees to correctly follow procedures to avoid an overridden sale.

Part

Function

Retail Overrides

Employees at your location will not be able to key in an override price on retail (DIY) transactions. However, you are able to increase the selling price on a DIY transaction without entering a reason. The following warning message will display if an override is attempted on DIY transactions:

"OVERRIDES ARE PROHIBITED FOR THIS TRANSACTION

PLEASE FOLLOW YOUR DIVISION POLICY

CONTACT YOUR DISTRICT MANAGER FOR ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

PRESS ANY KEY TO CONTINUE"

Wholesale Overrides

Wholesale overrides are permitted. However, the override price may not be lower than the floor price, which is the absolute lowest price that products may be sold at for a specific customer. The system will return a warning if a discount is attempted below the floor price. If it is necessary to discount a product below the floor price, contact your District Manager for approval. When a wholesale price override is entered and is accepted by the system as a valid override, a warning message will return:

"OVERRIDES WILL BE MONITORED BY YOUR DIVISION AND INTERNAL AUDIT

YOU MAY RECEIVE A PHONE CALL TO BETTER EXPLAIN THIS OVERRIDE

IF YOU CONTINUE WITH THIS OVERRIDE YOU WILL BE REQUIRED TO ENTER A REASON

(THE REASON WILL NOT PRINT ON THE CUSTOMER'S INVOICE)"

An explanation is required for the override before the sale can be processed. Price overrides for visiting accounts must be approved by the home store or the servicing Sales Representative. Contact the Manager (or, if unavailable, the Assistant Manager) of the home store to obtain verbal approval. In the event the price was quoted by a Sales Representative, contact the Rep to verify the overridden price.

Proper Use of the Discount Key

Under no circumstances should a walk-in retail (Type 1) customer be processed as a walk-in PC (Type 2) or walk-in CC (Type 3) customer in order to avoid a retail override.
