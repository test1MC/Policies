Military Discount Overview

Sherwin-Williams proudly supports those who have served and are currently serving in the military of the United States and Canada by offering a discount on purchases of merchandise through Sherwin-Williams retail paint stores.

Military discounts offer the following savings to retail customers:

Product Line Discount
POG Paints & Stains
  • 15% Off List
  • 15% Off Color-to-Go

Associated ProductsBrushes & Rollers

  • 15% Off List
Ladders
  • 15% Off List

Qualifying customers who elect to become PaintPerks members and set up a preferred customer account will also receive the military discount as one of their benefits.
