Military Discount Policy

The following policies are effective for military discounts. Any violation of this policy may result in discipline up to and including termination.

  1. The following individuals are eligible to receive the Military Discount:
    1. Members of the United States Armed Forces (Army, Marine Corps, Navy, Air Force, and Coast Guard).
    2. Members of the Canadian Forces (Royal Canadian Navy, Canadian Army, and Royal Canadian Airforce).
    3. All status designations including but not limited to:  active, retired, reserves, National Guard, ROTC, temporary, disabled.
    4. Spouses of members of the United States Armed Forces and the Canadian Forces.
  2. This discount is only available in the United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico.
  3. Individuals must present their Military ID or Veteran Identification Card (VIC) in order to receive the discount.  
    1. If the customer elects to create a preferred customer account, it is only necessary to show and validate the ID card upon creation of the account.
    2. If the customer elects not to create a preferred customer account, the ID must be presented for each purchase.
  4. The military discount is intended only for the purchase of goods for personal use. Commercial use of products purchased with the military discount is prohibited. 
    1. Individuals who wish to purchase goods to be used commercially should be encouraged to open a professional account.
  5. Merchandise purchased cannot be for resale. This will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
  6. The military discount does not apply to gift cards, wallcovering, window treatments, multi-purpose primers (US only), floorcovering, spray equipment, or installations.
  7. Employees are not permitted to process their own point of sale transactions. This includes all point of sale transaction types.
  8. Employees are not permitted to process transactions for family members receiving a military discount.
  9. Other discounts (offer, percentage off coupon, promotion, discount or any special price program) cannot be combined when using a military discount.  In the event that an item has already been discounted, the greater savings between the item discount and the military discount will prevail.
  10. Shipping charges, freight charges, and sales tax must be charged on all military discount purchases when applicable.
  11. Accommodation sales by company suppliers are prohibited.
