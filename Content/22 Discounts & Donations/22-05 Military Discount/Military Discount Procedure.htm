Military Discount Procedure

Military discounts can only be applied to cash or retail charge account with either a CAC of 1150 (United States Armed Forces) or 1170 (Canadian Forces). A pricing accommodation is required for customers who qualify for the military discount but do not want to set up a preferred customer account.

Follow the below procedure to process a military discount while in a Customer Order or a Sale Transaction:

Step Action

1

Does the customer have a Sherwin-Williams cash or retail charge account?

If Then
Yes

Does the existing account have a military CAC Code?

If Then
Yes The military discount will be applied to the order. Continue to process without any special handling.  Procedure is complete. 
No Proceed to Step 2.

Note:  Military accounts can be identified by CAC of 1150 (United States Armed Forces) or 1170 (Canadian Forces).
No Proceed to Step 2.

2

The customer’s eligibility for a military discount must be verified. Did the customer present their military ID/Veteran Identification Card (VIC)?

If Then
Yes

Would the customer like to open a Sherwin-Williams cash or retail charge account or convert an existing account for the military discount?

If Then

Opening a new cash account


Select the appropriate military CAC Code: 1150 (United States Armed Forces) / 1170 (Canadian Forces) -> Proceed to Step 4

Opening a new retail charge account

Indicate that the customer is an active/retired member or spouse thereof of the United States Armed Forces or Canadian Forces -> Proceed to Step 4
Converting existing account Proceed to Step 4
No Proceed to Step 3

For more information on creating new accounts, please see Create Cash Account Procedure and Create DIY Credit Account Procedure
No The sale must be run without the military discount. Procedure is complete. 

3

Once all products have been scanned, scan the Military Discount Barcode. The POS will apply the discount.  Procedure is complete. 

CAC UPC

1150 (US)

5-35777-30651-7

1170 (Canada)

5-35777-30652-4

Note:  By scanning the military barcode for an existing account and processing a sale transaction, the account will be assigned the military CAC Code overnight. If the military barcode is scanned for an existing account during a sales promotion, the customer’s account will not be assigned a military CAC Code. To convert the account, you must contact your DCO to request a change to the appropriate military CAC Code for the account:  1150 (United States Armed Forces) / 1170 (Canadian Forces).
4

Once all products have been scanned, scan the Pricing Accommodation Barcode for each item which qualifies for the discount.

Step Action
1 UP ARROW to each line that needs to be adjusted.
2 Press Disc [F11] -> Price Remedy.
3 Scan or enter the Pricing Accommodation remedy barcode.
4 Select to enter the remedy price as a percentage discount at 15%.
