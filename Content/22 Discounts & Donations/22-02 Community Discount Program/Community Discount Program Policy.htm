Community Discount Program Policy

This section is for The Americas Group locations only. This program does not apply to Automotive Branches and Product Finishes facilities.

  1. The Community Discount Program is intended to be used with community programs and local businesses. The coupon is not intended to be distributed to individuals outside of the objective of the program.
  2. Only Store Managers should make requests for Community Discount Postcards.
  3. All orders will require the District Manager’s approval. 
  4. Do not place postcards on the counter by the POS.
  5. Do not place postcards on car windshields, homeowner’s doors, mailboxes or anywhere that could have a negative impact on the perception of our brand or quality products.
