Community Discount Postcards Procedure

This section is for The Americas Group locations only. This program does not apply to Automotive Branches and Product Finishes facilities.

Allow for, at least, a 3-week lead time to receive the postcards; this allows time for approvals, printing, and shipping.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access SOURCE homepage -> Information Library -> Forms Repository.

2

Search for and launch the Community Discount Program Store Request Form. 

Note: Through 1/1/2018, you may also find this form on the Source homepage in the upper left corner under Community Discount Program.

3

Complete all fields on the form and save to your file directory.

Note:  The maximum order quantity is 500 postcards. The custom text area allows you to add a personalized message to the postcard;  there is a 150-character limit for your message. While not mandatory, it is highly recommended to add that personalized touch to your offer.

4

Email the form to your District Manager and District Coordinator.

Note:  If you are located in Quebec, this postcard can be made available in French.

Once the form has been submitted:

  • The District Manager will review and approve the request. 
  • If approved the District Coordinator will place the order. 
  • After approval, your store will receive a notification that the order has been approved and shipment/tracking information from Order Express.
