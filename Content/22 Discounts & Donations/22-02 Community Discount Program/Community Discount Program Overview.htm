Community Discount Program Overview

This section is for The Americas Group locations only. This program does not apply to Automotive Branches and Product Finishes facilities.

To help you grow DIY sales, the Community Discount Program allows you to order customized postcards to distribute to local businesses so that they may pass on savings to their employees and customers.  This program provides great incentives to bring more DIY customers into your store and encourage PaintPerks registration:

  • Local messaging
  • Custom text area for personalized message
  • Store Manager name and address printed on the card
  • Two one-time use coupons (all regular inclusions and exclusions apply):
    • $10 off $50 or more purchase
    • Buy One, Get One Color to Go sample quart
  • PaintPerks highlights
  • Up to a 90 day coupon expiration date

The postcard also invites community members to register for the Preferred Customer Program with PaintPerks™ benefits.  Customers are encouraged to sign up in-store or to register at sherwin-williams.com. Please see Preferred Customer Program Enrollment Overview for more information.
