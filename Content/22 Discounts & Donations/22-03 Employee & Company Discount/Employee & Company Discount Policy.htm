Employee & Company Discount Policy

The following policies are effective for company discounts. Any violation of this policy may result in discipline up to and including termination.

  1. The following individuals are eligible to receive the Employee Discount:
    1. Employees on active assignment.
    2. Employees on a leave of absence.
  2. The following family members of employees and retirees are eligible to receive the Extended Family Discount: spouse, surviving spouse, domestic partner, surviving domestic partner, parents, stepparents, children, stepchildren, children of a domestic partner, siblings, and grandparents, in-laws [mother, father, sister, brother] mother-in-law, mother of a domestic partner, father-in-law, father of a domestic partner, sister-in-law, sister of a domestic partner, brother-in-law, brother of a domestic partner, daughter-in-law, daughter-in-law of a domestic partner, son-in-law and son-in-law of a domestic partner.
    1. The Extended Family Member discount program is available to the extended family for as long as you and your spouse/partner are in active retiree status.
    2. The Extended Family Member discount program is available to the extended family for the lives of the retiree and their spouse/partner. The program will end upon the death of both the retiree and spouse/partner.
  3. The following individuals are eligible to receive the Retiree Discount:
    1. Retirees who retired from active service.
    2. Spouses and surviving spouses/domestic partners of retirees who retired from active service.
  4. The following individuals are eligible to receive a Special Agreement Discount:
    1. Ex-employees who received the discount as part of a separation agreement.
  5. Individuals must present their un-expired discount card in order to receive the discount.
  6. Employees or retirees who wish to open a cash or retail charge account must be set up with a Customer Type 9 (employee) account.
  7. Extended family members must open a Customer Type 9 (employee) cash or retail charge account in order to receive the company discount.
    1. The Extended Family Member account must be set up using the name of the family member, not the employee name.
  8. Eligible individuals may use a company discount to purchase products which will be used by someone other than themselves (e.g., as a gift or donation); provided, however, that the eligible individual does not stand to gain in any way from the transaction or that the eligible individual will not be reimbursed or otherwise compensated for the purchase.
  9. Commercial use of products purchased with a company discount is prohibited.
  10. Merchandise purchased cannot be for resale. This will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
  11. Employees are not permitted to process their own point of sale transactions. This includes all point of sale transaction types.
  12. Employees are not permitted to process transactions for family members receiving a company discount.
  13. Store employees’ personal cash accounts and charge accounts may not be used to charge merchandise for others, including extended family members eligible for the company discount.
  14. Discounts cannot be combined when using a company discount. In the event that an item has already been discounted, the greater savings between the item discount and the company discount will prevail.
  15. Shipping charges, freight charges, and sales tax must be charged on all company discount purchases when applicable.
  16. “Accommodation” sales by company suppliers are prohibited.
  17. The following policies apply to floorcovering and wallcovering purchases:
    1. Special discounts apply to Non-GOM products only. Calculate the DIY-selling price for each item.
    2. The $1000 list price total can include multiple items on the invoice. For example: Customer purchases wallcovering ($200), window treatment ($400), and floorcovering ($800). This purchase would qualify for the [over $1000 list price] and an overall gross profit of 15% (Discount does not apply to shipping and tax).
    3. Special discounts apply to items ordered at the same time. These products do not have to be picked up or installed at the same time. Make sure all invoices and documents are cross-referenced.
    4. Company discounts do not apply to installation.
  18. When convenient, store personnel will wait on non-employee customers first. Questions about price will be discussed privately with store personnel – away from non-employee customers.
  19. When employees separate from Sherwin-Williams, they are no longer eligible for the Employee Discount and their extended family members are no longer eligible for the Extended Family Discount.
