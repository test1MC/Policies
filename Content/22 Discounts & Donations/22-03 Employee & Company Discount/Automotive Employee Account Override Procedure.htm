Automotive Employee Account Override Procedure

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Step Action

1

Create an Employee Account.

2

When the system overrides the Customer Type from 9 (Employee) to Customer Type 2 (Professional Contractor), perform a manual override to price the transaction accordingly.

Note: See Company Discount to establish discounts by Product Line.

3

After completing the sale in POS, submit an eDiscount request to gain approval from your ASM. eDiscount requests must include the following comment: "APPROVAL IS BEING SUBMITTED FOR AN EMPLOYEE SALE."

Note: Approval should be given within 2 business days.

4

Once approval is obtained, set up a PRC for the Employee account to offer the proper discount(s).

Note: Retain the approved eDiscount for audit purposes.
