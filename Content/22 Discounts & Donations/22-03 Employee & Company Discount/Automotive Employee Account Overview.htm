Automotive Employee Account Overview

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Due to a POS constraint, the system defaults newly created employee accounts from a Customer Type 9 to Customer Type 2 (CAC code 8888 – UNKNOWN). In these cases, scanning the Employee Discount card provides the list price instead of the employee price. To bill the sale with the proper discount, branches must override the pricing to provide the employee with the approved percent discount (depending on the type of product).
