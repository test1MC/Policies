Convert Employee Accounts to Preferred Customer Accounts Procedure

When employees separate (other than retirement) from the company and they and their family members are no longer eligible for a company discount, accounts at paint stores should be converted from Employee Accounts to Preferred Customer Accounts.

Step Action

1

Request a CAC Code change of 9999 to 1140 from your Credit Office for the accounts to be converted.
