Employee & Company Discount Procedure

Company discounts can only be applied on either a Type 9 Employee Transaction or a Customer Type 9 (employee) cash or retail charge account with either a CAC of 9999 (Paint Stores) or 5999 (Automotive Branches). The processing employee must scan the discount card barcode to generate the appropriate company discount pricing. Until the discount card barcode is scanned, the transaction will price at List Price or Sale Price. Follow the below procedure to process a company discount while in a Customer Order or a Sale Transaction:

Step Action

1

Does the customer have their own Sherwin-Williams cash or retail charge account?

If Then
Yes Enter the Account Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to look up the employee. 
No They must set up a Customer Type 9 (employee) account in their name to receive the company discount.

Note: If the customer does not have their discount card, proof of current employment at Sherwin-Williams, or is not known to you as an individual eligible for the discount, the company discount cannot be applied to the sale.

2

At Order Detail, check that all prices were returned:

If Then

List price for Spray Equipment, Floorcovering or Window Treatments is not returned

Manually enter the employee discount price of the product.

List price for Wallcovering is not returned

Enter the manufacturer’s list price and the system will automatically calculate the discounted price. 

Note: Wallcovering should automatically be calculated at 25% off list price by the system.

3

Once all products have been scanned, scan the barcode on the discount card. The POS will apply the discount.

If Then

They do not have their card

Use the barcodes on the Price Remedy Scan Sheet.

The customer provides their barcode via Smartphone
  • Press DISCOUNT [F11] at the line item.
  • Select Coupon.
  • Enter the 12-digit discount number in the line item.

Extended Family Member Discount Card used

Employee Name and Relationship to Employee will be required.
