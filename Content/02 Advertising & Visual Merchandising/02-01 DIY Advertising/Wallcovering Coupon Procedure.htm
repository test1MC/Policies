Wallcovering Coupon Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Step

Action

Enter Net Selling Price in Purchase Order

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Change/Release Purchase Order -> At the Item Detail Screen, press the Up Arrow to the line item of the product for which you wish to change the price. 

2

Tab to the Price Field. Enter the Net Selling Price.

Change Selling Price in Window Treatment & Wallcovering Price List

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Maintenance -> Window Treatment/Wallcovering Price Maintenance from the Merchandise Management Menu.

2

Enter the Book Number and select the correct pattern number.

3

Adjust the selling price to reflect the Net Selling Price for the wallcovering. Press F1 [Accept].

When the customer orders the wallcovering and presents the extra dollar or percent off coupon, please follow these procedures to make sure that the Net Selling Price will be used at point of sale. The Wallcovering Order Memo should still reflect the retail and discounted price for the wallcovering. Either manually enter the Net Selling Price on the purchase order or change the selling price in the Window Treatment and Wallcovering Price List to give the customer the discount off their book order wallcovering purchase.
