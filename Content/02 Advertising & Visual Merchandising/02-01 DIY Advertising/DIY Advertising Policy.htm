DIY Advertising Policy

  1. The Marketing Communications Department in Cleveland must place all local supplemental advertising.
  2. Store Management cannot place local advertising.
  3. Payment for local advertising must be arranged by Headquarters or submitted on via Expense Invoice Submission Procedure coded to 7301-101.
