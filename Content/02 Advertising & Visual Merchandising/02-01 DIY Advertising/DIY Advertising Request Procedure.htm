DIY Advertising Request Procedure

Step

Action

1

Contact the District Manager to request local advertising.

2

The District Manager will review the request and determine its viability.

3

If the District Manager approves the request, the request is given to the Division Marketing Staff for approval

4

After the Division Marketing Staff approves the request, the request is given to the Marketing Communications Department in Cleveland to evaluate.

5

The Marketing Communications Department will communicate their recommendation to the Division Marketing Staff, who will then discuss the recommendation with the District Manager.

6

If the Division Marketing Staff and District Manager approve the request, both parties must determine the appropriate store(s) cost center to which the expense of the advertising should be coded.

7

The Marketing Communications Department will then develop and place the advertising, per the specifications of the division.

8

If the requests do not include an advertising evaluation and placement, creative can be requested through your District Manager, who will work with Division and Marketing Communications to deliver approved art files.
