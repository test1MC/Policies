Preferred Customer Program Policy

The Preferred Customer Program is only available to TAG locations in the US and Canada.

  1. Preferred Customer Program - PaintPerks™ Benefits membership is open to:
    1. Legal residents of the United States (including Puerto Rico and the US Virgin Islands), the Caribbean, and Canada.
    2. Persons 18 years of age or older.
  2. No purchase is required.
  3. There is no membership fee.
  4. Membership is not available to professional contractors, businesses, and customers with a Sherwin-Williams wholesale account.
