Preferred Customer Program Enrollment Overview

The Preferred Customer Program is only available to TAG locations in the US and Canada.

All retail charge accounts created after March 2007 are automatically enrolled in the Preferred Customer Program with PaintPerks Benefits.

Customers can enroll in-store or online:

If Then

In-Store

Store employee follows the Create Cash Account Procedure. 

Online

Customers can access swpaintperks.com and select Sign Up Now. They will be enrolled once they enter all required information. 

Note: An email address is required for online enrollment.
