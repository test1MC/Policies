Preferred Customer Program Overview

The Preferred Customer Program is only available to TAG locations in the US and Canada.

The Preferred Customer Program with PaintPerks™ Benefits provides retail consumers with in-store services and online benefits. Enrolled customers may receive promotional offers from Sherwin-Williams by postal mail, telephone, and email. These discounts and advantages make it easier for you to capture customer email information, which will ultimately increase sales.

PaintPerks Benefits include:

  • A Welcome Coupon*
  • Exclusive Discounts for Every Store Visit
  • Exclusive Sales Events
  • Sales Alerts
  • Tips and Ideas*
  • Online Project Management*
  • Tracking of Color and Paint Purchases for Ten (10) Years
  • Anniversary Gift*

*An email address is required to deliver these benefits.
