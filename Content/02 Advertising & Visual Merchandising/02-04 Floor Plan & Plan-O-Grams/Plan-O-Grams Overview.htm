Plan-O-Grams Overview

Applies to The Americas Group Locations only

Plan-O-Grams are developed by The Americas Group Category Development Dept in Cleveland using state-of-the-art merchandising software.  This software allows for the placement of new and established products in a practical and scientific method assuring maximization of the space and related product sales of every item.  A planogram defines location and quantity of products placed on shelves, which consists of categorizing merchandise, highlighting new and featured products, highlighting impulse items, and creating cross merchandising opportunities. A well Plan-o-Grammed store stays cleaner and better organized, helping customers  find what they need and makes inventorycontrol and product reorder much easier.Plan-O-Grams also take into consideration evolving trends in the paint and coatings industry and allows for the introduction of new products that serve the industry.

New products are often sell tested in our stores prior to being added to our Plan-O-Grams.  This gives Sherwin-Williams an opportunity to better understand the customers' need for these products as well as assisting in pricing strategies.

Plan-O-Gram resets are necessary to align our categories with changes in the marketplace and are required actions for all stores. Plan-O-Grams are updated monthly.  The updates will consist of either a Complete Update requiring major changes to product and/or fixtures, or simply a revision that will consist of minor changes to product locations.
