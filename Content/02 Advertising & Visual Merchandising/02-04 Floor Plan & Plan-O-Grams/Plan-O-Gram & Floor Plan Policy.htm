Plan-O-Gram & Floor Plan Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  1. During the first quarter each year, Plan-o-Grams are reviewed and revised.
  2. New Plan-o-Grams should be implemented as soon as received on SWeep.
  3. The District Manager and Category Development Department must approve any changes or variances from the Plan-o-Gram.
  4. The products are to be set following the Plan-O-Gram provided. A revision should be completed within 7 days of receiving the new Plan-O-Gram.  A Complete Update must be set up within 30 days of receipt.
  5. Stores are responsible to view the system to determine if an update is required on a monthly basis.
  6. New and Replacement products: New and immediate launch replacement products that are part of a National POG section will automatically be included in the suggested STAR order. Once these items are in stock and the new POG version is available, stores are expected to reset the sales floor display in accordance with the time frame outlined in section B above, unless otherwise directed in the new product launch materials from Marketing.

The Floor Plan & Plan-O-Gram viewer provides an electronic means for stores to view and print their individualized Floor Plans and Plan-O-Gram. It provides a way to identify which Plan-O-Grams have changed and to notify category development that the Plan-O-Grams have been implemented in the store.  The link below provides instructions on to utilize the online Plan-O-Gram process.
