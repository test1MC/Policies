Automotive Selling Aids Procedure

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Sale -> Enter Customer Information -> Press Enter on a new line to launch the Non-Merchandise Codes Menu.

2

Select Selling Aids.

3

Enter QTY and selling price.

4

Press COMMENTS [F9] and include detail to the line item (ex. AS1777 Color Manual).
