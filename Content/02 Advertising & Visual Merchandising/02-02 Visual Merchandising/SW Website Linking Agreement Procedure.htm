SW Website Linking Agreement Procedure

A completed Linking Agreement gives the customer permission to provide a Sherwin-Williams website link on their website. This process can be completed by the customer online.

Step Action

1

Refer the customer to www.sherwin-williams.com/brand, where he/she will be directed to the Corporate Identity Guidelines web page. They must read this page and agree to our terms and conditions.

2

They will be directed to the Logos and Linking Agreement page -> Select Linking Agreement at the top of the page and complete it -> Once completed and submitted, the customer can download Sherwin logos to their website.
