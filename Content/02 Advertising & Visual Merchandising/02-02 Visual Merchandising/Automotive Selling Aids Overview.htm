Automotive Selling Aids Overview

This section only applies to Automotive Branches.

Ad Service Items are charged directly to Selling Aids when ordered from Richmond.  In some cases, you may have the opportunity to bill a customer for all or part of the cost of these items. If you bill a customer for an Ad Service Item, please use Non-Merchandise Code #26 Selling Aids and the expense credit will reduce your controllable expense. You will find this expense credit on your PNL Analysis under Selling Aids (7303-111) with a description of “SMIS NON-MDSE TRANS”.

Note: To access the Ad Service Form, access the Auto Website -> Documents -> Forms -> Ad Service/Color Tools.
