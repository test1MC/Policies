Merchandising Aids Overview

Merchandising Aids include all in-store displays, fixtures, and signs that support the presentation and sale of our products and services. Most in-store merchandising aids are stocked in Order Express.  Items may also be available from Order Express, the DSC, or through the Division Real Estate/Installation Department.  Check the First Impressions In-Store Merchandising Catalog on Source for the items. 

Note: Refer to the Quick Reference Guide to answer any questions.
