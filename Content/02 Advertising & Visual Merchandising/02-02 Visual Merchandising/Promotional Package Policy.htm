Promotional Package Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Promotional Sign kits support and communicate the sale offerings taking place in store. These kits are intended to draw attention to sale(s) items and to increase purchases.

  1. Point of Purchase (POP) sign kits are sent to stores approximately one week prior to the start of a new sale.  Sales promoted in-store include Holiday Sales, Super Sales, Anniversary Sales, Probuy/ProDeal Sales and Bucket Sales.
  2. All elements of the POP package must be displayed during the sale promotion period.
