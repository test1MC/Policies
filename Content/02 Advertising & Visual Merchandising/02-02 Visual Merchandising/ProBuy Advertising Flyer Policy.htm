ProBuy Advertising Flyer Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

The Headquarters Advertising Department, in conjunction with the Division marketing staff, produce ProBuy wholesale advertising flyers several times throughout the year.

  1. The store will receive a predetermined standard quantity of ProBuy flyers for each promotion.
    1. Stores requiring additional copies of a specific issue must order them.
  2. The ProBuy pricing flag on the customer’s Price Record Card designates whether the customer gets wholesale promotional pricing. In other words, in order for the customer to receive wholesale promotional pricing at the point-of-sale, their Price Record Card must be set to YES ‘Y’ for ProBuy.
