Merchandising Operating Aids Form Procedure

Step

Action

1

Find the Merch Operating Aids Form in the Forms Repository

2

Search form name or keyword archway or operating in the Form Name field -> All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

3

When completing the form, order by feature number and description, and include all necessary shipping information.

4

If the order requires the District Manager’s approval, forward the original copy to the District Office

5

When all necessary approvals have been received, mail the original copy of the order to Archway. Keep a duplicate copy at your location.

6

When the order arrives, check it against the order form and the packing slip.
