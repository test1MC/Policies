Promotional Materials Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Step

Action

1

Upon receipt of promotional material, open carton and read instruction sheet for the timing and direction on material that is to be displayed.

2

Set up all material per the instructions. If parts are missing contact the vendor  as indicated on the instruction sheet for each promotion.

3

Note the ending date for the contest and remove the POP promptly
