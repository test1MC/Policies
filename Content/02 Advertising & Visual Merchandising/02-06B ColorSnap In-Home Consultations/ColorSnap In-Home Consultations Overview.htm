ColorSnap In-Home Consultations Overview

The ColorSnap In‐home color consultation program offers our customers a unique opportunity to take advantage of this service along with special savings:

  • DIY customers must be Preferred Customers in order to receive a $50 ColorSnap® Gift Card with their in‐home color consultation which can be used on a future purchase.
  • Wholesale customers are eligible for a discounted in‐home color consultation.
