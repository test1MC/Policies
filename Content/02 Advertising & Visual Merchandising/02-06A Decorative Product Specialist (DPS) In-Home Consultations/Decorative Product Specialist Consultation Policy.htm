Decorative Product Specialist Consultation Policy

  1. DIY Customers must be a Preferred Customers in order to receive a $50 Sherwin-Williams Gift Card with their in-home color consultation. This can only be applied to a future purchase.
  2. A Customer Account is required to process this transaction.
  3. The Preferred Customer 10% Discount does not apply to this program.
  4. Wholesale Customers are eligible for an in-home color consultation.
