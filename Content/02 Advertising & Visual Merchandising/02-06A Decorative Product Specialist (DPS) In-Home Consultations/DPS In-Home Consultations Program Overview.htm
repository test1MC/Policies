DPS In-Home Consultations Program Overview

Decorative product specialists offer in-home color consultations. All decorative product specialist POP can be found through Order Express. The in‐home consultation price is $95.00. Sales number 1004-30248 is excluded from all promotional schedules, sales cards, and coupons that offer any type of discount on associated products.
