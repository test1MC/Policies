Markets & Services Procedure

Step

Action

1

Use the Color and Design Marketing brochure, "Where Color Takes Shape," to show the customer examples of available Custom Color And Design Marketing Services.

2

If your customer would like to see an example of a custom designed proposal, order an Industrial Maintenance Sample Board from Order Express (See Sample Presentation Boards for specific markets).

3

Qualify the customer so potential sales figures can be supplied to Color and Design Marketing: refer to market pricing potentials.

3

If necessary, navigate the Forms Repository -> Search the request form name or keyword in the Form Name field -> All available forms containing your search word will appear

Note: District Manager approval is not required to initiate a job request for Color and Design Marketing services for any of the markets.

4

Identify the project leader for the project.

5

Find out if a paint competitor, interior designer or architect is involved.

6

Stress the Total Package Color and Design Marketing Offers. In addition, communicate the convenience of a one-source supplier, our quality products and the professional team of Sherwin-Williams experts.

8

While visiting the job site, ask for elevation drawings, photos and floor plans to be used in processing the request.

9

Contact the Color and Design Marketing Group prior to mailing the request form to discuss project needs and expectations.

10

Submit projects via CMD.Sherwin.com (Property Management and Home Builder) or allow 4-5 weeks turnaround and send the completed Project Request Form (Industrial and Marine, and Product Finishes) to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company,
Color and Design Marketing,
101 Prospect Avenue 1780 Midland,
Cleveland, Ohio 44115

11

If an initial on-site meeting is conducted, and the customer requests a return visit by the consultant to present the proposal, contact Melissa Chernow 216.566.7258.

Note: Completed job requests will be returned directly to the Sales Representative or store manager for customer presentation within 4-5 weeks.
