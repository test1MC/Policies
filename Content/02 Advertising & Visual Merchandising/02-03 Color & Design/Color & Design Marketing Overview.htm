Color & Design Marketing Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

In the Americas Group, Color and Design Marketing offers qualified customers professional color styling and design services, targeting customers representing the greatest sales, profits, and repeat business potential. Offered at no charge to your customer, the unique service attracts returning and prospective customers, enhances company image, creates a broad customer base, and improves sales/profits.

Division funds these services. Division and District Managers receive a monthly project report listing all projects, their estimated sales potential, and the estimated value of the services provided.

A Design Service Value is listed for each account type in the Policy and Procedures section. The Design Service Value designates the approximate savings your customer will incur by utilizing Sherwin-Williams Color and Design Marketing services versus those of an outside consultant. Communicate this to your customer.
