Color Card Production Services Policy

Requests for color card production services come mostly from Product Finishes and Industrial Maintenance customers. Color and Design Marketing will compile information provided by the sales representative and submit a faxed quotation.

  1. A maximum of 50 cards per year per customer can be processed  by the Color and Design Marketing Department.
  2. Large runs (1,000+) must be produced by a Color Card Manufacturer and Color and Design Marketing will facilitate these orders.
