Markets & Services Policy

  1. A complete, applicable project request form must be submitted for each job request.
    1. Property Management Market Segment does not require a specific project request form.
  2. Each request must have a sales potential of at least $20,000 +.
  3. On-site surveys by Color and Design consultants are available if the Industrial Maintenance account meets the following criteria:
    1. Potential sales must warrant $50,000 +.
    2. Competition from other paint manufacturers or interior designers sending their representatives on-site.
    3. Your customer's top management requesting to meet with a staff member to discuss project needs and expectations.
    4. If available, elevation drawings, photos and floor plans should be submitted with each request.
