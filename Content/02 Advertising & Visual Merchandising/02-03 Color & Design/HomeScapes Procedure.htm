HomeScapes Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

HomeScapes is a color selection system that allows a builder to customize their neighborhoods and provide selection centers with color scheme books. This program is also suitable for painting contractors that do residential painting.

Step

Action

1

Order a HomeScapes sell sheet (2001739) and a HomeScapes Color Selection Card (1100069) from Order Express. A pack of order forms is included.

Note: A special collection of pastel schemes (1100074) is also available at Order Express.

2

Complete the order form and fax to (219) 347-3781 at Colwell/General, Attn: Nancy Strater, telephone 219-347-4327.

3

A HomeScapes Color Collection will be delivered to you for presentation to your customer. The color collection is assembled in a sturdy 8 1/2" x 11" 3-ring binder personalized with the customer’s name.
