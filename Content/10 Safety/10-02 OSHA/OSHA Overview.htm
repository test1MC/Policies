OSHA Overview

This section and the corresponding sections in the LiveSafe Administrators manual specify the requirements for the stores to be in compliance with the Occupational Health and Safety Administration’s (OSHA) maintenance and operational procedures required in the workplace. The agency may have representatives inspect the store in order to determine the store's compliance status.
