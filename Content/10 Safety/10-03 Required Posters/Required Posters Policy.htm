Required Posters Policy

It is necessary to post the federal and state law posters and the Sherwin-Williams Policies Poster because failure to do so could result in a fine to the Company if discovered during a wage and hour audit.

  1. Effective 7/1/12, all Sherwin-Williams locations in North America are required to post the SW Ethics Hotline Poster. 
  2. Federal, state, and local law posters, as required in your jurisdiction, must be posted at all stores in an area where they can be readily seen by both employees and applicants.
  3. The Sherwin-Williams Policies Poster must be posted at all stores in an area where they can be readily seen by both employees and applicants. 
  4. The E-Verify Poster must be posted in the front of the store where potential applicants will be able to review.
  5. For Quebec only: All posters must be in French or, if bilingual, the English version cannot be any more prominent than the French.
