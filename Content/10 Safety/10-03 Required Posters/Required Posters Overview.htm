SW Ethics Hotline Poster

The SW Ethics Hotline Poster provides employees contact information if they are facing a situation wherein they believe Company values or compliance with the law may be in question. To report a concern, employees should contact their manager, Human Resource Manager, Loss Prevention, or report it anonymously through the ethics hotline, 800-SWC-TRUE (an ethics web portal is also available).

State and Federal Labor Law Posters

This section does not apply to any locations in Canada

State and federal regulating agencies can issue changes to labor law posters at any time throughout the year. On average, there are about 200 changes per year.  GovDocs provides an automatic update service for the state and federal posters. If there are updates made to these materials, then stores will automatically receive the compliant posters directly.

Note: Access the GovDocs site to verify you have the most recent poster updates or scan the QR code on the bottom of the poster. The revision date will change when mandatory revisions are made or when inventory is reprinted. Revision dates should be on or after the date of the most recent labor law change.

SW Policies Poster

This poster provides information regarding the internal Sherwin-Williams policies, including: EEO, Non-Harassment, Workplace Violence, Policy on Computers, and the Policy on Electronic and Telephonic Communications.

E-Verify Poster

This poster shows employees and potential applicants that Sherwin-Williams uses E-Verify, an Internet-based system that determines the eligibility of their employees to work in the United States.
