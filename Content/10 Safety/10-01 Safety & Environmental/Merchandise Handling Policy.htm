Merchandise Handling Policy

  1. All material handling and storage operations must adhere to the LiveSafe standards identified in chapters 1, 2, 3, 5 and 6 of the LiveSafe Administrators Guide.
  2. Store Managers must ensure that all employees are using proper merchandise handling techniques.
  3. Stores with lift trucks must have operators that are properly trained by completing the related Store Training and Reference Tool (S.T.A.R.T) course, "Powered Industrial Truck Safety."
  4. Any operator must complete the S.T.A.R.T unit, "Powered Industrial Truck Safety."
