Chemical & Controlled Substance Procedure

Step

Action

1

Verify customer identification.

2

Record required information when processing the sale transaction by using COMMENT [F9] when on the line item of the identified controlled substance.

3

Report unusual transactions to Division Loss Prevention and District Manager immediately. Be prepared to provide district or division management copies of the transaction and any supporting information for the purchase provided by the customer. 

Note: Stores are not to contact or report transactions to local authorities directly.
