Safety & Environmental Policy

The LiveSafe Administrator's Guide and the related Store Training And Reference Tool (S.T.A.R.T) courses provide the policies and procedures for dealing with safety and environmental issues. LiveSafe should be the first source for answering and resolving Safety and Environmental questions.

  1. All employees must complete the START Unit, "Basic Safety and Loss Prevention," within the first two weeks of employment.
  2. All employees must be trained annually on the "Emergency Response Plan" in Unit 2 of the LiveSafe Administrator's Guide.
  3. All Environmental remediation projects are performed and coordinated by Division Loss Prevention and the Stores Group Safety/Environmental Compliance Department.
  4. Store employees must adhere to the safety health, environmental and transportation requirements of LiveSafe.
  5. The LiveSafe Administrator’s Guide applies to The Americas Group operations typically conducted at retail, wholesale and commercial stores. If a particular operation is not discussed in the Guide, and safety or environmental concerns should be addressed, Division Loss Prevention or the Stores Group Safety/Environmental Compliance Department must be contacted prior to implementation or continuance of such operations.
  6. Store employees are not authorized to enter into safety, health, or environmental contacts. Such contracts must be coordinated through Division Loss Prevention and the Safety and Environmental Compliance Department.
  7. LiveSafe is for internal use within The Americas Group. Dissemination, editing or revision of LiveSafe is strictly prohibited.

Note: Direct any questions to Division Loss Prevention or email The Americas Group Safety and Environmental Compliance at storesgroupsafety@sherwin.com.
