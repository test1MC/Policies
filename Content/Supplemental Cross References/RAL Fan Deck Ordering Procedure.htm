Other selling aids

Ensure that you accept the receipts on the purchase order. Sales number 160-8132 is an expense sales number for Other Selling Aids. Therefore, there is no need to submit the invoice on an Expense Transmittal because the invoice will be paid through the normal process in Accounts Payable.

RAL Fan Deck orders should be placed with Carol Sobota at Dorn Color Inc. in Cleveland, OH. Purchase orders should be faxed to Carol’s attention at 216-634-2822. Dorn requires a $40.00 minimum order. Orders normally ship the day of receipt.

  • The RAL-K1 booklet looks like the deck that was stocked at Order Express. It includes 16 classic RAL color samples per page in a gloss finish.
  • The RAL-K5 fan deck includes all classic RAL swatches that are approximately 2”x 5”, with one color per strip. It is available in semi gloss or gloss finish.
  • The RAL-K7 fan deck is the same size as the K-5 but includes 5 colors per strip. The K-7 is only available in a gloss finish. Additional products are available on Dorn’s web site at www.dorncolor.com.
