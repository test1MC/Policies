Received Not Billed from External Vendor Report Overview

Received Not Billed from External Vendor Report (Pre-Inventory Closing Report B) shows external shipments to your store / customer direct shipments where the merchandise was received or billed to the customer and not billed to your store. Received Not Billed from External Vendor entries are based on receipts entered in the POS that have not matched to an invoice in Oracle Purchasing.
