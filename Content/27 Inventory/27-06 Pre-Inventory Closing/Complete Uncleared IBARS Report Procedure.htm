Complete Uncleared IBARS Report Procedure

Step

Action

Pre-Inventory Closing Report D

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Processes -> SWeep ->  Inventory Adjustments -> Review Closing Reports.

2

Select the IBAR report.


Note: Hover over icons to display hints or additional information.

3

Click on the IBAR.

4

Review the IBAR for accuracy:

If

Then

An item is inaccurate or needs to be adjusted,

Select the item and make the appropriate changes in the editable fields. Click the check mark when finished.

Note:  To view more information about specific product lines, click on the line to view its details.

All the items on the IBAR are accurate,

Stores have the option to individually select each item or to bulk review all items on the PO.

Note: Stores also have the option to select “do not use” for any or all of the items.

Note: If necessary, click Add Product to include any additional items not on the report. Stores have the option to search for existing products on the IBAR or to include a product not already on the report.

5

After reviewing the Closing Report, click Add an IBAR if you have not found the IBAR needed. You can then search for other existing IBARs.

6

Click Save Report.

7

Check the Review Status to confirm that all the necessary information has been reviewed.

Note: The Review Status turns green after all lines are reviewed and saved for that particular PO. All POs and IBARs must have the green indicator before the Approve Report button is enabled.


8

Click Approve Report.
