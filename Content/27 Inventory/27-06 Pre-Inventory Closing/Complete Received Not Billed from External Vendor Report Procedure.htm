Complete Received Not Billed from External Vendor Report Procedure

Step

Action

Pre-Inventory Closing Report B

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Processes -> SWeep ->  Inventory Adjustments -> Review Closing Reports.

2

Select the Received Not Billed from External Vendor report.

Note: Hover over icons to display hints or additional information.

3

Click on the Purchase Order:

 

4

Review each Purchase Order for accuracy:

If

Then

An item is inaccurate or needs to be adjusted

Select the item and make the appropriate changes in the editable fields.

Click the check mark when finished.

Note:  To view more information about specific product lines, click on the line to view its details. This following information will appear on the side panel.

All the items on the Purchase Order are accurate

Stores have the option to individually select each item or to bulk review all items on the PO.

Note: Stores also have the option to select “do not use” for any or all of the items.

Note: If necessary, click Add Product to include any additional items not on the report. Stores have the option to search for existing products on the Purchase Order or to include a product not already on the Purchase Order.

5

After reviewing the Closing Report, click Add Purchase Order if you have not found the Purchase Order needed. The screen below will appear and you can search for other existing Purchases Orders.

6

Click Save Report.

Note: Press Filter to sort and Show Only Changed Information.

7

Check the Review Status to confirm that all the necessary information has been reviewed.

Note: The Review Status turns green after all lines are reviewed and saved for that particular PO. All POs and IBARs must have the green indicator before the Approve Report button is enabled.

8

Click Approve Report.
