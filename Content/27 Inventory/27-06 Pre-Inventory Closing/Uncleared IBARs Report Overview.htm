Uncleared IBARs Report Overview

The Uncleared IBARs Report (Pre-Inventory Closing Report D) shows IBARs that are not listed on the Internal Purchases History Report or were not voided on the Store Billing Report (“VI” entries).

If you discover that an IST or a Store Expense needs to be added to closing report D, call Store Billing at 216-566-2297, option #5.

Report Section #

Report Section Displays

1
  • IBARs for billing discrepancies.
  • Not listed or voided on the SPR and Invoice History Report
