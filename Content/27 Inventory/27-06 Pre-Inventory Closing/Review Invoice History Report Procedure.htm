Review Invoice History Report Procedure

The Invoice History Report, which was previously mailed, is now located in FactFinder and lists all entries booked to an inventory account at the store during a given inventory period for both internal and external transactions. Stores should use this report while reviewing the Closing Reports to determine the accuracy of them. This report can also help determine if an entry should be added or removed based on whether the invoice, IBAR, expense, and/or chargeback was or was not booked to the store, and if it was, whether it was done at the appropriate time.

Step

Action

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Store Reports -> FACTFinder.

Note: FACTFinder can be accessed from mySherwin as well.

2

Click Reports tab.

3

Select Store Purchases.

4

Select Invoice History Report under Detail.

Note: Click the Details button on the right-hand side before running the report.

5

Review the report and reference it while completing the Pre-Inventory Reports process.
