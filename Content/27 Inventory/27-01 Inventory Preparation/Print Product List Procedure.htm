Print Product List Procedure

This method is recommended when manually recording inventory in a location that includes items of similar product types or vendors (all sandpaper, all mixing equipment, etc), especially if they do not display bar codes or sales numbers.

The Product List will assist in the counting and recording process. Once printed, the report provides a worksheet used to note product locations, quantities, and obsolete status, if applicable, and if the product has been recorded on the store/branch computer system.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Enter Employee Number and Password -> Reports -> Product List.

2

Enter a Department, Sub-Department, and Category, if required. After entering the criteria, press ACCEPT [F1].

Note: You can either view this report on screen or print it on the Manager's printer. This report lists products in Manufacturer Part Number order within a specific Manufacturer.

3

Assign a location to the area you are going to count (i.e. in-stock wallcovering area, location - F3). For each sales number in this area, write the location, quantity, and obsolete status, if applicable, on the printed report.

4

Once you have completed counting the location, take the printed report to the Manager’s workstation or POS and enter the location, sales number, and quantity (if product is obsolete enter an [O] prior to entering the sales number). After entering the quantities, put an X in the Recorded column to show you have recorded the inventory in the computer.

5

Put an inventory control sticker on the products in that location. Mark a [PL-X] (PL= Product List - X= number identifying the report used) on the QTY line and a checkmark on the REC line.

6

Keep track of all printed reports and keep the reports on file.
