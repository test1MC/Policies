Prepare Inventory Locations – Automotive Procedure

To help control the inventory count, your branch should be broken into locations. You will be able to assign up to 24 locations within your branch. Consider the different inventory methods options available as you choose locations. Recall that the same inventory method must be used throughout an entire location.

The location indicators are as follows:

  • Front Room F1-F8
  • Back Room B1-B8
  • Mix Room/OtherZ1-Z8.

After dividing your Branch into locations, assign employees to specific locations for the counting process. You should always count inventory by sections within a location. Count all the merchandise on each shelf - no exceptions. Do not skip around because you may miss items.
