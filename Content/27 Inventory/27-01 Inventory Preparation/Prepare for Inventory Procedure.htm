Prepare for Inventory Procedure

Step

Action

Prepare & Organize

1

Prepare the Store/Branch and employees for the physical inventory process.

2

Segregate all obsolete products.

3

Ensure all merchandise physically received in your store is received against a Purchase Order on the Merchandise Management System.

Note: Do not have any Purchase Orders in an In Process status.

4

Ensure the Hand-Held Scanner (PDT) is working properly and that both batteries are fully charged.
5 Ensure that all inventoriable items have sales numbers established for them. Where possible, ensure that bar codes and sales numbers are readily available as you enter counts, either on the package or a shelf label.

Count & Record

1

Prior to performing any counts, Clear Inventory. From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Clear Inventory.

2

Print any Product List reports to use for counting inventory and pre-number them.

3

Map your Store/Branch Locations.

4

Ensure all merchandise has been recorded, regardless of age or condition.

Review & Close

1

Review the available inventory reports.

2

Make any corrections necessary to the physical counts.

3

CLOSE Inventory. From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Close Inventory.

File

1

Create an inventory file containing the following items:

The final:

  • Physical Inventory Reports All Products
  • Physical Inventory Report Obsolete
  • Write-In Pages

Note: Both the Manager and Assistant Manager must sign these reports.

Signed copies of:

  • Perpetual No Physical
  • Significant Variances
  • Any of the Product Lists used for counting inventory
