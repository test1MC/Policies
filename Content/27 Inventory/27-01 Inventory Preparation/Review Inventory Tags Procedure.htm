Review Inventory Tags Procedure

Someone other than the person who counted a specific location needs to review the location counted and perform the following steps:

Step

Action

1

Ensure there is an inventory tag on all Manufactured Paint products.

2

Recount and check the REX NUMBERs, sizes, and other information on the inventory tag for at least 10 percent of the items counted.

If

Then

There are errors

Recount and check additional inventory items.

The information on the tag is correct

Initial the [VERIFIED BY] line of the tag.

3

Advise the Manager that the inventory has been completed in the area.

4

The Manager must assign someone to pull the white copy of the inventory tag and leave the yellow copy intact in the area.

Note: All tags pulled must be entered into the computer system.

Once entered, write REC on the Verified By line and then return to the person maintaining the control log.

5

The person maintaining the control log must verify that all the white copies of tags have been pulled and recorded and note this on the control log.

6

Retain the white copy of the Inventory Tags in a file with a copy of the Tag Control Log.
