Sign Off Pre-Inventory Checklist Procedure

Stores are now required to sign off in SWEEP to verify they have completed the Pre-Inventory Checklist. In addition, DMs will review these sign offs, and, upon completion, will verify and sign off that all checklist items have been completed. Do not sign off on future weeks (there are four weeks, total).

Step Action

1

Source -> Source Operations -> Processes -> SWeep

2

Select Pre Inventory Checklist.

3

As you complete the week’s checklist, mark the corresponding tasks as complete.
