Prepare Inventory Locations – Stores Overview

Use the Store Layout sample as a guideline to divide the Store into locations. This will help to identify where to go to research any counting variances. You will be able to assign up to 24 locations within the Store. The location indicators are as follows:

  • Front Room : F1-F8
  • Back Room : B1-B8
  • Other Room/Location: X1-X8

After dividing the Store into locations, assign employees to specific locations for the counting process.

  • Always count inventory by sections within a location.
  • Count all the merchandise on each shelf - no exceptions.
  • Do not skip around because you may miss items.
