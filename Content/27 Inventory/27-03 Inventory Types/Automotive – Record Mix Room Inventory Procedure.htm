Automotive – Record Mix Room Inventory Procedure

Finished mixes should be recorded under the sales number that pertains to the “AI” prefix product number representing the correct CNS suffix/prefix for the formula. (i.e...GCA in a gallon 224-9993 or B7F in a five 228-2234).

All open containers in the mixing room should be counted to the nearest full unit. If a unit is at least half full, count the item as 1 unit. If the item is less than half full, count the item as “0”.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Enter Employee Number and Password -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Record Inventory.

2

Enter the location of the Mix Room (the location will be between Z1 and Z8).

Note: You must use the zones starting with “Z” to record your Mix Room inventory.

3

Enter the Sales Number and Count of the Products in the Mix Room.

4

Once you have entered the items counted in the Mix Room, exit out of that Zone and continue onto the next Zone.

Note: The items that are counted in the Mix Room must be recorded in the Zone sequence starting with “Z.” Do not include the Mix Room Inventory with Zones “F,” “B,” OR “X.”

Note: The Mix Room inventory will be INCLUDED in the physical inventory count (recorded on the Inventory Analysis). However, it will not be included in the starting Perpetual Inventory quantities.  The quantities counted in the Mix Room will also not be included in the Significant Variances and Perpetual Inventory Reports.

MIX ROOM INVENTORY REPORT:

The Mix Room will have its own Inventory Report showing the products counted and the qty recorded.  The report is located within the Inventory Report Menu screen, option 11. Mix Room.  This report must be printed and retained along with the Significant Variances and Perpetual No Physical reports for Audit purposes.
