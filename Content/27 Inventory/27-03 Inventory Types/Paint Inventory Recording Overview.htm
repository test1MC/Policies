Paint Inventory Recording Overview

Liquid goods in full containers must be recorded based on package size up through the one-gallon size.

Record sizes over one gallon in number of [Gallons]. For example:

  • Three quarts must be recorded as "3"
  • Two five gallon pails must be recorded as "10"
  • Large drums must be recorded in the number of gallons (check the weight to ensure that the number of gallons recorded are accurate)

Liquid Goods in partially filled five-gallon pails and drums must be recorded in the nearest full gallons. For example:

  • A half full five gallon pail must be recorded as "3"
  • A half full 55 gallon drum must be recorded as "28"

Kits must be recorded in units. Do not record as gallons.

SW manufactured paint products have a manufactured date code on the lid or stenciled on the side of the pail, drum, or carton. The four numbers give you the following information: the first three digits are the day of the year and the last digit represents the year. For example:

  • 2678 = the 267th day of 2018 or September 24, 2018

All mistints must be counted. Gallons of paint mistinted during the current year (and on hand) must be entered as sales number 9999-13205 and quarts must be entered as 9999-13197. Mistints can be entered through the Hand-Held Scanner (PDT) or at the POS.
