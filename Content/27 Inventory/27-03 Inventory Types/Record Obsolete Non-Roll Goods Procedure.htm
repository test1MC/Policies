Record Obsolete Non-Roll Goods Procedure

Step Action
1

You are able to inventory non-rolled good floorcovering by entering the sales number while taking inventory and pressing the letter “O" key.
2

The non-roll good will not automatically print a tag. Please make sure that you identify the obsolete non-roll good with sales number 9901-1180-9. Be sure to use sales number 9901-1180-9 when you sell the obsolete non-roll good.
