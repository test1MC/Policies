Inventory Types Overview

Inventory Type Description
Associated Products

Current AP Merchandise includes:

  • AP merchandise included in the Store Plan-O-Gram.
  • AP merchandise purchased after December 31st three years previous.

Obsolete AP Merchandise include:

  • AP merchandise purchased prior to January 1st two years previous.
  • AP merchandise discontinued prior to January 1st of the current year.
  • Damaged AP merchandise not in salable condition.
Brushers & Rollers Current Brushes & Rollers include:
  • Brushes & Rollers included in the Store Plan-O-Gram.
  • Brushes & Rollers purchased after December 31st three years previous.

Obsolete Brushes & Rollers include:

  • Brushes & Rollers purchased two years prior to the previous year.
  • Brushes & Rollers discontinued prior to January 1st of the current year.

Damaged Brushes & Rollers not in salable condition.
Current Paint
  • Regular price listed products and package size manufactured after January 1st two years previous (Ex. If this is the October 2000 Inventory this date would be January 1, 1998 and the date code would be 0018 or later). Check the top of the can and the stenciling on cartons for this manufacturing date.
  • Tinting Color (Blnd-A-Clr), (Nuodex). Reminder: Estimate the amount of tinting color (to the nearest whole quart) in the tinting machine and record with the unopened quarts.
  • Price listed special products manufactured after June 30th of the previous year (Ex. If this is the October 2000 Inventory this date would be June 30, 1999 and the date code would be 1829 or later).
  • Products discontinued during the current year.
  • Mistints made in the current year.
  • Gallons of paint mistinted during the current year (and on hand) must be entered as sales number 9999-13205 and quarts must be entered as 9999-13197.
  • Mistints can be entered through the Hand-Held Scanner (PDT) or at the POS.
  • Paint intermixes created after June 30th of the previous year (Ex. If this is the October 2000 Inventory this date would be June 30, 1999 and the date code would be 1829 or later). These products must be entered on the write-in pages using the appropriate class number.
Floorcovering - Obsolete Rolled Goods
  • Floorcovering merchandise purchased prior to three years from December 31st of the current year (ex. For the November 2017 inventory, material purchased on or before 12/31/14 may be considered obsolete).
  • Damaged Floorcovering merchandise not in sellable condition.
  • Special order merchandise not returnable to the vendor for credit.
  • Remnants - There are two distinct types of remnants:
    1. Carpet remnants represent end cuts/leftovers of sold orders and are pieces of carpet that are 90 square feet or less. These remnants are to be inventoried as Obsolete.
    2. Resilient remnants that are less than 36 square feet are to be inventoried as Obsolete.
Obsolete Paint
    SW manufactured paint products discontinued prior to January 1st of the current year. Obsolete paint products that were on hand at the previous inventory should have been relabeled with one of the obsolete sales numbers. For a list of the obsolete sales numbers, see Previous Obsolete.
  • SW manufactured paint products that are past the product’s shelf life (usually IM and CC products). Check the top of cans and/or pails for the manufacture date as you record inventory (Ex. If this is the November 2005 Inventory and the product in question has an eighteen-month shelf life, any product made before May 1, 2004, batch date 1204 or before, is obsolete). Verify the product shelf life on the Data Pages if you have questions.
  • Price listed SW manufactured merchandise with a manufacturing date BEFORE January 1st two years previous (Ex. If this is the October 2000 Inventory this date would be January 1, 1998 and the batch date would be 3657 or before). You must check the top of cans and the stenciling on pails or cartons for this date as you record inventory.
  • Special S-W manufactured merchandise with a manufacturing date before June 30th of the previous year (Ex. If this is the October 2000 Inventory this date would be June 30, 1999 and the batch date would be 1829 or before).
  • Damaged merchandise not in salable condition.
  • Mistints made before the current year. Enter the letter [O], 9999-13205 (number of gallons) or 9999-13197 (number of quarts).
  • The only class-controlled products you can record as Obsolete are those products listed as allowable class numbers. For more information see 04-01 Class Numbers. These would be products with a manufacturing date before June 30th of the previous year (Ex. If this is the October 2000 Inventory this date would be June 30, 1999 and the batch date would be 1829 or before).
  • You must enter the landed unit cost for each of these products.
  • Do not use the GP % with the class number when recording quantities for inventory.

Note: If you have obsolete Class Number paint, enter on the obsolete section of the write-in pages.
Obsolete Powder Coatings Powder coatings that are past the 12 month shelf life. Verify product shelf life as you record inventory.
Spray Equipment Current Spray Equipment (Not Being Used As Rental) includes:
  • Spray Equipment merchandise included in the Store Plan-O-Gram.
  • Spray Equipment merchandise purchased after December 31st three years previous.

Obsolete Spray Equipment includes:

  • Damaged Spray Equipment merchandise not in salable condition.
  • Spray Equipment merchandise purchased prior to January 1st two years previous.
  • Spray Equipment merchandise discontinued prior to January 1st of the current year.
Wallcovering Current Wallcovering includes:
  • In-stock Wallcovering included in the Store Plan-O-Gram.
  • In-stock Wallcovering merchandise purchased direct from a vendor after December 31st three years previous.
  • Book Order Wallcovering awaiting customer pick-up not older than 30 days.

Obsolete Wallcovering includes:

  • Book Order Wallcovering refused by the customer and not returnable to the vendor for credit.
  • Book Order Wallcovering that has been on hand 31 days or more even if not refused by the customer.
  • Damaged Wallcovering merchandise not in salable condition.
Window Treatment

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Current Window Treatments include:
  • Window Treatment merchandise purchased after December 31st three years previous.
  • Window Treatment merchandise awaiting customer pick-up not older than 30 days.

Obsolete Window Treatments include:

  • Window Treatment merchandise refused by the customer and not returnable to the vendor for credit.
  • Window Treatment merchandise that has been on hand 31 days or more even if not refused by the customer.
  • Damaged Window Treatment merchandise not in salable condition.
