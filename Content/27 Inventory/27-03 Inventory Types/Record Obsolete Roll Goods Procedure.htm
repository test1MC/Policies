Record Obsolete Roll Goods Procedure

Step

Action

Obsolete Roll Goods

1

Scan the barcode at the bottom of the inventory roll tag while recording inventory. Verify the registration, dye lot, and quantity.

2

Press the letter “O” key to mark the roll good as obsolete while taking inventory.

3

Enter “Y” [YES] to confirm.

Note: The obsolete roll good will be costed at one penny per square foot after inventory and will be identified with a new obsolete roll good sales number 9901-1182-5.

4

Print an updated obsolete roll good inventory tag. 

5

Access the obsolete rolled good in Customer Order Entry or Point-of-Sale by using sales number 9901-1182-5. You will be able to see the relationship with the other inventory of the same style and color.

Note: You are not able to mix product inventory types [Obsolete, Asset Value and Perpetual] on the same line in Customer Order Entry and Point-of-Sale because each inventory type uses a different sales number.
