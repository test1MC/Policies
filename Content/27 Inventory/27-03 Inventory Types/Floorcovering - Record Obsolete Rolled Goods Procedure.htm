Floorcovering - Record Obsolete Rolled Goods Procedure

Step

Action

1

Is this a Rolled Good?

If Then
Yes
1 Scan the barcode at the bottom of the inventory roll tag while recording inventory.
2

Verify the registration, dye lot, and quantity. Press the letter O key to mark the roll good as obsolete while taking inventory. Enter Y [YES] to confirm.

Note: The obsolete roll good will be costed at one penny per square foot after inventory and will be identified with a new obsolete roll good sales number 9901-1182-5.
3 You will be prompted to print an updated obsolete roll good inventory tag.
No
1

You are able to inventory non-rolled good floorcovering by entering the sales number while taking inventory and pressing the letter “O” key.

Note: The non-roll good will not automatically print a tag. Make sure that you identify the obsolete non-roll good with sales number 9901-1180-9.
