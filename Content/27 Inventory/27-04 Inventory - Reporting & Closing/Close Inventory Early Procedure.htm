Close Inventory Early Procedure

Follow this procedure if you choose to close inventory before the official inventory day. [Example: You have been given permission by your District Manager to close your inventory on Friday because your store is closed on Saturday, inventory day.]

Step

Action

1

All point-of-sale terminals must be closed before you will be able to close inventory.

Note: Remember to perform the Close transaction on the main system last.

2

After closing the main point-of-sale system, press CANCEL [F5] when prompted.

If

Then

You want to continue

Press ACCEPT [F1]

You want to return to the Main Menu

CANCEL

3

After pressing CANCEL, the system will return to the Main Menu. Select Merchandise Management to continue the inventory close process.

Note: The system will automatically go through End of Day after closing inventory.
