Complete Inventory Reports for Close Procedure

After entering and reviewing  all of your physical counts, complete the inventory:

Step

Action

Complete Inventory Reports for Close

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management - > Enter Employee Number and Password -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Inventory Report Menu.

2

Select Inventory Reports for Close.

3

Access the reports to review [Shift + F4] and identify any corrections that must be made.

Corrections

1

Enter the appropriate location where the recording error occurred.

2

Enter the appropriate sales number.

3

You will see a message [You have already recorded a count of XX in this location (XX). Press ENTER to continue].

4

Press ENTER.

5

Now you can enter the correct quantity for the product.

6

For corrections to Write-In pages go to the appropriate write-in page and make the necessary corrections. See Record Products Write-In Pages.
