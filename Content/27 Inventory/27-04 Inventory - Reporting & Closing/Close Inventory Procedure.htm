Close Inventory Procedure

  • You cannot close your inventory if any other terminal (Hand-Held Scanner (PDT), POS station, or Manager’s workstation) is still in the [Record Inventory] process.
  • Before performing the Inventory Close you must generate the Inventory Reports for Close and complete the point-of-sale Close Transaction on all point-of-sale terminals. Once all point-of-sale Close Transaction are complete, you can continue with the Inventory Close at the main POS.
  • After completing and closing your inventory the End of Day process will automatically run.

Note: Inventory Close will not automatically begin. Ensure the following message appears before starting: Inventory Counts Have Been Detected for Close.

  • Ready-To-Go- Merchandise [Y/N]
  • Rental Equipment [Y/N]

Note: You will not be able to perform the Close Inventory until ALL write-in pages are completed. If you do not have any Write-In pages, answer “Y” because a blank write-in page will be sent to Cleveland.

Note: This message is alerting you that once you close your inventory the physical counts entered will become your new perpetual inventory balance. For any products you did not count the balance will be 0.

Note: If you answer Y(es) and you have a low number of physical counts completed, you will get the following message - YOU HAVE NO (or a number) PHYSICAL INVENTORY RECORDS (WHICH INCLUDES WRITE-IN PAGES) followed by the message in Step 4.
