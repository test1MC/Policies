Print Physical Inventory Reports Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Enter Employee Number and Password -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Inventory Report.

2

Select Inventory Reports.

The following three reports will be printed:

  • Significant Variance
  • Perpetual No Physical
  • Write-In Pages

Note: You must review these three reports and make adjustments prior to performing the inventory close.
