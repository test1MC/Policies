Physical Inventory Adjustments Overview

Stage

Description

PASS 1

  • Approximately 3-5 days after the count, you will receive an SW Field Communication notifying you that PASS 1 is Open. The PIA System can be found in SWEEP.
  • Pass 1 will only be open for two days. During this time, you can add missed counts and change quantities and costs of products already counted.
  • The mandatory review is required (view your Inventory Procedure Manual for more information).

PASS 2

  • Stores no longer have the ability to enter Pass 2 adjustments in SWEEP. Counts need to be accurate on 11/1. All adjustments must go through your DM.
