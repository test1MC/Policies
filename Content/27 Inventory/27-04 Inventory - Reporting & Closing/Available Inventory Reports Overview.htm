Available Inventory Reports Overview

Report

Description

Significance Variance

  • This report is required and shows unit variances based on a dollar variance between the perpetual balance and your physical count.
  • It will be sorted from the highest dollar variance to the lowest dollar variance. These variances must be researched. If there is an error in your physical count, make any corrections necessary to your physical counts. Make any corrections before you close your inventory. For more information, see Count Errors.
  • If there is an error in your perpetual balance, no adjustment is necessary. Keep in mind that you are reviewing comparisons between the perpetual balance and your physical count. Some of your variances could be caused by an incorrect perpetual balance created by inaccurate receiving or selling, not expensing a mistint, or an inaccurate physical count.

Perpetual No Physical

  • This report is required and shows products that have a perpetual balance but have not had a physical count recorded.
  • Verify that none of these products are available in the Store. If they are available, though, record a count for the product using the appropriate location.

Write-In Pages

  • This report is required and shows all the products that have been recorded on the write-in pages.
  • Review the report for completion and finish recording any products that need to be included on the appropriate write-in page.
  • For more information, see 27-02 Inventory – Counting & Recording.

Sales Number – Individual

  • Enter a sales number to generate a report showing the perpetual balance compared to physical count for the sales number entered (generate this report at any point during the counting process).

All Products

  • This report shows every product, including floorcovering roll goods you have counted, comparing the perpetual balance to your physical count.

Obsolete Products

  • This report shows only the products recorded with an [O] preceding the sales number to indicate Obsolete. Review this report to ensure that your obsolescence has been recorded properly.

Mixing Room* (Automotive only)

  • This report lists all items counted in the Mixing Room, Section Z, during Physical Inventory. Review this report to verify that the Mixing Room was counted completely during the physical count.
