Significant Variance Report Policy

  1. All stores must review and verify their Significant Variance Report ($>100 Variance Report) for accuracy of their Nov. 1st Physical Count.
  2. This report must be printed the day of Inventory and retained for audit purposes.
  3. Automotive Only: All Branches must review and verify their Significant Variance Report.
    1. This report must be printed, reviewed, adjustments made and double-checked, and signed copies must be retained.
    2. The Significant Variance Report can be customized locally from a minimum of $.01 to a maximum of $25.00.
