Inventory Closing Reports Overview

The purpose of closing reports is to account for timing differences between the physical count and the book inventory as of the given inventory date. These reports provide the store with an opportunity to identify any unknown discrepancies that would otherwise create inventory shrink or pickup. Generated entries can be deleted or changed. Similarly, omitted entries can be added, if necessary.

Questions regarding closing reports should be directed to Store Billing (216-566-2297, option 5).

The following closing reports are accessible through SWeep:

Internal Closing Report

Description

Billed Not Received - Internal (“A”)
  • A1: Plant or DSC shipments to either the store or directly to the customer where the merchandise was billed on the Store Purchases Report, but was not physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date.
  • A2: Inter Store Requests (ISR’s) where the merchandise was billed on the Store Purchases Report, but was not physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date.

Received Not Billed – Internal (“C”)
  • C1: Plant or DSC shipments to either the store or directly to the customer where the merchandise was physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date, but was not billed on the Store Purchases Report.
  • C2: Inter Store Requests (ISR’s) where the merchandise was physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date, but was not billed on the Store Purchases Report.

Uncleared IBARs  (“D”)
  • D1: IBARs generated on, or prior to, the inventory date, but have not been credited, charged, or voided on the Store Purchases Report.
  • D2: Inter Store Transfers (IST’s) OR Store Expense Transactions that were generated on, or prior to, the inventory date, but have not been credited or charged on the Store Purchases Report.

External Closing Report

Description

Received Not Billed – External (“B”)
  • B1: External shipments to either the store or directly to the customer where the merchandise was physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date, but was not billed on the Store Purchases Report.
  • B-Other: External shipments to either the store or directly to the customer where the merchandise is assumed to be physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date, but was not billed on the Store Purchases Report. Receipts for these orders have not been entered in MMS. The assumption that the material was received at the store is based on a pending invoice(s) attached to the purchase order.

Uncleared Chargebacks (“E”)
  • E: Chargebacks generated on or prior to the inventory date, but have not been credited, charged, or voided on the Store Purchases ReportNote: The Invoice History Report, which is located in FactFinder, only shows Inventory accounts, so stores will not see anything for freight or labor. In addition, chargebacks under $5.00 should not be created and will not be processed per 07-02.

Billed Not Received - External (“F”)
  • F: External shipments to either the store or directly to the customer where the merchandise was billed on the Store Purchases Report but was not physically received, counted, or billed to the customer on, or prior to, the inventory date.

Product Recodings (“R”)
  • R: Discrepancy Reports submitted on, or prior to, the inventory date, but have not been re-coded on the Store Purchases Report.

Note: For more information, review The Invoice History Report, which is located in FactFinder.

When reviewing the reports, users have the option to view any changes made to a specific IBAR/Purchase Order by clicking the Changes button. Information provided in this search includes: date of the change, user name of the person who made the change and action taken by the user.
