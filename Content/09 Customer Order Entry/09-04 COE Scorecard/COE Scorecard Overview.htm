COE Scorecard Overview

The purpose of the Scorecard is to assist with the continued review of Customer Order Entry [COE]. The Scorecard provides a summary of the key areas of COE and identifies areas that may need additional attention and areas that are progressing well. Review the Scorecard monthly and address areas that need attention to gain the full benefits of the system.

For more Customer Order Entry information, see Customer Order Entry

Type Area Summary
Orders by SOURCE

 

 

 

Hotkey/ Other [Quick Order]

Number of orders taken in Quick Order.

 

Store Interface Ent Order

Number of orders entered directly into Customer Order Entry.

 

Store Entered Order Total

Total number of orders entered by store employees.

 

E-Commerce Order Count

Number of order electronically transmitted to store.

 

Total Orders

Total employee entered and electronically transmitted orders.
Misc. Order Types

 

 

 

Home Account Cust Order

Number of orders taken for Home Store accounts.

 

Visiting Acct Cust Orders

Number of orders taken for visiting customer accounts.

 

Non-Acct Orders-DIY, EMP, etc.

Number of orders without S-W account numbers.

 

Pending Open Orders

Number of orders in pending status – not sold.

 

New Orders

Number of orders awaiting action to be taken at the store.

 

In Process Orders

Number of orders that are in the process of being filled at the store.

 

Closed Orders

Number of orders that have been completed and sold.

 

Canceled Order Count

Number of orders that have been canceled.
Line Counts

 

 

 

Sold Lines

Number of lines where a sale transaction was processed for the product line.

 

Committed Lines

Number of lines where stock was available and inventory was committed for the order quantity.

 

Partially Committed Lines

Number of lines where inventory was committed for part of the order quantity and the balance was placed ON ORDER.

 

On Order Lines

Number of lines where external items were placed on the Order Reminder Report and internal items were placed on Memo Demand.

 

No Action Lines

Number of non-committed and/or non-ordered lines (mainly happens with EDI orders. Employee is required to take action - either commit the inventory or place an order – then the item moves to the next status).

 

Cancelled Lines

Number of order lines that have been canceled.

 

Total Line Count

Total lines for all orders.
Miscellaneous

 

 

 

Partial Sales

Number of orders where Inventory was committed and sold for part of the order quantity and the balance was placed ON ORDER.

 

Average Lines Per Order

Average number of lines for all Customer Orders.

 

Open Orders Over 30 Days

Number of orders that have been open for over 30 days.

 

Delivery Orders

Orders marked as Delivery Orders.

 

STAR Order Lines F/OE Ord

Customer Order lines pulled into an internal STAR purchase order.
