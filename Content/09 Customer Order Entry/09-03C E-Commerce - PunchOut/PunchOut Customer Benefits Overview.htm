PunchOut Customer Benefits Overview

  • More Intuitive Interface
    • Our customers can now shop as they normally do in their personal lives within a more intuitive and satisfying experience.
  • Efficient/Real-Time Access to Data
    • Customer specific pricing, catalogs, and data updates will now occur in real time, and a more robust set of data will be available, all within a user-friendly interface that helps customers accomplish tasks quickly and accurately.
  • Personalization/Customization
    • PunchOut will archive the activities of our customers, surfacing frequently and recently purchased items, frequently used colors, and past purchases to aid efficient ordering.
  • Real-Time Pricing & Product Availability Updates
    • Customers do not have to wait for or seek price or product availability updates; it is all current on website.
  • Increased Compliance
    • PunchOut allows for more control over what is displayed and how customers’ properties interact with our products, which will improve property compliance.
  • Training & Support
    • PunchOut will be paired with robust training and support resources to ensure that all customers quickly realize the associated value and benefit.
