Create Customer PunchOut Order Procedure

Step

Action

1

Customers log into their procurement system.

2

Select Sherwin-Williams as the vendor, which punches out to a customer specific version of Sherwin-Williams.com.

3

Build cart.

4

Customers can punch out to shop across multiple vendors and categories within their procurement system.

Note: Orders from various vendors creates a single requisition/cart.

5

Checkout.

Note: After products are selected for purchase, Sherwin-Williams.com transmits order details back to the procurement system, where our customers can route purchases for approval, just as many of them do today.
