New PunchOut Customers Procedure

Customers likely to be appropriate candidates for PunchOut include:

  • National Account Customers utilizing a third-party procurement system (i.e. Ariba, Coupa, OPS, etc.)
  • Customers who maintain their own internal procurement system that has EDI or XML capabilities (i.e. Extra Space Storage Facilities)
  • Customers who are looking for an automated solution and have the IT resources to support the process

Step

Action

1

Collect the following information from the customer:

  • Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email
  • Parent Number (for National Account Customers)
  • Child Account Information (if it is a local property request)

2

Email ecatalogs@sherwin.com with your request and the information listed above.
