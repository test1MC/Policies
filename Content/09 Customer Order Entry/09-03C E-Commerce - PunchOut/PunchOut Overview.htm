PunchOut Overview

PunchOut is the term used to describe the link between a procurement system (e.g., Ariba, OpsTechnology) and Sherwin-Williams.com. Punching out to a new window will provide customers with a more robust shopping, browsing, and purchasing experience than was previously available in procurement platforms like Ariba. The PunchOut window displays a version of Sherwin-Williams.com customized for customers, giving SW control over what they see and how they interact with our products. National Account customers in particular are likely to use PunchOut to browse their custom catalog and order supplies for their properties.

The PunchOut landing page is designed to support findability of products through search, navigation, and personalized modules that allow users to quickly and easily add to their cart and return to the requisition process.
