Receiving E-Commerce Orders Overview

Customers that have their orders fulfilled at a POS and choose to submit their POs directly to Sherwin-Williams can do so by contacting the Stores E-Commerce Department at or 216-515-8998.  Once the implementation process is completed, the customer’s order will be submitted directly to Sherwin-Williams and routed through MySW to the appropriate store. This is an automated process which requires no manual intervention entering the customer’s order in the POS COE (Customer Order Entry) screen. The Sherwin-Williams store can then fulfill the order.
