Customer Website & Electronic Invoice Overview

In a web based process, the customer will give the suppliers access to a website. This is a manual process which requires our stores to log into the customer’s designated site. Once the suppliers have accessed the site, they can:

  • Review and print POs
  • Perform PO acknowledgments
  • Submit invoices

Customers who have elected to receive their invoices electronically can do so by contacting the Stores E-Commerce Department by email edi.stores@sherwin.com or by phone 216-515-8998. The customer may choose to receive their invoices as an attachment .CSV or .PDF file on a system generated email or directly through a system generated electronic format (i.e. EDI or xml). 

Customers using the email option require account numbers to be added to their file so that the correct account receives invoices for the correct email address. Customers electing to receive their invoices directly will require setup and testing with the implementation group in the Stores E-Commerce Department.

If the customer requests a previously billed invoice to be resent electronically, please contact the Stores E-Commerce Department with the transaction number and transaction date.

Note: Even though Sherwin-Williams can send invoices and credit invoices, it cannot send price corrections to the customer. When pricing needs to be corrected, a complete credit and re-bill at the correct price is required. If a pricing issues persists (reconciling PRC versus catalog or price billed), please contact Stores E-Commerce Department and have the account number, account name, sales numbers, the parent name, and parent number (if available). The E-Commerce Department will work with the customer, the sales contact, and national accounts to make the correct adjustments to the PRC or electronic catalog. Once the appropriate edits are completed, there may be corrective transactions to be run by the Sherwin-Williams store.
