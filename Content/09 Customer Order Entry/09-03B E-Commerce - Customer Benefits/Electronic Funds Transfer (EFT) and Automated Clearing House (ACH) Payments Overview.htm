Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) and Automated Clearing House (ACH) Payments Overview

Customers can elect to submit payments electronically via EFT or through an ACH. In this method of payment, the customer electronically transfers the payments to Sherwin-Williams and sends a remit with the details. The customer will have to fill out the EFT Form found in the Forms Repository on Source and submit it to Credit Operations for processing.  Please see Sherwin-Williams EFT Info for bank and A/R information to provide to customers.
