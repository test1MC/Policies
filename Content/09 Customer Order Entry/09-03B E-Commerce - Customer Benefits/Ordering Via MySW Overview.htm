Ordering Via MySW Overview

This web based service is provided by Sherwin-Williams. By clicking on the link and logging in, customers will be able to place their order online for all of the products we have to offer. This process is customer driven, and it requires the customer to setup their accounts in MySW. The customer is usually required to register themselves as a MySW user.  This can be done from the MySW website or by calling 800-4SHERWIN, Option 3 for assistance.
