Update COE Order Procedure

Employees will only be able to update and review NEW or IN PROCESS customer orders. This process should be followed to review all E-Commerce and EDI Customer Orders sent to your store.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Enter Employee Number and Password -> Update/ Review.

2

Enter the Control Number, if known, or enter specific information to narrow your search or press Accept [F1] to return a list of all orders in NEW or IN PROCESS status.

3

Select the correct order to review.

Note: Override the Committed, Ordered, and Available fields by pressing View [F7].

Note: Partially filled orders cannot be canceled. However, you can cancel an individual line on an order by selecting Update Order from the Order Entry menu. Select the line that you choose to cancel and press the DELETE key. Once all lines have either been filled or cancelled the order will no longer show up in point of sale.
