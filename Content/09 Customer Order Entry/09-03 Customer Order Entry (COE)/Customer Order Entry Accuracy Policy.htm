Customer Order Entry Accuracy Policy

Customer service improves and Point-of-sale transactions are processed more quickly when the customer order is accurate and contains the following information:

  • Required Date
  • Selling Price
  • Comments
  1. Employees must enter the date (REQUIRED) that the customer needs the product.
    1. It is important to enter an accurate date because internal products could be automatically placed on a Memo Demand and added to your next Suggested STAR Order if not currently available in your store.
  2. External products and those Internal products that are required before your next STAR delivery date will print on an ITEMS TO BE ORDERED report when you finish the current order.
  3. Employees are required to place orders for products on the Items to be Ordered Report. One of the future enhancements to Customer Order Entry will be the automatic generation of Internal and External Purchase Orders.
  4. Employees must ensure that the customer's price is accurate while processing the order.
    1. Discount information can be brought into the sale by pressing the Discount (DISC) key. Point of sale pricing will not be automatic for any customer order item with a zero (0) selling price.
  5. Employees must only enter appropriate comments on point-of-sale transactions and customer orders because all comments print on customer invoices and statements. Any racial, ethnic, or derogatory remark entered on a point-of-sale transaction or customer order is considered Gross Misconduct and usually results in termination.
