Customer Order Entry Delivery Overview

The Bill of Lading function is a feature that is designed to ensure that accurate shipping information is captured when a delivery order is processed for a customer. The COE Delivery and Pickup Schedule feature gives you electronic functionality in creating a Delivery and Pickup Schedule for your store.
