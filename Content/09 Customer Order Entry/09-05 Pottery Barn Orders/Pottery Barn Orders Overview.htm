Pottery Barn Orders Overview

Pottery Barn vignette orders processed through the S-W Credit department.

  • Do not set up an account with POS.
  • The cost of the paint and painting supplies provided to Pottery Barn, Pottery Barn Kids, Pbteen, and West Elm is incurred by Stores Group and processed with an IBAR 4.

Note: Tinted paint will create an order in Customer Order Entry. You will need to complete an Order Disposition to correct your inventory.
