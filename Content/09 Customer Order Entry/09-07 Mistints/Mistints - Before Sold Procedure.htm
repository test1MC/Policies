Mistints - Before Sold Procedure

Mistints may be processed when they occur during tinting or by recalling the order in Customer Order Entry.  If an order has been sold, recall it to access color functions.

Step

Action

1

Move to the Line Item, which has been mistinted and press Color [F6].

2

Select Tag as Mistint.

3

Enter a Mistint Reason Code.

4

Enter the number of containers being tagged as a mistint.

5

If reselling the paint, affix the mistint label to the can.

6

Process a Store Expense to remove the mistint from your inventory.
