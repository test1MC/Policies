Mistints - After Sold Procedure

Step

Action

1

Process a Return Transaction to return the merchandise to inventory and refund the customer.

Note: Do not remove any money from the drawer if the customer wants a replacement for the mistinted paint.

2

Locate the original Customer Order and flag the paint as mistinted and choose a mistint reason.

3

Place the Mistint label over the product barcode on the container that was mistinted.

Note: The manager will be required to finalize a Store Expense transaction to expense the mistinted paint with a cost of $00.

4

Process a new Quick Order to tint the paint if the customer wants a replacement.

5

Process a Sale transaction to sell the replacement paint product. If the transaction amount is the same as the Return transaction, the form of payment should be cash in order to balance the drawer. Obtain the difference if the customer owes more than the Return Transaction.

6

Affix the Mistint label over the product barcode on the mistinted product label.
