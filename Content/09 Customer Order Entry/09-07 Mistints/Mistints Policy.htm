Mistints Policy

Mistint is an error that is made during the tinting of paint.

  1. Mistints should be store expensed as they occur to properly relieve inventory.
    1. All store expenses must be approved by the Store Manager.
  2. Mistints are charged to Cost of Goods when store expensed (under P&L line 36).
  3. Merchandise must be placed in a location reserved for Mistints. (Normally not on an End Deck.)
  4. A mistint label must be place on each label over the product barcode.
    1. The mistint label contains the original order number and one of the following mistint sales numbers.
  5. Mistinted product should be physically segregated and labeled in the store warehouse.
  6. Efforts should be made to sell the Mistint providing it is not hazardous. Mistints should be sold at these suggested prices unless otherwise instructed by the District Manager:

    Size

    Sales Number

    Price (Suggested)

    Quart

    9999-13197

    $1.00

    Gallon

    9999-13205

    $3.00

    5 Gallon

    9999-13205

    $3.00 per gallon plus container
    Note: These are the minimum recommended prices. Recovers as must cost as possible.
  7. The mistint label must also contains the following message: MISTINT / OFF SPEC / NO WARRANTY
    1. Manually write OFF SPEC/NO WARRANTY on the mistint label if the above message does not automatically appear.
  8. Mistinted merchandise should not be sold to discount re-sellers that intend to sell the mistinted merchandise at discount stores or flea markets.
  9. Donated mistints should be expensed to Controllable Contribution - Other Prime-Sub 7615001.
  10. A Materials Acceptance Agreement – Purchase must be completed for any sale containing 25 gallons or more of mistinted merchandise. For more information, refer to Material Acceptance Agreement Purchase.
