Store Expense for Mistints Procedure

Step

Action

1

Select Store Expense from the Special Menu.

2

Select Mistinted Paint Prod from the Prime-Sub List Menu.

Note: All tint orders that have been flagged as Mistinted will be available in the selection window.

3

Press Enter when prompted Press ENTER to Select or Unselect.

Note: An X displays in the right column. The X designates that the item will be part of the Store Expense Transaction.

4

Continue this process until all of the Mistinted Orders have an X in the right column. Press Accept [F1] to continue.

Note: Users will have the option to add additional store expense items.

5

Press Total when complete and Print a receipt if necessary.

Note: The store manager is required to review and approve all expense transactions on the monthly Stores Purchases Report.
