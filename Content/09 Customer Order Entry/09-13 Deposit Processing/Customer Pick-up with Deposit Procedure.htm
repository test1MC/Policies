Customer Pick-up with Deposit Procedure

Step

Action

Customer Pickup – Same Location as Order Placement

1

When the customer picks up the merchandise, enter the sale and do not forget to apply the deposit that was previously collected.

2

Pull the utility copy of the deposit form from the "Deposit Holding" folder.

3

Select Correction from the Special Menu. On the negative side of the transaction enter the total amount of the deposit.

4

Enter the original Terminal and Transaction Number, Original Date of the Deposit and all of the other required information.

5

On the Positive Side enter the merchandise being picked up.

Note: Remember to include any cutting charges, shipping charges, and any special items the customer purchased.

6

Select the Method of Payment from the Form of Payment Screen. The cash correct transaction relieves the deposit account and applies it to the sale.

7

Give the "Customer" a copy of the correction transaction and place the store copy in the forms container.

8

Staple the Utility copies of both invoices (Sales Transaction and the Deposit Transaction) to the copy of the Purchase Order and retain it for future reference.

Note: The stapled set must include: a copy of the vendor invoice, purchase order, deposit transaction, and sales transaction.

Customer Pickup –  Redeemed at a Different Location

1

Manager from store B (redeeming store) should contact store A (originating store) to make sure that the deposit has not already been redeemed.

2

If everything is in order, Store B manager should process a Correction Transaction to give the customer credit for the deposit amount.

Note: Employee should use ALL information from the original deposit transaction -term/tran, date, amount, etc.
