Deposit Processing Overview

Whenever required by policy, a deposit must be processed by adding a deposit line item to the order. Then, once the customer is ready to pick up their goods or their service has been completed, the customer order is pulled into a Correction Transaction. The deposit is applied to the negative side while remaining line items are pulled into the positive side—this will ensure the correct balance due.
