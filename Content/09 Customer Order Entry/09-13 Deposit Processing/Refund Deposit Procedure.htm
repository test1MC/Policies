Refund Deposit Procedure

There may be circumstances where it is necessary to refund the deposit to the customer. These transactions must be recorded on a customer deposit log.

Step Action

1

Select Return from the Sale/Payment Menu. Enter the required information, including: original terminal and transaction number, date, customer’s name, address, city, state, and phone number. 

2

Press ENTER when prompted to enter a Sales Number, and select Deposit to show the amount of the refund.

3

Enter an explanation for the refund by pressing COMMENT [F9].

4

Select the method of payment from the Form of Payment Menu.

5

Have customers sign the return transaction before giving them their copy.

Note: Deposits are fully refundable as long as merchandise has not been shipped. If the merchandise has been shipped, retain any handling charges that will be assessed by the vendor.

6
If Then

The vendor has not shipped the merchandise

Refund the total amount of the deposit to the customer.

The vendor has shipped the merchandise

Process a refund for the difference between the original deposit and the retainage amount

7

Process a Miscellaneous Receipt for any deposit retained using prime-sub 7599-099. Attach a note to a copy of the transaction stating that you want to have the transaction recoded to one of the following prime-subs:

Associated Products

0406-802

Spray Equipment

0406-803

Brushes and Rollers

0406-808

Wallcovering

0406-804

Floorcovering

0406-805

Window Treatment

0406-806

8

Mail the transaction copy and note to your Division Controller.

9

Add a record of the deposit to the Customer Deposit Log (available in the Forms Repository).
