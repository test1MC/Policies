Process Deposits Procedure

Step

Action

1

Process the Deposit through the Point of Sale. [From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale]

Note: For Large Spray Equipment deposits, enter the customer’s LSEP account number and Job 50.

2

Access the Non-Merchandise Menu by pressing Enter at the Sales Number field.

3

Select Deposit from the Non-Merchandise Codes.

4

Enter ‘1’ in the Quantity field and the Deposit Amount in the Price field.

5

Determine if the order type requires further action:

If

Then

Equipment Deposit

Ensure that the line item for the deposit does not exceed $49,999.99 and that each transaction does not exceed $99,999.99. These are the maximum allowed by the POS.

Proceed to Step 6.

Large Spray Equipment Deposit

Ensure that the line item for the deposit and the final total of the LSEP sales transaction do not exceed the maximum dollar amount allowed by POS ($49,999.99 or $99,999.99).

Proceed to Step 6.

Note: There should be a total of four deposit transactions for each spray equipment sale (30/30/30/10%).

Same Day New Product Accommodation

Include the following:

  • A Comment [F9] on the Deposit Line Item with part number and description of product(s) ordered
  • A second Deposit Line Item as a placeholder for the product
  • Special Instructions that read: Same Day New Product Accommodation

Proceed to Step 6.

Note: Do not sell the second line item.

All Other Order Types

Proceed to Step 6.

6

Use a Customer Deposit Log, located in the Forms Repository, to track each deposit received and processed at the POS for a customer.

7

Give the customer a copy of the invoice. Place the store copy in the forms container under the Point of Sale Terminal. The utility copy should be filed alphabetically in the Deposits Holding folder.

Note: For vendor direct shipments, attach a copy of the purchase order to the utility copy of the form. File both in the Direct Shipments folder.
