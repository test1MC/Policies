E-Commerce Customer Business Rules Overview

Completing successful E-Commerce transactions for our customers consists of:

  • Having accurate purchase orders with every sale
  • Following Business Rules established by the customer

The chart below outlines the types of transactions and how to handle them:

If

Then

Orders are placed and transmitted electronically.
  • View customer orders via Update/Review Orders.
  • All E-Commerce orders are assigned purchase order numbers and MUST be referenced EXACTLY as they appear on the purchase order for all corresponding invoices

Offline orders are permitted, called into your location, or a walk-in  placed an order.
  • These are considered as OFFLINE orders. 
  • You should process these orders the same as if the order was generated electronically.
  • A purchase order must accompany all invoices.
  • If there are questions about the purchase order, contact the property manager.

Customer wants to add additional items or change quantities to the order.
  • A new PO needs to be submitted.

An invoice was incorrectly submitted.
  • The store will be notified and process a return transaction against the incorrect invoice.
  • Process a new transaction with the correct information.
