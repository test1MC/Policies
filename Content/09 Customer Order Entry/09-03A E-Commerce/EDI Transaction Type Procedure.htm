Step

Action

Invoice Only

1

Customer is sent invoices electronically rather than by paper.

2

All customers are marked as Packing Slip only and will not receive a paper invoice at the POS when the sale is processed.

Note: The customer’s invoice (transaction) number is 14 digits compared to the typical 5 digit invoice (transaction) number. This is used to ensure that duplicate invoice numbers are not submitted into the customer’s accounts receivable system.

The 14 digit invoice number consists of:

  • 5 digit transaction number
  • Terminal number
  • Month and year

Example: 34905117030312

Purchase Order Only

1

Customer Purchase Orders are sent electronically to the POS rather than calling, walking, faxing or emailing the order.

2

Confirm that the PO# provided on the electronic order is entered in the PO field when the order is billed.

Note: It is important to type the number exactly as provided and not to add anything else to the field (i.e., customer name, facility, room, etc.)

3

Invoices continue to be sent as paper copies.

PO and Invoice

1

Customer Purchase Orders are sent electronically to the POS rather than calling, walking, faxing or emailing the order.

2

Confirm that the PO# provided on the electronic order is entered in the PO field when the order is billed.

Note: It is important to type the number exactly as provided and not to add anything else to the field (i.e., customer name, facility, room, etc.)

3

Customer is sent invoices electronically rather than by paper.

4

All customers are marked as Packing Slip only and will not receive a paper invoice at the POS when the sale is processed.

Note: This helps to avoid confusion with the customer and eliminates invoices being submitted twice.

Catalog Only

1

Customers place orders as usual by phone, walk-in, fax or email.

2

Customer uses an online catalog that is provided by Sherwin-Williams that is either loaded to the customer’s internal site or through Sherlink.

3

If a PO# is provided for the order, enter the number in the PO field when the order is billed.

Note: It is important to type the number exactly as provided and not to add anything else to the field (i.e., customer name, facility, room, etc.)

4

Invoices continue to be sent as paper copies.
