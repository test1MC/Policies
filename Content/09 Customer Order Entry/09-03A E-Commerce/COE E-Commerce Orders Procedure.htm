COE E-Commerce Orders Procedure

Line item quantities for each order must be committed or placed on-order as soon as possible after receiving the order on the store computer system. Follow these steps when you receive an E-Commerce order at your store.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Enter Employee Number and Password -> Update/ Review.

2

Answer Yes to the question Review New E-Commerce Orders Now?

3

Select the desired sorting option and press Enter.

4

Press Enter to select the appropriate order.

Note: You will be asked to update the Header information. Select Yes or No and press Enter (Automotive E-Commerce orders only)

5

Press the UP ARROW key to move to the quantity field for an item.

Note: Blank lines with comments listed below must have the appropriate sales number identified before inventory can be checked.

6

If you currently have [Not Committed] stock, press Accept [F1] to commit inventory for this line and then View [F7] to move to the Inventory menu.

7

Enter either Commit or Order and the Available Date. Press Accept [F1].

Note: You will see a reminder message return to the screen if you do not currently have enough inventory at your location. If you will not receive your next STAR order before the required date, the information will print on a reminder report titled [ITEMS TO BE ORDERED] when you exit Order Entry (the order process for out of stock merchandise will be completely automated in one of the future phases of Customer Order Entry). The system will automatically place DSC merchandise on your next Suggested STAR Order if the required date is after your next STAR order delivery date.

8

Upon exiting the order, product availability information will be returned to the E-Commerce System to notify the customer.

9

The Customer Part Number and Purchase Order Line Numbers can be entered in Order Entry for EDI customers that require mandatory or optional input for the Customer Part Number and Purchase Order Line Number fields. The Part and Line Numbers will be automatically brought into the point of sale transaction.

Note: The following information applies to E-Commerce orders for Automotive branches and Floorcovering E-Commerce orders:

  • Employees may delete and add line items as you need. When finished with all the changes on the order press Accept [F1].
  • Select YES to view special instructions. Scroll through the special instructions using the arrow keys. Press Accept [F1] to finish reviewing the special instructions. Add the request items found in special instructions to the order.
