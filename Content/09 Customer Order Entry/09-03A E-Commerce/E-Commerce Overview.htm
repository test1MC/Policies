E-Commerce Overview

E-Commerce refers to business transactions completed via the internet. E-Commerce orders can either be submitted directly by a customer using the MySW website or by electronically transmitting them to Sherwin-Williams. Examples include:

  • Electronic Data Interchange (EDI)
  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)
  • Electronic Order Submission (EOS)
  • Procurement to Pay (P2P) Cycle

If you have any questions concerning EDI please call 216-515-8998 (Mon-Fri 8:00-5:00 EST) or email your questions to EDI Questions. Contact Store Computer Operations at 216-566-2740 with any questions concerning the Customer Order or EDI applications.

Refer to this document when a customer requests to conduct business electronically with your store. Once communication is established between the store and the customer, you will send and receive transactions electronically via your store computer.
