Process E-Commerce Orders Procedure

When processing E-Commerce orders, it is important to bear in mind the following:

  • Do not cancel orders and re-enter them
  • Do not change the PO field
  • Do not change the account number
  • Do not add line items
  • Delete orders or lines that cannot be filled

Note: Vendors may refuse to pay canceled/altered orders. Similarly, Open Orders must be processed immediately and they are displayed in the Status Reminder Box on your computer screen.

5

For each line item, press COMMENTS [F9]  to review customer notes. Take note of requested product, color, composite, sheen, and size!

Note: If requirements noted in comments do not match Sales Number, do not delete line item. Correct Sales Number through tinting process.

6

If required,  tint line item.  Press COLOR [F6].

Resolution

Competitor Color Type

Note: For Punchout Orders, only Sherwin-Williams Color or Custom Color may be selected.

  • Contact customer to verify requirements.

Product and Color Base do not Match

Use Sales Number LOOKUP [F3]  to select correct base matching customer requirements.

7

Commit inventory:

Step

Action

1

Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press VIEW [F7] to access the Inventory Menu.

Note: If your store does not have enough product on hand, the system will generate a Memo Demand and make the order quantity available on your STAR Order.

2

Enter the Quantity in the Commit Field.

3

Enter the Date the customer needs the order.  Press ACCEPT [F1].

4

Press TOTAL [F12] to proceed with the order.

8

Review and take action on Special Instructions.

9

If required, process order for delivery.

Note: E-Commerce orders cannot be processed as sales transactions until each order line has a sales number and all zero quantities have been changed.
