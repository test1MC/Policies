New EDI Customer Procedure

Step

Action

Implementation

1

National Account Executives (NAE) provide the SW Implementation Group with a list of properties or facilities.

2

Attain property/facility listing from the actual customer, rather than relying on 3rd party to provide this information.

3

Once received, the property listing goes through a process called geocoding.

4

To ensure orders are filled accurately and timely, accounts are mapped to Sherlink and property account numbers are connected to the correct SW store.

5

Account attributes, such as billing and invoice credits, are added to Sherlink.

Catalog Development

1

NAE provide the EDI Catalog group with parent code or account numbers for the customer.

2

Catalog group generates a sales history report and forwards information back to NAE for any product adds.

3

NAE must verifty that PRC is up-to-date for product selection.

4

For Floorcovering Catalogs, the E-Commerce Catalog team will work directly with the store based on the local PRC.

5

Any future product changes, along with PRC modifications, need to be sent to edi.stores@sherwin.com for update by the catalog group.
