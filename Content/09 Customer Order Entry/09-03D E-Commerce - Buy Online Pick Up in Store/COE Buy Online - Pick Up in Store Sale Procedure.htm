COE Buy Online - Pick Up in Store Sale Procedure

Buy Online Pick Up in Store orders are processed much like any customer order through Customer Order Entry. If you have any questions about the customer’s order, contact the customer quickly- you will find their name and contact information on the order header screen. As you process these orders, be aware of these key differences.

Order Required Date

Resolution

Same Day
  • Contact the customer to confirm timeframe that will allow you to transfer the stock needed from another location.
  • Commit only the amount available in the store which you plan on using for the order.  Once you have received the ISR, commit the full required amount on the order.
  • Proceed to Step 4. 

Before next STAR Order – Not same day
  • Contact the customer to set expectations for when stock will be available.
  • Place a request to transfer the stock needed from another location.
  • Commit only the amount available in the store which you plan on using for the order.  Once you have received the ISR, commit the full required amount on the order.
  • Proceed to Step 4. 

After next STAR Order.
  • Contact the customer to set expectations for when stock will be available.
  • Commit the entire amount required by the customer.  A Memo Demand will be created and included on your next STAR Order.
  • Proceed to Step 4.

Customer wants to cancel a line item (tinted)

Has the item been tinted yet?

If Then
Yes The item cannot be cancelled and the customer must be charged

Customer wants to cancel a line item (non-tinted)
  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press DELETE to remove the line from the order.  
  2. When prompted, type Y and press ENTER to confirm the deletion.

Customer wants to add a line item

A second sale will need to be made for any additions.

Customer wants to decrease quantity
  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and change the quantity.
  2. Press ENTER to confirm the decrease.

Customer wants to increase quantity

A second sale will need to be made for any additions.

Product and Color Base do not Match

Use Sales Number LOOKUP [F3] to select correct base matching customer requirements.

Note:  Do not substitute different sizes than ordered when correcting base requirements – price variances between sizes may result in the card authorization for the order to fail.

Customer wants to substitute product

Tinted Product

A second sale will need to be made.  If the originally ordered product has already been tinted, the customer will be required to purchase both the originally ordered tinted product and the new product.

Non-Tinted Product

A second sale will need to be made for substitute product.  Remove the product from the original order:

  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press DELETE to remove the line from the order.  
  2. When prompted, type Y and press ENTER to confirm the deletion.

Price Change

A second sale will need to be made for price changes.  Remove the product from the original order:

  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press DELETE to remove the line from the order.  
  2. When prompted, type Y and press ENTER to confirm the deletion.

Competitor or Custom Color cannot be Matched

Contact the customer. Remove the product from the order:

  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press DELETE to remove the line from the order.  
  2. When prompted, type Y and press ENTER to confirm the deletion.

Product Messaging

Customer Restricted from Product Purchase

Remove product from the order:

  1. When prompted to identify Order Lines to be Sold, select Selected Lines.  Select only lines to be sold.
  2. After the sale has been processed, access the order in COE and cancel the line items not sold.

Conditions / Documentation Required for Product Purchase

Verify that the customer is eligible to make the purchase and retain any required documentation.  If the customer is not eligible, remove the product from the order:

  1. When prompted to identify Order Lines to be Sold, select Selected Lines.  Select only lines to be sold.
  2. After the sale has been processed, access the order in COE and cancel the line items not sold.
