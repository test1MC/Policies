POS Buy Online - Pick Up in Store Sale Procedure

Customers enter their payment information online - you just need to complete the transaction through POS:

Step

Action

1

POS will populate the Job and PRC selected by the customer (noted by an asterisk). Confirm or change.

2

You will be prompted to ask the customer for their State ID and their Order Confirmation. 

  • Validate that the name on the State ID matches the name of the orderer or their designees within the POS Order.
  • Cross-check the Order Confirmation against your COE Order information and return the Order Confirmation to the customer.

Note:  If the person picking up the order is not the orderer or named in the prompt, contact the Order Placer to confirm that the individual is authorized to pick up the order.

3

Ask the customer if they have any coupons or an employee discount to be applied.

4

Line item level Issues & Resolutions:

Issue

Resolution

Customer wants to cancel a line item or the order

Has the item been tinted?

If Then
Yes The item cannot be canceled and the customer must be charged
No
  • When prompted to identify Order Lines to be Sold, select Selected Lines.  Select only those lines to be sold
  • After the sale has been processed, access the order in COE and cancel the line items which were not sold

or

  • Select Order Entry -> Cancel Order and confirm cancellation of the order

Customer wants to add a line item

A second sale will need to be made for any additions.

Customer wants to decrease quantity
  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and change the quantity.
  2. Press ENTER to confirm the decrease.

Customer wants to increase quantity

A second sale will need to be made for any additions.

Customer wants to substitute product

Tinted Product

A second sale will need to be made.  The customer will be required to purchase both the originally ordered tinted product and the new product.

Non-Tinted Product

A second sale will need to be made for substitute product.  Remove the product from the original order:

  1. Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press DELETE to remove the line from the order.  
  2. When prompted, type Y and press ENTER to confirm the deletion.

Price Change

A second sale will need to be made for price changes.  Remove the product from the original order:

  1. When prompted to identify Order Lines to be Sold, select Selected Lines.  Select only those lines to be sold.
  2. After the sale has been processed, access the order in COE and cancel the line items which were not sold.

Product Messaging

Conditions/Documentation Required for Product Purchase

Verify that the customer is eligible to make the purchase and retain any required documentation. If the customer is not eligible, remove the product from the order:

  1. When prompted to identify Order Lines to be Sold, select Selected Lines. Select only lines to be sold.
  2. After the sale has been processed, access the order in COE and cancel the line items not sold.

5

Scan or enter any coupons the customer presents.

6

Are you providing bags to your customer?

If Then
Yes You are located in a jurisdiction requiring the collection of a fee on the bag provided, add this fee to the sale
No Proceed to Step 6

7

The payment will process, including taxes, discounts, and fees applied. 

Note: If the payment is declined, select a new form of payment and process that form of payment.

Note: Buy Online Pick Up in Store returns will be handled the same as other returns. For more information, please see Return Transaction Procedure.
