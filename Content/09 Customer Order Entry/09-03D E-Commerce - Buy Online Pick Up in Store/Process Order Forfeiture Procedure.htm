Process Order Forfeiture Procedure

Occasionally, customers may not pick up the orders which they have placed. Customers will receive several reminders by email to pick up their order- your store will be copied on the final email to the customer notifying them of the order forfeiture.

Step

Action

Follow-up with Customer

1

Review the COE Header for the customer’s contact information.  Contact the customer one final time to try and make arrangements for the order to be picked up.  If the customer declines to pick up the order, cannot be reached, or fails to fulfill arrangements, proceed with these following steps to complete the order forfeiture.

Process Non-Tinted Line Items

1

Access the order in Customer Order Entry.

2

Cancel these line items from the order:

Step

Action

1

Use the UP Arrow Key to move to the Quantity Field and press DELETE to remove the line from the order. 

2

When prompted, type Y and press ENTER to confirm the deletion.

3

Exit the customer order.

4

Return these items from the will call area to stock.

Process Tinted Line Items

1

Pull the order into a Sales Transaction.

2

Process sale.

3

Generate mistint labels for the product(s).

4

Place these product(s) in the mistint area with a copy of the sale.

5

Mail or e-mail a copy of the invoice to the customer’s billing address.
