Buy Online - Pick Up in Store Overview

Buy Online - Pick Up in Store allows our customers with online profiles linked to their in-store account to place orders online at Sherwin-Williams.com and pick up prepared orders in the store. Not all customers are able to place their orders online- these customers will be prompted to call their home store for more information. 

Be aware of the situations that may prevent your customers from purchasing online and what steps may be taken to remedy the situation:

Issue

Resolution

Locked out accounts

Customer should be directed to contact the Credit Office.

Bad debt

These customers may place orders online but will not be able to use their SW charge as a form of payment. They must pay using a credit card.

Location

Only customers in the continental US will be able to place orders online.

No SW.com profile

Customer should create a profile at sherwin.com and link this profile to their in-store account.

Customers placing orders online have access to the Sherwin-Williams’ catalog of products as well as the vendor products we stock; products requiring special order, some regional products and local buys may not be available. To help make selection of the right products easier:

  • The online site takes into consideration all regulatory restrictions which may affect what products are available for purchase in the customer’s area. However, it will not prevent customers who may be restricted from ordering products for other reasons from placing the order. POS will either prevent you from selling or provide notification of purchasing requirements which should be validated before the sale is completed.
  • Customers are provided information to help them select a convenient store location with stock availability. Customers will not see your available inventory but will be provided with information on what may be available:

    • Inventory Status

    What this means

    In Stock

    Store is well-stocked.

    Limited Quantity

    Store has 3 days of stock available.

    Available by [DATE]

    Store does not have stock today but will have stock on [DATE].

    Not Available

    This product is not available at this store. This status could display for items which are out of stock, being phased out, or are regulated.
  • If an order needs to be fulfilled by multiple Sherwin-Williams locations, Buy Online - Pick Up in Store allows the customer to split the order between stores – you only see the order information for your location.

Customers provide payment information online and stores use this information to process the sale in the store.  Customers are limited to SW Charge and Credit Cards as forms of payment and multiple forms of payment are not permitted.

Orders placed online will take about thirty (30) minutes to arrive in the POS.  However, if a customer calls in with a question on an order they have submitted to your store, you can check your email to reference a copy of the order confirmation immediately.
