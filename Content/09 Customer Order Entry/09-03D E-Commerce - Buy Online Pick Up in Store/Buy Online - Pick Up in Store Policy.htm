Buy Online - Pick Up in Store Policy

  1. Product for orders must be committed in Customer Order Entry within two (2) hours of receipt of the order within POS.
  2. Orders must be ready for pickup:
    1. Within one hour of POS order receipt for same-day orders of ten (10) gallons or less.
    2. Night before customer specified pick-up date.
  3. Orders may only be released to the individual or designees thereof who placed the online order. The following must be verified:
    1. Customer’s State ID.
    2. Order Confirmation.
  4. Customers must be charged and cannot cancel order lines for tinted paint which have already been tinted.
  5. Orders which are not picked up by the customer within fourteen (14) days  of being assigned the status, ready to pick up, should  be disposed:
    1. Non-tinted line items may be returned to stock.  No restocking fees are to be assessed.
    2. Tinted items must be charged to the customer and handled as mistints.
