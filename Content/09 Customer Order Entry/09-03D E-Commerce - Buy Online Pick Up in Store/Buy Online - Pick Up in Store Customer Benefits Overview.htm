Buy Online - Pick Up in Store Customer Benefits Overview

Buy Online - Pick Up in Store builds on Sherwin-Williams’ foundation of trademark service, quality, and expertise.  E-Commerce doesn’t mean only online. Sherwin-Williams.com lets consumers buy products online and pick them up at the stores of their choosing when they can fit it into their schedules. Benefits of the online experience include:

  • Customers save time by planning ahead- taking advantage of seamless ordering online and pickup at Sherwin-Williams stores.
  • Consistent and transparent communication through order status emails ensures that online orders and reorders are prepared with the same best-in-class accuracy and customer service customers expect from in-store transactions.
  • Customers may pick up their order whenever and wherever they would like. They designate the preferred Sherwin-Williams location and request a time to pick up their items.
  • Access to prior order history online for a quick and easy reorder process.
