Transfer Store Maintenance Procedure

Before the Transfer Order function may be used, Transfer Store Maintenance must be completed. This one-time setup will define the stores having orders transferred to them.  Include only the stores the will fulfill your transferred orders. 

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Maintenance -> Transfer Store Maintenance.

2

Use the Store Lookup browse to search for and add the stores to which you will transfer orders:

  • Sherwin-Williams Commercial Wallcovering Center – (Duron Beltsville Wallcovering - #3802)
  • Duron Service Centers (Duron Stores ONLY)
  • Duron SDC Atlanta - Store #3901 (Duron Stores ONLY)
  • Duron SDC Beltsville - Store #3900 (Duron Stores ONLY)
  • Sherwin-Williams Service Centers (SW Paint Stores ONLY)
  • Spray Repair Centers
