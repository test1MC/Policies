ISR Transfer Receipt Policy

Once an order has been transferred back to your location, the "Transferred In" number in the Black/Yellow Status Window will increase.  When this happens, you may receive the ISR Purchase Order created when you transferred your customer order.

  1. You must review the IST document carefully to note any changes made to the products and quantities originally requested.  Receive according to the IST document.
  2. You must bill according to the changed order as this reflects what the customer received. If the Service Center/Tint Facility/Store made changes to the order based on product(s) quantity(ies) that may not have been able to fulfill or new requests the customer made at the time of pick-up, do not alter these changes.
