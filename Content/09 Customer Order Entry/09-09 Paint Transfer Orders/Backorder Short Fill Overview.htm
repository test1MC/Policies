Backorder/Short Fill Overview

If

Then

Line on an order cannot be fulfilled completely and must be backordered

The Service Center/Tint Facility will delete the line from the order in Customer Order Entry before transferring the order back to the originating store.

Line on an order can only be partially filled

The Service Center/Tint Facility will change the Order Quantity on the Customer Order before transferring the order back to the originating store.

Note: In either case, the Service Center/Tint Facility will include a note in the Special Instructions area noting the product number/quantity, which cannot be fulfilled so that the originating store knows to re-order later or find the product at another location.
