IST Transfer Fulfill Policy

As the shipping store in the ISR/IST process, once an order has been transferred to your location, the "Transferred In" number in the Black/Yellow Status Window will increase. When this happens, you may begin the IST process and fulfill the request.

  1. Review/update transferred ISRs in the Order Entry Lookup window. Transferred ISRs are noted with a blue box on the bottom right of the window.  
  2. If the ISR is to be delivered to a store or customer and you are utilizing the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service, ensure the order is transferred to the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service delivery routing software.
  3. Once you have fulfilled the requested order, you now process the IST through the IST process in Point-of-Sale.
  4. The fulfilling store in the ISR/IST process is responsible for completing the Bill of Lading process.
