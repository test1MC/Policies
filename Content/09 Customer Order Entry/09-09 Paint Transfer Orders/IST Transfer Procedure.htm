IST Transfer Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down key -> Select IST from the Special Menu.

2

Select Yes and press Enter to the prompt "Select a Transfer Order?"

3

Select the order from the All Active Transfer Order(s) menu.

Note: The Receiving Store and Inter-Store Request Number will automatically populate, Press Accept [F1].

4

Select Y to begin the process the Bill-of-Lading paperwork.

Note: If you have completed the Bill-of-Lading process and your location uses the Sherwin-Williams Delivery Service, send the order to the Delivery Service's delivery software.
