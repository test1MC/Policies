Cancel Paint Transfer Order Procedure

If an entire order cannot be fulfilled, the Service Center/Tint Facility will cancel the Customer Order and call your location to notify you of the cancellation. The Customer Order will be automatically canceled on your system, but you will need to cancel the ISR Purchase Order which was created when you transferred the order to the servicing location.

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Cancel Unnecessary Purchase Order.

2

Select Inter Store Request (ISR) from the Order Type List.

3

Select the correct order and answer Yes when prompted to confirm the cancellation.

4

Press Accept [F1] and locate the products at another facility.

Note: If another facility cannot complete the order, notify the customer of the cancellation.
