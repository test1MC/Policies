Receive ISR Transfer Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Receive / Verify -> Order.

2

Select the correct ISR.

3

Press the Up Arrow to move to the RCV-Q area. Enter the quantity received, press Enter and Accept [F1] to continue.

Note: Notice the Order and Ship quantity display on the screen and ensure that the C[complete] column has a Y. If something was not fulfilled or backordered, receive the quantity as zero and mark it as complete. Or, if the line was partially received, only enter the quantity you actually received.

4

Press Enter to verify the Actual Receipt Date and to bypass the reminder messages.

5

Answer Yes to the question "Accept all in-process receipts now?"

 

Answer Yes to the question "Close this purchase order now?" Press Enter after reading the reminder messages.

Note: There is no need to print a receipt or packing slip.

6

Process a point-of-sale transaction, pulling the Customer Order into the sale. 

Note: The Service Center/Tint Facility may have made changes to your Customer Order based on product(s) quantity(ies) that may not have been able to fulfill or requests the customer made at the time of pick-up.  Do not alter these changes.  Bill according to the changed order.

Note:  If you bill the customer without pulling the Customer Order into the sale, you will need to link the sale and the Customer Order through Order Disposition.
