SDC Transfer Order Return Policy

If a customer needs to return product that you fulfilled by transferring an order to the SDC in Beltsville or Atlanta, the SDC will need to coordinate the return with Cleveland Customer Service.  First, process the Return Transaction to credit the Customer account in SOURCE.  

  1. Returns made to the SDC for customer returned transfer orders must conform to Emergency Return Guidelines:
    1. Greater than 128 gallons of a product
    2. Greater than 80 gallons in five's of a product
    3. Product with very short shelf life that falls below the previously mentioned quantity guidelines
    4. High value products that fall below the previously mentioned quantity guidelines
