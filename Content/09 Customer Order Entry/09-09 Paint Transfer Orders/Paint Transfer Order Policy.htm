Paint Transfer Order Policy

The Transfer Order process is used to transfer a customer paint order to a Service Center or Tint Facility, spray repair orders to Spray Shops, and Inner-Store Requests/Transfers or Wallcovering Orders to the appropriate Wallcovering Fulfillment Center.

  1. If the customer requires that an order be delivered complete, but the transfer location cannot do so, you must include a Special Instruction on the order. It is the originating store's responsibility to handle the backorder process with the customer.
  2. Transfer Orders are not to be used to replenish store stock. Store stock replenishment must be completed via an Inter Store Transfer (IST) request between stores or a STAR Order against the warehouse. A transfer order should meet one or more of the following requirements:
    1. Customer Order cannot be fulfilled by originating store location.
    2. Customer Order for Delivery.
    3. Stock replenishment needed to fulfill a customer order by originating store location.
  3. The Service Center/Tint Facility may fulfill transfer orders for VOC requested products when:
    1. The order has been submitted via a Transfer Order (request cannot be made via ISR).
    2. A Customer Account number is used [generic 1-DIY cannot be used].
    3. The store has provided the required documents to the Service Center/Tint Facility. For more information, Duron stores should see 07-03H Duron Non OTC Compliant Order.
