Return Transfer to SDC Procedure

Step

Action

1

Call the SDC to inform them of the return:

  •   Beltsville [1-800-723-8766 X3216] / Atlanta [1-800-476-2367 X4166].

Note: The SDC will need the following information:

  • Customer Order Number
  • Sales Numbers
  • Quantities being returned

2

The SDC will obtain authorization for the return to the warehouse and call the store back with further instructions:

If

Then

Return is not authorized

You must keep the product.

Return is authorized

You will receive two phone calls:

  • The SDC will call you with an ISR number.
  • The SDC will also fax the store a copy of the ISR document.

Cleveland Customer Service will call you to arrange for pick-up of the product.

3

Once the store has received the faxed copy of the ISR from the SDC, an Inter Store Transfer [IST] must be created to move the inventory to the SDC.  Verify all products and quantities on the faxed document, as some product may not have been authorized for the requested return.  Only create the IST for product and quantity listed on the ISR.
