Adjust Tinted Color Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Users are able to use the Color Eye to adjust a previously tinted product to bring the originally tinted product up to the new color. This will work for any previously tinted product, even mistints.

Step

Action

1

Select Quick Order and press Recall [F10] to locate the original order.

2

Press Lookup [F3] to select orders that have been processed on point-of-sale transactions.

Note: Select from the first screen if the order has not been processed on a point-of-sale transaction.

3

Press Color [F6] when the cursor is at the quantity field.

4

Select Correct -> Color Eyes Addition.

5

Place the Target Color or Standard(the new color match) in the Color Eye holder and press Enter.

6

Place a dried sample of the original sample in the holder. Press Enter.

7

Select the correct light source and press either Accept [F1] or Proceed [F12] to complete the process.

Light Source Type

Description

Incandescent

Normal lighting in most homes [normally interior products]

Daylight

Paint will be used either outside or in an area with many windows [normally exterior products]

Fluorescent

Normal lightning in most commercial and industrial buildings [normally interior products]

Note: After completing this process you need to move to the Tint Workstation to dispense the additional tint. After dispensing the additional tint, an updated label that combines the original and additional formulas automatically prints. This new label will make it much easier to reproduce another container of the same color in the future.
