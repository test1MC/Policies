Handling Abandoned Product

Your actions will be determined by how long the paint has been in your store:

Time Period

Action

72 Hours - 2 Weeks

2 Weeks

Step

Action

1

Notify customer that their paint has been tagged as a mistint for sale.  Assure them that they may still pickup their paint without charge; but, warn them that it is now available for others to purchase.

2
  • Generate mistint label(s) for the product and move product to the mistint area. 
  • Maintain the pink copy of the Color Maintenance Request Form as an open item.  Retain with copies of documented contact with customer.

3

If sold, retain the pink copy with the record of that sale in daily batching.

Inventory
  • When appropriate, mark product as obsolete during inventory.  Retain pink copy of the Color Maintenance Request Form with inventory documents.
  • If required, destroy product.  Retain pink copy of the Color Maintenance Request Form with documents pertaining to destruction.

Mistint Sale Date – 18 Months

If the product has been mistinted and sold, and, the customer requests replacement, prepare order using Customer Order Entry and expense order to 7599-104 Destroyed Merchandise.
