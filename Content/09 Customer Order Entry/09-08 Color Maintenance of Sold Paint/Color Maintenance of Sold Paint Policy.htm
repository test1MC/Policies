Color Maintenance of Sold Paint Policy

  1. Customer requests to leave already purchased product in store must be recorded using the Color Maintenance Request Form.
  2. The Color Maintenance Request Form must be retained in the Batch box with other transaction forms. For more information, seeFiling & Retention Batch Box Overview.
  3. Paint left for color maintenance must be warehoused in the will call area along with a copy of the Color Maintenance Request Form and should be kept separate from orders prepared for will call or delivery.
  4. Paint left for color maintenance must be returned to the customer within 72 hours.
  5. Efforts to return abandoned product to a customer must be documented with date, person making contact, person contacted (if applicable), and notes concerning when the customer will return for their product.
  6. If product has been sold, customer may request replacement of listed products and quantities upon presentation of customer copy of Color Maintenance Request Form. 
  7. Replacements may be requested within 18 months of the initial color maintenance request.
  8. Returns and refunds may not be processed for abandoned product.
  9. Abandoned product must be identified as a mistint after two weeks and should be handled as any other mistint in terms of sale, obsolescence, and destruction thereafter.
