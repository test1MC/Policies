Sales Representative Samples Policy

Occasionally, Sales Representatives will need sample merchandise from the store to use as selling aids while making sales calls.

  1. The Store Manager is responsible for accounting for all sample merchandise taken from the store inventory.
  2. All samples must be expensed through the Point of Sale prior to leaving the store.
  3. The Store Manager must approve all expense transactions.
