Test Drive Discount Procedure

Step Action
Verify Purchase History

1

Press Inquiry [F4].

2

Select Sales History Menu.

3

Select by Terr/by Cust/by Product.

4

Enter password.

5

Enter the appropriate parameters.

Note: Only proceed if the customer’s previous sales for the particular product total no more than ten gallons in the past twelve months.
Process Order

1

Main Menu -> Customer Order Entry -> Add Order.

2

Enter Customer Account or press Lookup [F3].

3

Enter the order header details and press Accept [F1].

4

Enter products.

Note: Products must not have been previously purchased by the customer and quantities must be within the approved limits.

5

Process Test Drive Discount and press Accept [F1].

If Then

In EAD, MWD, SED, SED

 

Once all line items have been entered on the order, scan the Test Drive Coupon. If unable to scan, Press Discount [F11] and enter the Test Drive Discount Barcode: 5-35777-19257-8

In Canada

Scan the serialized barcode.

6

Select Y to include Special Instructions. Input “Test Drive Order” and press Accept [F1].

7

From Print/View Options, select Print Customer Order.

Note: The customer must  sign a copy that will be retained on file in a folder entitled “Test Drive Program MM/YYYY.”
