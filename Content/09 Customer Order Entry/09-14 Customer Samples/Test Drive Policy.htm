Test Drive Policy

  1. Test Drive is for wholesale customers that have a valid Sherwin-Williams cash or charge account number.  Test Drive is not to be used for walk-in customers.
  2. Test Drive is not to be used for donations, mistints, complaint settlement, obsolete, or damaged products.
  3. Test Drive orders must be processed at the account's home store or the home store(s) of the sales rep(s) servicing the account.
    1. In order for non-home stores to run test drive transactions for a customer account, they must have written DM approval on file.
  4. Test Drive orders must be processed through Customer Order Entry.
  5. The Test Drive Customer Order must be signed by the customer, retained, and filed appropriately.
  6. Test Drive products on a Customer Order Entry order must remain on a separate order from products that are not for Test Drive. This means that two separate picking tickets may be needed.
  7. The customer must have purchased no more than ten gallons of the product within the last twelve months.
  8. The following products are eligible for Test Drive Program discounts:  GOM product included in departments 11 Architectural, 18 Industrial Maintenance, 21 Chemical Coatings, 80 Brushes and Rollers, 50 Associated Products, including Caulk.
  9. Order quantities must remain within the following limits per type:
    1. Architectural Products:  20 Gallons
    2. Industrial Products:  10 Gallons
    3. Chemical Coatings Products:  5 Gallons
    4. Brushes and Rollers: 2 Units (for example: one roller and one cover)
    5. Requests for additional product beyond quantity limits established in policy (for job completion) must be approved by the District Manager.  Approval must remain on file with customer paperwork for audit review.
  10. These same limits also apply to total annual test drive purchases per product family (Cashmere, Duration, Emerald).
