Transferred Order Procedure

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Update / Review.

2

When asked to review Quick Orders, select No.

Note:  The Order Selection Browser window opens. Press Accept [F1] at this window so you may view all orders in the system.

3

Select the correct order and use Accept [F1] to navigate through the order.

Note: Pay attention to when the order is required. If the order is not to be fulfilled for a few days, you may wish to delay filling the order to concentrate on more urgent orders and save space.

4

If there are any Special Orders products or Local Buy Products, you will not be able to fulfill this line item. Delete the line from the Customer Order and let the store know they must request this product using the SOS Order Form.

Note: Orders for VOC/non-OTC compliant product may be transferred to your location. When this occurs, you may be required to provide Cleveland Customer Service with documentation of the customer exception form. This should be available from the originating store who can fax it. If the documentation is not on file at the originating store, the order should be canceled and resubmitted once the correct paperwork has been obtained. In the event of a customer walk-in, contact the home store if the customer has a form on file. If the customer does not, refer to Chapter 07-03H Duron NonOTC Compliant Order.

5

Once you get to the end of the order, choose the option to Print one copy of the Picking Ticket.

Note: Do not confirm the order as Ready for Delivery until all the products have been picked and any backorders noted.
