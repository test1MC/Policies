Transfer IST Order Back to Store Procedure

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

Once the order has been delivered or picked up by the customer, you must transfer the order back to the originating store so that the customer may be billed.  Be sure that you have made all adjustments to the Customer Order before you IST the order back.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Main Menu -> Point-of-Sale.

2

Press Page Down at the Sale/Payment Menu to access the Special Menu and select IST.

3

Select Yes when prompted to select a Transfer Order.

Note: The Order Selection Browser window opens. Press Accept [F1].

4

Select the correct order and press Accept [F1] throughout the entire order to transfer the order back to the originating store. Make no changes to the IST information.

5

Choose to print a receipt when prompted. Staple this receipt to the packet of paperwork located in the bin of orders waiting to be IST'd.

6

Staple the Confirmation of Delivery or Picking Ticket the customer has signed to the packet. File the entire packet with completed orders.
