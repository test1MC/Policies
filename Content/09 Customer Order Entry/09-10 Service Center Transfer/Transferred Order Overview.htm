Transferred Order Overview

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

You are able to monitor the transferred orders, which are coming into your system through the Black/Yellow Status box. Whenever an order is transferred to the store, the Transferred In quantity will increase. When the order is transferred out, this number will decrease. The Transferred Out number will increase when you initiate the order transfer from one of your customer orders. View the transfer status of an order in the Transfer Status report available in Customer Order Entry.
