Transferred Order Return Policy

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

Emergency returns could be required for major store ordering errors or warehouse picking errors. Cleveland Customer Service handles emergency returns on a case-by-case basis.  Emergency returns provides a method to return excess product due to order, picking, and shipping errors. 

  1. Since the SDC holds no inventory, the guidelines for emergency returns will be relaxed for the SDC only. This does not include, however, DSC returns for orders which have already been transferred back to the originating store location.  These returns will be governed by the Emergency Return Guidelines:
    1. Greater than 128 gallons of a product
    2. Greater than 80 gallons in five's of a product
    3. Product with very short shelf life that falls below the previously
    4. High value products that fall below the previously mentioned quantity
  2. If unauthorized product is returned, the store will receive no credit and will be assessed freight charges, restocking, and $3 per gallon handling charge.
  3. If a customer needs to return product that was fulfilled by transferring an order to the SDC in Beltsville or Atlanta, the SDC will need to coordinate the return with Cleveland Customer Service and complete the IBAR in SOURCE. 
  4. The SDC will not physically handle any returned product. Pick-up and handling of returned product will be managed by the warehouse through Cleveland Customer Service.
