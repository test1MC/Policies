SDC Hotseat Request Procedure

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

If a customer comes into the SDC for material and no order exists, please follow the below manual downtime process:

Step

Action

1

Write the customer requirements on a Manual B Prefix Purchase Order Form. 

2

Enter the B Purchase Order into the system and call Duron Customer Service [1-866-DURON98] to place the manual Purchase Order.

3

Provide a printed copy of the entered Purchase Order to a pick dedicated to the SDC process and sign the Purchase Order as your BOL/Packing List.

4

Make a copy of this paperwork for the SDC to retain (Beltsville SDC ONLY).

Note: If the Hotseat Purchase Order requires sundries, contact Scott Burke or Curtis Wise to arrange for sundry pick.

5

The pick must take the signed Purchase Order to the Shipping Office and notify them that order has been picked so that the SDC does not receive a double shipment of the product.

6

Immediately, call the customer's home store and let them know that they must enter and transfer a Customer Order to the SDC within the next hour. You will need to give them all information including the customer, products, and any color information.
