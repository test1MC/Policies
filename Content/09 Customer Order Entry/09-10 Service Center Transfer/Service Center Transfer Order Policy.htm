Service Center Transfer Order Policy

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

The Transfer Order process is used to transfer a customer paint order to a Service Center or Tint Facility, spray repair orders to Spray Shops, or Wallcovering orders to the appropriate Wallcovering fulfillment center.

  1. Always review Special Instructions to note whether or not the customer order must be fulfilled complete.
    1. If a store location mistakenly transfers an order to you, re-transfer the order back to that location via the IST process.
    2. If you are unable to fulfill an order line, must change an order line quantity, or the customer requests additional product, must modify the customer order to reflect these changes and them in Special Instructions.
  2. Transfer Orders are not to be used to replenish store stock. Store stock replenishment must be completed via an IST request between stores or a STAR Order against the warehouse.
  3. A transfer order should meet one or more of the following requirements:
    1. Customer Order cannot be fulfilled by originating store location.
    2. Customer Order for Delivery.
  4. The Service Center/Tint Facility may fulfill transfer orders for VOC requested products when:
    1. The order has been submitted via a Transfer Order because the request cannot be made via ISR.
    2. A Customer Account number is used (generic 1-DIY cannot be used).
    3. The store has provided the required documents to the Service Center/Tint Facility.  Duron stores, see 07-03H Duron NonOTC Compliant Order for more information.
