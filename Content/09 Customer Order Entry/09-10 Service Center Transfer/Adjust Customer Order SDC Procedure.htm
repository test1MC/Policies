Adjust Customer Order SDC Procedure

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

Once the order has been picked at the Service Center/Tint Facility, prepare the Customer Documents for delivery and adjust the Customer Order for any items which cannot be fulfilled at this time.

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Main Menu Customer Order Entry -> Update/Review.

2

Delete any lines, which cannot be completely fulfilled or change the order quantity to match what was picked from the order using the Backorder process.

3

When prompted, select All lines as Ready for Delivery. The Confirmation of Delivery papers will automatically print once confirmed that the order is Ready for Delivery. Place the Delivery papers in the appropriate bin for Delivery/Pickup.

Note: If the Order is not marked for delivery by the originating store, you will not be able to print a Confirmation of Delivery. Print a copy of the Picking Ticket from Customer Order Entry once you have made any required adjustments. Use this to obtain the customer signature.

4

Obtain the customer signature on either the Confirmation of Delivery document or the Picking Ticket.
