SDC Return Transferred Order Procedure

This does not apply to Automotive, General Polymers, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores (only select SW Paint Stores have access to the Transfer Order process).

Step

Action

1

The originating store will call the SDC to inform them of the transferred order return and provide the SDC with the following information:

  • Customer Order Number
  • Sales Numbers
  • Quantities 

2

Using the Customer Order Number, identify the Purchase Order on which the products were ordered from the warehouse.

3

Call Cleveland Customer Service [1-866-DURON98] to request authorization and an E-trac number and provide the following information:

  • Purchase Order Number
  • Bill-of-Lading Number
  • Invoice Number
  • Sales Numbers
  • Quantities

Note: If the return is not authorized, call the originating store and inform them that they must retain the product in their inventory.

4

Create an ISR Purchase Order to request the returned goods from the originating store.  Include only those products and quantities which Customer Service has authorized for return.

5

Fax a copy of the ISR Purchase Order to the originating store and  inform them that the return has been authorized. They must create an IST based on the faxed ISR. Cleveland Customer Service will call to arrange pick-up.

6

Receive against the ISR Purchase Order you have just created.  Receive for the full amount of the product and quantities on the Purchase Order.

7

Process a Reason Code 2 IBAR.  Product number/sales number, quantity, RGA# [E-trac Number], carrier name, description of the return, ship date, freight terms, destination, BOL, and Invoice Number must be entered on the IBAR.
