Custom Formula Maintenance Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Custom Formula Maintenance enables users to save and delete Custom Colors to/from the Custom Palette, which consists of all formulas previously saved through Tint-A-Color Manual Entry and all Custom Colors specifically “Saved” in the new Sher-Color system. All orders and color information are available in the new Sher-Color system, but when you specifically “Save Color” from the Custom Color application you are also adding it to your Custom Palette.

Step

Action

1

Select Custom Formula Maintenance and narrow down the formulas by entering the following information:

  • Account Name
  • Color Number
  • Color Name
  • Notes

Note: If no Selection Criteria is entered, all Custom Palette formulas will return.

2

Select the appropriate line.

3

Press Enter to change the Saved to Custom Palette indicator from Yes to No or No to Yes.

Note: Items that are “Saved” will be readily available from the Recall Custom Palette.

Note: If you want to add custom formulas to your Custom Palette, locate the order that contains the color through Recall [F10] and choose the Save Color option from the Sher-Color screen.
