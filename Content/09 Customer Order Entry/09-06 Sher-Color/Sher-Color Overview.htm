Sher-Color Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Sher-Color provides a more accurate dispense of color across the entire breadth of our product line and a method to retain customer-specific tint information. It also provides employees with the ability to recreate the color on the customer's next visit accurately. Access Sher-Color on the POS terminal.

Benefits

Features

Accurately tint colors regardless of base product.

Easy access to TINTED PAINT RECALL from INQUIRY that provides customer color and quantity for recently tinted orders.

Sher-Color and point of sale transactions linked together.

Attach notes to tinted product for quick access to customer color information that will not print on the customer's receipt.

Automatically print COLOR NUMBER, NAME and FORMULA on the customer's invoice.

Enter COMMENTS while tinting the paint or while processing the point of sale transaction.

Quickly review pending color orders.

Enter customer Full Name or Phone Number when a cash or charge account is not used so the transaction may be found at a later date.

Look up customer color history by transaction, account number, name, and phone number.

The color number, name and formula of each tinted line will appear on the customer's receipt, laser printed invoice, and centralized invoice. For more information see CONFIGURE FORMULA PRINTING PROCEDURE.

Import mistinted paint information into a store expense transaction.

Any Sher-Color formula updates will be automatically downloaded to your store and require no additional work.

If you have the Tinter or Color Eye attached to the workstation, you can perform the color function. If you do not have a device attached, you can create tint orders and hold for later dispense and you have complete LOOKUP capabilities.
