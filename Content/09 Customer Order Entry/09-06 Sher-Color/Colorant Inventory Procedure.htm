Colorant Inventory Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

This process incorporates barcode scanning with colorants, just like finished goods.  This allows for reductions in the perpetual colorant inventory, min/max levels to be incorporated, and colorants to be automatically added to STAR orders. Upon scanning a bottle of colorant, it automatically creates an inventory adjustment report using employee “0.”

Determine whether or not there are any new colorant bottles to scan:

If

Then

N is selected

There are no further steps in the process and the system will update the colorant(s) fill level to 100% full or increase by 1 quart, depending on which option was selected.

Note: If N was accidentally hit, go back in to scan barcodes of newly opened colorant bottles.

Y is selected

The system will proceed to the next screen to allow for the scanning of the barcodes of the newly opened colorant bottles. Proceed to Step 4.

4

Scan the barcode or enter the colorant sales number(s) of the newly opened bottles.

Note: Each time a barcode is scanned or a sales number is entered, that colorant will appear in the gray box titled New Colorants Scanned. When scanning is complete press the TAB key.

5

Modify the list, such as delete a line, or press Accept [F1] to complete the process.

Note: By pressing Accept [F1], the perpetual inventory will be reduced for each scanned line displayed and the system will update the colorant(s) fill level to 100% full or increase by 1 quart, depending on which option was selected.

Note: The barcode scanning process can be used for colorants in manual dispenses.
