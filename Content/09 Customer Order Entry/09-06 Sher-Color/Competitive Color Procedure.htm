Competitive Color Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Competitive Color should be used if you want a gallon of paint based on one of our competitor's colors.

Step

Action

1

Press Lookup [F3] to view the list of competitors when prompted to enter the Company Name.

2

Enter any characters contained in the Company Name, Color Number, or Color Name.

Note: You must enter the beginning letters of the color i.d. in the Color# field for a successful search.

3

Review the list and select a color.

Press Accept  [F1] or Clear [F8] to start a new search.

4

Enter the Sales Number, Quality, Composite, Finish, Rex, Size or press Lookup [F3] to view all bases.

5

Enter the Sales Number for the product and light source.

Note: Once the light source is selected, you will be prompted through the tinting process. For more information see ADJUST TINTED COLOR PROCEDURE.
