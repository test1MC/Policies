Sher-Color Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

You have several options to begin the tinting process using Sher-Color on POS:

  • Process an order and press Color [F6] from the tint workstation and dispense immediately.
  • Start a Quick Order from any POS terminal and hold for dispense.
  • Start a customer order in Customer Order Entry from any terminal and hold for dispense.
  • To access Sher-Color from the tint workstation, press the QUICK ORDER button at the bottom of the POS Desktop.

Step

Action

1

Enter the Account Number and Press Accept [F1].

Note: If the customer does not have a charge or cash account, simply enter the customer's name when prompted Enter Bill To Name or Press RECALL key to browse pending orders.

2

Press Color [F6] when prompted to Enter Sales/ Product Number or:

  • Scan the product barcode
  • Manually enter the sales number
  • Manually enter the product and side code
  • Press Lookup [F3] to search for the product

3

Press Color [F6] to begin the tinting process.

Note: Quick orders can be easily saved and exited by pressing the Suspend [F10] key any time you are prompted to enter a sales number.

4

Make a selection from the Color Types Menu.

Note: If you have the Tinter or Color Eye attached to the workstation you can perform the color function. If you do not have a device attached, you can create tint order to hold for later dispense, and you have complete Lookup capabilities.
