Create Color Eye Match Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Custom Color enables users to tint a gallon of manually formulated paint or custom matched paint. If the employee simply wants to customize the color by adding colorant, they should make this selection. Stores with Color Eyes will choose Custom Color to create a Color Eye Custom Match.

Step

Action

1

Select Custom Color -> New Custom Match.

Note: Users may also select New Manual Entry.

2

Place the Sample in Holder when prompted, and ensure it is at least the size of a dime and has consistent color throughout the target. Press Enter when ready.

Note: To verify the target is positioned correctly you can press the black button on the top of the Greta Macbeth ColorEye XTS unit and the face will open up. From here, you can see the target to be measured within the cross hairs of the inside panel.

3

Enter a Sales Number, Product Number (i.e. REX), scan the UPC of the Product, or Lookup [F3] for a Color Product Lookup.

4

Enter the Quality, Composite, Finish, Rex, Size to narrow the search or press Accept [F1] to see all products in the recommended base.

5

Use Lookup [F3] to select the necessary product.

6

Choose the primary light source.

Note: Formulation will occur and then you will be prompted to choose from the Options Menu.
