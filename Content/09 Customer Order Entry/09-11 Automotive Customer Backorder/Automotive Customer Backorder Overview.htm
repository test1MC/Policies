Automotive Customer Backorder Overview

All Branches will use one consistent method to track and fill back-orders for our Customers, and maximize sales. Benefits of the Backorder process include:

  • Allows partial shipments for an order which might have products on backorder.
  • Allows the user to bill the amount of material which was shipped.
  • Allows the user to bill customer order entry lines without having to make the entire order "ready-for-delivery."
  • Enables a Bill of Lading to be created directly from the delivery screen without having to go into the Bill of Lading menu to produce the document.
  • The Confirmation of Delivery Ticket will now show the “order” quantity and the “ship” quantity. This will let the Customer know that the order was received correctly.
  • When the backorder is received by the Branch, the original order details including Purchase Order number (if any), original quantity, amount previously shipped, and original pricing will all be contained in a Customer Order Entry in POS.
  • Provides uniform process for tracking and fulfillment of back-orders.
  • Provides reporting tool – Daily Customer Service Report - to measure fill rate by dollars and units.

Note: For more information, please visit: POS desktop -> Browser -> SOURCE homepage -> Information Library -> Release Notes -> SOURCE Update 8701.
