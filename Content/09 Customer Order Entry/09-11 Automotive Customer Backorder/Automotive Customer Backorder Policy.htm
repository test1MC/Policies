Automotive Customer Backorder Policy

  1. All Branches are required to enter and show all back-orders for all Customers, including Cash Sale Accounts. Use of the Backorder Process is now mandatory.
  2. All backorders are to be filled unless written permission is provided by the Area Sales Manager for a particular Customer.
  3. The Branch fill rate will be tracked and the initial goal for the dollar fill rate is 95.0%.
  4. A BOLDOC is required for all orders delivered via common carrier or Branch truck. This includes AP or non-paint orders, and is the law for all U.S. states and Canadian Provinces. All companies that transport goods for sale are required to provide upon demand documentation reflecting exactly what kind of goods are being carried.
