Update Confirmation of Delivery Price Printing Procedure

To ensure all backorders for cash sale accounts are tracked, it is recommended to change the Confirmation Of Delivery so it prompts you each time if pricing should be printed on the ticket.

Step

Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Enter Employee Number and Password.

2

Select Maintenance -> Order Entry Controls -> Print Price -> Always Prompt If Pricing Should Be Printed On Ticket.
