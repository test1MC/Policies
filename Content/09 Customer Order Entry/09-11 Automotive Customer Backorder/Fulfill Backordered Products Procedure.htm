Fulfill Backordered Products Procedure

Step Action

1

When the backordered product is received, an automatic report called “Customer Order Entry Items Received” will print. This is the notification that the back-order can be filled.

2

Once the backordered products have arrived. Select Update/Review Orders. Search for the backordered customer order.

3

Select Y to the prompt “Are lines on this Order ready for Delivery?” Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

4

Select the Store Truck as the carrier from the SCAC Code List.

5

Select the Delivery Date.  Calendar navigation instructions are at the bottom of the screen.

6

Confirm the Ship Quantity of the backordered products.

7

Select Y to submit a Bill of Lading Request to BOLDOC. Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

Note: This will communicate your transaction information to HEARS. If you see an error message during any of the transaction submissions, please note the on-screen message and call the helpdesk.

8

Select Y to open HEARS to complete the a Bill-of-Lading.

9

If necessary, print the Confirmation of Delivery tickets.

10

Process the Sales Transaction, select option #3 Shipped Lines Not Billed.
