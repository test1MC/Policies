Kronos Inbox Overview

Kronos has its own internal communication system through Inbox.

All employees have access to Inbox and the tasks and messages received will be personal to each employee. Most of these messages will be auto-created by Kronos based on actions you and your employees take. As a manager, you will see information on actions you need to take due to employee requests or exceptions which require resolution. Employees will receive messages when you approve or refuse a request.

Most information available through Inbox is also communicated to you through your alerts and exceptions. As a manager, know that comments made by employees while punching out will only be visible through Inbox.

Inbox is accessible to you from the widgets on the left-most panel of your landing page in Kronos.
