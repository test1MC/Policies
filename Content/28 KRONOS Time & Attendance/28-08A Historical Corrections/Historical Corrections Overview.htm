Historical Corrections Overview

Historical corrections are used to make changes to timecards in a signed-off pay period. Any timecard activity allowable in an open pay period can also be changed through a historical correction. Only district staff can perform historical corrections. If you have a need for a correction, contact your District Coordinator. Note there may be a delay in when your coordinator processes your historical correction request. There is a short period of time after Timecard SignOff in which previous pay period timecards cannot be edited (this is called the black-out period). The black-out period is controlled by Payroll. Payroll will release the timecards for editing when they are finished processing payroll. Once a correction is made, it will display in the timecard and in the audit trail.

Note: Historical corrections will appear in the Accruals and Totals Tab for the current pay period timecard. For example, say you add eight hours of vacation time in a previous signed-off period. In the current timecard, the Accruals and Totals Tab will reflect the reduction in the vacation accrual balance.
