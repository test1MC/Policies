Manual Attendance & Time Policy

  1. When the system is not working, it is store management’s responsibility to see that each employee completes and signs the Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet.
    1. The Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet must reflect the exact number of hours worked for each nonexempt employee each day, and must show the unworked benefit hours for which the employee is to be paid i.e., vacation, holiday, disability.
  2. The Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet must be retained for 3 years plus the current year.
  3. As soon as the system is operating properly, a member of store management must edit the Kronos timecard to account for all employee activities recorded on the Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet.
  4. If the system is not operating properly by the Monday of pay period approval (timecard signoff), store management should fax the Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet(s) to their district coordinator for manual entry into Kronos to ensure employees are paid.
