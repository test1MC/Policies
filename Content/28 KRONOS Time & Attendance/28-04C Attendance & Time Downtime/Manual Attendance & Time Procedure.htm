Manual Attendance & Time Procedure

Step Action
Record Attendance Manually

1

Access your printed Manual Attendance and Time Worksheets. These should be stores in a locked filing cabinet in a Downtime Readiness Folder.

Note:  Find the Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “manual” or “attendance” in the Form Name Field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

2

Complete a form for each employee (all fields are required).

3

Employee and manager signatures are required on the form.

4

Forms must be retained per retention policy. See Filing & Retention Policy for details.
Enter Manually Recorded Time into Kronos

1

Refer to the procedure to Add Pay Code to Timecard found in Timecard Edits Overview.

Note: Enter the data recorded on the Manual Attendance and Time Worksheet into the Kronos timecard for each employee.
