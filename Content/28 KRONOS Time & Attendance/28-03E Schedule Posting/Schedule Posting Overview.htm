Schedule Posting Overview

Online and physical schedules must be posted two weeks before the pay period to be worked. This timeframe allows employees ample time with their schedule so that fewer changes are needed, and, when changes are needed, provides managers with more time to react and re-work the schedule.

With online posting, employees can check their schedule online via mySherwin and do not need to come to the store or call in for their schedule. For employees who may work at multiple locations, they are able to see their complete schedule in one view through their personal calendar. If changes are required to a posted schedule, managers should make these changes in Kronos and re-post the schedule both electronically and physically. 

  • This will ensure that employees have full access to changed schedules online and in the store, avoiding any scheduling mistakes. 
  • The Kronos reporting tool uses data from your completed schedule to report on your target to scheduled to worked hours. If you make changes to the schedule and do not re-post, these reports will not be accurate.
