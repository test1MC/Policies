Post Schedule Procedure

Step Action
Post Electronic Schedule

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

From the Manager Landing Page, select the Schedule Planner widget.

3

Use the Select Dates Button to navigate to the proper pay period you wish to post:  Sunday to Saturday Start to End Date for the pay period you are posting.

4

Select Tools -> Manage Schedule Posts.

5

Select Post.

6

Confirm time period and posting as the Sunday to Saturday Start to End Date for the pay period you are posting.  Select Yes.

7

Select Done.

Note: Once the electronic schedule is posted, employees can view their assigned shifts for the corresponding work period in My Calendar
Post Physical Schedule

Note: The electronic schedule must be posted before a physical copy of the schedule is printed.
1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

Restore full view of Kronos by selecting the minimize button ()

3

Select Reports (widget panel) -> Scheduler -> Employee Schedule - Weekly.

4

Toggle People field to All Home and Transferred-in.

5

Toggle Time Period to Range of Dates and specify upcoming work schedule dates.

6

Select Run Report.

Note: You may have to Refresh Status for the report Status to update from Waiting to Complete.

7

Highlight the appropriate schedule and select View Report.

8

Print PDF and post.

Note: If an already posted schedule needs to be edited, edit the schedule and repeat the steps above to re-post (both the electronic and physical) schedule. For more information, see Kronos Schedule Edits Overview.
