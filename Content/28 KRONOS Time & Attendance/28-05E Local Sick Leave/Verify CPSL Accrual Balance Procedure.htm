Verify CPSL Accrual Balance Procedure

Employees and managers may view available CPSL for any employee by viewing the employee’s accruals in Kronos. For more information, see View Accruals in Accruals Overview.

Employees may also monitor their accrued CPSL on their paystub available through Payslip on mySherwin.com from a personal device. Please note that the Payslip application available via SOURCE will not display accrual information.
