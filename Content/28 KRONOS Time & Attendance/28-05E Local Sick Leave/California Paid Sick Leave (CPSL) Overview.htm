California Paid Sick Leave (CPSL) Overview

Effective 1 July 2015, California employees accrue paid sick leave (CPSL). CPSL may be used for yourself or a family member for preventive care or care of an existing health condition or for specified purposes if you are a victim of domestic violence, sexual assault or stalking. Examples of CPSL usage:

  • Preventive care such as annual physicals or flu shots. 
  • When an employee is physically or mentally unable to perform his/her duties due to illness, injury, pregnancy, or medical condition.
  • To obtain a professional diagnosis or treatment of his/her medical condition or undergo a physical examination.

To aid or care for a family member (state of California) or designated person (City of Oakland & San Francisco only) who is ill, injured, or receiving medical care, treatment or diagnosis. For more information on CPSL, please contact your Area HR Manager.
