California Paid Sick Leave (CPSL) Policy

  1. CPSL is accrued:

    2. Location

    Paid Sick Leave Earned…

    Per Hours Worked

    California*

    1 Hour

    30 Hours

    City of Oakland

    1 Hour

    30 Hours

    San Francisco

    1 Hour

    30 Hours
  2. Employees may take accrued sick leave in minimal increments of one(1) hour with additional time allowable in increments of fifteen (15) minutes.
  3. New employees are eligible to use CPSL accruals after:

    4. Location

    Requirement

    California*

    Ninety (90) days of employment with Sherwin-Williams and thirty (30) days of employment within California in a calendar year.

    City of Oakland

    Ninety (90) days of employment with Sherwin-Williams. Employees that perform at least two (2) hours of work within Oakland in a week are eligible.

    San Francisco

    Ninety (90) days of employment with Sherwin-Williams. Employees that perform work in San Francisco on an occasional basis are eligible if they perform fifty-six(56) or more hours of work within in a calendar year in San Francisco.
  4. Employees may use up to three (3) days of twenty-four (24) hours of accrued sick leave per year.
  5. Accrued sick leave carries over from year to year subject to maximim carryover per jurisdiction:

    6. Location

    Maximum Carryover

    California*

    48 Hours

    City of Oakland

    72 Hours

    San Francisco

    72 Hours
  6. Accrued sick leave is not paid out at the time of separation.
  7. CPSL balances will be immediately restored and available for use upon rehire for terminated employees rehired within one (1) year of termination.
  8. Employees may use accrued CPSL for their own medical care or to care for a family member. Family members include the employee’s parent, child, spouse, registered domestic partner, grandparent, grandchild, and sibling.
    1. Children include biological, adoptive, foster, and stepchildren, as well as, legal wards or children for whom an employee stands in loco parentis.
  9. City of Oakland & San Francisco Only:  If an employee has no spouse or registered domestic partner, he/she may designate one individual that they will aid or for whom they will care. This designee will be identified at the time of hire and reviewed and updated during the annual benefits enrollment.
  10. Employees must request to use CPSL time and should do so as soon as practicable for any unforeseeable absence from work for which CPSL will be used.
  11. Managers must verify accrued CPSL time before approving an employee request and coding time in Kronos.
  12. CPSL time may not be entered in excess of accrued time.

*Accruals information for California is exclusive of the City of Oakland and San Francisco.
