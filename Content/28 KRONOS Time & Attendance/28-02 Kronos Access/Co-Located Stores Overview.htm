Co-Located Stores Overview

Managers who are responsible for managing more than one store location, must contact the SCO Helpdesk (216-566-2740 / scohelpdesk@sherwin.com) and request an HRDC Case be opened with Kronos Development to have Kronos manually assign the manager to both locations.
