Accruals Report Procedure

Managers may run reports for accruals to determine time used and time remaining in a year for all employees:

Step Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

Select Reports from the Widget Panel (right side of screen).

3

Under the accrual tree, select Accrual Detail.

4

Select the time period. Open the drop down and select the date range. To see current:

  • Do not change the beginning date.
  • Enter 12/30 as the end date.

Note: You want to exclude 12/31, as a grant is applied that day.
