Accruals Overview

Accruals are available for vacation, personal days, and local sick time through Kronos. Accruals are displayed in total number of hours. If a full time employee needs to figure out how many days of vacation or personal holiday they have taken or have left, divide by eight (8). Kronos will display the current state of accruals for an employee for the current calendar year using the following information:

Field

Definition

Yearly Grant

Amount in hours at the beginning of the calendar year

Already Taken Time Off

Amount in hours that have been taken and paid year-to-date

Scheduled Time Off

Amount in hours that has been requested and approved but not yet taken

Remaining Balance

Amount in hours that has not yet been taken or scheduled

Contact your District Coordinator for vacation and personal holiday entitlement if zero hours are displayed.

Note: Employees may also monitor their accruals on their paystub available through Payslip on mySherwin.com from a personal device. Please note that the Payslip application available via SOURCE will not display accrual information.

WARNING: If an employee has unapproved requests pending, Kronos will allow them to submit requests over allowed accruals—requests made do not “count” for accruals until approved by management.
