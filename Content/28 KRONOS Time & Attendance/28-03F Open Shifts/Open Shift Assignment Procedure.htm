Open Shift Assignment Procedure

Managers may assign open shifts to employees:

Step

Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

From the Manager Landing Page, select the Schedule Planner widget.

3

Launch a full-screen view of the Schedule Planner by selecting the maximize button ().

4

Select Staffing Assistant Tab (bottom left).

5

Specify Date/Shift.

6

Select Load. 

7

System will return employees listed in order of best fit. Assign shift to the best employee choice.
