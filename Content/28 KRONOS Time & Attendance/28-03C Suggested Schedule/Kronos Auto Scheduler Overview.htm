Kronos Auto Scheduler Overview

The Kronos auto scheduler runs every Wednesday at 12:00AM and generates a schedule two weeks out. Factors influencing the suggested schedule include:

Factor

Influencers

Store Hours

Store open/close times are fed to Kronos based on what is maintained in Source.

Kronos is aware of extended hours for national events.

Kronos is aware of truncated hours for certain holidays (ex. Christmas Eve).

Kronos is aware of target hours and will not create a schedule that goes above these hours.

Employee Information

Kronos knows employees’ job titles and status and the hours associated to each role. Kronos will not schedule an employee above their base hours (ex. Store Manager = 48 HRS, Part-Time = 28HRS).

Kronos will only schedule keyholders to open or close the store.

For employees with availability, Kronos will only schedule employees during times in which they are available.

For employees with patterns, Kronos will schedule the assigned pattern.

Kronos will honor an approved time-off requests and not schedule employees during their requests time-off.

Labor Demand

Kronos forecasts time and resources needed to accommodate sales and tinting. Kronos will schedule to these forecasted hours; the forecasted hours will be less than your target hours.

  • Kronos has historical tinting data by day/hour.
  • The sales forecast is informed by historical volume of sales by day/hour to both DIY and wholesale customers. 
  • Kronos is also aware of national store events occurring during the schedule and anticipates extra volume for these events. 

Kronos knows when a STAR Order should arrive and will schedule a resource to be on-hand for receipt.
