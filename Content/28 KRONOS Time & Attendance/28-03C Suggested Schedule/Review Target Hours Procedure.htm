Review Target Hours Procedure

You should be aware of a trick to view target hours. The all hours column shows total hours for the pay periods and Sunday shows total hours for one week.

Step Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

From the Manager Landing Page, select the Schedule Planner widget.

3

Launch a full-screen view of the Schedule Planner by selecting the maximize button ().

4

Select Metrics Tab.

5

Select Target Hours.

6

Select Load.

7

Select Summary Drop Down.
