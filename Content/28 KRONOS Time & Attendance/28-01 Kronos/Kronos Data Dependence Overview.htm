Kronos Data Dependence Overview

Kronos relies on accurate data. If the data in Kronos is not right, your schedules will not be as efficient as they could be, and you may not even be able to manage all of your employees. Where you have control to enter and maintain data, review and update it often.

  • Keep your store hours up-to-date in Source. 
  • If promoting, hiring, terminating an employee, then process the GEMS transaction as soon as possible. Keep data you enter in Kronos fresh and validate employee information often.

If you find an issue with a missing employee or an employee with the wrong job title and access in Kronos, then contact your district coordinator so they can troubleshoot any incorrect information in GEMS and help resolve your issue quickly.
