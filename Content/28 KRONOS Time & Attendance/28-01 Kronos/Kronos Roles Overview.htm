Kronos Roles Overview

Who

Does
  • Store Managers
  • Assistant Managers
  • Operations Managers
  • All management employees will have the same level of access and responsibility in Kronos.
  • All management employees will be able to balance daily reviews and exception processing. 
  • Store managers still own ultimate responsibility of ensuring correct and accurate timekeeping for their store.
  • District Coordinator
  • District Manager
  • City Manager
  • If a store happens to be without a management employee or management is on vacation when a time-sensitive function such as schedule posting or pay period approval needs to be performed, district management (DMs, City Managers, District Coordinators) will be able to access Kronos and complete these functions for their stores.
