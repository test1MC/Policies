Kronos Overview

Kronos is a web-based timekeeping and scheduling application. There are key features to Kronos:

Function

Features

In-app request and exception handling

All manager and employee functions can be performed online within the app; this includes all time-keeping and all scheduling functions. If your employee needs to request a day off, they do this in the app, and a manager can approve their request in the app. If you need to add in a lunch break and shift ending time for someone who worked a sales call yesterday, do this in the app.  If you need to post the next pay period’s schedule, it is in the app.

Accessibility & Online Schedule Posting

Some parts of Kronos are available to you via mySherwin while at home. No employees are able to log attendance outside of the store. But, managers are able to edit and post schedules from home and employees are able to see their schedules online by accessing Kronos from mySherwin.

Integration with Attendance & Time Functions

One of the most powerful aspects of Kronos is the scheduling function which is integrated with the attendance and time functions. A scheduled vacation automatically feeds into timekeeping and does not need to be recorded a second time for payroll purposes. 

Auto-generated schedules driven by store data

 

The scheduler application takes into consideration factors surrounding each employee and the needs of your store throughout the week to form the basis of an intelligent schedule that addresses anticipated coverage needs. This schedule is based on employee info, planned store events, historical sales/tinting data. The suggested schedule is meant for efficient allocation of resources when your store needs it most and to help avoid overtime and cut staffing costs.

Real-Time Data

Manage over-time and employee requests/needs in app. Real-time accruals.

Transparency

Approvals, Alerts, Exceptions delivered in single-source dashboard to store and district management. Easy dashboard for store management and district management will allow for easy review of action items needing attention.

Borrowing

All employees in a district are able to work in any other store in the district with no additional setup in Kronos.
