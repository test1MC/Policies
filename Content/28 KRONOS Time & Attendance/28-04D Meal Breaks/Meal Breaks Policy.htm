Meal Breaks Policy

  1. A meal break is a period of at least 30 consecutive minutes during which the employee is relieved of all duty and free to leave the work premises.  Any break less than 30 consecutive minutes or during which the employee is not relieved of all duty and free to leave the work premises shall be considered on duty time for which the employee is entitled to pay for hours worked.
  2. Meal breaks that meet the definition above are unpaid. An employee who is taking a meal break must punch out using the meal break code. At the conclusion of the meal break, he/she must punch back in as returned to work.
  3. Any employee who falsifies meal break records or who encourages another employee to falsify meal break records is subject to immediate dismissal.
