Kronos My Calendar Overview

A useful view for all employees is the My Calendar view. In this view, employees have access to:

  • View just their schedule for any pay period or date range selected
  • View accruals for vacation, personal holidays, and local sick time
  • Make and manage requests for time-off
  • View and request to take open shifts
  • Request to swap shifts with another employee

None of what an employee can do in this view is official or on the schedule until a manager approves their request.
