Kronos Widgets Overview

The widget panel is on the right side of the screen:

Widget

Description

Genies

(Managers Only)

View absences, missed punches, and unscheduled hours worked by employee. Select the employee name to take action.

Schedule Planner

(Managers Only)

View/edit generated schedule.

My Inbox / Inbox

Select Inbox -> Messages to view notifications, such as approved time-off, submitted request, etc.

Exceptions

(Managers Only)

View exceptions, including unexcused absences, overtime, and missed punches.

My Timecard / Timecards

View timecards.

Note: Managers can toggle employees to edit timecards.

Timecard Sign-off

(Managers Only)

Review outstanding requests and system alerts to approve and sign-off on timecard.

Reports / My Reports

View system generated reports.

Out of Office

(Managers Only)

Launch online out-of-office calendar.

My Audits

(Employees Only)

View how and when punches were logged on your timecard (i.e. manager or payroll edits, etc.)

My Calendar

Request time off, shift swaps, and open shifts. View accruals and schedule.

Requests

(Managers Only)

View time-off requests.

Store Hours Review

Adjust scheduling hours for store specific events such as sidewalk sales or seasonal changes (national events and holidays are pre-loaded).
