Kronos Manager Landing Page Overview

There are two key areas which should be reviewed daily by management:

Area

Description

Alerts  / Exceptions

Here, you can see tickers for alerts and exceptions. These are to be reviewed and resolved daily by someone in management. You will find time-off requests and missed punch issues in this area.

Manage my Department

The main panel shows a highlight of key timekeeping information by employee for the current pay period: whether or not an employee has absences, missed punches, has worked unscheduled hours, or (if a new employee) you can see if the Gems record is complete (this is important because employees without a complete record can punch in and out but will not be paid until their record is complete).
