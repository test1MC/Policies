Kronos Timecards Overview

Area

Description

1

Specify employee (manager only).

2

Specify pay period.

3

Displays timecard punches.

4

Displays scheduled hours.

5

Select the menu bar to open the Totals and Accrual menu.

Managers can edit their own timecards and their employees’ timecards (including time in/out, meal breaks, and job transfers) by selecting the appropriate cell. Exceptions are noted by red symbols next to the appropriate date (for absences) or in the appropriate punch cell (for missed punches). Only managers can edit timecards (select the appropriate cell) to address exceptions.

Note: For time entries, be sure to add P or PM for punches 12:00 PM or after—the system defaults to AM.
