Holidays Overview

The standard holidays are automatically loaded into Kronos for consideration in scheduling and inclusion in payroll. Employees will automatically receive holiday hours for these standard holidays. The divisions will notify you of holidays and any shortened operational hours before the start of the next year. The need may arise to meet special state holidays. When your District Manager informs you of a designated local personal, each employee will automatically receive the appropriate number of holiday hours. In some locations (excluding Canada), personal holidays may be designated to match local holidays. In these instances, your District Manager will inform you of this decision.

For details on holidays and truncated store hours, see Store Hours Overview.
