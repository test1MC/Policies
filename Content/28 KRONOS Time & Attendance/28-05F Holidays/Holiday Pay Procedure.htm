Holiday Pay Procedure

Holiday pay is handled automatically in Kronos for all scheduled holidays. Holiday pay will loaded into Kronos at the end of the pay period after your perform timecard signoff. Managers will need to manually code floating holidays.

Holiday Type Details
Non-Worked Holiday (Christmas)

For Christmas, when your store is closed, there is no need to make any entries on anyone’s timecard. Kronos will automatically ensure anyone entitled to holiday pay receives it.
Worked Holiday (Labor Day)

For holidays, like Labor Day, where you are open, only the employees working should log their hours and they should only log worked hours. Holiday pay for all employees will be handled automatically.
Floating Holiday

Floating holidays must be scheduled or added to the timecard by a management employee using the FLOATER-CTO.To schedule a floating holiday:

Step Action

1

Contact your district coordinator to request that they add an entry to the timecard for the employee for a holiday paycode in negative time equal to the time alloted to the holiday time calculated by Kronos.

Note: This is required to cancel out the actual holiday as holidays cannot be removed from the timecard.

2

Either a district management or store management employee should schedule or edit the timecard for the floating holiday. Time assigned for the holiday should equal the holiday time calculated by Kronos.

For more information timecard edits, see Add Pay Code to Timecard in Add Pay Code to Timecard Procedure.
