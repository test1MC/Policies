Holiday Pay Policy

  1. All regular full-time, regular part-time store employees are eligible for holiday pay. Temporary employees are not eligible for holiday pay.
  2. TAG US Part-time employees must average 20 or more hours per week in the four weeks prior to the holiday week to qualify for holiday pay. Part-time employees will receive 4 hours of holiday pay.
  3. A part-time employee will receive straight time for hours worked on a designated holiday, providing his/her total hours for the week do not exceed 40 hours. Any hours over 40 for the week will be paid at time and one half.
  4. A non-exempt full-time employee who is required to work a designated holiday cannot be forced to take an alternate day off.
    1. If the employee requests an alternate day off, the Manager may grant such a request depending on business needs.
  5. Automotive and Product Finishes Only: An exempt employee, who is required to work a designated holiday, will be given an alternate day (floating holiday) off during the same biweekly pay period in which the holiday falls. You must contact your district coordinator per the Floating Holiday Procedure to facilitate a floating holiday.
  6. No TAG employee should be using a floating holiday unless instructed to do so by HR.
