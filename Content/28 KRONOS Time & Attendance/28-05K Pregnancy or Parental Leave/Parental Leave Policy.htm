Parental Leave Policy

It is recognized that in the event of a birth or newly adopted child into the family, a parent may need or desire time off work to become acquainted with the child and to adapt to the new situation. The company will comply with all federal and state laws regarding parental leaves of absence.

  1. The company will consider approving an unpaid leave of absence for up to twelve (12) weeks, or the amount of time required by law whichever is greater, for a regular full-time or part-time employee who becomes a parent of a new born or a newly adopted child.
  2. A request for a Parental Leave of 29 calendar days or less may be granted with the approval of the District Manager and the Division VP of Human Resources.
  3. A request for a Parental Leave in excess of 29 calendar days requires the approval of the District Manager, Division VP of Human Resources, and the Corporate Employee Benefits Department.
  4. Requests for a Parental Leave of Absence should be made in writing and signed by the requesting employee.
  5. An employee who is granted a Parental Leave of Absence will return to the same job he/she held prior to the leave, unless the position has been eliminated during the employee's absence. If the position was eliminated, the employee will have the same rights upon notification of return to work, as he/she would have had at the time the job was eliminated.
  6. An employee who is absent on an approved Parental Leave of Absence will continue to participate in the Group Life Insurance Plan at no cost to the employee and the Health Care and Dental Insurance Plans as long as the employee pays the appropriate monthly premium.
