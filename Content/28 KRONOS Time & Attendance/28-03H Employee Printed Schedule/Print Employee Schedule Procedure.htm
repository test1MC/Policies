Print Employee Schedule Procedure

Step Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

Select myReports from the widget panel on the right-hand of the landing page.

3

Select Schedule.

4

Select Current Pay Period or Range of Dates (enter dates).

5

Select View Report.

6

Print. Press Ctrl+P or File -> Print.

Note: Changing the printing selection from Shrink to Fit to 80%-90% makes the schedule easier to view after being printed.
