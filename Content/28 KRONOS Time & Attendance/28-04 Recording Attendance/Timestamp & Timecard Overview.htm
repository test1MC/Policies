Timestamp & Timecard Overview

Punches feed the timecard which is used by payroll. Scheduled unworked hours to be paid such as vacation time or jury duty will automatically feed to an employee’s timecard. Managers will still have to approve that timecard for payroll. Worked hours will not feed from the schedule into the timecard; these rely on actual punches from employees.
