Record Attendance & Time Procedure

Step Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

Enter your mySherwin credentials.

Note: Do not select the arrow after entering your credentials unless you intend to enter the full application, which is not necessary for recording attendance and time.

3
If Then

Clock In (Home Store)

Select Punch In:

Clock In (Borrowed Employee)

Note: Store managers cannot logon at another store.

Select the Transfer box -> Punch In -> Specify the Store you are working at -> Punch:

Clock In (Transfer between Work In Store to Store Support, i.e. switching from working in-store to SWDS driver)

Note: Store Managers cannot switch between roles.

Select the Transfer box -> Punch In -> Specify the Shift you working -> Punch:

Taking/returning from Break

Select Meal In/Out:

Keyholder Punchout
  • Select Punch Out
  • Review your punches for the day -> Approve or Disapprove daily timecard:


    • Note: If you have incorrect or missing punches and do not approve your daily punches, include comments to your management staff so that they are aware of how to correct your timecard. Kronos will alert management employees via Inbox that a correction has been requested.
  • Specify whether or not you are making a bank deposit:

    • Note: If you indicate that you will make a bank deposit, seven (7) minutes will be added to your timecard. If your store’s bank is more than seven (7) minutes away, management will need to edit the timecard for the bank deposit to punch to add the extra time.

Non-Keyholder Punchout
  • Select Punch Out
  • Review your punches for the day -> Approve or Disapprove daily timecard:


    • Note: If you have incorrect or missing punches and do not approve your daily punches, include comments to your management staff so that they are aware of how to correct your timecard. Kronos will alert management employees via Inbox that a correction has been requested.
