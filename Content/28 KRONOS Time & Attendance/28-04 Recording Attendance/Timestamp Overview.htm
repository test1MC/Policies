Timestamp Overview

Timestamp is used to record daily time in, time out, and meal breaks. All punches are made in real-time (no rounding occurs with employee punches). If a punch has been missed or made at the incorrect time, employees will not be able to correct their own punches.

Timestamp prompts employees to approve their time on a daily basis in a process called attestation.  If incorrect, employees will not approve and will provide comments to management on what corrections are required. Management employees will be alerted that corrections need to be made to that employee’s timecard. Management employees should review and correct any missed punches or unapproved (employee) timecards on a daily basis. See Alerts, Timecards, and Manage My Department in Manager Alerts Overview for more information on reviewing and addressing exceptions.
