Overtime Management Overview

Managers should review their worked hours to scheduled hours along with the projected hours during the pay period and accordingly adjust and re-post the schedule to remain within target hours and avoid employees from incurring overtime. Managers should also review any individual who has worked unscheduled hours (while this may not put the store over target hours, it may put an individual into overtime).

Employees’ worked time is displayed in hours and minutes to allow for easy calculation of time still required to work in a week or pay period.
