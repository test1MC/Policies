Projected Hours Review Procedure

Step

Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

Launch a full-screen view of the Schedule Planner by selecting the Schedule Planner Widget.

3

Review all employees’ total worked, scheduled, and projected hours. If this compare projects that an employee will work over their scheduled hours, you may need to adjust their schedule and move hours to ensure no overtime is incurred.
