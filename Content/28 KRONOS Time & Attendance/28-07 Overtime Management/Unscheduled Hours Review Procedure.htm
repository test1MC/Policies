Unscheduled Hours Review Procedure

Step Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

Review your main panel, Manage my Department, for any individuals with a tick mark in the Unscheduled Hours Column.

3

Launch a full-screen view of the Schedule Planner by selecting the Schedule Planner Widget.

4

Locate the employee and review total worked, scheduled, and projected hours. If this compare projects that an employee will work over their scheduled hours, you may need to adjust their schedule and move hours to ensure no overtime is incurred.
