Vacation Time Accrual Overview

Regular full-time and regular part-time employees earn vacation time based on length of service with Sherwin-Williams. On December 31 of each year, qualifying employees become eligible for vacation based their Service Date. Using the employee’s Service Date, the amount of vacation is based on the years of service the employee will achieve during the qualifying year (January 1st through Dec. 30th).

Employees accrue vacation at the rate of four (4) hours for each full calendar month of service (to a maximum of forty (40) hours) commencing with the date of hire.

Temporary Employee

Employee does not earn vacation.

Reclassified Employee

Employee will be eligible for vacation based on his/her classification for the majority of the qualifying year.

Note: Vacation pay is based on the employee's current rate of pay at the time vacation is taken.
