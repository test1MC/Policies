Kronos Timecard Signoff Overview

The Kronos Timecard Signoff is your official stamp of approval to payroll regarding hours for the pay period. Employees with exceptions such as missed punches on their timecard cannot be processed by payroll. It is critical to keep on top of the daily alerts and exceptions to resolve these situations before they become issues with an employee’s pay. Managers will have an opportunity during the final timecard sign-off process to review and resolve any outstanding exceptions. If a store does not perform their final timecard sign-off, the timecards will be sent to Payroll unapproved and the store will be locked from making further changes to the timecard or approving the timecard. You need to print out a copy of the final hours and obtain signatures from all employees regarding their pay period.
