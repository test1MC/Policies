Print Kronos Timecard Procedure

Do not forget to print your employee timecards and gather signatures from your employees. Retain these signed timecards in your files. See Filing & Retention Drawer 4 (Personnel/Property) Overview for details.  

Step Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

In the Manage My Department Tab on your Manager Landing Page, select the row of the employee's name (this will open the timecard you are trying to print).

3

Select the Previous Pay Period in the timecard.

4

Select the Print Timecard Button.

5

Print the timecard.

6

Gather signatures.
