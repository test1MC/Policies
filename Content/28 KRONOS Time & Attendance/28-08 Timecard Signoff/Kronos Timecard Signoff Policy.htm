Kronos Timecard Signoff Policy

Verifying and approving hours worked does not necessarily mean that an employee will be paid for every hour recorded; considerations will be given to company policy and eligibility.

  1. Pay period approval (timecard signoff) must be performed at the end of each biweekly pay period by noon on the Monday of the new pay period.
  2. Store management must print approved timecards after the pay period has been approved. The manager must ask the employee to sign their timecard in order to abide by Wage and Hour laws.
