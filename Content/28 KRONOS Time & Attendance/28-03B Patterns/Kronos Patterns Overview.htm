Kronos Patterns Overview

Before Kronos can create a schedule, Kronos needs information about when employees work: availability and patterns. These functions should be set once and maintained infrequently.

Patterns allow you to create a default schedule for an employee which will be used every week until a change is made to the pattern. When the scheduler runs in Kronos, employee will be assigned the schedule set in the pattern. 

Patterns are recommended for the following employees:

Employees Reason
  • Management Trainees

Note: These employees are considered store support and will be identified in Kronos with an orange mark by their name.

These employees are training with other management staff and must be scheduled in accordance with their schedule.
  • Blenders
  • Bookkeppers
  • Color Consultants
  • All Drivers
  • Spray Techs
  • Tinters
  • Warehousers

Note:  SWDS Drivers are considered store support and will be identified in Kronos with an orange mark by their name.

These positions tend to work a set schedule week to week and their workload is not as affected by sales and labor demand.

All employees in the following facility types:

  • Automotive
  • Floorcovering
  • Product Finishes
  • Spray Source

These employees work in location without evening and weekend hours and, as a result, tend to work a set weekly schedule.

WARNING: When using patterns for employees other than MTPs or SWDS Drivers, the schedule created may exceed target hours if the patterns result in a schedule greater than target hours.
