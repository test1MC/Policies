Assign Borrowed Employee Procedure

Step

Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

From the Manager Landing Page, select the carat next to your name and switch from your view to the DAD view. 

3

Specify Date/Shift.

Note: System will return employees listed in order of best fit. Employees with full availability to fill a shift are starred.

4

Select employee name and the Assign Button.

5

Select Save.
