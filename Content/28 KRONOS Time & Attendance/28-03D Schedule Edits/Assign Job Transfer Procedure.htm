Assign Job Transfer Procedure

Use this function to:

  • Assign an SWDS driver in-store work such as tinting or STAR Order receipt.
  • Indicate an employee is going on sales calls, training, or to a meeting and will not be in the store.

Step

Action

1

Access Kronos using the Time Stamp button on the POS toolbar. Use your mySherwin credentials to log in.

2

From the Manager Landing Page, select the Schedule Planner widget.

3

Launch a full-screen view of the Schedule Planner by selecting the maximize button ().

4

From the Schedule Planner toolbar, toggle to Gantt View:

5

Select the dates for the upcoming work schedule using the calendar button:

6

Select shift box.

7

Select Add/edit shift.

8

Select the Job Transfer Cell. 

9

Select Search.

10

Select the appropriate job.

  • 01 – Work in Store
  • 02 – Work in PSR
  • 06 – Work out of Store
  • 07 – Delivery
  • 08 – District Meetings
  • 09 - Training

11

Select Save.
