Borrowed Employee Overview

From Kronos Timestamp, employees are able to fully manage most any special situation including working in a non-home store within their division.

If an employee is borrowed or loaned, no extra work is required on the part of either store manager in KronosEmployees may log time at any store in their district and will be paid correctly using a store transfer optionUnless the borrowed time was pre-scheduled, managers will need to adjust their borrowed time later (once they return to their home store). Managers will need to create an employee profile and assign an employee number in POS if the borrowed employee will be using POS.
