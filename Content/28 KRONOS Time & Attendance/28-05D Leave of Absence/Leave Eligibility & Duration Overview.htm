Leave Eligibility & Duration Overview

Employee Status

Maximum Leave of Absence

Full-time Employee with More Than One Year of Service

30 months or 25% of the employee’s service at the start of the absences (whichever is greater)

Full-time Employee with Less Than One Year of Service

A period of time equal to the amount of service the employee had at the start of the absence

Part-Time Employee

12 weeks or the amount of service the employee had at the start of the absence (whichever is greater)

Temporary employee

Not eligible for leave of absence

Note: A leave of absence will not be approved beyond the maximum period of time eligible for an employee. No leave will be approved for a period longer than one year. A request can be made for beyond one year but cannot exceed the maximum amount eligible for the employee. An employee who fails to return to work at the end of a leave of absence will be terminated as Failure to Return from a Leave of Absence.
