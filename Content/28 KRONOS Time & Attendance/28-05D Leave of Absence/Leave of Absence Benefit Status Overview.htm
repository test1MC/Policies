Leave of Absence Benefit Status Overview

All LOA / FMLA time must be entered by the district coordinator. This time will not be automatically added into Kronos, on the employee timecard. During a period of any approved absence covered by FMLA (including disability leave of absence), the following benefits will continue as described below. All other benefits will terminate in accordance with the particular benefit plan. With respect to other types of leaves of absence, benefits also terminate in accordance with the particular benefit plan.

        Type

Description

Life Insurance

Remains active without cost to the employee for the entire leave period.

Health Care and Dental

Remains active as long as the employee pays his/her share of the monthly premium. When an employee defaults in paying the monthly premium, coverage may be terminated.

Stock Purchase and Savings Plan and 401K

The employees will continue to participate in the plan(s). Authorized deductions will be made from short-term disability benefit payments.

Retirement Plan

The employees will continue to participate in the Retirement Plan. The amount of service credited for vesting and pension purposes will be governed by the plan.

Note: For more information regarding benefit status during a period of leave of absence, contact your Division HR Administrator.
