Leave of Absence Request Policy

With the exceptions noted below, leaves of absence for thirty (30) calendar days or more require completing a Request for Leave of Absence Form, approved by the Division VP of Human Resources and the Corporate Employee Benefits Department. Please contact your Division Human Resources Administrator for more information regarding this form.

Exceptions where a Request for Leave of Absence form is not required:

  1. Twenty-Nine (29) Day Leave - Requests for leaves of twenty-nine (29) consecutive calendar days or less require only the approval of the District Manager and the Division VP of Human Resources.
  2. A Request for Leave of Absence Form is not required in the following situations:
    1. Disability absences while the employee is receiving short-term disability benefits.
    2. Military service including call to active duty.
