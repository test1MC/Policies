Store Scheduling Overview

Scheduling is the starting point for Attendance & Time and is critical to store operations. When utilized well, scheduling:

  • Optimizes payroll budgeting through planning and scheduling of non-overtime hours.
  • Increases management visibility of staffing needs in store.
  • Improves management communication with employees by providing schedule information in a timely manner.
