Personal Business Policy

  1. Up to forty (40) hours of excused (not necessarily paid) personal time off during a twelve (12) month period, immediately prior to and including the current absence is considered reasonable. The immediate supervisor and the next level of management must approve absence for personal business.
  2. The Division VP of Human Resources must approve requests for personal time off in excess of 40 hours during a twelve-month period.
  3. Of the 40 hours, regular full-time employees may be paid, with the approval of the District Manager, for up to sixteen (16) hours. The remainder of the excused time would be unpaid unless the employee chooses to use vacation or personal holiday time.
