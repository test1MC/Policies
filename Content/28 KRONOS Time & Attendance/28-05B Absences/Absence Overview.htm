Absence Overview

Excused personal time off is not an automatic benefit. Employees are expected to anticipate certain personal situations which could interfere with their ability to be at work as scheduled. Employees are expected to make reasonable accommodations to minimize the amount of lost work time should such a situation occur. Although an occasional absence is unavoidable, excessive tardiness and/or absenteeism will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination. For information on leave of absences, please see Leave of Absence Benefit Status Overview.
