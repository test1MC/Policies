Kronos Absence Recording Procedure

Store Managers, Assistant Managers, and Operations Managers will be able to record absences for all employees.  Absences may be recorded as:

Sick Day

 Disability Cascade

Personal Business

Funeral / Bereavement

Weather

 EXCUSED-NO PAY

Weather

 EXCUSED-PD-CTO

Note: Use of the Disability Cascade will result in the employee automatically being assigned time with 100%, 60%, or no pay based on their available accruals. For information on how to process absences, see Schedule Manager Controlled Time Off in 28-05A Time-Off Requests.
