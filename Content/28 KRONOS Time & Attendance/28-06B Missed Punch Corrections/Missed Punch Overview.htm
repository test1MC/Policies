Missed Punch Overview

With real-time punches in Kronos, employees are not able to edit or override their punch-time. If an employee misses a punch or requests a correction through attestation, management employees will need to edit the employee’s timecard. Managers will be alerted to corrections which need to be made in two ways:

  • Missed Punch Alert
  • Attestation Comments in Inbox
