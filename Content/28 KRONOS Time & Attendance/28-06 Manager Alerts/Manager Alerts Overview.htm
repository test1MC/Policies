Manager Alerts Overview

Managers are expected to manage exceptions on a daily basis. There are several different situations which may require review on a daily basis: 

  • TimeOff Requests
  • Missed employee punches
  • Employees not approving their daily punches
  • Recording punches for any time employees worked outside of the store
  • Handling absences
  • Reviewing overtime

Items for review and action will be easy to identify:

Review Item Item Detail Alert Location Corrective Action

TimeOff Requests

Approve in conjunction with request being made; do not hold time off requests.

Request Alert in upper middle of Manager Landing Page.

Approve or Deny request.

For additional information, see see Time-Off Request Overview.
Missed Punches

Employee did not punch in or out.  If employee missed both punches, this would be identified as an absence.

Missed Punch Alert in upper middle of Manager Landing Page.

Edit employee timecard.

Employee Absence / Unscheduled TimeOff

Employee scheduled and did not punch.

Review Manage my Department and look for absences.
  • If employee worked, edit timecard.
  • If employee did not work and was excused, edit timecard with appropriate unworked hours code.
  • If employee did not work and was not excused, no action required.
Correcting Employee Punches

Employee was unable to punch in or out in conjunction with their shift activities.

Inbox Messages. Review for detail from employee attestation.

Note:  Employee may not record in attestation if the missed punch was the last punch of the day.

Edit employee timecard.
Overtime Review

Employee worked unscheduled hours.

Review Manage my Department and compare worked hours to scheduled hours to projected hours.  Employees whose projected hours exceed their scheduled hours are in danger of incurring overtime or working above target hours.

Adjust schedules through edit of employee timecard.

For additional information, see Overtime Management Overview.
