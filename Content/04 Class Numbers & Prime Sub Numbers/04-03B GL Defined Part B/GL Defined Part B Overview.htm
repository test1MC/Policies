Service & Expense Accounts
  • 7XXX - Marketing S & E
  • 8XXX - General and administrative S & E

The account code scheme for service and expense prime accounts provides for the first digit of the account to identify major category of expenses. The second, third and fourth digits will be common for all categories. The first digit will be as follows:

  • (7XXX): Marketing S & E - All service and expense to support the marketing and direct selling function.
  • (8XXX): General and administrative S & E - All service and expense to support the managerial supervision and basic costs of doing business.

When an account is valid for use by only one of the above major categories, the first digit will be preassigned. The account is therefore not available for use by any other location category.

An account preceded by an "X" denotes that it can be used by other locations. The "X" must be replaced by the location's preassigned digit.

For example:

Prime Sub

X141

825

For store’s use: 7141-825
