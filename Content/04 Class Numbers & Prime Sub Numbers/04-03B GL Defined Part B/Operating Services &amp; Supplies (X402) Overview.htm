Operating Services & Supplies (X402) Overview

This code describes the cost of miscellaneous operating services and supplies, such as rags, stencils, adhesives, janitor supplies, janitorial services, security services, fire protection, oil, paints, soaps, detergents, and ice.

Operating Services & Supplies X402 Description

*Hughes Network Service

 

-103

 Allocate satellite expenses to each store location.

Store Moving Costs

 

-102

Expenses associated with moving stock, furniture, fixtures, equipment, and signs from the old to the new location. This account should include the following:

  • Transportation on all store property.
  • Cost of dismantling and removing all signs at the old location.
  • Cost of transporting and re-erecting all signs at new locations.
  • Clearing cost at both the old and new locations.
  • Wages on all extra or temporary employees engaged through an outside agency for this purpose. (All S-W employee wages are charged to regular service expense accounts.)
  • Supper money of regular employees not entitled to overtime pay.
  • Cost of special signs and advertising announcing the move.
  • Painting new location.
  • Rent on old location less sub-lease income.
  • Penalty payment for cancellation of lease on old location.
  • Cost of transferring telephone service.

Other Operating Serv. & Supplies

 

-005

 Used primarily for store cleaning expenses and miscellaneous supplies.

* Hazardous Waste Removal

 

-015

 Record the expenses associated with the removal of hazardous waste and materials from a store’s premises.

* Trash/Rubbish Removal

 

-101

Record the costs associated with removal of normal trash/rubbish. This sub-account is NOT to be used for hazardous waste removal.

Packaging Materials

 

-101

 Record the cost of all packaging materials including but not limited to wrapping paper, twine, bags and tape.

* Spray Repair Misc.

 

-104

 Record miscellaneous items (e.g. rags, solvents) used in the repair of spray equipment.

Miscellaneous

 

-099

 Record the cost of temporary help, such as through an agency.
