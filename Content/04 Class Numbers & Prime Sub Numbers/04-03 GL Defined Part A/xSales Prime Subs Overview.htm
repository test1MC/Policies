Sales Prime Subs Overview

Gross External - Cash Sales (3000):

Gross revenue received in cash for products sold exclusive of sales taxes collected, but before deductions for discounts (3140). Includes VISA, Master Card, and Discover Card sales.

Sub-Account Description
Recorded Sales, sub-account (001) Used for all cash sales including VISA, Master Card, and Discover Card as they are reported.
Estimated Sales, sub-account (002) Used by Headquarters to record sales that are not completely identifiable. Sales are booked in this account until specifically identified and then moved into sub-account (001).

Gross External - Credit Sales (3050):

Gross revenue not received in cash for products sold exclusive of sales tax collected, but before deductions for discounts (3150).

Sales Returns - Cash Sales (3120):

This account records the credit issued to customers for the return of inventoriable and resalable items. Entries in this account essentially reverse prior entries in the sales accounts for inventory items originally shipped to customers. This account relates to sales originally recorded in account (3000).

Sales Returns - Credit Sales (3130):

Same as definition for account (3120) except relating to sales recorded in account (3050).

Discounts - Cash Sales (3140):

The amount of all discounts given to the customer on the invoice for products or services for which the billing is being made - relating to account (3000).

Discounts - Credit Sales (3150):

The amount of all discounts given to the customer on the invoice for products or services for which the billing is being made relating to account (3050).

Sub-Accounts Description
Customer Discounts, sub-account (001) Used to record discounts given because of the customer, such as employee discounts.
Payment Terms Discounts, sub-account (002) Used to record discounts given for payment within a certain period of time, such as 2%, 10 days.
