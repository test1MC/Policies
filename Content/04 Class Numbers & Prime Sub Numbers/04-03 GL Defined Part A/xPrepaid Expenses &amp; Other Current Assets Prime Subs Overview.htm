Other Current Assets (0515)

Other receivables or prepaid expenses, due within one year.

Examples: employee salary advances, temporary travel advances, claims receivable, miscellaneous drafts receivable, scrap sales receivable, miscellaneous deposits, etc.
* Sub account (122) Used to pay an employee’s wages in the event that their check is lost. Sub account to be used only for Sherwin-Williams employees and must be approved by Corporate Payroll.
Carrier Claims Receivable Sub-Account (002) Used to record a claim against a freight carrier for items damaged during shipping.
Recoverable Loss - Insurance, sub-account (006)

Should be used to code all expenses incurred as a result of a casualty, such as flood, fire, windstorm, burglary, etc. The expenses are cleared from this account when insurance payments are received. Loss of store funds and defalcations should not be coded to this account.

Cleveland Temporary Travel Advance, sub-account (003)

Used for all travel advances of a temporary nature to any employee.

Bid Deposits, sub-account (027)

Used for money submitted with a bid on any contract.

Pallets, sub account (022), are used to record the inventory value of pallets on hand at the store.
Temporary Travel Advance - Local, sub-account (003) Used by the stores when they advance employee money for travel on a temporary basis or a trip-by-trip basis.
Miscellaneous Holding, SMIS Invalid, sub-account (123)

 

 Should be used by Cleveland for offset entries made by SMIS for estimated sales.
Operating Fund Clearance, SMIS, sub-account (124) Will have entries generated from SMIS to show the amount of money an individual stores petty cash balance has dipped below its standard amount.
Miscellaneous Holding - SMIS Invalid, sub-account (126) Should be used by Cleveland for any entries showing up through Store Merchandise Billing to Invalid accounts.
Miscellaneous Other Assets, sub account (099) May be used by Headquarters in special situations, if no other sub-account is deemed appropriate.
