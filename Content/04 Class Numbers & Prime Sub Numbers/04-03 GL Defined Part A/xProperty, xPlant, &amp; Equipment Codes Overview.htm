Property, Plant, & Equipment Codes Overview

Furniture and Fixtures (0807):

Account represents cost of furniture, equipment, machines, fixtures, etc. not permanently affixed to buildings, which support selling and administrative functions. Examples: desks, calculators, paint shakers, shelving, merchandising aids, files, safes, etc.

Sub-Account Description
Beginning Balance, sub-account (001)

Represents the total original cost of all such assets, including freight, but excluding sales tax at the beginning of the year.
Current Year Additions, sub-account (002) Represents the total original cost of items purchased in the current year.
Current Year Removals/Transfers, sub-account (003)

Represents credits for the original cost of all items sold, scrapped or transferred to another location. Transferred items received will be charged to the transfer account and not to additions.

Allowance for Depreciation - Furniture and Fixtures (0808):

Depreciation reserve for furniture and fixtures to reduce the cost to the current estimated realizable value.

Sub-Account Description
Beginning Balance - Accumulated Depreciation, sub-account (001)

Represents all depreciation accumulated prior to the beginning of the year.
Current Year Depreciation, sub-account (002) Is the addition to the reserve for the current estimated reduction in realizable value of the asset.
Current Year Removals, sub-account (003) Represents reductions to the reserve related to items sold, scrapped or transferred to another location. Transferred items received will be charged to the transfer account.

Machinery & Equipment (0811):

Net cash invoice price of machinery and equipment, excluding sales tax, but including transportation charges, cost of motors, accessories, and attachments thereto which are necessary for the proper functioning of the machine or equipment. Proper costs should include installation costs and any other costs necessary to place the asset in condition for use.

This account represents accruals for bonuses budgeted but not yet paid.

Pension, Interest & Other - Other Payables & Accruals (1694)

Note: These items should represent payables and accruals for expenses incurred but not yet paid and being due within one year. Examples: accrued rent, accrued advertising, customer's down payments, etc.

Sub-Account Description
Accrued Rent - Property Base, sub-account (001) Used to record accrued rent for the facility in use where the rent is a fixed amount every month.
Accrued Rent - Property Percent, sub-account (002) Used to record accrued rent liability where the rent is tied to sales or something else that could make it vary from month to month.
Accrued Rent - Other, sub-account (003) Used to record rent payable that is not described specifically in the other rent sub-accounts.
Accrued Rent - Common Area Maintenance, sub-account (004) Used to record the liability to the landlord related to common area maintenance expenditures.
Accrued Rent - Advance Pay, sub-account (005) Used to record advance payments required in a specific lease.
Accrued Rent - Real Estate Tax, sub-account (090) Used to record the liability to reimburse the landlord for real estate taxes paid.
Accrued Rent - City Tax, sub-account (091) Used to record the liability to reimburse the landlord for city taxes paid.
Accrued Rent - School Tax, sub-account (092) Used to record the liability to reimburse the landlord for school taxes paid.
Accrued Rent - County Tax, sub-account (093) Used to record the liability to reimburse the landlord for county taxes paid.
Accrued Rent - Town Tax, sub-account (094) Used to record the liability to reimburse the landlord for town taxes paid.

Used to record deposits received with orders. The balance remains in the account until the merchandise is actually shipped to or picked up by the customer.
