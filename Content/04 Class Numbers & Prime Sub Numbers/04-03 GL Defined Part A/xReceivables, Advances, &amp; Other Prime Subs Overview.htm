Receivables, Advances, & Other Prime Subs Overview

Sundry Notes, Accounts Claims, Etc. (0620):

Miscellaneous long-term items, i.e., items not due us within one year. An example would be meter deposits with a utility company. These amounts should be reduced as necessary to record current portions of the long-term item.

Sub-Accounts Meter Deposits, sub-account (001) :

Should be used for deposits given to utility companies for gas meters, water meters, electric meters, or other types of utility meters.
