Cash Prime Subs Overview

Petty Cash (0100):

Amounts advanced to and accountable by petty cash custodians as permanent working funds for normal daily conduct of business. Used only when the fund is originally set up or the amount is changed by proper authority.

Cash items received in the normal conduct of business and deposited to the credit of The Sherwin-Williams Company and/or subsidiaries.

Sub-Accounts:

Local bank sub-account (001) is used to record the daily deposit of all cash and checks as noted on the daily Close Transaction. Withdrawals are made by Headquarters only.
