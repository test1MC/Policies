This account records the value transferred out of Finished Goods Inventory each accounting period and shipped to customers. Entries in this account relate to the various sales accounts (3000-3499). Normally, these amounts are from standard or absorbed costs and/or based on gross profit estimating, depending on the various divisional costing procedures. This account is for manufactured goods only.

Sub-Account Description

Cost of Goods Sold - Paint Products sub-account (201)

 Represents the store cost of paint products sold that have been manufactured by any of the Corporation's Divisions.
Cost of Goods Sold - Mfg. Paint Associated Products, sub-account (202) Represents the store cost of associated products sold that have been manufactured by any of the Corporation's Divisions.
Cost of Goods Sold - Brushes and Rollers, sub-account (208) Represents the store cost of brushes and rollers manufactured by any of the Corporation's Divisions.

Margin adjustments, sub-account (70X)

Used to record special adjustments to margin due to divisional programs related to things such as special pricing protection, large customer credits, etc.
Tint Usage, sub-account (848)

Used to record the amount of tinting color shipped to a store throughout the year. At year-end, this account will be adjusted to record the amount of tinting color actually used, based upon the physical inventory.
Mistints, sub-account (849) Used for the amount of paint that is tinted improperly or such that it is not acceptable by the customer.

Cost of Obsolescence (3700):

Adjustment related to the write-down of inventory, which should not remain at full value, due to items being non-current, old, no longer in use, etc.

The various sub-accounts under (3700) should be used in accordance with their descriptions and the above prime account definition.

Sub-Accounts Uses
Purchased Paint Products, sub-account (101)

Used to book the cost of goods sold of paint from all sources other than divisions of the Corporation.

Purchased Paint Associated Products, sub-account (102) Used to book the cost of goods sold of paint associated products from all sources other than divisions of the Corporation.
Purchased Spray Equipment and Accessories, sub-account (103) Used to book the cost of goods sold of spray equipment and accessories from all sources other than divisions of the Corporation.
Purchased Wallcoverings and Accessories, sub-account (104) Used to book the cost of goods sold of wallcoverings and accessories from all sources other than divisions of the Corporation.
Floorcovering and Accessories, sub-account (105)

Used to book the cost of goods sold of all such items from all sources other than divisions of the Corporation.
Window Treatments, Decorative Associated Products, and Other, sub-account (106) Used to book the cost of goods sold of all such items from all sources other than divisions of the Corporation.
Non-manufactured Brushes and Rollers, sub-account (108) Used to book the cost of goods sold of brushes and rollers from all sources other than Divisions of the Corporation.
Subs (120-128) Used when adjustments are made to Cost of Goods Sold based on freight variances between the Purchase Order and actual vendor invoice.
