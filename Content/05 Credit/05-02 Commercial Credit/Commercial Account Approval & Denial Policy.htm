Commercial Account Approval & Denial Policy

Office/Service Center will notify the store by telephone or in writing.

  1. The Store Manager must notify the customer when an account has been approved or denied. Any questions by a customer as to why an account was denied or approved for a lesser limit than requested should be referred to the District Credit Office/Service Center.
  2. New accounts will be mailed a letter outlining their initial credit limit and payment terms.
  3. Accounts that are denied will also be provided with a Statement of Credit Denial, Termination or Change Form from the District Credit Office/Service Center. This form provides specific information and reasons for denial.
