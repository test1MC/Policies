Credit Reference Inquiry Procedure

Step

Action

1

Are you phoning to check a trade reference?

If Then
Yes Complete a Credit Reference Inquiry Form in the Forms Repository
No Proceed to Step 2

Note: Find the Credit Reference Inquiry Form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “credit reference” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

2

Are you mailing to check a trade reference?

If Then
Yes Complete a Credit Reference Inquiry and/or Request for Bank Credit Information from the suppliers and/or banks listed on the application
No Proceed to Step 3

Note: Find the Bank Credit Info Request Form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “bank” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

3

If faxing to check a trade reference, use a Credit Reference Inquiry and/or Request for Bank Credit Information from the suppliers and/or banks listed on the application.  When faxing the Credit Reference Inquiry Form to the District Credit Office, include your Store Number.
