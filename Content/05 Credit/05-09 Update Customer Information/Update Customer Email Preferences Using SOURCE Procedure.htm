Update Customer Email Preferences Using SOURCE Procedure

Step Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Browser -> Access the SOURCE homepage.

2

Under the Home Tab, click the Customer Email Capture link (top lefthand corner).

3

Enter the customer’s account number.

4

This will return the customer’s account number, name, address, phone, and existing email. From here you can update the customer’s email address, as well as mark the customer’s preference as Opt-In, Opt-Out, or Don’t Ask.
