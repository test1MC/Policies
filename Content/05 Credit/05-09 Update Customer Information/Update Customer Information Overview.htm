Update Customer Information Overview

Customer demographic information for cash and charge accounts can now be updated online via SOURCE without the need to contact your Credit/Financial Office. The following information can be updated:

  • Accounts Payable Contact
  • Billing Address
  • Customer name
  • Do Not Call option
  • Invoice & Statement Delivery Method
  • Phone & Fax Numbers
  • Preferred E-mail Address

Changes made to the address field will be updated within 30 minutes. E-mail address changes will be available the next day.

All customer accounts may be viewed though the Edit function is only available to the home store. The Customer Information screen will be available for inquiry from approximately 7 am EST to 11 pm EST daily. 

The Edit function will be available after CPR is done processing in the morning (approximately 7 am EST) and will be shut down at approx 8:15 pm EST.
