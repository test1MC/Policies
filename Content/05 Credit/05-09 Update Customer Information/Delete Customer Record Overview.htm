Delete Customer Record Overview

The delete customer button does the following:

  • The customer record will no longer appear in a name search on POS.
  • POS transactions can no longer be processed against the account.
  • The customer account is excluded from most reporting & marketing promotions.
  • After a certain period of time, deleted accounts and associated data will be removed from the system.
