Do Not Call - Customer Preference Policy

  1. Retail or Commercial Accounts marked with a check mark in the Do Not Call field should not be contacted for any prospecting or marketing reasons.
  2. This does not exclude calling the customer for credit collection issues or delivery/special order calls.

Note: Please see Update Customer Information Procedure for steps to complete the customer's Do Not Call preference.
