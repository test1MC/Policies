National Accounts Client Management System Overview

The National Accounts Client Management System was created to foster clearer lines of communications between the National Accounts team, stores, and reps. Some advantages of the new client management system include:

  • Real time information
  • Simplified setup for new accounts
  • Streamlined search functionality
  • Improved communication with NA Executives
  • Increased Sales
