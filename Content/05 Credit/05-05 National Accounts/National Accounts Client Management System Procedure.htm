National Accounts Client Management System Procedure

Step Action

1
If Then

SOURCE

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Marketing Tab -> National Accounts -> National Account Customer List and Information -> National Accounts Client Management System

SherLink

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access SherLink homepage -> Marketing Tab -> National Account Customer List and Information -> National Accounts Client Management System

Note: Additional training is available on SOURCE/SherLink, directly below the file paths listed above.

2

Search any of the parameters using complete or partial entries.

3

The system will return all pertinent Parent Profiles and the correlating Parent Number, Customer Type, and NA Executive.

Note: When NA Executives update program details, they become accessible in real time.

4

Click the appropriate Parent Name to launch the Client Detail screen.

5

From the Client Detail screen, you can:

  • Review client information, roll-out/special instructions, general product information, attachments, and individual account and property expectations
  • Directly access the Customer/Account Lookup and PRC applications, which will retrieve all existing accounts and pricing information associated with the Parent Account
  • Email the NA Executive directly from within the system, and the email will route to their Sherwin-Williams box for review
