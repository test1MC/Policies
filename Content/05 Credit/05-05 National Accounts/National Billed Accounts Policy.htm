National Billed Accounts Policy

  1. Account numbers for national billed accounts are assigned by the National Billed Accounts Department in Cleveland.
  2. Stores are responsible for verifying the authority and authenticity of the individual purchasing for the national billed account.
  3. All national billed accounts have assigned credit limits that may not be exceeded without credit approval from the National Billed Accounts Department.
    1. Large customer orders or potential jobs should be communicated to the National Billed Accounts Department to insure that adequate credit limits are established.
    2. National billed accounts that generate a Credit Warning Indicator require credit approval from the National Billed Accounts Department prior to release of merchandise.
  4. Stores are to respond promptly to correction memos issued by the National Billed Accounts Department.
