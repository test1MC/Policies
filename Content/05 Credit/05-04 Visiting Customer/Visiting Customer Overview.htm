Visiting Customer Overview

When a cash or charge account is opened, it is assigned to a home store. The home store is responsible for sales and account maintenance including: Credit, Collections, and Pricing. Customers may use their Sherwin-Williams charge account at most Sherwin-Williams locations: SW Paint Stores, SW Automotive, Duron, MAB. A visiting customer is any charge or cash account that shops at your store, but is assigned to another store. Information on the price record cards created at a customer’s home store is automatically shared with all stores. Shared information includes:

  • Customer Information (name, address, email, etc.)
  • Pricing Flags (ProBuy, Store Special, etc.)
  • Tax Exception Information - Stores using the Tax Record Card System may view Tax Exception Information on the Price Record Card; however, this field is disabled from prompting the exception during COE or the sale.  A Tax Record Card MUST be created in order to run a tax-exempt sale.
  • Pricing Rules and Exceptions  (only if within the same Division)
  • Comments
  • Authorized Buyer Information

In regards to visiting customer account activity:

  • Any account downloaded to your system (home store account or visiting customer account) can be viewed on the Accounts Receivable Activity Report (A/R Activity RPT).
  • Press REPORT [SHIFT+F4] at any screen behind the SOURCE desktop to obtain access to the A/R Activity Report.
  • The column Home Store identifies the Home Store for each account. Accounts downloaded without a store number in this column are those assigned to your store.
