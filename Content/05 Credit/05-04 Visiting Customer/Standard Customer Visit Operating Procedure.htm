Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Add Order -> Use the LOOKUP [F3] function to search for the visiting customer account.

Note: You may need to search the headquarters database if the account is not found in your lookup of your local database.

2

Did you locate the customer?

If Then
Yes Proceed to Step 3
No Press LOOKUP [F3] again to search the headquarters database

Note: You will need to supply additional information: Customer Home Store, Zip Code, or Area Code.  The search of the headquarters database may take a few minutes.  The prompt in the lower left corner of the screen will read "Retrieving Customer Information from Cleveland” while searching.  Once the listing returns to the screen, note that you can identify which accounts are visiting accounts: their home store will display in the column entitled VISIT.

4

A Credit Warning Message may be returned to the screen. If a credit warning exists for a customer, call the District Credit Office prior to completing and filling the order. For more information, please review the credit warning code descriptions in Chapter 05-06 Credit Approval.

Note: If an approval from the District Credit Office is required for the visiting customer charge transaction, enter the Approval Number in the Comments Field of the Sale Header.
