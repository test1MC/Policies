Visiting Customer Transaction Policy

  1. Stores should realize that Credit Warning Indicator updates are immediately viewable at all locations, home and visiting.
  2. If the Credit Warning Indicator Box specifies that an approval number is required for a transaction, contact your District Credit Office for approval to extend credit.  If approval is extended, enter the approval number in the Comments Field of the Sale Header.
  3. If a visiting customer account is not on central invoicing and unless you are specifically instructed otherwise by the home store, invoices are to be mailed directly to the customer. The original (white) customer copy should be mailed weekly to the customer.
  4. Any questions related to sharing account information with other stores should be addressed to the District Manager.
