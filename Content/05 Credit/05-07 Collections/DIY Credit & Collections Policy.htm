DIY Credit and Collections Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  1. DIY account statements instruct customers to mail their payments to an address belonging to the home store.
  2. If a DIY customer makes a payment at the store, a Payment on Account Transaction may be processed.
  3. DIY accounts are not included on the Trial Balance or as part of the past due calculation.
  4. Problem DIY accounts will appear on the Weekly Exception report and the monthly DIY Trial Balance. These accounts will be identified at the Point of Sale by the credit warning indicators. For more information on account credit warnings, please see Account Credit Warning Overview.
  5. You will receive full credit for any finance charge income that is generated and any bad debt recoveries that are received for all DIY accounts.
  6. Your store will be charged 1% interest per month on receivables expense for any outstanding receivable associated with DIY sales from your store.
  7. Bad debt expense or uncollected invoices will be charged to your store as well as any collection fees paid to an outside agency.
  8. The Retail Department (PH:1-800-256-7058 / FAX: 216-830-0362) at headquarters will open DIY accounts and review credit limits. For more information, please see DIY Credit Account Policy.
  9. Any questions related to DIY collection should be directed to the DIY Collection Manager at 216-566-3946.
  10. No fee will be charged to your store for the DIY Collection Manager.
  11. Any questions related to increasing a DIY account credit limit should be referred first to the Retail Department (PH: 1-800-256-7058 / FAX: 216-830-0362) at headquarters.  If they are unavailable, contact your District Credit Office.
