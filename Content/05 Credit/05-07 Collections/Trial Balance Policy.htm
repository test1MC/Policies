Trial Balance Policy

The Trial Balance is an alphabetical listing all commercial charge accounts and balances due.

  1. Headquarters issues the Trial Balance with information current as of the last day of each month. Contact the District Credit Office if the Trial Balance is not received.
  2. The Store Manager and/or Assistant Manager must review the Trial Balance as soon as it is received to determine the status of all customer accounts.
  3. Accounts that are past due or overlimit must be contacted immediately for payment if your store is responsible for account collections.
  4. Commitment for payment must be recorded on the Trial Balance in the space provided under the customer’s name. The customer name must be recorded on the Collection Calendar on the day payment is expected to be received based on promise made.
  5. No additional credit may be extended to overlimit accounts without:
  1. Contacting the District Credit Office for approval.
  2. Increasing the credit limit (requires prior approval from the District Credit Office.)
