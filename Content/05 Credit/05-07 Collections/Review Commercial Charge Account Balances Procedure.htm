Review Commercial Charge Account Balances Procedure

Step

Action

1

From records of payments received at the store, post each payment beside the account name to the right of the balance being paid.

2

Every day, review the most recent Store Exception Report. This will aid in controlling overlimits and identifying past due conditions.

3

If the District Credit Office determines that no further sales are allowed to an account they will lockout certain customer transactions at the Point of Sale.  For more information on account credit warnings, please see Account Credit Warning Overview.

4

To adjust a customer’s balance, you might have to request that an ARJV be completed. Fill out the ARJV Form in the Forms Repository and fax it to either your District Credit Office or the fax number on the bottom of the form.
