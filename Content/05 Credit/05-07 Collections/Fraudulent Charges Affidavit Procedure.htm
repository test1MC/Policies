Fraudulent Charges Affidavit Procedure

Step

Action

1

Include specific transaction information, (i.e. – account name and number, transaction number, dollar amount of sale).

2

Find the Fraudulent Charges Affidavit Form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “fraudulent” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

3

Notify your District Credit Office of a fraudulent or disputed charge.

4

Email or deliver the affidavit to the account holding customer or business owner for signature. The signed affidavit must be notarized.

5

Once our customer has returned the signed and notarized affidavit to the store, the store manager should file a police report and retain the report number.

6

The signed and notarized affidavit, copies of the invoices, and the police report or police report case number should be forwarded to the District Credit Office.

7

Upon receiving approval from the District Credit Office, complete the following steps:

  1. Process a Charge Return Transaction for the merchandise reported on the Fraudulent Charges Affidavit.
  2. Process a Store Expense Transaction for the merchandise. Code the expense to UNAUTHORIZED CHG SW ACCT 7510-008.
  3. Follow your division’s LOI fraud charges process.
