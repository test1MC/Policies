DIY Account Approval & Denial Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or non-US TAG locations.

Only the Retail Credit Department has the authority to approve or deny credit based upon the customer's credit history. If an application is denied, the Retail Credit Department will notify the store by telephone and will receive an e-mail notification.  Stores can also view the status of an approval, referral or denial in Account/Job Processing by using the advanced search option.

  1. The Store Manager or Assistant Manager must notify the customer when an account has been approved.  Notify the customer that they will receive a Sherwin-Williams DIY Credit Card by mail within thirty (30) business days.
  2. If credit is denied, the Retail Credit Department will mail the customer a Statement of Credit Denial/Termination or Change Form.
