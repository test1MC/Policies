DIY Account Payment Terms Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or non-US TAG locations.

The payment terms of an account specify the amount to be paid and when each payment is due. DIY accounts have revolving terms with a portion of the balance due each month.

  1. The customer's minimum payment is determined by highest balance as per the table below.
  2. Each month the customer will receive a statement listing the accounts previous balance, purchases, payments, credits, finance charges, new balance, and minimum payment due.
