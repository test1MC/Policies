Create DIY Credit Account Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or non-US TAG locations.

Step

Action

1

Have the customer complete and sign a Revolving Retail Credit Account Application.

2

The Flexible Revolving Credit Agreement portion of the application should be returned to the customer.

3

Once you have the completed and signed Retail Credit Application from the customer, you must complete the online credit application process.  You will use the completed credit application as a guide in completing the online application.  Please refer to the Retail Credit Application Online Help for additional information.

Note: Do not complete any of the application on behalf of the customer.

4

From the POS desktop, select Browser -> Acess Source homepage -> Select Account/Job Processing from the SOURCE Operations portlet.

5

In general, please refrain from using any punctuation other than hyphens or ampersand [&].

Note: Any other use of punctuation [including periods, commas, apostrophes, slashes, etc.] creates inaccuracy in the alphabetization process and name search capability in the A/R Database.

6

NAME - Last names should be entered in the first field and the first name in the second field.  For example: Smith* John & Mary.

7

ADDRESS FIELDS - If the BillTo address may be used as a ShipTo address, Taxware will validate the City, State, and Zip Code combination. The City name must be spelled out completely.  If you have having difficulty determining the correct Zip Code, refer to the United States Postal Service website Zip Code Locator.

8

If opening a new retail charge account, indicate if the customer is an active/retired member or spouse thereof of the United States Armed Forces or Canadian Forces. 

Note:  The customer’s eligibility for a military discount must be verified.  View the military ID/Veteran Identification Card (VIC).

9

Select Finish and Submit after completing the first screen.

10

You will see a message in the center of the screen. If your computer appears to "lock up" during this process, please call the Help Desk [216-566-2740].  Please be patient and do NOT touch the keyboard during this process as the authorization may take a few minutes. Sherwin-Williams is charged for every attempt to submit the information and a new inquiry is generated on the consumer’s credit report each time an application is processed.  Do NOT submit a request more than once unless instructed to do so by the Retail Credit Department.

11

Was the application approved?

If Then
Yes Fax the completed and signed application to the Retail Credit Department in Cleveland at 216-830-0362. Don't forget to write your store number and the new customer account number on the credit application before faxing it to the Retail Credit Department.
No Tell your customer that you need to obtain additional information from the Credit Office before you can open the account. Fax the completed and signed Retail Credit Application to the Retail Credit Department at 216-830-0362. Don't forget to write your store number on the credit application before faxing it to the Retail Credit Department. You may contact the Retail Credit Department at 800-256-7058 with questions regarding the status of the request.

12

Once you have successfully faxed the retail credit application to the Retail Credit Department, make sure that any Social Security Number(s) have been crossed out and are no longer legible. The application is to be retained on file at your store until you receive email notification (see example below) that Retail Credit Department has received a copy of the application. Upon receipt of this email confirmation the application must be shredded.

E-mail: "The credit application for the Retail Credit request below has been successfully received by the Retail Credit Department. Now you are to shred the original application."
