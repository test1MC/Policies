DIY Cash on Completion Transaction Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

Step

Action

1

Complete a Floorcovering Proposal and Contract or Window Treatment Proposal and Contract Form (655-0347). Make sure that both you and the customer sign the form in all designated areas.

2

If the customer wants Sherwin-Williams to install the goods, complete a Rescission Notice and explain its importance to the customer.  Ask the customer to sign the Rescission Notice. Give two copies to the customer. Attach the third copy to the Proposal and Contract.

3

Process the full payment for the material as a deposit on the customer’s account.

4

After five (5) business days have passed, order the merchandise.  The ordering delay is to ensure the customer has not invoked the Rescission Notice.

5

Forward the Proposal and Contract with the Rescission Notice attached to the Retail Credit Department.

6

After the goods have been installed and after the customer has accepted the job, process the sale transaction through the POS.
