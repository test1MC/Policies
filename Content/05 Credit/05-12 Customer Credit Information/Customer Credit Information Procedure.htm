Tool Procedure
Customer Invoices

Customer Invoices are available on POS as well as SOURCE and SherLink.

Access Customer Invoices to:

  • Research an invoice
  • Determine where to apply a customer’s payment
  • Print
  • Access invoices from any account in your District*
  • Email or fax an invoice*

Note: *Indicates function available only on SOURCE and SherLink.

When using SOURCE and SherLink:

  • Invoices are available within 48 hours of processing.
  • Invoices processed on Saturdays will be available the following Tuesday.
  • Invoices made prior to December 2005 are not available.

Access from SOURCE

Enter the Customer Account (Lookup [F3]) to search for the account.

Note: Narrow your search results by adding additional parameters.

Access from POS

1

3

Enter the appropriate parameters.

DCO Comments Enter Customer Account Number with no dashes.

Customer Balance

Use the Customer Balance Lookup when a customer wants to know the balance on their account by job number or when you need to know how to apply a payment.

 Enter Customer Account Number with no dashes.
Customer Transaction Inquiry

Use the Customer Transaction Inquiry when a customer wants to know the activity on their account by job number.

Enter the Customer Account Number -> Enter a Job Number -> Select the Month.

Customer Statements

Use the Customer Statement screen when you want to:

  • Review outstanding balance on an account
  • Research specific job accounts
  • View, print, fax or e-mail statements

Enter Customer Account Number with no dashes -> Select the appropriate option:

If Then
View or print a statement View/Print
Fax, email, or print a statement Fax/Email/Print
