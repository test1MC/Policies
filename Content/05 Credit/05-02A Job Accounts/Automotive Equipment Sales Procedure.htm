Automotive Equipment Sales Procedure

Applies ONLY to Automotive Division, United States and Canada

Step

Action

1

Charge the customer by processing a Correction transaction.

2

Verify the WAC for the products updated appropriately prior to processing.

3

The top side of the transaction Changed From should total the amount of deposits received from the customer and the bottom side Changed To should list all of the sales numbers for the products and include tax.

4

The net effect of the Correction transaction should be $0.

Note: If more than one Correction transaction is required due to the POS limitations, verify the top side of the transaction total the amount of deposits and bottom side of the transactions plus tax total the entire sale amount. The net effect of the transactions should be $0.

5

Use the non-merchandise code “3” (Freight – FOB Destination) only to charge the customer for freight if freight was billed separately by the merchandise vendor or if freight was requested to be broken out by the customer.

6

Track each Correction transaction using the “Customer Deposit Log.”
