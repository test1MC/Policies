Order & Receive Automotive Equipment from Vendor Procedure

Applies ONLY to Automotive Division, United States and Canada

Step

Action

Order/Receive

1

Set-up and/or verify the sales numbers in the POS for all parts from the merchandise vendor. If help is needed when establishing the correct sales number for all products, contact:

  • US Branches – Tim Schmidt
  • Canadian Branches – Tim Gallant

Note: Merchandise Control will assign the product category (53-76-039) for each part.

2

From the POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order

3

Create line items on the PO using the sales numbers and enter the appropriate quantity and cost of each part. Verify the total cost of the PO(s).

4

Does the customer need freight billed as a separate line item on the invoice?

If

Then

Yes

The freight will be charged to the branch separately and can be included on the POS invoice to charge the customer separately, using the non-merchandise code “3” (Freight – FOB Destination).

No

Freight must be included in the total PO so that the cost of freight is built into the merchandise WAC.

Issue Payment(s) to Merchandise Vendor

5

Determine how the vendor will require payment so the branch knows the lines on the PO to receive accordingly.

Note: To minimize the company’s risk, at no time should the total of vendor payments be greater than the amount of customer deposits.

6

Is a down payment required by the vendor?

If

Then

Yes

Contact Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297 (Select option #1 Automotive and then #4 Merchandise) and speak to a Merchandise Correspondent. Release the PO lines and receive accordingly (based on how the vendor requires payment) in order for Accounts Payable to issue a check. Receive each line on the PO to match the vendor invoices

No

Proceed to Step 8.

7

Does the vendor requires multiple payments?

If

Then

Yes

Do not close the PO until after the last line is received and matched against the final invoice. The quantity might have to be received partially (using decimals if necessary) to match each payment to the vendor.

No

The process is complete.

Note: Refer to Process Vendor COD Shipments QuickCheck Procedure for instructions regarding prepayment of merchandise if required by the vendor.
