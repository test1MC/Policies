Booth Lease Policy

This section applies only to Automotive Division, United States, and Canada.

  1. The Automotive Division does not finance spray booths.
  2. If a Sherwin-Williams customer requests financing via a lease, the potential lessee must go through an approved Leasing Company. In the United States Automotive Branches, the existing approved leasing company is Survivor Financial Services. All other Leasing Company agreements in the United States and Canada must be guided by the following:
    1. Prior to entering into an agreement for a booth sale other than cash, the credit worthiness of the Leasing Company purchasing the booth must first be approved by the Division Credit Office.
    2. Do not agree to or order a booth until the Credit Office approves the sale to occur.
    3. The Leasing Company must complete a SW Credit Application and submit a Purchase Order.
    4. Terms of the Leasing Company’s Purchase Order must be approved by the Division Credit Manager.
    5. Purchase Order terms cannot be contingent upon actions of a 3rd Party; i.e., the lessee.
    6. Installation charges cannot be part of the agreement and/or a contingency of any payment terms.
    7. Sherwin-Williams is not responsible for hiring or paying for installation for any type of equipment, including spray booths.
