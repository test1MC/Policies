Hard Equipment Plans - Automotive Overview

This section applies only to Automotive Branches

Hard Equipment Plans apply only to hard equipment. Paint, paint-related products, toners, and associated products are not to be included on any hard equipment plan. This policy does not apply to spray booths.

*Minimum Down:  Never offer zero down payment without the prior approval of the Credit Office/Financial Service Center unless it is part of an approved published promotion.

**Finance Charge:  Not all customers may qualify for the zero finance charge and a finance charge of up to four percent (4%) may be added. The Division Credit Office will advise that a finance charge be required.

***Security Forms:  Unless directed by the Division Credit Office, a UCC1 financing statement is not required for the above hard equipment plans.

Note: All Hard Equipment Plans are subject to the final approval of the Division Credit Office and not every customer will qualify for extended payment terms.  In some cases, a higher down payment may be required and/or a customer may be approved for shorter payment terms than requested.
