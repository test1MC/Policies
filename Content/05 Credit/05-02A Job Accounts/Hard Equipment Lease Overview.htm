Hard Equipment Lease Overview

Sherwin-Williams’ partnership with Northpoint Commercial allows us to offer customers leases of twelve (12) to eighty-four (84) month terms for "hard equipment" with flexible payment options. The District Credit /Financial Manager is the main contact to assist with the lease application process.  For additional information on the details and benefits of leasing, sales tips, payment options, and program procedures, please visit the Sher-Lease Program Website available on the SOURCE Intranet under the Operations portlet.
