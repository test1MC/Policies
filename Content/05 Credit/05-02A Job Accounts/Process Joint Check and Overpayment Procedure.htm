Process Joint Check and Overpayment Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Upon receipt of a joint check, contact the District Credit Office/Service Center for instructions on how to process the payment in SOURCE.

Full payment for the merchandise must be made before refunding any money to the customer on a joint check. Contact the District Credit Office/Service Centerto determine what amount, if any, should be returned to the contractor. The District Credit Office/Service Center will provide you with an approval code. Do not refund any money until the District Credit Office/Service Centerhas approved the amount.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale ->  Sale/Payment Menu -> Account Adjustments.

2

Select Joint Check as the Adjustment Reason.

3

Look up theOriginal Payment Transaction:

If Then

You have a receipt

Scan the Barcode on the Customer Receipt.

The receipt will not scan

Enter the Terminal Id, Transaction Number, and Date in the Payment Return Window.

4

Enter the Amount to be refunded to the customer.

Note: The amount is usually the difference between the cost of material used and the amount of the joint check.

5

Press COMMENT [F9] to enter explanation and the District Credit Office/Service Center approval number.  Press ACCEPT [F1].

6

Enter the Manager Password and Press Enter.

7

Enter the Store Draft Number in the Pay-To Info Screen.  Press ACCEPT [F1].

8

Validate the store copy of the draft. Both the customer and employee must sign the form.

9

File according to batching instructions. Cross-reference documents.
