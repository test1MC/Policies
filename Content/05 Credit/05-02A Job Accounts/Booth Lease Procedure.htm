Booth Lease Procedure

This section applies only to Automotive Division, United States and Canada

Step

Action

1

Email or fax the Purchase Order and Credit Application to the Division Credit Manager.  Include the appropriate sales territory and branch number.

Note: If approved, a new charge account will be assigned to the Leasing Company.

2

Obtain a down payment sufficient to cover the cost of the booth (typically at least 45%).

Note: Only the Division Credit Office can waive the down payment.

3

Process the down payment through the POS as a Deposit (5) under a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt.

4

Order the booth.

5

When the booth is shipped to the lessee, bill the Leasing Company.

6

The leasing company will make a one-time payment to SW for the booth. When payment is received, process a payment on account.

7

At that time, also process the aforementioned deposit as a payment on account to pay the transaction in full (see step 4 above).

  • To do this, process a Miscellaneous Cash Disbursement for the amount of the original deposit.
  • Then, process a cash payment on account for the same amount. This will clear the balance of the invoice.

Note: The financing agreement and any quality issues related to the booth are solely between the lessee and the Leasing Company.
