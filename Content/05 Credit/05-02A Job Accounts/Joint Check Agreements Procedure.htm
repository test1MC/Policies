Joint Check Agreements Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Step

Action

1

Obtain the following forms in the Forms Repository: Request for Special Credit Approval, Sherwin-Williams Joint Check Agreement Form (Joint Check Agreement Form:  W Power of Attorney-Notary/Joint Check Agreement – Canada) or a letter from the General Contractor or Property Owner, approved by the District Credit Office/Service Center. Search for the forms by using keyword “special” or “joint” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

2

Submit a request for special credit approval via SOURCE.

3

Upon approval by the District Credit Office/Service Center, prepare (in duplicate) a Joint Check Agreement Form (Joint Check Agreement Form:  W Power of Attorney-Notary/Joint Check Agreement – Canada)  and have it signed by all parties involved, or obtain a letter from the General Contractor or Property Owner (the letter must meet the requirement of the District Credit Office/Service Center).

4

Mail the original copy of the Request for Special Credit Approval Form and Joint Check Agreement Form, or a letter from the General Contractor or Property Owner to the District Credit Office/Service Center.

5

Ensure all transactions processed on the job specify the particular job name and are signed by the painter or contractor.

6

When draws are to be made by the painter, ensure Sherwin-Williams receives payment on the draw date.

7

If payment is not made timely and jointly, contact the District Credit Office/Service Center immediately.
