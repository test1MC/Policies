Commercial Job Accounts Overview

Commercial accounts can be assigned separate job accounts for specific purchases. The following types of job accounts exist: Paint and Contractor, TRC Rollup, Hard Equipment, and Joint Check.

It is critical that store employees understand the importance of running invoices against the correct job.  Customers may request that invoices be run against the incorrect job when they are experiencing some type of cash flow problem or credit issue. If there is a collection problem on the job, the following could occur:

  • In reviewing the invoices, the GC or Owner could notice material that is not the right product or color for their job, and they begin to question the accuracy of our accounting.
  • The customer’s Financial Service group could file a lien. Filing a lein for an inaccurate amount is both illegal and can result in civil damages that Sherwin-Williams would be required to pay to the GC or Owner.
