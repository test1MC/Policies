Commerical Job Accounts Policy

  1. The customer must have a commercial account in good standing prior to establishing a separate job account.
  2. All job accounts must be approved by the District Credit Office/Service Center prior to releasing merchandise.
  3. A Request for Special Credit Approval Form must be prepared for all job accounts; this is completed on SOURCE and sent to the Credit/Financial Office.
  4. The job account can ONLY be used for purchases and payments related to that specific job. Only materials incorporated into or expended on the job should be charged to the job.  Knowingly putting material on the wrong job is a policy violation.
  5. Painter job payment terms are managed by draw schedules. The District Credit Office/Service Center will determine the payment terms.
  6. All Sherwin-Williams store employees and sales representatives are not authorized to sign any lien releases or waivers on behalf of the Sherwin-Williams Company unless specific prior written authorization has been provided by the appropriate District Credit/Financial Manager.
