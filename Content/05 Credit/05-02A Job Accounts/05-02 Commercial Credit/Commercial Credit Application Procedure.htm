Commercial Credit Application Procedure

The new account process will automatically attempt to order a personal credit report based on the information you enter as you submit a new account application in SOURCE. To help ensure the company stays compliant with the law violations, which can result in substantial fines and even criminal action, follow these steps:

Step

Action

1

Ensure the customer has fully completed a Sherwin-Williams' Commercial Credit Application.

If

Then

The applicant has completed the Guarantor Section of the commercial credit application and has also entered their social security number

Order a personal credit report.

The applicant did not initially complete the Guarantor Section of the commercial credit application, and it is later determined this information is required

Contact the customer and request that another commercial application is filled out with the Guarantor Section in order for SW to be able to order a personal credit report.

Note: As an SW employee you should not enter any information (especially a social security number[s]) in the Guarantor Section on behalf of a customer.  

2

Verify the customer signature.

3

Find the Commercial Credit Form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “credit” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

Select the appropriate language to view, print, or complete this document on your system.  Completed credit applications cannot be saved.

4

Once you have the completed and signed Commercial Credit Application from the customer, complete the online credit application process by using the completed credit application as a guide.

5

From the Source desktop, select Browser -> Access SOURCE homepage ->Operations ->Credit ->Account / Job Processing ->Commercial Account Processing Menu.

Note: If a credit application has been altered select the appropriate option when entering information in Source.

6

Indicate if the customer has any existing cash accounts.

If

Then

Yes

Enter the customer’s cash account numbers (up to three account numbers).

Any cash account number(s) entered here

They will be delete-flagged in the system thirty days from the date the charge account number is approved. 

A charge account is not approved for the customer

Any cash account number(s) entered will not be delete-flagged.

Flag reset

Contact the Credit Office. Move any pricing associated with the cash account(s) immediately to the new charge account.

7

In general, refrain from using any punctuation other than hyphens or ampersand [&].

  • Only use a comma to separate the first name near the end of the entry.  
  • Any other use of punctuation (including periods, commas, apostrophes, slashes, etc.) creates inaccuracy in the alphabetization process and name search capability in the A/R Database.
  • Accounts Receivable System converts commas to asterisks, and our programs take anything to the right of the asterisk as the first name.

8

BUSINESS NAME - Be sure to type in last name first, comma, first name if the business name is a person’s name.

9

ADDRESS FIELDS - If the BillTo address may be used as a ShipTo address, Taxware will validate the City, State, and Zip Code combination. The City name must be spelled out completely.  If you have having difficulty determining the correct Zip Code, refer to the United States Postal Service website Zip Code Locator.

Note: The parent store can update an account’s address and phone number on-line via SOURCE.

10

Select Finish and Submit.

Note: The authorization process may take a few minutes. If the computer locks up during this process, call the Help Desk [216-566-2740].  Be patient and do not touch the keyboard during this process- Sherwin-Williams is charged for every attempt to submit the information.

11

U.S. and Canadian Accounts:

Once the commercial credit application is faxed to the Credit Office, ensure that any Social Security Number(s) and / or bank account number(s) have been crossed out and are no longer legible. The application is to be retained on file at the store until you receive email notification that the credit office has received a copy of the application. Upon receipt of this email confirmation, the application must be shredded.

"The credit application for the Commercial Credit request below has been successfully received by the Credit/Financial Services Office. Now you are to shred the original application."

Once you have received the above message in an email, you must shred the original credit application.

12
If Then
Approved You will either receive approval, an account number, and credit limit or a message to refer to your District Credit Office for instructions. After entering customer information in the on-line SOURCE credit application, follow the instructions in the message.
Not Approved Tell your customer that you need to get additional information from the Credit Office before you can open the account. Fax the completed and signed Commercial Credit Application to the District Credit Office. Don not forget to write your store number on the credit application before faxing it to the District Credit Office. 

Note: Contact the local credit office with any questions.
