Reopen Commercial Account Policy

  1. If the customer's charge account has been deleted due to inactivity, the Store Manager/Assistant Manager must contact the District Credit Office/Service Center to establish a new account number.
  2. Your customer may need to complete a new Commercial Credit Application. You will need to complete the online approval process and forward the application to the District Credit Office/Service Center.
  3. Upon reactivating, a new account number will be assigned to the customer by the District Credit Office/Service Center.
