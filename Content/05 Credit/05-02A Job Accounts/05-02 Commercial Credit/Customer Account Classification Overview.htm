Customer Account Classification Overview

Customer Account Classification (CAC) Codes are used to classify customers into groups that have the same focus, interests and, who, most likely, would use the same type of Sherwin-Williams products. Sherwin-Williams can focus advertising and promotions to these select groups of customers when they are properly classified.

For example: CAC #6513 "Residential Building Managers" may offer a ProBuy (special pricing) for such products as Pro-Mar 700, base grade apartment replacement carpet, select wallcovering, and the appropriate associated products related to paint, floorcovering, and wallcovering. If your residential building account is not coded to CAC #6513, they would receive neither the ProBuy mailing advertising the specials nor the special pricing when purchasing.
