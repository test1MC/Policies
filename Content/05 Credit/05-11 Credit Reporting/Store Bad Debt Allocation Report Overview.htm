Store Bad Debt Allocation Report Overview

Monthly reporting of bad debt customer information includes:

  • account number
  • account name
  • collection
  • bad debt details
  • recovery details
  • write off details

Starting with the September 2013 reporting cycle, the Store Bad Debt Allocation Report will be available on SOURCE. Reports generated prior to September 2013 are available via the old Mobius link.
