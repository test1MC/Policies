Canadian Penny Rounding Policy

  1. All cash transactions must be rounded referencing the Remaining Due (Cash/Rounded) Field.
  2. Electronic form of payment transactions, including credit cards and debit cards, should not be rounded. The customer must pay the exact amount in the Remaining Due Field. 
    1. When a customer uses a debit card, always verify that the POS receipt matches the merchant receipt printed from the debit card terminal.  
  3. All gift cards must be processed as cash but managed like an electronic form of payment: use the exact amount and not the rounded amount.
