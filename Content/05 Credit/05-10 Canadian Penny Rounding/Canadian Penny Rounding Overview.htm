Canadian Penny Rounding Overview

The Royal Canadian Mint has stopped the manufacture and distribution of pennies. As pennies phase out of circulation, our cash drawers and customers may not have the pennies needed for payment or making change. To accomodate this transition, POS allows for cash transaction total amounts to be rounded. POS displays the rounded amount to be collected as the Remaining Due (Cash/Rounded) within the following POS Form of Payment screens, receipts, and associated reporting: 

  • Sales Transactions
  • Returns
  • Corrections
  • System Close
  • Inquiry
  • Sales Journal

If

Then

Paying by cash

Refer to the Remaining Due (Cash/Rounded) Field for the rounded total.

Paying by debit card

Refer to the Remaining Due Field for the exact total.

Paying by credit card

Refer to the Remaining Due Field for the exact total.

Paying by multi-tender including cash

Refer to the Remaining Due (Cash/Rounded) Field for the rounded total.

Note: There will be instances where the cents column ends in a zero for both the Remaining Due and Remaining Due (Cash/Rounded) Fields. This occurs when the transaction ends in multiples of five.
