Customer Credit Applications Procedure

Step Action

1

From the POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Credit -> Account/Job Processing -> Enter the customer information into the online Credit Application.

2

Fax a copy of the original credit application to the Credit Office.

3

Cross out any Social Security and bank account numbers on the customer signed application so that they are no longer legible.

4

All applications, approved or referred should be faxed to the Credit Office at the number provided in the Information Directory.

5

All original applications, approved, referred, or denied must be shredded, immediately once you have received an e-mail confirmation advising the Credit/Financial Office has received a copy of the application.
