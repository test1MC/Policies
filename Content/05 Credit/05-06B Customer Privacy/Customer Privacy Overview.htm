Customer Privacy Overview

Sherwin-Williams values the privacy and personal information of its customers. Sherwin-Williams maintains a customer privacy policy explaining how we handle our customers' information and how important its protection is to us:

The Sherwin-Williams Company Privacy Policy (English)

Politique de Confidentialité (French)

Follow these best practices:

  • Remember!  A customer’s name, address, phone number, or email are not required to make a purchase at Sherwin-Williams. 
  • Exceptions include services that require additional information to service the customer as they have requested such as delivery, rental, repair, installation, or special orders.
  • Customers may elect to enroll in our Preferred Customer Program, and you may invite them to provide their email address in order to extend many of the most important benefits of being a Preferred Customer, which are communicated primarily through email.
