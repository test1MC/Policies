Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) Policy

In order to ensure compliance with the FCRA (Fair Credit Reporting Act), you must ensure you and your staff adhere to the following:

  1. Never tell a customer that Sherwin-Williams will not pull a personal credit report.
  2. Never discuss with a customer whether or not a personal credit report will be used in the credit approval process. As part of our new charge account approval process, sometimes we utilize personal credit reports to help in making a determination whether or not a charge account can be approved.
  3. Before we order a personal credit report, we are required by the Fair Credit Reporting Act to have prior written approval from the individual(s) applying for the credit account. This approval comes in the form of our credit applications (retail and/or commercial), which must be completed and signed by the individual(s).
  4. Failure to comply with these policies can expose the Company to unnecessary risk and can result in civil and/or criminal penalties.

Note: If a customer has specific questions regarding the new account approval process, please contact the Financial Service Center.
