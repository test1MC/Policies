Opportunity Accounts Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive branches and Product Finishes facilities.

Opportunity Accounts are new accounts opened up for customers with whom the store believes a high volume of sales potential may exist. To better track sales for an Opportunity Account under the store number contact your District Manager or Sales Manager to request an account transferred from 0100 to the store number as a target account in Varicent.

The following account types may not be set up as Store Opportunity Accounts: 

  • IPA Accounts
  • Deleted Accounts
  • Bad Debt Accounts
  • Accounts already flagged as Territory key Accounts.
  • Account already flagged as Territory Opportunity Accounts.

Note: You will receive an error message if you try to add these accounts.

From the POS desktop, select Browser -> Access SOURCE homepage -> Operations -> Credit -> Store Opportunity Account Maintenance.

Note: Current opportunity accounts will be displayed. You can add/maintain up to a maximum of 20 accounts belonging to your store.

Note: You will be prompted, “Are you sure you want to remove this Store Opportunity Account?”
