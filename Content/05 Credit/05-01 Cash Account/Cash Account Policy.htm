Cash Account Policy

Cash accounts offer several benefits for customers: consistent pricing, ability to place special order types (delivery & rental), access to six years of color and ordering history, and participation in preferred customer programs. Any customer requesting special pricing is required to have an account with a price record card.

  1. Store Managers are responsible for designating cash customers that will receive special pricing. Pricing information must be reviewed when opening a new account in order to establish the correct pricing level for the customer.
  2. At the Form of Payment Menu, company charge will not be an available form of payment option for a cash account. All other forms of payment may be applied.
  3. Territory changes to move an account from the house territory to another territory must be approved by the District Manager. The District Manager will then update the account information in Varicent.
  4. Cash transactions, including those on Manual POST Forms, must be processed before the material leaves the store. Cash transactions cannot be held for any reason.
  5. Payment in full must be received before the material is released to the customer.
  6. Retail Cash Accounts are automatically enrolled in the Preferred Customer Programunder CAC Code1140. Refer to chapter08-01D Preferred Customer Program for more information on the program.
