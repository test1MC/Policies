Monitoring Customer Credit Status Policy

This section ONLY applies to Automotive branches

These policies are in place to protect the company’s assets and maximize profits. Call the Automotive Credit Office in Warrensville if you have any questions. Keys to managing branch credit:

  1. Print and review weekly exception reports.
  2. Review overlimit accounts and request limit increases when necessary.
  3. Make collection calls.
  4. Adhere to POS messages pertaining to credit.
