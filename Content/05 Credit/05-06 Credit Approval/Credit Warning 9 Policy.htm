Credit Warning 9 Policy

Credit Warning 9: No activity is allowed for this Deleted account/job. A new charge account may be opened upon receiving proper credit approval or a Cash account may be opened to ensure consistent pricing for the customer.

  1. Accounts assigned to Credit Warning Code 9 are deleted accounts and all transactions are locked out.  No further transaction activity can be performed against the account.
  2. If the decision is made to allow further activity to the customer, a new credit application must be submitted.  Process the application as you would for any new customer account.
