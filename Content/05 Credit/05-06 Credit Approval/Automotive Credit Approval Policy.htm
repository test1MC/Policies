Automotive Credit Approval Policy

This section ONLY applies to Automotive branches

If an account is only one (1) to thirty (30) days past due, Warrensville Credit Office approval is NOT required to release merchandise; selling to such an account is at the discretion of the Branch Manager.

Conversely, the Warrensville Credit Office locks out any charge account that is more than thirty (30) days past due or overlimit. These accounts will require Credit Office approval before merchandise can be released. Merchandise may not be released to locked out accounts unless another tender type is offered such as cash, check, or credit card. If a payment is received from the customer that satisfies the past due AND overlimit condition, the Warrensville Credit Office will remove the lock upon notification from the branch. You will be given an approval code to be referenced on the transaction in the Comments Field of the Sales Header.

  1. If you are unable to reach the Warrensville Credit Office, you may release merchandise if payment is received from the customer that pays the past due balance AND overlimit condition. Immediately notify your Warrensville Credit Manager via email that the payment was received and that you released merchandise. Warrensville will send you an approval number via email.
