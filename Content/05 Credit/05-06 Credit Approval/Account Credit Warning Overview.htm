Account Credit Warning Overview

Accounts Receivable Credit Warnings return to the screen after a Customer’s Account Number is entered in COE or POS. These warnings only appear if the Home Store Account is Past Due, Bad Debt, Deleted, Overlimit, or flagged by the Credit Office.

Job level credit warnings will return to the screen after entering the customer's account number in Customer Order Entry or Point-of-Sale. These warnings will only appear if the home store account job is past due, bad debt, deleted, overlimit, or has been selected by the District Credit Office. Follow the instructions noted in the credit warning before continuing with the sale.

Note: These messages do not take the place of Trial Balance and Exception Reports maintenance. Follow the message attached to the Credit Warning before continuing with the sale. For more information, see Credit Warning 1 Policy.
