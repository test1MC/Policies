Credit Warning 1 Policy

Credit Warning 1: Call Credit Office. Cash, Check & Credit Card are the only authorized forms of payment for this account/job. The Credit Office will give instructions if Charge processing is authorized.

  1. The District Credit Office sets this flag.
  2. Locks whole account or by job and requires manual unlock.
  3. Payment on Account and Return transactions will be allowed.
  4. If the Credit Office gives approval, they will provide a release number.
    1. Process a Customer Order to release the approved merchandise.
    2. Select the Customer Service Option for Delivery while processing the Customer Order.
    3. Enter the Release Number in Special Instructions.
    4. Make sure that the customer signs the Confirmation of Delivery.
    5. You should be able to process the point of sale transaction within 30 minutes to 1 hour. Call the District Credit Office if the account is not unlocked in one (1) hour.
  5. If approval is not given, the customer may choose to pay by cash, check, or credit card. Company Charge will not show as an option for form of payment.
