Credit Overlimit or Past Due Policy

  1. Accounts one (1) to thirty (30) days past due:
    1. If your store is responsible for account collections, no Sherwin-Williams charge sale can be made to a retail or commercial customer with an account that is one (1) to thirty (30) days past due without a firm commitment for payment of the past due amount from the customer. A firm commitment is promise by the customer to pay the past due amount by the 20th of the month.
    2. If your store is NOT responsible for account collections, sales can be made as long as the following conditions are met: the customer is not locked out to any other activity, is not currently overlimit, and will not be put overlimit by the potential sale.
  2. EAD, CDN, MWD, SWD:  No Sherwin-Williams charge sale can be made to a customer with an account that is thirty-one (31) to sixty (60) days or more past due without approval from the District Credit Office.
  3. EAD, CDN, MWD, SWD:  No Sherwin-Williams charge sale can be made to a customer with an account that is over limit without approval from the District Credit Office.
