Credit Approval Parent Policy

  1. For credit control purposes, a customer may only have one main account. The District Credit Office must approve exceptions.
  1. Hard Equipment and Job Accounts may be opened in addition to one main account after receiving proper credit approval for commercial accounts only.
  1. The account is the responsibility of the parent [home] store (normally the store that opened the account).
  2. Unless notified otherwise, the District Credit Office will handle the collections for all accounts.
  3. The selling store is charged 3.5% of the credit sale as an expense.
  4. The parent store will receive the compensating income for carrying the accounts receivable.
  5. All bad debt losses will be proportionally charged to all stores selling to an account. Any recoveries will also be proportionately credited to all selling stores.
  6. Positive identification (driver's license) is required at the time of the sale by the selling store.
  7. EAD, CDN, MWD, SWD:  No sale can be made to a customer with an account that is thirty-one (31) to sixty (60) days or more without approval from the District Credit Office.
  8. EAD, CDN, MWD, SWD:  No sale can be made to a customer with an account that is over limit without approval from the District Credit Office.
  9. Use the Customer Balance application found under Credit  in the SOURCE Operations Portlet of the SOURCE Intranet to determine if an account is over limit or past due. 
  10. AUTOMOTIVE BRANCHES ONLY:  If the account is not past due or over limit and the sale will not make the customer exceed their credit limit, give a credit approval number and complete the Credit Approvals Given Log located in the Forms Repository.
