Eliminate Excess Merchandise Procedure

Step

Action

1

Review the IMS Vendor Direct No Movement Report and research why merchandise has not sold.

2

Perform Cycle Counts to make sure the correct sales number was used to sell the product.

3

To eliminate inventory the store is not able to sell:

  • Contact the Vendor and try to return excess merchandise
  • Transfer the merchandise to another store in the area that sells the product
  • Adjust the selling price in the Inventory Price List
  • Work with the District Manager to locate a liquidator
  • Provide list to Sales Reps who may find a buyer
