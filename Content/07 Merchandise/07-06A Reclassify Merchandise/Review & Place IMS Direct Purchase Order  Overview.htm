Review & Place IMS Direct Purchase Order Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

If

Then

Inventory Management System (IMS) Vendor Direct Zero Movement Report

  • Identifies current, Vendor Direct purchased products received prior to January 1st that have not generated sales in the past 2 years.
  • Designed to help track and eliminate such products that have never sold.
  • The report will be updated monthly to show inventory movement progress.

Inventory Management System (IMS) Obsolescence Store Direct Purchases Report

Identifies direct vendor purchased products that were received and have no sales activity for 1-4 months.

If

Then

4 months or more with no sales

Merchandise will be written off to obsolescence. Any merchandise that is automatically written off to obsolescence should be sold under the product's sales number.

3 months with no sales

Take action now to sell this merchandise and eliminate a write off to obsolescence.

2 months with no sales

Research why the merchandise is not selling. Perform Cycle Counts to make sure that the correct sales number is used to sell the product.

1 month with no sales

Check the perpetual inventory to make sure that the units and weighted average cost are correct. Process an inventory adjustment, if necessary.

Use the following guidelines when purchasing directly from a vendor:

  • Only purchase merchandise that the store can sell.
  • Only purchase stock merchandise from vendor if there is immediate demand for the product,. The quantity is limited to a 3 month supply based on rate-of-sale, and approved by DM prior to purchase.
  • Purchase the quantity necessary to meet current customer demand .
  • Purchase directly from a vendor if the merchandise is pre-sold.

Note: Products previously written off will be valued at zero (0) if counted as current during the physical inventory counting process.
