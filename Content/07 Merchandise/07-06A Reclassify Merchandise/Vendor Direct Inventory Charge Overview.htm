Vendor Direct Inventory Charge Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

Effective January 1, 2002, an extra 2% inventory interest charge will be assessed on Vendor Direct purchases that are in excess of 1 year supply. Review the Vendor-Direct section of the IMS Store Detail Excess Inventory Aging Report (review Over 36 Months on Hand, 25 to 36 Months On Hand, and 13 to 24 Months on Hand) to address aged inventory.
