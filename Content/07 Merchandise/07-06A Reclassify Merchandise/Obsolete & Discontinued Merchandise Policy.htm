Obsolete & Discontinued Merchandise Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

  1. You are required to comply with the Permanent Markdown Notices to eliminate obsolete/discontinued non-manufactured merchandise.
  2. Merchandise identified as Progressive Markdown items must be sold according to the markdown schedule.
  3. Min/Max levels should be set to zero to prevent reorders from the DSC for all obsolete/discontinued merchandise identified on the IMS Excess Inventory Aging Report.
  4. Merchandise may only be placed in obsolescence at the annual physical inventory. DO NOT store expense merchandise prior to inventory to recognize obsolescence.
