Return Purchased Merchandise Procedure

Step

Action

Merchandise directly purchased from a vendor

1

Contact the vendor and try to return excess merchandise

2

Adjust the selling price in the Inventory Price List

3

Transfer the merchandise to another store in your area that sells the product.

4

Work with your District Manager to locate a liquidator

Merchandise purchased from the DSC

1

Transfer the merchandise to another store in your area that sells the product.

2

Implement the Progressive Markdown process according to communication from Headquarters

3

Follow your normal procedures to try to return excess stock to the DSC.

4

Promptly follow DSC Return instructions

5

Adjust the Min/Max level to prevent any reordering of the product.
