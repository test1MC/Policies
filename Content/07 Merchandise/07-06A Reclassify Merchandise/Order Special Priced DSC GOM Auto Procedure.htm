Order Special Priced DSC GOM – Auto Procedure

This section does not apply to Chemical Coatings Facilities, Duron Stores, General Polymer, and SW Paint Stores.

On occasion, the DSC will offer slow-moving or soon-to-be discontinued GOM to store at a reduced price.

Step

Action

1

Order product on the regular STAR order.

Note: Stores will be billed at the regular store cost, and a credit will be issued by Merchandise Accounting as a Cost of Goods Adjustment for the discounted item.

2

Add to inventory and sell special priced GOM utilizing the normal SMIS number.
