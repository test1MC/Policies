Store Direct Order Writeoffs Overview


A monthly debit to P&L line 34 [INV ADJ…OBSOL] will be made for the amount displayed in the SKUS WRITTEN OFF (shaded) section of the current report:

  • The WAC will be adjusted to [0] value for the write-off quantity.
  • The product will remain in perpetual inventory at the reduced value under the original sales number.
  • Sales recorded after write-off will allow for recuperation of prior margin penalty.
