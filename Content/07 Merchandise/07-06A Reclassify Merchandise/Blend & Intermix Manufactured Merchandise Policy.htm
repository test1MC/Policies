Blend & Intermix Manufactured Merchandise Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  1. To request a blend, the order must meet the criteria set by the blending station. Refer to the Division Blending Station Manual for current instructions. Follow the points below if a Blending Station Manual is not available.
  2. An initial qualifying order for a blend can be for 1 to 20 gallons. Follow-up orders must be for production quantities (minimum 20 gallons).
  3. The blending station ships blends to the store freight collect. Blends shipped directly to customers are sent pre-paid and the store is charged for the freight on an IST.
  4. If the customer asks store personnel to make a special color to match carpeting or wallpaper, the store may include a mixing charge in the selling price of the product.
  5. If a store employee pours an intermix into new containers for the customer, the employee charges the customer for the containers.
  6. When the store buys damaged merchandise for resale at a special price, the store manager must re-label the merchandise using a sales number. The sales number indicates the point of origin of the damaged merchandise. For more information, see Reclassified Merchandise Policy.
  7. An order for damaged goods will be filled according to the products' availability from the Distribution Service Center.
  8. All special merchandise must be labeled accurately with the correct sales or class number and gross profit percentage.
