Obsolete & Discontinued Merchandise Overview

Products discontinued prior to January 1st of the current year will be written off as obsolete if not sold prior to the annual physical inventory. In most cases, products will be declared [discontinued] during the year's first quarter, allowing 20 - 22 months to sell through merchandise. The discontinued section of the IMS Store Summary Excess Inventory Aging Report will help you identify and track these products.

Merchandise may only be placed in obsolescence after the annual October 31st inventory. After inventory, re-label using the following sales numbers.
