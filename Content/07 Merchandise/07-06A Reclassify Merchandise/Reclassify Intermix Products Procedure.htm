Reclassify Intermix Products Procedure

Step

Action

1

Consult the Sherwin-Williams formula for the proper product mix to produce the required color.

2

Use the store's Colormeter, if necessary, to add colorant to the intermix.

3

Calculate the gross profit and selling price for the product. If the store employee had to select a color to match customer furnishings, consider including a mixing charge as part of the selling price. Also consider charging the customer if the Intermix is poured into new containers.

4

Label the Intermix with class number 8185 and the appropriate gross profit percentage.
