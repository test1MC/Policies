IMS Excess Inventory Aging Report Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

The IMS Excess Inventory Aging Report will help employees to identify and sell through existing excess inventory. Consequently, employees can reduce monthly interest on inventory charge. The report consists of two parts: IMS Store Summary Excess Inventory Aging Report and IMS Store Detail Excess Inventory Aging Report.

  • The IMS Store Summary Excess Inventory Report provides an excess inventory overview for the store in the following inventory groupings:
    • Plan O Gram
    • In Stock Wallcovering
    • DSC - Direct
    • Vendor - Direct
    • Discontinued Color Conversion
    • Discontinued Other
  • Each inventory grouping contains your excess inventory dollars by the month's supply-on-hand based on the store's rate-of-sale in the past 12 months. Supply is categorized in the following Slow Moving Periods:
    • Over 36 months
    • 25-36 months
    • 13-24 months
    • 6-12 months
    • Less than 6 months
    • No Inventory / Had Sales

Note: Products discontinued prior to January 1st of the current year will be written off as obsolete if not sold prior to the annual physical inventory. For more information see Obsolete & Discontinued Merchandise Policy.
