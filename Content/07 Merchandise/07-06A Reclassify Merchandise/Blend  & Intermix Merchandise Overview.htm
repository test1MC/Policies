Blend & Intermix Merchandise Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  • A blend is custom-mixed paint to match a special color at customer request. Such colors are not available from Sherwin-Williams' standard custom mix charts. Blends are produced at blending stations, not at Sherwin-Williams stores.
  • Blends shipped to a store from a blending station are transferred by using an Inter Store Transfer (IST) Transaction.
  • An intermix is a mix of two or more colors of the same product line. Sometimes colorants are also added to produce the required color. Sherwin-Williams has a chart of standard intermixes which are to be used as a guide in providing these colors for customers.
  • Damaged goods are sometimes intentionally ordered from a Distribution Service Center for sale at special, reduced prices. Stores can also order manufactured merchandise at a special price less than the listed cost.
