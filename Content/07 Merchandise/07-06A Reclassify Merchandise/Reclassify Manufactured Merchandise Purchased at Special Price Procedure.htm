Reclassify Manufactured Merchandise Purchased at Special Price Procedure

Step

Action

1

When it has been determined that the store can order merchandise at special prices, telephone the Distribution Service Center to place the order.

2

Use the Price and Profit Index to establish a selling price.

3

Label all merchandise with class number 8342 and the appropriate gross profit percentage. Also include the selling price on the label.

4

If the selling price is reduced even further in order to move the merchandise, do not re-label. Instead, use the original special price and take a dollar discount when entering the sale into the POS terminal.
