Freight Receiving - Automotive Policy

Branches receive all manufactured (GOM) and associated products (A/P) from the Distribution Service Center. All merchandise shipments received by the Branch are subject to immediate inspection for accuracy of order quantity, damages, or loss.

  1. The Branch must accept all deliveries which are accompanied by a legitimate Delivery Receipt.
  2. The Branch Manager is responsible for verifying the accuracy of all received shipments.
  3. All received items are to be entered into the MMS system within 24 hours of receipt to update the Branch perpetual inventory records.
