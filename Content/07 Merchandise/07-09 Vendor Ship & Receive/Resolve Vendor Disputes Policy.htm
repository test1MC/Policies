Resolve Vendor Disputes Policy

This only applies to External Vendor.

All Sherwin-Williams' National Vendors are instructed to contact Headquarters to resolve open disputes. From time to time it still may be necessary for a vendor to contact the store directly.

  1. Account Payable Department at Headquarters will resolve all vendor disputes within 90 days.
  2. The Store Manager must provide all information available on a dispute within ten days of the request.

Then

Contacted by a vendor

Provide all information that is available at the store level. If the information is not available, refer the vendor to the appropriate correspondent at Headquarters (refer to Information Directory for the telephone number).

Contacted by Headquarters Correspondent

Accounts Payable will notify the store by letter of all unresolved vendor disputes. Provide the information necessary to resolve the dispute within 10 days. The District Manager will be notified by letter if no response is received within 10 days. If the store and the district fail to respond within the time limit, the store will be charged for the vendor dispute in full.
