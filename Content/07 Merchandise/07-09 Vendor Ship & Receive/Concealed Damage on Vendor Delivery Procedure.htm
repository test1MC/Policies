Concealed Damage on Vendor Delivery Procedure

This only applies to External Vendors.

Any damage noticed after opening the package is considered Concealed Damage.

Step

Action

1

Notify the vendor and request that the vendor file the claim. Keep the damaged merchandise in its original shipping container.

2

Request the vendor ship the replacement merchandise. Reorder the merchandise from the vendor using a new Purchase Order number.

Note: Refer the new P.O. to the old P.O. and note "replacement for damaged merchandise" on the order.

3

Process a Chargeback. The vendor may request that it be returned to them. Attach a copy of the processed Chargeback to the merchandise.

Note: If the vendor does not want the damaged merchandise returned, destroy the merchandise locally.
