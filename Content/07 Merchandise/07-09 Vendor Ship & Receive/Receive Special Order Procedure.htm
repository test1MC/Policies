Receive Special Order Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Step Action

1

Notify the customer the same day you receive their special order material.

2

Log all customer contact on the order form to include: your initials, the date and time of the contact.

If Then

Customer does not pick up the special order merchandise within 10 days of initial contact.
  • Make a follow-up call to the customer.
  • Capture all information on the order form.

Customer has still not picked up merchandise.
  • Continue to make weekly contact with the customer.
  • Send the customer a letter reminding them to pick up their merchandise, if appropriate.

Customer tells you they will pick-up the merchandise in several weeks.
  • Ask the customer for a specific date.

Customer fails to pick-up the merchandise within a week of a second agreed upon date.
  • Contact the vendor to see if the material could be returned.

For more information see Vendor Merchandise Return Overview or Vendor Special Order Return Overview .
