Vendor Loss or Damage Claim Policy

This only applies to External Vendors.

Sherwin-Williams files a claim against a common carrier when goods are lost or damaged. The Chargeback form assists stores in recording and receiving credit for damaged and missing merchandise shipped from a vendor.

  1. The Store Manager must inspect all merchandise upon delivery. This practice eliminates the time and cost involved in Loss and Damage claims.
  2. The store should file a claim for loss or damage on any vendor order for claims over $5.00 using prime-sub 7599-104 (Destroyed Merchandise). Any claim under $5.00 will be store expensed.
    1. If the store receives a damaged shipment from a vendor, it is the vendor's responsibility to file the claim.
    2. If the entire shipment is lost, destroyed, or returned to the shipping point, the shipping location should file the claim.
  3. Damaged goods should be sold as soon as possible. The amount realized from the sale is used as the salvage value when filing the claim.
