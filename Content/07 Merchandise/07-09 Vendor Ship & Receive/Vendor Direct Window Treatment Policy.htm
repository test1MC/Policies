Vendor Direct Window Treatment Policy

This only applies to External Vendors.

Window Treatment merchandise is custom-made according to measurements given by the customer. The merchandise is special ordered for a customer from one of Sherwin-Williams Window Treatment vendors.

  1. Employees are required to process a Deposit transaction to accept payment in full for the direct shipment before placing the order.
  2. Large blinds shipped by common carrier must be shipped to the store.
    1. Window Treatments cannot be shipped to a Post Office Box.
  3. If payment in full is not received a deposit of at least 25% is required, and the order must ship to the store.
  4. Custom-made window treatments are not returnable. A Window Treatment Order Form and Contract [655-0347] must be completed for all orders. Search for Window Treatment Contract in the Forms Repository under form description.
  5. The Store Manager must approve all window treatment purchase orders.
  6. A separate purchase order must be used for each vendor used on a customer order.

Note:  Access the Levolor Order Form from the Forms Repository.
