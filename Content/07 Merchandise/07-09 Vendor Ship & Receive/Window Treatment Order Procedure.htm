Window Treatment Order Procedure

This only applies to External Vendors.

Step

Action

Complete a Window Treatment Order Form and Contract.

Note:  If unable to charge the customer’s Sherwin-Williams’ charge account, obtain a deposit for 100% of the merchandise. If the order is being shipped to the store and the customer refuses to make payment in full, collect a deposit of at least 25%.

Prepare a purchase order. Identify the purchase order as "direct shipment" if merchandise is being shipped to the customer's home.
