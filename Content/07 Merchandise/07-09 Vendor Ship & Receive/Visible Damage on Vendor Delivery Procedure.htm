Visible Damage on Vendor Delivery Procedure

This only applies to External Vendors.

Any damage noticeable at the time of delivery would be considered visible damage.

Step

Action

1

Inform the delivery person of the damage immediately and inspect the contents for damage.

Note: If the contents are damaged make sure the damage is noted before signing the receiving documents.

2

Keep the damaged merchandise in its original shipping container.

3

Reorder the merchandise from the vendor using a new Purchase Order number. Remember to refer the new P.O. to the old P.O. and note "replacement for damaged merchandise".

4

Call the carrier to request a claim number. Give the carrier the package's destination and a description and value of the damaged items.

Note: When the inspector has seen and accepted responsibility for the damage, the store will receive a claim form reflecting a loss or damage investigation number.

5

Complete all the necessary information on the claim form. Send the required copies to the carrier and retain one copy in the Carrier Claims file.

6

When the settlement check arrives send the check with attached documentation to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
Store Merchandise Billing
ATTN: Eric McGinness
1030 Guildhall

Cleveland, OH 44115
