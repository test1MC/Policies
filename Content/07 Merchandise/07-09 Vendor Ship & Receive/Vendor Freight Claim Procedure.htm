Vendor Freight Claim Procedure

This only applies to External Vendors.

Step Action

1

Inspect the contents for damage. If the contents are damaged, immediately inform the driver and ensure the damage is noted before signing the receiving documents. Ask the delivery person for the company's telephone number to report the claim.

2

Contact the vendor to reorder the damaged merchandise.

3

Reorder the replacement merchandise from the vendor using a new Purchase Order. Remember to refer the new P.O. to the old P.O. and note "replacement for damaged merchandise - NOT TO BE BILLED" on the order.

4

Keep the damaged merchandise in its original shipping container and call the Freight Company to request a claim form.

Note: The Freight Company reserves the right to inspect the damage before processing claims.

5

Complete all the necessary information on the claim form. Send the required copies to the Freight Company and retain one copy in the Freight Claims file.

6

When the settlement check arrives send the check with attached documentation to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
FSS Retail Disbursements
ATTN:  Debbie Brucato -  1530 Guildhall
101 Prospect Avenue NW
Cleveland, OH 44115

Note: FSS Retail Disbursements will process the check and credit the store under the appropriate cost center 0406-80X.
