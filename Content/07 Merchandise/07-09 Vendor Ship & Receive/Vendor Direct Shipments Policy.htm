Vendor Direct Shipments Policy

This only applies to External Vendors.

Merchandise shipped direct to a customer from a vendor is a direct shipment. Any merchandise sold by Sherwin-Williams can be direct shipped to our customers.

  1. Direct Shipments require either: 1) Sherwin-Williams' Charge Account in good standing, or 2) Deposit paid in full.
  2. If payment in full is not received, the merchandise must be shipped to the store.
  3. All external receiving policies are applicable for vendor direct shipments. For more information, see Receive External PO Procedure.

Note: For more information on how to process a deposit, refer to 09-13 Deposit Processing.
