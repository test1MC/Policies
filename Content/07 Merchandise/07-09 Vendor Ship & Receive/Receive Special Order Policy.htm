Receive Special Order Policy

This section applies only to External Vendors.

  1. Customers must be notified the same day after receiving special order material.
  2. Customer contact must be logged on the customer order form to include your initials, and the date and time of the contact.
  3. Follow-up must continue if the customer does not pick-up their merchandise within one week. All follow-up information must be included on the customer order form to include your initials, the date and time of the contact, and any customer comments.
  4. If the customer gives a specific pick-up date, weekly contact is not required. The follow-up process must continue if the customer does not pick-up their order within one week after the pick-up date they gave you.
  5. A letter is to be sent from the Store Manager if the customer does not pick-up their order within 30 days from initial contact.

Note: Find the Customer Notification letter in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “customer no" in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.
