Change & Assign Min & Max Levels Procedure

Step Action

1
Stage Description
Changing Min/Max Level Change Notification
  • From POS desktop, select Store -> Select Merchandise Management
    ->Enter Employee Number and Password.
  • Select Maintenance from the Merchandise Management Menu -> Min – Max.
  • Select Individual Sales Number, Sales number by Department, or Product (Rex) Number by Department to start the review process.
Changing Min/Max Level within a STAR Order
  • When reviewing a SKU on a STAR Order, press COLOR [F6] -> After making changes, press ACCEPT [F1].
Assigning Min/Max Level for Non-STAR Order products
  • Enter the desired SKU in the Individual Sales Number Screen.
  • Press Enter -> View [F7].
  • Select Yes to have the product appear on the Non-STAR Order product sheet.
Changing Min/Max Level if a product will appear when generating a Suggest STAR Order
  • Enter the SKU in the Individual Sales Number Screen and press Enter -> View [F7].
  • Select Yes to generate a Suggested STAR Order or No to have it appear on the Suggested STAR Order.

2

If you make changes, you will be required to select a Reason Description from the Min/Max Change Window:

  1. Pallet Rounding or Pack Size Adjustment.
  2. New Customer or Current Customer Switching to Product.
  3. Loss of Customer or Current Customer Switching from Product.
  4. Large Job Lasting More Than 2 Months.
  5. Other

Note: If a change is made to a product from a STAR Order product to a NON- STAR Order product that change could automatically be overwritten by the Prescient system. This is not to be used for DSC stocked items and will be monitored to ensure that DSC stocked items are not being put on the report.

3

When finished, press ACCEPT [F1] to apply the changes.
To utilize the Min – Max Changes made for Non-STAR Order Products

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select Merchandise Management ->Enter Employee Number and Password -> Select Maintenance from the Merchandise Management Menu -> Min – Max-> Select Generate a Non-STAR Order Worksheet from the Min – Max Maintenance Options Menu.

Note: A worksheet of products to be ordered will generate based on the Min-Max levels and the current inventory levels for Non-STAR Order products. The products on the worksheet are grouped by vendor and can be either displayed or printed.
