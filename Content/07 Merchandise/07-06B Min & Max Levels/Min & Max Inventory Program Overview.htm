Min & Max Inventory Program Overview

Sherwin-Williams utilizes a Min/Max Inventory Program as part of its Store Level Replenishment (SLR) system.  A Min/Max program establishes an inventory level threshold that will automatically include an item on a Suggested Star Order once the Min level has been reached. The Suggested Star Order will include a quantity up to the Max level on the Purchase Order.

There are many benefits to the Min/Max Inventory System:

  • The process is automated, saving time when placing orders
  • Helps keep store costs down by reducing excess inventory
  • Greater item availability for selling
  • Prevents stores from accidentally omitting items on orders

Establishing and maintaining accurate Min/Max and Perpetual Inventory levels is key to Sherwin’s Inventory Optimization program. This program is a joint effort by both Store Managers and the Stores Operations Team in Cleveland. The Stores Operation Team in Cleveland will review and adjust the Min/Max levels at each store based on dramatic changes in demand either up or down. Managers are encouraged to contact their Division Inventory System Analyst with questions. Make the necessary min/max adjustments required to maintain Trademark Customer Service levels in your store while also remaining cognizant of expense containment obligations. When making a min/max adjustment, enter a reason code. This is intended to provide real-time market intelligence to the Stores Operations team in Cleveland to ensure accurate store level sales forecasts for the product.  The more information provided the better the Store Operations team's ability to take the action required to ensure your input is utilized.

Good communication between the two groups ensures accurate levels to meet customer needs and minimize excess inventory costs. These levels are set by looking at historical sales trends at the store SKU level with more weight given to current sales trends.

Stores have the flexibility to:

  • Set or change Min/Max levels based on need
  • Create a Memo Demand
  • Place an Emergency Order
  • Adjust order quantities on a PO
  • Import outside buys suggested on the Non-STAR worksheet into a PO
