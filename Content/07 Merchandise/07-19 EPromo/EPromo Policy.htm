EPromo Policy

  1. The completion of an ePromo form is required for all merchandise given to a customer totaling $500 or more.
  2. All Store Expense Transactions, including Promos, FCAs, and other expenses,  over $500 require Management Approval using the eAccounting System. This approval must be obtained in advance for all expenses, regardless of the reason. Once approved, the eAccounting system will provide a valid, approved six digit number to process the Store Expense Transaction.
