Promotional Expenses Procedure

Step

Action

1

Select Automotive Promo (0515-125) from Store Expense Menu.

Note: Only items on the Sherwin-Williams Approved Equipment List and approved paint will hit the Promo line on the P&L.  When prompted, enter the approved ePromo number.  Automotive Accounting will track all promo numbers.  Store Expenses less than $500 do not require an ePromo, or a six digit approval number, but do require Branch Manager & Area Sales Manager approval.

2

Enter Customer Account information in the BillTo and ShipTo fields.

3

Input all merchandise given to the customer and accept the transaction.

Note: This process can also begin in Customer Order Entry and be brought into the Store Expense transaction.

4

Retain a copy of the transaction in the customer’s file, along with the approved ePromo information and signed Mixing Equipment Lease Agreement and/or Consignment Agreement and Consignment Inventory Proposal Form.
