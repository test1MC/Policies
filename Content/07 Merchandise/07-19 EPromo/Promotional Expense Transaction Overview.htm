Promotional Expense Transaction Overview

After being notified that the ePromo has been approved, a Mixing Equipment Lease Agreement must be signed by the customer for all equipment. Also, a Consignment Agreement must be signed for consigned inventory, followed by a store expense transaction when the merchandise is given to the customer.  Find the Mixing Equipment Lease Agreement form in the Forms Repository by searching for the form name or keyword “mixing” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.
