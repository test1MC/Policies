Create an EPromo Procedure

Step

Action

1

From SOURCE hompage -> Automotive tab -> Quick Links -> eAccounting.

2

Click on the ePromo tab.

3

Click on the New button.

4

Click Yes when asked to confirm.

Note: A Promotional Approval number will be automatically assigned to the document at this time.  You will need to know this number when you process the store expense.

5

Complete all the applicable fields. All items (equipment, AP, selling aids i.e. color tools, prospectors, etc) given to the customer should be listed on the ePromo, and paint should be entered as one lump sum on the Paint Investment line.

Note:  Only equipment on the Sherwin-Williams Approved Equipment List shown on the ePromo template will be amortized over 36 months.  Any items not on the list (located on SOURCE -> Automotive  -> Operations -> eAccounting -> ePromo) that are included in the ePromo will be charged to the following accounts in the month expensed:

  • The amortization of items on the Sherwin-Williams Approved Equipment List (amortized over 36 months) and approved paint (all in the month of the expense) will hit Line 34 Promo on the P&L (3890-301Line 34-MGN ADJ-PROMO EXPENSE).
  • Non-approved equipment and AP will hit Line 63 Samples 7300-001.
  • Other non-approved AP will hit Line 64 Selling Aids 7304-302.

6

Click Add/View Notes  to add notes that detail the reasoning/justification for the promo.

7

A Consignment Inventory Proposal Form must be completed and attached to the ePromo for approval. Find the Proposed Consignment Inventory Epromo form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “epromo” or “proposed” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

Note: For procedures on consignment requests, see Request for Consignment Agreement Procedure. 

8

Save or Submit the ePromo.

Note: The ePromo application times out after 15 minutes, so you must save your document if working in the application longer than 15 minutes, otherwise your work will not be saved.

Note: Approval will automatically be routed according to the total dollar of the promo. Once the ePromo has been approved, an email will be automatically sent to the Branch, notifying them of the approval.  The Branch should follow the link to review and print off the approved ePromos and notes/attachments.  The approved ePromo and email should be printed off and filed in the customer’s file.
