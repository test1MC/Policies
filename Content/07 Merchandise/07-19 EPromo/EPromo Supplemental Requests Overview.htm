EPromo Supplemental Requests Overview

In the event additional equipment and/or paint or consignment needs to be placed with a customer subsequent to the original placement, a supplemental ePromo request will need to be submitted if the following criterion is met:

  • Within one year of the original placement
  • Total dollar amount of the supplemental request is greater than $500

To create the supplemental request, follow the same steps as creating the original ePromo.  All additional equipment given to the customer should be entered on the ePromo, and the Supplemental Request section near the bottom left of the form will need to be completed.  The original approved promotional number and amount of the original promo will need to be entered in this area.  Approval will be routed according to the total amount of the original and supplemental request.

Note: If the ePromo is more than 1 year from the original request, a new ePromo should be created (non-supplemental).
