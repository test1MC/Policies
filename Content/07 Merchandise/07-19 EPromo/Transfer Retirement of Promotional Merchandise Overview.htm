Transfer / Retirement of Promotional Merchandise Overview

If the event-placed promotional merchandise is removed from a shop with the intent of transferring to another shop, segregate the equipment until ready to transfer and tag it with the original store expense transaction information.  If the equipment remains in the shop at inventory, do not count the used equipment in the physical inventory count.

Once the equipment is ready to be transferred to another shop, complete an ePromo for approval to the transfer location.  Once approval is obtained, complete the Promotional Merch Transfer/Retirement and fax to Automotive Accounting at 216-830-2064.  Find the Promotional Merch Transfer form in the Forms Repository. By searching for the form name or keyword “transfer retirement” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word(s) will appear below in the form description field.

No store expense should be processed when tG/Lhe equipment is brought back to the branch or put in another shop.

In the event promotional merchandise is removed from the shop, and the equipment may no longer be used, complete the Promotional Merch Transfer/Retirement to scrap the equipment and fax to Automotive Accounting at 216-830-2064. The equipment may then be disposed of.  No store expense transaction needs to be processed.
