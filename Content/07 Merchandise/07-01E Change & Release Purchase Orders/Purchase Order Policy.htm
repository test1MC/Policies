Purchase Order Policy

  1. The Store Manager must verify the accuracy of all Purchase Orders.
  2. The Purchase Order should be completed and released the same day.
  3. Someone with at least an MMS Security level 2 is required to release the Purchase Order.
  4. A Once it has been released, a Purchase Order cannot be changed in the system. Before it has been released, employees can Change or Cancel a Purchase Order Procedure.
  5. Unreleased Purchase Orders will remain on the store computer system indefinitely.
