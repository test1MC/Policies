Cancel a Purchase Order Policy

  1. The vendor must be called prior to canceling the Purchase Order on the system.
  2. A member of the management staff is able to cancel a Purchase Order before the Purchase Order is received.
  3. The Store Manager must see that the vendor is notified and Purchase Orders are canceled, if applicable.
  4. Purchase Orders cannot be canceled once the order is received.
  5. A canceled Purchase Order cannot be reactivated; therefore, ensure the merchandise will not be shipped before canceling the Purchase Order.
