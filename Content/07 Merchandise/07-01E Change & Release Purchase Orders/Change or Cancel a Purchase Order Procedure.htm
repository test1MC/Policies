Change or Cancel a Purchase Order Procedure

Step

Action
Change a Purchase Order

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Orders.

2

Make any necessary additions or corrections. Press Accept [F1].

3

Answer Y to release the Purchase Order.

Note: Someone with at least an MMS Security level 2 is required to release the Purchase Order. 

Cancel a Purchase Order

1

Call the vendor to verify that the merchandise has not been shipped.

If

Then

Merchandise has not been shipped

Proceed to Step 2.

Merchandise has been shipped

Receive the merchandise against the purchase order. Process a chargeback if you decide to return the merchandise to the vendor.

Note: Cancel the Purchase Order only after confirming that the merchandise has not been shipped. Do not cancel the order if it has already been shipped.

2

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Orders -> Cancel Unnecessary Purchase Order.

3

Select the correct Purchase order.

4

Press OK and Yes when prompted to terminate the Purchase Order.

Note: If unable to change a released Purchase Order,  issue a new one for any additional items and process an IBAR or Chargeback as necessary to return any unwanted merchandise.
