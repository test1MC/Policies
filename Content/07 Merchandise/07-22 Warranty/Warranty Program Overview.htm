Warranty Program Overview

This policy is only available within the South Western Division

This policy has been established specifically to address situations where warranties are being requested for non-label warranties. Presenting an extended warranty to a customer exposes Sherwin-Williams to increased liabilities. Therefore, it is important to understand the policy and abide by all issues relating to:

  • Pre-approval
  • Structuring of the warranty
  • Completeness of specifications
  • Completion of a master file of materials relating to the specific job

This chart is intended to provide a general guideline for the length of warranty available for a specific product/system. The actual length of any warranty will depend on many factors including:

  • Current condition of the substrate
  • Primer being used and
  • Attributes being warranted 

All non-label warranties must be pre-approved  by the District Manager and Rob Mehnert.
