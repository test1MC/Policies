Extended Warranty Types Overview

Types of Extended Warranties available from the South Western Division with proper approval:

Warranty Type

Description

Elastomeric Coatings

 

New Structures / Initial Painting
  • New structures using ConFlex XL and/or SherLastic products.
  • The length of this warranty for above-grade, vertical surfaces varies based on the number of coats and the current condition of the substrate.
  • While this warranty will cover the cost of labor, the maximum exposure is limited to the original cost of the materials.
  • These warranties do have other terms and conditions.
  • Contact the District Manager when an Elastomeric Project requires a written warranty.

Existing Structures / Repainting
  • Offer a limited warranty on SherLastic Elastomeric product and on ConFlex XL.
  • The length of this warranty for above-grade, vertical surfaces varies based on the number of coats and the current condition of the substrate.
  • While this warranty will cover the cost of labor, the maximum exposure is limited to the original cost of the materials.
  • These warranties do have other terms and conditions.
  • Contact the District Manager when an Elastomeric Project requires a written warranty.

Other Sherwin-Williams, Kwal, and Frazee Exterior Architectural  Coatings
  • Offer the ability to structure warranties to meet the needs of specific jobs.
  • All warranties will be written by the Marketing Department based on the information provided through the procedures section.

Protective & Marine
  • Offer the ability to structure warranties on Protective & Marine products when they are used on architectural type projects.
  • Sherwin-Williams may require the participation of a P & M Sales Rep on the project.
  • Requests will be reviewed on a project by project basis.
  • Follow the procedures section.

Projects where Protective & Marine products are used for corrosion control, secondary containment, or other typical “heavy end” projects

  • Must be submitted through procedures established in the Protective & Marine Warranty Policy by the local P & M Sales Rep and/or P & M Sales Manager.
