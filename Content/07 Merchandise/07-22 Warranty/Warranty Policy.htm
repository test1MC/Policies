Warranty Policy

  1. No Store Manager, Sales Representative, Sales Manager, City Manager, or District Manager has the authority to extend, offer, or write a warranty not expressly offered on the label of the product being used on a project.
  2. An employee may restate a warranty extended on the label of the can in a letter to a customer. If requested, simply restate the entire warranty verbiage from the can. Do not make any changes or omit information.
  3. Products that offer Lifetime Warranties are for single family residential properties only.  These statements expressly void the Lifetime Warranty when these products are used on any commercial property (i.e., Apartments, Shopping Centers, etc.) or by any commercial entity (i.e., HOA’s).
  4. All non-label based warranties must be approved and issued by the Southwestern Division Marketing Department. This approval must occur prior to the beginning of the project. 
  5. No warranties for National Account projects (i.e., Walmart, Costco, etc.) should be considered as pre-approved by the District Manager and the Marketing Department.
  6. Occasions for proposing an extended warranty:
    1. At the request of the property owner. If the owner requests an extended material or labor warranty, collect details and speak in general terms. Do not commit to anything. All warranties must be pre-approved by the District Manager and the Marketing Department.
    2. To meet a specific warranty issued by our competition.
