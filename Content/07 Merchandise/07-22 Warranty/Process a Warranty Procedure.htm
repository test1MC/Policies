Process a Warranty Procedure

Step Action

1

Contact the District Manager to discuss the customer’s needs.

2
If Then

District Manager supports the potential warranty.
  • Visit the job site with the District Manager or Sales Manager.
  • Complete a Site Visit Worksheet located in the forms repository.

Note:  The exact street address of the project and the full legal name of the owner are required. The District Manager or the Sales Manager must sign the Site Visit Worksheet.

3

Complete a Price Quotation form for the project.

4

Write the Paint Specifications for the project and provide the following:

  • Surface Preparation
  • Weather condition limitations
  • Wet Film and Dry Film thickness requirements
  • A sound painting system

Note: SherSpec does not include all of this data. If using SherSpec, edit the information to ensure it includes the items listed.

5

Forward a copy of the Paint Spec, the Price Quote, and the Site Visit Worksheet (retain a copy) via fax: 214-553-2988 or email: rwmehnert@sherwin.com.

6

The Marketing Department will review the documents. If approved, the signed Site Visit Worksheet will be returned to the District Manager.

7

Present the Building Owner and the Contractor/Applicator with a copy of the Specification. It is the owner’s responsibility to ensure that the specification is followed.

8

Periodically observe the application of paint to verify that the specification is being followed. Aside from periodic job site visits, our warranty clearly states that is the responsibility of the owner to verify that the painter is following the written specification. Do not provide or sign any type of inspection reports for the painter or owner.

Note:  Any discrepancies to the specification should be reported immediately to the Marketing Department.

9

At the completion of the job, perform a final walk through of the project. If the job appears to have been finished satisfactorily, complete the Request for Execution of Warranty form located in the Forms Repository. Attach a Sales by Territory/Customer Product report detailing all sales on the project, copies of all invoices, and forward to the Marketing Department.
