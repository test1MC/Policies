Emergency Purchase Orders Procedure

Step

Action

1

Verify stock availability in the DSC by using My Paint Connection Processes Procedure.

If

Then

Merchandise is not available

Check other DSCs for stock availability.

Stock is available in another DSC and you still wish to place the Emergency Order

Follow the Out of Territory Order process.

2

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu - > Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Add New Purchase Order.

3

Move to the Order Detail screen and enter the Sales Numbers and Quantities required. Press Accept [F1].

Note: Specific batch requests must be indicated this information at the Line Item Comments [F9] level. Stores must also call Customer Service to verify batch requests. If no phone call is made to customer service, no guarantee can be made that the requested batch will be received in the store.  For more information, see Order Text.

4

Enter the Estimated Receipt Date.

Note: When prompted to enter the Special Instructions, you may do so if your order requires any special handling. This information may include, but is not limited to, information such as order delivery date/time, phone calls required to schedule delivery, specific LTL carrier, pallet jack required, lift gate required, etc.  These instructions must also be communicated to Customer Service via phone.  For more information, see Order Text.

5

Once the order information is complete, release the order.  

Note: If your order is successfully released, you will be returned to the Purchase Order Menu.  If your order has not been successfully released, you will see an error message.  Follow the EDI Downtime Procedure.
