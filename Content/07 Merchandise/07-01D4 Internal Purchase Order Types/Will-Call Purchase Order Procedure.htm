Will-Call Purchase Order Procedure

Step

Action

1

Verify stock availability in the DSC by using My Paint Connection Processes Procedure by calling Automotive Customer Service [Auto: 1-800-357-4956].

If

Then

Merchandise is not available

The Will-Call order cannot be placed.

Order will be picked up by the customer at the DSC

Process a deposit via a Correction Transaction. The deposit amount for Will-Call Orders is payment in full. No partial deposits are permitted.

 

2

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Add New Purchase Order.

3

Select Will-Call Order from the Order Type List.

Note: The "A" Purchase Order Number will be automatically generated.  If the Purchase Order was created during downtime using the B-Prefix Manual Purchase Order form, please override this number with the "B" Purchase Order Number.

4

Select the Customer Number and include Deposit Terminal and Transaction information for the customer. Press Enter.

Note: The Customer Purchase Order Number may also be indicated if required.

5

Enter the required Sales Numbers and Quantities in the Order Details. Press Accept [F1].

Note: Specific batch requests must be indicated this information at the Line Item Comments [F9] level. Stores must also call Customer Service to verify batch requests. If no phone call is made to Customer Service, no guarantee can be made that the requested batch will be received in the store. For more information, see Order Text.  Automotive Branches, please refer to Automotive Special Blend Order.

6

Edit the Pickup Date and Pickup Time, if necessary.

Note: The Pickup Date will default to today's date and the Pickup Time to four and a half [4.5] hours from now. You may adjust both these values; however, the Pickup Time must be at least four and a half hours from the current time. Automotive Customer Service [Auto: 1-800-357-4956] must be called to arrange a pickup time for the Will Call Order.

7

Instructions for special handling must be included in Special Instructions. This information may include, but, is not limited to, information such as order delivery date/time, phone calls required to schedule delivery, specific LTL carrier, pallet jack required, lift gate required, etc.  Stores must also communication this information to Customer Service via phone. 

Note: You MUST include the Customer Phone Number in the Special Instructions area of the Purchase Order. If the order is for Store, indicate the Store Phone Number in this area. For more information, see Order Text. Contact Automotive Customer Service [Auto: 1-800-357-4956] with any Special Instructions.

8

Once the order information is complete, release the order.  If your order is successfully released, you will be returned to the Purchase Order Menu.  If not, follow the EDI Downtime Procedure.

9

Once the store has picked up the merchandise or the DSC has verified that the customer has picked up the merchandise, the Will-Call should be received in SOURCE through MMS.

10

If the Will-Call was for a customer pick-up and this has been verified with the DSC, process the Sale Transaction to apply the deposit to the customer account and include the following information:

  • Customer Name and Address in the Bill To section,
  • A notation stating "Customer Direct Shipment" in the Special Instructions.

Indicate who ordered the material when capturing signature information in the Sale Transaction (only required for charge transactions) and mail the customer copy of the computer generated receipt.
