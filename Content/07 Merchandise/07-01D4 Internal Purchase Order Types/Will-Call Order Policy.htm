Will-Call Order Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

The Will-Call Order allows for stores to place orders directly with the DSC for store or customer pickup. No call to Customer Service is required unless you are requesting a special batch or you require a pick-up earlier than4.5 hours from the time the order is released in POS.

  1. Will-Call orders have an order size limit of 5 order lines or less and require Date and Time entry indicating when the order will be picked up at the DSC.
  2. There is a 4.5 hour lead time for Will-Call Orders. This means that orders placed for Will-Call will not be ready for pickup for 4.5 hours from the time the order is released in SOURCE.
    1. South Western Division stores – Will-Call Orders must be approved by the District or District Financial Manager prior to being placed.
  3. The order must be received by the DSC by 4PM EST for Same Day pickup. Orders must be picked up at the DSC by 9PM EST.
  4. Will-Call orders must be picked up by either Store personnel or the customer directly. If a store wishes to send in a carrier to pick up the order, the order is not a Will-Call order. The store must place an Emergency Order, call Customer Service, and pay for the freight or the shipment will ship on the normal LTL trucks.
  5. Will-Call Orders being picked up by customers require a deposit-in-full.  Partial deposits are not permitted.

Note: These policies do not apply to the Duron Atlanta SDC process.
