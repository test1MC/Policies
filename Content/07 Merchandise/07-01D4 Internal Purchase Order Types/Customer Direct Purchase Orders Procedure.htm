Customer Direct Purchase Orders Procedure

Step

Action

1

Verify stock availability in the DSC by using My Paint Connection Overview.

If

Then

Merchandise is not available
  • Call [1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364 / Auto: 1-800-357-4956] Customer Service and inquire when the stock is expected.
  • If the customer is willing to wait for stock to become available and would still would like to place the order, proceed with the Customer Direct procedure.

Request product from another DSC

Proceed with the Customer Direct Order as an Out-of-Territory shipment.  Call Customer Service immediately after placing the order to ensure correct shipment. For more information, see Out of Territory Order.

2

Process the deposit via a Correction Transaction.

Note: The deposit amount for Customer Direct Orders is payment in full. These funds must be guaranteed before the purchase order may be placed with the DSC. If the customer has a Sherwin-Williams Charge Account in good standing, you will not be required to take a deposit.  If unsure of the credit status of the account, contact your District Manager for verification. If a partial deposit is provided, ship the merchandise to the store when creating the Customer Direct Purchase Order.

3

Access Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order
-> Add New Purchase Order.

4

Select Customer Direct Order from the Order Type List.

Note: The "A" Purchase Order Number will be automatically generated. If the Purchase Order was created during downtime using the B-Prefix Manual Purchase Order form, override this number with the "B" Purchase Order Number.

5

Select the Customer Number for the customer and include the Customer Phone Number in the Ship To Address area.

Note: If the customer has not paid for the merchandise in full, the Customer Address and Phone Number on the order must be that of the store. The store will receive the shipment.

6

Enter the Deposit Terminal and Transaction information. Press Enter.

Note: If requested, input the Customer Purchase Order Number.

7

Enter the required Sales Numbers and Quantities in the Order Detail screen. Press Accept [F1].

Note: Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, MAB Paints, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores must indicate specific batch requests in the Line Item Comments [F9]. Stores must also call Customer Service to verify batch requests. If no phone call is made, no guarantee can be made that the requested batch will be received in the store. For more information, see Order Text.  Automotive Branches, please refer to Automotive Special Blend Order .

8

Instructions for special handling must be included in Special Instructions. This information may include, but is not limited to, information such as order delivery date/time, phone calls required to schedule delivery, specific LTL carrier, pallet jack required, lift gate required, etc.  Stores must also communicate special instructions to Customer Service via phone. 

9

Once the order information is complete, release the order.

Note: If your order is successfully released, you will be returned to the Purchase Order Menu.  If not, you will see an error message and must follow the EDI Downtime Procedure.

10

Once the DSC has verified shipment of the merchandise to the customer, process the Sale Transaction to apply the deposit to the customer account. You must include the following information:

  • Customer Name and Address in the Bill To section,
  • A notation stating "Customer Direct Shipment" in the Special Instructions.

Note: If the customer only provided a partial deposit, call the customer once the merchandise is received at the store. Process the Sale Transaction with the customer present. Apply the deposit and process the remaining owed amount for the merchandise.

11

Have the customer sign the computer generated receipt, if present (only required for charge transactions). If the customer is not present at the time of billing, indicate who ordered the material when capturing signature information in the Sale Transaction and mail the customer copy of the receipt.
