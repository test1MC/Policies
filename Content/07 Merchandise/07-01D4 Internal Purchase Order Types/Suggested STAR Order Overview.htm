Suggested STAR Order Overview

  • If the maximum level is less than one of the minimum ship quantities, all items on the Suggested STAR Purchase Order that have Memo Demand and/or a previous on-order quantity are placed at the bottom of the Suggested STAR Purchase Order. It is important to view the entire Suggested STAR Order and check for any zero quantities. All of these quantities should fall to the bottom because they would have been previously ordered but not received. Press VIEW [F7] to scroll through Memo Demands, on order, etc. for each product. 
  • If the product has a Minimum Ship quantity associated with it, the nearest multiple of that Minimum Ship quantity, without exceeding the original suggested quantity, is used. If the original suggested quantity is smaller than the Minimum Ship quantity then the Minimum Ship quantity is used instead. After this calculation is done, the quantity is adjusted for Twos, Fives and Drums based on the product size code to the nearest factor of 2, 5, or 55 respectively. This finally gives the number that is placed on the STAR order for that product.
  • You may add products to your Suggested STAR by typing in the Sales Number and quantity desired at the end of the order.

If

Then

The sum of the On Hand quantity and the On Order quantity exceeds the minimum in stock quantity

Order nothing for the store stock. If there is any outstanding memo demand then the memo demand quantity is ordered.

The sum of the On Hand quantity and the On Order quantity does not exceed the minimum in stock quantity

Order only the amount that, when added to what is currently On Hand and currently On Order, will total the Maximum in stock limit. If there is any outstanding memo demand, then that quantity is added to the total.

Note: In the event that the On Order quantity falls below zero [0], which is normal in the midst of the receiving process, the On Order quantity is not considered in the above calculations.
