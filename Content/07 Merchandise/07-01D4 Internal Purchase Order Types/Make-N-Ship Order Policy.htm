Make-N-Ship Order Policy

The Make-N-Ship Order is available off the Order Types List for Purchase Orders. The Make-N-Ship Order allows for stores to place Make-N-Ship orders with the Plant.  Arch/IM stores should contact their Division Reps for Make-N-Ship orders at 1-800-797-8010.

  1. Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, MAB Paints, General Polymers, SW Paint Stores:  Make-N-Ship Orders require special fill/label information at the Line Item COMMENTS [F9] level. Customer Service will not know specific requests unless the store has entered them correctly using Line Item COMMENTS [F9] for product specific requests.  Automotive Branches, please see  Automotive Special Blend Order for additional information.
  2. Make-N-Ship orders require accurate Ship To Address, and Ship To Phone Number information.
  3. Make-N-Ship Orders will be stopped for review prior to release to Manufacturing.
  4. Make-N-Ship Orders must be released by 1pm EST for all warehouses.
  5. Automotive Branches ONLY. Call Automotive Customer Service [1-859-623-3200] to verify order requirements.
