Emergency Order Policy

  1. Emergency need orders have a limit of five order lines and five hundred gallons.
  2. Spray Equipment should NOT be placed on an Emergency Order. If Spray Equipment is included on an Emergency Order, it will be shipped with the next regularly scheduled STAR Order.
  3. Emergency Orders must be placed by the following cut-off times for same day shipment:

    Waco

    12:00PM

    Sierra

    2:00PM
  4. Orders received after cut-off will ship the next day. As well, orders over the limit of five order lines and five hundred gallons will automatically ship the next day even if the order is received before cut-off.
  5. Be aware that your store may be assessed a fee for placing an Emergency Order for emergency need. This fee will not be assessed when placing an Emergency Order for Out of Territory, DOT, or Marine orders. All stores with a single STAR per week will receive one free Emergency Order per month.  Additional Emergency Orders in the same month will be charged a fee of $150 per order.
