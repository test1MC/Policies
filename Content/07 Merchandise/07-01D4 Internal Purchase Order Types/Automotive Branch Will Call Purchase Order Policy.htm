Automotive Branch Will Call Purchase Order Policy

This section does not apply to Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, MAB Paints, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores

  1. Will Call Orders require Date and Time entry indicating when the order will be picked up at the DSC.
  2. Automotive Customer Service [Auto: 1-800-357-4956] must be called to arrange a pickup time for the Will Call Order.
  3. Will Call orders must be picked up by either Store personnel or the customer directly. If a store wishes to send in a carrier to pick up the order, the order is not a Will Call order. The store must place an Emergency Order, call Customer Service, and pay for the freight or the shipment will ship on our normal LTL trucks.
  4. Will Call Orders being picked up by customers require a deposit-in-full or a charge account in good standing. Partial deposits are not permitted.
