Fulfill ISR Purchase Orders Procedure

Step

Action

1

Select IST from the Special Menu.

2

Select Yes and press Enter to the prompt Select a Transfer Order?

3

Select the order and products to transfer from the All Active Transfer Order(s) menu. Press ACCEPT [F1].

Note: The Receiving Store and Inter-Store Request Number will automatically populate. The products listed to be transferred will appear in the window.

4

Select Y to begin the process the Bill of Lading paperwork.

5

Complete the Bill of Lading and Store Delivery procedures.

Note: The only scheduling option for ISTs and ISRs is Anytime.
