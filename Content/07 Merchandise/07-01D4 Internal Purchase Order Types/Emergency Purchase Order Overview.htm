Emergency Purchase Order Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

The Emergency Order will be used for fulfilling emergency need orders, Out-of-Territory Orders shipped directly to the store, Arch/IM Marine Orders, DOT Orders, CC Auxiliary Warehouse Orders, and KST Special Pricing orders.  Please refer to the sections on Out-of-Territory Orders, Marine Orders, DOT Orders, KST Orders and CC Auxiliary Warehouse Orders for specifics on creating these orders.  Normally, the Emergency Order for emergency need should be a sold order to fulfill an immediate special need for a customer.  If placing an Emergency Order becomes a regular occurrence, Min/Max Levels should be reviewed and adjusted appropriately to handle the increased demand.  No call to Customer Service is necessary for Emergency Orders to fulfill emergency need.  Calls to Customer Service are required when the Emergency Order is being used for an Out-of-Territory, Marine, or DOT order.
