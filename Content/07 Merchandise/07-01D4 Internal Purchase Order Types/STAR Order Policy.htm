STAR Order Policy

  1. STAR Orders must be released by the end of the scheduled order day. The STAR punch schedule must be adhered to. DSC cut-off times for orders and order types remain in effect with EDI processing.
  2. STAR Orders should be created on the SOURCE system on the scheduled order date. This way, current inventory levels will be taken into consideration and the most accurate Suggested STAR can be created.
  3. All order quantities must be placed in multiples of the minimum shipment (Min Ship) quantity.
  4. A Memo Demand should be used to record specific customer demand for a product.

    5. If

    Then

    Order a sales numbered product that is not on your store database

    The store computer system will connect with Headquarters and search the product database for that product.

    The product cannot be found

    You will not be able to order the product on the STAR order. An External Purchase Order is required if you choose to order the product. Contact Store Merchandise Billing at 216-566-2533 if it is a Sherwin-Williams manufactured product with no sales number or if the product is invalid.

    The product is found on the database at Headquarters

    It will be added to your store's product database after selecting the product and pressing ENTER.
  5. At Headquarters, the unit of measure, order quantity, and cost are verified against the product database to confirm the order information.
  6. In-Stock Wallcovering, 3M products [Automotive ONLY], and Wooster Products (MAB ONLY) placed on the STAR Purchase Order will be removed from the STAR Order and placed on an "E" prefix Purchase Order, which will be forwarded to the vendor.  For more information, see In-Stock Wallcovering , 3M Orders, or Wooster Orders.
  7. Add to Star Orders no longer exist. If necessary, a small additional Standard STAR Order may be placed on the day after the punch day for the STAR Order. This additional STAR order must be placed by the appropriate cut-off time for your DSC:
