STAR Order Overview

Scheduled Transfer and Replenishment [STAR] Orders are to be used for creating the weekly replenishment order(s) from the DSC. Once released, the STAR order will immediately be routed to the DSC through EDI. Stores may create and release as many STAR Orders as required per scheduled order date. On your STAR Order day you will see the following status message in the black and yellow Status Box:  Remember to place your STAR Order today. If you have not placed it before you close your main point of sale you will see the following message: A STAR Order has not been placed today. Do you want to place a STAR Order now? Y or N.

After placing your STAR Order, reference STAR Order Pending & Cancelled Lines Overview to verify which ordered products may not ship.

Note: If your store is located in the Eastern Division, please refer to both this STAR Order information and the special Eastern Division STAR Order information.
