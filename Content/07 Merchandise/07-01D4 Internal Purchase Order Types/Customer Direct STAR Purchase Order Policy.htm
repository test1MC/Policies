Customer Direct STAR Purchase Order Policy

The Direct STAR Purchase Order has been renamed the Customer Direct Order. Merchandise shipped directly to the Customer from the DSC should be requested by placing a Customer Direct Purchase Order. No call to Customer Service is necessary unless requesting special instructions, a special batch, or creating a Customer Direct Order for Out-of-Territory shipment.

  1. Customer Direct orders must meet the requirements of either forty-eight [48] gallons or a total store cost of $250.00. Please note that this gallonage / cost policy does NOT apply to Automotive Branches.
  2. Customer Direct orders may only be placed if the merchandise has been paid for in full via a deposit processed through the Correction Transaction in Point-of-Sale or if the customer has a Sherwin Williams Charge Account in good standing as determined by the District Credit Office.  If unsure of the credit status of the account, contact the District Credit Manager for verification.
  3. Deposit funds must be guaranteed before the purchase order may be placed with the warehouse, i.e. checks must clear the bank and SW credit accounts must be in good standing.
  4. The actual Sale transaction may not be processed until the store has received verification of shipment from the DSC. Sherwin-Williams is not permitted to pre-bill customers for merchandise. The merchandise must either be received by the customer or the store must have verification of shipment from the DSC.
  5. The Ship To Address must be included on the Customer Direct Order. Phone Number information for the shipping destination is also required so that the carrier may call the consignee.
  6. If the customer has not paid for the merchandise in full, shipment may not be made directly the customer.  The merchandise must be shipped to the store.
  7. Customer Direct Orders must be placed by the following cut-off times for same day shipment:
