Place ISR Purchase Orders Procedure

An ISR Purchase Order is automatically created through the transfer process. You will need to receive against this ISR Purchase Order once the order is transferred back to your location. Once an order has been transferred back to your location, the Transferred In number in the Black/Yellow Status Window will increase.

Step

Action

1

Use the Stock Search on SOURCE to determine what locations near you have the stock available to fulfill the request.

2

Contact the store from which you would like to receive product. Be sure to agree on the terms of the purchase order, including Cost.

3

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Customer Order Entry -> Add Stock Transfer Order [ISR/IST].

4

Enter the product[s], quantity and required date.

Note:  If the product needs to be tinted, include the color information.

5

Enter Special Instructions, if necessary and select Print Review Options.

6

Transfer the ISR to the servicing store:

From the Order Entry Menu, select Transfer Order.

7

Narrow search in the Order Lookup Window or press ACCEPT [F1].

8

Highlight the correct order and press Enter.

9

Enter the store number of the transfer location. Press ACCEPT [F1].

10

Choose YES when prompted, Transfer this order?

Note: Once the transfer function has been completed, the originating location no longer has control of the order and changes cannot be made. Orders with the status In Update may be orders that have been transferred.
