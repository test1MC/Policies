Inter-Store Request Policy

An Inter Store Request (ISR) Purchase Order is a request to receive merchandise from another Sherwin-Williams location, which often allows for easier and faster results. ISR Purchase Orders are not sent via EDI to the transferring store. For information on creating an ISR to request a special Product Finishes Blend, please see CC Blend ISR. For information on creating an ISR to request an Automotive Special Blend, please see Automotive Special Blend Order.  

  1. An ISR must be processed anytime you choose to get merchandise from another Sherwin-Williams store.
  2. Merchandise may not be transferred between stores without the agreement of both participating Store Managers or specific instructions from the District Manager.
  3. Class Numbers and Sales Numbers can be entered several times on an ISR after specifically identifying them with a unique descriptor.
  4. You will be required to enter a cost for any merchandise purchase vendor direct on an ISR. Don't leave the cost at zero (0.00) on this merchandise. It is critical that the sending and receiving store employees agree upon a cost when completing the ISR and IST, which will print on outside buy purchased products if entered.
  5. An ISR Purchase Order needs to be created for products requested from each shipping store. The system generated ISR Purchase Order Number must be communicated to the shipping store for documentation (via fax), as this information is included in creation of the Inter Store Transfer [IST] transaction. You are not required to retain a manual copy of the ISR. The store computer system will match the ISR to the IST.  For additional information on the IST process, please refer to Inter Store Transfer (IST) Overview.
  6. Once the merchandise is at your location, you must receive the merchandise against the ISR in order to properly adjust the inventory.
  7. Include any freight or shipping charges. Freight charges are allocated back to the unit cost for WAC-costed products only. If the product being transferred is a standard cost product, the freight charges will be expensed.
  8. The shipping store is responsible for completing the Bill of Lading process.
