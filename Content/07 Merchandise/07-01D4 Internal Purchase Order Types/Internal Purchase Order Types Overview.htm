Internal Purchase Order Types Overview

All internal purchase order types can be accessed from the SOURCE desktop through the Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Add New Purchase Order. All employees will be able to and edit unreleased internal purchase orders; but, only employees with an MMS security level of two through four will be able to release internal purchase orders to the DSC.

Note: The perpetual inventory and inventory cutoffs of Internal Purchase Orders older than 24 hours are directly affected by improper receiving techniques.
