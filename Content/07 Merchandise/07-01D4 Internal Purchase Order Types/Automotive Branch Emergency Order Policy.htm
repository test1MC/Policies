Automotive Branch Emergency Order Policy

This section does not apply to Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, MAB Paints, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores

  1. Emergency Orders must be sold orders. Emergency Orders will be handled by the DSC separately from scheduled STAR Order. Emergency Orders are necessary if there is a special order for a customer. If emergency orders become a regular occurrence, the Min/Max requirements should be increased to avoid emergency orders.
  2. Stores should contact the Automotive Customer Service [Auto: 1-800-357-4956] to alert them of the Emergency Order.
  3. Automotive Branches are assessed the entire freight charge for Emergency shipments.
  4. Emergency Orders placed by 12:00PM EST will ship the next day. Orders placed after 12:00PM EST will ship the following day.
