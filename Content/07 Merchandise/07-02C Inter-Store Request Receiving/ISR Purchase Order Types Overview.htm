ISR Purchase Order Types Overview

The Inter Store Transfer (IST) point-of-sale transaction from the shipping store and the Inter Store Request (ISR) Purchase Order from the receiving store are matched at Headquarters by the ISR Purchase Order number to verify order quantities and costs and report any differences between the shipping and receiving store.

Type

Description

New

The Purchase Order has not been released.

Released

The Purchase Order is not yet received. Nothing has been received against these Purchase Orders.

In-Process

The Purchase Order has been received against or opened up during receiving but receipts have not been approved.

In-Receiving

Someone is receiving this Purchase Order on another terminal. You are not able to enter the Purchase Order while someone else is in it.

Partially Received

Some merchandise has been received and approved but there are still some open items on the Purchase Order.

Received

Everything on the Purchase Order has been received and accepted. The Purchase Order is waiting for review by the Store Manager.

Closed

Purchase Orders have gone through the entire Purchase Order process from ordering, releasing, receiving, approving and closing. Closing is performed by the Store Manager for external purchases and someone with at least LEVEL TWO security for internal Purchase Orders.
