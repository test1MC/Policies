Receive Direct Shipment Overview

If the order was placed directly with the vendor, the merchandise will be received based on the shipment information obtained at the time of the order. The sale should be processed immediately after receiving. To process, enter the sales term/tran number on the original Purchase Order. The sales transaction number will tie the sale to the direct shipment Purchase Order. Direct Shipment files should contain the following copies for audit review:

  • Received Purchase Order
  • DSC Packing Slip / Plant Invoice
  • Sales Transaction
