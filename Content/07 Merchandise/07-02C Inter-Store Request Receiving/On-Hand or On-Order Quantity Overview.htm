On-Hand or On-Order Quantity Overview

Quantity on Hand and Quantity on Order display each time you enter a sales number while processing a point-of-sale transaction.

The information shown below will display in the upper right corner of every point of sale screen. Please make sure that each employee understands the value of referencing this information while processing a transaction.

INVENTORY

Quantity on Hand:

50

Quantity on Order:

0

Instruct each employee to let you know about any negative or zero quantity inventories. (Negative or zero quantity inventories could show merchandise not received properly or incorrect sales number usage.)
