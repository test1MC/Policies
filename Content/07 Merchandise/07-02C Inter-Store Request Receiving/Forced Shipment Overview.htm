Forced Shipment Overview

A Purchase Order is downloaded from Headquarters for all forced shipments. This Purchase Order is used to receive the merchandise at your location. Locate the Purchase Order on your system and receive the order in the same manner as any order. For more information, see Purchase Order Number Overview.
