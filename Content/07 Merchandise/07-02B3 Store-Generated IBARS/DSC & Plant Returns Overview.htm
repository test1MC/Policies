DSC & Plant Returns Overview

DSC/Plant Returns provide many benefits, including:

  • Greater control over the return process
  • Scheduling with the stores and DSC so they are stocked adequately
  • Reduction of obsolescence
  • Quicker credit

Returns of products to the DSC or plant are initiated with Return IBARs. Four types of returns are available:

  • Stop Sale (Plant/DSC, Vendor, No Return)
  • Quality Return
  • Emergency Return
  • Approved Return

Stores receive credit within ten days of receipt of the product at the designated location. If unauthorized product is returned, the store will receive no credit and will be assessed freight charges, restocking charges, and a $3 per gallon handling charge.
