Concealed IBAR Policy

  1. In-process receipts must be accepted and the purchase order must be closed prior to processing a Concealed Damage / Shortage / Overage IBAR.
  2. Concealed Damage / Shortage / Overage IBARs are generated after all pallets are received and BOL has been signed and dated.
  3. The IBAR must be prepared and released within fourteen days of delivery.
    1. Any credits processed after that time will not be honored.
    2. Credit will be given within forty-eight hours of filing by the store as long as the IBAR was completed correctly.
    3. Store credit will be based on the results of audits of actual DSC shipments, store deliveries, as well as history.
  4. Any merchandise listed on a Concealed Damage / Shortage / Overage IBAR must be sold using an obsolete sales number. Do not process a sale using the product sales number. For more information, see Obsolete & Discontinued Merchandise Overview.
