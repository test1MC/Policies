Transfer Products to Non-Stores Outside of Stores Group Policy

  1. Reasons to select to transfer products via an IBAR include, but are not limited to:
    1. Transfer to Headquarters or a technical center for test product
    2. Remodeling
    3. Supplies transferred to non-store locations outside of Stores Group.
  2. Stores are permitted to specify and select a carrier for this IBAR Type.
