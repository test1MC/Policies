Return Carrier Policy

This section is for Automotive Branches, TAG Stores, and PFG Facilities only.

When an IBAR results in the return of goods to either a DSC or plant, this information will be directly communicated to Stores Customer Service (e-trac) and Sherwin-Williams' preferred common carrier (Con-way).  Stores do not need to contact a freight carrier to arrange for the return of their goods.

  1. Stores are not to arrange for pick-up of return goods with any carrier when the return is being made to a Sherwin-Williams DSC or plant.
  2. Con-way is the preferred returns carrier to either a DSC or plant.
  3. Con-way handles returns that are 5,000 lbs. or less.
  4. Returns equal to or greater than 5,000 lbs. are handled by either CTS or Con-way.
  5. Con-way and Sherwin-Williams Transportation will make the choice regarding which carrier will pick-up return goods at store locations. Typically, this carrier will be Con-way, CTS, or FedEx Ground.
  6. If a store requests a carrier to pick-up return goods, the store must use the first carrier to arrive and will be liable for charges billed by both the carrier they have contacted and the carrier arranged through Con-way or CTS.
