Approved Returns Policy

  1. Approved Returns require approval from Store Operations.  Approval must be requested using the Approved Return Request Form found in the Forms Repository.  This form is reviewed by Stores Operations who can be contacted at stores.operations@sherwin.com.
  2. The Approved Return procedure should be followed when the store would like to move product due to overstock, space restrictions, or shelf life.
  3. Store Operations reserves the right to suspend a store’s privilege to participate in this program if abused.
  4. The following products will not be approved for return to the DSC:
    1. Associated Products
    2. Discontinued Products
    3. Custom Tinted Products
  5. When a request is approved for return to the DSC, an IBAR will be downloaded on the following Friday and the store will have two weeks to complete and release the IBAR.  If the IBAR is not completed within these two weeks, the IBAR will be closed and the store will have to make another request to return the product.
  6. A copy of the completed IBAR must accompany the shipment.
  7. Standard return charges will apply for any material approved for a return to the DSC.  These charges will include, but may not be limited to:
    1. Freight Fee of 30 cents per gallon (or gallon equivalent) of paint
    2. Restocking Fee of 2x the standard pick rate
  8. Returned product must be in perfect package condition.  Credit will NOT be given for returned product not in perfect package condition.
    1. Products must be returned in original packaging. 
    2. Gallon cases must be unopened. 
    3. All packaging must be free of tears, dents, leaks, tape, markings, overspray, rust, and any and all imperfections of any kind.
  9. All products returned must have at least four months of shelf life remaining at the time the products are returned to the DSC. Credit will not be given for returned product with less than four months of shelf life remaining.
  10. Credit will not be given for unauthorized / unsaleable product and will be subject to the following fees and actions:
    1. $3 per gallon handling charge.
    2. Restocking Fee of 2x the standard pick rate.
    3. Freight Fee of 30 cents per gallon of fuel.
    4. The product will not be returned to the store.
    5. The IBAR will be voided and an entry booked to the store's shrink account.
  11. If no product is returned, no credit will be given.
