Scrap on Location Procedure

This section is for Automotive Branches and Product Finishes Facilities only.

Step

Action

Hazardous Waste Disposal Fee:

1

Process a Return IBAR with a Quality Reason Code for the paint material.

Note: If a Stop Sale Bulletin is issued, process with a Stop Sale Reason Code.

2

Dispose of the hazardous waste, if necessary.

3

Pay the invoice through SWeep.

4

Send the SWeep information to the Accounting Manager of the plant or individual who authorized the Scrap on Location.

Note: The plant will credit the branch or facility for the hazardous waste fee.
