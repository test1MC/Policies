Product Recall or Stop Sale Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> IBAR ->  Add New IBAR ->  Returns

2

Select the Reason Code indicated on the Product Recall/Stop Sale Bulletin

3

Release the IBAR.

If

Then

The Stop Sale is to the plant or DSC

An e-trac will be created and Con-way will contact the store for pick-up details.

The Stop Sale is to the vendor

An e-trac will be created and Con-way will contact the store for pick-up details.

The Stop Sale does not result in a return of product

Follow the bulletin instructions relative to product disposition.

4

Place the store copy of the IBAR in the Awaiting SPR folder and retain the driver-signed copy of the Bill-of-Lading.
