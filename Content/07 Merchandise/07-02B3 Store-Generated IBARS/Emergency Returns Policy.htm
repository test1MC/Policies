Emergency Returns Policy

  1. Stores Operations must be contacted for approval for all Emergency Returns resulting from picking, shipping, or damage errors for Architectural, IM, or CC product.
  2. Customer Service must be contacted for approval for all Emergency Returns resulting from picking, shipping, or damage errors for Automotive, Marine, or GP product.
  3. Automotive Branches:  Contact Automotive Customer Service at 1-800-357-4956 for approval for an Emergency Return.
  4. Excess product under an Emergency Return is defined as:
    1. Greater than 128 gallons of an architectural product.
    2. Greater than 80 gallons in fives of an architectural product.
    3. Any product type with very short shelf life that falls below the previously mentioned quantity guidelines.
    4. High value products of any product type that fall below the previously mentioned quantity.
