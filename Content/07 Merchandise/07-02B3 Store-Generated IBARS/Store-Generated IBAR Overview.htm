Store-Generated IBAR Overview

An Inventory Billing Adjustment Report (IBAR) is a transaction that adjusts items billed to the store. For internally purchased products, the system will automatically match received Purchase Orders to the invoice.

Several IBAR Types exist to assist stores with the rebalancing of inventory, return of product to the DSC, handling of recalled or stop-sale goods, or transfer of products to a non-store entity or location. For more information on system-generated IBARs, please see MMS IBAR Overview.

Store management employees (those with MMS Security Level 3 or Level 4) are required to address open IBAR issues.  If open IBARs exist in the system, employees will be alerted to these open items with the following message: IBARS should be RELEASED within 48 hours of delivery; however, you have up to fourteen days to release the IBAR. Store management should be aware that any credits processed after this 14 day time frame will not be honored.
