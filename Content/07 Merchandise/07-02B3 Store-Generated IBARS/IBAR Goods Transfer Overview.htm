IBAR Goods Transfer Overview

Stores may select to transfer goods to a non-store within or outside of The Americas Group, typically a non-selling location used by administrative cost centers, which should be charged to administrative expense. Benefits of using this process to transfer material are:

  • Paperless – no IBAR or supporting documentation needs to be sent to Headquarters as long as all required information is provided.
  • Faster approval, limited disapproval.
