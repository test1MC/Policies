Emergency Return Overview

The Emergency Return is used to address major store ordering errors, customer service errors, or DSC picking errors. These errors result in excess product that would cause either storage problems or major obsolescence. In addition, product that a store is not allowed to stock can be sent back to the DSC through an Emergency Return.
