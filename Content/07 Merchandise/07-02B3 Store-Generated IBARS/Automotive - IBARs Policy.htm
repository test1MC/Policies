Automotive - IBARs Policy

This section applies to Automotive Branches only.

  1. IBARs are for Branch use only. 
  2. DO NOT process an IBAR to a sales representative’s territory.
  3. Material used by a rep for a local demonstration should be store expensed to the local Branch.
  4. Material used by a rep for another Branch should be transferred to the other Branch and expensed there.
    1. If a Tech rep needs a piece of equipment essential to a job, the rep needs to receive approval from the ASM and then fax a copy of the ASM approval along with the Store Expense to Joseph Medina at 216-263-5437. The expense will be moved from the Branch to the Tech rep territory.  For questions, contact Joseph Medina at 216-566-3309.
