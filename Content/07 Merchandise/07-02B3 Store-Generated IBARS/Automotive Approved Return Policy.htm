Automotive Approved Return Policy

  1. The downloaded Approved Return IBAR will include only items and quantities allowed for the return.
  2. Associated Products will not be included in the Approved Return. These products can be returned locally, sold at a discount, etc.
  3. Recalled products must be returned separately as advised on Stop Sale Bulletins and not returned as part of an Approved Return.
  4. No quantities in excess of the downloaded product quantities can be returned and no additional products can be added to the Approved Return IBAR.
  5. Approved Returns must be processed within two weeks of the downloaded Approved Return IBAR or it will be closed and the return authorization is void.
  6. Returned product must be in perfect package condition.  Credit will not be given for returned product not in perfect package condition.
    1. Products must be returned in original packaging with the current label. 
    2. All packaging must be free of tears, dents, leaks, tape, markings, overspray, rust, and any and all imperfections of any kind.
  7. Returned product must have at least nine months of shelf life remaining at the time the products are received at the DSC. To ensure the products being returned are within shelf life when they arrive at the Richmond DSC, reference the following forms:
    1. A copy of the DSC Stocked Items with Lifecycle Status can be located on the SOURCE homepage -> Automotive -> Branch Operations.
    2. The Richmond Sorting Batch Matrix can be located in the Forms Repository.
  8. A copy of the completed IBAR must accompany the shipment.
  9. Standard return charges will apply for any material approved for a return to the DSC.  These charges will include, but may not be limited to:
    1. Restocking Fee of 2x the standard pick rate
  10. Credit will not be given for unauthorized / unsaleable product and will be subject to the following fees and actions:
    1. $3 per gallon handling charge.
    2. Restocking Fee of 2x the standard pick rate.
    3. The product will not be returned to the store.
  11. If no product is returned, no credit will be given.
  12. All products received at the DSC will be credited to the branch’s perpetual inventory. Any unsalable, obsolete, or recalled products will be charged back to the branch as a margin expense to “INV ADJ/MISTINTS/OBSOL” on line 35 of the branch P&L.
  13. Approved Return IBARs will be posted by the DSC within 30 days of receipt of the Approved Return and/or the IBAR’s availability in the DSC Etrac system. The IBARs are closed by Stores Billing Accounting for final branch credit.
