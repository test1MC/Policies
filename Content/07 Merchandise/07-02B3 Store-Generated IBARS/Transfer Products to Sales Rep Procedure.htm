Transfer Products to Sales Rep Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches. Automotive Branch employees are not permitted to transfer merchandise to a sales representative.

Step

Action

1

Process an IBAR transaction to transfer sales numbered items to Sherwin-Williams Sales Representatives.

Note: Ensure that you select IBAR Reason Code [Transfers to Non-Store within Stores Group] while processing the IBAR.

2

Enter [today's date] as the Ship Date, your division, and the cost center. Press LOOKUP [F3], if necessary. 

3

Press LOOKUP [F3] and arrow down to 7599-099, DIST & WHSE -> Enter.

4

Enter the Sales Representative Name -> Enter.

5

Enter the sales numbered merchandise and the correct quantity. Press Accept [F1].

6

Enter the reason for the IBAR in the Comments. Press Accept [F1].

7

Select Yes to release the IBAR.
