Powder Coating Customer Color Purchase Order Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

For more information on Powder Coatings Class Numbers, see .

Step

Action

When creating the Purchase Order

1

Enter one of the temporary generic powder sales numbers listed below. 

Note:  After the powder is made, a sale number is assigned

When receiving the Purchase Order

1

Make sure the temporary generic powder sales number received quantity is zero.

2

Add the new sales number to the purchase order with the actual received quantity. 

3

Enter the correct freight amount on the Purchase Order using sales number 9901-11767. Leave the freight field at $0.00 when prompted to enter estimated freight, handling, and other.

Note: Upon receiving the merchandise, freight charges are not calculated in the Weighted Average Cost of Powder Coatings but are recorded in a Margin Account. If a customer is invoiced for freight charges, this account will be relieved accordingly.

Adjust Customer Order (if necessary)

1

If the temporary generic powder sales number was placed on a Customer Order, access Update/Review Order. 

2

Move to the order line for the temporary generic powder sales number and press delete to remove the line.

3

Add the new sales number to the Customer Order with the correct quantity.
