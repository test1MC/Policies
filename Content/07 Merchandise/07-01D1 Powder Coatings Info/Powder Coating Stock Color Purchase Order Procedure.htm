Powder Coating Stock Color Purchase Order Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

Since all available stock colors are standardized, they can be ordered, received, and sold using the same sales number. For more information on Powder Coatings Class Numbers, see .

Step

Action

When creating the Purchase Order

1

Complete an External Purchase Order to SW Powder Coatings [Vendor #0025199146]

When receiving the Purchase Order

1

Enter the correct freight amount on the Purchase Order using sales number 9901-11767. Leave the freight field at $0.00 when prompted to enter estimated freight, handling, and other.

Note: Upon receiving the merchandise, freight charges are not calculated in the Weighted Average Cost of Powder Coatings but are recorded in a Margin Account. If a customer is invoiced for freight charges, this account will be relieved accordingly.

Note: Your perpetual inventory should be accurate for all stock colors. You are required to include any stock colors in your weekly cycle count routine.
