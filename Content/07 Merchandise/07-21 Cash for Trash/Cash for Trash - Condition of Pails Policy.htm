Cash for Trash - Condition of Pails Policy

  1. Pails are to be made of HDPE plastic with a “2” recycle designation on their bottom.
  2. Pails that originally contained Sherwin-Williams paint will receive the $0.25 credit.
  3. Pails that originally contained paint or drywall mud manufactured by a company other than Sherwin-Williams will be taken in and recycled, but no credit will be given. This is done as a service to the community.
  4. Pails must have residue only from leftover latex paint and not contain any foreign material.
  5. Empty is defined as having leftover latex paint residue no more than 1/4’ thick in the bottom of the pail.
  6. Dry is defined as no liquids of any kind in the pail.
  7. It is the sole discretion of Sherwin-Williams whether the buckets are eligible for the $0.25 credit and if they meet the dry and empty definitions.
