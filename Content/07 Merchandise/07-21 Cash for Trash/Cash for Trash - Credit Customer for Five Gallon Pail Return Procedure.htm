Cash for Trash - Credit Customer for Five Gallon Pail Return Procedure

Step

Action

1

Process a Return Transaction using the customer’s cash or charge account number if available.

2

Press ENTER when prompted to enter a sales number and select non-merchandise [12 Recycle Empty 5-gal Plastic].

3

Enter the quantity of 5-gallon containers being returned for recycling.

4

Enter .25 when prompted to enter the price.

5

Press TOTAL [F12] when complete.
