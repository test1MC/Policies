Company Logo Usage Policy

  1. Store employees are not permitted to authorize vendors to private label merchandise that is purchased directly from vendors. In other words, products such as rags, screwdrivers, solvents, etc., purchased by a store employee directly from a manufacturer, vendor, or distributor are not to have anything printed on them that refer to Sherwin-Williams or any of its subsidiaries. Such permissions can only be granted by the Stores Group Headquarters Purchasing department.
  2. For the purposes of this policy, the term private label merchandise applies to any product purchased from a vendor, manufacturer, or distributor for resale through one of our stores that has been marked with any of the following:
    1. Our Cover the Earth logo
    2. The Sherwin-Williams logo
    3. The Sherwin-Williams name
  3. Per our Corporate Logo Policy, established in 2005, no third party is permitted to use our name or logo without specific permission granted by Stores Group Marketing or Stores Group Purchasing.
