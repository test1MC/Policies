External Product Identification Overview

Several choices are available when prompted to Enter Sales/Product Number:

If

Then

The product has a Universal Price Code (UPC)

Scan it or enter the sales number or Product (Rex) Number and Size Code.

The product is found on the store’s database

Press Enter with the highlight bar on the product and enter the correct product cost.

Note: If the product is found but does not have a UPC number on file, note the sales number and UPC number on the product and fax the information to (216) 566-2851.

The product is not found on the store’s database

Follow the Product Not Available Internally Error Message Procedure.

Note: You will be reminded that the product is available internally if you choose to order DSC stocked merchandise from an outside vendor.

The product’s selling price from an external vendor needs to be changed

Refer to the Inventory Price List to make the adjustment.

The Purchase Order is marked for stock

The selling price and description designated on the Purchase Order is placed in the Inventory Price List and the SELL PR Selling Price entered on the Purchase Order is used to price all customers.

Note: It is suggested that the Manufacturer's Suggested Selling (List) Price is always put on the Purchase Order for any item for stock because it ensures that the List Price will return to the screen when the item is sold. The Selling Price will not print on the Purchase Order.
