Add & Maintain SWeep Products Procedure

Step Action
1 From the SWeep homepage, select Enter Manufacturer Number -> Lookup [F3] -> enter the manufacturer's name.

Note: The vendor is the company with which the order was placed. The manufactureris the company who actually makes the product. The vendor and the manufacturer may or may not be the same.

2

If the product is not found by scanning the barcode or manually entering the UPC barcode number, enter the sales number or press Lookup [F3] to search the Store and Headquarters database.

3

Select SWeep Product Request and review the updated SWeep process. You will find new features that will enable you to request new sales number.

For more information, see New Vendor Request Procedure.
