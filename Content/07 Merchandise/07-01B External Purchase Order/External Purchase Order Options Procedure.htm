External Purchase Order Options Procedure

Step

Action

1

If

Then

The order is for stock

Select Stock

Note: The merchandise must be labeled with the sales number and manufacturer's suggested selling (list) price if stocked on the sales floor. Refer to the Inventory Price List if the suggested selling price was not entered as the selling price on the Purchase Order. For more information, see Inventory Price List Policy.

The order is for a specific customer

Select Special

Note: Remember that the merchandise must be labeled with the sales number and manufacturers suggested selling (list) price if it will be stocked on the sales floor. Refer to the Inventory Price List if the manufacturer’s suggested selling (list) price was not entered as the "selling price" on the Purchase Order. For more information, see Inventory Price List Policy.

2

Determine whether or not merchandise will be shipped directly:

If

Then

Yes
  • Enter the customer account number or enter the appropriate customer type if there is no account number. If the customer has a cash or charge account, press the LOOKUP [F3] key to enter the customer's name.
  • Enter deposit terminal/transaction number, if available.
  • Enter up to fourteen-digit alpha/numeric customer Purchase Order number, if provided. You may also enter the customer's telephone number if there is no Purchase Order number.

No

Proceed to Step 3.

3

Enter the correct Shipping Charge and Shipping Method. For more information, see External Purchase Order Shipping Information Overview.
