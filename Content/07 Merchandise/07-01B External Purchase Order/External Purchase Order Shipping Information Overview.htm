External Purchase Order Shipping Information Overview

Type

Payment

PrePaid and Add

Shipping charges are paid by the vendor but included as a separate line item on the vendor's invoice.

Collect

The vendor does not include the freight. Freight is billed to your store by the freight company on a separate invoice. If you receive a freight bill, verify that the correct Purchase Order is associated with the freight invoice, then forward it to Accounts Payable.

PrePaid

Cost of shipping is included in the unit cost of the product. Freight will not show on the invoice as a separate line item or billed by the freight company.

Standard

No priority - Normal shipping procedures used by the shipper.

Overnight

Receive next day.

2 Day Air

Priority to receive in 2 days.

Commercial

Common Carrier, Commercial freight carrier.

Other

Any other type of shipping method.
