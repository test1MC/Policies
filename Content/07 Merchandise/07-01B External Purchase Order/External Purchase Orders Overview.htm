External Purchase Orders Overview

External Purchase Orders are used to purchase merchandise from external sources and certain Sherwin-Williams' Divisions. The completed Purchase Order needs to be released by someone with at least MMS Level Two security. Mail, fax or telephone the Purchase Order directly to the vendor.

Expense items that require a Purchase Order should be entered into the Merchandise Management System. For more information, see Expense Purchase Order Procedure
