J29 Policy

  1. J29 orders must meet the minimum batch size for the product quality ordered.  10% plus/minus manufacturing variance is considered acceptable.   Below is a guide to minimum quantity accepted as a J29 in your prime DSC regardless of product quality:

    2. Division / DSC / Plant

    Minimum Batch Size

    CDN

    500 gallons

    300 gallons (General Paints)

    EAD, MWD, SED, SWD

    1000 gallons
  2. Two types of J29 requests are permitted: 
    1. Temporarily Stocked Special: This stocking arrangement is typically used for new products where we have no history of demand, when we do not have a stable relationship with the customer, or where product usage is small.

    2. Forecasted Special: This type of stocking should only be used for extremely stable customers who will continue to use the product on a long-term basis. These products must have an ordering pattern repetitive enough to create a forecast model. You are required to request a new J29 for any forecast changes that are required - including increased demand, decreased customer usage and the discontinuation of a product. No forecast will be accepted for amounts under 3000 gallons annually.

  3. Stores are financially responsible for managing all requested J29 inventories placed into the DSC.
  4. Stores are subject to DSC retention policies for J29 inventory. DSC retention varies by DSC and division.
