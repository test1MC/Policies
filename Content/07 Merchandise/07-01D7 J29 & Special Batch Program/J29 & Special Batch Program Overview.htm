J29 & Special Batch Program Overview

Store managers are able to request that inventory of GOM (goods of our manufacturer) paints be placed into the DSC to meet the needs of unique situations/customers. This type of inventory is referred to as a J29. J29 requests go through an automated approval process. The J29 application will allow stores to place and manage J29 requests for temporarily stocked specials only. J29 can be accessed via the SOURCE Intranet.

Once the J29 has been approved, there is a minimum 10 business day lead-time to manufacture and stock the paint at most DSCs. However, some DSCs and products may have up to a 5 week lead-time.

Use mypaintconnection to monitor J29 stock.  For more information on mypaintconnection, please see My Paint Connection Processes Procedure.
