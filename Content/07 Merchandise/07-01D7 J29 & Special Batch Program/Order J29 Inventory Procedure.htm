Order J29 Inventory Procedure

Step

Action

1

Create a purchase order but do not release this order in the system. 

For more information on purchase orders, please see Internal Purchase Order Inquiries Overview.

Note:  J29 orders may be placed on the following purchase order types:  STAR, Customer Direct, Emergency (fees will apply).

2

Call Customer Service [Arch: 1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364] before releasing the order.

Note:  Call this purchase order in to Customer Service to communicate that the store is requesting material from your J-29; otherwise, inventory will be shipped from general stock.

3

Customer Service will instruct the store to release their order when appropriate.
