Rapid Fire J29 Overview

This section applies to locations in EAD and SED divisions only

RapidFire is the automated and streamlined process for the request, approval, and manufacture of J29 products.  RapidFire’s elimination of forms and manual administrative processes will drastically reduce the time your customer waits to receive their product from weeks to days.  Rapidfire J29 has several benefits:

  • Eliminates all J29 forms, special divisional policies, all approvals outside of District Manager
  • Automates the request and approval system through SOURCE and alert emails when the product is ready
  • Reduces the time between request and manufacture

Your store will initiate both new and existing J29 requests for manufacture through a new Customer Service Option available in Customer Order Entry that will validate that bases, colors, sizes, and quantities for the J29 are all within specifications.  From the Customer Order the J29 will be submitted for District Manager approval.  Once approved, your order will automatically flow through the manufacturing process.  You will receive an e-mail when your product is ready to order.
