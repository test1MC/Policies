Rapid Fire New Requests Procedure

Step Action
Rapid Fire New Request

1

Select Customer Order Entry -> Customer Service Options -> Rapid Fire J29.

2

Complete the Order Header. Press Accept [F1].

3

Type [Yes] when prompted Is this a new J-29?

Note: To reorder or create a manufacturing request for additional existing J-29 product, type No and then use Lookup [F3] to find the Sales Number of the J-29 reorder. Your store MUST have approval to order the J-29 selected.  Also, you will receive an error message if you select a Sales Numberwhich is not for a J-29.

4

Enter all information required to fulfill the request.

Step Action

1

Use Lookup [F3] to select the DSC which will stock the product after manufacture.

Note: Select the same DSC that fulfills the STAR Orders. Do not select plants or entries where the numeric code begins with an “0.” After selecting the DSC for the first time, it will default for all future orders.

2

Leave the default at No when prompted for Customer Approval or Wet Sample.

3

Product Size defaults to Size Code 20 for five-gallon pails. Use Lookup [F3] to select other sizes.

4

Enter the total quantity required for your customer's request.  

Note: Batch size is determined by both base and manufacturing location.  SOURCE will provide warning messages when your quantity does not meet or exceeds batch size requirements.

5

Enter the Memo Demand Quantity.

Note: Memo Demand Quantity is the amount of the first draw the customer will take against the product and must be entered in increments of 80 gallons. Estimated Annual Volume is optional for items that may require a future order.

6

Use Lookup [F3] to select the base and color.

Note: SOURCE will validate that selected base and color are compatible and warn you if they are not.

7

Indicate the number of months within the allowed range of 0-4 months that you expect this batch to be warehoused at the DSC.

Note: Customer Color Description is not required. For all Sherwin-Williams fan deck colors, it is recommended that this field be kept blank. Any entry made here will print on the labels of your product. If this field is kept blank, the Sherwin-Williams color number will be listed as the color description.

8

Enter the District Manager’s email address. After this initial order, this entry will be saved.

Note: Enter any miscellaneous instructions or information  to the DM.

5

Press Accept [F1].

Note: Two line items for your RapidFire request will automatically generate: the Memo Demand Quantity with the Required Date set at 35 days from the creation of the RapidFire request and the remaining quantity for the total order.  The Required Date for this line item will be set out to the number of months indicated for storage of the J-29 product at the DSC.

6

Select YES to transmit your order for DM approval.  Selecting NO will delay your J29 request.  The plant will not manufacture until you have released this order and it has been approved by the DM.

Note: Once transmitted, no changes can be made to the order.

7

The store will be copied on the approval request e-mail sent to the DM.

Note: If the e-mail does not reach your DM, you will receive a failure notice by e-mail similar to the one shown below.  Call the SCO Helpdesk at 216- 566-2740 for assistance.

8

Once manufactured and stocked at the DSC, your store will receive an e-mail confirmation with the product's Sales Number. Verify that the date of the Memo Demand and your ability to order the product agree.  

Note: Update your Customer Order with the new Sales Number to ensure that the Memo Demand Quantity is created.  Use Update/Review to access the Customer Order and use the home key to move to the Sales Number Field to enter the new J-29 Sales Number.  If you do not do this, you will not receive prompts for this Memo Demand when placing your STAR Order.
