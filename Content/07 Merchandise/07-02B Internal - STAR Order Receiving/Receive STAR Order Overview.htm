Receive STAR Order Overview

The following information provides an overview for the STAR Order receiving process and the automatic generation of Visible Shortage and Visible Damage IBAR's. If visibly damaged or visibly missing products are detected during delivery, the receiving employee must identify and document the damaged or missing products. The delivery driver and receiving employee must sign the system-generated Shipment Variance Report and BOL noting the damaged or missing products.

Visible Damage could include: paint leaking from the bottom of the pallet - the receiving employee should locate the source of the paint leak or crushed boxes - the receiving employee should open the entire box to inspect each piece

Visible Missing products would include products missing from received quantities, but WOULD NOT INCLUDE Visible Missing products from a pallet if the ASN WAS NOT scanned.  If you notice missing product and you have received the order by product/line, process an IBAR for Concealed Damage / Shortage / Overage.
