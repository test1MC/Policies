Downtime STAR Order Receiving Procedure

Step

Action

1

Manually indicate the number of pallets on the BOL and count the number of pallets of product taken from the delivery truck.

Note: If you are short pallets, sign and date the BOL short by noting the number of pallets of product missing.

2

Inspect the order for any visible damage.

Note: If you need to break down a pallet to put it away make sure that you save your ASN pallet labels.

3

When the ASN information is in your system please follow the normal receiving process by scanning every ASN Pallet Label.

Note: For more information, see STAR Order Receiving Overview.
