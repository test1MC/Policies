CTS Night Deliveries Overview

All shortages and damages must be called into the shipping DSC by noon the day of delivery. The DSC will log the discrepancies and issue an OSD (Overage / Shortage / Damage) Number.  Your store will not receive credit for any discrepancy if the OSD Number is not noted on the IBAR.  This procedure replaces the visible shortage, overage, damaged check conducted during an attended delivery.  Stores still have up to two weeks from receipt to submit IBAR claims for concealed shortage, overage, and damage.
