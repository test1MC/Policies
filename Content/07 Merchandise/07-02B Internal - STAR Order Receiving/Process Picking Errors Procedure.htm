Process Picking Errors Procedure

Step

Action

1

Add the product that was picked in error to the order. Receive "0" quantity for the product that was not received.

If

Then

Picking Error results in extra product

Add the product to the order by moving to a blank line and entering the appropriate sales number and the quantity received.

Receive a Dealer product

Enter the Dealer product on the purchase order using number 9901-12484 PICKING ERROR and enter the quantity received. The order quantity and cost will be zero. Press the COMMENTS key to add the product name and REX number. Headquarters will generate an IBAR for the store.

2

Contact Customer Service to determine the procedures for returning or keeping the merchandise. If the merchandise is returned, perform a Reason Code 2 IBAR.
