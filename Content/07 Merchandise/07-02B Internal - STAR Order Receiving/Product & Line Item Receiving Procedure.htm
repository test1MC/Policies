Product & Line Item Receiving Procedure

Step

Action

1

Scan the Universal Product Code [UPC], enter the sales number, or enter the product number [REX] and size. Select the proper Purchase Order.

Note: If the order number is known, select ORDER from the Receiving Options list. Select  the proper Purchase Order from the Released, In-Process, In-Recvng, and Part Rcpt Purchase Orders list.

2

Ensure the accuracy of each product/line item. You must generate a Reason Code 1 IBAR for any variances between the quantity billed [SHIP-Q] and quantity received.  A Shipment Variance Report **ASN vs. Line Received ** report will automatically print designating the items to include on the Concealed Damage/Shortage/Overage IBAR once the STAR Purchase Order is CLOSED. Purchase Orders must be CLOSED within 48 hours of receipt.

3

Process the Concealed Damage/Shortage/Overage IBAR. IBAR must be prepared and released within 14 days of delivery. Any credits processed after that time will not be honored.

Note: IBAR must be prepared and released within 14 days of delivery. Any credits processed after that time will not be honored.
