Receive STAR Order Procedure

Step

Action

1

Use the Handheld Scanner to begin the STAR order receiving process.

Note: The scanner is the fastest and most efficient way to receive your order. It also reduces the chances of making mistakes when receiving an order. The moment an Advanced Shipping Notification (ASN) is scanned, all the information is immediately sent to the Merchandise Management purchase order on the store’s system. If receiving against the purchase order at a SOURCE terminal instead of using the Handheld Scanner, ensure that you either scan the ASN Barcode or manually enter the ASN number for each received pallet.

2

Scan each ASN Shipping Label on each pallet of product.

Note: While scanning the ASN labels, check for obvious damage such as: paint leaking, crushed boxes, ripped bags, or any other visible signs. As, ensure that nothing is missing from the order as well.

3

Press the CANCEL key on the Handheld Scanner if you have scanned the ASN labels for each pallet of product and completed the receiving process.

Note: From a SOURCE terminal press Accept [F1] to accept the information before pressing Cancel [F5].

4

After scanning all the ASNs for the shipment, determine whether or not there is any visible damage or missing products:

If

Then

Yes

Scan the product barcode or manually enter the sales/rex number for the damaged or missing products.

Note: If products are received against the purchase order at a SOURCE terminal, you will be able to press Lookup [F3] to choose from a list of received products. If there is missing product, the BOL must be signed Free-Astray and dated by the receiving employee. Include the number of missing pallets. The driver must also sign the BOL.

No

The receiving process is complete. Proceed to Step 5.

Note: If you do not note any damages or missing products and have the delivery driver sign off on the Shipment Variance Report, you may not receive credit for the missing or damaged items. A Shipment Variance Report will not print if all ASNs are scanned and there are no variances. Therefore, the driver and store employee need only sign the bill of lading.

5

Sign and date the BOL, accept the Receipts, and close the Purchase Order.

Note: Remember to review the received STAR Purchase Order on the system to make sure that everything is correct. Purchase Orders must be CLOSED within 48 hours of receipt.

6

Once complete, a Shipment Variance Report will generate and automatically print.

Note: A copy of the report will also be retained in the Reports [Shift F4] file for seven days. Reprint by selecting Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Reports -> Shipment Variance Report.

7

Ensure the delivery driver signs both copies of the report. Give one copy of the report to the driver and sign and date the BOL noting Missing/Damaged products. Retain the signed copy.

Note: Any product with a SHIP-Q quantity that is not received could be coming from another DSC. Do not close the purchase order until you are sure that you will not receive the SHIP-Q quantity.
