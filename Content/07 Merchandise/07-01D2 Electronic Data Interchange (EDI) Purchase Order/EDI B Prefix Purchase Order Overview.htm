EDI B Prefix Purchase Order Overview

B Prefix Purchase Orders are considered downtime purchase orders and must be phoned in to the vendor. This type of purchase order can also be handed to the Vendor Sales Representative if you purchase merchandise directly from the representative to receive a better cost. Use a Manual [B] Prefix Purchase Order when ordering sample books, samples or special cost provided vendor builder programs or in place of an inventory adjustment for materials received on an external purchase order including situations where the external purchase order was:

  • Canceled but merchandise shipped.
  • Closed prior to receipt of backordered merchandise.
  • Closed having received a quantity lower than that actually received by the store.

Note: Use a Manual B Prefix Purchase Order if you will receive a special cost from the vendor. For more information on the significance of using purchase orders, refer to PO SIGNIFICANCE.
