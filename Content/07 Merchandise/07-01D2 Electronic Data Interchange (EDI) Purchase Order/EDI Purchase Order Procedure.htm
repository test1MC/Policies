EDI Purchase Order Procedure

Specific business rules have been implemented to ensure that EDI Purchase Orders meet Sherwin-Williams' requirements. EDI Purchase Orders are processed at select store locations.  EDI-capable vendors will review purchase orders electronically to confirm products and the estimated freight charges.  The vendor will send a Purchase Order Acknowledgement to the store.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management-> Add Purchase Order.

2

Press Lookup [F3] to locate the vendor number and enter a portion of the vendor name. Press Enter.

Note: Do not call the order in to the vendor if they are EDI-capable; the order will be sent once the PO is released.

3

Answer the prompt: Accept partial shipment?

If

Then

No

The order should be shipped in full or it should be canceled. If the vendor does not have part of the order they have been instructed to cancel the entire order.

Yes

Partial shipments are acceptable. This means: if an item is backordered, you have given the vendor permission to ship a portion of the order instead of waiting until the complete order can be shipped.

4

If the order is a Direct or Drop Ship Order, indicate that the order is Special and Direct. Enter the complete address information. 

5

Release the order once complete:

If

Then

The Purchase Order cannot be processed at the time

A message will appear with two options:

  • Automatically send PO when EDI is available: the system will return you to the Purchase Order menu. The order will be in RELEASED status and show as an Unacknowledged PO in the Status Reminder Box until the vendor acknowledges the order.
  • Call vendor to place the order: After making this selection you will be taken to the PO number field. It is critical that you complete a manual "B" prefix purchase order and enter the "B" prefix purchase order number.

After releasing the "B" prefix Purchase Order, it becomes the EDI Confirmation of your phone order to the vendor. If you EDI an "A" prefix purchase order and phone the "A" prefix purchase order to the vendor you could receive a double shipment.

It is processed immediately

The procedure is complete.

Note: The bottom of the Purchase Orders contain a message "Order was sent via EDI" when the order was sent to the vendor electronically.

6

Once the vendor receives and verifies the order you will receive a purchase order acknowledgement. The EDI Acknowledgment should contain: Product availability and the difference between the Purchase Order estimated freight and the vendor's actual freight.

Review the EDI Acknowledgement  when available in the EDI Status Reminder Box by pressing Reports [Shift + F4]. An EDI report will also identify Purchase Orders that have been canceled. For more information, see Status Reminder Box Overview.
