EDI PO Shipping Methods Overview

You must enter the correct Shipping Method while creating an EDI Purchase Order. After entering the vendor information select the correct method of shipment from the Shipping Method menu. Then select the correct shipping charges from the Shipping Charges List. If the merchandise is to be picked up at the vendor’s warehouse, enter the Shipping Method of Pickup. Otherwise, the vendor will ship the merchandise once approved. For Saturday deliveries, use a Manual B Prefix Purchase Order and phone the order in to the vendor. You must also enter an estimated freight charge.

The Shipping Methods include:

  • Standard
  • Overnight
  • 2-Day Air
  • Commercial
  • Pickup

Note: All Overnight and 2nd day air orders must be place before 2:30 EST. Also, any orders placed after 5:30pm EST will not be sent until the next business day. For more information, see External Purchase Order Shipping Information Overview.
