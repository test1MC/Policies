EDI Purchase Order Acknowledgement Overview

To launch reports from any POS screen, press Shift + INQUIRY [F4]. The EDI Purchase Order Acknowledgment Report provides you the Accepted or Rejected status of purchase orders placed with vendors. It is important to review this report frequently to understand the status of orders placed with vendors, and it is recommended that you print this report to see messages from the vendor. At the bottom of the report, the following messages will indicate appropriate next steps.

If

Then

Purchase Orders Accepted with Vendor Changes

Review the report to determine if action is necessary by the store. If there is no action required, there is nothing more to do with the PO until the merchandise is received.

Purchase Order Rejected By Vendor

Review the reason the vendor gave for rejecting the purchase order and determine the proper course of action. This may include creating a new purchase order with correct information and/or contacting the vendor for clarification.

Purchase Order Has Been Canceled Due to Vendor Changes

The order has been canceled by our system due to invalid changes by the vendor, such as quantity or price. Contact the vendor to notify them that their order has been canceled and determine the proper course of action. This may include creating a new purchase order with the correct information.
