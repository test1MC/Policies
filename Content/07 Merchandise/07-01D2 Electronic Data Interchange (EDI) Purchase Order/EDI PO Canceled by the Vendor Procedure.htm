EDI PO Canceled by the Vendor Procedure

If the vendor cannot fill the order or if the vendor cannot meet the purchase order requirements, they can cancel it. It is the responsibility of the store location to resubmit another purchase order if the order is canceled and you still want the merchandise.

Cancel Reason

Description

Incorrect Cost

If the Vendor does not accept the cost entered on the purchase order it will be automatically canceled.

Incorrect Product

The Vendor could need additional information because they do not recognize the product or part number.

Ship Complete

A product could be backordered and you told the Vendor that you would only accept a complete order.
Step Action
1

Press Reports [Shift + F4] to view an EDI report that identifies Purchase Orders that have been canceled by the vendor.

2 If the vendor ships the merchandise after the purchase order is canceled, generate a Manual [B Prefix] Purchase Order -> Receive against the manual purchase order -> Select Comment to capture the original purchase order number and the reason for generating the new P.O. -> Contact the vendor to let them know the new purchase order number-> Determine if you will keep the product or charge the vendor back for the merchandise by processing a Chargeback.
