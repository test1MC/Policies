EDI Purchase Order Overview

Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) provides a method of sending purchase orders electronically to the Vendor and receiving an electronic confirmation of the purchase from the vendor. If the vendor is EDI-Capable, this message will appear:

  • "THIS VENDOR IS EDI-CAPABLE ORDER WILL BE SENT TO THE VENDOR ONCE THE PO IS RELEASED DO NOT CALL THE VENDOR TO PLACE THE ORDER."

You will see the following message if the vendor is not EDI-Capable:

  • "VENDOR IS NOT EDI-CAPABLE. MUST CALL VENDOR TO PLACE ORDER. <OK>"

While placing a Purchase Order you MUST make sure that you RELEASE the Purchase Order. The Purchase Order will not be automatically sent to the vendor until the Purchase Order is released.

  • Once a purchase order is RELEASED for a vendor that is able to receive purchase orders electronically, the purchase order will be transmitted to the vendor in one of the following time segments:

  • The store will receive a return confirmation within 2-4 hours (EST).
  • If the vendor is not able to confirm the order contact Store Computer Operations at 216-566-2740.
  • Vendors who are EDI-capable with Sherwin-Williams will not accept an “A” prefixed purchase order by phone or Fax.
  • Stores should only call in orders over the phone in special circumstances, such as downtime and special purchase orders that require direct communication with vendors because vendors will only accept “B” prefix purchase orders, ones called in over the phone, or faxed. Stores will still be required to enter the “B” prefix purchase order on the computer with the correct information to ensure payment.  MMS will only pay for merchandise received on your purchase order at the cost on the purchase.
  • Vendors cannot accept purchase order additions called in over the phone. You must generate an additional purchase order.
  • Ensure the information and WAC is correct on the purchase order and receive against the purchase order when the merchandise arrives at the store. The vendor will only get paid the amount accepted by them on the purchase order. Failure to receive timely will delay vendor payments and may require additional work at a later time.
  • If you are getting a special cost from a vendor sales representative ensure that the vendor representative communicates the special cost to the vendor’s billing department or the purchase order could be canceled.
  • If the cost on the purchase order is incorrect, the vendor has been instructed to reject the purchase order.
  • Ensure that the order is in the vendor’s ship-pack quantities or the vendor will cancel it.
