Internal Purchase Orders Overview

All internal order types will now be submitted for fulfillment using Electronic Data Interchange (EDI). There are some exceptions to this rule requiring phone calls to Customer Service; however, these scenarios have been greatly reduced. The VRU has been eliminated as a method for submitting orders. These new ordering procedures will result in many benefits for stores:

  • Stores have become more empowered in their ordering procedures.
  • With certain exceptions, phone calls will not need to be made to Customer Service.
  • Customer Service will be better able to handle order exceptions and special orders.
  • The DSC will receive orders within minutes rather than overnight.
  • The Plants will be able to anticipate customer demand earlier.
  • Order Advisory Reports will be received within hours of placing an order rather than next day.
  • Additional Order Types will allow for entry of Will Call and Make-N-Ship orders into the SOURCE system.
  • Line Item Comments and Special Instructions information will be sent to the DSC.
  • Naming conventions for order types have been standardized between Stores Group business units and Consumer Division.
