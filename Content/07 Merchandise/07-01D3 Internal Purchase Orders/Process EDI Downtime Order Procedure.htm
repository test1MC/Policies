Process EDI Downtime Order Procedure

If the order has met the criteria for submitting the order via Customer Service, follow a down-time procedure:

Step

Action

1

Complete a Manual B-Prefix form.

2

Enter the B-Prefix Purchase Order Number in the system without releasing the order.

3

Call Customer Service [1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364 / Auto: 1-800-357-4956] immediately to place the order.

Note: Do not release the order until Customer Service directs you to. If you do not enter the same B-Prefix purchase order number on your SOURCE purchase order that you have provided to Customer Service, you may receive a duplicate shipment.
