Special Instructions for Purchase Order Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Special information may be communicated to Customer Service and/or the DSC through the purchase order. Stores should be careful with order text. Certain text automatically attaches to a store's purchase orders based on how Customer Service has requested that store be set up in their system.  Include only text, which does not automatically feed from the Customer Service system for your location.  If you are unsure what this text may be for your location, contact Cleveland Customer Service [1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364] for clarification.  If you find that you are including the same text on most of your purchase orders, contact Customer Service to have this information defaulted for you in their system for your location.

All order text should be directly relevant to the order and indicate further instruction not already made clear in the order information. This may include special one-time shipping information.  Special one-time shipping information must be communicated to Customer Service via a phone and be included on the purchase order.  Information pertaining to the entire order shipment should be indicated in the Special Instructions. Information pertaining ONLY to a single item must me indicated ONLY through Line Item COMMENTS [F9].

Special instructions that should be communicated to Customer Service via phone call and order text include:

  • Instructions for Emergency, Customer Direct, Make-N-Ship, or Will Call orders requiring special handling should be included in Special Instructions.  This information may include, but is not limited to, information such as order delivery date/time, phone calls required to schedule delivery, specific LTL carrier, pallet jack required, lift gate required.
  • Emergency, Customer Direct, and Will Call orders requiring a specific batch request must indicate this information at the Line Item COMMENTS [F9] level. Stores must call Customer Service to verify batch requests. If no phone call is made to Customer Service, no guarantee can be made that the requested batch will be received in the store.
  • Make-N-Ship Orders require special fill/label information at the Line Item COMMENTS [F9] level. Customer Service will review all Make-N-Ship Orders prior to releasing them to the Plant. However, they will not know specific requests unless the store has entered them correctly using Line Item COMMENTS [F9] for product specific requests.
  • Free or "no charge" merchandise must be placed on the same purchase order with the merchandise that qualifies you for the free or "no charge" merchandise. The promotion name must be noted in the COMMENTS [F9] section of the free line item. This is necessary for proper pricing of the promotional line item.
  • Make-n-Ship Pavement (Highway Paint / Traffic Paint) Orders with special pricing MUST have the special pricing information noted in Special Instructions.
