SOURCE Downtime Procedure

If SOURCE is unavailable on STAR Order day or you must place another purchase order type with the DSC, follow regular downtime procedure for processing B-Prefix Purchase Orders.  You will not receive a duplicate shipment for the merchandise as long as you remember to enter the manual B- Prefix Purchase Order Number.  Customer Service will not automatically fill manual B-Prefix Orders placed on the store computer system. Manual Orders are only filled if placed by phone to the Customer Service Department.

Step

Action

1

Complete a Manual B- Prefix Purchase Order and attach a list of every item ordered if the order is too large to fit on the Manual Purchase Order form.

2

Call Customer Service [Arch: 1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364 / Auto: 1-800-357-4956] and provide the following information:

  • For manufactured paint products, state the Sales Number or REX number, size and quantity.
  • For manufactured brushes and rollers, state the sales number and quantity.
  • For associated products, state sales number and quantity.

3

Enter the complete Order into the Merchandise Management System as soon as the store computer system is working properly.
