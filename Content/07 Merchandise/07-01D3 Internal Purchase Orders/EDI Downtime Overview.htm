EDI Downtime Overview

If you experience an error when sending your Purchase Order using EDI, refer to the EDI Transmission Log for additional information on why your order was not sent.  Use the EDI Transmission Errors & Resolutions to determine what course of action, if any, is required on your part.  If SOURCE is unable to send the order via EDI when released, you may have the option of sending your purchase order once the system is available or calling Customer Service [Arch: 1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364 / Auto: 1-800-357-4956] to place the order. Always select the option to send the purchase order once the system is available UNLESS your order is being placed Monday through Friday and meets one of the following criteria:

If

Then
  • Order Type is Emergency and it is one hour or less before your Emergency Order DSC cut-off time
  • Order Type is Customer Direct and it is one hour or less before your DSC cut-off time for Customer Direct Orders
  • Order Type is Will Call and you need to pick the order up within five hours

Call Customer Service [Arch: 1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364 / Auto: 1-800-357-4956] to place the order.
