Cancel Internal Purchase Order Procedure

All purchase order types require a two-part cancellation process. 

Step Action
1 The store must contact Customer Service [Arch: 1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364 / Auto: 1-800-357-4956] or the vendor directly to cancel the purchase order.  In the event that the purchase order has been fulfilled, the store will be notified by Customer Service that the purchase order cannot be cancelled.
2 If the purchase order can be cancelled by the vendor or Customer Service, the store should then cancel the purchase order created in SOURCE by accessing Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Cancel Unnecessary Purchase Order.
