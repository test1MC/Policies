Process Kit Sale Procedure

Step

Action

1

Kits are processed the same as any normal sale. If the product is not priced in Customer Order Entry, it will price at the Point of Sale transaction.

2

The kit SKU will explode to the Bill-of-Material on the large printer receipt and the packing slip. Receipts and invoices will be sent to the laser printer. You can reprint a thermal receipt, but it will only show the kit SKU without component information.
