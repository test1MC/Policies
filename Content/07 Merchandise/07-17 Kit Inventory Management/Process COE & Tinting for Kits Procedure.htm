Process COE & Tinting for Kits Procedure

Step

Action

1

Enter the Sales Number and the size of the kit. The formula color and dispense amounts are calculated for each kit.

2

To see the component parts, select Kit Components.

Note: If you need to look up a kit SKU, use the search feature on the Order Entry screen before selecting [F6] Color.

3

The kit components show the breakdown of your Part B products.

4

Scan the tintable component at the dispense preparation window. Do not enter or scan the kit SKU (I Rex number). Both the individual gallon and kit gallon product will be displayed.
