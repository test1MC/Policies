Kit Inventory Overview

Kits were designed to make the customer ordering process more efficient, especially orders for large commercial projects. Kits are sold as one product number and they are assembled at the store as needed, which will relieve inventory behind the scenes. New size codes have been created and begin with the letter w or v. Kit product codes begin with the letter I and are accessible through: Product Inquiry, Purchase Order, and Memo Demand.

Kit SKUs appear as a single line item on an order. The kit SKU will explode out all of the components to the Bill-of-Material and print on the Pick Ticket, the Confirmation of Delivery, and the Centralized Invoice.

In regards to Min/Max Levels and Kits, note the following:

  • Min/Max levels cannot be maintained for kit SKUs.
  • Kit usage will be captured in the sales of the component parts and the min/max levels of these products will be adjusted to reflect the appropriate demand and re-order points. 
  • Kits can be added to Memo Demand to prompt additional ordering of all component parts.

Note: There is no physical inventory for kits, so they are not accessible through Inventory Adjustment, IBARs, or Cycle Counts. Quick Order will not show pricing for kits.
