Invoice Match Overview

External Purchasing: External invoices will be paid based on the amount received. Each line on an External Purchase Order will not be compared for payment until the line is approved as received. External invoices do not get paid until you approve the receipts on the Purchase Order.

Internal Purchasing: Internal purchases automatically get charged to the store. A review process will be performed that takes the Internal purchases that have been charged to the store and compares them to the approved receipts. Correcting adjustments will automatically be made for any quantity variances.

Note: You are required to review the SPR for expense items, not external or internal purchases.
