External Purchase Reconcilement Overview

The Merchandise Management System will automatically handle the pricing and quantity Chargebacks.  Stores are required to handle all expense invoices. Invoice payment and reconcilement is an automatic process for all merchandise based on the comparison between what was billed and what was received. Purchase Orders and the vendor's invoice are systematically matched at Headquarters. Any variance for costing or quantity will be automatically supported by a Chargeback.

If you receive any vendor invoices mail them to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
Merchandise Accounts Payable Department
P.O. Box 5819, Cleveland, Ohio 44101

Only original copies of invoices and transmittals with original approving signatures will be accepted for payment.

Note: If you have any questions concerning, contact Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297. This process is successful if you are careful to receive all Purchase Orders accurately and timely. It helps reduce the possibility of duplicate, erroneous, or unauthorized payments.
For more information, see 07-13 Inventory and 01-07 Expense Invoice.
