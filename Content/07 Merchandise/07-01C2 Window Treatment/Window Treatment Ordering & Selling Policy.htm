Window Treatment Ordering & Selling Policy

Window Treatment ordering and selling on the store computer system is no different than any other product type. The unique window treatment sales number is a combination of the Style Number, Width, and Height.

  1. Employees are required to order, receive, and sell window treatments using the procedures outlined below.
  2. Levolor Order Form(found in the Forms Repository) and an External Purchase Order must be completed for all Levolor Orders.
    1. The Levolor Order must be faxed to 800-826-6653.
  3. Receiving must be handled the same as any other external purchase order.
  4. Receive the merchandise against the appropriate purchase order when the merchandise physically arrives at the store. This is important for the sale to automatically reflect the correct gross margin.
