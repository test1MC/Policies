Same Day New Product Accommodation Overview

Applies Only to Automotive Branches

Customers often make requests for same day fulfillment of new product not currently sold by branches. Automotive Management allows branches to order and sell these new SKUs on demand. Products that need to be sold immediately must be handled using a manual system.
