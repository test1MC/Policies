Same Day New Product Accommodation Policy

Applies Only to Automotive Branches

  1. The Same Day New Product Accommodation Procedure may only be used by Automotive Branches.
  2. This process is only available upon customer request and may not be used for stock orders.
  3. This process is not available for new vendors; vendors must already be approved by Sherwin-Williams.
  4. The Same Day New Product Accommodation Procedure may not be used for charge accounts during the last two business days of the month to prevent invoice re-aging and credit terms extension. During this time:
    1. Cash and check are the only allowable FOP.
    2. Full deposits are required for all accommodations.
  5. Either a Sherwin-Williams' Charge Account in good standing or deposit in full is required for all customers prior to placing an order with the vendor.
  6. Customer credit availability must be confirmed before accommodating the same day request.
  7. The product request in SWeep must be completed on the same day as the accommodation.
  8. The B-PO Form and Manual POST Form must be processed using the new Sales Number within three business days of the accommodation.
  9. Taxing discrepancies discovered when the POS Sale Transaction is run must be immediately handled:

    10. Customer Type

    Action

    Charge

    Run Correction Transaction against the POS Sale Transaction to collect or refund tax.

    Cash
    • Contact customer to collect or refund tax.
    • If customer owes tax and cannot be reached or will not resolve by last day of month, store must assume taxing liability.
      • Process a Cash Correction Transaction and Disbursement Transaction.
  10. A separate file of documents for Same Day New Product Accommodations must be retained. For each accommodation, the following documents must be retained together:
    1. Store Copy of the Manual B-PO Form
    2. Store Copy of the Manual POST Form
    3. Copy of the POS Sale Transaction and/or Correction Transaction
    4. Copy of the Product Request in SWeep
