Automotive Store Expense Transaction for Mixing Room Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Point of Sale.

2

At the Sale/Payment Menu press Page Down for the Special Menu.

3

Select Store Expense. Select Other.

4

Select PSR Expense –Account #0400-340 from the G/L Account List. Press Accept [F1].

5

Enter the Sales Number and Quantity for the products.

6

Enter the Price/Cost as $0.00. Press Accept [F1] to complete the transaction.
