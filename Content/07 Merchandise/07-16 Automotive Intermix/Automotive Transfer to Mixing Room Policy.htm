Automotive Transfer to Mixing Room Policy

When mixing toners are removed from warehouse inventory and placed into the Branch mixing room (for use in intermixes), a Transfer to the Mixing Room transaction must be processed.  Completing this transaction will properly account for branch usage and ensure accuracy of branch perpetual inventory.

  1. A transaction must be processed through the POS system to record movement of product from the warehouse into the mixing room.
  2. A Transfer to the Mixing Room transaction must be processed in POS the same business day as a new toner or empty can is moved into the mixing room. To ensure that the transaction is not overlooked (or duplicated), the Mixing Room Transfer and Expense Log must be maintained of the toners being moved.
    1. Remove the label from the empty toner can and retain it as a reminder until the transaction has been completed.
  3. A file must be maintained in the branch with copies of the Transfer to Mixing Room transactions. Once the Mixing Room Transfer and Expense Log is full, the completed log page should be filed with the transaction copies.
  4. Only product contained in a mix (toners, dryers, reducers, empty cans, etc.), and the can may be transferred into the mixing room. Do not transfer other Associated Products (clearcoat, gloves, etc.), to the mixing room.
