Automotive Mixing Room Expenses Policy

When products such as gloves, rags, and new mixing lids, etc., are removed from warehouse inventory and placed into the Branch mixing room for store use, a Store Expense transaction must be processed in POS.  Completing this transaction will properly account for branch usage and ensure accuracy of branch perpetual (POS/ computer) inventory.

  1. A transaction must be processed through the POS system to record movement of product from the warehouse into the mixing room.
  2. A Store Expense transaction must be processed in POS the same business day as the products are used in the mixing room. To ensure that the transaction is not overlooked (or duplicated), the Mixing Room Transfer and Expense Log must be maintained listing all of the products being moved.
  3. A file must be maintained in the branch with copies of the Store Expense transactions.
    1. Once the Mixing Room Transfer and Expense Log is full, the completed log page should be filed with the transaction copies.
  4. PSR Expense - Only products such as gloves, rags, new mixing lids, etc. may be processed in a PSR store expense transaction.
