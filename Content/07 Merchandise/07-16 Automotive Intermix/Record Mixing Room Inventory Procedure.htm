Record Mixing Room Inventory Procedure

Automotive Store Mixing Rooms are included in the inventory. There is an option under the Merchandise Management System (MMS) for recording the count of these products.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management System -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Record Inventory.

2

Choose Mix Room Z1-Z8.

3

Enter Sales Number.

4

Press Accept [F1] to finish.
