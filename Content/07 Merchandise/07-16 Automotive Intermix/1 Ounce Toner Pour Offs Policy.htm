1 Ounce Toner Pour Offs Policy

  1. Sales of 1 ounce pour offs are restricted to Shop Customers
  2. Do not sell 1 ounce pour offs to Production Shops or Jobbers
  3. Pearl Tones CANNOT be sold in 1 ounce pour offs
  4. Full product sizes should be used for toner sales greater than 8 Oz.
  5. Only the following sales numbers are approved for 1 ounce toner pour offs:
