Transfer to the Mixing Room Procedure

Find the Mixing Room Transfer and Expense Log in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword mixing room in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Point of Sale.

2

At the Sale/Payment Menu press Page Down for the Special Menu.

3

Select Store Expense.

4

Select Transfer to the Mix Room –Account #0400-340 from the G/L Account List. Press Accept [F1].

5

Enter the Sales Number and Quantity for the products.

6

Enter the Price/Cost as $0.00. Press Accept [F1] to complete the transaction.

Note: All sales of intermixes should be processed using AI (Automotive Intermix) sales numbers to properly relieve inventory.  Although this procedure utilizes the POS store expense function, this process will transfer product within inventory accounts with no P&L impact.
