Automotive Intermix Orders Overview

SOURCE and Internet Scale do not directly interact.  The only interface that takes place is the retrieval of ingredients and inventory adjustment information from Headquarters. Should the communication between the branch and Headquarters be interrupted, a manual process for adjusting mixing room inventory will be required.

All Intermix orders must begin in Quick Order or Customer Order Entry. While creating the customer order look up the formula and blend I.D. in Formula Express. This information can also be accessed from the Internet Scale PC.

The Work Order System will allow you to plan your production in SOURCE. All Intermixes ordered in COE will be sent to the Work Order System. All orders for Intermixes placed in COE will appear in the in the CUSTOMER /SHOP ORDER LINES field (the left field).

Note:  Contact Store Computer Operations at 216-566-2740 if experiencing problems with the computer system. Contact the Internet Scale Support Line at 1-800-798-5872 [option 3] if experiencing problems with the application. [can't find a formula or can't log in.]
