1 Ounce Toner Pour Offs Procedure

Step Action
Pour off Ounces in FE 2.0
1 Log on to gmscolor.com with MySherwin credentials.
2

In the Enter Search Criteria box on the Dashboard, search for the Toner formula code (example: U7217).

3

Select the appropriate Formula hyperlink.
4

Select Make a Batch.
5

Ensure the Mix Ratio reads None Selected.
6

Enter the desired amount and select oz as the unit of measure. Select Ok to continue.
7

Select the Batch Number that was just created.
8

Select Print Batch Ticket or Send to Scale.
Bill Ounces in POS
1 Follow the regular POS Billing Instructions.
