Create GHS Intermix Label Procedure

Step

Action

1

From SOURCE homepage -> Automotive -> Quick Links -> Formula Express.

2

Complete the intermix formula.

3

Close the batch:

  • Click on the Batch Number from the Open Batches section on the Recent Batches page.
  • Enter the actual amounts of the ingredients in the batch and click Close Batch.

4

Click into the Closed Batch from Recent Batches by Batch Number.

5

Click Request GHS Label.

6

Apply the intermix can label.

7

Print the Batch Label from FE 2.0 and apply to intermix can label in its proper place.

8

Shake the intermix.

9

Click on Print GHS Label from FE 2.0.

Note: If an error message appears, wait and try again later.

10

Apply the DYMO GHS caution sticker(s).

If

Then

Two DYMO caution stickers are generated
  • Pint—use the quart label instead.
  • Quart – cut the excess space on the second sticker right below the copy. 
  • Quart and Gallon – place the full DYMO caution sticker above followed by the second sticker containing less copy below.

One DYMO caution sticker is generated

Apply the caution sticker (s) to the back panel per can label instructions.

Note: Look for the GHS icon code(s) on the caution sticker(s) and use the GHS Code / Icon Cross Reference to determine the ones needed.

11

Apply appropriate GHS red diamond icon stickers under the caution sticker.

Note: Do not cover the emergency contact or supplier copy at the bottom of the label.
