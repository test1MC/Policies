Intermix Lookup Procedure

Approximately 10,300 Intermix sales numbers have been set up and approximately 2,500 Intermix sales numbers will automatically price from price schedules. If an Intermix sales number does not retrieve a selling price, conduct a lookup:

Step Action
1
If Then
This will be a repeat purchase Add the sales number as an exception price to the customer's Price Record Card. Each time the customer purchases the intermix, the transaction will price correctly.
This is a one time sale Manually enter the price on the Customer Order or Point-of-Sale Transaction.
2

Step

Action
 

1

Press Inquiry [F4] and select Product and Prices.
 

2

Press Lookup [F3] and select Intermix from the Product Lookup List.
 

3

Enter the entire Intermix Number or a part of the Intermix Number.
 

4

Press Lookup [F3] and select the appropriate Intermix Number.
 

5

Observe Inventory levels and Store Cost.
