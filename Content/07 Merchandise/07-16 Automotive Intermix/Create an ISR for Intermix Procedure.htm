Create an ISR for Intermix Procedure

Step

Action

1

Obtain the Intermix Number and cost from the Facility producing the Intermix.

2

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu  -> Merchandise Management System -> Purchase Order-> Add New Purchase Order.

3

Select Inter Store Request (ISR).

4

Select the appropriate order option: Stock or Special.

5

Enter the Branch Number of the facility producing the Intermix.

6

Press Lookup [F3] in the Sales Number Field.

7

Specify the shipping method and charges information.

8

Select Intermix Blend from the Product Lookup List.

9

Enter the Intermix Number from the producing facility and press Enter.

10

Select Yes to create Intermix if it does not exist in the database. Press Enter.

11

Enter producing Branch number. Press Enter.

12

Select Done.

13

Enter quantity and cost. Press Accept [F1].

14

When Intermix arrives, receive and close the ISR PO.
