Previously Received Quantity Overview

The Previously Received Quantity column [PRV-Q] indicates that a store employee has already received some merchandise for a specific product. Always review this column before you receive any product to eliminate receiving errors. Confirm the Previously Received Quantity if you see this question: Received quantity is greater than the ordered quantity, is this correct? 

The Received Quantity Column [Rec-Q] indicates all currently received merchandise.
