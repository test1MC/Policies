Receive Merchandise Procedure

Step

Action

1

Receive the merchandise against the purchase order inside the Merchandise Management System.

2

Verify that the quantities and unit costs are accurate prior to accepting the receipts.

3

Verify the receipts on the manager's workstation. Make sure that the complete flag is set to Y [Yes] for all received merchandise.

4

When the following message appears, Store employees with at least Level 2 Security should select Yes if sure the quantity and cost are accurate.

5

The purchase order does not have to be completely finished in order to accept the In Process receipts. After making this selection, you will still be able to receive additional quantities until all lines are marked with a complete indicator of Y and the store manager closes the purchase order. Accepting all In Process receipts to give permission for Sherwin-Williams to pay the vendor for received merchandise where the complete flag is set to Y [Yes] for a specific line on the purchase order.

6

The status box in point of sale helps to remind store employees of the required actions to take. The statement Unverified in-process receipts must be reviewed immediately. Until these receipts are accepted, Sherwin-Williams will not pay the vendor for the merchandise.

7

If the merchandise has not shipped, select Cancel Unnecessary Purchase Order from the Purchase Order Menu.

Note: If there is any chance that the merchandise could be delivered to your location, do not cancel the purchase order.

8

You have two choices to Cancel the purchase order if the merchandise has been shipped:

If

Then

The merchandise has already shipped

Receive the merchandise, then process a Charge Back to return the merchandise to the Vendor

The vendor tells you to refuse the shipment

Tell the driver that you are refusing the shipment and then enter the quantity of zero (0) when you receive against the purchase order. Add a comment to the received purchase order documenting your action.
