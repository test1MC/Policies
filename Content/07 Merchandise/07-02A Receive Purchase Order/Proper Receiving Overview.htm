Proper Receiving Overview

  • Improper receiving causes:
  • Incorrect perpetual inventory
  • Incorrect Store WAC
  • Incorrect costing of sales
  • Incorrect margins
  • Delayed or non-payment of vendor invoices
  • Loss of early payment discounts

Proper Receiving is a crucial part of the Merchandise Management System. If the merchandise is not received on the store computer system, your perpetual balances won't be updated, store WAC won't be accurate, and the vendor invoice won't get paid on time. Verify that all merchandise is received on the store computer system as soon as it arrives at your store. Make sure that you accept the receipts with the proper quantity and unit cost as soon as possible.

  • The external invoice is not set to be paid until you accept the lines that have been completed.

Merchandise from the DSC, outside vendors, or another Sherwin-Williams store must be received against a Purchase Order. Merchandise Management provides the tools to properly receive and verify the accuracy of all orders. Compete the receiving process by granting approval for Headquarters to pay the vendor invoice.
