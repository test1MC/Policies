Automotive Branch Freight for Merchandise & Expenses Policy

This section does not apply to Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, General Polymers, and SW Paint Stores

All freight invoices must be handled through DDI, the auditor of freight bills for the Automotive Division. Freight carriers are advised on the Store BOL to submit their freight bills directly to DDI and, in most cases, the freight carrier complies.

  1. Branches must use one of the approved freight companies listed on the Approved Carrier List on the SOURCE hompage - > Automotive - > Operations.
  2. If a freight carrier requires payment at the time of delivery, you are not permitted to write a store draft.

Note: For questions regarding this program, contact Tony Coifani at 216-515-8581.
