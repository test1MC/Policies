Receive & Verify Procedure

On Hand Inventory + Received Merchandise = New Inventory Level

Any line item on the Purchase Order that has a difference between the quantity shipped and the quantity received will be flagged as a variance. Immediate action in correcting these variances will ensure the accuracy of your inventory and billing.
Note: For more information, see Receive STAR Order Procedure and Receive ISR Merchandise & Variances Procedure.

Processing Loss and Damage when Receiving/Verifying

1

Inspect all cartons for any discrepancies or damage on the freight bill.

2

Ask for the driver's signature to acknowledge the damage or discrepancy.

3

Ask the driver to include the OSD (Over Short Damaged) number on the bill of lading.

Note: For more information, see 07-05 Loss And Damage Claims.

Processing Special Orders when Receiving/Verifying

4

Contact the customer and inform them that their order has been received

5

Keep a log with calls to the customer to help facilitate follow-up on special orders.

Note: For more information, see 07-10 Special Order Received
