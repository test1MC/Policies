Freight for Merchandise & Expenses Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

All freight invoices must be handled through Accounts Payable. In most cases the freight invoices are sent directly to Accounts Payable.

Step

Action

1

After receiving the merchandise, ensure that  it is the correct freight amount on the Purchase Order.

Note: Ensure the Purchase Order and Store Number  are clearly show on the original freight invoice.

2

Process the freight invoice through SWeep. Include a note stating, FREIGHT INVOICE PLEASE PAY IMMEDIATELY and all purchase information.

Note: To process an original freight invoice for expenses, do so through Expense Invoice Submission Procedure.

3

If a freight carrier requires payment at the time of delivery, do not write a store draft. Use one of the approved freight companies:

  • American Freight Ways
  • U.S. Freight
  • Central Freight
  • Conway

Note: If  unable to use one of the approved freight companies, contact Accounts Payable at 216-566-2297 and provide details as to why the freight company requires a store draft, a contact person, and vendor phone number. To complete a new Vendor Request, see New Vendor Request Procedure for more information. Accounts Payable will notify you if they establish a open account with the vendor/freight.
