Replenish & Invoice Merchandise Overview

Inventory can be replenished in one of two ways:

  • The Sherwin-William’s Sales Representative, Branch Manager, or designated employee will visit the shop and inspect the consignment cabinet’s inventory.  Any inventory consumed and/or removed from the consignment cabinet should be replenished to the levels on the Consignment Inventory Master Form.  A shop order should be initiated based on the inspection of the cabinet. The branch will ship and invoice the merchandise as indicated on the shop order. A Consignment Inventory Tracking Form is available in the forms repository for tallying reorder quantities.
  • A shop can phone in an order to the branch.  The branch will invoice and ship the products ordered.
