Consignment Inventory Reconcilement Form Procedure

Step Action
1 Find the Consignment Inventory Reconcilement form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “reconcilement” or “inventory” in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.
2

Use the Consignment Inventory Reconcilement document as a guide to process transactions.
3 Access the customer’s account number.
4 Select the order when prompted Review Order?
5

Select 2. Selected Lines from the Order Lines To Be Sold menu.
6 Press ENTER on the correct item. Once you have selected all of the items for the transaction press ACCEPT to add the items to the sale.
7 Adjust the quantity if necessary.
8 Total the transaction.
9 Staple a copy of the Backorder [identifies all merchandise remaining on customer order] to a copy of the point of sale transaction and Consignment Inventory Worksheet. Retain in a folder for audit review.
