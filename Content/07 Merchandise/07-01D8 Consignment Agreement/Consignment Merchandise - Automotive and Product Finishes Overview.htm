Consignment Merchandise – Automotive and Product Finishes Overview

This section does not apply to Chemical Coatings Facilities, Duron Stores, General Polymers, or SW Paint Stores

The Region Director of Sales must approve all new consignment arrangements prior to merchandise being given/ consigned to a customer. This approval must be completed and documented through the E-Promo process. Please click here for more information on Consignment Merchandise – Automotive and Product Finishes Overview.

The Area Sales Manager must perform the following duties:

  • Oversee the entire consignment process
  • Ensure the consignment merchandise is replenished and invoiced accordingly
  • Confirm monthly that the consignment documentation is properly maintained
  • Verify that the Master Agreements are posted in the consignment cabinets, and accurately reflect levels approved in the consignment agreement

The Sales Representative must perform the following duties:

  • Obtain approval for any consignment prior to installation by initiating/completing an E-Promo consignment request (instructions found in the Forms Repository)
  • Maintain the inventory in the cabinets
  • Frequently inspect the cabinets, initiate orders for stock replenishment, monitor inventory levels
  • Ensure all paperwork is handled correctly
  • Assist branch management with semi-annual physical inventory inspections
