Purchase Order Controls Policy

  1. Someone in Store Management is required to enter the current STAR Order days exactly as assigned.
  2. Purchase Order Controls are part of the set-up for the Merchandise Management System. Maintenance will need to be performed if your order and/or delivery day changes in the future.
  3. STAR Order entry and delivery days are required for proper operation.
  4. Any movement from one STAR Order to two STAR Orders a week must be reflected in Purchase Order Controls. If not, the system will not suggest Memo Demands properly.
