Price Maintenance – WT & WC Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Store employees are able to adjust the selling price for Window Treatment and Book Order Wallcovering that the store carries in stock. This enables you to enter your selling price so that it is returned at the time of the sale.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, select Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Maintenance -> Window Treatment/ Wallcovering Price Maintenance.

2

Enter the appropriate window treatment Style number or Wallcovering Book Number.

3

Select the appropriate window treatment size or Wallcovering Pattern.

4

Enter the appropriate selling price for the merchandise. Press Enter.

Note: You will notice that the <OK> in the bottom right corner of your screen is highlighted. Make sure the selling price is correct and press ENTER. If the selling price is incorrect press the TAB key and adjust the selling price.

Note: Any time you order merchandise, the purchase order selling price will overwrite the stocked price when the purchase order is released.
