Inventory Price List Policy

Inventory Price List may only contain products that are not nationally priced listed.

The selling price for stock merchandise that is not nationally priced is placed in a special Inventory Price List. The selling price entered on the Purchase Order is used to price all customers. Always capture the Manufacturer's Suggested List Price on the Purchase Order for any item for stock. This will ensure that the List Price will return to the screen when you sell the item. Enter the sales number and special price on the customer's Price Record Card if the customer requires special pricing on Inventory Price Listed products.

Merchandise must be labeled with the sales number and selling price if it will be stocked on the sales floor.
