Adjust the Window Treatment or Book Order Wallcovering Selling Price Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, select Main Menu -> Merchandise Maintenance ->Maintenance -> Window Treatment/Wallcovering Price Maintenance.

2

Enter the appropriate Window treatment Style Number or Wallcovering Book Number and select the appropriate Window Treatment Size or Wallcovering Pattern.

3

Enter the appropriate selling price for the merchandise. Press Enter.

4

Ensure the selling price is correct and press Enter.

Note: If the selling price is incorrect press the TAB key and adjust the selling price. Any time you order merchandise, the purchase order selling price will overwrite the stocked price when the purchase order is released.
