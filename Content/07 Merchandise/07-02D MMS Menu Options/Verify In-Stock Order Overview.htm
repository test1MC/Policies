Verify In-Stock Order Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

After ordering In-Stock wallcovering on your Suggested STAR Order, the store will receive an Instock EDI Confirmation Report that will identify the original order quantity and quantity shipped. You might not receive in-stock orders sent to the vendor if the wallcovering is backordered. This type of backorder is automatically canceled. Reorder the In-Stock wallcovering if the report designates that it will not be shipped because the manufacturer will not fill any backorders and they will not send any partial orders.

Note: Access the In-Stock EDI Confirmation Report by holding down the SHIFT key and pressing the Reports [F4] key.
