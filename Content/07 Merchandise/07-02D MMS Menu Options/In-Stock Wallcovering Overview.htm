In-Stock Wallcovering Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

You are required to manually enter and maintain Min/Max Levels for any optional in-stock wallcovering patterns. The optional in-stock patterns are selected from the approved Optional In-Stock Wallcovering list issued by Stores Group Marketing.

Imperial Gallery is considered an optional program. This means that if the Imperial Gallery is in their store, an employee is responsible for setting and maintaining their own Min/Max Levels. Gallery stores are each unique as to which patterns are stocked. By the nature of the program, the vendor should rotate out non-selling patterns and fill in with patterns. Manually adjust Min/Max Levels each time your in-stock wallcovering is rotated. Once the Min/Max Level has been established, the system will suggest an order quantity like the national Plan-O-Gram’d patterns.

If you want to review Min/Max Levels for Optional In-Stock patterns that have already been established, follow the procedure but select 08 **STORE ADDITIONS" instead of "37 IN-STOCK WALLCOVERING. If one of your optional in-stock wallcovering patterns becomes one of your Plan-O-Gram’d patterns in the future, the sales number will be moved from 08 * * STORE ADDITIONS ** to 37 IN-STOCK WALLCOVERING.
