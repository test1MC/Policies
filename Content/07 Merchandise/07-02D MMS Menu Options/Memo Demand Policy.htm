Memo Demand Policy

  1. Any store employee can create a Memo Demand.
  2. The Memo Demand information should be considered while placing STAR Orders. Memo Demands are automatically considered when a Suggested STAR Order is generated.
  3. Memo Demand quantities must be considered for STAR Orders with delivery dates closest to the required date.
  4. Memo Demand history will remain on the store computer system.
  5. Advance the required date to reflect a future STAR Order date.
