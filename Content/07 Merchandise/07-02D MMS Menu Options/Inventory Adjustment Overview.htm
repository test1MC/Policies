Inventory Adjustment Overview

The Inventory Adjustment process will modify the perpetual inventory to match actual on-hand inventory. Inventory Adjustments should be performed whenever merchandise has been incorrectly received on an internal purchase order or a variance is noted as a result of a cycle count. An accurate perpetual inventory is critical to the success of the Merchandise Management System.Inventory Adjustments will not affect shrink or pick-up in your inventory. Shrink or pick-up will continue to be calculated after physical inventory.
