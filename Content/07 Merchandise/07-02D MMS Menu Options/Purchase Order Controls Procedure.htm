Purchase Order Controls Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, select Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Maintenance -> Purchase Order Controls.

2

Enter STAR Order/Delivery Days.

3

If the store orders and receives one STAR Order per week, complete the first column only. If the store orders and receives two STAR Orders per week, complete the first and second columns.

4

Press ENTER after completing Purchase Order Controls maintenance.
