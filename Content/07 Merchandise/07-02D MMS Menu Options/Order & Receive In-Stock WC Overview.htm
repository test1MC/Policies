Order & Receive In-Stock WC Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches or General Polymers

Remember to receive in-stock wallcovering against the "E" prefix Purchase Order that was downloaded from Headquarters.

The in-stock wallcovering lines on your STAR Purchase Order will display as zero "0" quantity ordered received and marked Y complete in the C column after the "E" prefix Purchase Order has been created. Receive in-stock wallcovering against the "E" prefix Purchase Order not the original STAR Order.

  • If only in-stock wallcovering was ordered on the STAR Purchase Order, the STAR Purchase Order will be automatically closed. If in-stock wallcovering is on your regular STAR Order with other items, the Store Manager is required to review and close the Purchase Order.

To receive against the "E" prefix Purchase Order, select Merchandise Management -> Receive/Verify-> Order. Use the ARROW keys to find the correct "E" prefix Purchase Order.

Note: You will not be able to place In-Stock wallcovering orders on an Add to STAR or Emergency order.
