Conversion Adjustment Procedure

The Conversion Adjustment allows you to decrease the perpetual balance of a kit or a case of merchandise and the increase the perpetual balance for the individual pieces of merchandise in the kit or case.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, select Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Adjustment - > Conversion Adjustment.

2

Enter the sales number for the product you want to deduct a quantity from. Then enter the quantity to be deducted from the on-hand quantity.

Note: The extended value is calculated based on the quantity to be deducted and the current WAC for the product.

3

Enter the sales number and the quantity for the product you want to add quantity to. 

Note: An extended value for the quantity added is calculated. This extended value must add up to the cost of the extended value for the product we are deducting from plus no more than one half (1/2) cent per unit.

4

Press Accept [F1] and answer Yes to the question; Process this Breakout?
