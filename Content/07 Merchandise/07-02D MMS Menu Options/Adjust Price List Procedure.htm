Adjust Price List Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, select Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Maintenance -> Inventory Price List.

2

Enter the sales number for the product that needs adjusted.

Press Enter.

Note: If necessary, press the END key to move to a blank line. The system will quickly move to the appropriate line.

3

Change the selling price when you find the product. Press Enter and Accept when finished.

Note:  The new sell price will be used to price all sales transactions.
