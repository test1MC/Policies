Hand-held STAR Purchase Order Procedure

Step

Action

1

After selecting STAR Order, the employee will be automatically placed in the current STAR Purchase Order, if available. If there is no current STAR Order in the system, the employee will be allowed to begin a new STAR Purchase Order.

2

You have several options when placing a STAR Order:

  • Add or change entries on the current STAR Purchase Order by scanning products while walking around the store.
  • Begin a new standard STAR Order by scanning products while walking around your sales floor and back room.

3

Regardless of the method chosen, the process is the same. Enter the Sales Number, scan the UPC Code, or manually enter the UPC Number when prompted for the Sales/Product Number.

4

Press the Up Arrow Key if you choose to change the current Ordered quantity.

5

To increase the Ordered Qty, enter the additional amount in Add QTY, press Enter when complete.

6

The current sales number stays on the screen until you either scan or enter a new number.

7

When you are finished entering products, press ACCEPT [F1].

8

Select Change/Release from the Purchase Order Menu on any terminal to release and complete the order.
