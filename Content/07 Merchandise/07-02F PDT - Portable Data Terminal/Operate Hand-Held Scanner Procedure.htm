Operate Hand-Held Scanner Procedure

Step

Action

1

Point the scanner at the bar code and press the trigger.

2

The thin, red laser beam covers the entire length of the bar code. The terminal indicates a successful scan by illuminating the green scanner LED, beeping one or more times, and/or displaying the decoded bar code on the screen.

3

If you are having trouble, try a slow up and down scanning motion. Try to move the scanner slowly down toward the bottom of the bar code, keeping the beam horizontal, and then slowly back upward toward the top.
