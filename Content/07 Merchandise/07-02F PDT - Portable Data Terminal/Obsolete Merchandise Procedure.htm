Obsolete Merchandise Procedure

Merchandise may only be placed in obsolescence during the October 31st inventory.

Step Action
1

To record an obsolete product while taking inventory, press O on the keyboard. Obsolete returns to the screen.
2 The product number that entered or scanned will be recorded as obsolete
3

Press the letter O while recording each obsolete product.
