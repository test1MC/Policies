Receive Purchase Orders on Hand-held Procedure

Step

Action

1

After entering your Employee Number and Password, employees are able to receive incoming merchandise with the Hand-held Scanner. Once the entire receiving process is completed, review and verify the Received Purchase Order(s) for any discrepancies between the quantity ordered and the quantity received.

2

From the POS Desketop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Receive/Verify to perform  the review on a store computer system terminal. All variances will filter to the bottom of the Purchase Order.

4

Store Manager closes all external orders, while employees with at least Level Two Security can close all other purchase orders.

5

Enter the Terminal/Transaction Number before closing Direct Ship Orders.

6


After completing the receiving process on the hand-held unit and pressing ACCEPT [F1], press CANCEL [F5] to return to the MMS System Menu. [Hold down FUNC and press ACCEPT [F1] to accept or CANCEL [F5] to cancel.]

7

Due to space limitations on the hand-held screen, the Purchase Order numbers do not include the first 5 digits (does not include your store number) after the alpha prefix. (i.e. A012340000005 will show as A0000005).
