Change Hand-Held Battery Procedure

Step

Action

1

Detach the hand strap. Insert the battery pack in the bottom of the compartment. Two posts on the battery pack must fit in corresponding spaces in the housing.

2

Push the battery pack's upper surface down until it lies flat in the compartment, flush with the housing.

3

When it lies in the position, the lift tab snaps in and the contacts meet. Employees may wish to charge another battery pack to have a spare available.

4

Reattach the hand strap.
