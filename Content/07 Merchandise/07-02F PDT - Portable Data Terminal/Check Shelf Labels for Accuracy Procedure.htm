Check Shelf Labels for Accuracy Procedure

The Price/Stock Check option from the MMS Main Menu allows you to check shelf labels for accuracy.

Step

Action

1

Appoint someone in your store to routinely maintain the shelf labels.

Note: It is critical that Shelf Labels be put in place when they are received but not before the shelf label Effective Date.

Verifying Shelf Label Accuracy

2

Select Price/Stock Check from the MMS Menu.

3

Scan the bar code with the hand-held scanner. The information displayed below will return to the screen.

NO: 1118-99993

SZ: 16 GALLON

CL 99 FLT LTX BSE X

EFFECTIVE DATE

02/23/97

 

D.I.Y. PRICE

20.49

 

 

ON HAND QTY

124.00

 

 

ON ORDER QTY

0.00

 

4

Verify the accuracy of the DIY Price.

5

Check the Effective Date on the Shelf Label. If the Effective Date is a prior date, remove and replace with an updated shelf label immediately.

6

Verify that all multiple locations have an updated Shelf Label.

7

Continue this process until complete. Press CANCEL [F5] to return to the MMS System Menu.
