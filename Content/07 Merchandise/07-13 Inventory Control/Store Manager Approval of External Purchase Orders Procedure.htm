Store Manager Approval of External Purchase Orders Procedure

Verify that all External Purchase Orders, appearing in Received status, are being Closed timely by the Store Manager. This is the automated Store Manager approval to ensure that the Store Manager is aware of all External Purchase Orders.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management > Receive Verify.

2

Select Order from the Receiving Options menu.

3

Confirm that the only Purchase Orders in Received status are within the past week.

Note: The Store Manager must accept each Purchase Order appearing in Received status. When the Store Manager completes this process, the Purchase Orders appear in Closed status.
