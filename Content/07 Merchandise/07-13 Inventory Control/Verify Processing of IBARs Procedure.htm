Verify Processing of IBARs Procedure

Purchase Type

Description

External Purchasing

External invoices will be paid based on what is received. Each line on an External Purchase Order will not be compared for payment until the line is approved as received. External invoices do not get paid until a store employee approve the receipts on the Purchase Order.

Internal Purchasing

Internal purchases automatically get charged to the store. A review process will be performed that takes the Internal purchases that have been charged to the store and compares them to the approved receipts. Correcting adjustments will automatically be made for any quantity variances.

Note: Employees are not required to review the monthly Stores Purchases Reports (S.P.R.) related to Internal and External purchases. (Remember that it is required to review the S.P.R. for expense items).

Step

Action

Verify that IBARs are being processed

1

After receiving a copy of the store’s IBARs, staple a copy of the IBAR with a copy of the Purchase Order. Place the stapled IBAR and Purchase Order in a Waiting for S.P.R. review folder.

2

After receiving the S.P.R., verify that the IBAR cleared on the S.P.R.

Note: This review process is not required. These procedures are in place to outline the process if stores wish to review the S.P.R. for cleared IBARs.

3

Eventually, after all Sherwin-Williams' stores are using the Merchandise Management System th,e payment of both Internal and External invoices will be based on your approved receipt of merchandise.
