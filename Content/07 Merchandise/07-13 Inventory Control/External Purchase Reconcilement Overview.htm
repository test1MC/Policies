External Purchase Reconcilement Overview

The Merchandise Management System will automatically handle the pricing and quantity Chargebacks. Store employees are required to handle all expense invoices. For more information, see Expense Invoice.

Invoice payment and reconcilement is an automatic process for all merchandise based on the comparison between what was billed and what was received. Purchase Orders and the vendor's invoice are systematically matched at Headquarters. Any variance for costing or quantity will be automatically supported by a Chargeback.

If the store receives any vendor invoices mail them to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
Merchandise Accounts Payable Department
P.O. Box 5819, Cleveland, Ohio 44101

Note: Only original copies of invoices and transmittals with original approving signatures will be accepted for payment. By strictly enforcing these policies, we will be able to reduce the possibility of duplicate, erroneous or unauthorized payments.
