Cycle Counts Overview

The Inventory Control option enables employees to perform Cycle Counts and record and print your inventory.

A Cycle Count is a periodic counting of a small selection of products to verify actual on hand to perpetual quantity.  Attempts should be made by the store manager to reconcile any variances that are noted between what is physically counted in the store compared to what should be on hand per MMS inquiry.

When the store is audited, the field auditor will conduct a cycle count of randomly selected items.  Unreconciled variances during the auditor cycle count could result in an audit infraction being noted. A status box reminder message indicates the total number of unique cycle counts performed during the current week (Sunday through Saturday).

Product Service Room (PSR) Store: Cycle Counts should be performed for any product that is ORDERED, RECEIVED, and SOLD using the same sales number.
