Automated Bill Paying Overview

Purchase Orders that reflect received merchandise are compared to the vendors open invoice file at Headquarters. The comparison will first take the Purchase Order number from the invoice and compare it to the Purchase Order file to find a match:

If

Then

No match is found

The invoice is returned to the invoice file. If the invoice remains unmatched, the invoice will be returned to the vendor requesting them to follow-up with the store to validate the Purchase Order number.

Match is found

The merchandise amount on the invoice is matched to the merchandise amount on the Purchase Order.

If

Then

No discrepancy

The invoice is sent to Accounts Payable.

Pricing or quantity discrepancies

Chargeback is automatically prepared. Both the invoice and Chargeback are sent to Accounts Payable.

Expense invoices are not automatically processed. Follow the current procedures for processing / paying expense invoices. For more information on these processes, please refer to the Expense Invoice Submission Procedure.
