Review STAR Order Status Procedure

Review STAR Orders and ensure that all previous orders are in Closed status. A New status STAR Order must be released on the STAR Order day. A New status STAR Order has not been polled and the order will not be filled. A Released status STAR Order has not been received in the system. 

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Print/View Purchase Order.

2

Enter the appropriate From and To dates. Press Enter.

3

Select STAR from available PO Types. Press Accept [F1].

4

View the Status column to ensure that all previous STAR orders are in Closed status.

Note: A STAR order that has not yet arrived in the store will appear in Released status.

Note: This procedure can be used to review Suggested STAR Orders as well.
