Check Star Order Status Procedure

Review Star Orders and ensure that all previous orders are in Closed status. A New status Star Order must be Released on the Star Order day. A New status Star Order has not been polled and the order will not be filled. A Released status Star Order has not been received in the system.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management Menu -> Purchase Order.

2

Select Print/View Purchase Order.

3

Enter the appropriate From and To dates.

4

Press Enter until you get to the P.O. Types selection. Select Star from available P.O. Types Menu.

5

Press ACCEPT [F1].

6

View the Status column to ensure that all previous Star Orders are in Closed status. A Star Order that has not yet arrived in the store will appear in Released status.

Note: Suggested Star Orders -02 follows the same review process as Total Star Orders (Above). The perpetual inventory and the Min/Max levels should be adjusted to reflect the changes made to the Suggested STAR Order. Lines 09 SKU's with Min/Max changes and 11 Total SKU's on perpetual should show adjustments if line 03 is high. If adjustments are not reflected in the numbers, contact the Store Manager.
