Ensure Complete Review of Min & Max Tabs Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Maintenance -> Min/Max -> Plan-o-Gram Sequence or Product/Rex Number Sequence.

Note: A date will appear in the Completed Column when a thorough review of the entire tab has been conducted. This date represents the date that a Min/Max level was accepted for a sales number to indicate that the tab was completely reviewed.

2

Press Page Down twice to view all 35 tabs -> Navigate the highlight bar to Store Additions to check that all DSC stocked products have established Min/Max levels in the system .

3

Ask the Store Manager to review this tab -> If a discontinued product appears in this tab, please set the Min/Max level to zero.

4

If the Store Manager needs to enter regional DSC stocked products into the system, select Individual Sales Number -> Enter Appropriate Sales Number, APR-SEP Current Min and Max, and OCT-MAR Current Min and Max.
