Review Manual Purchase Order Entries Procedure

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop -> Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Purchase Order -> Print/View Purchase Order.

2

Enter the Start Date when prompted Enter Starting PO Entered Date -> Press Enter when prompted Enter Ending PO Entered Date to select today.

3

When prompted to Enter Beginning PO Number, enter B to bring back Manual Purchase Order Information (Prefix B) -> Press ACCEPT [F1] .

4

View the Purchase Order Number column to ensure that all have been entered into the system.

Note: Every Manual Purchase Order not received by the end of the second day of MMS Implementation must be entered into the system.
