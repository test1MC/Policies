Ready-to-Go Inventory - Record Potential Obsolescence Procedure

Record all Potential Obsolescence using the POS or handheld scanner. Do not clear the inventory.

If

Then

POS

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, go to Main Menu -> Merchandise Management -> Inventory Control -> Inventory -> Record Inventory.

2

Enter F1 as the location.

3

Press spacebar and then enter a sales number.

Note: After pressing spacebar, Ready-to-Go will appear at the bottom of the screen. Make sure to press spacebar before scanning each product.

4

Enter the quantity to be recorded as Ready-to-Go.

5

Repeat this process for each item to be recorded as Ready-to-Go.

6

Once finished entering all of the counts, press Cancel [F5] until you return to the MMS Menu.

Note: It is not required to close inventory.

Step

Action

1

From the Handheld Scanner Main Menu -> Record Inventory -> Rolled Inventory.

2

Scan product.

3

Press spacebar.

Note: After the first entry, the handheld defaults to RTG.

4

5
