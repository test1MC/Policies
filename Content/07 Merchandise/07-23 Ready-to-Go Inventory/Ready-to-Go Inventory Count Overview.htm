Ready-to-Go Inventory Count Overview

The purpose of the RTG count is to bring awareness to inventory on hand that will be approaching Shelf Life expiration, determined by product batch date. This process is meant to assist with inventory management and stock rotation (sell RTG inventory first). There is a designated timeframe to review on-hand inventory and enter the counts based on the criteria below. If items identified as RTG are not sold before November Physical Inventory, they will be counted as current year obsolescence and the branch will incur the obsolescence expense.
