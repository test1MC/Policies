Purchase Order Cost Overview

Store employees are required to enter the current store cost for each item placed on external purchase orders. This configuration helps reduce the number of Chargebacks for external purchase orders and to eliminate the number of EDI rejections due to incorrect costs.

The unit cost field in Purchase Orders and Inventory Adjustments allows up to four decimal places. There are two options while entering the unit cost:

Option

Action

1

Enter the unit cost without pressing the decimal key to automatically capture two decimal places (e.g., .45, $12.99)

2

Press the decimal key at any time while entering the unit cost. The decimal will be fixed, allowing you to enter up to four digits after the decimal (e.g., $1.25, .435, .5436)
