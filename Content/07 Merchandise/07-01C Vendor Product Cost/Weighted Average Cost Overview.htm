Weighted Average Cost Overview

The Merchandise Management System uses a Weighted Average Cost (WAC) for all outside buy products. This accounting function allows you to purchase the same product at different costs from different sources and calculate an average cost for every unit of that product in your inventory.

Here are some examples of how the Weighted Average Cost is calculated:
