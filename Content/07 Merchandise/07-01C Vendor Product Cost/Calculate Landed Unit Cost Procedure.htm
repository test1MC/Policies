Calculate Landed Unit Cost Procedure

Step

Action

1

Calculate the received line items percent of the total extended cost.

2

Calculate the received line items amount of total received freight by multiplying the received line items percent of total extended cost.

3

Divide the received line items amount of received freight by quantity received.

Note: This process will provide a true landed cost for all products except book wallcovering where the freight is sold as a separate line item and not included in Landed Unit Cost.
