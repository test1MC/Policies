Change & Release a System IBAR Procedure

Change/Release a System IBAR for visible shortage and damage.

Step

Action

1

From the POS Desktop, select Main Menu -> Merchandise Management-> IBAR.

 

Select the IBAR created for your order.

Note: The system-generated IBAR will have a status of NEW.

2

Verify that the IBAR accurately lists all damaged or missing products. Products may be deleted from the IBAR before it is released, but additions of product and changes to quantity are not permitted.  If these types of changes are required, process a Concealed Damage / Shortage / Overage IBAR once the system-generated IBAR has been released.

3

After releasing the system-generated IBAR, credit will be granted once reviewed by Paint & Coatings. If Paint & Coatings rejects the claim, provide Stores Merchandise Billing with a signed and dated copy of the BOL within 10 days.
