MMS IBAR Overview

An MMS IBAR is generated by the Merchandise Management System [MMS] for internally purchased products to identify any variances between the quantity the store received on the purchase order versus the quantity that was billed to the store.  Each MMS IBAR number will be sent in a separate e-mail and require someone in store management to review the MMS IBARS Inquiry screen, available through My Paint on SOURCE, and complete the MMS IBAR – Store Response associated with each IBAR number.  Questions regarding the MMS IBARs should be addressed to mmsibars@sherwin.com or contact Store Billing at 216-566-2297. You may also print the MMS IBARs Job Aid for quick reference.

IBARS may be either system-generated (IBARS created during receiving and MMS IBARs) or store-generated (Concealed Damage / Shortage / Overage, Returns, and Transfers.)  For more information on IBARs generated manually by the store, please see Store-Generated IBAR Overview .

System-Generated IBARs are automatically created while receiving an internal STAR purchase order via Personal Data Terminal [PDT] for any items that have been identified through the receiving process as visibly missing or damaged. This generated IBAR identifies any variance between the quantity received [RCV-Q + PRV-Q] compared to the quantity shipped [SHP-Q] to the store. For more information on receiving STAR purchase orders, please see Receive STAR Order Procedure.
