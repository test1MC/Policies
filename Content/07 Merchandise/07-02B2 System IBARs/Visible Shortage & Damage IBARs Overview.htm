Visible Shortage & Damage IBARs Overview

The system generates IBARs for visible shortages and visible damage discovered during receipt. This provides many benefits to the stores:

  • Paperless - no IBAR or supporting documentation needs to be sent to Headquarters if store employee accurately documents shortages/damages and has driver's signature.
  • Faster approval/disapproval and credit.
  • Easier receiving and greater chance of having the driver stay for pallet receiving so the driver can sign for any Visible Shortage/Damage.

A system-generated IBAR is created for any visibly damaged or visibly shorted product have identified while receiving the STAR Order once the STAR Purchase Order is closed.  In order to receive credit for these, the IBAR must be reviewed and released by a member of store management.
