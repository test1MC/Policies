MMS IBAR Policy

  1. MMS IBARS must be reviewed upon receipt of an email notification that an MMS IBAR has been generated for the store.
  2. After reviewing, the MMS IBAR Questionnaire – STORE RESPONSE must be completed within 7 days of the IBAR Trans Date shown on the MMS IBARS Inquiry through My Paint. If the 7 day time period has expired, the response must be completed immediately upon review of the entry on the MMS IBARS Inquiry in order to avoid IBAR shrinkage expense being booked to the store unnecessarily.
