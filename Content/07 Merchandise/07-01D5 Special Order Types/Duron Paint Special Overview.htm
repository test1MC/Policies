Duron Paint Special Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches, Product Finishes Facilities, MAB Paints Stores, or SW Paint Stores

Part

Description

Stocked Product Specials

Stocked in the warehouse but restricted to a District Manager approved list of stores. This type of stocking should only be used for extremely stable customers who will continue to use the product on a long-term basis.  These products must have an ordering pattern repetitive enough to create a forecast model.  The District Manager is required to file a new Stocking Special Approval Form for any forecasted special to be stocked in the DSCs. No forecast will be accepted for amounts under 6000 gallons annually. Once stock is confirmed on-hand in the DSC, order Stocked Product Specials by placing a STAR Purchase Order in MMS.

Make-N-Ship Product Specials

Not stocked in the warehouse.  This arrangement is typically used for new products when there is no history of demand, stable relationship with the customer, or where product usage is small.  You will be required to specify a specific amount of product required.  No requests below 250 gallons will be honored and you must accept a 10%+/- variance on all requests.  To order Product Specials complete a Special Order Log and submit to the Plant Manufacturing Coordinator [Beltsville – 1-800-723-8766 X3321 / Atlanta –1-800-476-2367 X2367]. A Manual “B” prefix Purchase Order must be completed to order any Product Specials.
