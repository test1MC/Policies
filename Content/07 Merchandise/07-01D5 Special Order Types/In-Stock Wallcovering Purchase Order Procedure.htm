In-Stock Wallcovering Purchase Order Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Place In-Stock Wallcovering orders at any time by following by ordering the product on a STAR Order. In-Stock Wallcovering will be taken from the STAR Purchase Order and placed on an E-Prefix Purchase Order, which will appear on the store computer system. The in-stock wallcovering order will be forwarded to the vendor.

Step

Action

1

Receive in-stock wallcovering against the E-Prefix Purchase Order and not the original STAR Order.

2

The in-stock wallcovering lines on the STAR Purchase Order will display as zero "0" quantity ordered received and marked Y complete in the C column.

Note: If only in-stock wallcovering was ordered on the STAR Purchase Order, the STAR Purchase Order will be automatically closed. The store might not receive in-stock orders sent to the vendor if the wallcovering is backordered. This type of backorder is automatically canceled. The store will not be able to place In-Stock wallcovering orders on an Emergency Order.
