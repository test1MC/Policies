KST Orders Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches, Product Finishes, or General Polymers

KST products should be ordered using internal purchase orders.  The order type and process to follow will be determined by the Stocking Code on the product. Please refer to the Stocking Code on the KST Product Listing to determine which process is correct for the product(s) the store wishes to order.

Orders for DSC stocked KST products (Stocking Code 1) may be placed using STAR, Emergency, Customer Direct, or Will Call order types and will route to one of the top six DSCs depending on the product stocking location.  Please use mypaintconnection to verify stocking locations before placing orders.  Be aware that product not stocked in your regular replenishment DSC may take additional time to reach your location. 

Orders for non-stocked KST product (Stocking Code 2) must be placed using the Make-n-Ship order type and will route to Largo, Olive Branch, or Victorville with a 10 day lead time.
