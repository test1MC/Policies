Out-of-Territory Order Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Out-of-Territory Orders are used to obtain product from an alternate warehouse when your prime warehouse is out of stock. Out-of-Territory orders are placed using the Emergency Order when being shipped directly to the store. No Emergency Order fee will be assessed for an Out-of-Territory order.  Out-of-Territory Orders may also be placed using the Customer Direct Order Type to ship product directly to the customer.

  1. Division approval is required for any orders placed under the 240- gallon minimum through the Overstock Inventory Reduction Program.
  2. Stores should create an Emergency Order or Customer Direct Order for their Out of Territory Order but should NOT release this order in the system.
  3. Stores MUST call Customer Service [Arch: 1-888-677-8673 / CC: 1-888-792-4364] before releasing the order in SOURCE.  Customer Service will instruct the store to release their order in SOURCE when appropriate.
