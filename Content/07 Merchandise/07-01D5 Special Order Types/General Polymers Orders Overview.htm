General Polymers Orders Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

General Polymers product may be ordered using any internal purchase order type.  Orders must be called into Customer Service before being released in SOURCE.  Orders will route to one of the top six DSCs depending on the product stocking location.  Please use My Paint Connection Processes Procedure to verify stocking locations before placing orders.  Be aware that product not stocked in your regular replenishment DSC may take additional time to reach your location.  Make-N-Ship Orders should be placed using the Make-N-Ship procedure and will automatically route to either Cincinnati or Victorville.  Please refer to Make-n-Ship Orders for additional information.  Sample Order requests should be referred to Cincinnati at 1-513-761-0011 X3201.
