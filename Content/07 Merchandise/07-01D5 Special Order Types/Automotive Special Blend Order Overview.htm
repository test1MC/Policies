Automotive Special Blend Order Overview

This section only applies to Automotive Branches

The Richmond DSC has the capability of blending small batch orders in minimum sizes of 24 gallons. Requests for special blends and stocked intermixes are to be made by completing a Special Blend Product Order Form and an Inter Store Request (ISR) to Branch #9929. Complete the required information, including any special comments and instructions. Products on Small Batch Orders are to be entered utilizing Intermix Prefix/Suffix part numbers.  Once completed, mail or fax the Special Blend Product Order Form to the following:

Sherwin-Williams Automotive Finishes Corp
Richmond Distribution Service Center
401 Boggs Lane South
Richmond, KY 40475
Fax: 859-623-2050
