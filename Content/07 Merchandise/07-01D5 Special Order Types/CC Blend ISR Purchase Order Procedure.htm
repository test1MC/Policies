CC Blend ISR Purchase Order Procedure

Step

Action

1

Generate a customer order to capture customer specific information.

Note: If the Blend Rex Number is unknown, press the tab key to quickly enter the customer request. You'll be able to update the order later when you have all of the information. Use the Customer Order as a guide to begin generating an Inter Store Request Purchase Order.

2

Create the Purchase Order:

Step

Action

1

Select Inter Store Request from the Purchase Order Types List.  Press ENTER on the correct Order Option.

2

Choose SPECIAL if the merchandise is to be shipped directly to the customer. If choosing Special, include the Customer Address and, if requested, Customer Purchase Order information. Choose Stock if the merchandise is to be shipped to the store location.

3

Enter the Product Finishes Facility store number, the correct Shipping Method, and Shipping Charge.

4

Press Lookup [F3] to enter sales or product number and select Intermix Blend from the Product Lookup Menu.

5

Enter the Blending Facility Store Number.  Then, press Enter and select the blend from the Blend Search Menu.  If this is a new blend which has never been ordered:

  • Enter the Blend Rex Number when prompted for Formula Name. 
  • Answer YES to the question Formula Name not in system, do you want to create it?
  • Enter the customer's color name, part number, product size, and gloss, if available.
  • Select DONE when finished.

5

Enter the quantity and cost. Press Proceed [F12].

6

Enter the Estimated Receipt Date.

Note: Add any Special Instructions.

7

Release the ISR Purchase Order.

Note: Receive against the ISR purchase order when the product is delivered to the facility.

8

Update the original customer order:

  • Select Update/Review Orders from the Order Entry Menu.
  • After locating the order, press UP ARROW to move to the Sales Number Field. Press LOOKUP [F3] and select INTERMIX BLEND from the Product Lookup Menu.
  • Press TAB and change the store number to reflect the store number for the CC Blending Facility and press ENTER.

9

The information will appear on the Blend Search Menu. Highlight the blend and enter the product size and press ENTER and select DONE.
