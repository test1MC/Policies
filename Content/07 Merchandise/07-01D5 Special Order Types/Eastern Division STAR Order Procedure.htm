Eastern Division STAR Order Procedure

Step

Action

1

The Store Manager must complete an Outside of Division Product Form and fax this form to the District Manager for approval. The District Office will then fax the approved form to the Marketing Services Manager in the Division Office at 610-640-4506.

Find the Outside of Division Product form in the Forms Repository. Search form name or keyword “outside div" in the Form Name field. All available forms containing your search word will appear below in the form description field.

2

Upon receipt of the completed form, Vice President of Sales approval will be obtained.

3

The order will be placed by the Division Marketing Department.

Note: The store will only be notified if the request has been denied.
