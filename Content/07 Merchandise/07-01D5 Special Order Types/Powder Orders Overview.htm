Powder Orders Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches, Duron Stores or MAB Paints.

Product Finishes Stores ONLY:

  • Color Card Powder Orders may be ordered on STAR, Emergency, Will Call and / or Customer Direct.  Customer Color Powder Orders may be placed on Emergency, Will Call and / or Customer Direct orders.  NEVER use a Make-N-Ship order for ordering powder.  The system will not recognize the order and will error.  Please refer to Powder Coating Customer Color Purchase Order Procedure for additional information.

Architectural/IM Stores ONLY:

  • Powder Orders for Architectural/IM stores should be placed on External Purchase Orders to the vendor, SW POWDER COATINGS. Orders to this vendor are not EDI-capable.  Please refer to External Purchase Order Procedure or Powder Coating Customer Color Purchase Order Procedure for additional information on this process.
