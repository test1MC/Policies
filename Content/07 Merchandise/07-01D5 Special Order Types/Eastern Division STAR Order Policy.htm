Eastern Division STAR Order Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Please be aware that Eastern Division stores are only approved to order National Line products and Eastern Division products. On occasion, a customer from another division may be working on a job in the local area. When an employee attempts to order another division’s product on your STAR Order, the product will be automatically dropped from the order.

  1. Eastern Division stores are not permitted to order a Divisional/Regional product from another Division without prior District Manager and Vice President of Sales approval.
  2. Each specific request for another division’s product must be approved in writing prior to ordering the product.
