Duron Paint Special Orders Procedure

Step

Action

1

Determine if the store must place a Product Special (Make-N-Ship) or Forecasted Product Special Order.

2

Complete, sign and fax the Special Order Log form to the Duron Plant Manufacturing Coordinators. Remember to include the Manual “B” Prefix Purchase Order number on the form. Also remember to enter the manual purchase order information into Merchandise Management. Please refer to MANUAL PURCHASE ORDER for more information.

3

Staple a copy of the Manual Purchase Order to the store copy of the Special Order Log form. Retain in a folder titled Special Order Log.

4

Continue with the normal lead-time for stock to be in placed at the DSC. 

5

Obtain the inventory status from the SPAM Report or the Plant Manufacturing Coordinator.

6

Review the SPAM Report weekly for inventory levels.

7

After receiving District Manager approval, order Stocked Special Products on the normal STAR Order in Merchandise Management.
