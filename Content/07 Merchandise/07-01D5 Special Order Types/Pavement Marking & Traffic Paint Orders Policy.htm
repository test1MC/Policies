Pavement Marking &Traffic Paint Orders Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

  1. Long line and agency accounts purchasing traffic paint in totes, drums, tank wagons, and/or tons (thermoplastic and beads) are serviced direct through the Pavement Marking Business.  No sales should be made through stores of these items.  The following states are exempt from this policy and will be able to place purchase orders and make sales of these items:  MT, WY, CO, NM, AZ, UT, WA, OR, NV, and CA. 
  2. All stores are able to place purchase orders for five-gallon pails of traffic paint in truckload quantities or less using the process outlined below.
  3. Pavement Markings (Highway Paint / Traffic Paint) Orders for Architectural/IM stores should be placed on the appropriate Internal Purchase Order Type to fit the ordering situation.  Any Internal Purchase Order Type may be used to order Pavement Markings.  Please note that any special pricing for a Make-N-Ship Order MUST be noted in Special Comments.  Questions concerning any Pavement Markings Orders should be emailed to hwycustserv@sherwin.com or called into 1-800-368-2026.
  4. Stores should create an Internal Order but should NOT release this order in the system.
  5. Stores MUST call Customer Service [GP: 1-800-368-2026] before releasing the order in SOURCE. Customer Service will instruct the store to release their order in SOURCE when appropriate.
