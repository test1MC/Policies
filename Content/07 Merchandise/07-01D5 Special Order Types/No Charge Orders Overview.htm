No Charge Orders Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

No Charge Orders that will not include any cost will be handled by the Division Office and placed manually with Customer Service. Promotional free or no charge merchandise must be placed on the same purchase order with the merchandise that qualifies the buyer for the free or no charge merchandise as long as the free merchandise is a different sales number. The promotion name must be noted in the comments section of the free line item. This is necessary for proper pricing of the promotional line item. Additional free merchandise at your regular cost must be placed on a separate purchase order because the sales number already appears on the purchase order at a zero cost.
