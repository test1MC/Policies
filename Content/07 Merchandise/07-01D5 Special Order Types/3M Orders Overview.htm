3M Orders Overview

This section does not apply to Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, MAB Paints, or SW Paint Stores

Automotive Branches ONLY: 3M products (excluding Striping Tapes and miscellaneous slow-moving items) may be ordered on your STAR Order to the DSC. The DSC will NOT honor backorders for those DSC-stocked 3M products. Products not stocked at the DSC will be routed directly to 3M for fulfillment. Products being filled directly by 3M will not be received against the STAR Order. These products will be received against using a special E-prefix Purchase Order, which will be downloaded into the branch system. Only those items being fulfilled by 3M will appear on this order. 3M will not honor backorders for material not available at the time of the order. Stores may:

  • Reorder the backordered items on your next STAR Purchase Order. This order should follow the process described above.
  • Order the products locally through a local Distributor or another vendor. You will have to manually enter an external "A" prefix Purchase Order. This will require that you manually enter the Store Cost and Quantity.
  • Issue an external "A" prefix Purchase Order directly to 3M. You will have to provide the cost and quantity; however, the purchase order will be electronically submitted to 3M.

Automotive Branches will receive a PO Acknowledgement from 3M for this automated order. The Status Reminder window of SOURCE will note that a PO Acknowledgement is available under Reports once 3M has sent the document. This report will be viewable in the Reports Menu [access using SHIFT+F4] and will show the E-prefix Purchase Order Number, those products ordered on the order, and the line item status for each product.
