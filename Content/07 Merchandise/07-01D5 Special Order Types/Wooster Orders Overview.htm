Wooster Orders Overview

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches, Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, or SW Paint Stores

MAB Paints ONLY. Wooster products may be ordered on a STAR Order to the DSC.  Wooster products will be stripped from the STAR Order and forwarded directly to Wooster.  Products being filled by Wooster will not be received against the STAR Order. These products will be received against using a special E-prefix Purchase Order which will be downloaded into the store system. Only those items being fulfilled by Wooster will appear on this order. Wooster will not honor backorders for material not available at the time of the order. Two options are available:

  • Reorder the backordered items on the next STAR Purchase Order. This order should follow the process described above.
  • Order the products locally through a vendor using the SOS Order process for Local Buy Products.

Stores will receive a PO Acknowledgement from Wooster for this automated order. The Status Reminder window of SOURCE will note that a PO Acknowledgement is available under Reports once Wooster has sent the document. This report will be viewable in the Reports Menu [access using SHIFT+F4] and will show the E-prefix Purchase Order Number, those products ordered, and the line item status for each product.
