Chemical Coatings (CC) Auxiliary Warehouse Order Procedure

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches, Duron Stores, MAB Paints, General Polymers and SW Paint Stores.

Product Finishes facilities may need to create purchase orders for product to be stored at an auxiliary warehouse location other than their main facility warehouse.

Step

Action

1

Create an Emergency Order, but do not release the order in the system.

2

Facilities must call Customer Service [1-888-792-4364] before releasing the order in SOURCE. Customer Service will manually change the Ship To Address on the order per the CC Facilities needs. Customer Service will instruct the store to release their order in SOURCE when appropriate.
