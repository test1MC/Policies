Vendor Complaint Policy

  1. An In-Store Resolution Product Quality Report (PQR) must be completed for non-GOM product complaints.
    1. For more information, see the 08-12 Product Quality Reporting.
  2. Settlement authority levels must be followed when processing a vendor complaint.
  3. Customer receipt is required. If customer does not have receipt, purchase must be verified using POS or online statements.
  4. Refund must be processed using the same form of payment as the original purchase.
  5. The vendor must be contacted for specific processing procedures.
  6. POS Return Forms must be signed by the customer, Store Manager, and employee preparing the form.
  7. A Release Form must be completed and signed for all complaint settlements totaling at least $100.00.

Note: Products manufactured by outside vendors (Non-GOM) are the responsibility of the vendor. For a current listing of approved vendors and phone numbers, please look in the Source -> Information Library -> Information Directory -> Store Direct Vendors.
