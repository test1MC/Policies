Vendor Direct Deposits Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches

Any product (excluding spray equipment) that is purchased directly from a vendor and has had no sales for four consecutive months will be automatically written off to obsolescence. The IMS Obsolescence of Store Direct Purchases Report will help track the status of these products.

Follow these guidelines when you choose to purchase directly from the vendor. These guidelines should not limit the ability to purchase pre-sold merchandise directly from a vendor.

  1. Only purchase stock merchandise directly from a Vendor:
    1. If approved by the District Manager prior to purchase.
    2. If an immediate demand already exists for the product.
    3. If the quantity purchased is limited to a 3 month supply based on your store rate-of-sale.
  2. Purchase directly from a Vendor if the merchandise is pre-sold.
  3. Purchase the quantity necessary to meet current customer demand.

For more information, see01-07 VENDOR REFUNDS.
