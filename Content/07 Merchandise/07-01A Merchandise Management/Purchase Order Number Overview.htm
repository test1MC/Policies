Purchase Order Number Overview

Purchase Order Numbers are used to match receipts to the corresponding vendor's invoice. While processing a customer sale transaction, be sure to enter the appropriate Purchase Order information in the PO field. Be aware:

  • Not all customers require a PO, but this information may be essential in order for SW to get paid for the invoice.
  • Whatever is typed in this 22-position field will appear on the customer’s invoice and charge account statement.
  • Be certain to accurately enter  information.

If the Purchase Order Number you created differs from the Purchase Order Number on the vendor's invoice, the match does not occur and the processing of that invoice is delayed. This could also impact your inventory, which affects your Weighted Average Cost [WAC].

Our Vendors have been instructed to only accept valid Purchase Orders with either an A or B prefix. Invalid Purchase Orders will delay the order and payment process.

Purchase Order prefices enable you to identify the Purchase Order source:

Description

Purchase Orders generated at your store will have one of the following alpha characters

A

Automated Purchase Orders placed through the store computer system

B

Manual Downtime Purchase Orders placed at the store. Manual Purchase Orders must be entered on the store computer system once the system is working properly.

Purchase Orders generated by the Store Installation Manager for your store

I

Prefix used for new store initial opening orders

Purchase Orders generated for your store by Headquarters

AT

Purchases placed by Sherwin-Williams Answer Team (S.W.A.T.)

E

In-stock wallcovering ordered on a STAR Order and shipped from the vendor

F

Auto shipments from DSC, Plant, or External Vendor

H

Call-for-Order for promotional merchandise at special costs generated by your District Manager

W

Wallcovering Books (Automatic Shipments)

X

Orders generated by Headquarters for expense items used in store operations
