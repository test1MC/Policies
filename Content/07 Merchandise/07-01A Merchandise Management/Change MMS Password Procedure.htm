Change MMS Password Procedure

All employees are required to enter their employee password to perform any Merchandise Management functions.

Step

Action

1

When prompted, enter the same employee number used to process Point of Sale transactions .

2

Select CLEAR  [F8] to change your password.

Note: For more information, see SOURCE Intranet Passwords Overview.
