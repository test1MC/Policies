Merchandise Management System Overview

Merchandise Management automates the processes for ordering, receiving, and counting inventory for most products. Stock Keeping Unit (SKU) sales numbers are assigned to all merchandise ordered and sold through your location. The SKU enables Sherwin-Williams to accurately track items purchased, inventoried, and sold in every location. Merchandise Management keeps an inventory on all items ordered, received and sold at your location. Inventory levels for Intermix Products and Spray Equipment Repair may not be accurate.

  • Products in an Intermix ordered under a sales number and used in an Intermix should be adjusted in your inventory by performing a Perpetual Inventory Adjustment. If your inventory does not properly reflect intermix products, do not use the Suggested STAR Order.
  • Spray Equipment repair items sold using a sales number will properly relieve your perpetual inventory. If you lump together all items that go into the repair and use a class number, your perpetual inventory will not be adjusted properly unless you perform Perpetual Inventory Adjustments.

All internal and external purchases are recapped on the Stores Purchases Report. The Stores Purchases Report lists all purchases charged to your location and enables you to quickly and accurately identify all purchases. The new SPR review process has been moved to FACTFinder. For more information, see Stores Purchases Report (SPR) Procedure

Note: For more information, refer to 04-01 Class Controlled Exceptions.
