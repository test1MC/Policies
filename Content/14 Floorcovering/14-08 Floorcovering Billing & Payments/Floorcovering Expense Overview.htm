Floorcovering Expense Overview

The floorcovering process allows for the expense staging of material at several points:

  • Material identified as a Roll Balance through the cutting process.
  • Product added to the Work Order as an expense item not to be billed to the customer.
  • Product identified through Work Order Reconciliation for Expense Disposal.
