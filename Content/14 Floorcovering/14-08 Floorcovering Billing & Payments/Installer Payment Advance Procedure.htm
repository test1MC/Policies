Installer Payment Advance Procedure

Step

Action

1

After verifying that the customer is satisfied with the installation, process the installer payment.

2

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft -> Enter installer information -> When prompted for the Terminal/Transaction Number, press LOOKUP [F3] or Enter.

3

If

Then

A Terminal/Transaction Number is not entered.
  • Select Pay Installer Prior To Billing Customer
  • Enter the cash or charge account number for the customer that is billed for the floorcovering
  • Press COMMENT [F9] and enter reason for early payment.

You choose to pay the installer and will not charge the customer.
  • Select Customer Will Not Be Billed and provide the reason.
  • If appropriate, select Trip Charge, Measure Charge, or Other and enter the reason for not billing the customer.

3

After completing the transaction and validating the store draft, attach the store copy of the draft to the pink store copy of the installer transaction.

4

Place a copy of payment information in the customer job file.

5

Cross-reference all documents when you are able to charge the customer for installation. Capture the installer payment terminal/transaction number and date in the first screen while billing the customer for installation.
