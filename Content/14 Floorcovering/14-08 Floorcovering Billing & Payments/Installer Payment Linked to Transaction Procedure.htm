Installer Payment Linked to Transaction Procedure

Use this procedure to automatically tie the customer's transaction to the installers' IPA Account.

Step

Action

1

After entering Non-Merchandise Labor on the customer's invoice select the name of the installer or press CANCEL [F5] if you do not wish to link the labor line to an installer.

Note: If you enter more than one labor line on the customer’s invoice you may choose to only link one of the lines to the installer. Doing this you will only have one labor line per customer transaction when you pay the installer. You will be prompted to enter the total amount of labor when you pay the installer.

2

Once you have invoiced your customers for merchandise and labor you are ready to pay your installers.

3

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft.

4

When you are prompted, enter Term # or press Enter for TERM/BILLING INFO or LOOKUP [F3] for transactions.

5

Labor lines will return to top of the screen for all completed and processed sales transaction installed by this installer.

6

Select the installed job that you want to pay the installer for -> Press Enter.

Note: All customer transaction information returns to the screen except for the dollar amount. You are required to enter the amount paid to the installer. This will enable you to quickly see how much you charged the customer and how much you pay the installer (the S column indicates that you have selected this transaction).

7

Once a selection from the Customer Labor Charges menu is linked to an installer payment transaction, it will not be available for future installer payment transactions.
