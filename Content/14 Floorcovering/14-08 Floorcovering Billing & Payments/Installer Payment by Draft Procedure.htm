Installer Payment by Draft Procedure

Step

Action

Receiving the installer’s bill

1

Compare it to the customer’s invoice copy that reflects the installation charges.

2

Check the accuracy of the installer’s bill and note any profit or loss made on the transaction.

Paying an Installer by Store Draft

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft -> Enter installer information.

Setting up Installer Payment Account

1

Call your District Credit Office.

2

Set up the accounts as follows:

  • IPA - Last Name, First Name or IPA - Business Name
  • Complete address and phone information
  • Installer Federal Identification Number or Social Security Number

Note: The IPA account numbers will be opened with a credit lock-out status. Installers should be instructed to submit installation invoices/bills for each job.
