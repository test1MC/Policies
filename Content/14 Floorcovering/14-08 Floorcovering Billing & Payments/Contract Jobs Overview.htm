Contract Jobs Overview

The Contract Job option should be used:

  • For Installed orders when a contract customer requests that their invoice displays only one line item for all material and one line item for all labor.
  • When there is a difference in billing and relieved inventory.

By selecting Contract Job, the Contract Job Class Number will automatically be applied in Point-of-Sale to ensure the customer invoice displays correctly while inventory is still appropriately relieved.
