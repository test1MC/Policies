Floorcovering Sales Overview

Before billing the customer, be sure that you have made any changes required to the customer order if material was not used or labor charges required adjustment. You will receive an error if you have not properly adjusted the Customer Order after reconciliation. If the quantity reconciled does not match the COE Quantity, the line will not be sold to the customer. If the entire amount was reconciled, you can elect not to adjust the COE and complete Order Disposition.
