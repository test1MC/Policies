Installer Retainage Refund Policy

  1. Retainage amounts are required for installers. See . Installation service companies that are bonded and insured and warranty their work may be exempt from retainage based on our agreement for their services.
  2. Retainage must be held for installers while they are active installers for Sherwin-Williams and for a minimum of twelve (12) months after discontinuation of installer services to cover any settlements that may arise after installation.
  3. Refunds to installers require District Credit Office approval. This approval code must be noted in the Account Adjustment Transaction. Installer refund over the required minimum amount does not require an approval.
  4. The installer MUST sign the Computer Generated Form as proof of account adjustment and receipt for any retainage refund. Include the home/business address and phone number.
  5. Installer Retainage Refunds will only be processed on installation accounts defined with a CAC code of 5555.
  6. Installer must be contacted to make repair/etc. prior to deduction to retainage account (on sub-contract agreement).
