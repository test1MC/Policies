Installer Payment Advance Policy

  1. Installers are not to be paid in full for a job until the job is completed and inspected.
  2. If necessary, a partial payment (draw) can be made to the installer for the amount of work completed.
  3. Before issuing a partial payment, contact the customer to make sure that the completed portion of work meets their satisfaction.
  4. Advanced payment can only be made for the completed portion of installation. The installer is not to be paid prior to performing the installation.
  5. The customer must be satisfied with the completed installation prior to payment.
  6. The partial payment (draw) must reference a current job in process.
  7. All payment information must be kept in the customer job file.
