Multiple Installers or Single Payment Procedure

Step

Action

Process Multiple Installers/Single Payment
1 From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Add Order -> Installation.

2

Add separate labor lines for each installer in COE.

3

Create separate Work Orders from the COE for each installer. You will be able to approve and pay each installer and bill the customer for the total installation for all installers.

Handling Previous Overpayment/ UNDERPAYMENT

1

To handle overpayment or underpayment, modify payment on the next job by modifying the installer price within customer order entry or the work order.  

Note: The customer price should NOT change.
2 From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft -> Enter installer information and labor details -> Press TOTAL [F12] and you will be prompted to enter the retainage amount.

3

Press COMMENT [F9] while processing the installer payment to document the reason for the change in installer payment. Notice the original job reflects either an installer loss (overpayment) or installer gain (underpayment) and that the job with the correction reflects the loss (underpayment) and gain (overpayment).
