Installer Garnishment Overview

This section is for all Floorcovering Facilities and any location that installs floorcovering.

A garnishment is a court order attaching an installer's earnings to satisfy a debt incurred. Sherwin-Williams is notified of the garnishment by service of papers on them.These papers vary from state to state but usually are one of the following:

  • Garnishment Summons
  • Writ of Garnishment
  • Writ of Attachment.
  • Order to Withhold Income
  • Order of Garnishment
  • Request for Garnishment on Wages
  • Income Withholding Order
  • Wage Deduction Summons

Other types of wage attachments, which require S-W Draft deduction, are:

  • Child Support Order
  • Federal Tax Levy
  • State Tax Levy
