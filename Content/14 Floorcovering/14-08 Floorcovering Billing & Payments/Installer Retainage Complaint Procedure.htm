Installer Retainage Complaint Procedure

To process an installer complaint against retainage amount when a second installer is used, follow these steps:

Step

Action

1

If the original installer has a retainage balance, ask your District Credit Office for a debit ARJV.

Note: The ARJV will remove the requested amount from the original installer's account and it cannot be issued for more than the retainage balance on the installer's IPA account.

Paying the installer that performed the work

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft -> Enter installer information and Press Enter when prompted for the Term/Tran Number.

2

Select Customer Will Not Be Billed.

3

Select Customer Satisfaction.

4

Select Original Installer Not Available.

Note: The installer payment transaction done in this mode will debit the complaint account. The ARJV will credit the complaint account and remove the amount from the original installers account. The two entries will wash.
