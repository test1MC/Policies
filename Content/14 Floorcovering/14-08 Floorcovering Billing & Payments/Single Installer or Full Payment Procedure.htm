Single Installer or Full Payment Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Press the Page Down Key to access the Special Menu.

4

Select Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft.

5

Enter the Manager Password.

6

Enter the Draft Number and use LOOKUP [F3] to search for the Installer Account.

7

 

Use LOOKUP [F3] to search for approved work orders payable to the installer whose IPA has been recalled when entering the Pay-To Info on the draft. Approved work orders are those that have undergone Reconciliation.

WARNING: Failure to pull work orders into the installer payment will result in unclosed work orders remaining in the system—these will be recorded on the Floorcovering Scorecard and may be resolved through Work order Disposition. For more information, please see the Work Orders Overview.

8

The Installer Labor Charges Screen will show labor charges and labor breakdown. You can also see if the customer has not yet been billed. ARROW to the order and press ENTER to select.

9

Details for the work orders and the payable amount for labor will display.

If

Then

The labor will not be billed to the customer for a satisfaction settlement
  • Press ENTER on the line below the labor and select Customer Will Not Be Billed.
  • Select Customer Satisfaction from the Reason List.
  • Make the appropriate selection from the Customer Satisfaction Labor menu. All of these selections will clear against prime-sub 7504-101 Customer Complaint F/C Installation.

10

Press ENTER on a blank line to include payment for a Trip Charge or Measure Charge to the installer. 

  • Select Customer Will Not Be Billed.
  • Select the type of charge for which you are paying the installer.
  • Enter a Quantity of 1 and the amount to be paid the installer.

Note: If you manually key in a transaction number that had a work order, then something is not linking. The work order will remain open and no clear out. Work orders will show up in the Open Work Orders Report. This will affect your score card.

CANADIAN STORES ONLY: 

  • Press ENTER on a blank line to include payment HST. 
  • Select Customer Will Not Be Billed.
  • Select Other as the Reason.
  • Enter HST Payment as the Reason.
  • Enter a Quantity of 1 and the amount to be paid the installer.

11

Press ACCEPT [F1]. 

12

Make the appropriate Retainage Menu selection. 

Note: For more information on retainage, see .

13

Press ACCEPT [F1] to complete the transaction. Manually write the Store Draft for the installer.
