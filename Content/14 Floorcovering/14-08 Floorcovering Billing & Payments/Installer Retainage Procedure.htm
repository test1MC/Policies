Installer Retainage Procedure

Retainage can be used for retaining a portion of the installer’s check, future installer repairs, or to retain.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft -> Enter installer information and labor details -> Press TOTAL [F12] and you will be prompted to enter the retainage amount.

2

If

Then

Retainage does not apply

Press Enter with the highlight bar on No Retainage

Retainage does apply

Select either Percent or Dollar Amount.

Note: The amount retained will print on the installer payment invoice.

3

The following Store Draft information will return the screen -> Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue or CANCEL [F5].

STORE DRAFT INFO
Draft amount:$490.52
Pay to the order of: Joseph Installer
Draft number:1011-0001-4

4

Validate the draft by lining up the arrow on the draft with the line on the Validation Printer Plate, then press Enter.

5

Make the draft out to the installer and have an authorized signer sign the draft.

6

Attach the store copy of the draft to the utility copy of the installer transaction.

7

Retain these documents in the Validated Store Drafts file.
