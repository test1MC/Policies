Installer Payment by Draft Policy

  1. Each installer must be set up with a special Installer Payment (IPA) Account prior to making any payment to the installer (installers with these special accounts will be the only installers that you will be able to pay).
  2. The Installer’s Identification Number/Social Security Number, is required when the Installer Payment Account is opened at the District Credit Office.
  3. Each installer must have a criminal background check performed by your District Office before doing any installations.Contact your District Coordinator for more information.
  4. Open a separate installer charge account for each installer, if approved, for miscellaneous purchases. DO NOT deduct supplies from the installer’s bill.
  5. A completed Subcontractor’s Agreement, W-9 Form, Declaration of Liability Insurance and Worker’s Compensation Insurance must be on file at EACH store (or District Credit Office) that uses the installation services of the installer.
  6. A completed Subcontractor’s Agreement outlining retainage must be completed and on file prior to retaining.

    Sherwin-Williams Company
    Stores Group Bookkeeping

  9. Installers are to submit a detailed bill or request for payment for each installation before payment is received. The installer’s bill must be stapled to the installation copy of the Proposal and Contract.The installation copy must be signed by the customer and the installer.
  10. The accuracy of the installer’s bill must be checked against the transaction charging our customer.
  11. Customer satisfaction must be verified before paying the installer.
  12. Sherwin-Williams employees must properly complete the Installer Payment transaction and include all relevant information for all installer payments.
  13. The store copy of the draft must remain in the book until the book is completed. Print a Store Draft Register and account for every draft.
