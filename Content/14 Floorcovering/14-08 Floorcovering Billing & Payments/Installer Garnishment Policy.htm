Installer Garnishment Policy

This section is for all Floorcovering Facilities and any location that installs floorcovering.

  1. Garnishment documents and other wage attachments are court orders. Response to these orders must be taken seriously and must be acted upon immediately.
  2. Failure to respond promptly could result in fines and/or court costs that will be charged to the floorcovering facility.
  3. Any person who willfully obstructs this legal process could be subjecting themselves to contempt of court proceedings.
