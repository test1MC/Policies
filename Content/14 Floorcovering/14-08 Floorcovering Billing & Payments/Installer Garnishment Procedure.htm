Installer Garnishment Procedure

This section is for all Floorcovering Facilities and any location that installs floorcovering.

Step

Action

1

Immediately contact your District Manager if you receive a garnishment notice or any other type of wage attachment.

Withholding installer earnings to satisfy the court order

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Disbursement -> Installer Payment by Store Draft -> Enter installer information and labor details -> Press TOTAL [F12] and you will be prompted to enter the retainage amount.

2

If you retain a portion for payment of the garnishment and for future repairs, you will need to add retaining amounts together.

3

Enter the total dollar amount to be retained.

4

The installer payment transaction will be processed minus any retainage amount.

5

Process an Account Adjustment against the installer's account to write a check to the agency requesting payment.

6

Select Retainage Refund from the Adjustment Reason to process the garnishment check to the requesting agency.

7

Enter the Terminal/Transaction Number and the date of the installer payment transaction.This will cross reference the retention amount with the payment amount.

8

Press the COMMENT [F9] key to capture relevant information to tie the payment to the court order.

9

After entering the check number at form of payment, overwrite the installer’s name with the requesting agency payee information. Reference the Garnishment letter for the payee and submittal address.

10

Follow the guidelines given in the court order to submit the store draft for payment of monies owed.

11

Retain the following documents in a folder titled Garnishments, Installer:

  • Original court order documentation.
  • Copies of all corresponding Account Adjustment Retainage Refund transaction.
