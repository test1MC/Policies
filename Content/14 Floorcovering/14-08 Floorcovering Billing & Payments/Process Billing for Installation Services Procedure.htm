Process Billing for Installation Services Procedure

Step

Action

1

If

Then

The customer will eventually be charged for the installation or services

Select Pay Installer Prior to Billing Customer from the Term #/Billing Info screen.

Enter the Account Number for the customer that will be billed for the Installer Payment.

You wish to pay the installer and not charge the customer for the installation or services

Select Customer will not be billed from the Term #/Billing Info screen. Proceed to Step 2.

2

If you selected Customer will not be billed, selections from the Customer Satisfaction Labor Menu will appear:

REASON

  1. Trip Charge
  2. Measure Charge
  3. Customer Satisfaction
  4. Other

Note: Selections 1, 2, and 4 will show as an installer loss.

3

Choose the appropriate selections from the menu and they will clear against GL Description Customer Complaint F/C Installation – 7504 – 101.

Note: If the IPA Transaction does not tie to a customer billing, the IPA Transaction will not cross reference on the Installer Inquiry Reports.
