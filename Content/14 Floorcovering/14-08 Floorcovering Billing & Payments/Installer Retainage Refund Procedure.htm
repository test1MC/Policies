Installer Retainage Refund Procedure

Step Action

1

Contact the District Credit Office to determine what amount, if any, should be returned to the installer. Do not refund any money until the District Credit Office has approved the amount.

2

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale.

3

Select Account Adjustments from the Sale/Payment Menu.

4

Select Retainage Refund from the Adjustment Reason Menu.

5

Identify the transaction:

  • Enter the Terminal Number.
  • Enter the Transaction Number.
  • Enter the Original Date of the sale.
  • Enter the Installer Account Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search.

6

Enter the amount of the refund. Press the COMMENTS [F9] key to input an explanation for the transaction.

7

Enter the Manager Password.

8

Validate a copy of the Store Draft.

9

The installer and Sherwin-Williams employee MUST sign the form.
