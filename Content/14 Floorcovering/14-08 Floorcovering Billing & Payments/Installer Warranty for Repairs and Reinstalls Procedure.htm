Installer Warranty for Repairs and Reinstalls Procedure

If you have to use a retained amount to cover repair or re-installation charges:

Step

Action

1

Contact your District Credit Office to determine what amount should be withdrawn from the original installers retainage account.

2

Do not continue with the transaction until the amount is verified with the District Credit Office. They will remove the amount retained from the installers account and credit your Complaint Expense Account 7504-001.

3

Process the installer payment following normal procedures.
