Floorcovering Sales Policy

  1. Adjustments made in reconciliation to either labor or installed product must be noted in the customer order before billing the customer. This will ensure the match between material billed the customer and material relieved from inventory.
  2. Billing the customer and paying the installer must occur after Work Order Reconciliation. Either transaction may be processed first.
  3. Customers must be billed within 96 hours of install completion. 
    1. A completed install is defined as one for which the store has received a signed customer installation confirmation agreement or an installer invoice. If these documents are unavailable, the Expected Installation Date in POS is also acceptable.
  4. Taxation
    1. Use Tax should be applied as required by state and product type on installed orders in the United States. See Determine Product Taxability Procedure.
    2. Canadian provinces (British Columbia, Ontario, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Newfoundland and Labrador) subject to HST must collect HST on labor and product.
    3. Canadian provinces (Alberta, Manitoba, Prince Edward Island, Quebec, Saskatchewan) not subject to HST, GST and PST should be collected on the product and GST only collected on labor.
  5. The following policies apply only to stores in Canada:
    1. The CanWest Invoice will contain a line for metals used for the installation. This should be added to the sale using non-inventoriable sales number 908-5465.
    2. Metals are NOT to be included in installer payment amounts for labor. Pay the installer for metals used via SWeep.
