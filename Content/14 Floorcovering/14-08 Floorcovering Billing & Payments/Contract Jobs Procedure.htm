Contract Jobs Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Customer Order Entry -> Add Order -> Enter order information -> Select Installation from the Customer Service Options Menu.

2

Select Contract Job as the Billing Type. 

3

Continue entering your Customer Order and create your Work Order as you normally would.

4

Once installation is complete and Work Order Reconciliation has been processed, the customer may be billed.

5

When processing the sale, you will be prompted to select the customer order line items to combine into a single class number that will display a single material price for the customer.

6

After the materials have been entered you will be prompted to enter the labor line.
