Installer Scheduler Overview

The Scheduler allows you to view scheduled installers in a calendar format and to schedule installs without entering work order information. When creating a Work Order, you have the option of entering a Scheduled Date or you may press LOOKUP [F3] to access the Scheduler directly from the Work Order.
