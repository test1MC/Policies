Automotive Floor Coating Policy

  1. The sale of Product Finishes Division Floor Coating products to automotive customers requires the review and approval of the Automotive Region Director of Sales. Written approval, in the form of an email or other document, must be obtained from the Automotive Region Director of Sales for these transactions and kept in the customer’s file at the Branch. For questions about this policy, contact your Area Sales Manager and/or Region Director of Sales.
