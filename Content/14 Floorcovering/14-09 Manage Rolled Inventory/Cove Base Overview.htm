Cove Base Overview

If you send carpet to an external workroom to have the carpet bound or surged and made into carpet cove base, this must be notated in both the customer order and work order. The carpet workroom should be added as a service vendor to SWeep and invoices paid via SWeep. The profit will increase on the carpet cove base line because the selling price should be increased to include the workroom labor charges.
