Roll Color Change Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Merchandise Management.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select Inventory Adjustment from the Merchandise Management Menu.

4

Select Perpetual Adjustment from the Inventory Adjustment Menu.

5

Enter the Sales Number or Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the product.

6

Enter the Color Number or Press LOOKUP [F3] to view Past Color Numbers.

Note: Additional fields can be filled in to help narrow your search.

7

Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue.

8

 

Edit the Color and Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue.

9

Enter a reason for your adjustment in the Memo Field.

10

Do you want to re-print the Tag ID for this roll?

If Then
Yes Select YES and replace the roll tag showing the incorrect color information with this new tag.
No Select No to exit the screen.
