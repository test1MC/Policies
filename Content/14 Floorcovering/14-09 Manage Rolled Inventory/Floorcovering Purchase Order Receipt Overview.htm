Floorcovering Purchase Order Receipt Overview

The receipt of rolled goods into MMS required additional information to be recorded about the product. When receiving a roll good, the RCV-Q Field will not allow direct entry. Type in this field and you will see the Tag ID Options Menu. A new roll may be received by choosing Add New Tag ID. It is important to note that when receiving multiple rolls of the same style and color, each roll MUST be received separately. Modify Existing Tag ID and Delete Tag can be used to correct information during receiving. Once you have accepted all receipts, you will not be able to modify or delete a Tag ID. See Receive & Verify Purchase Order Policy for additional information on the receipt of External Purchase Orders.
