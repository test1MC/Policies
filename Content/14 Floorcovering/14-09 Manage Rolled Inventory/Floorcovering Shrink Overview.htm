Floorcovering Shrink Overview

Common causes of floorcovering shrink are:

  • Shortage of received merchandise
  • Shortage upon delivery
  • Shortage after all cuts have been made from the roll.
  • Failure to file Chargebacks (if the roll is confirmed short) regardless of time elapsed
  • Cutting Material
    • Improper cutting of material
    • Improper calibration of the cutting machine
  • Taking Material from Facility
  • Installers loading materials without the presence of a SW employee
  • Incorrect WAC used to process the transaction: Transactions containing the incorrect Weighted Average Cost (WAC) can be a major cause of shrinkage. It is also the factor over which we have the most control.
  • Vendor charges incorrect price for floorcovering: Make sure that you properly receive all floorcovering and verify the quantity and store cost. Chargebacks will be automatically generated for store costs different than your Received Purchase Order.
