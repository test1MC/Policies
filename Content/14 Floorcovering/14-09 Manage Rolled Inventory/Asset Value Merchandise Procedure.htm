Asset Value Merchandise Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select Merchandise Management.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select Inventory Adjustment from the Merchandise Management Menu.

4

Select Perpetual Adjustment from the Inventory Adjustment Menu.

5

Enter your Sales Number or Press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the product.

6

Press ACCEPT [F1] to continue.

7

Enter/scan the Tag ID or Press ACCEPT [F1] to search for the Tag ID.

8

Select the roll from the returned listing.

9

Use the arrow key to navigate to the Inventory Type Field.

10

Select Asset Value Merchandise (AVM) from the Inventory Type Menu.

11

Confirm your change by typing Y when prompted.

12

A new Roll Tag will print. This must be used to identify the material.
