Floorcovering Bin Locations Overview

If your location inventories product by bin, you may designate a Bin Location in receiving or assign a product to a Bin Location. Bin Locations can be very useful; your cutter will be able to know exactly where the product they wish to cut is located in your warehouse if you inventory by Bin Location. It is recommended that facilities with multiple sites for warehousing or Canadian stores with inventory at multiple CanWest facilities use Bin Locations to identify in which location each roll of carpet is stored.
