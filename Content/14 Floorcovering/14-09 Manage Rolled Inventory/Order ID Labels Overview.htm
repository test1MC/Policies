Order ID Labels Overview

Order ID Labels may be printed during receipt of a special order purchase order created from a customer order or may be printed for any order. These tags will contain Customer Order information such as Order Number, Site Address and can be placed on rolled goods, boxes of tiles, andboxes of wood for easy identification at the store and by the installer.
