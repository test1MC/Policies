Remnant Handling Policy

  1. After a roll has reached a remnant status (either through store expense or asset value assignment), one of the following options should be employed:
    1. IST to select stores to sell as remnants.
    2. Run classified ad in local newspaper under building materials section.
    3. Sell remnants in bulk to specialists for [Close Out Merchandise] (i.e., remnant houses, thrift shops, and property managers).
