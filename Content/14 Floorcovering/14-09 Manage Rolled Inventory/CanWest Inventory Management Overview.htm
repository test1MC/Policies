CanWest Inventory Management Overview

Canadian Stores Only.

CanWest holds floorcovering inventory and performs installations for Canadian stores selling floorcovering. Canadian stores holding their own inventory should follow standard inventory procedures. For stores warehousing inventory at multiple CanWest locations, please see the section on Floorcovering Bin Locations Overview in this chapter for recommendations on how to best track rolls.
