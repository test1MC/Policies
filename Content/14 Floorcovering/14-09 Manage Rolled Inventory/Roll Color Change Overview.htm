Roll Color Change Overview

It is critical that roll color information be accurate. If not correctly recorded, the roll will not show as available when selecting from available rolls to cut for a job as the system will display rolls based on the sales number and color requested in the customer order. Correct Roll Color Number changes through Inventory Adjustment.
