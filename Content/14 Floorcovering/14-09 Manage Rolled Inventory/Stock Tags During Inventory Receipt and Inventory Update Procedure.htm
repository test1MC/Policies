Stock Tags During Inventory Receipt and Inventory Update Procedure

If

Then
Stock Tags during Inventory Receipt

IN-STORE INVENTORY
  • Stock tags should be affixed to their corresponding roll.

OFF-SITE INVENTORY
  • Stock tags should be affixed to their corresponding roll when possible.
  • Stores with off-site inventory should build a Stock Tag Book of all tags received. 

Note: Stock tags will automatically print during the receiving process
Stock Tags during Inventory Updates

IN-STORE INVENTORY
  • Roll Balance Tags should be affixed over the original Stock Tag printed upon receipt of the product or the last updated Roll Balance Tag on the corresponding roll.

OFF-SITE INVENTORY
  • Roll Balance Tags should be affixed over the original Stock Tag printed upon receipt of the product or the last updated Roll Balance Tag on the corresponding roll when possible.
  • Roll Balance Tags should be affixed over the original Stock Tag printed upon receipt of the product or the last updated Roll Balance Tag in the Stock Tag Book. 
  • Roll Cut Tags should be added to the Stock Tag Book as cuts are made. 

Note: Roll Balance Tags and Cut Tags may print when cuts are made to existing inventory.
