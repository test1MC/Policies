Billing Extension Request Policy

  1. Billing extension approvals may only be sought during the open approval period. 
  2. Approvals needed outside the open approval period should be gathered from the DM via email and retained with the Billing Extension Approval List.
  3. The Estimated Completion Date in the Work Order should also be populated with the expected completion date for the installation.
