Billing Extension Request Overview

Bi-annually, your division allows you the opportunity to seek approval for billing extensions (billing occurring after the 96-hour window of completion of an installation or receipt of installer invoice, signed customer installation confirmation agreement, or the Expected Installation Date in the POS) of customer orders in POS to allow for the time needed to manage customer purchase order and installation requirements. Scenarios appropriate for approval requests could include:

  • Delayed PO Approval
  • Customer requiring single invoice over multiple installs
  • Internal approval process for National Accounts
  • Customer approval process delays our process
  • Length of time required to complete installation

Requests to extended billing timeframes will be reviewed and approved by your District Manager and Division Controller.  Approved billing extensions will be available for review: from POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Floorcovering Tab ->  Operational & Installation Excellence
-> Approved Billing Extensions.
