Billing Extension Request Procedure

Requests for extended billing are made using the Billing Extension Request Form found in the Forms Repository. To complete this form during an open approval period:

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Information Library -> Forms Repository.

2

Search for Floorcovering Billing Extension Request Form.

3

Complete all fields of the form in full.

4

Submit complete forms for approval by the deadline established for the open approval period.

Note: You may complete this form as many times as required during the open approval period.
