Floorcovering Draft Payment Procedure

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down to access Special Menu -> Process a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt to 7402099 Service and Supplies – Other.

2

Include the Purchase Order Number and F/C Material Purchase - Recode to 0406805 in the Comments.

3

Fax/Email a copy of the transaction and District Manager approval to 216-566-3084/storedrafts@sherwin.com.
