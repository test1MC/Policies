Floorcovering Material Procurement Policy

  1. Floorcovering Stores may make purchases for material using store drafts only after receiving written approval from the District Manager and SW Accounting. These approvals must remain on file.
  2. Prior to paying with a store draft, have terms setup with Accounts Payable for the vendor and have the vendor pay through A/P.
    1. Approval from Accounting does not need to be on file for each purchase, but must be on file for each vendor paid by draft.
    2. DM approval must be on file for each purchase.
    3. All other PO and invoice policies apply.
