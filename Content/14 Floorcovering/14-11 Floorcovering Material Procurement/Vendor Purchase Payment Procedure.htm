Vendor Purchase Payment Procedure

When selecting vendors with whom to conduct business, keep in mind the procurement hierarchy for inventory purchases and begin by locating the Nationally Approved Vendors List: from POS desktop, select Browser -> SOURCE homepage -> Information Directory ->Store Direct Vendors or referring to the preferred National FC Vendor Listing on the Floorcovering Tab. If the vendor is not available on this list, refer to the chart below:

If Then

New Vendor

The vendor will need to extend terms and be set up in our systems:

  • Provide the vendor with the New Vendor Packet. This packet contains information for the vendor as well as forms which must be completed and returned to us.

Note: If there are questions from the vendor on terms or other questions you cannot answer, please direct them to the Floorcovering Buyer at 216-566-2327

Immediate sales need which can only be filled by a vendor who refuses to give terms

Process a one-time Quick Check to fulfill your customer’s needs. 

Note: See the section on Vendor COD Shipments in Process Vendor COD Shipments QuickCheck Procedure for processing instructions.

Store Draft

Contact the District Manager for approval. Refer to the procedure below: FC Draft Payment.

Note: Contact your District Manager with any situations that cannot be fulfilled using the options above.
