Update Order Details & Apply Use Tax for Installed Floorcovering Procedure

Consider the customer’s taxing situation. Installed floorcovering may be subject to use tax or sales tax. Please review 13-01 Tax Info (non-TRC Stores) or Use Tax Calculation Procedure (TRC stores) for more information on the calculation and application of use tax. If the customer will be exempt and has submitted a valid and current tax exemption certificate, review 13-03 State Tax Bulletins and Forms to determine how the customer will be exempt. All the selected products and quantity (square footage) will be brought into the order. Pricing will only appear if you have set it up on the Customer Price Record Card. 

Step

Action

1

Modify Materials Pricing:

To add pricing for products not found on the Price Record Card, use your ARROW KEY to move to the product line and TAB to the Price Field to enter pricing information. 

2

 

Modify Labor Pricing:

To change pricing for labor imported from the Site Repository, press VIEW [F7] on the labor line item.

3

 

Apply Use Tax:

If Use Tax applies to your customer, you must designate the line items for installation for use tax. 

Use your ARROW KEY to move to the product line and press TAX? [Shift+F11] to access the Tax Exception Window. 

  • Select Use Tax. You will notice that a U displays for the product in the T [ax] Column.

4

Modify Quantity:

  • Press VIEW [F7] on a line item to view and/or modify area requirements imported from the site repository. 
  • Delete the area(s) and/or cut(s) not required.

5

Partial Quantity Adjustment:

  • If the customer has requested only an area for replacement, press VIEW [F7] on the line item. 
  • Delete the area(s) and/or cut(s) not required.
  • Labor (if billed by quantity) and padding quantity should always be adjusted if a single area is being replaced. 

6

Add Material Not in Site Repository:

If the customer has requested a roll good product not in the site repository, it can be added to the order. When prompted to add area requirements, you have two options: 

  • Select Yes to access Area Requirements. Press LOOKUP [F3] at Area Requirements to select from those established in the Site selected for the order. Press ACCEPT [F1] twice to return to line items.
  • Select No to bypass Area Requirements and simply enter the width and length required for a cut.

7

 

FC Material Reservation:

By reserving material in the Customer Order, it will no longer show as available when product is being assigned for cuts against other Work Orders.

  • Press VIEW [F7] to view and/or modify area requirements for the line item for which you would like to reserve product.
  • If you wish to reserve specific material for this order, type Y in the Reserve Column.
  • Area Requirements for the selected area display in yellow. Available rolls show dye lot and dimension information. Move to the roll to reserve and enter in the length you would like to reserve. Press Enter.
  • Press ACCEPT [F1] to return to the Reserve Screen.

8

 

At the Order Detail, you will see Tag Ids reserved for the areas requiring the product selected to reserve and that the product is Committed. Also, note that if, instead, the product were placed on special order with the vendor, you would be able to view the PO# in this area.

9

If the warehouser is using the Cut Zone to manage required cuts, entering a Required Date for each rolled good line item will allow the warehouse to plan cuts as they are needed.

10

Any Disposal Fee charged to the customer should be handled through labor. Always show the Disposal Fee as a separate labor line. 

  • Press ENTER for the Non-Merchandise Codes Menu.
  • Select Installation Labor – No Tax from the Non-Merchandise Codes Menu.
  • Select Do Not Breakdown Labor Line.
  • Enter Disposal Fee as the description, a Quantity of 1 and the associated fee as the Customer Price. Do not make an entry in the Installation Cost.
  • Press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

Note: If Special Instructions were entered on the Site Repository for a customer, these would flow into the Customer Order and this prompt will default to YES. Press ENTER to view and/or edit. Press ACCEPT [F1] to complete the order. If your order is for a customer will call, select Print Picking Ticket from Print/Review Options and provide this to the cutter so that they may make the required cuts. If your order involves installation, the cutter will receive their cut information from the work order.
