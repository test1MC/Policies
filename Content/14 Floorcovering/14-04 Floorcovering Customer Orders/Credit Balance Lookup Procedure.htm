Credit Balance Lookup Procedure

The Customer Balance application online will allow you to quickly check the balance on a customer account for credit worthiness before you release the Work Order.

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access Source homepage -> Select the Credit Tab located under SOURCE Operations.

2

Select Customer Balance from the Credit Tab.

3

Enter the Customer Account Number or use LOOKUP [F3] to search for your customer.

4

 

Balance Information will display for each job.
