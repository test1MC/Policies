Floorcovering Complaint & Credit a Customer to Create a New Invoice Procedure

Step

Action

1

Process a Return Transaction to return the defective materials to your inventory. Remember to include everything on the original invoice including labor.

2

Once the complaint merchandise is back in your inventory determine if this inventory should be charged back to the vendor or expensed to the Floorcovering Complaints G/L Code.

3

If the installer pays for the materials, fill out an ARJV Form and fax to Cleveland and circle Debit which will deduct the funds from the installer’s retainage.

Note: The ARJV cannot be processed for an amount greater than the installer retainage account. If there is not enough in the account, you will need to have another ARJV processed once the retainage amount is able to cover the balance. Your ARJV must be reconciled monthly against the SVR. An alternative method would be to charge the installer for the products through the POS.

4

Finally, create a new customer order for the replacement material and have the new material cut and ready for installation. Handle the replacement installation just like a new customer order and new work order.
