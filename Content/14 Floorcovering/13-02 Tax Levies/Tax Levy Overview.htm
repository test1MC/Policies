Tax Levy Overview

Tax levies are claims that the government places on a taxpayer for funds that are due. Stores may be faced with several types of tax levies.  The most important thing to remember is that a tax levy cannot be ignored - some action must be taken. This section will explain the differences in levies and how they should be handled.

Tax Levy Description Required Action
Wage Garnishments An IRS or state tax department levies on current or former Sherwin-Williams employees.

When the store receives a notice in the mail that concerns a present or former employee and directs Sherwin-Williams to pay over money or is an inquiry from the IRS about that person, mail all of the paperwork to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company
Corporate Payroll
12th Floor Republic Building
101 Prospect Avenue N.W.
Cleveland, Ohio 44115

Note: Do this the same day the store receives the letter. Corporate Payroll will take care of the details.
Other IRS or state tax departmentlevies on carpet installers, paper hangers, contractors, customers, and/or vendors.

When the store receives a notice in the mail, mail all of the paperwork to:

The Sherwin-Williams Company

Corporate Tax Department

P.O. Box 6027

Cleveland, Ohio 44101

Note: The store may be contacted by Stores Group Bookkeeping or the Corporate Tax Department later regarding the taxpayer named on the levy.

Any questions regarding tax levies should be directed to the Corporate Tax Department at 216-566-2648.
