Tax Levy Installer Payment Plans Overview

Payment plans whereby Sherwin-Williams withholds a portion of each check and forwards it to the taxing authority are generally reserved for employees only; therefore, installers would not normally qualify. Any arrangements for payment plans for carpet installers on an ongoing basis require approval of the Corporate Tax Department. A store draft on the installer’s IPA account should be made payable to the US Treasury for funds due and should be sent to:

Sherwin-Williams Company
Corporate Tax Department
P.O. Box 6027
Cleveland, Ohio 44101

The ID number of the installer should appear in the box at the top of the draft although the draft is made out to the IRS. Any funds paid to the IRS on behalf of an installer are taxable to the installer and those funds will be included in the installer’s 1099 form at the end of the year.
