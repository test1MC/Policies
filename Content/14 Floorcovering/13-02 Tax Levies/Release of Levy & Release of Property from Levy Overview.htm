Release of Levy & Release of Property from Levy Overview

  • As long as a tax levy has been served on The Sherwin-Williams Company, S-W must comply with it. Do not believe that the installer has “worked this problem out with the IRS" or “it’s another mix-up." 
  • Note that by the time the store is called by Bookkeeping, the Corporate Tax Department has sent a copy of the levy to the installer at the address the IRS has supplied to Sherwin-Williams. Because this may or may not ever reach the installer, it is recommended that employees notify the installer of the existence of the tax levy as soon as possible.
  • The installer must resolve any tax problems directly with the IRS without involving Sherwin-Williams. It is not resolved until Sherwin-Williams receives a written release of the levy either at the store or in the Corporate Tax Department.  Otherwise, the Store Manager must speak directly with an IRS Agent to obtain a verbal release before any funds can be paid to the installer.
  • If funds are due the person named in the levy, first contact the Corporate Tax Department 216-566-2576. Generally, all funds due that person must be paid to the IRS. A store draft on the installer’s IPA account should be made payable to the US Treasury for funds due and should be sent to:

    • The Sherwin-Williams Company
    Corporate Tax Department
    P.O. Box 6027
    Cleveland, Ohio 44101

  • The ID Number of the person named in the levy should be included on the draft although it is payable to the IRS.
