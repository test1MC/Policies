Floorcovering Scorecard Procedure

Follow these steps to access your Scorecard:

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select the Floorcovering Menu.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select the Reports from the Floorcovering Menu.

4

Select Scorecard from the Reports Menu.

Address scorecard captions with high counts as follows:

Problem

Resolution
Labor Lines Not Approved

Work Order Reconciliation is not complete.

Complete Work Order Reconciliation, approving labor lines. Note that once this step is complete, this line will be move into the caption for Labor Lines Approved Not Linked to Payment.
Labor Lines Approved Not Linked to Payment

Installer Payment has not been processed.

Process installer payment. Be sure to select the work order being paid in the Disbursement Transaction or this line item will not close and a Work Order Disposition will be required to close the line item. Properly processed payments will close the line item, removing it from the scorecard.

Installer Payment processed incorrectly and work order was not linked to payment.

Process Work Disposition, linking installer payment to the sale of the related customer order. This will close the line item and remove the line from the scorecard.

Lines Noted as Expense, but Not Approved

Note: This caption includes roll ends identified by the cutter and lines not to be charged to the customer.

Work Order Reconciliation is not complete.

Complete Work Order Reconciliation, approving expense lines. Note that once this step is complete, this line will be move into the caption for Expense Lines Reconciled Not Linked to POS Expense.

Expense Lines Reconciled Not Linked to POS Expense

Note: This caption includes roll ends identified by the cutter, lines not to be charged to the customer, and product designated for expense in the reconciliation process.

Store Expense has not been completed.

Process Store Expense. Be sure to select the line item to be expensed from the Expense Item Browse in the Store Expense Transaction or this line item will not close, and a Customer Order Disposition will be required to close the line item.  Properly processed expenses will close the line item, removing it from the scorecard.

Store Expense processed incorrectly and customer order was not linked to expense transaction.

Process Customer Order Disposition, linking expense line to the sale of the related customer order. This will close the line item and remove the line from the scorecard.
Lines with No Action

Work order is out for installation- not reconciled.

Complete Work Order Reconciliation. Process related Sale Transaction and/or Expense Transaction and Installer Payment. Be sure to process these transactions correctly by pulling work orders and customer orders in to be processed.  Properly processed transactions will close the line item, removing it from the scorecard. Incorrectly processed transactions will remain on the scorecard under a different caption.

Canceled customer order.

Cancel work order. Be sure to untag any material cut for the order to return this material to available inventory.
