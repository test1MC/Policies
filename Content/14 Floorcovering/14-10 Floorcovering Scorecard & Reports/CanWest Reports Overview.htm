CanWest Reports Overview

This section applies only to stores in Canada.

Job Summary Report - Detailed

The CanWest Job Summary Report is received every Monday and Thursday (excepting holidays) and details the date, location, and status of planned jobs.  This report should be reviewed for:

  • Verify approval has been provided for all open jobs.
  • Completion of known outstanding jobs.

Roll Inventory

The Roll Inventory Report is received every Monday and Thursday (excepting holidays) and details available rolled inventory for each SW location. For each roll, the report will display: Style, Color, Dye Lot, Roll Number, Date Received, Age, Location, Beginning Length, Width, and Available Length. This report should be used to match the Available Length on the report to:

  • Stock levels in MMS and length noted in Roll Tag Book on a weekly basis.
  • Perpetual Tag History Report and Physical Roll Tag Information when completing quarterly site visits.

Item Inventory

The Item Inventory Report is received every Monday and Thursday (excepting holidays) and details available inventory of non-rolled goods such as pad for each SW location. For each roll, the report will display: Product Name, Product Description, Item#, Date Received, Age, Received Quantity, and Physical Inventory. This report should be used to match the Physical Inventory (available quantity) on the report to stock levels in MMS. 

Purchase Order Summary Report

The Purchase Order Summary Report is received every Monday and Thursday (excepting holidays) and shows inventory received into CanWest for each SW store. Verify that items on this report have been received into inventory at your store.
