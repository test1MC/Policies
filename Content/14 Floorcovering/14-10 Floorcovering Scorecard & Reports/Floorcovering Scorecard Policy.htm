Floorcovering Scorecard Policy

  1. Facility Managers should review their stores’ scorecard once a week and work with staff to decrease counts in captions with high scores.
  2. Facilities should use the following high warning counts as a bellwether in determining the status of their system:
    1. Open Work Orders: 500
    2. Labor Lines Not Approved: 250
    3. Labor Lines Approved Not Linked to Payment: 50
    4. Lines Noted as Expense but Not Approved: 250
    5. Expense Lines Reconciled Not Linked to POS Expense: 50
    6. Lines with No Action: 1500
