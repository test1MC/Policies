Cut Options Overview

Several functions exist in the system to allow facilities to better manage how carpet is cut:

  • Material Split
    • Some orders may require you to split the width of a roll. You may access Material Split in one of two ways:
      • If the width of the area requirement is less than the width of the material in stock, the system will prompt you to split the roll once you have entered the Length you wish to cut.
      • You may type S at the Length Field to identify that you wish to split a roll.
  • Stretch Variance
    • Stretch Variance allows facilities to assign a “shorter than required” piece of carpeting (up to six inches), if it is known that the carpeting can be stretched to fit. An Inventory Adjustment is automatically handled to account for this variance and there is no change to customer billing.
  • Roll Balance Threshold
    • You may specify a Roll Balance Threshold for the system. This value will reduce the number of small pieces left in system inventory by prompting you to send the remaining roll balance to a job when the roll balance would fall below the threshold after the required cut is made. Customer billing is not affected and the extra amount sent is staged for Store Expense under CY INV SHRINK 3710-125. See 14-02 FC System for additional information.
    • CANADIAN STORES ONLY: Complete the CanWest Roll Balance Disposal Form available in the Forms Repository. Submission of this form sends the form to CanWest Headquarters. CanWest will adjust inventory to remove the roll balance from available inventory.
