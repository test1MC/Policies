Cut Zone Access Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Select the Floorcovering Menu.

2

Enter your Employee Number and Password.

3

Select the Cut Zone from the Floorcovering Menu.

4

Do want to cut an individual roll?

If Then
Yes Enter the Sales Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the Sales Number.
No Proceed to Cut Rolls for Order Procedure.

Note: To cut a roll where no Customer Order or Work Order exists, select Cut Individual Roll - this could be used to remove damaged or shorted material from available inventory to accommodate a chargeback.

5

Enter the Color Number or press LOOKUP [F3] to search for the Color Number.

6

Select the type of inventory for display:

  • All Inventory.
  • Obsolete Inventory.
  • Asset Value Merchandise.

Note: A listing of available matching rolls in inventory will be returned to the screen. Move to the line of the roll you wish to cut and press enter to select the roll.

7

Enter the Cut Length and press ACCEPT [F1] to proceed.

8

Select Cut Roll from Cut Options.

9

Confirm that you wish to cut the roll when prompted.

10

Scan or enter the Tag ID of the roll to be cut.

Note: Handle any roll balances or stretch variance amounts appropriately.

11

Attach the printed Roll Cut Tag to the cut and update the tag on any remaining inventory with the updated Roll Balance Tag.
