Cut Rolled Goods Policy

  1. Before a job is physically cut, the system must be checked to make sure the appropriate roll is used.
  2. Avoid cuts that will result in a less than room size remnant.
  3. Cuts should NEVER be assigned in the work order until the carpet is actually cut. 
  4. If you want to designate inventory for a specific order but not cut right away, you may reserve the inventory in COE for the order. This will earmark the material for the order and not allow it to be selected for other orders or change inventory levels of the parent roll.
