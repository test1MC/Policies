Cut Zone Overview

The Cut Zone can be used when you need to make cuts for a customer pickup (there will be no work order created), when you have several work orders requiring cuts of the same material and you wish to make these easily at the same time, or when cuts needs to be made to accommodate a chargeback. For more information on using the Cut Zone for chargeback accommodation, please see the Chargeback Section in Isolate Damaged Material, Material Shortages, or Process No Inventory Procedure
