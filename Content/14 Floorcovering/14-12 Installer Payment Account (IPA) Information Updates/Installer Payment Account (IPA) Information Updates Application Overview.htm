Installer Payment Account (IPA) Information Updates Application Overview

The IPA Information Updates Application is an intranet application available on SOURCE that can be used by store personnel to make the following updates to active IPA accounts:

  • Automotive Liability
  • General Liability
  • Workers Compensation Form & Date
  • Workers Compensation Insurance
  • Employers Liability

When information on an installer record expires, the system will generate automatic email reminders to the installers requesting that they submit updated information and documentation.

Note: For any other changes or updates such as change of address, contact your District Credit/Financial Office.
