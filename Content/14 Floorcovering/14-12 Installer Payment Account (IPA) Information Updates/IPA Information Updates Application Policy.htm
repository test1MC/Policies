IPA Information Updates Application Policy

The following policies apply to all dedicated floorcovering facilities and those Paint Stores arranging for the installation of floorcovering:

  1. Usage of the IPA Information Updates Application is required as of 1/1/2013.
  2. Insurance policy and Workers Compensation information received at the facility or store is to be updated by store personnel using the Information Updates Application.
  3. Insurance policy documentation submitted by the installer must be retained in the store in the IPA Documents File per Filing & Retention Drawer 3 (Financial/General Office) Overview requirements. You do not need to send copies of this documentation to your credit/financial office unless you have been directed to do so by your Division.
  4. The Workers Compensation documentation submitted by the installer must be retained in the store in the IPA Documents File per Filing & Retention Drawer 4 (Personnel/Property) Overview requirements. You do not need to send copies of this documentation to your credit/financial office unless you have been directed to do so by your Division.

Note:  The DCO will make insurance policy and Workers Compensation updates when the documentation is sent directly to the DCO by the installer.
