IPA Information Updates Application Procedure

Step

Action

1

From POS desktop, select Browser -> Access SOURCE Intranet -> SOURCE Operations -> Credit / Pricing -> IPA Information Updates.

2

Enter your IPA Account Number or use Find to search for the account you wish to update.

3

Click on the Edit Button.

4

Do you have an insurance policy update to process?

If Then
Yes

To update either Automotive Liability or General Liability, provide the following information from the documentation provided by the installer:

  • Actual Coverage
  • Insurance Company
  • Expiration Date
  • Indicate if SW has been named ‘Additional Insured’
No Proceed to Step 5.

5

Do you have a Workers Compensation Form on file?

If Then
Yes Enter the Date of the form. Proceed to Step 7.
No Proceed to Step 6.

6

Do you have Workers Compensation or Employers Liability to update?

If Then
Yes

To update either Workers Compensation or Employers Liability, provide the following information from the documentation provided by the installer:

  • State(s) Covered
  • Actual Coverage
  • Insurance Company
  • Expiration Date
No Proceed to Step 8.

7

Indicate that you performed this update. 

  • Enter your Name.
  • Select your Position.

8

Click on the Submit Button to save your changes.

Note: Selecting the Cancel Button will cancel the changes made and revert the record back to its prior state.
