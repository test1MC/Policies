Work Order Reconciliation Overview

All work orders require finalization through Reconciliation. Once a job is complete, the installer may bring material back to the facility or may notify a facility that adjustments need to be made to labor. Reconciliation should be handled after the facility receives the installer invoice and allows the facility one final change to adjust material and labor before billing the customer and paying the installer. Encourage installers to provide an invoice within forty-eight hours after the installation is complete. Once Reconciliation is complete, the Work Order will be in Approved status and staged for Installer Payment.
