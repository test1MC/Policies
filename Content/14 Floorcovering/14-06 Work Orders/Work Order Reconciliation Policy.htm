Work Order Reconciliation Policy

  1. Reconciliation is not to be completed until an invoice is received from the installer.
  2. There is currently no process to record non-rolled goods as asset value during reconciliation. Follow these procedures if you have non-rolled goods coming back from a job that you want costed into the job.
    1. During reconciliation, do not bring the non-rolled asset value merchandise back into inventory.
    2. Manually label the non-rolled good as asset value merchandise.
    3. Use sales number 9999-1325-4 to sell non-rolled good asset value merchandise.
