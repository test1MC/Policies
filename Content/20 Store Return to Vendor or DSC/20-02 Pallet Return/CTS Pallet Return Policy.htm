CTS Pallet Return Policy

This section does not apply to Automotive Branches.

  1. All pallets used in shipment of GOM and A/P merchandise are the property and asset of The Sherwin-Williams Company.
  2. Our Company has a recycling program in place to pick up empty pallets, repair if necessary, and return to the manufacturing plants.
  3. CTS is the only carrier who should pick up the pallets.
  4. The CTS drivers are instructed to pick up the pallets when they make STAR order deliveries. The CTS drivers are asked to take back with them at least the same number of pallets they delivered. However if they have a backhaul scheduled, they may have to make a trip back to the store to pick up the empty pallets or request the store to catch the next truck.
  5. Empty pallets should not be allowed to accumulate or be disposed of locally in any way.
