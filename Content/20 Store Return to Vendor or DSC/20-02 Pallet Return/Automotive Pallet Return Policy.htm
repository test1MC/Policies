Automotive Pallet Return Policy

U.S. Automotive Branches Only.

  1. All pallets used in the shipment of GOM and AP merchandise from the DSC or plant are the property and asset of The Sherwin-Williams Company.
  2. Automotive Division operates an approved pallet recycling program and stores should arrange for pallet returns to the Pallet Return Sites as indicated for their state.
  3. Empty pallets should not be allowed to accumulate or be disposed of locally in any way.
  4. Empty pallets should be returned in a minimum quantity of 15 pallets and a maximum quantity of 45 pallets per pallet return. Pallets must be stretch wrapped or banded for return shipment.
    1. Southeastern Freight Lines (SEFL) and AAA Cooper (AACT) maximum pallet quantity is 20 per the contract terms.

Note: If you have any questions or problems regarding pallet returns, please contact the DSC Freight Coordinator at (859) 623-3200 x40.
