Damaged  Containers Procedure

Paint containers may break open after they are in your inventory, and it requires processing an expense for the lost product.

Step Action

1

From POS desktop, select Store -> Point of Sale -> Press Page Down key -> Select Store Expense from the Special Menu -> Destroyed Merchandise 7599-104 -> Enter information.

2

Complete a Quality Assurance Substandard Report. Give complete details as to the cause of damage and the expense involved.

3

Send to the Customer Relations Department at Headquarters. You will then be notified of the disposition of the complaint.
