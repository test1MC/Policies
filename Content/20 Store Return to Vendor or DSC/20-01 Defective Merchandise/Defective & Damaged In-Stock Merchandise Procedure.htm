Defective & Damaged In-Stock Merchandise Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Step Action

1

Determine the manufacturer and the cost of the defective/damaged product using the In-Stock Assortment List.

2

Prepare a chargeback to the appropriate manufacturer.

For more information, see Chargeback Overview.

3

Select the Reason from the Reason Codes to describe the defect/damage:

If Then

Merchandise

Defective

Labor  agreed upon by the store and the manufacturer

Customer Satisfaction/Labor

4

The chargeback must be coded to 7599-859 for merchandise and 7504-837 for any labor agreed upon by the store and the manufacturer.

5

Mail a sample of the defective/damaged product with a copy of the chargeback to the manufacturer.

6

After completing the chargeback, process a store expense using the same prime-sub number.

7

Destroy the merchandise.

Note: Dispose of the merchandise in accordance with all local ordinances and follow the vendor's guidelines for accounting for the disposed materials.

8

If the wallcovering return to the customer is more than $100.00, the customer must sign a release form.

For more information, see Release Form.
