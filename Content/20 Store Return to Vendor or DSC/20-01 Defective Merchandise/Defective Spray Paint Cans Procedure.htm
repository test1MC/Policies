Defective Spray Paint Cans Procedure

Step Action

1

Complete a Quality Assurance Substandard Report.

2

Once the Quality Assurance Substandard Report is approved, the store will receive further instructions for receiving credit and returning the merchandise.

3

Write a letter explaining the facts.

4

Complete an IBAR using Reason Code Two.

5

Prepay the delivery and record the amount on the IBAR.

6

Mail the letter and two copies of the IBAR to:

Sherwin-Williams Company
Store Merchandise Billing
875 Guildhall
101 Prospect Avenue NW
Cleveland OH, 44115

7

Mail or ship a utility copy of the IBAR, the letter, and the product to the Sprayon Quality Control Director, Sprayon Products, Division of Sherwin-Williams' Products, 26300 Fargo Ave., Bedford Heights., Ohio 44146.
