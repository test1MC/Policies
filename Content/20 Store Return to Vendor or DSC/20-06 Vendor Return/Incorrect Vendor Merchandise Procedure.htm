Incorrect Vendor Merchandise Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Occasionally you might receive a customer special order and realize that it was ordered incorrectly by one of your employees.

Step Action

1

Immediately re-order the merchandise for the customer if appropriate using a new purchase order.

2

Check in the special order as you would any other order.

3

Note the problem on the purchase order and designate that the merchandise is on the sales floor.

4

Sticker the merchandise with the appropriate sales number and retail selling price.

5

Mark down the merchandise if necessary. When you sell the merchandise, enter the original sales number and retail selling price. Dollar Discount the merchandise to properly reflect the selling price.

6

Staple the Utility copy of the transaction to the purchase order.

7

Inventory the merchandise as obsolete if not sold prior to the year-end inventory. After the year-end inventory, re-label the merchandise using the correct obsolete sales number.
