Vendor Credit Memo for Returned Goods Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Step Action

1
If Then

You receive a credit memo from the vendor for the goods returned.

The credit memo and Chargeback agree.
  • Make sure it agrees with the store's Chargeback.
  • Attach the credit memo to the store's copy of the Chargeback

The credit memo and the Chargeback do not agree and the vendor made the error.
  • Write the vendor a letter of explanation.
  • Attach a copy of the letter to a copy of the Chargeback.

The credit memo and the store's Chargeback do not agree and the store made the error.
  • Issue another Chargeback showing the amount of the error.
  • File all copies the Merchandise Invoices Holding File and make sure that they clear properly on the SPR.

You issue a Chargeback to a vendor and you do not want a credit memo.
  • Send the vendor a written request for a check.
  • Keep a copy of the letter and a copy of the Chargeback.

Note: During downtime, a manually written chargeback to the Accounts Payable Department. For more information, see 07-02E2 Chargeback. Any unreviewed credit memos sent to Headquarters Accounts Payable will be sent to the store for proper handling.

2

When the settlement check arrives, process a Miscellaneous Cash Receipt transaction coded to 7599-099. Attach a note to a copy of the transaction stating that you want to have the transaction recoded to one of the following prime-subs.

Associated Products

0406-802

Brushes and Rollers

0406-808

Floorcovering

0406-805

Spray Equipment

0406-803

Wallcovering

0406-804

Window Treatment

0406-806

3

Mail the transaction copy and note to:

  • Division Controller - Product Finishes Facilities, Duron Stores, General Polymers, SW Paint Stores
  • Branch Accounting - Automotive Branches

4

Stamp the store's copy of the Chargeback form as "PAID". Attach a copy of the receipt/disbursement form to the store's copy of the Chargeback.
