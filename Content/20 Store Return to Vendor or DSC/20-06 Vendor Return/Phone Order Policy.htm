Phone Order Policy

This section applies only to External Vendors.

  1. You must ask for:
    1. The customer’s name (exactly as it appears on the card), the card account number, and the expiration date.
    2. His or her billing address and the address where the merchandise is to be shipped, if different from the billing address.
  2. The deposit or total purchase amount must be processed as a deposit. You are not permitted to release any merchandise to the customer without getting a credit card imprint and customer signature.
    1. Use the downtime credit card form to obtain an imprint of the customer’s credit card.
    2. If additional manual Credit Card forms are needed, contact Chase Merchant Services at 1-800-252-7762 to request the carbonless Sales Slip [30343] and Credit Slip [10181].
    3. Call 216-566-2255 if you need a new credit card imprinter or if you have questions about your store's credit card authorization numbers.
  3. You must receive voice authorization for the deposit before placing the order.
  4. All processed manual forms including imprinted manual credit card forms and processed manual POST forms containing the complete customer credit card number cannot be retained in the forms container. Retain these forms in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe until you are ready to batch the forms. All completed manual forms must be retained in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe after batching in a folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS – USED. For more information on credit card security, please refer to Payment Card Industry Compliance Overview.
  5. If you have not received a signature and credit card imprint prior to delivery, a signature and credit card imprint must be obtained at delivery.
