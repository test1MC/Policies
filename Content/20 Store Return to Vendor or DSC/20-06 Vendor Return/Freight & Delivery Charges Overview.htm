Freight & Delivery Charges Overview

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Freight charges are based on the cost of incoming freight and the weight of merchandise (such as floorcovering or wallcovering) and are always charged to the customer. The amount collected is entered in POS as part of the total sale using the appropriate freight class number. For more information, see Class Numbers.

Term Definition

Freight In

Transportation charges to get the customer's merchandise to the Sherwin-Williams' store.

Freight Out

Transportation charges for delivery to the customer's home or job site.

Delivery charges are the cost of delivering merchandise from the store to the customer. Delivery costs may or may not be charged to the customer based on the decision of the Store Manager. The amount collected is entered in POS.

Note: If these charges are non-taxable in your store, press the No Tax key before entering the class number, quantity is always 1, then the price.
