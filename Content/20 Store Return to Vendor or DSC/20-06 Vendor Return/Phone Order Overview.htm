Phone Order Overview

This section applies only to External Vendors.

If you take a customer order over the phone, determine if the order is for a regular or new customer.

If Then

Regular Customer – Pickup

Paid by credit card without a deposit
  • You are not required to get a credit card imprint. You will be able to get a card swipe when the customer picks up the merchandise.
  • Create the customer order and place the merchandise in Will Call for customer pickup.
  • Pull the customer order into the point-of-sale transaction when the customer picks up the merchandise.
  • Swipe the customer’s card at form of payment.

Regular Customer – Delivery

Paid by credit card
  • Make sure that you have a customer signed PCI agreement to capture and retain the customer’s credit card information. For more information see Payment Card Industry Compliance Overview.

New Customer
  • They may place orders over the telephone and either put a deposit or pay for the transaction in full.
  • Regardless of the amount, process a deposit to capture the partial or full payment.
  • Before releasing any merchandise, you are required to get a credit card imprint and have the customer sign for the merchandise.
