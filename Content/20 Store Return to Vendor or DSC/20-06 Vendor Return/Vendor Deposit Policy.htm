Vendor Deposit Policy

This section applies only to External Vendors.

  1. 25% - 50% deposit is required if the customer does not have a Sherwin-Williams' Charge Account in good standing. You are required to obtain payment in full if the merchandise is not shipped to the store.
  2. Payment of the deposit must be made using cash, check or credit card.
  3. No merchandise is to be ordered until the necessary deposit is received.

For more information on deposits, refer to Deposit Processing Overview
