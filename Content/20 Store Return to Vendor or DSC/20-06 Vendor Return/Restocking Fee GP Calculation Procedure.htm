Restocking Fee GP Calculation Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Step Action

Are you returning product to the vendor?

If Then

Process the Return/Correction transaction.

Note: Do not change the gross profit percentage. The gross profit percentage automatically adjusts to accommodate the 20% Restocking Fee so it will correctly report the inventory dollars for the returned merchandise. Press ENTER when prompted to enter the last 2 digits of the Sales Number.
No Occasionally you may process a correction or return transaction to correct the billing for the customer. If the wallcovering will not be returned to the vendor, make sure that you change the gross profit to reflect the same gross profit captured on the original sales transaction. This change is necessary to report the correct margin excluding the 20% Restocking Fee.
