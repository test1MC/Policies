Phone Order Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Step Action

1

To accept a credit card for a telephone order, ask the customer for:

  • His or her name exactly as it appears on the credit card.
  • The card Account Number, expiration date on the card.
  • His or her billing address, and the address where the merchandise is to be shipped, if different from the billing address.

2

Process a Deposit Transaction to capture the partial or full payment amount.

3

Enter Telephone Order on the customer signature line.

Note: Remember that you cannot charge the customer for the purchase until the order is shipped. If you process a transaction more than 60 days after the original authorization, you must call to obtain a new approval code.

4

Prior to releasing the merchandise, obtain a credit card imprint and customer signature for the payment amount.

5

All processed manual forms including imprinted manual credit card forms and processed manual POST forms containing the complete customer credit card number cannot be retained in the forms container. Retain these forms in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe until you are ready to batch the forms. All completed manual forms must be retained in a locked filing cabinet or locked store safe after batching in a folder titled MANUAL POST FORMS – USED. For more information on credit card security, please refer to Payment Card Industry Compliance Overview.

6

Process a Correction Transaction to apply the deposit and sell the merchandise.

Note: Make sure that you have a credit card imprint and customer signature on file to verify customer authorization for the purchase.
