Unwanted Vendor Special Order Procedure

This section applies only to External Vendors.

Step Action

1

Use the deposit to cover any return or handling fees.

2

Process a cash return.

Note: For more information see Deposit Refund Procedure.

3

Process the Vendor Handling Charge as a miscellaneous receipt. Code to the appropriate purchase account. Refer to the Prime Sub chart.

4

Give the customer the amount due after any handling charges are deducted.

5

Cross-reference the two transactions and have the customer, employee, and Store Manager sign the transaction form.
