Freight & Delivery Charges Policy

This section applies only to External Vendors.

  1. Charge the customer tax on all incoming freight if the store is reimbursed for the freight or shipping by the customer (charging a customer tax on shipping does not apply if the customer is tax exempt).
    1. Stores in Maryland are not to charge tax on any freight or shipping charges.

Note: Carpeting may have a delivered cost (including freight charges). Use the delivered store cost as your total store cost when figuring the gross profit for a sale.
