Refund Customer Deposit Procedure

This section applies only to orders placed to external vendors.

There may be circumstances where it is necessary to refund the deposit to the customer. For more information see Deposit Refund Procedure.

A deposit is declared “uncleared" if it has been held at the store for more than 90 days. Try to contact the customer and arrange for pickup of their special order material.

If Then

The customer does not pick up their special-order material.
  • Arrange a refund.

You can't contact the customer.
  • Process a special order deposit cash return. For more information see Deposit Refund Procedure.
  • Enter the amount of deposit as a Miscellaneous Receipt coded to 9130-999.
  • Press the COMMENTS [F9] key to enter specific information about the uncleared deposit.

The merchandise cannot be returned to the vendor (i.e. cuts of carpet).
  • This receipt should be processed to 9130-999 (Misc. Income).
  • This will show on P&L Line 72.
  • The merchandise should then be re-labeled for a Quick Sale and OBSOLETED on the next inventory.
