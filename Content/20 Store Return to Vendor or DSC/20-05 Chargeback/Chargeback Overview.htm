Chargeback Overview

A return of merchandise to a vendor/supplier, other than a Sherwin-Williams Distribution Service Center, involves completing a Chargeback. Chargebacks are prepared in your store to:

  • Correct freight variances
  • Return merchandise
  • Process complaint claims against a vendor
  • Confirm an issued credit memo
  • Price adjustment

Choose the correct Chargeback Reason to ensure that the Chargeback is processed correctly.
