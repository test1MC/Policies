Chargeback Policy

  1. The vendor must be contacted and authorization obtained prior to merchandise being returned to the vendor. All allowable returns must be returned to the vendor promptly.
  2. Chargebacks for quantity and pricing errors are automatically processed by Headquarters based on the comparison between the Purchase Order, receiving record, and invoice.
  3. Do not prepare a Chargeback if the error is less than $5.00.
  4. The Chargeback must be released when complete.
  5. Chargebacks must be filled out completely before they will be processed by Headquarters Accounts Payable. Retain a copy of the chargeback with the call tag and return information.
  6. Defective merchandise must be packaged properly when returned to the vendor. For more information, please see Defective Merchandise Overview.
  7. The appropriate return or handling fee must be added if the return involves a customer's special order merchandise.
  8. If you receive a Freight Variance Report, you will be required to review the freight billed amount to determine if a chargeback is required.
