Change Epson Thermal Printer Ribbon Procedure

Step

Action

1

Power on the printer, and then open its front cover.

Note: Do not open the unit during printing.

2

Lift the used ribbon cassette out of the printer.

3

Turn the knob on the new ribbon cassette to tighten the ribbon.

4

Insert the new ribbon cassette into the printer in the position shown on the inside of the printer. Turn the knob again to seat the ribbon.

5

Close the front cover of the printer.
