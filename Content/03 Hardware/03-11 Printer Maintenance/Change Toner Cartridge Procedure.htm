Change Toner Cartridge Procedure

You will need to change the Lexmark toner cartridge shortly after you notice the print quality’s degradation. A message saying "Toner Low" will display. Before changing the toner, use the ink that is left. Remove the cartridge, point the arrows down, grip the handles, and shake the cartridge back and forth. Reinstall the cartridge and press GO.

Note: For more information about ordering a new cartridge, see All Stores – Reorder Printers Overview

Step

Action

1

Make sure that the power is on. Push the upper release latch on the front left side of the printer to release the upper front door.

2

Grasp the old toner cartridge by the handgrips and pull the cartridge up and out. Never touch the photoconductor drum on the underside of the print cartridge! Use the handhold on the cartridge to avoid touching this area.

3

Take the new toner cartridge out of the box and all packaging. Install the new toner cartridge by aligning the side slides on the cartridge with the slots on top of the cradle inside the printer. Use the colored arrows on the cartridge and cradle as your guide.

4

Close the upper front door.
