Replace Cisco Router with Motorola Access Point Procedure

This Access Point was sent to replace the Cisco router. It is used to interface between the AML inventory scanner and the POS system.

New Motorola Access Point Kit Contents:

  1. Motorola AP
  2. Power Injector
  3. 2 Patch Cables
  4. Power Cord

Step

Action

Replace Cisco Router

1

Locate the Cisco router.

Note: For stores that have a network wiring cabinet, it is usually sitting on top of the cabinet.

Other stores may have them in their office: sometimes near the manager’s station, mounted on the wall somewhere, on top of a file cabinet, or even on the floor under a desk. There are some that will be located higher up in the warehouse as the signals are best transmitted if they are mounted as high as possible.

2

Power off the AML scanner before beginning the installation.

Power down the Cisco router before removing this device. Locate the patch cable that is plugged into the back of the Cisco router. This cable will either be blue or yellow.  Follow the cable down and disconnect it from either a Cisco switch or an ATI switch. Unplug the power cord to the power supply of the Cisco. Put the Cisco router and the power supply with the power cable in a box to return with the return tag to return to 5R for proper disposal.
