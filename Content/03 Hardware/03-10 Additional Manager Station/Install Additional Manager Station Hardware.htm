Install Additional Manager Station Hardware

Step Action

1

Plug the Tripp-Lite surge protector into an available outlet. Turn the Surge on and verify the protection working light is green and the wiring fault light is off.

2

Unpack the LENOVO ThinkCentre PC, the LENOVO 17” LCD Display, the 7’ Ethernet Patch Cable, and the mouse pad.

3

The new PC and Display will both be placed on the desk or counter. Connect the display, mouse, and keyboard to the PC. Connect the power cords to the equipment and plug them into the Tripp-Lite surge.

4

Plug one end of the 7’ Ethernet patch cable into the wall jack and the other end into the Ethernet port on the ThinkCentre.

5

In the network cabinet, install the 1’ Ethernet patch cable for the corresponding data jack on the patch panel to the ATI switch. Verify there is a LINK/ACTIVITY light lit.

6

Turn on the PC and Display. The Source Computer Desktop should appear:

Note: When the Source Desktop appears, the manager’s station will be ready for use.

Call the Sherwin-Williams Store Computer Operations Help Desk at 216-566-2740 with any installation questions.
