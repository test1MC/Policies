Install Manager Computer Hardware Procedure

Step

Action

1

Go to the Manager’s office.

2

The new PC and display will both be placed on top of the Manager’s desk.  Do not place the PC on the floor.

3

Connect the display, keyboard, and mouse to the PC. If unsure of the connections, look at the diagram in Main POS & Main Manager Station Installation Overview. Connect the power cords to the equipment and plug them into the Tripp Lite surge protector.

Main Manager Layout

4

Connect one of the 7’ Ethernet cables from the ThinkCentre PC to the network jack.

5

Go to the network cabinet.  Install a 1’ Ethernet patch cord for the corresponding data jack on the patch panel to the ATI switch. Verify there is a LINK/ACTIVITY light lit.

6

Go back to the Manager’s desk.

7

Attach the keyboard cover on the keyboard if not already installed.

8

Turn on the PC and Display.

9

The system will stop at a 300 second count down. Press ENTER to bypass and continue the boot sequence. Appearance of the Source screen will indicate completion after approximately 7-10 minutes.

10

Go to the network cabinet and verify there is a LINK/ACTIVITY light lit.
