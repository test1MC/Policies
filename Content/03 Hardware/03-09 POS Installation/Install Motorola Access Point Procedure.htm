Install Motorola Access Point Procedure

Step Action

1

Go to the Network Cabinet and plug the Ethernet cable from the “Data in” jack of the power injector to the ATI Switch.

2

Plug the power cord from the power injector into the surge protector in the network cabinet.

3

Once the power has been plugged in, it will take about 15-20 minutes for the access point to be ready to work. When it is ready, the amber light will remain on steady and the green light will flash slowly.

4

Turn on the AML gun.

5

Once the AML gun reaches the login screen, peel off the back of the mounting tape on the back of the bracket on the Motorola access point and mount it up as high as possible.

Note: Do not place the Motorola access point inside the cabinet.

6

The installation of hardware for this installation is complete.

7

Contact the SCO Help Desk at 216-566-2740 and notify the coordinator that main POS and manager station installation is complete, and the system is in polling.
