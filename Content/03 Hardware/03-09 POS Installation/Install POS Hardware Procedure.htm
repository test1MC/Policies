Install POS Hardware Procedure

Below is a picture of where to place the POS equipment in and on the counters.

Step

Action
POS & Primary Server Hardware

1

Give the 3 Batching Bins to the Store Manager.

2

Install a Tripp Lite surge protector inside the counter. Use an open AC receptacle.  Do not plug into another power tab or extension cord. Turn the Surge Protector on and verify the protection light is green and the wiring fault light is off.

3

Unpack the Lenovo ThinkCentre PC that is labeled primary. Place it in the POS wrap counter. Plug in the power cord to the Tripp Lite surge protector.

4

Unpack APG cash drawer.

5

Remove the shipping cardboard from the APG cash drawer.  The drawer should open.

6

Remove the keys from the cash till inside the drawer.

7

Close the drawer and use the keys to turn the lock to normal operation.

8

Plug the large end of the gray cash drawer cable into the bottom of the cash drawer.

9

Install the APG cash drawer on a shelf inside the POS counter.

10

The cash drawer keys are to be given to the Store Manager.

11

Unpack the Epson printer and place it on the POS counter. Plug it into the new Surge Arrest.

12

Plug the small end of the APG cash drawer cable into the port marked DK on the Epson printer.

13

Connect a USB cable from the Epson printer to the Lenovo Think Centre PC.
ThinkCentre POS Connections

14

Turn on the Epson Validation Printer.

15

The validation printer will shuttle. Only the power light should be on at this time.

16

Unpack the LENOVO 17” Display. Connect the display to the Think Centre PC.  Plug in the power cord to the Tripp Lite surge protector.

17

Connect the mouse and keyboard to the ThinkCentre PC.

18

Attach the keyboard skin to the keyboard if this has not already been done.

19

Unpack the Motorola scanner gun, cable, and base.

20

Place the base on the POS counter.

21

Connect the cable to the Motorola scanner gun.

22

Plug the Motorola scanner gun into one of the front USB ports on the ThinkCentre PC.

23

Unpack the Magtek USB card reader.

24

Attach the Magtek card reader to the left or right side of the display using the tape provided.

Note: If possible, ask the manager what side of the LCD is preferable for Magtek location.

25

Do not cover the serial number of the card reader.
Magtek Card Reader Placement

26

Plug the Magtek card reader into any open USB port on ThinkCentre PC (Not the Epson printer).

27

Connect one of the Ethernet cables from the ThinkCentre PC to the network jack under the counter.

28

In the network cabinet, install a 1’ Ethernet patch cord for the corresponding data jack on the patch panel to the ATI switch.

29

Unpack the Lexmark USB laser printer and place it in the POS counter.  Open the front covers and remove all of the shipping retainers and foam from the starter toner cartridge. Attach the paper hold down wire to printer. Load paper into the Lexmark laser printer.

30

Connect the power cord to the Lexmark printer and plug directly into the outlet, not the surge protector.

31

Connect the 15’ USB cable from the Lexmark to the ThinkCentre PC.

32

Turn on all the equipment.  Turn the ThinkCentre PC on last. Go back to the network cabinet and verify there is a LINK/ACTIVITY light lit.

33

Click on the Store button. The Main Menu should appear.

Note: The boot-up time takes approximately 5-10 minutes. It is complete when the STORE COMPUTER SOURCE desktop appears.

34

Select Option 4 – Help Desk from the Main Menu.

35

Select Option 17 – Validation Printer and press ENTER.

36

Enter the Help Desk password, f1xm3up, and press ENTER.

37

The Test Validation Printer screen should appear:

38

Press ACCEPT [F1].

Note: A sample thermal receipt should print out of the Epson receipt printer.

39

The following message should appear:

40

Insert a sheet of blank paper into the validation printer of the Epson printer.  Press the SPACE BAR when the piece of paper is inserted.

41

A sample validation should print out of the Epson printer. The following message should appear:

42

Remove the slip.  Press SPACE BAR when the piece of paper is removed. Check for printing on the test slip. An Epson can pass the VP test without a ribbon, so if nothing prints, inform the manager to order ribbon from Office Max part #:S9ERC32B.

43

The cash drawer should open and the following should appear:

44

Close the cash drawer. Press SPACE BAR when the cash drawer is closed.

45

The follow message should appear:

46

Press SPACE BAR to return to the HELP DESK Menu.

47

Select Option 20 - Invoice Printer, and then press ENTER.

48

The Test Invoice Printer screen should appear:

49

Press ACCEPT [F1].

50

A sample invoice should print to the Lexmark printer.  Press Y, then press SPACE BAR to return to the Help Desk Menu.

51

Select Option 19 – Bar Code Scanner and press ENTER.

52

Use the Motorola barcode scanner gun to scan any available product. Verify the UPC number is captured.

53

Press CANCEL [F5] to return to the Help Desk Menu.

54

Select Option 18 – Mag Stripe Reader and press ENTER.

55

The Test Mag Strip Reader screen should appear:

56

Swipe any available credit card through the Mag Stripe Reader. The display should change to showing the data from the card swiped.

57

Press CANCEL [F5] to return to the Help Desk Menu.

58

Select Option 3 – Polling and enter the Help Desk password, f1xm3up, and press ENTER.

59

Choose 2. Upload and Download. Press ACCEPT [F1]. “Waiting for line connect” will appear in the message box.

60

Instruct the Store Manager that this system must remain at this screen for the store’s information to be downloaded.

61

The main POS SOURCE terminal should be in working order before this workstation installation is completed.
